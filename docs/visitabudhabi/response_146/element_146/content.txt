Beach Rotana Abu Dhabi

























Abu Dhabi






 + Add to trip planner







Review your trip









This five-star beachfront hotel is located in the centre of Abu Dhabi and is the flagship property of the Rotana Group. Guests are a short stroll from one of the city’s most renowned shopping destinations, Abu Dhabi Mall, which is directly linked to the hotel via an exclusive entrance.

Its comfortable rooms and suites are complemented by award-winning restaurants, a tranquil spa and an atmospheric beach club. A total of 11 global dining destinations await, including Finz, a speciality seafood eatery situated on the water.  Dining options range from French-Polynesian, Indian, Italian, German, and more.

For leisure, guests can enjoy the 120-meter private beach or unwind at Zen the Spa. For business, the hotel’s regal ballrooms and conference & meeting facilities are ideal for groups of 10 to 1,500. The well-appointed facilities include the state-of-the-art video communications technology, TelePresence.

The hotel has over 3,700 sqm² of indoor and outdoor conference space and seafront outdoor function space, making it a favourite with discerning MICE organisers. The 1,250 sqm Al Thuraya Grand Ballroom can accommodate up to 1500 guests. Equipped with excellent acoustics and an intricate lighting system, it features sophisticated audio-visual systems.
Business travellers can also connect with the rest of the world through the state-of-the-art TelePresence Suite – video communications technology that allows guests to communicate with their counterparts in up to six different locations worldwide. There’s an elegantly marbled pre-function area, along with 12 meeting rooms.
