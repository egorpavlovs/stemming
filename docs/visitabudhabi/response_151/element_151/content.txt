Corniche Hotel Abu Dhabi

























Abu Dhabi





 + Add to trip planner







Review your trip









Situated on Abu Dhabi’s central Khalifa Street, Corniche Hotel Abu Dhabi offers 305 luxurious guestrooms and suites, featuring breathtaking views of the Arabian Gulf and the Capital Garden. Guests will benefit from being in one of the city’s most convenient locations, a stone’s throw from the main malls and only 35 km away from Abu Dhabi International Airport.
There are luxurious guest rooms and suites, ranging to the palatial Royal Suite, and a wide range of culinary options, including three restaurants, two bars, a lobby lounge, swimming pool and a fitness centre with a sauna and steam room to aid travellers recovering from jet lag. The hotel also offers a free scheduled pick ups and drop offs to the city’s main attractions: Yas Waterworld, Ferrari World, Corniche Public Beach, and World Trade Center Mall.
 
Guests have a variety of dining options to suit any palate – from traditional wood-fired pizzas at Biscotti, to authentic Middle Eastern dishes at Marakesh. Or they can choose from a variety of local and international selections at Citrus, or step into our traditional English bar at Cristal for a relaxed atmosphere suitable for family and friends.
