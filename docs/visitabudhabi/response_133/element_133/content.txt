NSO celebrates important milestones in the history of Hollywood, renowned worldwide as the home of movies. From the town's Golden Age, the rise of movie idols in iconic films, to fabulous musicals and the Oscars, Hollywood’s legacy continues into the modern day with such memorable orchestral scores: An American in Paris, Apollo 13, Adventures of Robin Hood, The Magnificent 7, Harry Potter, and a few surprises.

Come celebrate some of the most memorable film scores with NSO and relive the magic of Hollywood! 
 
Note:
• Doors open 60 minutes prior to concert start.
• Latecomers will be seated, when appropriate, during the performance.
• The use of mobile phones, audio and video devices are strictly prohibited during the performance.  This concert is being professionally recorded.
• Food and beverage will be sold prior to the performance and during the interval


 





 
Organised by: NSO Symphony Orchestra (UAE)
