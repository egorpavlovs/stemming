The Abu Dhabi World Professional Jiu-Jitsu Championship 2019 will host more than 7,000 elite athletes from over 100 countries, who will fight it out for the championship titles.
This week-long event features the Abu Dhabi World Youth Championship, the Jiu-Jitsu Masters Championship, and blue to black belt tournaments.

Categories include:
- White belt for players above 18 years old
- Para Jiu-Jitsu
- World Youth Championship for players below 18 years old
- Jiu-Jitsu Masters Championship for ages above 30
- Male and female black belt

This event is open to all nationalities.






Organised by: UAE JIU-JITSU Federation
