Bett MEA is the region’s leading education technology summit and expo.
 The event is a unique opportunity to discuss the latest trends, challenges and advancements in education technology across the world, with others that have the power and responsibility to make a difference.
 The most innovative figures in education will be sharing their experience, views and ideas for shaping the future of education in Asia.
