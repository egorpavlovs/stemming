Crowne Plaza Abu Dhabi

























Abu Dhabi





 + Add to trip planner







Review your trip









This upscale hotel is in the city centre, just minutes away from the Corniche, the main shopping districts and key business locations. It provides a friendly, personalized service and a range of facilities from WiFi access throughout the whole hotel, to complimentary shuttle bus services to key tourism hot spots.

Its range of well-appointed rooms are spacious and guests have the choice of award-winning international restaurants and bars. Spaccanapoli Ristorante dishes up authentic Italian cuisine including its signature one-meter wood-fired pizza whilst Cho Gao is a unique contemporary Asian dining experience – Abu Dhabi’s only non-smoking restaurant, bar and lounge. The legendary Heroes Bar has live music, a DJ and enough wide screens to satisfy any sports lover.

The Garden Restaurant provides a different themed buffet daily. It offers some of the best Abu Dhabi nightlife at Level Lounge, a rooftop chill-out bar, with international DJs and designer cocktails. The long rooftop pool offers skyline views and an opportunity to exercise or relax. Other facilities include a well-equipped fitness centre, massage services, fitness classes such as Zumba, a steam, sauna and a whirlpool overlooking the city.
