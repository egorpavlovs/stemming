Cruises



The Arabian Gulf’s clean, calm waters and almost guaranteed sunshine are perfect for cruise tourism. 




































Abu Dhabi Pearl Journey



Get a better understanding of the emirate’s ancient pearling industry with this once-in-a-lifetime experience.




 

















Abu Dhabi Festival



The multi-discipline celebration of music, theatre, dance, and visual and traditional arts – returns to the capital.




 

















Falcon Show



Learn more about the one of the emirate’s most important traditions with a historical journey through falconry – an art that started over two millennia ago.




































Dave Matthews Band



The Dave Matthews Band is set to kick off their 2015 International Tour this October in du Arena on Yas Island.




 

















Nurai Island



Just a 10-minute boat ride from Saadiyat Island, the stunning Nurai Island is a secluded piece of paradise in the pristine waters of the Arabian Gulf.




 

















Dining
