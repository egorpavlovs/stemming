Golf, Glorious Golf!



The Abu Dhabi HSBC Golf Championship celebrates its 10th anniversary edition! The game’s greatest golfers will descend upon Abu Dhabi Golf Club in January as they prepare to battle it out for the iconic Falcon Trophy and a share of the $2.7million prize fund. 



















 






 



 Simply Spectacular Stopover



The boats have arrived and the Destination Village is now open! Make your way to the Corniche Breakwater to ensure you don’t miss any of the amazing action at this fantastic festival of sailing, entertainment, watersports, heritage and family fun. 




































Marvellous Marinas



The capital has a glorious maritime history stretching back hundreds of years. Connect with this legacy, whilst also immersing yourself in the luxurious surroundings of one of Abu Dhabi’s many stunning waterfront marinas!




 

















Piano Prodigy 



Don’t miss Georgian pianist Khatia Buniatishvili performing at the Manarat Al Saadiyat on Saadiyat Island at 8pm on Wednesday 7th January as part of Abu Dhabi Classics 2014/15 – the international concert season.




 

















Capital Corniche



Make the most of the glorious winter weather by taking the whole family down to Abu Dhabi’s spectacular Corniche. Spread across eight kilometres of manicured waterfront, you can take advantage of children’s play areas, bike hire, cafés, restaurants and, of course, the Blue Flag-awarded beach.




































Watersports



From wakeboarding, waterskiing and surfing to kayaking, diving and stand-up paddling, Abu Dhabi truly has something for every watersports’ enthusiast!




 

















Wadi Adventure



‘Wet, wild and wonderful’ is the only way to describe Wadi Adventure, the region’s first man-made white water rafting, kayaking surf facility built in the foothills of Al Ain’s majestic Jebel Hafeet. 




 

















Boat Tours 



The city of Abu Dhabi is encircled by an expanse of beautiful coastline and surrounded by a plethora of islands. And where better to experience all this natural beauty? Why, from the sea of course!
























 










Desert Safari and Camping 



Endless rolling dunes and breathtakingly scenic sandscapes are in plentiful supply all over our beautiful emirate and, now the cooler season is upon us, there’s never been a better time to get out there and explore Abu Dhabi’s deserts.
























 










Tour Bahr



Dinner with a view – dine in style and take in the stunning views of the Corniche skyline aboard The Taj – Tour Bahr’s custom-built wooden dhow.
