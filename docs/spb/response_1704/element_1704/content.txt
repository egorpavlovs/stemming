                    INDUSTRIAL ELECTRICAL EQUIPMENT AND DRIVES
                
                
                    
                        
                            19 - 21 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.farexpo.ru/pet/exhibition/about/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Exhibition Industrial Electrotechnics Exhibition has been held since 2008. By its topic and exposition the event is closely related to the well-known exhibition automation. Since the year 2014 the exhibition profile has been extended by adding Drive mechanisms section.</p>

<p>Purpose of the exhibition is to help along establishing efficient business contacts between customers and producers of novelties in the field of industrial electrotechnics and drive mechanisms.</p>

<p>EXHIBITION PROFILE OF THE INDUSTRIAL ELECTROTECHNICS EXHIBITION<br />Factory-assembled controls, switchgears, protective appliances<br />Electric machines and power supplies<br />Drive Mechanisms<br />Manufacturing electrical equipment<br />Industrial lighting facilities<br />Electrotechnical components and materials, tool<br />ACS and drive mechanisms<br />Automated design engineering system</p>
