                    11 International Conference on Environmental Management of Enclosed Coastal Seas
                
                
                    
                        
                            22 - 27 aug 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Ecology and Environment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://emecs-sc2016.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The 11th International Conference on Environmental Management of Enclosed Coastal Seas</strong> (EMECS11) will be held as with &quot;Sea Coasts&quot;&nbsp;Working Group of the Russian Academy of Science Council on World Ocean, from 22nd to 27th of August, 2016 in St.Petersburg, Russia.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Main topics of the &nbsp;Conference:</p>

<ul>	<li>Coastal systems and their dynamics (from coast to water and from water to coast);</li>	<li>Coastal erosion and dynamical processes in the nearshore zone;</li>	<li>GIS &amp; marine spatial planning;</li>	<li>Climate change in the changing world. Coastal adaptation to climate change;</li>	<li>Construction and exploitation of hydraulic engineering structures and dredging in the coastal areas;</li>	<li>Study and monitoring of coastal and marine ecosystems;</li>	<li>Approaches to and issues of processes in the coastal areas modelling and monitoring;</li>	<li>Interactions between coastal zone and the open sea: impact on the ecosystems;</li>	<li>Ecological sensitivity of coastal areas: anthropogenic loads and natural disasters;</li>	<li>ICZM &ndash; case study and new experience;</li>	<li>Sustainable use and development of coastal resources: effective management and approaches;</li>	<li>Legal and political issues of enclosed coastal seas management;</li>	<li>Human resource and indigenous dimension of enclosed coastal seas management.&nbsp;</li></ul>
