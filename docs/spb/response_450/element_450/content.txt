                                  Shalyapin apartment Museum was opened on 11 April 1975 in St. Petersburg in the last apartment of the singer. Here on Aptekarsky island, the house 2–B on Perm street (now Graftio street), the great singer lived from 1915 to 1922, and from here he left for abroad.
