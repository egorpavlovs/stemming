                Chinese companies look forward to hold events in Saint Petersburg
            
            August 20 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                Russian Union of Exhibitions and Fairs gave the presentation dedicated to the convention activity as the tool for development of Russian-Chinese economic and trade affairs. The event was held in the Trade Representation of the Russian Federation in the People's Republic of China on August 7.

Russian exhibition companies from Vladivostok, Ufa, Kazan, Moscow, Perm, Novokuznetsk, Sochi and over 70 Chinese businessmen, government employees, representatives of associations participated in the event.

General director of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Anna Chetvergova made the presentation about the potential of the city in the field of business tourism. 1 million 125 thousand Chinese arrived in Russia in 2014. Saint Petersburg becomes ever more hospitable and participates in the national program for more comfortable environment creation for travelers from China known as China Friendly. Transport accessibility is developing, direct flights from Beijing, Shanghai, Ürümqi and Lanzhou are functioning. Chinese Business Centre works in Saint Petersburg since March and hosts meetings of official delegations 2-3 times per week.

Communications continued in the course of the b2b negotiations. The participation of delegations from Russia and China in specialized forums and exhibitions became one of the topics. China Chamber of International Commerce and Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau discussed the organization of the round-table. As envisioned by representatives of Chamber, the event in Saint Petersburg logically will continue the exhibition of building, equipment and healthy living and will conduce networking between companies from China and North-West region of Russia. Such requests confirm high interest of the government and business circles of China to the expansion of business tourism to Saint Petersburg.
