                    Novotel Saint Petersburg Centre Hotel
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Located in the very heart of the city, the Novotel St. Petersburg Center hotel has everything you need for business meetings or city tours. Each room has free high-speed Internet access and satellite TV. Guests can enjoy the restaurant, lobby bar offering a range of drinks, snacks, cocktails and desserts, a fitness center with hammam, free car park, concierge and gift shop. A warm welcome and first-class service await you at Novotel.
