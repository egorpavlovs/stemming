                                  The concert-sport complex SIBUR ARENA is the multi-functional building on Krestovskiy Island.The main arena with a capacity of 7120 places and 12 VIP-lounges for 9-44 persons is suitable for holding concerts, circus shows, exhibitions and corporate events. There are also two additional halls, 8 dressing rooms, press center, buffets in the lobby, café on the entry level, hotel and restaurant.The ceremony of Top-50. Sobaka.ru, the exhibition of international real estate, the celebration of Machinist day were held in SIBUR ARENA for the last year.You can go to SIBUR ARENA by car through Yuzhnaya or Severnaya road. To go from Krestovskiy island metro station it takes 15 minutes walking through the main alley in the park. The 10, 14 and 25 buses stop is located 10 minutes walking from the complex.
