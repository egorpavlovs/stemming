                    Saint-Petersburg Express established in 1992 is a part of the RESTEC Group.The company organizes and holds international exhibitions, fairs, seminars, and business meetings in the field of tourism industry in St. Petersburg as well as organizes collective stands at leading international and Russian exhibitions. We have held over 180 international and regional events since 1992
