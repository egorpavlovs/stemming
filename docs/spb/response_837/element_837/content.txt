                    StemCellBio-2018
                
                
                    
                        
                            15 - 17 nov 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                        Solo Sokos Hotel Palace Bridge
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://stemcellbio.ru/english/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    International Conference “StemCellBio-2018: Fundamental Science as the basis of Tranclation al Medicine”.The official languages are Russian and English.The scientific program of SCB-2018 will reflect the most current investigations of fundamental functioning mechanisms of stem cells. Special attention will be paid to the newest technologies of cell products creation for biomedical purposes.Main subjects:

Genetic and epigenetic mechanisms of differentiation and reprogramming;Mesenchymal stem cells;Haematopoietic stem cells;Induced pluripotent stem cells;Resident stem cells;Stem cells for modeling pathological processes;Cell technologies for clinical application;Immunotherapy and dendritic vaccines.
