                                  Park Inn by Radisson Nevsky offers comfort and convenience. Guests of the hotel enjoy easy access to attractions and transportation like the Moskovsky Railway Station. Discover nearby sites of interest such as The Holy Trinity Alexander Nevsky Lavra, the Hermitage Museum and Stockmann shopping mall. Stay in one of 269 comfortable rooms and suites featuring heated bathroom floors, Free Wireless High-speed Internet access and satellite television. Dine on delicious Bavarian cuisine and freshly brewed beer at the Paulaner Bar and Restaurant. An on-site fitness centre and ample underground parking make the trip easier, and 2 modern conference rooms help keep it productive.
