                    Investfunds Forum IX - Institutional Investor Conference
                
                
                    
                        
                            17 - 18 may 2018

                         Finance

                    Investfunds Forum is the largest in Russia independent event for institutional investors. It has been gathering over 200 investment market players in St. Petersburg for nine years in a row. 

Traditional conference topics include financial market regulation, pension industry prospects, investment ideas and strategies. The Forum is attended by regulators, private pension funds, asset managers and insurance companies, brokers, banks, endowment funds, self-regulatory organizations and representatives of the market infrastructure.
