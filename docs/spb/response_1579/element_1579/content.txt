                    The International Exhibition and Congress High Technologies. Innovations. Investments (Hi-Tech) 
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        High technology and Innovations
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.ptfair.ru/exhibition/high-technologies/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The International Exhibition and Congress High Technologies. Innovations. Investments (Hi-Tech) </strong>is one of the oldest, dedicated and respectful events in Russia intended to promote advanced technologies, innovations, investment projects in science and technology, and to ensure effective cooperation between scientific organizations, industry players and potential investors.</p>

<p>Traditionally, state research centers, R&amp;D establishments, higher educational institutions, technoparks, and regional expositions are the mainstay of the Exhibition featuring their innovative potential.</p>

<p><strong>Sections of &ldquo;High Technologies. Innovations. Investments&rdquo;.</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>High technologies: aerospace technologies, biotechnologies, healthcare, telecommunication systems, information technologies, electronics, environment, educational programmes</li>	<li>Industrial innovations in mechanical engineering, metallurgy, casting and foundry, automotive industry, power industry and energy efficiency, chemicals and new materials</li>	<li>Nanotech: nanomaterials and nanotechnologies, equipment, tolling, application</li>	<li>Investments, research funding, venture funding</li></ul>

<p>The exhibition also features the contest &ldquo;The Best Innovation Project and the Best Scientific and Technological Development of the Year&rdquo; serving to foster innovative activities at enterprises. Throughout the Contest history over 3000 developments of the national economy were awarded.</p>

<p>The exhibition programme is organised along with a diverse business programme HI-TECH Day. With its motto &ldquo;From innovation to implementation&rdquo; the exhibition and congress serves as a bridge between science and production, developers and investors.</p>
