                                  Grand Peterhof Spa Hotel is a new country hotel built in a style of 19th century manor house. The hotel is located between the Konstantinovsky Palace and Peterhof State Museum. 37 rooms of the hotel are decorated in elegant style and fully equipped allowing comfortable rest and creating a special cosy atmosphere. Korkuli Restaurant, LobbyBar, swimmingpool, Spa.
