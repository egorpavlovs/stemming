                                  Restaurant "The Big Column hall" is the official partner of the State Museum-Preserve "Pavlovsk". We are located at a distance of about 15 km from St. Petersburg in historic building of The Pavlovsk Palace. The restaurant has beautiful hall for 250 guests and terrace. This spacious venue in classical style is suitable for business meetings, presentations and seminars as well as for organizing gala-dinners, buffets, meetings "without ties". We will be glad to organize catering service in the palace halls and parks. Moreover we and our partners will help you in the creation of cultural and entertainment part of your event. Unique atmosphere, high quality service and excellent cuisine - our distinctive features for over 20 years.
