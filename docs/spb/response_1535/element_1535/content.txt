                    The 23rd exhibition of building and finishing materials InterStroyExpo
                
                
                    
                        
                            19 - 21 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Construction, Infrastructure
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.interstroyexpo.com/en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>InterStroyExpo / WorldBuild St. Petersburg exhibition</strong> is an effective tool to increase sales of building and finishing materials, attract new trade partners and allow a direct contact with representatives of building companies of the Northwest Russia.</p>

<p>Since 2017 InterStroyExpo exhibition will be a part of the largest international portfolio of building and finishing materials exhibitions WorldBuild. This portfolio includes more than 20 exhibitions in various countries and regions representing promising sales markets for building and finishing materials.</p>

<p>InterStroyExpo / WorldBuild St. Petersburg sections:</p>

<ul>	<li>Building materials</li>	<li>Facades &amp; Roofing</li>	<li>Windows, profile, accessories</li>	<li>Gates</li>	<li>Building equipment &amp; tools</li>	<li>Finishing materials</li>	<li>Flooring</li>	<li>Doors &amp; Locks</li>	<li>Bathrooms</li>	<li>Ceramics</li>	<li>Paints and coatings</li>	<li>Lighting &amp; Electrical products</li>	<li>Wallpaper</li>	<li>Construction machinery</li>	<li>Lifts &amp; Escalators</li></ul>
