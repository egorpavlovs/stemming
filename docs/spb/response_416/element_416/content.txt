                                  The Grand Hotel Emerald is conveniently located in the historical part of St. Petersburg where the tranquil charm of the old city blends with business and greatest attractions. The classical interiors have absorbed the fascinating controversy of the city on the Neva river and they are in perfect harmony with the modern building executed in the late rational art nouveau style.Since 2003 the hotel has been welcoming its guests who appreciate luxury and thoughtfulness in detail. Few St. Petersburg hotels can offer such level of comfort to those who want to enjoy historical and cultural heritage of the city and to business travelers. The 90 rooms and suites of different kinds, from Standard to King DeLuxe Executive suites, prove that the hotel is one of the best in St. Petersburg. The high standards are verified officially: The Grand Hotel Emerald is the only Russian member of the international association Prestige Hotels of the World which unites more than 1600 prestigious luxury hotels all over the world.The hotel features Emerald spa centre, Top Gym fitness centre, Velvet beauty parlour, Gzhel designer-cuisine restaurant, Versailles café and Suvorovsky lobby bar. To our guests and partners we offer two elegant banquet halls for both private and corporate parties as well as professionally equipped meeting rooms Amethyst, Alexandrite and Diamond which are perfect for every type of convention – business meetings, seminars or conferences.

Enjoy all the benefits of the central location without its usual disadvantages: namely, noises from the traffic. Customized approach, elegant interiors and magical atmosphere guarantee that you’ll have only fond memories of your stay with us.
