                Urban Week in Saint Petersburg
            
            November 9 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

International Urban Week is held in Saint Petersburg on November 9-15. Leading Russian and foreign experts come here to identify new perspectives and possibilities for urban space and lifestyle transformations.  

Profurban forum openes the week and presents projects transforming the city. The value of each for citizens, authorities and business will be discussed by forum participants. The program of the week includes more than 30 different events such as conferences, exhibitions, film shows, site visits, lectures and master classes. It is arranged to provide all possible formats for business, experts, authorities and community groups to express their vision on urbanism.

International experts play a special role in the program. Michiel Van Iersel from the Netherlands will give the open lecture «Profession of Architect in the changing cities», his compatriot Thom Aussems will speak about the transformation of cities.

The International Urban Week was held for the first time in November 2014, and was attended by more than 3000 people.
