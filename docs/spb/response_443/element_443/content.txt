                                  Radisson Sonya Hotel, St. PetersburgReflecting the themes and imagery of the novel Crime and Punishment, this St. Petersburg hotel's interior is one of a kind. Along with the unique décor concept, guests also enjoy an enviable location in Liteyny Prospect. An on-site restaurant and bar as well as conveniences like Free high-speed, wireless Internet ensure a delightful stay. Rounding out the list of amenities are four meeting rooms, all outfitted with everything you need for a successful gathering.
