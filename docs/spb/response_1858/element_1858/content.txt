                    The "White Nights" International Ophthalmology Congress 
                
                
                    
                        
                            30 may - 3 jun 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.ocwn.org/en/congress/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The &quot;White Nights&quot; International Ophthalmology Congress</strong> is Russia&#39;s leading event for local, regional (former Soviet Union countries), and international Ophthalmology professionals.</p>

<p>The Congress, founded by&nbsp;the&nbsp;Chair of&nbsp;Ophthalmology, St.&nbsp;Petersburg Medical University and&nbsp;the&nbsp;Regional Association of&nbsp;Ophthalmologists, is&nbsp;devoted to&nbsp;the&nbsp;new technologies of&nbsp;treatment for&nbsp;glaucoma; cataract surgery; corneal and&nbsp;conjunctival dystrophic-degenerative disease treatment; neuro-ophthalmology; pharmaceutical and&nbsp;surgical treatment of&nbsp;retinal and&nbsp;choroidal vascular disease; orbital pathology; refraction surgery; vision correction and&nbsp;other therapeutic, technological and&nbsp;research aspects of&nbsp;the&nbsp;modern Ophthalmology.</p>

<p>The Congress takes place annually in&nbsp;St.&nbsp;Petersburg, Russia, in&nbsp;late May, during the&nbsp;White Nights arts festival, a&nbsp;spectacular tourist attraction.</p>

<p>Lasting about a&nbsp;week, the&nbsp;Congress programme is&nbsp;rich with lectures featuring the&nbsp;World&#39;s Ophthalmology opinion leaders, various training sessions and&nbsp;entertainment.</p>

<p>Every year the&nbsp;Congress attracts well above 1&nbsp;500 participants from all over Russia, former USSR, Europe, Middle East and&nbsp;Americas.</p>

<p>The conference exhibition is&nbsp;the&nbsp;main flagship event in&nbsp;the&nbsp;region for&nbsp;most major pharmaceutical andmedical device companies.</p>
