                                  Gogol`s library is a multimedia space for education, study, work and leisure. This is modern library with St. Petersburg`s traditions. There are five rooms in the library, two of them have а big area enough for mass events, activities and performance. Hall of specialized literature can comfortably hold a 70 persons. Сonference hall is provided with the new special equipment allowing to hold conferences and round tables.
