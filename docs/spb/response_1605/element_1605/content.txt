                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has entered into the Saint-Petersburg Chamber of Commerce and Industry
            
            July 3 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau became the member of the Saint-Petersburg Chamber of Commerce and Industry. It is the biggest union of entrepreneurs and manufacturers in the North-West of Russia.

This step opens new business opportunities for Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau, increases the number of partners in Russia and abroad, allows to explore successful experience and innovative ideas in different fields of business and entrepreneurship.

Saint-Petersburg Chamber of Commerce and Industry was founded in 1921. Today it is a non-governmental, non-profit organization. The Chamber has about 4000 members, including Russian and international companies. The organization develops international ties, cooperates with over 150 foreign Chambers of Commerce and Industry and associations from more than 70 states continually. Over 50 large events featuring representatives of business communities from abroad is held at the Chamber annually. The Chamber renders practical assistance its members in the establishment of a business connection with Russian and international partners, facilitates economy development of the region and investment promotion for the Russian business.
