                Convention Bureau will present Saint Petersburg at the Worldwide Exhibition IBTM World
            
            November 25 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                Convention Bureau will present Saint Petersburg’s opportunities for business tourism at the IBTM World on November, 29 – December, 1.

IBTM World is the leading global event for the meetings, incentives, conferences, events and business travel industry, taking place in Barcelona. The event gathers meetings industry professionals for three days of focused business opportunities, thought provoking professional education and networking to drive your business into the future. Annually The event brings together over 15,500 international industry professionals representing over 150 countries and 3,000 leading brands showcasing their products and services. With more than 100 events and networking opportunities across 3 days to attend.

Presentations of Saint Petersburg as MICE destination are planned during three exhibition days in Barcelona. Representatives of Convention Bureau will tell about support programs for meeting organizers and will share the ideas for incentive tours. The schedule of business negotiations at Saint Petersburg stand is totally planned for one-to-one meetings with incentive companies and professional associations.

 Leading companies will work at Saint Petersburg stand alongside with Convention Bureau:

AZIMUT Hotels Company

DIUIM DMC

ExpoForum-International Limited

GALAKTIKA DMC

GLOBAL RUSSIA DMC & PCO

GoingRussia / GoingBaltic

Kalinka International DMC

OLTA Travel

RUSMICE

Russian Empire DMC

Sokos Hotels Saint-Petersburg

Tsar Events DMC & PCO RUSSIA

 

Saint Petersburg stand’s number is H40.
