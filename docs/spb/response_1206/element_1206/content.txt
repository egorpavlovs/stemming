                    Russian International Energy Forum. RIEF 2018
                
                
                    
                        
                            25 - 27 apr 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Energy. Energy efficiency
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    https://rief.expoforum.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    An effective platform for dialogue between the leaders of the fuel and energy complex and equipment manufacturers / service providers / technology developers.

The forum is supported by  the Ministry of Energy, the Energy Committee of the State Duma, the Federation Council, the Government of St. Petersburg and Leningrad region, the Center for the Development of Communication Energy, NP "Council of Power Producers" branch unions, associations and partnerships.

The unique advantage of RIEF is the consolidation of congress and exhibition programs. Exhibitors as experts are actively involved in specialized congress events that form the basis of the business program – technical discussions, workshops, excursions to the enterprises and objects of fuel and energy complex of the region.
