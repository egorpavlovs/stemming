                     12th international forum WORLD OF BRIDGES
                
                
                    
                        
                            28 - 29 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Transport, Shipment, Warehouse, and Logistics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.mostdor.com/congress/info/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>In September 2016 within the RBT business program the <strong>12th international forum WORLD OF BRIDGES</strong> will be held in St. Petersburg.</p>

<p>The International Forum WORLD OF BRIDGES organized by the Government of St Petersburg, DORMOST Association of Enterprises of road and bridge complex of St Petersburg has been held since 2004 with the support of RESTEC&reg; Exhibition Company. It offers a fine opportunity for professionals from different countries to discuss new projects, technologies and materials, as well as bridge maintenance problems. Field trip is included into the program</p>

<p>The Forum is attended by representatives of industry-related committees of the Government of St Petersburg, Government of the Leningrad region, other regions of Russia, heads and leading experts of bridge constructing enterprises, engineering institutes and companies, survey contractors, higher educational institutions and insurance companies from Russia, the CIS and European countries</p>
