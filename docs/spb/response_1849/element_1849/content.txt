                    3 ICMM Pan Asia Pacific Congress on Military Medicine
                
                
                    
                        
                            8 - 12 aug 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        Crowne Plaza St.Petersburg Airport
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.cimm-icmm.org/page/anglais/3rdPanAsiePacificCongressRussie2016ANG/3rdPanAsiePacificCongre
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>In accordance with the decision of the Minister of Defence of the Russian Federation Main Military Medical Directorate of the Ministry of Defence of the Russian Federation is honoured to organize<strong> the 3rdAsia Pacific Congress on Military Medicine</strong> under the auspices of the International Committee of Military Medicine which will be held 8-12 August, 2016 in St-Petersburg Russia.</p>

<p>The Congress will bring together members of the ICMM and observer countries to share their valuable knowledge and expertise on the following major topics:</p>

<ul>	<li>organization of medical support in disaster relief operation;</li>	<li>modern issues of war surgery;</li>	<li>prevention and treatme nt of infectious diseases endemic for the region;</li>	<li>aviation medicine and occupational health issues related to the military;</li>	<li>mental health of the servicemen engaged in combat, peacekeeping and disaster relief operations;</li>	<li>medical protection from CBRN threats.</li></ul>
