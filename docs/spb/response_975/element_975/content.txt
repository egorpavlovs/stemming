                                  First the hotel opened its door for the guest in 19th century. It was built opposite the entrance to Upper Garden of Peterhof. The restaurant was especially popular. Alexander Dumas praised its cuisine in his memoirs. Pestle, Schwartz, Tolstoy and Grigorovitch used to come here.In the Soviet years the hotel was destroyed by a fire, and now, almost a century later, in May 2008 “Samson”, totally reconstructed, opened its doors again to create the unique atmosphere for guests in the new building near the emperor palace.
