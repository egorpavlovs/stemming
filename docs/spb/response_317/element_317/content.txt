                    With soothing colors and elegant yet chic décor, our guestrooms were exquisitely designed to envelop you in serenity. Discover St.Petersburg rich heritage and architectural masterpieces: the hotel boasts prime location just 50 meters away from the Nevsky prospect and a 5-minute walk from the world-famous tourist attractions such as the Hermitage museum, the Palace square and the Savior on the Blood and Kazansky cathedral. Impeccable service, nourishing and delicious breakfast as well as gracious hospitality will make your stay at Golden Triangle hotel a truly comfortable and rewarding experience.
