                    Almazov National Medical Research Centre
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    The Almazov Centre is a large modern facility that has been regularly holding various events like scientific and practical medical conferences, seminars, trainings and lectures with international participation. The Center has highly qualified professionals and a wealth of experience hosting successful events attended by top Russian officials and eminent foreign guests.We can provide opportunities to support scientific, medical, educational and business communities in establishing and strengthening business contacts, developing cooperation in all fields, including international participation. The unique technical facilities of the Congress and Exhibition Centre allow holding various events, such as congresses, conferences, seminars, round tables, press conferences, exhibitions, presentations, educational programs, master classes, lecture courses, special events, business negotiations and many other kinds of meetings. The halls are equipped with everything necessary for business events: presidium tables, stands, projectors, screens, plasmas, interactive voting systems, online broadcasting and video conferencing, simultaneous translation and participant registration, catering.
