                    International Medical Fair and Congress MEDIZ SPB
                
                
                    
                        
                            12 - 14 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.mediz-spb.ru/en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The exhibition <strong>MEDIZ SPB</strong> will be held in the framework of Petersburg International Health Forum which unites medical projects of the four leading fair organizers.</p>

<div><div>The Forum is aimed at the creation of the main presentation platform in the North West of Russia to find innovative solutions for the development of the effective system for health protection and increase of life quality.</div>

<div><div>In October 2016&nbsp;the exhibition MEDIZ SPB will take place on the territory of EXPOFORUM&nbsp;fairgrounds.</div>

<div><h3>The fair will be staged in the new congress-exhibition center that soon will become one of the largest in Europe. The complex will contain three exhibition pavilions with space capacity of 40000 sqm., congress center, hotels, business center, customs infrastructure, open and underground parking.</h3></div></div></div>

<h3><strong>Fringe Program:</strong></h3>

<ul>	<li>Conferences, seminars, discussions, master-classes</li>	<li>Contests of innovative solutions</li>	<li>Business center</li>	<li>Youth club</li></ul>
