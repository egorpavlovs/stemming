                                  St. Petersburg State Academic Capella is the oldest domestic professional musical institution. Concert Hall of Capella enjoys a well-deserved reputation in the music world. This room was used for the glory of Russian art. The masterpieces of music by Tchaikovsky, Rachmaninov, Borodin, Mussorgsky, Glazunov, Shostakovich, Prokofiev, Sviridov showcased here. Capella rooms can be used for cultural meetings and events.
