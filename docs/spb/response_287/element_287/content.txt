                The Committee for tourism development will hold the first Restaurant Festival in St. Petersburg
            
            October 25 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                St. Petersburg will host the first Restaurant Festival on November 1-30. The best city restaurants and cafes will present a set menu at a fixed price, it will provide the unique opportunity to try special designed for the festival dishes at an affordable price. Varies formats of restaurant and cafes will participate in the Festival, so every guest will be able to form his own view of the world of Saint Petersburg gastronomy.

Present day restaurant life is rich and diverse in Saint Petersburg. The festival guests will be able to get acquainted with a variety of cuisine: from traditional for Russians Italian and Japanese cuisine, to unusual Indian or modern Jewish cuisine. The set of each restaurant is a concentration of the best recipes, which are the pride of the chief.

The application "Afisha-Restaurants" will help to orient in variety of city gastronomy. Downloading the app for iOS, Windows or Android, attendees will be able to learn all the necessary information about the restaurant, view visitor reviews, book a table and get the itinerary to the place.

On October, 31 the Tsentralny Restaurant will host a press conference dedicated to the first Restaurant Festival in Saint Petersburg featuring Andrey Mushkarev, the Chairman of the Committee for tourism development, participants and partners of the Festival.
