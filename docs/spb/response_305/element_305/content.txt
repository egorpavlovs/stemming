                The first VeganFest in Saint Petersburg
            
            July 1 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                The first VeganFest in Saint Petersburg is for everyone who is involved with veganism, vegetarianism and rawism. It is held on the  July 2.

On VeganFest you could fully  immerse in vegetarian culture. There are lections  and masterclasses on vegetarian, veganic and raw cooking,  ethno music show, gastronomic market, Eco market of ethic things, vegetarian cinema, art exposition of vegetarian artists and quests and creative workshop  for children.

Moreover pre-revolutionary cook-shop will be worked as a part of VeganFest, there are popular in that time dishes in menu.
Time: July 2, 10.00-19.00

Place:  Saint Petersburg, Kazanskaya 7

Tickets: 100 RUB on buying online, 150 RUB in the day of event.
