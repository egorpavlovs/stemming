                    IEEE International Conference on Video and Audio Signal Processing in the Context of Neurotechnologies
                
                
                    
                        
                            29 may - 1 jun 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    https://spcn.co/?lang=en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    The conference is organized by The Federal Agency for Scientific Organizations (FASO Russia), Pavlov Institute of Physiology Russian Academy of Sciences, Saint-Petersburg State University for Cinema and Television, Neuroiconic-Neuromechanics Ltd. and technically co-sponsored by the IEEE Russia Northwest BT/CE/COM Chapter.

The conference provides an international forum where people from industry and academia will be able to interact, exchange experiences, share ideas and research results in all areas of Video and Audio Signal Processing in the Context of Neurotechnologies.
