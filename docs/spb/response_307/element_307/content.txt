                Saint Petersburg will contend for the title “Best city for business event tourism”
            
            July 7 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                Russian edition of the Business Traveller magazine will host Business Traveller Russia and CIS awarding ceremony for the first time in Russia on September 26th, 2016. Representatives of the Russian business market will be able to compete for the prestigious award in 6 categories: airline, airport, hotel, rent-a-car service, roaming and direction. Saint Petersburg will vie for two trophies “Best city for business event tourism in Russia and CIS”and  “Best city for business event tourism in the world” along  with such biggest cities as  Abu Dhabi, Paris and Singapore.

More than 300 companies have already registered as nominees of Business Traveller Russia and CIS Awards. The participants include both well-known international brands and regional companies of Russia, the CIS and the near abroad.  The voters are readers of the Russian edition of Business Traveller: frequent business travellers, graduates of the leading international and Russian business schools, chief executives of large international companies, representatives of banks, Russian state corporations, as well as individual travellers.The reader's online voting will last until September 11th, 2016 on the official website of the awards.

Due to the authoritative audit of voting results and the carefully selected respondent audience, only the best of the best will become laureates of Business Traveller Russia and CIS Awards.

The grand awarding ceremony of Business Traveller Russia and CIS Awards will be held on September 26th, 2016 in Izvestiya Hall. The guests will be offered a fantastic performance by the winners of Art Vision VJing contest of the Circle of Light Moscow international festival in a special category of the Business Traveller Magazine – “Travel. Vision of the Future”.

The awards are organized with the support of Moscow City Government.

Official website of the awards: businesstraveller.com.ru

About Business Traveller Awards:

For more than 30 years, the Business Traveller Awards have been some of the most reputable awards conferred every year for outstanding achievement and innovations in business travel. The first Business Traveller Awards ceremony was held in 1988 in Great Britain. Nowadays the awards are held in Great Britain, Germany, Poland, USA, Asia-Pacific region, Africa and Middle East.
