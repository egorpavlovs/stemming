                    25th  International Travel Trade Fair INWETEX-CIS Travel Market
                
                
                    
                        
                            12 - 14 oct 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://travelexhibition.ru/en/calendar/inwetex2016.html?subPage=132
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>INWETEX &ndash; CIS TRAVEL MARKET</strong> is the largest trade fair of travel services in the North-West of Russia which gathered near 400&nbsp;exhibitors from 32 countries of the world and over 6700 experts of the sector in the North-Western and other regions of Russia and the CIS countries.</p>

<p>For more than&nbsp;20&nbsp;years <strong>INWETEX &ndash; CIS TRAVEL MARKET</strong> has been keeping the leading position on the market and is among the five major events of the Russian travel industry, contributing to the development of professional contacts of Saint Petersburg and the North-Western region with representatives of Russian travel business and foreign partners.</p>

<p><strong>INWETEX &ndash; CIS TRAVEL MARKET</strong> is a unique complex congress and exhibition event that includes international Forum and exhibitions and makes its participants have a focus on cooperation with the key players of travel market of the North-Western region of the Russian Federation.</p>
