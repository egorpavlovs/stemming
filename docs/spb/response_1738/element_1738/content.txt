                Saint Petersburg International Economic Forum summarizes the results
            
            June 24 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg International Economic Forum that was held on June 18-20 had finished its work and beat the previous records.

If the forum united more than 7500 participants last year, then over 10 000 delegates took part at SPIEF 2015. 120 countries directed to the forum their representatives, 200 foreign heads of companies worked at the event.

The outcome of consultations and meetings of businessmen, economists and financiers from over the world became over 200 agreements, contracts, memorandums to the value of 293,4 billion rubles.

Preliminary figures indicate that investment package in Saint Petersburg economy composes about 40 billion rubles. First in Russia Novartis Swiss Pharmaceuticals Corporation full-cycle factory was opened within the forum. Also the agreement with Toyota was signed. The company plans to increase the automobile manufacture twice from next year. The agreements about the building of the walking bridge by the 2018 FIFA World Cup and the establishment of the film manufacturing and tourism complex were reached in the framework of the forum.

Informal program was active as traditionally. Guests were surprised by overtone signing, orchestra with gusli players and balalaika, playing by duduk, dancing of the band from Northern Caucasus at the gala reception featuring Governor of Saint Petersburg Georgy Poltavchenko. The evening was held at the Alexander Theatre. Closed parties organized by major delegated companies were placed at the Petrodvorets, the Yusupov Palace, in the best restaurants and bars of the city.
