                Tourism Business Week showed all tourist routes in Russia
            
            February 2 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Tourism Business Week has been held in Saint Petersburg on January 26-30. The congress program of the event was organized by Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau.

Two panel discussions, 12 round tables, presentation of the regions has taken place within Tourism Business Week. Round tables were dedicated to the cultural, family, religious, event tourism, investment potential of the Russian regions. «154 objects of the tourism infrastructure will be built in the framework of the Federal Program on Tourism Development until 2018. 76 objects have already been built. Meanwhile every ruble allocated from the federal budget attracts 3.6 rubles non-budget investments» - Deputy Head of Federal Tourism Agency Roman Skoryj said.  

110 companies from 30 Russian regions participated in the exhibition within Tourism Business Week. Tour operators, hotels, organizers of excursion programs, museum were among them. Representatives of b2b tourism sector as well as individual travelers visited Tourism Business Week.
