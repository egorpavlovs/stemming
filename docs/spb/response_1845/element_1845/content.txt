                    International Agricultural Exhibition and Trade Fair AGRORUS
                
                
                    
                        
                            30 aug - 2 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Agriculture
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://agrorus.expoforum.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>&quot;AGRORUS&quot;</strong> is the largest exhibition platform in&nbsp;Russia for demonstration and experience&nbsp;exchange&nbsp;in&nbsp;achievements of small&nbsp;farms&nbsp;of Russian and foreign agro-industrial complex. &quot;AGRORUS&quot; serves as&nbsp;excellent platform for realization the State program for agricultural&nbsp;development&nbsp;and regulation of the markets of&nbsp;agricultural production, raw materials and food for 2013-2020.</p>

<p>Exhibition statistics:&nbsp;total area of the&nbsp;exposition &ndash; 52 456 sq.m; number of participants &ndash; 1094 companies, among&nbsp;them 535 farms; number of visitors &ndash; 117 307 people; number of&nbsp;countries &ndash; 19.</p>
