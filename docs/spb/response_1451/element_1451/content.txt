                Convention Bureau and Russian Nurses Association unite efforts in attracting of events 
            
            September 15 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau and Russian Nurses Association have concluded the cooperation agreement on September 3. The collaborative work will be aimed at the organization and holding of interregional and international events in the sphere of healthcare in Saint Petersburg, the participation in national and foreign exhibition and congress projects.

The Russian Nurses Association was established in 1992 through the initiative of medical workers and the Russian Federation Ministry of Health. Today it unites over 180 000 members – nurses, obstetricians, laboratory assistants, and 56 regional organizations.

The association is the part of such important medical unions as the European Forum of National Nursing and Midwifery Associations, the World Health Organization, HORATIO – the European Association for Psychiatric Nurses, the Infusion Nurses Society, the World Sterilization Forum. Since 2005 the Russian Nurses Association is the member of the International Council of Nurses, which unites over 130 national specialized organizations. It entered into the European Operating Room Nurses Association in 2012. The Russian Nurses Association is the member of the European Society of Gastroenterology and Endoscopy Nurses and Associates (ESGENA) since 2013. It collaborates with the Ministry for Public Health and Social Development of the Russian Federation and the Russian Medical Association.

The association takes part in Russian and international congresses regularly and organizes events dedicated to healthcare and medicine. Particularly National Congress of the Russian Nurses Association with the international participation will be held in Saint Petersburg on October 11-13.

Convention Bureau and Russian Nurses Association plan to compete for the right of holding an international specialized congress of a professional union in Saint Petersburg. This event is held one time per several years and attends 5000 delegates in average. Such reputable event will improve an image of the profession, will increase an interest to the nursing care among young specialists, will create a perfect conditions to the development of up-to-date nursing technologies and the perfection of the quality of the professional training in Russia. The Government of Saint Petersburg has declared the official support of the participation of the city in the contest.
