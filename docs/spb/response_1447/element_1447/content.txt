                Congratulations on the World Tourism Day!
            
            September 25 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau congratulates all specialists of the tourism sphere on the World Tourism Day!

2015 year became important for the tourism industry of Saint Petersburg. The city got the prestigious trophy World Travel Awards as Europe’s Leading Destination. The world’s biggest travel portal Trip Advisor named Saint Petersburg as the best tourism destination in Russia. Best wishes to all industry professionals!

Since 1980, the United Nations World Tourism Organization has celebrated World Tourism Day on September 27.
