                Olga Mugatina is appointed to CEO of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau
            
            May 6 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                Olga Mugatina holds office as CEO of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau since April 28.

Mrs. Mugatina has 10-years work experience in the meetings industry. She cooperated with leading convention and exhibitions companies in Saint Petersburg and Moscow, where was responsible for organization and holding congress programs, government and community relations, development of convention infrastructure.

“I’m really happy to head the professional team of Convention Bureau in my born city. Saint Petersburg is world famous tourism destination. Our goal is to promote its unique opportunities for hosting large-scale business and live events. We will do our best to get into European Top-10 and World Top-20 business meetings destinations by 2020” – said Mrs. Mugatina.
