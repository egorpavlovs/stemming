                Don’t miss the annual Vedomosti Event Board
            
            July 28 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                On September 8th – 9th September the Event Board Conference will take place under the informational support of Saint Petersburg convention bureau. The meeting is dedicated to live entertainment industry development, and will be held within the framework of the annual Vedomosti Event  Show.

Successful and efficient event organization, event industry challenges are among the topics of the programme.  Conference’s speakers are world and Russian experts, who will share their experience.

Find more information on website.
