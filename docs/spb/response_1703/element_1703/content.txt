                    <p><strong>The International festival of events anf live communication&nbsp;&quot;Eubeafestival&quot;</strong>, established in 2006 by ADC Group as a key European award in experience marketing. In 2014 EuBea established new landmarks for the Event industry, by&nbsp;transforming a traditional Event Festival&nbsp;into a&nbsp;truly Live Communication experience.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Participating at EuBea 2016 offers events&rsquo; companies the opportunity to:</p>

<ul>	<li>&nbsp;Increase the importance of events in the marketing mix of their company/client</li>	<li>&nbsp;Present their event(s) live, if shortlisted, to the Corporate Jury Panel at the EuBea Festival</li>	<li>&nbsp;Participate as a Delegate</li>	<li>&nbsp;Meet different cultural approaches to enrich the creativity and innovation of their events</li>	<li>&nbsp;Face the challenge at European level as a stimuli to improve the quality of their events</li>	<li>&nbsp;Receive qualified visibility and press return at European level</li>	<li>&nbsp;Make their events last for the whole year with their presence in the EuBea website</li>	<li>&nbsp;Win one of the awards: Category, Technical Excellences, Special, Press and Delegate Awards</li>	<li>&nbsp;Be published, with all other winners, on a special directory and on the digital issue of the EuBea Award Gold Book</li></ul>
