                    <p>St. Petersburg International Innovation Forum is&nbsp;a unique platform for business communication of all participants of innovation: from research and education to investors and representatives of the federal institutes of innovative development.</p>

<p>Each year, the forum is attended by representatives of scientific, technical and educational spheres: Russian and foreign universities, common use centers, innovation and technology centers, leading Russian and foreign companies, scientists and experts in the field of innovation, various foundations, including representatives of venture financing.</p>
