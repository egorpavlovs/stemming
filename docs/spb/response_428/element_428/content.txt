                                  Jenavi Club is a unique place that combines a small family-run hotel and three beautiful halls, equipped with professional sound and lighting equipment.

There are 14 comfortable rooms equipped with everything that guests need. Home atmosphere gives them the tranquility and comfort. There are three halls of Jenavi Club: Big, Average and Small that allow any activities from family holidays to international conferences.Jenavi Club is located near the metro station "Elizarovskaya." Any point of St. Petersburg can be reached from there easily and quickly.
