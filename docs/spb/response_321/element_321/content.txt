                    About Global RussiaGLOBAL RUSSIA DMC & PCO is an expert in MICE, Tailor-Made Tours and Regular tours. GR is one of the leading DMC & PCO company in RussiaThe secret of our success is based on an experienced team, both efficient and creative, bright ideas and the knowledge of necessary skills. Share your idea with us and we will bring them to life. Come to Russia with “Global Russia”

We do-Incentive tours (exclusive corporate tours)-Business trips (exhibitions, meetings, forums etc)-Tailor-Made tours-Regular tours-FIFA2018 b2b hospitality program

Our services-Booking of airfare and train tickets.-Visa support for foreign tourists (tourist and business visa).-VIP meeting, Fast track service, waiting room of premium class.-Accommodation at hotels of different categories by special prices.-Restaurants & catering services-Transport services: cars of economy, business and premium class, minivans (up to 6-20 seats), comfortable buses (30 to 50 seats)-Venue search for event-Technical support of the event, specialists database-Design and decoration of events-Talent Celebrities Artist booking: artists, media specialists, experts in separate sphere-Restaurant services and catering-Design and production of advertising materials and souvenirs-Loading and unloading operations, installation works-Providing of emergency medical care with the help of partner companies “Sogas” and “Euromed” at the event

AwardsGovernment of St. Petersburg – get the badge of honor “For Quality, Work and Services 2016.”
