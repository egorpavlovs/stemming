                                  The hotel is located in the heart of the city, 5 minutes walk from the metro stations Sennaya Square, Sadovaya, Spasskaya. In walking distance of all the main attractions of St. Petersburg. In the hotel "Scarlet Sails" there are comfortable rooms of various categories. All are equipped with comfortable, new furniture, private bathrooms, air conditioning and televisions. Free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. All rooms have views on the Fontanka.
