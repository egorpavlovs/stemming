                    Russian International Energy Forum
                
                
                    
                        
                            25 - 28 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Energy. Energy efficiency
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://rief.lenexpo.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>RIEF-2017</strong> combines the experience and traditions of the two major international industry events &ndash; <strong>the International Specialized Exhibition &quot;ENERGETIKA &amp; ELEKTROTECHNIKA&quot;. (&ldquo;Power and Electrical Engineering&quot;)</strong> with more than 20-year history and the Russian International Energy Forum, which will held in St. Petersburg for the firth&nbsp; time.</p>

<p><strong>Forum </strong>will address many subjects that impact on energy, economic growth, political integration and dynamics of the region. It is obvious that the Forum, which will convene the leading government officials and influential names of the business world from many countries, will ensure an exchange of views covering all the cooperation possibilities in the field of energy.</p>

<p>The main objective of the project is to consolidate the interests of exhibitors presenting their products at the exhibition with the expertise of the <strong>Forum</strong>.</p>
