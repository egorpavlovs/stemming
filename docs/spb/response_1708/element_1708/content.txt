                    Specialized exhibition-contest BIOINDUSTRY
                
                
                    
                        
                            12 - 14 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://bio.expoforum.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The annual industry event <strong>Specialized exhibition-contest BIOINDUSTRY&nbsp;</strong>aimed at the effective interaction of suppliers of scientific developments in the field of biotechnology with the potential investors and manufacturers in order to create innovative products in various industries.</p>

<p><strong>Sections of the exhibition:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Biotechnologies in health industry:</li></ul>

<p>&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; -&nbsp;Biomedical Engineering;<br />&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; -&nbsp;Biomedicine;<br />&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; -&nbsp;Innovative medical equipment and new generation materials;<br />&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; - Biopharmaceuticals;<br />&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; - Innovative Treatments.</p>

<ul>	<li>Agricultural and Food Biotechnology;</li>	<li>Biotechnologies in the field of environmental safety;</li>	<li>Laboratory and analytical equipment.</li></ul>
