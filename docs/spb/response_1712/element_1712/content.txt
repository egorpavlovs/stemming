                    Offshore Marintec Russia Exhibition
                
                
                    
                        
                            4 - 6 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Energy. Energy efficiency
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://offshoremarintec-russia.com/exhibition/about/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Offshore Marintec Russia </strong>will demonstrate the potential of the shipbuilding industry in Russia and in the world and present the advanced experience in offshore and transport construction as an important segment of the shelf infrastructure development.</p>

<p>Participants of the event will show their innovations in the field of design of all types of vessels, equipment and materials for construction of offshore platforms and technological systems for oil/gas exploration and production on the shelf.</p>

<p><strong>Exhibit profile:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Technics and Technology of Energy Resources Development</li>	<li>Engineering and metalworking</li>	<li>Drilling equipment</li>	<li>Floating and Subsea Equipment</li>	<li>Subsea Pipelines</li>	<li>Chemicals, additives and materials</li>	<li>Ship Design/Naval/Ship Architect</li>	<li>Ship Engineering</li>	<li>Ship Registry/Marine Inspection</li>	<li>Shipbuilding</li>	<li>Ship repairers</li>	<li>Association / Government Bodies</li>	<li>Classification Society</li>	<li>Coating &amp; Paints / Corrosion Control</li>	<li>Communications Equipment (Radio Communications/Telecommunications)</li>	<li>Computer Systems &amp; Software</li>	<li>Engines/Diesel-overhaul &amp; Repairs</li>	<li>Fire Protection Equipment /Safety Equipment/Safety/Search Light</li>	<li>Environmental and Industrial Protection</li>	<li>Marine Equipment &amp; Supplies</li>	<li>Navigation &ndash; Equipment &amp; systems</li>	<li>Pumps</li>	<li>Valves</li></ul>
