                    VI INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC CONFERENCE "Architecture of Finance"
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 15 apr 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Economics and Management
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>THE VI INTERNATIONAL SCIENTIFIC CONFERENCE &quot;Architecture of Finance&quot;</strong>: geopolitical imbalance and potential for development of national financial systems&rdquo; was held in The St. Petersburg State University of Economics (UNECON).</p>

<p>The conference was attended by leading scientists and experts from universities in Europe and Asia, representatives of federal financial and regulatory authorities, the administration of St. Petersburg and Leningrad region, foreign government agencies and venture capital funds, Russian and foreign banks, insurance, financial, valuation and venture companies.</p>
