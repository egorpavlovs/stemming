                Calendar of events 2016 was discussed in Saint Petersburg Chamber of Commerce and Industry
            
            October 28 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg prepares for hosting 2018 FIFA World Cup Russia, organizers make calendar of events for the next year, hotels plan sales. The scheduled meeting of Saint Petersburg Chamber of Commerce and Industry was dedicated to these topics.

The star classification is one of points of the preparation for 2018 FIFA World Cup Russia. It should be finished until July 1, 2016. At the present time only 25 hotels with 2529 rooms have received certificates, by comparison 659 means of accommodation with 30 538 rooms are located in Saint Petersburg. Hotels create an accessible environment for physically challenged people. 5% rooms in the hotel should be specially equipped. 5 hotels that concluded agreements will become official bases for the accommodation of national teams. The preparation for FIFA World Cup has other directions. There are about 800 guides-boards in Russian and English languages and 11 informational pavilions in the city.

The realization of the Strategy for Socio-Economic Development of the North-Western Federal District for the period until 2020 became the next issue of the meeting. According to this plan Saint Petersburg will get into European Top-10 and World Top-20 business meetings destinations during 5 years. The inventory holding by Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau allows to determine a readiness of the industry for such important changes. The results of the research will evaluate the potential of convention and exhibition complexes, congress hotels, palaces for hosting different events. The international standards are being used within the work. The inventory will be finished by early next year. At the same time Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau monitors calendar of events. There are about 160 exhibitions and fairs and 70 international and national congresses in Saint Petersburg in 2015. “We have made some proposals to develop such thematic directions as industry, education, transport, sea sphere, medicine. Many initiatives were dedicated to sport theme. It is more than 10 different events for the public and professionals. Many sport venues will be built in the city in 2016. We are ready to transmit these projects to operators and venues” - said Deputy CEO of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Nataliya Serebrovskaya.
