                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau to Host the Session «The Role of Convention Bureau in the Industry Development»
            
            January 22 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                In the framework of the Europe + Asia Event Forum 2015 Saint-Petersburg Convention Bureau took part in the Session «The role of convention bureau in the industry development».


During the Session the actual case studies showing the role of Convention Bureau from a different perspective: a client one (an event agency and a venue) as well as a destination (city and national Convention Bureau) were presented.

Moderator: Anna Gorska, ICCA Central European Chapter Chairperson, Development Director, Expo XXI Warsaw International Expocentre Poland (Warsaw, Poland) 

Participants:


	Gebert Janssen, Managing director, De Feest- & Eventarchitect B.V. (Netherlands);
	Milos Milovanovic, Former Head of Serbia CVB (Belgrade, Serbia);
	Ksenya Boykova, Director General, Moscow Convention Bureau (Moscow, Russia); «Promotion of the city in the international market of meeting industry: achievements of Moscow Convention Bureau»
	Dmitry Levykin, Deputy Director, City Tourist Information Bureau of Saint Petersburg (St. Petersburg, Russia);
	Olga Voytenko, Project manager in MICE activities, Department for International Relations, Ekaterinburg City Administration (Ekaterinburg, Russia), «Ekaterinburg City Administration and Ekaterinburg Convention Bureau experience cooperation»
