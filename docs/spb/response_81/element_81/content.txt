                    Association of Youth international cooperation and tourism
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    «ASSOCIATION OF YOUTH INTERNATIONAL COOPERATION AND TOURISM» — thedeveloping organization of a multidimensional international youth cooperation and tourism, promotesactive involvement of youth in tourism processes.Main activity of the organization: development of institute of the international youth cooperation andtourism, their promoting, as in the territory of Russia, and abroad.Building up the relationship within forming of a single all-Russian platform with the profile organizationsand structures functioning in the field of the international youth travel, the Association realizes variousprojects and suggests to consider forming of a cooperation in the following directions: Implementation of the international programs, projects and participation in forming of nationalprofessional community and the communicative platform of the international youthcooperation and tourism with attraction to a cooperation of the interested participants – theorganizations, structures, professional specialists; Creation of new conditions for increase in efficiency of actions and professional, creative, socialself-realization of youth in the field of the international youth cooperation and tourism; The organization of fixed actions (forums, conferences, meetings, congress and exhibitionactions of a youth orientation, etc.) directed to expansion of geography of internationalbackgrounds with various regional, national and international youth, student's organizations,organizations, the companies in the sphere of the international youth cooperation and tourism; Implementation of the organizational and practical actions aimed at the development ofinstitute of the international youth cooperation and tourism, and also creation of the fixedoperating international event of platforms (the multipurpose international centers) for expansion of multidimensional activities of participants of national young people, and use of the most effective forms of interaction with the organizations and structures working in this area; The organization of the fixed joint international, interregional actions and actions directed to creation of quality tourist products and services for the purpose of development of the industryof youth travel, and respectively, forming of an acknowledged youth national tourist brand.
