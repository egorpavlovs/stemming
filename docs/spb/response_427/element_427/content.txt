                                  The Country-side Club "Dacha" is located at the Suhodolskoye (in Finnish - Suvantojärvi) Lake on 50 hectares of guarded woodland territory provides the following facilities: 28 hotel rooms, 8 cottages, 16 town-houses, 20 little "gingerbread" houses.Everything is in combination with good and hospitable service.

We can arrange individual and corporate programs as well as family holidays.
