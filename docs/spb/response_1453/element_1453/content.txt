                Saint Petersburg at the online exhibition “Know Russia! Winter”
            
            September 17 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The online exhibition “Know Russia! Winter” has finished its work. Tourist information centres, tour operators, hotels from different regions of Russia took part in the event on September 7-13 and told about the most interesting tourism destinations and ideas for travels in autumn and winter. 

Head of marketing department of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Denis Samodelov held the webinar “Business tourism as four seasons product”. Tours to Saint Petersburg in autumn and winter have the substantial advantages such as low prices or discounts in hotels, no lines to Hermitage, Kunstkamera and other museums, season of premiers in theatres. The city is original decorated for New Year and Christmas that creates a special festival atmosphere and good mood among tourists.

Moreover the cold season is the time of business activity, the period of forums, exhibitions and conferences. Such large-scale business events as Neva with its over 700 exhibitors, Saint Petersburg International Energy Forum, Saint Petersburg International Health Forum, Saint Petersburg International Cultural Forum, will be held in Saint Petersburg this autumn and winter. Also guests of the city can visit entertaiment events. Among them are International Film Festival Message to Man, International Festival Earlymusic, one of the key projects in the fashion sphere in Russia Aurora Fashion Week and many others.
