                    VI International Forum «Arctic: the Present and the Future»
                
                
                    
                        
                            5 - 9 dec 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        High technology and Innovations
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.forumarctic.com/eng/conf2016/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>VI International Forum &laquo;Arctic: the Present and the Future&raquo;</strong> will be held in December 2016 in St. Petersburg in EXPOFORUM Convention and Exhibition Center. This Event will be another step in development of the social Arctic agenda.</p>

<p>The annual results of the Country Arctic agenda will be summed up and the actual directions of cooperation between the authorities and society for the development of the Arctic zone will be formed on the Forum.</p>

<p>As in previous years the main outcome of the Forum will be a Resolution, which constitutes a consolidated response of society in terms of practical implementation of the state policy of the country in the field of socio-economic development of the Arctic zone of the Russian Federation.</p>

<p>The Resolution is based on the proposals of the Forum participants, as well as the executive bodies of subjects of the Russian Federation, and sent to the State Commission on the development of the Arctic in order to improve governance in the field of the Russian Arctic zone development.</p>

<p>It is expected that the Forum will bring together more than 1,200 delegates. <strong>The VI International Forum &laquo;Arctic: the Present and the Future&raquo; </strong>will be attended by representatives of the relevant federal ministries and departments, regional authorities, commercial companies and research organizations, the mass media.</p>
