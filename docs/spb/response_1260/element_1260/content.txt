                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau and North West division of the Russian Union of Travel Industry have partnered up
            
            October 15 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau and North West division of the Russian Union of Travel Industry have concluded the cooperation agreement within Saint Petersburg Hotel Forum on September 9.

The realization of the agreement will ensure the consolidation of tourism and convention community, will aid the development of inbound and domestic tourism in the city. The plans of Convention Bureau and North West division of the RUTI include the organization of collective events, the market researchs, project assistance in the sphere of the business tourism, information support. The companies will cooperate with international, national and regional industry and professional associations and will support their initiatives at the Saint Petersburg travel market.

The North-West division of the Union is the largest and the most fast-developing regional branch of the RUTI. It is one of the organizers of regional and international tourism exhibitions and festivals, as well as seminars, conferences, business meetings, and training programs for tourism industry employees.
