                    Baltic Club Hotel
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    The Ambassador Hotel is a calling card of Saint Petersburg. Its name was chosen for a good reason, as it bears the hotel’s mission, which is to be an ambassador of original Russian hospitality and the city’s cultural traditions. The hotel facilities are situated not far from the Mariinsky Theatre and Saint Isaac's Square, the most prominent cultural landmarks of Saint Petersburg. Combining a 19th century manor with a modern building, the hotel naturally integrates into the architectural ensemble of the Rimsky-Korsakov Avenue. Comfortable rooms and halls for formal receptions decorated in various styles from the traditional classic one to Art Nouveau; restaurants and bars, the Health & Beauty Centre, jewellery and gift shops — all this is the Ambassador Hotel.The hotel offers 251 rooms of various categories to its guests, including artist-built rooms: Marrakesh, Belgrade, Whitenights, Rimsky-Korsakov Suite and Presidential Suite.
