                    International specialised exhibition "Non-metallic materials" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.ptfair.ru/exhibition/non-metallic/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The exhibition<strong> &quot;Non-metallic materials&quot;</strong> &nbsp;covers the whole range of non-metallic materials, whose use in the field of mechanical building and other industrial sectors is broadening year by year. These materials are easily manufacturable and economically efficient &ndash; in makes them indispensible in many branches of economy.</p>

<p>The exhibition is part of the <strong>Petersburg Technical Fair.</strong></p>

<p><strong>&quot;Non-metallic&quot; materials sections:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Polymer materials</li>	<li>Various plastics</li>	<li>Non-metal based composite materials</li>	<li>Caoutchouc and rubber</li>	<li>Glues and sealants</li>	<li>Graphite</li>	<li>Glass</li>	<li>Ceramics</li></ul>
