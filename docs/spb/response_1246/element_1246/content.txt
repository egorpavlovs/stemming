                Results of the Saint Petersburg International Cultural Forum
            
            December 17 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

From 14 to 16 December Saint Petersburg was hosted the biggest event in the history of a cultural Forum devoted to the 70th anniversary of UNESCO. It gathered more than 10 000 participants at 82 venues in the city. The event was attended by the President of the Russian Federation Vladimir Putin, the General Director of UNESCO Mrs. Irina Bokova, Deputy Chairman of the Government of the Russian Federation Olga Golodets, Minister of Culture of the Russian Federation Vladimir Medinsky, as well as other dignitaries and artists.


The Forum held more than 250 events, including


3 Awards - All-Russian action "Philanthropist of the Year", award presentation by the Agency for Management and Use of Historical and Cultural Monuments "Best Implemented project of preservation and adaptation of cultural heritage to the modern use" and the first presentation of the award named after Anatoly Lunacharsky;

40 round table discussions and more.

At the venues were held up to 50 events simultaneously.

Within the Forum took place other major conferences and meetings, for example, III St. Petersburg International Tourism Forum, the Forum of Slavic Cultures, II International Forum in the framework of the partnership "Northern Dimension" in the field of culture "Culture as a factor of growth", VI International Congress "Russian literature in the global cultural context. The results of the Year of Literature", the program of UN activities and many other events.

The most prominent events were the Plenary Session dedicated to the 70th anniversary of UNESCO, the Ceremony of passing the baton from Year of literature to Russian Year of cinema, the Opening ceremony of Circus on Fontanka river after renovation and the Opening ceremony of the exhibition «The territory of Culture».

The festival program "Under the auspices of the Forum" is this year’s innovation. The inhabitants and guests of Saint Petersburg have an opportunity to visit more than 140 cultural events from september till the end of 2015.
