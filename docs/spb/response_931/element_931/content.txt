                Champions League of the Russian tourism
            
            March 31 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

"Leisure without borders. Summer 2016" International Exhibition will be held in Saint Petersburg on April 1-3. The main goal of the event is to present Champions League of the Russian tourism – destinations recognized by international awards and rankings.

Over 150 Russian and foreign companies will take part in the exhibition this year. Within three days, they will share with visitors different ideas for spending summer vacations: the best beach, wellness, cultural, educational, cruise tours. Visitors will be pleasantly surprised at the wide choice of travel destinations in Russia. There are the city from Top-10 located by the ocean according to National Geographic, the lake from the dozens of the leading destinations for four seasons’ travels in Lonely Planet opinion, and many others. The program will pay a special attention to Saint Petersburg as the repeat winner of tourism awards and prizes. It will be recalled that the State Hermitage was number four among the best European museums in the ranking by TripAdvisor in 2015. Unique Church of the Savior on Spilled Blood took 12st position in the Top-25 cultural places in the world, Saint Petersburg Amusement Park was placed on the 15st spot in the ranking of the best European amusement parks in 2015. Finally, Saint Petersburg was recognized by World Travel Awards as Leading European Destination.

Art of Tourism, International Travel Trade Workshop will be held with the exhibition. The goal of this workshop is allowing travel business to get first-hand information on latest trends of countries and tourist destinations, special aspects of tourist visa procurement, and to enquire the experts on hotel business and tourism advertisement issues. The program of the exhibition includes concourses, quizzes, prize drawings, too.
