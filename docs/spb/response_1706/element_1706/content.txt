                    <p>A new ambitious project &mdash;<strong> the International Fashion Week AURORA FASHION WEEK Russia</strong> - was created 5 years ago in St. Petersburg. Since that moment AURORA FASHION WEEK Russia has been the most remarkable fashion event of the Northern capital and one of the key events of fashion industry, which are held in Russia. Throughout its existence the project gained deserved recognition among world professional communities as well as fans of fashion. Millions of inquiries in search engines, hundreds of thousands online broadcasts of runway shows and tens of thousands Fashion Week guests prove the popularity and significance of AURORA FASHION WEEK Russia.</p>
