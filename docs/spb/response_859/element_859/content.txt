                    2nd International Congress on Echocardiography. "ECHO of the White Nights-2018"
                
                
                    
                        
                            4 - 6 oct 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.onlinereg.ru/site.php?go=283&lang=ENG
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    Many distinguished cardiologists and specialists in the field of echocardiography have agreed to join the faculty participating in this Congress. The plenary sessions and symposia will cover lectures and other scientific presentations. A special separate session will be devoted to poster presentations. In addition, workshops and round tables will be organized which will provide education in all practical aspects of echocardiography.ORGANIZERS

• International Society of Cardiovascular Ultrasound (ISCU)

• International Academy of Cardiology (IAC)

• International Foundation for Development of Medical Science and Education "Healthy Heart"

• North-Western State Medical University named after I.I. Mechnikov

• St. Petersburg I.P. Pavlov Medical University

• St.Petersburg State Pediatric Medical University

• St.Petersburg Echo Club

• Government of St. Petersburg.
