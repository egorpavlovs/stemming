                Aurora Fashion Week Russia is on the catwalk
            
            October 13 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Aurora Fashion Week Russia will take place in Saint Petersburg on October 13-18. The legendary DLT | TSUM – the flagship store of the main Russian department store TSUM (Moscow) - will become the main stage of the week. Some events will be held in other different venues of Saint Petersburg as well. An unprecedented number of events makes this project unique in the fashion industry. 

Aurora Fashion Week invariably seeks to show all the best of fashion industry of Saint Petersburg and to reveal the most outstanding Russian and international fashion projects. The program includes presentations and private viewings in art galleries, film premieres and special screenings, sport activities, gastronomic events, lectures and workshops, Showcase Saint-Petersburg exhibition of clothing and accessories, runway shows and presentations of leading designers.
