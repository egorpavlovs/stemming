        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    All-Russian scientific and practical conference "Emergency Medical Service — 2016"
                
                
                    
                        
                            9 - 11 jun 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.spb-gmu.ru/ru/nauka/konferentsii/1834-25-26-iyunya-2015-goda-vserossijskaya-nauchno-prakt
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Program questions of the&nbsp;conference:</p>

<p>&bull; Prospects of development of&nbsp;emergency medical service in modern conditions.<br />&bull; Training problems of&nbsp;emergency medical service and disaster&nbsp;medicine.<br />&bull; Results of the pilot project on the continuous medical education (CME) of experts of emergency medical service and allied&nbsp;specialties.<br />&bull; Examing the&nbsp;quality of&nbsp;emergency medical service at pre-hospital and hospital&nbsp;stages.<br />&bull; Thermal and thermomechanical injuries &ndash; succession&nbsp;of pre-hospital and hospital&nbsp;stages of emergency medical service and treatment.<br />&bull; A hospital&nbsp;stage of emergency medical service as the new organizational technology providing increase in&nbsp;efficiency of&nbsp;medical care for&nbsp;the population.<br />&bull; Equipment and facilities&nbsp;for&nbsp;emergency medical service &ndash; an innovation in practical health care.<br />&bull; Current&nbsp;issues on&nbsp;information support of emergency medical service.<br />&bull; Upgrading&nbsp;medical equipment of emergency medical service and disaster medicine&nbsp;vehicles.<br />&bull; Interaction between&nbsp;emergency medical service, disaster medicine and other&nbsp;services while&nbsp;eliminating&nbsp;medical consequences of anthropogenic&nbsp;and natural disasters, acts of terrorism.</p>
