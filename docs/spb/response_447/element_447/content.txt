                                  International Business Center (IBC) “Neptun” - is a multifunctional structure with Hotel, Restaurant, Sports-and-Leisure Complex as well as Office center.

IBC Neptun includes:office premises of 11,000 sq.m with a branch office of Open Joint Stock Company Bank VTB; hotel for 150 rooms; restaurant, banquet rooms, bars, shop Russian Vodka; five conference rooms for 10 to 110 persons; sport & Leisure Complex, including swimming pool, Turkish bath, saunas, fitness room, aerobics and shaping studio, bowling, billiards, indoor tennis court; active Chapel of the Nativity of Christ.
