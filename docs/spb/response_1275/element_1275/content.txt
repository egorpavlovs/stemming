                                  NashOtel is a four-star hotel in St. Petersburg, located in the historical center of the city. Guests of NashOtel have an excellent opportunity to feel the rhythm of life of a cultural megalopolis. NashOtel provides everything you need for quality events. The hotel has developed conference packages, greatly facilitating the process of preparing events for business meeting organizers.
