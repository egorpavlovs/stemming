                    Congress "Vocational education, science and innovation in the XXI Century"
                
                
                    
                        
                            24 - 25 nov 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The congress is organized by the&nbsp;committee on science and the higher school of St. Petersburg on the basis of National mineral and raw university &quot;Gorny&quot;.</p>

<p>Participants of the Congress will have&nbsp;active business program in the form of the round tables and plenary sessions, organized for discussion of the priority directions of development and a role of youth in scientific,&nbsp;technical and innovation&nbsp;activity, prospects of the international cooperation and specifics of the competition in science and education in the modern world.</p>
