                    EGO Translating Company
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    EGO Translating Company (established on May 27, 1990) is a leader on the Russian market for comprehensive linguistic-support services.

EGO Translating Company offers translation services in 99 world languages and more than 50 economic sectors, employing 205 staff and over 2,000 freelance specialists: project managers and supervisors, translators and editors, desktop designers and proofreaders. EGO Translating Company has provided comprehensive linguistic support for such high-profile events as the St. Petersburg International Energy Forum, the St. Petersburg Technical Fair, the St. Petersburg International Forestry Forum, the Russian-German Business Forum, the Russian-Chinese Economic Forum, the International Summit of Islamic Business and Finance KAZANSUMMIT, the ENES International Forum on Energy Efficiency and Energy Saving, the EFEA Europe + Asia Event Forum, and many others.

EGO Translating Company’s key advantage lies in its provision of an entire range of professional linguistic support for a client on projects at any level of difficulty: interpreting (simultaneous, consecutive, whisper) at conferences, presentations, ceremonial events,, negotiations, specialist training and equipment installation; provision of simultaneous-interpretation equipment; guide-interpreter services; translation of audio/video materials; translations of all manner of documents, stylistic editing by an area specialist and native speaker, plus desktop and graphic design; legalization of translations, including apostille and consular legalization; localization of websites, software, directory systems, electronic libraries.

Due to its impeccable work, premium-class service, adherence to stringent quality standards, constant technological refinement and commitment to innovation, EGO Translating Company has proven to be a reliable and professional partner of major enterprises: the leaders of such sectors as the MICE-industry, the government sector, energy, mechanical engineering, the oil & gas industry, the food industry, medicine, pharmaceuticals and many more. The company is a member of the European Language Industry Association, ELIA, the Union of Translators of Russia, UTR, the Chamber of Commerce and Industry for St. Petersburg and Leningrad Region, the St. Petersburg Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs, and the Association of European Businesses, AEB. The company’s quality-management system is certified by international standard ISO 9001:2008.
