                Stylish light at the Design&Décor exhibition in Saint Petersburg 
            
            September 9 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

General Assembly of the United Nations declared this year as the International Year of Light and Light-based Technologies. Maybe for this reason the lighting of a living space plays the main role at the International Interiors Exhibition Design&Décor. The event is held in the Expoforum, Saint Petersburg on September 9-11.

Visitors of the event will have a unique opportunity to find and buy nice items for home decoration. The exposition Art Village presents designer lamps, chandeliers, light shapes and other objects for interior lighting. All art items are made in a single copy. Moreover exclusive decorative paint and plaster, new collections of murals and wallpaper, a wide selection of glass, stone and mosaic are collected at the venue.

Professional guests of the exhibition will visit the architectural forum ArchiSpace and Designers Club, where master classes of famous architectures and designers will be held. The Retail Expert conference will be interesting for representatives of trading companies.
