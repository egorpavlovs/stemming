                In full sail: Saint Petersburg Sea Festival 
            
            August 14 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg International Sea Festival will be held within the Big Saint Petersburg Sailing Week against the walls of the Peter and Paul Fortress on August 15-16. 

Saint Petersburg International Sea Festival is the bright thematic family-friendly fest. The event is dedicated to the city living by the sea. Guests will admire sailing-ships and fast yachts, visit art exhibitions, hear live music, participate in adventure games, concourses and master classes. Those who wish can get to know about education in sailing schools for adults and children. Famous groups will feature within the Music festival.

The festival is supported by the Committee of Culture of Saint Petersburg. The entrance is free.
