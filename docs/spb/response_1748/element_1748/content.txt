                ICCA has issued city ranking report 2014
            
            May 13 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The International Congress and Convention Association ICCA published the official statistic for 2014.

Saint Petersburg saved its position in the worldwide ranking for the number of conducted events. The city takes up 77st position along with Lyon, Reykjavik and Rotterdam. Saint Petersburg places at 41st  notch in the rating of European convention destinations. The city took up 79st position in the worldwide ranking and 42st position in the European ranking one year ago. Three leaders among cities are as before Paris, Vienna and Madrid.

Germany, Spain and United Kingdom lead country ranking. Russia takes up 22st position for the number of conducted events and gets ahead of Estonia, Romania, Serbia and Lithuania. Russia placed at 21st  notch one year ago.
