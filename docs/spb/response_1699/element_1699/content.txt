                    International Food Exhibition "Peterfood"
                
                
                    
                        
                            15 - 17 nov 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Consumer goods
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://peterfood.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <div class="page" title="Page 2"><div class="layoutArea"><div class="column"><p><strong>&quot;Peterfood&quot;</strong> is the main exhibition of the North-West in sphere of food trade. For 25 years it helps providers to enter the retail networks and promote their products in one of the largest markets in Russia. Every year over 8000 negotiations on supplying 50 North-West Federal Region networks are held at the exhibition. Participation in these negotiations will allow Participants to conclude supply agreements with the key buyers of the region.&nbsp;<br /><br />Forum Trade in the Big City at the Peterfood exhibition is a key platform for dialogue between the retail networks and food suppliers. Only here, directly from the network top managers participants will learn clearly about the networks&rsquo; development plans, assortment policy, selection criteria required for conclusion of a contract with the network.</p></div></div></div>
