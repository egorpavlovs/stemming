                                  Hotel Aston 4 * is located in a historic building in the very center of St. Petersburg - on Vladimirsky Avenue, within walking distance of the most famous cultural monuments. The hotel Aston has 40 modern rooms of various categories, equipped with everything necessary for a comfortable stay and meeting European standards.The view from the windows of the rooms - on the beautiful Vladimirsky Prospect or in the cozy and quiet courtyard. There are rooms with a balcony. All rooms are designed in classic style, have an exceptional design and equipment.
