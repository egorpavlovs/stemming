                                  Bordering Babolovskiy Park, a popular recreation area on the outskirts of Pushkin, Park Hotel Potemkin impresses with its elegant interior and state-of-the-art facilities.Park Hotel Potemkin is located not so far from St Petersburg.Hotel has 114 comfortable guest rooms and studios, most including a private balcony.Start the day with a swim in the indoor pool. After a visit to the sauna, you can relax on the terrace or watch your children have fun in the games room. Hotel Potemkin also has its own restaurant. Dine in style in the elegantly furnished dining room, followed by a drink at the hotel bar. Alternatively, you can order in room service.
