                    Saint Petersburg Automation Exhibition is a well known in Russia specialized exhibition on industrial automation and introduction of information and computer technologies to the industrial sphere.

AUTOMATION EXHIBITION has being held for 19 years and has got international recognition among the industry specialists as professional business platform for automation facilities and systems promotion in Russian market. In recent years the exhibition rightfully got the leading role among Russian exhibitions devoted to automation for the industrial sector.
