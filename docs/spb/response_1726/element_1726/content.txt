                    The ХХth anniversary Forum “Russian Industrialist”
                
                
                    
                        
                            21 - 23 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://promexpo.expoforum.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Forum is the largest platform in the Northwestern region of Russia for demonstration of innovative industrial equipment, products and technologies of companies with the goal to promote them on the regional and international markets, and discussion of the pressing issues of modernization of the machine building enterprises.</p>

<p>Forum&rsquo;s goals:</p>

<p>-&nbsp;to promote native competitive innovation products of machine-building complex;<br />- to hold a dialogue with representatives of authority, business, education and scientific community to eleborate collective decisions on industrial sector development;<br />- to promote and enhance prestige of blue-collar job and engineers;<br />- to reinforce an image of Saint-Petersburg as leading innovation region of Russia.<br />&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Exhibition program:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Regional innovation clusters, Russian regions</li>	<li>Machine tool building. Metalworking</li>	<li>Machine industry</li>	<li>Laser equipment and technologies</li>	<li>Robotics. Automation</li>	<li>Tools</li>	<li>Innovation and technologies</li>	<li>Electronics and Instrument making</li>	<li>Lighting technology</li>	<li>Advanced materials for industry</li>	<li>Industrial design</li>	<li>Training and professional development for blue-collar job</li>	<li>NEW!! Woodworking industry<br />	&nbsp;</li></ul>

<p><strong>Congress program:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Conference on machine tool building</li>	<li>Conference on energy saving</li>	<li>Conference on lighting</li>	<li>Conference &ldquo;Robotics&rdquo;</li>	<li>Conference &ldquo;Advanced techniques of specialist training in the field of machine industry technologies&rdquo;</li>	<li>Round table &ldquo;Import substitution in metalworking&rdquo;</li>	<li>Round table &ldquo;Forestry machine-building&rdquo;</li>	<li>Seminar on polymers</li>	<li>Business-dialogue &ldquo;International experience in machine building&rdquo;</li></ul>
