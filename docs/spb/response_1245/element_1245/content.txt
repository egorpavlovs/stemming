                Paris secret for successful attraction of business events
            
            December 16 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Representatives of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau took part in the Meet Europe event which was organized by European Cities Marketing for its members in Paris on December 10-11. The aim of the projects was the holding negotiations between convention bureaus from different European cities from one side and professional associations, corporate clients, DMC, PCO from another side.

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau participated in the event of such format for the first time, and it proved its effectiveness. About 20 meetings with event organizers and companies specialized on incentive tours were held at the booth of the city.

The business breakfast with Director of Marketing of Paris Convention and Visitors Bureau Clement Laloux took place within Meet Europe. He tried to explain colleagues from other countries why Paris heads the ranking by ICCA.  The development strategy of Paris Convention and Visitors Bureau includes continuous networking inside the industry, organization of educational programs for local PCO and DMC, strong partnership with hotels, cooperation with ambassadors, government support. It is interesting that Paris develops convention infrastructure constantly. Mr. Laloux thinks that Paris Bureau wins concourses for holding of events often thanks to the city itself – its history, culture and architecture.  The main thing is sharing information about organized events with ICCA. Such information provides higher positions of the destination in rankings.
