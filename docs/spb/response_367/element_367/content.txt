                                  The modern three-star hotel “Sputnik” is a hotel complex in the northern part of Saint-Petersburg. There are 192 suits.

Sputnik Hotel offers to rent a conference room for business meetings, presentations, conferences, seminars and training sessions.The hotel complex is situated in an old part of Vyborgsky district of St. Petersburg on Maurice Thorez Prospect – within convenient thoroughfare. There is Ploshchad Muzhestva metro station, Ring Road and exit from the city in the resort area direction near the hotel.This area is easily accessible by both public and private transport. At the territory of the hotel there is a large parking, that allows to avoid any problems with parking of private vehicles and buses.Conference room has been recently renovated and equipped with furniture, special equipment and supplies for professional events, and is within the range of a wireless high-speed Internet channel (WI-FI).The room is located on the second floor (an administrative one), what provides the isolation of conferences and training participants from the main flow and the movement of visitors, as well as quietness and businesslike atmosphere.The area of the room is 140 sq. m., capacity is up to 120 people.Rent of conference roomHourly payment of the conference room rent is under request.The conference room rent payment includes:Room preparation – furniture arrangement;Free Internet connection;Presentation equipment (VGA multimedia projector, screen, interactive board, document-camera, acoustic system, LED-display and microphones, flipchart)Additionally, you can order copy and fax services, as well as organization of business events catering, coffee breaks, lunches, cocktail parties (buffets or banquets). Menu is to be developed within the budget of the organizer.Discounts and special offers for conference room rentA special discount package for a conference room rent, designed for corporate and group clients, can be offered.When booking the conference room for more than 8 hours you get 10% discount.For groups of 6 persons (and more): when booking a conference room, you get 10% discount for accommodation.Free rent of the conference room and equipment for the organizers of sporting events, when accommodating teams at the hotel.
