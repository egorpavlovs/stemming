                                  Hotel Astoria is a place with true Russian hospitality, where personal service is delivered with gracious ease and a local touch. 169 comfortable rooms including 86 suites. Each of our rooms complements the beauty and heritage of the city. Individually designed by Olga Polizzi, they blend contemporary elegance with classic charm. Guests can discover Russian-French cuisine at Astoria Café, cocktails at Lichfield Bar and desserts which made by Astoria Chocolatier at Rotonda Lounge.

Luxurious Hotel Astoria, a Rocco Forte Hotel was opened in December 1912. Great location opposite St. Isaac’s Cathedral and secular traditions make Hotel Astoria one of the best hotels in the city. Main attractions, including the Hermitage Museum and the Mariinsky Theatre, are within walking distance.

Hotel Astoria offers 5 meeting rooms and 2 conference halls for up to 350 guests. Big variety of outstanding services and interesting offers will satisfy the most demanding guest.
