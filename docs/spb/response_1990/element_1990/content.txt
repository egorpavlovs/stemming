                    8th International Forum on Urban Planning, Architecture and Design A.City 2015
                
                
                    
                        
                            18 - 19 mar 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Construction, Infrastructure
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Forum A.city annually confirms the status of popular venue&nbsp;and professional platform for&nbsp;creating a&nbsp;dialogue between professionals and for all-encompassing&nbsp;discussion of urban planning and architecture.</p>

<p>The central topic&nbsp;of A.city 2015 is polycentric urban development considering&nbsp;surrounding areas and transport infrastructure. The Forum provides an opportunity for architects, designers and builders to have a&nbsp;direct dialogue with the representatives of the government and business. There are three panel discussions in the programme, plenary sessions, round tables and the panel exhibition &quot;The city and the temple.&quot;</p>
