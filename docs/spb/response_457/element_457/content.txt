                                  The VYBORG Hotel is located in the historical center of  the city  surrounded by buildings of Finnish architecture. The building of the hotel was built in 1929 by an outstanding Finnish architect Uno Ulberg. Today the Vyborg Hotel is a seven-storey  complex of 94 rooms, able to accommodate up to 180 guests. Bus- and  railway stations are just nearby  and make the Hotel even more convenient for the guests. The Hotel can offer both standard and suite rooms. The bright rooms are decorated in warm colors in a classical style. They are equipped with a TV and a private bathroom.There are restaurant, banqueting hall, lobby-bar, sauna, massage room, the BAMBOO night club in the Hotel complex. There is a car parking at the entrance of the Hotel. Wi-Fi is available in public areas and free of charge. Personnel of the Hotel complex is always happy to welcome its guests and can offer not only different services to tourist groups and corporative guests but also organization of seminars, banquet events, corporate parties, events for children.
