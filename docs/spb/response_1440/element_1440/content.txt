                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau flies to the IMEX America
            
            October 8 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau flies to Las Vegas where will take part in the Worldwide Exhibition for Incentive Travel, Meetings & Events IMEX America on October 13-15.

USA is the number one of the ranking by ICCA in 2014. Headquarters of numerous professional associations and global corporations which organize events in different countries and incentive tours are located here. American business tourists are known as active visitors of international exhibitions and forums far beyound USA. These facts prove that the market of Northern America furnishes great opportunities for the development of business of industry companies and for the promotion of cities and regions as convention destinations.

Presentations dedicated to the unique pecularities and the advantages of the city in the field of incentive tourism and meeting industry will be held at the Saint Petersburg booth during three days of IMEX America. Special event will be organized  regarding ICE Hockey World Championship. Saint Petersburg will host this big sport fest next year and will unite fans from all countries especially from Russia and America.

2900 hosted buyers are invited to the exhibition traditionally. Representitives of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau will conduct negotiations with them according to the preformed shedule. Association evening will become an effective platform for networking. The event organized by ICCA will gather over 200 professional unions, North American basically, major PCO and destinations.
