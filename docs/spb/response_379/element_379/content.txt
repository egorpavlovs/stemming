                                  A  business and a cultural centre State Complex "The National Congress palace" is located on the coastline of the Gulf of Finland in the park area of St. Petersburg. Magnificent Konstantin palace, the revived residence of Grand Dukes of the House of Romanovs, is an architectural dominant of the Complex. It is surrounded by parks which are decorated with the inimitable ensemble of fountains, canals, draw bridges and splendid rosaries. The integral part of the State Complex "The National Congress Palace" is the "Baltic Star" business hotel and the villas of the Consular village. Besides marvelous halls and unique accommodation the State Complex "The National Congress Palace" offers exquisite cuisine of the restaurants, excellent service and transport accessibility by sea, land and air. State Complex "The National congress palace" provides perfect conditions for organising and holding events of any level and format, effective work and high-quality leisure.
