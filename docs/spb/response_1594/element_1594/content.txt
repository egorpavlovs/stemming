                Convention Bureau took part in the Strategic Session of Saint Petersburg clusters
            
            August 18 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Representatives of more than 20 Saint Petersburg-based clusters gathered together for the first time within the Strategic Session on August 14. The aim of the meeting was the discussion the realization of co-projects. Head of Development Department of Convention Bureau Elena Rechits took part in the event.

Specialists got into three groups where they could exchange opinions effectively and agreed on joining efforts. Inter-cluster portal, development project Clever City, technopark of Russian jewelers and many others initiatives made the final list of projects. More than third of suggested projects were linked with the education.

The Strategic Session became realized thanks to the first monitoring of the cluster environment by Saint Petersburg Cluster Development Center. It has been discovered that Cluster for the «Development of Information Technologies, Radioelectronics, Instrumentation, Communications and Telecommunications» and Cluster of the Pharmaceutical and Medical Industries, Radiation Technologies are the most advanced. Significantly key industries for Saint Petersburg economy development took the first steps towards the cluster. Among them are convention sphere.

Next step of the research will allow to compile the specification of all cluster projects and to determine the most promising. The results will be presented at Saint Petersburg International Innovation Forum on October 7.
