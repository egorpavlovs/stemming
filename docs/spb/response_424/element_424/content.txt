                                  Situated in the heart of the city on the Moika River, in the aristocratic mansion built in 1853, Kempinski Hotel Moika 22 is flanked by the famous landmarks of Palace Square and the Hermitage museum. The hotel is just five minutes’ walk from the Nevsky Prospect and close to all major sights and shopping, an ideal location whether you are travelling for business or personal reasons. You can expect an exceptional welcome here, and we gladly greet our guests with a unique, personalized European service in every single one of the 200 rooms and suites, at our restaurants and bars, and event venues.
