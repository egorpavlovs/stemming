                Saint Petersburg is preparing for the Light Festival
            
            March 23 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

The preview event of the Light Festival “The city of the light culture” will be held in Saint Petersburg this day month, on April 23. The main show is planned at the end of year. The festival will be annually. The event will give citizens and tourists a unique chance to see from a new angle the architectural space of the city, which light designers and 2D and 3D graphic designers utilize as canvas for multimedia and light installations. It is estimated that the Light Festival will complement the cultural life in Saint Petersburg and will increase the interest of tourists to the city.

The scene of the festival will be laid in the centre of the city. Alexandrinsky Theater, which will celebrate its 260th anniversary this year, will become one of the venues during the holding of the event.
