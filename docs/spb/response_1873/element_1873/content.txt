                    "Smelt Festival"
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 15 may 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Consumer goods
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://lenexpo.ru/node/109869
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The annual <strong>&quot;Smelt Festival&quot;</strong> - an important social event for the entire city. It annually attended by over 50 thousand people, regardless of age, social status and interests.</p>

<p><strong>&quot;Smelt Festival&quot;</strong> - a bright holiday for all Petersburgers and guests.</p>

<p>The cultural program includes concerts and performances of the St. Petersburg stage. Active guests will be able to become parties to the various thematic competitions and games. Different rides are working for children. The holiday will held the traditional competitions on fishing from the shore and from boats.</p>

<p>On the shore of the Gulf of Finland will be a solemn stocking event: the release of salmon fry into the bay.</p>
