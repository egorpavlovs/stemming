                Direct flight connection between Saint Petersburg and Ürümqi has been opened
            
            July 21 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Direct flight connection between Saint Petersburg and Chinese city Ürümqi has been opened. First scheduled flight has been held on July 16.

The launch of the air route Lanzhou – Ürümqi - Saint Petersburg is highly important for Russia and China. “New perspectives for tourism and business development will be opened for citizens of Saint Petersburg and all Northwest,” commented the Deputy Chairwoman of Committee for Tourism Development Rimma Sachunova. China is number one in amount of trips to Russia. The largest part of Chinese tourists flies to Moscow because its airports take up numerous flights from cities of China. However 98% tour packages from China to Moscow include the visit to Saint Petersburg. Tourists arrive to the city by train and pass 70% time of travel to Russia here.

The launch of the new flight will promote increasing of direct passenger flow from China, strengthening of Saint Petersburg appeal as a tourism destination and a convenient harbor for travels to Northern Europe.
