                    Landscape architecture: Towards sustainable urban environments
                
                
                    
                        
                            6 - 7 jun 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Landscape design
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    https://iconlaorg.wordpress.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    This international scientific conference in landscape architecture in St.-Petersburg, Russia, is devoted to the very relevant theme of creating urban environments that are comfortable to live in and at the same time supply a whole range of ecosystem services. We call upon foreign and local landscape architects, the academic community, city administrations and other appropriate stakeholders to share visions and innovative approaches that can help create a new generation of sustainable cities.
