                                  The House of Finland is the center of Finnish culture, science and business in St. Petersburg. The Finnish House is located at b. Konushennaya St. 8. An area of 4500 m2 rented from the City State property management Committee (COOGEE) for a period of 49 years. The House of Finland was built in the year 1847, reconstruction works were completed in 2010 and passed with the support of St. Petersburg and the Institute of Finland in St. Petersburg.House of Finland uses the administrative support of the Finnish Foundation for St. Petersburg and its branch in Russia. Institute of Finland is responsible for cultural and scientific cooperation between the two countries. From September 1, 2012 year to the post of Director of the Institute of Finland in St. Petersburg began Elina Kahla. Director of Finland is Arto Mikonsaari. Responsible person of the Foundation of St. Petersburg-master of jurisprudence, lawyer Panou Stahlberg. Foundation Chairman-PhD Juhani Leppâ. The Chairman of the delegation of the Foundation of St. Petersburg is the Executive Director of the Finnish industry East Office, Raimo Valo.
