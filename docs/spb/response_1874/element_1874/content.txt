                    International Exhibition-Congress "Anti-Corrosion defence"
                
                
                    
                        
                            17 - 20 may 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://corrosion.expoforum.ru/main
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The exhibition dedicated to the most important scientific, technical, economic and environmental problems - corrosion protection of metals and materials in a variety of industries, held in St. Petersburg for more than 35 years, since 1981.</p>

<p><strong>Thematic sections:</strong></p>

<p>modern methods and means of protection against corrosion of buildings, structures, industrial equipment, production facilities and pipeline transport in a variety of industries;<br />insulating and protective materials;<br />equipment for surface preparation;<br />coating equipment, the technology of their application;<br />instruments for determining the quality of insulation and protective coating, non-destructive testing and technical diagnostics</p>
