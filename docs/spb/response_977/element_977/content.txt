                                  "Matisov domik" is small comfortable hotel (55 rooms), it ideally situated on the quiet banks of the River Prjagka-in center of Saint Petersburg, in Russia. The famous Mariinsky Theatre is located in a few minutes walking from the hotel.The ideal location of hotel near cultural and historical sights and near the banks, and also shopping in the city, makes the hotel attractive for tourists who want to explore the culture of the city, businesses tourists who are here on a business trip and casual visitors. Many Russian and foreign musicians, stars of TV and journalists would like to stay here.
