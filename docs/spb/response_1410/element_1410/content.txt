                            18 - 20 oct 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Transport, Shipment, Warehouse, and Logistics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://smart-transport.expoforum.ru/en/
