        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    VII Nevsky International Ecological Congress
                
                
                    
                        
                            28 - 29 may 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Ecology and Environment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://ecocongress.info/en/congress/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Nevsky International Ecological Congress</strong> held in 2008. It aims to promote the formation of an international system of environmental safety by strengthening cross-border cooperation, improving international environmental law and the approximation of legislation states - CIS member states to regulate social relations in the area of interaction between society and nature.</p>

<p>In the framework of the <strong>VII Nevsky International Ecological Congress</strong> will be held plenary sessions and thematic round tables. There are a broad discussion, exchange of information and experience in implementing successful strategies and programs to ensure environmental safety, improvement of legislation for the protection and sustainable use of natural resources, conservation of ecosystems and biodiversity, joint efforts to mitigate man-made environmental catastrophes and natural disasters.</p>

<p>To participate in the congress invited the heads of legislative and executive branches of the government, representatives of international organizations, businesses, educational and research institutions, the media. The event will be organized exhibitions.</p>
