                        Наука
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Сайт мероприятия

                    This year Ioffe Institute - the world famous research center in physics and technology - celebrates 100-year anniversary of its foundation in 1918 under guidance of Abram Ioffe. One of the main events of the celebration is the International Multidisciplinary Conference “Frontiers of 21st Century Physics and Ioffe Institute” in Saint-Petersburg.Conference Program includes talks of about 50 prominent scientists from Russia and abroad and about 30 poster presentations of young researches working in Ioffe Institute, which cover major fields of the Institute research activity:plasma physics, astrophysics, solid state physics, physics of semiconductors, excitons and spintronics, nanoheterostructure technology, semiconductor photonics and electronics, energy conversion and storage, novel materials and carbon nanostructures, biophysics and life sciences.
