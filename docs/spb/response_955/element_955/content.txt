                                  Hotel Lel is a quiet and cozy 3-star hotel under the St. Petersburg Center for Postgraduate Education, Federal Biomedical Agency of Russia. The hotel first opened its doors to guests in 1988.The hotel is located in the Vyborgsky District of St. Petersburg, close to green parks. The location is great with excellent transport access and developed infrastructure. It is located close to several metro stations - Ozerki, Akademicheskaya and Politekhnicheskaya.Hotel Lel specializes in receiving guests visiting for medical checkups and treatment in clinics in St. Petersburg. All the necessary conditions are available for convenient accommodation of relatives of hospital patients. The hotel is also focused on business guests and specializes in providing services for conferences, seminars and trainings. One of the main competitive advantages of Hotel Lel is its wide range of conference services. The hotel is a wonderful place for seminars, conferences and training sessions at the highest level.
