                    25th Saint Petersburg International Conference on Integrated Navigation Systems (ICINS)
                
                
                    
                        
                            28 - 30 may 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        High technology and Innovations
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.elektropribor.spb.ru/icins2018/eindex
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    This annual Conference has become a traditional event for scientists and engineers from all over the world involved in navigation and motion control to share the results of their investigations and discuss prospects for the future.

CONFERENCE TOPICS: Inertial sensors, navigation and orientation systems,Integrated navigation and motion control systems,Global navigation satellite systems,Gravimetric aids to navigation.CONFERENCE LANGUAGES: All presentations at the Conference will be held in Russian or English and will be simultaneously translated.
