                The representative of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau took part in the enlarged meeting of the National Tourism Academy’s Presidium
            
            April 9 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The enlarged meeting of the National Tourism Academy’s Presidium took place on March 26 in the Baltic Academy of Tourism and Entrepreneurship.

In the framework of the meeting annually scientific readings in memory of Doctor of Science, Economic and the first President of Saint Petersburg Council for Tourism and Excursions Valery Nikiforov were held. He played the main role in the development of science and practice of Russian tourism in the period of a formation of new market relations in the tourism field.

The past readings were dedicated to the issues of the organization of convention cluster in Saint Petersburg. President of National Tourism Academy and Baltic Academy of Tourism and Entrepreneurship Tamara Vlasova, Chief Specialist of Saint Petersburg Government Committee on Tourism Development Alexey Gorlo, Head of Marketing Department of “Expoforum-International” Eugenia Lukashevich, Deputy Director of Saint Petersburg Tourist Information Bureau Lev Kuznetsov, Director of Convention Cluster, Head of GR-Department of RESTEC Group of companies Ivanov Sergey and many others spoke in the framework of the discussion.

The representatives of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau also took part in the meeting. Deputy General director of Bureau Natalia Serebrovskaya delivered a message “Convention Bureau and Convention Cluster: a search of cooperation ways”. She represented Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau to the audience. The speaker said that the Bureau’s mission is making Saint Petersburg as a leading world congress and exhibition destination. The realization of the mission and the most important Bureau’s goals depends on effective cooperation of the government and the professional community. Convention Bureau has to contribute to building such relationships. Meanwhile, Convention Cluster is a forceful tool for communications with the industry.
