                    VI Russian-Chinese forum «Business with China» 2015 
                
                
                    
                        
                            9 - 10 dec 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Business
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://vectorexpo.ru/our-events/6-forum-bcexpo6/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>VI Russian-Chinese forum &laquo;Business with China&raquo; 2015 is one the most important event in the field of the business communication between Russia and China, where more than 2 500 people cooperate every year.&nbsp;</p>

<p>The experts will discuss the problems of Russian-Chinese wholesale trade and come forward with their practical solutions. The questions of cross-border trade and e-commerce are basic subjects of the Forum`s congress section. The particular way of the Forum is the exchange of the wholesale purchases. It includes three types of goods: electronics, materials for home &amp; repair, clothing &amp; shoes. The audience of the Forum is importers, exporters, online-retailers and service-providers.</p>
