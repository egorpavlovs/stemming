                                  Hotel Indigo St.Petersburg-Tchaikovskogo is located in the center of St.Petersburg.Located amongst the stunning architecture and historical prestige of Russia’s capital of culture, our hotel is in the heart of the historic centre between the Neva and Fontanka rivers, the Summer Garden and Tavricheskiy Garden, just few steps from Liteiny bridge – an ideal spot for guests wanting to explore the city. The main street of Nevskiy Prospect is just ten minutes away.Besides accommodation in rooms of four types, we are glad to offer the following services.The pool is located on the ground floor of the Fitness Studio and free of charge for our hotel guests. Our Finnish sauna will help to relieve tiredness and strengthen your immune system. The fitness studio interior reflects our neighbourhood through the architectural character of the city mansions’ facades. 

5 comfortable, well-equipped meeting rooms offer a solution for all kind of events.

Afisha Bar is an inexhaustible and reliable source of information about the highlights of St.Petersburg
