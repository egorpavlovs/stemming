                    Cluster of conferences "Orgkhim-2016"
                
                
                    
                        
                            27 jun - 1 jul 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Education
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://onlinereg.ru/orgchem2016
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Cluster of complementary conferences and symposia on organic chemistry include:</p>

<ul>	<li>19TH Youth Conference-school organic chemistry;</li>	<li>Conference &quot;chemistry of heterocyclic compounds&quot;;</li>	<li>Conference &quot;medical and Bioorganic chemistry; VI Symposium on Organometallic Chemistry (under the auspices of the RNF);</li>	<li>1-th all-Russian Conference with elements of scientific school &quot;computer modeling of heterocyclic polymers&raquo;.</li></ul>

<p>Cluster activities will also include:</p>

<ul>	<li>Excursions in St. Petersburg State University resource centers with the opportunity of measurement of samples submitted (by appointment);</li>	<li>Master-class &laquo;modern methods of searching and processing of chemical information.</li>	<li>CAS SciFinder &quot;;</li>	<li>RFBR Roundtable; Exhibitions instrumental and laboratory chemical equipment companies-sponsors.</li></ul>
