                    <p><strong>&quot;Forum of business and investment activity. Northwestern Federal District&quot;&nbsp;</strong>is a significant event for the region, where participants will have the opportunity to meet in St. Petersburg with Russian and international potential investors. The Forum will be a platform for business dialogue with&nbsp;regional representatives of government and business, industry and professional associations.</p>

<p>&quot;Forum of business and investment activity. Northwestern Federal District&quot;&nbsp;is one of the main parts of the regional program &quot;Invest in the North-West&quot;, whose strategic goal is&nbsp;creating favorable conditions for the growth of attracting investments in the Northwestern Federal District.</p>
