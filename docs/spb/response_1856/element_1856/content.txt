                    Conference of the European Association for Chinese Studies -EACS-
                
                
                    
                        
                            23 - 28 aug 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The 21st biennial conference of the European Association for Chinese Studies </strong>will be held in St. Petersburg, Russia on August 23-28, 2016. It is jointly organized by St. Petersburg State University, the Institute of Oriental Manuscripts RAS and the State Hermitage Museum.</p>

<p>The scholars from Europe and beyond are invited to submit their paper and panel proposals online at the conference webpage. An international board of referees will evaluate the proposals submitted to the organizers. The language of the conference is English.</p>

<p><strong>The sections of the conference include:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Linguistics</li>	<li>Teaching Chinese as a Foreign Language</li>	<li>Premodern Literature</li>	<li>Modern Literature</li>	<li>Cinema, Theater, Performing Arts</li>	<li>Translation Studies</li>	<li>Art, Archeology &amp; Material Culture</li>	<li>Chinese Manuscripts, Books, Artifacts Abroad</li>	<li>Religion</li>	<li>Philosophy</li>	<li>Premodern History</li>	<li>Modern History</li>	<li>East-West Contacts &amp; Perceptions</li>	<li>Politics &amp; International Relations</li>	<li>Sociology &amp; Anthropology</li>	<li>Gender Studies</li>	<li>Economics</li>	<li>Law</li>	<li>Media Studies</li>	<li>Environment</li>	<li>Cross-sectional</li></ul>
