                New film center by the cultic film studio Lenfilm 
            
            September 3 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The veteran Russian film studio Lenfilm has opened a new film center in Saint Petersburg.

The main role at the complex plays redesigned historical film hall which is located in the famous building with columns. It has not been used last years, now film hall is completed with up-to-date equipment and is revived. The film center includes an exhibition hall, a café, and a souvenir shop. Film festivals and meeting with artists will be held here.

The creation of art venues at deserted historical buildings is the successful European practice that can make more interesting the cultural life and the leisure of citizens and guests of Saint Petersburg. Film center Lenfilm is opened for everyone since August 28.
