                    All-Russian scientific-practical Conference "Physical rehabilitation in sports medicine and adaptive physical education»
                
                
                    
                        
                            1 - 30 jun 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Scientific directions of the Conference:&nbsp;<br />1. Training for improving and developing directions of physical rehabilitation in the system of higher and secondary vocational education.&nbsp;<br />2. Physical rehabilitation, as an essential element in the system of medical rehabilitation.&nbsp;<br />3. Methods of physical rehabilitation in recovery programs.&nbsp;<br />4. Physical rehabilitation in working with persons with health problems.&nbsp;<br />5. Modern developments and technologies of physical rehabilitation. Scientific &nbsp;substantiation of methods of physical rehabilitation.&nbsp;<br />6. Regulatory and legal support activities in the field of physical rehabilitation.&nbsp;<br />7. Future directions for advanced training of specialists in physical rehabilitation.&nbsp;<br />8. A multidisciplinary approach in the rehabilitation of patients and persons with disabilities.</p>
