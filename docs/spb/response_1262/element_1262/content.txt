                International Health Forum is held in Saint Petersburg
            
            October 15 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The development of the health care system and the improvement of the quality of life will come into focus at the Saint Petersburg International Health Forum. The event is held in CEC Expoforum on October 14-16. 

The forum is a united platform for professionals in the sphere of health care, consolidating five specialized exhibitions in one exhibition brand. The program covers such directions as medical equipment, pharmaceutics, health and medical services, aesthetic and cosmetic medicine, optics and bio-technologies. Execution of topically similar events at the same venue will provide comprehensive approach to development of the wellness industry.
