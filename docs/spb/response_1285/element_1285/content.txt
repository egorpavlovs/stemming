                                  Economy class hotel "Aliot". This is a respectable hotel, which successfully combines business rhythm and cozy atmosphere. Architectural solutions and finishing works are made by Austrian and Italian experts. Rooms are decorated with paintings by famous Petersburg artists. An amazing panorama of the northern capital opens from the windows of an economy class hotel “Aliot”: the golden domes of St. Isaac’s Cathedral, the blue of the Gulf of Finland, the mast of ships and boom cranes in the Sea Port.
