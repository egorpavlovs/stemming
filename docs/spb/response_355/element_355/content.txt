                    State Hermitage Museum
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Museum of fine and decorative arts, located in the complex of the Winter Palace in the center of St. Petersburg. One of the largest and most significant art, cultural and historical museums in the world.

The Museum is located in five main buildings on the Palace embankment, connected with each other and forming a single Museum complex. One of the buildings of the Hermitage is the Winter Palace, which in the past was the main Imperial Palace, the current building of which was built in 1754-1762.
