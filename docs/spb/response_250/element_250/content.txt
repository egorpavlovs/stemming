                    Pending issues of etiotropic therapy of priority disease
                
                
                    
                        
                            11 - 12 dec 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                        Saint-Petersburg Hotel
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://congress-ph.ru/event/etiotrop18
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    All-Russian scientific-practical conference «Pending issues of etiotropic therapy of priority disease».

Top specialists from the Ministry of Health and Russian Ministry of Defense, academic staff from medical universities, doctors from Saint Petersburg, Leningrad region and other regions of the Russian Federation health facilities are invited to participate in the Conference.

The number of participants is expected to amount to more than 500 persons

The conference will be accompanied by an exhibition of Russian and foreign pharmaceutical companies, and medical device manufacturing companies.
