                                  Hotel "Vintage" opened in April 2010 in a dynamically-developing business district of St. Petersburg, in the immediate vicinity of Proletarskaya metro station and 15 km from Pulkovo Airport.

Nowadays “Vintage” hotel is the only hotel in St. Petersburg that is located in the vicinity of the Ring Road, and is the one providing a convenient transportation connection to the city’s main arteries, and just as easily to access the Ring Road, making the hotel a popular for people travelling by car.
