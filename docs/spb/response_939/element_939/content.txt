                Congress bureau – focus on cooperation and foreign expertise
            
            January 28 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

The special session «Congress bureau – focus on cooperation and foreign expertise» opened Europe + Asia Event Forum on January 21. The session was organized by Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau.

«The business tourism is the part of tourism industry which develops very active in Saint Petersburg. 6,5 mln. people visited the city in 2015», - CEO of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Anna Chetvergova said. The key goal of the bureau is to increase the number of business tourists in Saint Petersburg through attracting international congresses and conferences. Over 230 congress hotels and other venues are able to host business events here. Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau submitted 15 applications for the concourses of international professional associations for the right to hold their meetings. Which events are necessary for the city? What intensifies the campaign of the city promotion? PCO and DMC companies, convention bureau of Russian regions and foreign countries, representatives of the government discussed these and other topics.  

The participants of the session told about the conception «Beauty. Strategy. Cost». It shows how professional associations choose destination for holding events. Saint Petersburg, the cultural capital of Russia and the open-air museum, has the strong advantage as for the beauty. The strategy means the situation when the economic profiles of the city and the association coincide. Saint Petersburg is specialized in IT, heavy machinery, shipbuilding, science. These industries should become the priorities in selecting events. The participants of the session agreed that current economic situation is profitable for foreign associations. Professional unions can learn from ambassadors about financial and other profits of events in Russia. Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau will launch the ambassador program this year.
