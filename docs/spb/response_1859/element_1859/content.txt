                    The annual International Festival "The Imperial Gardens of Russia"
                
                
                    
                        
                            10 - 19 jun 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Agriculture
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://igardens.ru/en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The Festival is the annual significant event attracting about 100 000 visitors. It lasts 10 day in early June during the period of White Nights. The Festival is renowned abroad and all over Russia. Participants and landscape specialists have many contacts with their foreign colleagues.<br />One of the most exciting events of the Festival is a show-gardens contest. About 25 participants are judged by the International Professional Jury. The visitors vote for their favorite gardens as well. Every year the top ranked Russian and foreign landscape designers participate in the Garden Festival. For the providers of plants and hard materials the participation in garden construction is an opportunity to advertise their products among professional audience and general public. The Festival attracts tourists and high media attention.</p>

<p>The Festival is traditionally supported by the Government of Saint Petersburg. It is also under the patronage of Her Royal Highness Princess Michael of Kent.</p>
