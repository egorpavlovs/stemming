                    RESTEC® Exhibition Company
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    RESTEC® Exhibition Company is part of RESTEC® Group. For more than 25 years, RESTEC® Exhibition Company has held leading positions in the Russian exhibition market. We formed a team of professionals with extensive, unique experience in the exhibition business.

We use the latest global technologies to work with our target audience, allowing both participants and visitors to receive services that increase the number and effectiveness of business contacts, and we offer programmes to specialists including meetings with the heads of leading industrial enterprises and representatives of regional administrations using the most innovative communication tool: matchmaking.

Other efficient technologies of RESTEC® Exhibition Company include carrying out network regional and federal cross-industry projects, establishing industry clubs and more.

RESTEC® Exhibition Company makes extensive use of such social business support resources as young specialist days, job and labour resource fairs, conducts socio-educational and charitable activities for a wide audience, and participates in municipal social programmes.

RESTEC® Exhibition Company developed as a multi-format enterprise: our portfolio includes B2B and B2C format exhibitions, congresses, conferences, and festivals in 40 spheres of industry.

Membership of the Chamber of Commerce of the Russian Federation and a number of Russian and international unions and associations provides effective business contacts for the target audience of exhibition and congress projects, while active support of Russian ministries and agencies, the government and administration of Moscow, St. Petersburg, and Leningrad Region and other regions ensures a high level of event organisation.

RESTEC® Exhibition Company has been a member of the Russian Union of Exhibitions and Fairs (RUEF) since 1992, a member of the Global Association of the Exhibition Industry (UFI) since 1997, and a member of the Association of Organizers of Exhibitions Abroad and of National Group Participations in International Trade Fairs and Exhibitions (InterEXPO) since 2007.

We invite companies and organisations developing their business in Russian and foreign industrial markets to work in cooperation with us.

We have been proving that exhibitions are effective for 25 years already!
