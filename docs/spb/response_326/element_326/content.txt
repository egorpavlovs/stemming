                    OLTA Travel
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    OLTA Travel is an experienced Russian DMC. Founded in 2010 the company serves leading tour operators and agencies from France, United Kingdom, USA, Canada, Germany, Czech Republic, Poland, Romania, Thailand, Singapore, India and many other countries. Our offices of the company are located in the very center of Saint-Petersburg and Moscow.

Goal of OLTA Travel is to offer unique itineraries to make your clients fall in love with Russia! Working with only reliable suppliers we provide the highest quality services and best prices for: incentive tours, teambuilding programs; conferences, educational events; exhibitions, business trips; festive events, gala-dinners, corporate parties, the awards ceremony; as well as group and individual leisure tours, VIP-tours.

In 2016 Olta Travel has received a major award in the field of world tourism World Travel Awards, and became Russia’s Leading DMC. In 2017, the company is again nominated for this title.

OLTA Travel is a regular participant in major international industry events (ITBM, WTM London, ITF Taipei, IFTM Top Resa and others), joins the prestigious international associations (MPI, SITE, ADMEI, World without borders (Мир без Границ)), and also works closely with major travel publications (Echo Touristique, DMC Mag, Quotidien de Tourisme, etc.).

OLTA Travel has all the necessary licenses and certificates for the organization of events and tours at all stages.

Creative thinking, unconventional approach to problem solving and excellent knowledge of the market enable our team to create memorable programs of business and leisure trips. Our slogan – "Great is not enough! We can do even better!" That is why we are always head on long-term relationships and work hard to achieve these common team goals.

We are ready to organize all types of business events for you!
