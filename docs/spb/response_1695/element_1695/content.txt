                    Electronic Governance and Open Society:  Challenges in Eurasia (EGOSE 2016)
                
                
                    
                        
                            22 - 23 nov 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://egov-conf.ifmo.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Electronic Governance and Open Society:&nbsp;Challenges in Eurasia (EGOSE 2016)</strong> will be held in St. Petersburg, Russian Federation on November 22-23, 2016.&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong><span style="font-size:13px; line-height:1.6">The&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size:13px; line-height:1.6">EGOSE 2016</span></strong><span style="font-size:13px; line-height:1.6">&nbsp;aims to address the main issues of concern within the Information Society, e-government, e-governance, and e-democracy. The Conference provides a platform for networking and collaboration of eGovernance experts in the Eurasian area.</span></p>

<p><strong>TOPICS OF INTEREST</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Information Society and eGovernance&nbsp;</li>	<li>Open Government Prospects&nbsp;</li>	<li>Convergence in E-Governance Services</li>	<li>Citizen Centered E-Government</li>	<li>Participatory Governance</li>	<li>Open Government Data&nbsp;</li>	<li>eGovernance and Policy&nbsp;Modeling&nbsp;</li>	<li>eGovernance and Eurasian Integration&nbsp;</li>	<li>Social Media: Tools for analysis, participation, and impact</li>	<li>Building Smart City&nbsp;</li>	<li>Smart Citizens and Quality of Life</li>	<li>Disruptive E-Governance</li></ul>
