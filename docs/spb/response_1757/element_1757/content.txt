                Arabian Travel Market: tourism in digital trend
            
            May 8 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                 



22th International exhibition Arabian Travel Market was held in Dubai on May 4-7. General Director of the Saint-Petersburg Convention Bureau Anna Chetvergova went on a business visit there.

It is no coincidence that the United Arab Emirates host Middle East’s largest travel trade event. The tourist flow to Dubai shows a solid growth annually - 8,2 percent in 2014. It is significantly higher than the global average of 4,7 percent. The city prepares actively for the World exhibition Expo-2020 and plans to achieve 20 mln. people tourist flow.

The exhibition space of Arabian Travel Market was 22000 sq. meters. 2700 exhibitors, 64 national pavilions and 23000 visitors took part in the show. 

All directions of the international tourism industry and tourism projects of the biggest development and investment companies were presented at the exhibition. Arabian Travel Market accompanies a varied congress program: 3 full days, 3 presentation performance venues, packed audiences of listeners. The accent of this year is a using digital- and smart-technologies in the tourism: online-booking, social media marketing, mobile applications, new services. The meeting of Ministers for Tourism from Arab countries was held within congress program.

Arabian Travel Market was an excellent interactive platform for the acquaintance of Middle East partners with Saint Petersburg’s convention peculiarities.
