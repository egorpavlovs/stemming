                                  Group of companies “Astra Marine” is one of the largest companies - ship-owners in Saint Petersburg who provides passengers traffic on the rivers and canals. Own fleet consist of 15 motor vessel. Boat rent is the main service provided by company, but in order to make your rest become really comfortable, company may also offer event organization service, as well as other additional services.
