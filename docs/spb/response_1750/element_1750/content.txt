                Tourism is in the list of Saint Petersburg priority industries 
            
            May 14 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg priority industries were mentioned within the City Program of foremost actions providing persistent economic development and social stability.

Tourism got shortlisted. Pharma, IT, medicine, engine, electrical fixtures, engineering tools manufacture, education, research and development, shipbuilding and transportation manufacture, transport infrastructure building, freight and passenger traffic were designated as priority industries too.

The list was composed for a range of criterions. Among them are status of key and life-supporting Saint Petersburg industry, human and scientific resources, investment, export and import phase-out potential, rating as industry with higher value added.
