                    International exhibition of services in the field of health and medical tourism "Health & Medical Tourism"
                
                
                    
                        
                            13 - 15 oct 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.healthtourism-expo.ru/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>On October 13-15, 2017 the <strong>International exhibition Health&amp;Medical Tourism</strong> will take place at the CEC EXPOFORUM in St. Petersburg. &nbsp;&nbsp;<br /><strong>International exhibition Health&amp;Medical Tourism</strong> is an important event in the sphere of medical and health tourism in the Northwest Russia for both Russian and foreign clinics, resorts, and tour operators specialised in medical tourism.<br />&nbsp;<br /><strong>Exhibitors:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Medical clinics and diagnostic centres;</li>	<li>Rehabilitation and healthcare centres;</li>	<li>Health resorts;</li>	<li>Sanatoriums.</li></ul>

<p>Visitors:&nbsp;<br />Healthcare professionals, medical practitioners, representatives of travel agencies and insurance companies, representatives of ministries and departments developing tourism and healthcare in the Northwest Russia, as well as people interested in rehabilitation and health-related recreation.</p>
