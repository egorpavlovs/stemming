                    Festival of contemporary animalistic art "Zooart-2015"
                
                
                    
                        
                            1 aug - 30 sep 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Culture and art
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.zoo-art.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Festival of contemporary animalistic art &quot;Zooart&quot;&nbsp;is a new format of animalistic exhibitions.</p>

<p>The Zooart festival&nbsp;is an art perspective&nbsp;on&nbsp;wildlife by caring eyes, attempt to comprehend spontaneous means of art, to find a beauty source in nature&nbsp;and to show perfection of life in all its manifestations.</p>
