                Digital for Saint Petersburg museums
            
            June 29 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The conference Internet and Modern Society was held in Saint Petersburg on June 23-25. The main aim of this science project is the development of researches dedicated to information technologies and resources.

Information systems, e-databases, technologies, new media and information society, computer linguistics, e-business, online services became the central issues of the program. These knowledges can be used in different fields: from e-government and e-health services to e-libraries, culture or art mobile applications.

For instance museum information systems help guests to plan visit to an exhibition, buy a ticket online and orientate better at a venue. The biggest museum of the Russian art applies advanced technologies. The project called the Russian Museum. The Augmented Reality exists since 2011. The user sees the world around in the real time by using a mobile camera. The special program determines objects from QR-codes and posts full information about it.

The Russian Museum and the Kunstkamera are the partners of the project Russian Linked Culture Cloud. Its aim is the foundation of the database dedicated to Russian cultural heritage. The project has the strong user interface and the set of tools for information gathering and setting up of own excursion tours.

Undoubtedly similar projects allow to make museum visiting more informative and comfortable and favor tourist flow and increasing of repeated visits to the city.
