                The foundation of new convention centre is laid in Saint Petersburg
            
            April 22 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

New convention centre named Time will be built on the bank of the Neva River. The Governor of Saint Petersburg Georgy Poltavchenko laid down the time capsule on the site of the future complex.

The project will be made as 24-floors building where modern convention venue for 1150 delegates and business offices with a total area of 20 thousand sq.m. will headquarter. Congress hotel by Holiday Inn with a capacity of 414 rooms will be built too.
