                    Exhibition of equipment, feed and veterinary products for animal and poultry farming "Smart Farm"
                
                
                    
                        
                            30 nov - 1 dec 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Agriculture
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.smartfarm-expo.ru/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <div>For the first time in St. Petersburg the exhibition of equipment, feed and veterinary products for animal and poultry farming&nbsp;<strong>Smart Farm</strong>&nbsp;will be held on 30 November - 1 December 2016.&nbsp;<br /><br /><strong>Exhibitors will present:</strong><ul>	<li>Equipment for breeding, keeping and feeding</li>	<li>Equipment for milking</li>	<li>Equipment for meat and milk primary processing</li>	<li>Feed and combined feed&nbsp;for farm animals and poultry</li>	<li>Equipment for feed production and storage</li>	<li>Veterinary products, instruments and services</li></ul></div>

<div>Animal and poultry farming is the leading industry of agricultural production Northwest region of Russia.<br />&nbsp;<br />Leningrad region takes the 1st place in Russia on milk and egg production and the 3rd place on animals and poultry production.<br />&nbsp;<br />Animal&nbsp;farming, poultry farming and feed production in the Leningrad region is represented by:</div>

<ul>	<li>216 agricultural plants</li>	<li>near 1000 peasant farms</li>	<li>over 104&nbsp;000 private farms&nbsp;</li></ul>

<div>Visitors:&nbsp;managers, engineers, zootechnicians, agronomists, veterinarians of stud farms, animal and poultry&nbsp;farms, agrofirms, peasant farms and state farms.</div>
