                Neva exhibition will celebrate 25th anniversary
            
            September 22 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Neva, International Maritime Exhibition and Conferences of Russia, will be held in Saint Petersburg on September 22-25. The event will celebrate 25th anniversary and take place at the CEC Expoforum for the first time.

Maritime sphere is one of the important themes, which forms the convention image of Saint Petersburg. Neva ranks among the biggest civil maritime exhibitions in the world and is considered the most large-scale industry project in Russia. Over 480 firms and enterprises from 32 countries will participate in the event this year. Companies from Kazakhstan, Belarus, Philippines, United Arab Emirates will debut at the exhibition.

Business program will cover such topics as equipment and technologies for ports, security of ports and shipping, economic, technological and maritime cooperation in the Arctic, prospects of Russian-Finland partnership in shipbuilding, development trends of Russian fishing fleet and many others.
