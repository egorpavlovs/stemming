                Employees of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau visited round-table discussion for intercluster interaction
            
            March 26 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                 

On March 25 round-table discussion “Intercluster interaction: interregional and international coopetition” was held in CEC “Expoforum”. The customer of the event was Committee for External Relations of Saint Petersburg.

In the framework of the round-table discussion actual issues of cooperation of clusters, their interregional cooperation and the role in the field of import substitution were considered.

Employees of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau visited the round-table discussion. General director Anna Chetvergova, Deputy General director Natalia Serebrovskaya and Head of department of industry’s development Elena Rechitc were among them.

Participants and guests of the event noted a high significance of discussed issues for development of congress and exhibition industry in Saint Petersburg.
