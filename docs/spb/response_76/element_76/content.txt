                    Dashkova Residence
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Hotel "Dashkova Residence" is located in a house with a rich history, built in the XVIII century.

On November 1, 1782 the house was sold to Empress Princess Ekaterina Romanovna Dashkova. Ekaterina Dashkova lived in the mansion for 12 years."Dashkova residence" preserves the historical appearance of the mansion, combining its unique features with stylish and modern interiors.

Saint Petersburg since the Foundation is inextricably linked with Holland and its culture, the style of the hotel makes the connection to allow guests to fully enjoy the unique atmosphere of the time, to feel the connection of times.
