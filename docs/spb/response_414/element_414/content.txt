                                  Nestled on the banks of Griboedov Canal, the hotel's location is just minutes away from the Mariinsky Theatre and the city's rapidly growing business area. The complimentary hotel shuttle bus conveniently connects you with the St. Isaac's Cathedral and the attractions of Nevsky Prospekt. Discover the comfortable atmosphere of a well-designed lobby, equipped with a business center and free Wi-Fi.  We offer you bright and spacious guest rooms, modern gym, 9 comfortable conference rooms, the stylish Onegin Lounge, Bierstube Restaurant with classic German cuisine and its own micro-brewery.
