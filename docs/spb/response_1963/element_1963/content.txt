                    International Forum "Transport Security"
                
                
                    
                        
                            6 - 7 apr 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Transport, Shipment, Warehouse, and Logistics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://global-port.ru/en/conferences/44.html
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>International Forum &quot;Transport Security&quot;&nbsp;</strong>is traditionally hold in Saint Petersburg in March. The purpose of the Forum is to develop effective solutions aimed to&nbsp;create&nbsp;safe transport environment in the Russian Federation.</p>

<p>Together&nbsp;with the business program of the Forum, there is&nbsp;an exhibition of innovative technologies and masterclasses. The exhibition presents the latest samples of equipment and facilities to ensure the safety of transport infrastructure and vehicles, projects promoting&nbsp;safe transport environment, information and communication systems and technologies, and services&nbsp;providing&nbsp;complex&nbsp;security. Also,&nbsp;several&nbsp;issues&nbsp;are&nbsp;discussed on the forum:&nbsp;international cooperation in the field of transport security; reducing&nbsp;the accident rate on all modes of transport and other.</p>
