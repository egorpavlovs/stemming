                                  Hotel “Vyborgskaya” is an inexpensive three-star hotel in St. Petersburg, it has been receiving guests since 1968. In one of the hotel buildings renovated, the rooms are equipped with new modern furniture and appliances. In addition to the low price, the hotel is attractively situated in a convenient location - it is located in the Black River area, next to the metro station of the same name. Hotel "Vyborgskaya" since 2006 is under the leadership of the company "Turris". The hotel has 267 rooms.
