                    Domestic tourism development Association " League of tour operators of St. Petersburg"
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    The Association is a public Association of tour operators involved in the reception of Russian tourists in St. Petersburg, established on the basis of joint activities to protect common interests. The activities of the Association are based on the principles of voluntariness, equality, self-government and the rule of law.
