                    European Congress on preventive, regenerative and anti-aging medicine
                
                
                    
                        
                            8 - 10 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.esaam-org.eu/ecopram2016/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>European society of preventive, regenerative and anti-aging medicine (ESAAM)</strong> exists since 2004 year.</p>

<p>The society brings together research and clinical experts in the field of Gerontology, endocrinologists, dermatology, nutrition, sports medicine, Pharmacology and other professionals engaged in the prevention of aging human body.</p>

<p>At the <strong>5th European Congress on preventative regenerative and anti-aging medicine</strong>, most of the time will be devoted to presentations and discussions on latest scientific achievements and practical recommendations in the field of health, increasing spare capacity and slow down the aging process of the human body. The Congress is expected to involve Europe&#39;s leading specialists in this direction-Francesco Romanelli (Italy), Christos Zubulis (Germany), Vladimir Havinson (Russia), Vladimir Anisimov (Russia), Jean-Pierre Naim (Switzerland) , Jocelyne Beilin (France), as well as academics and practitioners from Europe, Japan, China, South Korea, United States and other countries.<br />The Congress will be held in the format of plenary lectures, seminars and training certification cycles. In addition, the Congress held an international exhibition of the leading manufacturers of products for medicine active longevity. All participants will be offered an extensive cultural program.</p>
