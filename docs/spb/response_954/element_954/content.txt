                                  NashOTEL is a 4 stars hotel with 58 spacious and comfortable rooms of different categories. Lenexpo ExhibitionComplex and Passenger Seaport are located nearby. NashOTEL offers conference facilities and equipment for conferences and seminars. Fully equipped conference hall is located on the second floor of the hotel. It is fitted up with modern equipment and has big windows to let daylight in. The conference hall can host up to 50 participants.
