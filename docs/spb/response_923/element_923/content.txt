                Saint Petersburg hosts the International Festival of Events and Live Communication
            
            April 14 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg, Russia has been chosen as the host city for the 11th edition of EuBea, the International festival of events & live communication. This was announced at the gathering of the EuBea Advisory Board held in Italian town Courmayeur on 1st April. The dates for EuBea 2016 were appointed for 13-15th October.  

“It’s not for nothing, that the festival has been already held in atmospheric European cities like Milan, Seville, Brussels. It’s quite a logical step to choose Saint Petersburg, been considered as cultural capital of Russia, as a venue of 2016 EuBea Festival”, Salvatore Sagone, EuBea Festival President and ADC Group CEO says.  

Change of the venue underlies the concept of the festival, as the event’s goal is not just to award best of the best, but also to create inspiring environment to exchange ideas and develop new business opportunities among top professionals from all over Europe.

The remarkable fact is holding the EuBea 2016 right in the period when Saint Petersburg pays a special attention to the promotion of the city as leading convention destination and to the development of the event and congress tourism.

“The solid experience of holding widely different events in Saint Petersburg, from congresses and conferences organized by international professional associations to city festivals allows to project the EuBea 2016 at the highest level”, - says Acting CEO of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Nataliya Serebrovskaya.  

Over 60 speakers from Europe and representatives of more than 500 multinational companies such as Cisco, Lego, Airbus, Fiat, IBM, Nissan, Amway, heads of leading European associations and media, key opinion leaders of the EU industry will be gathered at the EuBea in Saint Petersburg to discuss the evolution and innovation processes in European events and live communication industry. The theme of EuBea 2016: Inspire – Empower – Succeed!

A President of the Jury appointed Gerd De Bruycker, Marketing Leader in the Cisco for Northern Europe. He is heading up the EMEA Events Team and is leading the EMEA Marketing Learning and Development Program. "EuBea's value lies in setting the cutting-edge standards of the event industry and in uplifting the professionalism perception. I am therefore very honored to have been appointed as the President of the 2016 Jury. For all events professionals around Europe, attending EuBea Festival will be a great chance for networking, exchanging ideas and experiences, and finding valuable content", Gerd De Bruycker comments. 

INFORMATION

EuBea is International Festival of Events and Live Communication, established in 2006 by ADC Group as a key European award in experience marketing. In 2014 EuBea established new landmarks for the Event industry, by transforming a traditional Event Festival into a truly Live Communication experience

ADC Group is media-holding company, founded in 2002 by Salvatore Sagone. The group includes the communication news agencies ADCexpress and E20express, and Italian Nuova Comunicazione magazine. ADC Group has also issued “Communication Notebook” (I Quaderni della Comunicazione) reading series, and since 2006 it has held International Festival of Events and Live Communication.

General partner of the 11th edition of the EuBea is Live Communication Group – group of companies, including professional media on live com industry, mice and event agency, production and design studio.
