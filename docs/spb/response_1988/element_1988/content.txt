                    IV All-Russian Forum of students from medical and pharmaceutical universities of Russia
                
                
                    
                        
                            23 - 25 mar 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Education
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://szgmu.ru/eng/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>This event has round tables and exhibitions. Round tables devoted to topics such as research areas in educational institutions, evaluation of the quality of education in medical schools, extracurricular activities of students.</p>

<p>The exhibition is devoted to various topics. In 2015, one of the exhibition was dedicated to carrying out in the Russian Federation, the national fight against cardiovascular disease.</p>

<p>However, there is a quota for participation. It is 4-5 people from the educational institution.</p>

<p>The Forum will be attended by representatives of the Ministry of Health of the Russian Federation, medical students, as well as rectors of educational institutions subordinated to the Ministry of Health of Russia.</p>
