                    International specialised exhibition "Compressors. Pumps. Valves. Drives" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.ptfair.ru/exhibition/compressors/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The Exhibition </strong>gathers companies offering equipment, technologies and components for construction, power industry, metallurgy, housing and utility systems, oil and gas extraction, chemical industry, shipbuilding, machine tool industry.</p>

<p>The exhibition is part of the <strong>Petersburg Technical Fair.</strong></p>

<ul>	<li><strong>PIPE ACCESSORIES:&nbsp;</strong>regulators, valves, mechanisms, plumbing fittings, operation and maintenance of pipe fittings</li>	<li><strong>DRIVES:</strong>&nbsp;reductors and motor reductors, hydraulic drive systems and their elements, prepacked electric drives and motors, pneumatic drives, special drives, servo drives, clutches and brakes, transmission belts and chains, seals, oils and lubricants, equipment for centralised lubrication systems, regulating systems, cooperation in production of drives and their elements</li>	<li><strong>COMPRESSORS:</strong>&nbsp;oil type and oil-free compressors, gas compressors, air compressors, reciprocating compressors, gear compressors, centrifugal compressors, rotary screw compressors, liquid ring compressors, movable and portable compressors, pneumatic tools, repair and maintenance of compressors, components for compressors, blocks</li>	<li><strong>PUMPS:</strong>&nbsp;high-pressure pumps, general purpose industrial pumps, household pumps, rotary vane controlled and uncontrolled pumps, chemical pumps, centrifugal pumps, submersible pumps, outdoor pumps, drain pumps, disk pumps, manual pumps, electric pumps, pneumatic pumps, electric drives, pumping stations, components for pumping equipment</li>	<li><strong>VACUUM TECHNOLOGY</strong></li></ul>
