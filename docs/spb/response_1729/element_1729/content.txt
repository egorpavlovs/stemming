                    International Beauty Expo "Nevskie Berega"
                
                
                    
                        
                            22 - 25 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Culture and art
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.nevberega.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Saint Petersburg International Beauty Expo NEVSKIE BEREGA</strong> is one of the largest exhibitions in Russia, which takes place in St.-Petersburg two times a year.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Target audience: heads of the companies-distributors, directors of salons and beauty centers, hairdressers and hair stylists, beauty therapists, nail-designers, make-up artists, pedicure masters, directors of professional beauty shops and many other beauty-orientated professionals.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Attendance: 100 000 people within 4 days of the exhibition, more than 50 % of them are experts.</p>

<p><strong>Saint Petersburg International Beauty Expo NEVSKIE BEREGA includes:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>International Professional Beauty Exhibition</li>	<li>The International Competitions on Hairdressing Art, Nail design and Make-up</li>	<li>The Business program for all e beauty orientated professionals.</li>	<li>Bright trade shows</li></ul>
