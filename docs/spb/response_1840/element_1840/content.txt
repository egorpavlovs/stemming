                    UArctic Congress 2016
                
                
                    
                        
                            12 - 16 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.uarctic.org/about-uarctic/events/uarctic-congress-2016/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Hosted by Saint Petersburg State University, <strong>the UArctic Congress 2016 </strong>will feature Science and Meeting sections, including:</p>

<ul>	<li>Acclaimed keynote speakers and scientific experts presenting their latest research.</li>	<li>Parallel sessions on an array of Arctic science, policy, and education topics.</li>	<li>Formal meetings for representatives of the Council of UArctic and UArctic Rectors&rsquo; Forum.</li>	<li>Pre-Meetings to foster contacts and enhance networking.</li>	<li>Opportunities for promoting and marketing your organization and activities.</li>	<li>A UArctic Student Forum with workshops.</li>	<li>A Cultural and Social program like no other.</li></ul>
