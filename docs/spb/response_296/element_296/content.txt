                St. Petersburg in the list of The 50 Best Romantic Getaways
            
            August 29 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                Travel + Leisure made a list of the 50 Best Romantic Getaways, St. Petersburg has a pride of place in it alongside with New York, Istanbul, Buenos Aires. The Venice of the North is the only Russian city in this rating.

Travel + Leisure is an authoritative travel magazine based in New York City, with 4.8 million readers. Magazine publishes news and information about the most exciting destinations in the world.

“This former seat of imperial Russia has a surreal beauty, especially in the colder months when its fairy-tale architecture—the mint-green Winter Palace, the multi-striped onion domes of the Church on Spilled Blood—stands in sharp relief to the crystalline snow”,– Travel + Leisure describes St. Petersburg.
