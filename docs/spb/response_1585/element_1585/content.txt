                    International Professional Beauty Exhibition "Nevskie Berega"
                
                
                    
                        
                            23 - 26 feb 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Beauty, Cosmetics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.nevberega.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>International Beauty Industry Exhibition &laquo;Nevskie Berega&raquo; </strong>occupies more than 10 000 sq. m. of exhibition area, 3 floors of the St-Petersburg Sport and Concert Complex. The exhibition attracts more than 20 000 visitors a day.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Target audience: heads of the companies-distributors, directors of salons and beauty centers, hairdressers and hair stylists, beauty therapists, nail-designers, make-up artists, pedicure masters, directors of professional beauty shops and many other beauty-orientated professionals.</p>

<p><strong>&nbsp;Exhibition Profile:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Professional face, body and hair cosmetics.</li>	<li>Hair and beauty equipment.</li>	<li>Decorative cosmetics and make-up.&nbsp;</li></ul>
