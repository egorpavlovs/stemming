                                  Today "Oktiabrskaya" is an elegant and comfortable hotel with renovated rooms and high level of service suggesting accessible prices for accommodation in the city center. The Hotel consists of two buildings – Oktiabrskiy – 373 rooms and Ligovskiy – 111 rooms.

At your service comfortable rooms for one, two or three persons and suites that satisfy the requirements of luxurious accommodation. All rooms are equipped with telephone, refrigerator, Satellite TV and Internet access. High ceiling and big windows create pleasant atmosphere.
