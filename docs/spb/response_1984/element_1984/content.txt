                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>This year&rsquo;s open seminar has two main themes:</p>

<ul>	<li>Theoretical approaches in and for forest policy research</li>	<li>Science-policy and practice interaction: navigating the forest science-policy interface.</li></ul>
