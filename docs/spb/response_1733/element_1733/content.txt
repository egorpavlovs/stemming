                    The World Forum of Universities of Resources on Sustainability (WFURS) 
                
                
                    
                        
                            28 sep - 1 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Education
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://wfurs2016.spmi.ru/?lang=en_us
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>World Forum for sustainable development mineral universities (WFURS) is a non-profit organization bringing together Universities, whose activity is connected with mineral resources, natural raw materials and the entire technological chain on their receipt and use of: prospecting and exploration, mining, mineral processing, recycling and reuse of waste.</p>

<p>Initiators of the Forum are Petersburg Mining University and Freiberg mining Academy (Germany), and two of his first Conference took place in Norway and Austria.</p>
