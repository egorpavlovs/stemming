                                  Anichkov Palace is a magnificent monument of Russian architecture of XVIII-XIX centuries. It is located in the heart of St. Petersburg, on the corner of Nevsky Prospekt and the Fontanka river.  Anichkov estate opened a theatre and concert hall "Carnival" in 1986. Stage area "Carnival" regularly takes business events, concerts and performances of the best creative teams of Russia and abroad. "Carnival" has everything you need for entertainment: cozy auditorium, technically equipped stage, spacious and light three-tier theatre foyer with a dance floor and a fountain, a spacious wardrobe and a welcoming cafe.
