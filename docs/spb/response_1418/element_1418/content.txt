        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    XIX St. Petersburg International Forestry Forum
                
                
                    
                        
                            3 - 4 oct 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        Crowne Plaza St.Petersburg Airport
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://spiff.ru/en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>THE MAIN TOPICS OF THE FORUM:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Forest industry complex. Growth potential. How to gain profit in terms of political crisis. Overview of government incentives for the industry.</li>	<li>Law on timber recording and timber-related deals. Mechanisms of enforcement of Federal law No. 415 and related problems.</li>	<li>The ways to increase sales. How to grow in terms of crisis.</li>	<li>New forms of trade, special features of online stock exchanges. Tendering and organisation of estate sales by auction.</li>	<li>Improvement of forest state management economic aspects.</li>	<li>Analysis of secondary woodworking industry. Products and growth potential.</li>	<li>Analysis of sectors representing key wood products consumers.</li>	<li>Intensification of production. Automation of business and production processes.</li>	<li>Cases of organisational, technological and programme improvements at enterprises.</li>	<li>Forestry development. Looking into scientific and technological future.</li>	<li>Forest bioenergetics. Potential and development trends of the segment.</li>	<li>Types of fuels: long-term global demand forecast. Political regulation of consumer market. Recent technological advances in biofuel production.</li>	<li>Following the recommendations of the ICFPA Annual Meeting and the 55th Session of ACSFI FAO.</li>	<li>Companies&rsquo; media strategy, marketing, branding, good will and competition for clients.</li>	<li>Timber and board industry market trends. Growth and dynamics forecast. The main constraint of development. OSB vs. plywood: what will dominate the markets?</li>	<li>Equipment. Recent technologies in wood production.</li>	<li>Overview of all technologies and solutions in low-grade wood processing.</li>	<li>Establishment of highly automated logistics integrated with storekeeping, transportation and demand management.</li>	<li>The aspects of receiving financial support, search for investors and partners.</li>	<li>Architecture, construction and design. Recent trends in the use of timber.</li>	<li>End customers&rsquo; needs and review of the woodworking and house-building market.</li></ul>
