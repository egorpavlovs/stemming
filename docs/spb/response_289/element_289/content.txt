                Saint Petersburg convention bureau representatives opened an academic year with lecture in the St. Petersburg State University of Economics (UNECON)
            
            September 9 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                Olga Mugatina, CEO, St. Petersburg Convention bureau, and Elena Rechitc, the Head of Meeting Industry Development Department, St. Petersburg Convention bureau conduct a classes within the course “MICE industry development in Russia and in St. Petersburg” for 3rd graduated UNECON students on September, 2.

Olga Mugatina, CEO, SPbCB, told students about convention bureau activity and targets, industries challenges and prospect development, and about St. Petersburg event industry special aspects. Also Ms. Mugatina initiated students into the profession nuances. Audience was very interested in employment issues and job market demand for event industry specialists, so opportunities for students engagement   were discussed.

Elena Rechitc, Head of Meeting Industry Development Department, SPbCB, conducted interactive lectures. Students were initiated with industry insiders activity: venues, PCO, DMC, service companies within the classes, also a purpose of event calendar was discussed. At the end of the lecture students and lecturer altogether composed a list of St. Petersburg as a destination benefits.

Studies were organized within St. Petersburg Convention bureau and UNECON cooperation.
