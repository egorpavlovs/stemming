                    International exhibition for restaurateurs and hoteliers "EXPOHORECA" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            2 - 5 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://horeca-expo.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>ExpoHoReCa</strong>&nbsp;is&nbsp;the largest specialized exhibition of the hospitality industry in the North-West region of Russia. The purpose of the project is&nbsp;to unite all HoReCa market participants for the promotion and development of business and industry. The exposition presented the full range of goods and services required for quality work and development of enterprises of hotel and restaurant business. All products are adapted to the needs of the regional market.</p>

<p><strong>Sections of the exhibition:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Equipment</li>	<li>Automation</li>	<li>Franchising</li>	<li>Food</li>	<li>Beverages</li>	<li>Tea, coffee</li>	<li>Design and Decor</li>	<li>Professional clothing, textiles</li>	<li>Printing &amp; Publishing</li>	<li>Service</li>	<li>Job fair</li></ul>
