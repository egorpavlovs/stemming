                The intensive education at the Forum of Young Professionals
            
            November 25 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Sales manager of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Olga Petrenko took part at the Forum of Young Professionals in Barcelona on November 15-17. Short but intensive education program was organized by lnternational Congress and Convention Association and IBTM World.

The forum united 35 young industry professionals from a variety of demographics. These were representatives of convention bureaus, professional associations, PCO, venues, transport companies. This composition showing the model of meeting industry allowed participants to understand the specifics of each other's work and to plan effective schemes of the cooperation. Young professionals studied all three days of the forum until 10 p.m. The education program included a variety of topics, from future technologies of the industry to the tools for the organization of FAM trips. Respected speakers tried to inspire new ideas and nonstandard work handling by their speeches. Chief Executive Officer of ICCA Martin Sirk and Director of Vienna Convention Bureau Christian Mutschlechner were among them.

The practical work in small groups was the final stage of the program. The task sounded as the organization of FAM trip. The team with Olga Petrenko supported her idea and chose Saint Petersburg as the destination. The projects were submitted for approval of speakers. They evaluated the presentations not strictly because the friendship wins always in such occasions.
