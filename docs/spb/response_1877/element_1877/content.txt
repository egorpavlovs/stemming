                    The 12th International exhibition "Customs Office of North-West: FOREIGN TRADE, transport, logistics"
                
                
                    
                        
                            24 - 25 may 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Transport, Shipment, Warehouse, and Logistics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.ruscustomsinform.ru/piter2015.html

Screening tools.&nbsp;<br />&bull; Air, road, rail, sea and river transport. Logistics.&nbsp;<br />&bull; Freight forwarding services. Customs-logistic terminals.&nbsp;<br />&bull; Ports.
