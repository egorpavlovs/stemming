                                  Multifunctional contemporary cluster "Olympia" Palace is located in the mansion of Princess Varvara Dolgorukaya in the historic center of St.Petersburg at Liteiniy prospekt 14 in 7 minute walk from Nevsky Prospekt and Chernyshevskaya underground station. The history of this magnificent late classical style palace constructed by architect A.Nizovcev begins in the 40's of the XIX century and connected with the names of Princes Golitsyn, Dolgorukov, Shakhovskys, Count Nesselrode, and other well-known residents of St. Petersburg. Nowadays historical halls of the palace are supplemented with modern lighting and sound equipment, as well as the unique LED screen with HD-quality 6x4 m screen with 3 mm pixel pitch, that is located in the great hall of the Palace - Congress Hall
