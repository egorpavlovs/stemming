                                  Red Stars Hotel St Petersburg is a design hotel in the historical centre of St Petersburg, Russia. Smart and stylish, the Hotel combines innovative technical solutions with inspiring contemporary design.There are 54 guest rooms from Single to Suite, European restaurant, ethnic bar and business facilities. The Hotel is perfect both for business and leisure.Get a truly individual experience and enjoy: convenient central location, within few-minute walk from the famous Mariinsky Theatre and 10-minute drive to main tourist and business attractions of the city centre and Nevsky Prospect; 54 guest rooms featuring air-conditioners, high speed Internet access (LAN and Wi-Fi),state-of-the-art in-room LED TVs with satellite channels, direct-dial phones and mini-bars; business and conference facilities including free Wi-Fi Internet access in all guest areas, PC workstations, facsimile/copier/printer service; fully equipped meeting room (capacity of 6) and conference room for up to 25 persons are available, restaurant and ethnic bar, halfboard and fullboard menu; 24/7 concierge desk offering transportation, excursions and private guides, city maps and updated information on city's major events; English-speaking staff delivering personalized service and comfort.
