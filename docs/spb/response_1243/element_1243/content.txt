                Russian-Chinese wholesale trade will be discussed at the forum «Business with China»
            
            December 2 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

VI Russian-Chinese forum «Business with China» 2015 will be held in Lenexpo Exhibition Complex on December 9-10. It is one the most important event in the field of the business communication between Russia and China, where more than 2 500 people cooperate every year.

The experts will discuss the problems of Russian-Chinese wholesale trade and come forward with their practical solutions. The questions of cross-border trade and e-commerce are basic subjects of the Forum`s congress section. The particular way of the Forum is the exchange of the wholesale purchases. It includes three types of goods: electronics, materials for home & repair, clothing & shoes. The audience of the Forum is importers, exporters, online-retailers and service-providers.

The forum «Business with China» 2015 will be held with the support of the China Business Center that was opened in Saint Petersburg one year ago.
