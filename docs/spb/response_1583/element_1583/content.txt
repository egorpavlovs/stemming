                    International specialised exhibition "Mechanical Engineering" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.ptfair.ru/exhibition/mechanical-engineering/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Each year <strong>the Mechanical Engineering exhibition</strong> gathers representatives of Russia&rsquo;s and foreign industry &ndash; as well as specialists professionally engaged in the real sector of Russia&rsquo;s economy. The biggest enterprises and leading market players regularly participate in the exhibition, presenting equipment and products for machine-tool building; instrumentation industry; automobile construction; metallurgy, chemical, and aircraft industries; transport, energy, oil-and-gas mechanical engineering; shipbuilding and metalworking.</p>

<p>The exhibition is part of the<strong> Petersburg Technical Fair.</strong></p>

<p><strong>Mechanical Engineering sections:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Drives</li>	<li>Bearings</li>	<li>Engines</li>	<li>Pneumatics</li>	<li>Hydraulics</li>	<li>Tools</li>	<li>Oils and lubricants</li></ul>
