                    Xth annual conference «Vreden's Readings»
                
                
                    
                        
                            22 - 24 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        Holliday Inn and Staybridge Moskovskie vorota
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://vredenreadings.org/eng/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The organizer of the annual scientific conference &quot;Vreden&#39;s&nbsp;reading&quot; is the Russian Research Institute of Traumatology and Orthopedics named after R.R. Vreden, one of Russia&#39;s largest clinical, research and educational institutions with more than 100-year history.&nbsp;The idea of the scientific-practical conference &quot;Vreden&#39;s reading&quot;&nbsp;was born in 2006 during the celebration of the anniversary of the institute.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Since 2007, the conference is held annually and is named after the first director of the Institute for Roman Romanovich Vreden. It is attended by leading experts not only from Russia and the CIS countries, but also by foreign specialists as well.</p>

<p>In addition to exploring new technologies and advances in the treatment of multiple injuries and diseases of the musculoskeletal system, the conference is educational in nature, where young orthopedic specialists have a chance to learn and participate.&nbsp;</p>
