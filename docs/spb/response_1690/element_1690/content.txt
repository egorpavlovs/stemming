                     Exhibition of small and medium business in St. Petersburg within the framework of the Forum of small and medium business of Saint-Petersburg 2016
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 15 dec 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Business
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://mb.expoforum.ru/main
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The exhibition is held annually within the framework of the <strong>Forum </strong>of small and medium&nbsp;businesses in St. Petersburg in order to provide the capacity of small and medium&nbsp;enterprises in the real sector of the economy of the city. This is an opportunity to discuss topical issues of entrepreneurial activity, and identify ways to address them in the format of a constructive dialogue between the authorities, business and infrastructure, support for small and medium&nbsp;businesses.</p>
