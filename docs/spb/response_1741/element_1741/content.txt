                Eco-style of Green Saint Petersburg
            
            July 1 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The eco-festival Green Saint Petersburg was held on June 27-28 at the Park of 300th anniversary. The project was dedicated to environmental friendliness, healthy living, saving of cultural traditions, creative works.

The yoga marathon, the eco-quest, the photo flashmob, eco-film shows, exhibitions of paintings, master classes on restoration of clothes and creation of grass gobelins were organized for visitors of the event.  ‪

Architect Maria Luisa Palumbo gave the lecture about Italian experience of regional sustainable development which allowed to stop the consuming of new grounds, to work at exist terrains and to revive crafts there. Finnish expert in the matter of ecology Stephanie C.Lefrere spoke about Arctic ecological development. Participants of the festivals discussed about eco-tourism and separate waste collection too. Organizers of the project Clean City proposed to clean up parks and streets in the quest format. Representatives of worldwide movement We Do! uniting over 12 countries and about 11 millions of volunteers spoke about achievements of unsanctioned landfill removal.
