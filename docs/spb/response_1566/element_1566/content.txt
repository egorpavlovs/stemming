                    International specialised exhibition "The Metalworking"  
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.ptfair.ru/exhibition/metalworking/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The Metalworking exhibition</strong> showcases the leading Russian and world manufacturers and distributors of machine-tools and press-and-forge equipment, who present the latest novelties of their produce and technologies for metalworking industry. The metalworking exhibition is the biggest project of the St. Petersburg Technical Fair, devoted to the most important sector of industrial production &ndash; i.e. metalworking.</p>

<p>The exhibition is part of the <strong>Petersburg Technical Fair</strong>.</p>

<p><strong>Metalworking sections:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Primary mechanical treatment of metals</li>	<li>Sheet metal, pipes and metal profiles (black and non-ferrous metal)</li>	<li>Technologies and equipment for metal surface treatment</li>	<li>Forge-and-press machinery, bending production</li>	<li>Laser equipment and technologies</li>	<li>Machinery and technologies for high performance metal treatment</li>	<li>Component parts and production accessories</li>	<li>Press-forging equipment</li>	<li>Tools for industrial production</li>	<li>Welding</li>	<li>End-products, fittings</li>	<li>Renewed equipment</li></ul>
