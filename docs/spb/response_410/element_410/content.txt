                                  Okhtinskaya is one of the largest hotels in St. Petersburg. The hotel is located on the bank of the Neva River opposite Smolny Cathedral. The magnificent panoramic view of the Neva, the ensemble of the Smolny Cathedral and three bridges offers from the observation deck, the rooms and halls of the hotel.
