                    V International Conference "Russian Shipbuilding. Global Trends". The purpose of the conference is to analyze the current state and main directions of development of Russian shipbuilding, state and business activities to create a competitive image of the Russian shipbuilding industry. The conference will be attended by profile ministries and departments; Russian shipbuilding and shiprepairing enterprises, including the river profile; shipping companies; design bureaus and scientific organizations; classification societies; Russian and foreign suppliers of marine components.
