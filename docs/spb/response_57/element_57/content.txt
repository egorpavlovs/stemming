                                  EXPOFORUM Convention and Exhibition Centre is located in St. Petersburg, the most European city in Russia and one of the most beautiful in the world. The venue facilities include 3 exhibition halls, a modern congress centre with 30 rooms, 2 on-site hotels (Hilton and Hampton by Hilton), customs and logistics terminal.The venue is the versatile and flexible space which can be adopted for hosting events of any size and complexity: conventions (up to 8 000 pax), exhibitions (up to 50 000 sq. m gross), banquets (up to 6 500 pax), concerts (up to 10 000 pax), sport events (up to 5 000 pax)
                                

                                

                                
                                    
                                        
                                            Total exhibition area, sq.m.
                                            90 000
                                        
                                    
                                    
                                        
                                            Total capacity, pers.
                                            10 000
                                        
                                    
                                    
                                        
                                            Number of meeting rooms
                                            45
                                        
                                    

                                    
                                        
                                        
                                            Distance from airport, km.
                                            7
                                        
                                    

                                    
                                        
                                            Distance from Moskovskiy railway station, km.
                                            20

                                            Parking
                                            
                                                Free parking
