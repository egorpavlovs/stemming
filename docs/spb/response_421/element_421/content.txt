                                  The Rossiya Hotel is one of the best 3 star conference and business hotels in Saint Petersburg. A 10-storey building which can accommodate 700 guests at the time. Professionally trained staff, individual attention to each guest, comfort and friendliness – these signs has become the visit-card of the hotel "Russia". According to its functional purpose the hotel successfully combines the three areas.The hotel has a unique location in the prestigious Moskovsky district of Saint Petersburg, close to the airports Pulkovo, railway stations and the historical part of the city. There is a Petersburg’s Sport-and-Concert Complex 10 minutes walk from the hotel. It is the traditional place for conducting international and domestic exhibitions and entertaining events.
