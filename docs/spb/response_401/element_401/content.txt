                                  “Baltic Star” business hotel is located on the picturesque coast of the Gulf of Finland in the park area of St. Petersburg. It is an integral part of the State Complex “The National Congress palace”. We offer President suite for the true luxury admirers. “Baltic Star” business hotel possesses a wide spectrum of facilities for holding conferences and events of any level. The cottages of the “Consular village” will fascinate accommodation in 106 modern, comfortable and cozy rooms of different categories. All the rooms are equipped with the electronic key card system and fire protection system. We are glad to offer you with their design.
