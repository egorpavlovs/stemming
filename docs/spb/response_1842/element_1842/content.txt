                    3rd International Eastern European Conference "Interdisciplinary aspects of diagnosis and treatment of craniomandibular dysfunctions"
                
                
                    
                        
                            10 - 11 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        Park Inn by Radisson Pulkovskaya
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.dtcd.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The 3rd International Eastern European Conference &quot;Interdisciplinary aspects of diagnosis and treatment of craniomandibular dysfunctions&quot;</strong>&nbsp;will be held on September 10-11th 2016 in St. Petersburg.&nbsp;</p>

<p>&nbsp;The conference will provide a series of master classes on the most urgent topics. The recognized lecturers from around the world (Germany, USA, Italy, Lithuania, China , Russia) will take a speech on the conference with reports and presentations on the most relevant topics in the field of KMD.</p>

<p>The conference has already received a lot of attention from the guests, and we look forward to participation of 350-500 people from among dentists-general practitioners and specialists in the field of implantology, orthodontics, periodontics, orthopedics, dental surgery, oral surgeons, dental technicians and specialists of general medicine (otolaryngologists, neurologists, osteopaths, physiotherapists, psychologists).</p>
