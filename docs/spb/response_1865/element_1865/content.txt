                    VIII International Petrine congress
                
                
                    
                        
                            6 - 8 jun 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://ipv.spb.ru/chronicle/207.html
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The Congress will discuss the following topics:</strong><br />&bull; Monuments and memorials associated with the name and activity of Peter I and his associates: the history of origin, localization, fate<br />&bull; The architecture of the time of Peter: ensembles and monuments, the problem of their study, the current state<br />&bull; Monuments and memorials military history of Peter the Great: the battlefields, forts and monuments<br />&bull; Necropolis of Peter&#39;s time<br />&bull; Archaeological sites Petrine era<br />&bull; Problems of preservation, restoration of monuments of Peter museumification<br />&bull; Museums and Collections Peter&#39;s artifacts as a place of memory of the Petrine era<br />&bull; Peter monuments in the visual arts<br />&bull; Peter&#39;s place names - official and folk<br />&bull; &laquo;Petrovsky myth&quot; and its reflection in the monuments of Russia and Europe<br />&bull; New Peter monuments: the phenomenon of emergence, customers and performers<br />&bull; Peter monuments and cultural tourism: routes, guides, electronic information</p>
