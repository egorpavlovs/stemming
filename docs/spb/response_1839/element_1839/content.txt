        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    13th European VLBI Network Symposium and Users Meeting (13 European VLBI Network Symposium 
                
                
                    
                        
                            20 - 23 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.ipa.nw.ru/EVN2016/index.html
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <div class="mytext" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-size: 14.6667px; text-align: justify; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-family: 'Open Sans', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; line-height: normal;">The Institute of Applied Astronomy of the Russian Academy of Sciences, on behalf of the European VLBI Network Consortium Board of Directors, will host the&nbsp;<strong>13th&nbsp;European VLBI Network Symposium</strong>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<strong>Users Meeting</strong>&nbsp;on&nbsp;<strong><strong>September</strong>&nbsp;20-23,&nbsp;2016</strong>. The Symposium will be held at the&nbsp;<strong>St. Petersburg Scientific Center</strong>, located in the heart of the city.</div>

<div class="mytext" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-size: 14.6667px; text-align: justify; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-family: 'Open Sans', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; line-height: normal;">The latest scientific results and technical developments on Very Long Baseline Interferometry with the EVN and related facilities around the word will be reported and discussed. Scientific sessions will include the life cycle of matter in stars and galaxies, AGN and cosmic star-formation, Extreme Astrophysics, Astrometry and Geodesy, planetary and space science, as well as techniques and developments. The programme will also include the EVN Users Meeting, which is an important forum for interaction between the EVN users and the EVN organization.</div>

<div class="mytext" style="margin-top: 20px; margin-bottom: 0px; font-size: 14.6667px; text-align: justify; color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-family: 'Open Sans', Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; line-height: normal;">The Scientific Organizing Committee invites all scientists who have interests in the various research fields of VLBI and in related fields to participate in the Symposium, submit contributions to the topical sessions and share their research with colleagues and friends. We are looking forward to welcome you in St. Petersburg!</div>
