                    The 6th St. Petersburg International Gas Forum (SPIGF-2016)
                
                
                    
                        
                            4 - 7 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Energy. Energy efficiency
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://gas-forum.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Gas Forum</strong> is the leading venue to discuss topical issues of the industry. During the Forum industry leaders make their decisions that provide direct impact on formation of the global gas market.</p>

<p>Among Forum participants - top managers of large world leaders on the oil and gas market, representatives of the innovation centers and design institutes, industry experts, academicians, heads of specialised universities and research institutions.</p>

<p><strong>Key subjects:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Global tendencies and state policy in the gas industry;</li>	<li>Resource base for the largest gas projects and key aspects of their realisation;</li>	<li>Evolution of the role of the liquefied natural gas and its share in the future global energy balance;</li>	<li>Gas engine fuel as the factor of economical efficiency of freight traffic;</li>	<li>Gas chemistry as the catalyst of the innovative development;</li>	<li>Service and infrastructure for the gas industry;</li>	<li>Energy-efficient technologies in the gas industrty.</li></ul>
