                    10th Specialized Exhibition "Industrial Electrotechnics And Driving Acuators"
                
                
                    
                        
                            21 - 23 nov 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.en.farexpo.ru/pet/exhibition/about/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Industrial Electrotechnics Exhibition</strong>&nbsp;has been held since 2008. By its topic and exposition the event is closely related to the well-known exhibition automation. Since the year 2014 the exhibition profile has been extended by adding Drive mechanisms section.</p>

<p><strong>Purpose of the exhibition</strong> is to help along establishing efficient business contacts between customers and producers of novelties in the field of industrial electrotechnics and drive mechanisms.</p>

<p><strong>Exhibition Profile</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>	<p><strong>Factory-assembled controls, switchgears, protective appliances&nbsp;</strong><br />	High-voltage equipment&nbsp;<br />	Low-voltage installations&nbsp;<br />	Boxes, consoles, boards</p>	</li>	<li>	<p><strong>Electric machines and power supplies&nbsp;</strong><br />	Generators&nbsp;<br />	Electric motors&nbsp;<br />	No-break and uninterraptible power supplies&nbsp;<br />	Accumulators&nbsp;<br />	Converter equipment</p>	</li>	<li>	<p><strong>Drive Mechanisms</strong>&nbsp;<br />	Electric motor drives&nbsp;<br />	Stepping motors&nbsp;<br />	Mini- and micro- motors&nbsp;<br />	Mechanical transmission elements&nbsp;<br />	Allhydraulic drives - systems, blocks, elements&nbsp;<br />	Pneumatic actuators - systems, blocks, elements&nbsp;<br />	Automated process control systems with the use of electric, pneumatic and hydraulic&nbsp;<br />	Software&nbsp;<br />	Design, assembly, starting-up and adjustmentа, maintenance and service</p>	</li>	<li>	<p><strong>Industrial lighting facilities&nbsp;</strong><br />	Lamps for fabrication facilities&nbsp;<br />	Explosion-proof luminaire&nbsp;<br />	Lamps for lighting working places and devices&nbsp;<br />	Light display panels and indicators&nbsp;<br />	LED</p>	</li>	<li>	<p><strong>Electrotechnical components and materials, instrument&nbsp;</strong><br />	Switching, connecting, protective components&nbsp;<br />	Wiring products&nbsp;<br />	Solid-state power devices&nbsp;<br />	Transformers&nbsp;<br />	Cables, wires, cable fittings&nbsp;<br />	Electrotechnical materials&nbsp;<br />	Wiring equipment and instruments&nbsp;<br />	Personel protection facilities</p>	</li>	<li>	<p><strong>ACS and automation means in electrical facility of enterprises&nbsp;</strong><br />	ACS power supply, lighting and electric installations of enterprises&nbsp;<br />	Systems of energy accounting and quality control in enterprises&nbsp;<br />	Electrical parameters and automation measuring instruments and means</p>	</li>	<li>	<p><strong>CAD electrotechnical systems</strong></p>	</li></ul>
