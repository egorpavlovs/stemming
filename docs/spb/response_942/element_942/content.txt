                The meeting schedule of Convention Bureau at Convene is packed by 95 per cent 
            
            February 4 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau will participate in Baltic Sea Region Exhibition for Meetings, Events and Incentives. Convene will be held in Vilnius on February 10-11.

The exhibition is organized by Vilnius Convention Bureau. Convene is an annual business to business two day event for the meetings industry for the countries of the Baltic. It is an international showcase for the regional meetings industry, which accelerates the interest in, and demand for, holding meetings, incentives and conferences in the region. Convene will unite up to 100 exhibitors including convention bureaus, hotels, conference centers, special venues, DMCs, PCOs, specialist suppliers from Lithuania and seven other countries of the region such as Latvia, Estonia, Poland, Russia, Finland, Sweden, Denmark. 

The key principle of the event sounds as «a customer chooses a supplier». Over 160 companies of corporate sector, professional associations, organizers of incentive tours participate in the Hosted Buyers Program this year. They can use online service to pre schedule meetings with suppliers. Hosted buyers are selected according to the qualification criteria such as number and size of purchased meetings, events, incentives, conferences, geographical representation, potential for future business in the Baltic Sea region meetings industry. 2-days meeting schedule with the representatives of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has already been packed by 95 per cent.  Great Britain, France, Belgium, Poland, the Netherlands, Denmark, Austria, Germany, Israel, Australia were among countries which showed interest in Saint Petersburg.

The educational program is relevant for all those involved in creating, planning and managing meetings, events and conferences as well as professionals in the advertising, marketing and communication fields. High-profile speakers from across Europe shape their knowledge-saturated sessions with a strong focus on the best practice examples in conferences and events management, latest trends and research, marketing and sales.
