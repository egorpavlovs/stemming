                                  Shopping Mall "LETO" is a special atmosphere of shopping and entertainment in the South of St. Petersburg. On two floors are occupied by more than 170 shops: grocery hypermarket "AUCHAN" hypermarket of electronics and home appliances Media Markt, the Luxor cinema, hypermarket Decathlon sporting goods, a spacious food court. The skating rink.

Here are all the most fashionable brands that are present in the Northern Capital, including the city's first sports hypermarket Decathlon.

Shopping Mall "LETO" is located at Pulkovo highway. It is easily accessible and personal, and public transport. Free Shuttle buses run every day from the metro station "Leninsky prospect", "Victory Park", "Kupchino", "Prospekt VETERANOV", "Leninsky prospect "Avtovo" and Pushkin, Kolpino.

It offers motorists - free surface Parking for 3,200 cars, convenient interchanges and entrances.
