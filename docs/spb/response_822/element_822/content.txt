                    ST.PETERSBURG PALACES  INTERNATIONAL MUSIC FESTIVAL
                
                
                    
                        
                            23 may - 1 jul 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Culture and art
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.palacefest.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    St.Petersburg Palaces  International Music Festival  is the unique holiday of «classics among classics». It has been a trademark of the great city for a long time.

The Festival has his own rich history and purely St.Petersburg exclusive idea.Thanks to the Festival projects now it's possible to listen to the music at the  Yusupov and Menshikov Palaces, The Hall of Mirrors of the Sheremetev Palace, Rotunda of the Mariinsky Palace, "Hall on the Island" Pavilion  and The Alexander Palace in the Tsarskoe Selo.
