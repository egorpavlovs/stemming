                                  Hotel "Voyage" in St. Petersburg is located in the south of the city on Pulkovsky Highway 107, in Moskovsky district. The hotel is ideal for those traveling by car, because next to the building there is free guarded parking for buses and cars. There is a cafe-restaurant on the second floor of the old building of the Voyage hotel. The space of the cafe is divided into two rooms: a large capacity of 80 seats and a small one for 12 people. The large hall is perfect for celebrations - weddings, anniversaries and corporate parties. In the small hall it is possible to organize family and children's parties, negotiations and business meetings. For tourist groups developed a special set menu. From morning to late evening, room service is available.
