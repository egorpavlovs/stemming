                    White Nights. Petersburg Cancer Forum
                
                
                    
                        
                            22 - 24 jun 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://forum-onco.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The Forum provides a platform for the presentation and discussion of modern approaches to diagnosis, surgical, drug and radiation treatment of patients with cancer of various localizations.</p>

<p>The Forum held workshops on endovideosurgical technologies in oncology from leading Russian and foreign experts, cycle training (24 training hours) on the theme &quot;Actual problems of diagnosis and treatment of cancer&quot; with the issuance of state certificates.</p>

<p>The forum is supported by the European Society for Radiotherapy and Oncology (ESTRO).</p>
