                    52nd World Congress of the International Federation of Landscape Architects (IFLA)
                
                
                    
                        
                            10 - 12 jun 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Agriculture
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://ifla2015.com/ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>IFLA 2015, <strong>52nd World Congress of the International Federation of Landscape Architects</strong> is regarded as one of the major event on Landscape, Landscaping, Architecture, Ekistics, Sustainable Development and Landscape Design practices.</p>

<p>52nd World Congress of the International Federation of Landscape Architects is organized annually.</p>

<p>Principal theme of 52nd World Congress is &quot;History of the Future&quot;.</p>

<p><strong>&quot;History of the Future&quot;</strong>&nbsp;caters for a wide range of interests on the challenges and opportunities of the past, the present and the future for landscape architecture and landscape architects:&nbsp;</p>

<p>1. East to West: methods of integration and innovations in modern landscape architecture</p>

<p>2. Historical and &lsquo;Natural&rsquo; Landscapes in the 21st century - conservation, reconstruction and restoration; research for integration into modern urban and rural landscape&nbsp;</p>

<p>3. Green-blue infrastructure and sustainable urban development</p>
