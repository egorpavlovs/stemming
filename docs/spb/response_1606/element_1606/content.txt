                The parade of ships at the International Maritime Defence Show
            
            July 6 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The Seventh International Maritime Defence Show was held on July 1-5 in Saint Petersburg. The event was organized by the Ministry of Industry and Trade of the Russian Federation with the participation of the Ministry of Defence, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Federal Service of Military-Technical Cooperation, the Government of Saint-Petersburg and JSC Rosoboronexport.

The exhibits profile included naval and commercial shipbuilding, arms and weapons, complexes of naval armament and weapon, navigation, communication and control, power plants, naval aviation, location and logistic infrastructure, new materials and technologies. Significant areas of the exposition were occupied by the companies which supply marine hardware and materials, devices and electronics, information technologies, consulting, financial and insurance services in the field of design, shipbuilding and development of armament.

The show united in one area exposition of military products, demonstration of armaments and naval equipment, congress and business events, visit to defence industry enterprises and VIP negotiations.

Over 400 participants including 40 foreign ones were registered. The exposition occupied pavilions of Lenexpo Exhibition Complex on total area more than 17000 sq. m., outdoor exposition area and water area of the Gulf of Finland and also near the quays of the Sea Terminal. The demonstration part presented over 30 ships and boats. As traditionally aerobatic teams Russkiye Vityazi, Strizhy and Rus took display flights. 10 naval artillery and small-arms weapon were demonstrated in their action for official foreign delegations. Under support of the sailing union of Saint Petersburg there was a traditional prize sailing regatta of IMDS-2015.
