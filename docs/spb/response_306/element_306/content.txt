                Saint Petersburg prepares to European Mobility Week
            
            July 6 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                EuropeanMobilityWeek will be held in Saint Petersburg on the 16th-22nd of September. The week culminates in the ‘Car-Free Day’, when participants change their cars in favor of ecofriendly transport, cycles and public transport for the whole day.

First “Car-Free Day” took place in Toledo (Spain) in 1994, and in 2000 European Commission expended this campaign on whole Europian Union and “Car-free Day” was reformed into the “European Mobility Week”. Saint Petersburg’s first participation was in 2015.

The aim of European Mobility Week is to encourage people to choose more sustainable travel modes, such as cycling, walking, public transport, and to promote communities with walking distance to home and place of work.

EuropeanMobilityWeek’s events:


	Bicycle rental
	Charity event
	“Take over” of trolley bus
	Educational and craft areas
	Advanced transport fest
	Show and many other interesting events.
