                Saint Petersburg is Europe’s Leading Destination according to World Travel Awards
            
            September 5 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                Saint Petersburg has been recognised as Europe’s Leading Destination for the second year in succession by the World Travel Awards, with Saint Petersburg Committee for Tourism Development Chairman, Andrey Mushkarev, collecting the trophy at a glittering ceremony in Italy on September, 2.  The Venice of the North has won the tourist Oscar for the second consecutive year.

World Travel Awards are considered as the top achievement in the tourism sphere. Annually prizes are given after thousands of experts and tourists voted for a candidate. The selection priorities are safety, extensive infrastructure and hotel chain in destination. 

“Voters recognized Saint Petersburg as the best continent destination, welcoming foreign tourists all over the world”, – Graham Cooke commented.

The Chairman of Saint Petersburg Committee for Tourism Development Andrey Mushkarev represented Saint Petersburg on the Ceremony, he said: “Such authority award is an evidence that tourism is at the high level of development in Saint Petersburg. We are proud of the acknowledgement and of the fact that Saint Petersburg is not the only tourist capital of Russia, but entire Europe”.

Alla Manilova, Deputy Minister of Culture of the Russian Federation, commented the Award: “Saint Petersburg is a tourist pearl with rich historical and cultural heritage; it might be envied by many leading destinations”.

Sergei Korneev, Deputy Chairman, Federal Agency for Tourism – “Saint Petersburg is worth of this Award. It is the result of great work, made by the City Government for the city promotion in the world.  Saint Petersburg is the most active participant of Visit Russia projects and it always presents itself within the project. In my opinion, Saint Petersburg active promotion with Visit Russia national offices contributed to this success.
