                                  Hotel "Dynasty" is situated in a restored 19th Century mansion at 29 Rubinshteyna Street, 7 minutes’ walk from Nevsky Prospect and 2 minutes from Dostoevskaya and Vladimirskaya metro stations.

The street acquired its present-day name in 1929 in honor of the renowned composer and pianist, founder, director and professor of the St. Petersburg Conservatory, Anton Grigoryevich Rubinshteyn (1829-1894), who used to live in the street. 

Hotel "Dynasty" opened its doors in 2007 , quickly earning its reputation as a comfortable and hospitable hotel. The hotel’s name speaks for itself. The styling of the hotel’s interior features the use of the heraldic symbols of the noble houses of the Russian Empire and Europe.

The hotel has 62 rooms.Awards: 2013 TripAdvisor quality certificate.
