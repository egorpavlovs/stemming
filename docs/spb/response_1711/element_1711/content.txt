                    International exhibition "Transtech"
                
                
                    
                        
                            4 - 6 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Transport, Shipment, Warehouse, and Logistics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://dolphin-uk.cergis.com/home/transtec/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The exhibition &quot;Transtech&quot;</strong>, which was created in 1994 with the support of the Ministry of Transport of the Russian Federation, the Russian Academy of Sciences, is traditionally held with the active participation of JSC &quot;Russian Railways&quot;, FSUE &quot;Rosmorport&quot;, the Government of St. Petersburg and a number of coastal regions of Russia, trade unions and associations of transport .</p>

<p><strong>Exhibition:</strong></p>

<p>- Russian Port complex as part of the international port and transport sectors;<br />Development of infrastructure and superstructure of marine and estuarine ports. Port and hydraulic construction, port equipment and systems;<br />- Public-Private partnership (PPP) projects in the port, the prospects for the creation special economic zones in ports;<br />- Interaction of Ports with adjacent modes of transport;<br />- Reform of the internal waterways management in Russia and its influence on the development of the port sector and water transport modes;<br />- Private Investment program of modernization of existing and development of new ports;<br />- Creating container hubs in the sea, river estuaries and ports;<br />- Interaction of Ports with national and international administrative structures and associations in the field of transport (port and shipping associations);<br />- The Development of ports in order to support the development of offshore oil and gas fields in the Arctic, the Far East and the Caspian region;<br />- The Development of ports in order to support cargo operations on the &quot;Northern Sea Route&quot; and the organization of smooth supply of hydrocarbons produced in offshore fields;<br />- European Policy in establishing dry ports and outside harbors - opportunity to use the experience in Russia;<br />- International standards and technical regulations in marine activities, the use in the Russian transport industry;</p>
