                                  Mini-hotel "Accent" is located in a 7-minute walk from the metro station "Frunzenskaya", and in 15-minute drive from the center of St. Petersburg and Moscow railway station.Apart from inexpensive accommodation in comfortable rooms equipped with everything needed for a comfortable stay? the hotel "Accent" offers the rent of the conference room for up to 22 people. There are Wi-Fi, TV with hdmi port, whiteboard, projector screen, the possibility of organizing coffee breaks and a lot more if you wish!
