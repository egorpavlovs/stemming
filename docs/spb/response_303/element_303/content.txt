                Saint Petersburg convention bureau representatives discussed challenges and opportunities for the meetings industry at ICCA CEC Summer Meeting.
            
            August 23 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                Summer Meeting of the ICCA Central European Chapter was held in Moscow on August, 18-20. A traditional meeting of the ICCA Central European Chapter is an important communication platform for professionals of the meetings industry from Russia, Austria, Germany, Switzerland, Poland, and other Europe countries.

The First ICCA CEC Summer meeting in Russia took place in Saint Petersburg in 2013, and this year Moscow hosted the meeting for the biggest ICCA Chapter.

68 delegates took part in the Meeting, including Saint Petersburg convention bureau representatives Olga Mugatina and Elena Yazeva. The key topic was “From the New Normal to the New Future. Challenges and opportunities for the European Meeting Industry now”.

The topics include:


	Forecast for the New future. New horizons: artificial intelligence and future technologies
	New marketing strategies
	Smart technologies
	European models working in Russia. Specifics and peculiarities of working in Russia.
	Welcoming Culture Today: how to bring event to the city


“ICCA CEC Summer Meeting is not only educational conference but also an important networking event. This is why we made sure that the participants would have many opportunities to establish new business relations," - says Anna Górska, ICCA Central European Chapter Chairperson.
