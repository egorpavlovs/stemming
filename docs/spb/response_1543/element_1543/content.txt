                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Other

                    <p>CleanExpo St. Petersburg is the only exhibition of equipment and materials for professional cleaning, sanitation, hygiene, dry-cleaning and laundry in Northwest Russia.</p>

<p>CleanExpo St. Petersburg exhibition is an effective tool to attract new trade partners and allow a direct contact with representatives of the cleaning companies, enterprises of dry-cleaning, laundry and end-users in the region.</p>
