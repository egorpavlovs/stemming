                    31st Edition Fetus as a Patient
                
                
                    
                        
                            11 - 13 jun 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://fetus2015.eu/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Our international society of the fetus as a patient is a brotherhood and sisterhood of world leaders in perinatal medicine dedicated&nbsp;to improving all aspects of care to the pregnant, fetal,and neonatal patients. We are delighted that the most beautiful city of Saint Petersburg&nbsp;welcomes us to an intellectual festival of dynamic interchanges which will enable all who participate to return home better equipped to care for&nbsp;the patients they serve throughout the world. The spectacular &ldquo;white nights&rdquo; of Saint Petersburg is an appropriate symbol &nbsp;of the enlightenment that will transpire during the congress. Our society is grateful to Anton &nbsp;Mikhailov and his team for their warm Russian hospitality&nbsp;which &nbsp;brings &nbsp;speakers and participants together in such a beautiful and friendly environment.</p>

<div style="text-align: justify; font-family: Verdana;">Frank A. Chervenak MD</div>

<p>President, International Society of the Fetus as a Patient<br />Asim Kurjak, MD<br />Founding President, International Society of the Fetus as a Patient</p>
