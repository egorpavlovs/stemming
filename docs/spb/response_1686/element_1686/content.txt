                    2nd International exhibition of transport, logistics and customs services, technical equipment and warehouse technologies. TransBaltic
                
                
                    
                        
                            30 nov - 2 dec 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Transport, Shipment, Warehouse, and Logistics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.transbaltic-expo.ru/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>TransBaltic</strong>&nbsp;is the only one&nbsp;in the North-West of Russia International&nbsp;exhibition, which presents&nbsp;transportation, logistic and customs services,&nbsp;technical means, warehouse technologies at one sight.&nbsp;</p>

<p>TransBaltic enters the portfolio of transport exhibitions of ITE Group alongside with TransRussia (Moscow), TransSiberia (Novosibirsk), TransUkraine (Kiev), TransCaspian (Baku), TransUzbekistan (Tashkent), TransitKazakhstan (Astana), TransAsia (Singapore), TransPoland (Warsaw).&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Exhibition profile:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Transport and logistic services</li>	<li>Warehouse technologies, cargo handling&nbsp;and in-plant logistics&nbsp;</li>	<li>Transport and technical vehicles</li>	<li>Information technologies</li>	<li>Customs services, insurance and leasing&nbsp;</li></ul>
