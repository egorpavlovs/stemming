        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    Frontiers of 21st Century Physics and Ioffe Institute
                
                
                    
                        
                            29 oct - 1 nov 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.ioffe.ru/ioffe100/index.html
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    This year Ioffe Institute - the world famous research center in physics and technology - celebrates 100-year anniversary of its foundation in 1918 under guidance of Abram Ioffe. One of the main events of the celebration is the International Multidisciplinary Conference “Frontiers of 21st Century Physics and Ioffe Institute” which is organized by the Institute from October 29 to November 1, 2018, in Saint-Petersburg.Conference Program includes Invited talks of about 50 prominent scientists from Russia and abroad and about 30 poster presentations of young researches working in Ioffe Institute, which cover major fields of the Institute research activity:plasma physics, astrophysics, solid state physics, physics of semiconductors, excitons and spintronics, nanoheterostructure technology, semiconductor photonics and electronics, energy conversion and storage, novel materials and carbon nanostructures, biophysics and life sciences.
