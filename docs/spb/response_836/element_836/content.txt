                    Conference «The Status of Historical Gardens and Parks as Museums»
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 17 nov 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Culture and art
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://gatchinapalace.ru/en/events/other/3105/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    The Gatchina Palace and Estate Museum is hosting an applied research conference on The Status of Historical Gardens and Parks as Museums on 14 -17 November 2018 as part of its Imperial Gatchina series. The event will commemorate the 100th anniversary of country palace museums outside of St Petersburg and will be held within the framework of the VII St Petersburg International Cultural Forum.The conference invites you to engage in discussions on a wide range of issues around the main topic: 

1. Heritage development of historic, cultural and natural sites 2. Public and private gardens: Transformation through the centuries 3. Garden designers, gardeners and researchers of parks and gardens 4. World War II and the state of gardens and parks 5. A museum park as a platform for environmental education 6. Current challenges in maintaining historic and natural museum complexes
