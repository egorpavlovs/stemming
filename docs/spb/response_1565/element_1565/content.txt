                    International specialised exhibition "The Metallurgy and Casting"
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.ptfair.ru/exhibition/metallurgy/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The Metallurgy and Casting International specialised exhibition </strong>embraces the whole production cycle &ndash; from raw materials&rsquo; extraction and concentration to manufacture of metallic end-products &ndash; as well as equipment for metallurgy and casting.</p>

<p>The exhibition is part of the<strong> Petersburg Technical Fair</strong>.</p>

<p><strong>Metallurgy:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Extraction, concentration and processing of raw materials for ferrous and non-ferrous metal industries</li>	<li>Ferrous and non-ferrous metallurgy</li>	<li>Powder metallurgy</li>	<li>Equipment and technologies for metallurgical manufacturers</li>	<li>End-products. Manufacture and trade</li></ul>

<p><strong>Casting:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Technology and equipment for forms and cores&rsquo; production</li>	<li>Technology and equipment for pressure casting and precision casting</li>	<li>Molding materials, technologies and equipment for their preparation, molding sands regeneration</li>	<li>Equipment for cast alloys&rsquo; inoculation, molding boxes proportioning and pouring-in</li>	<li>Heat treatment furnaces</li>	<li>Pattern-making</li>	<li>Measurement techniques</li>	<li>End-products</li></ul>
