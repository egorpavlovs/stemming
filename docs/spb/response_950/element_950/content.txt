                                  Dom Boutique Hotel is a stylish design hotel located in St. Petersburg’s city center, near the Summer Garden. The interiors were created by the designers of the British architectural bureau Project Orange. The hotel embodies the atmosphere of a stylish home, combining luxury items, elements of original design and works of art. In total, the hotel has 60 rooms, decorated in the trends of modern English design, lounge library, restaurant, lobby bar and conference rooms. Life always boils here: festive evenings, business meetings, weddings, banquets, lectures in the lounge library and jazz.
