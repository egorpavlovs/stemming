                    Restaurant «Charlie»
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Restaurant with panoramic views of the channel and creative cuisine in the heart of St. Petersburg.

The chef Yurii Manchuk is an author of the creative menu with an average check of 1.000₽. The menu includes interesting interpretations of traditional Russian dishes and author paraphrases European hits.

Every morning at "Charlie" breakfast menu is on: freshly baked croissants, porridge, toasts with poached eggs. We serve our favorite types of coffee and fresh juice, also we offer sparkling wine to our guests which adds inspiration and lightness to a new day.

The interior design is based on Art Deco motives with catchy Pop Art details. The restaurant is successfully divided into three rooms with total capacity for 60 persons. Meditative view of the channel opens from the panoramic windows.
