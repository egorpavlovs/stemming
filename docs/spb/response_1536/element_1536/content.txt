                    4th International exhibition of heating, water supply, ventilation, air conditioning and pool equipment Aquatherm St. Petersburg
                
                
                    
                        
                            19 - 21 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Construction, Infrastructure
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.aquatherm-spb.com/en/Home/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Aquatherm St. Petersburg</strong> is the leading professional event aimed to cover all the topics of HVAC &amp; pool industry and reflects the perspectives of industry development in the North-West Federal District.</p>

<p>Exhibition profile:</p>

<ul>	<li>Heating and water supply</li>	<li>Water supply equipment</li>	<li>Pipes. Fittings. Valves</li>	<li>Control and measuring appliances for engineering systems. Automation systems</li>	<li>Ventilation and air conditioning equipment</li>	<li>Pool equipment</li></ul>

<p>Exhibitors will be able to allow a direct contact with the visitors interested in the purchase of equipment. Annually the exhibition is visited by key buyers of equipment for engineering systems:</p>

<ul>	<li>CEOs and specialists of design, installation, engineering, investment and building, asset management companies</li>	<li>CEOs and procurement specialists of wholesale and retail trade companies</li>	<li>Service specialists of engineering systems</li></ul>

<p>Aquatherm St. Petersburg is the part of the international portfolio of exhibitions Aquatherm - the largest by the coverage of sections, the number of exhibitors and visitors exhibitions dedicated to engineering systems in Russia, the CIS and Eastern Europe.</p>
