                    Favorit DMC
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    More than 20 years the destination management company “Favorit” provides a high level touristic service for business and scientific activities – congresses, conferences, forums, symposia. Our team thinks over all the details of the transport logistics, excursion provision, restaurant and catering services, informational and visa support. “Favorit DMC” is accredited both to Russia and abroad so that we are able to elaborate the conducting of Russian and foreign events. Our suppliers and contractors are approved with a longstanding collaboration, and brilliantly professional coordinators control every step of interacting with them from the very beginning planning stage to the final phases. We work personally and particularly with every client and project picking up for your participants the most creative and comfortable variants to show the new sides of Russia to the compatriots and to make foreigners choose it as a favorite country for touristic and business trips. “Favorit” has experienced providing the destination management for lots of conferences, business-summits and meetings in Germany, Italy, Poland, Hungary, Greece and other numerous countries and Russian regions.
