                    City Tour Pass tickets booklet
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    City Tour Pass is a touristic programme in Saint Petersburg based on the international standard of City Pass. City Tour Pass is a ticket-booklet on TOP-10 sights of Saint Petersburg. Advantages of City Tour Pass: if you use all tickets, you save about 47%, also you can skip the queue. Fantastic discounts in St.Peterburg cafes and restaraunts, shopping, entertainment, etc. for City Tour Pass holders.
