                    The 5th international exhibition on country house construction Country Living 
                
                
                    
                        
                            25 - 27 nov 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Construction, Infrastructure
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.zagorodom-expo.com/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The&nbsp;4th international exhibition on country house construction&nbsp;Country Living&nbsp;</strong>will take place from 22-24 April 2016 in&nbsp;CEC EXPOFORUM,&nbsp;St.Petersburg.</p>

<p><strong>Country Living exhibition </strong>is an effective instrument for promotion of the companies in the market of low-rise and country housing construction.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Exhibitors include leading Russian manufacturers and distributors of pre-fabricated houses and products and materials for building and installing interior fittings in country houses.</p>

<p><br /><strong>Exhibition profile:</strong>&nbsp;</p>

<ul>	<li>Services of designing and building country houses and garden structures</li>	<li>Building and finishing materials and tools</li>	<li>Engineering systems for country houses</li>	<li>Services and products for interior of country house</li>	<li>Goods for the countryside recreation</li>	<li>Ready houses, cottages and land plots</li>	<li>Countryside real estate &nbsp;</li></ul>
