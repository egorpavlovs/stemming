                    The 5 International exhibition of medicines, food and dietary supplements Pharma
                
                
                    
                        
                            13 - 15 oct 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.pharmaexpo.ru/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The 5th International exhibition of medicines, food and dietary supplements Pharma </strong>will take place on 13-15 October 2017 in EXPOFORUM, St. Petersburg, Russia.<br />&nbsp;<br /><strong>International Exhibition of medicines, food and dietary supplements Pharma</strong> is an effective tool for increasing sales of medicines, food and dietary supplements in the North-West of Russia.<br />&nbsp;<br />The exhibition is held within the St Petersburg International Health Forum.<br />&nbsp;<br /><strong>Exhibitors:</strong><br />Russian and foreign manufacturers and distributors of pharmaceutical ingredients, substances and medicines, substances, food and dietary supplements;<br />Companies presenting technology for pharmaceutical production.<br /><strong>Visitors:</strong><br />Representatives of farmacies and medical institutions as well as companies-distributors of medicines, food and dietary supplements, medical practitioners, pharmacy directors, heads of medical centers and clinics, interested in finding suppliers of pharmaceutical products</p>
