                    XIX Saint Petersburg International Equestrian Exhibition “Hipposphere”
                
                
                    
                        
                            7 - 10 sep 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://horses.lenexpo.ru/en/
                
            
            
                
                    Organizer
                    EF-INTERNATIONAL LTD
                
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>International Equestrian Exhibition &quot;Hipposphere&quot; </strong>is the largest forum for breeders, horse owners and representatives of the equestrian industry in the North-West Region and a convenient educational platform, where all research and educational centers of Equine Russia are presented. It&rsquo;s a platform for sharing experience and discussing the latest trends in the industry.<br /><strong>&quot;Hipposphere&quot;</strong> is held in St. Petersburg since 1999 and is the leading, most representative and popular annual exhibition in the Northwest region and in the whole Russia.</p>

<p><strong>The exhibition</strong> is supported by the Ministry of Agriculture, Government of St. Petersburg, Government of Leningrad Region, All-Russian Research Institute of Horse Breeding, Equestrian Federation of St. Petersburg, Equestrian Federation of the Leningrad region, associations of horse breeders and clubs.</p>
