                    V International Conference on Colloid Chemistry and Physicochemical Mechanics
                
                
                    
                        
                            10 - 14 sep 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    https://events.spbu.ru/events/icc2018?lang=Eng
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    Saint-Petersburg State University, Mendeleev Russian Chemical Society and the Branch of Chemistry and Material Sciences of the Russian Academy of Sciences announce the V International Conference on Colloid Chemistry and Physicochemical Mechanics. 

Traditionally, the Conference will embrace all problems of colloid and interface science.

You are cordially invited to participate in this scientific event. Please, inform your colleagues about the Conference.
