                    Nevsky radiological Forum (NRF 2017)
                
                
                    
                        
                            20 - 23 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.spbra.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Nevsky Radiological Forum</strong> - a large meeting of the St. Petersburg radiological society, uniting Radiologists (TP, ultrasound, CT, MRI, RD) of Russia, CIS and foreign countries, the statutory purpose of which is to improve the efficiency of diagnosis through the exchange of experience, discussion at the scientific sessions, symposiums, conferences, workshops, interactive seminars and exhibitions of topical issues of diagnostic radiology.</p>

<p>The program provides opportunitites for exchange of expirience, research results, discussion about actual problems of radiology, ultrasound diagnostic radiology. The agenda now covers almost all the main sections of radiodiagnosis and radiotherapy.</p>

<p>Educational program with the participation of renowned experts of departments, research institutes, medical centers, departments and laboratories is implemented in the Forum.</p>
