                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau and ITMO University have signed the cooperation agreement 
            
            August 7 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau and Institute of International Development and Partnership of ITMO University have signed the cooperation agreement on July 31. The сompanies have agreed to engage, organize and hold jointly different conferences, seminars, forums, business meetings and exhibitions in Saint Petersburg.

The increase of number of international business events is one of the main goals of Convention Bureau. But its realization is impossible without cooperation with Saint Petersburg professional science communities, which present the city abroad.

ITMO University is one Russia’s leading higher education and research institutions, specializing in Information Technology, Optical Design and Engineering. It is National Research University, the basic college of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation University, the member of European University Association, the organizer and the participant of science conferences, round tables, Russian and international exhibitions. The agreement with Institute of International Development and Partnership of ITMO University allows Convention Bureau to implement activities on the attraction of events organized by international science associations and to advance Saint Petersburg as business and education centre. At the same time the cooperation will strengthen unique environment for the science development and the training of specialists in ITMO University. It is particularly topical now, when the university is taking part at Russian Academic Excellence Project 5-100.
