                    Specialized exhibition "Hunting and fishing"
                
                
                    
                        
                            24 - 27 mar 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Consumer goods
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://sovenkon.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Exhibition &quot;Hunting and fishing&quot; is specialized exhibition of the hunting and fishing&nbsp;equipments and stocks.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Subject sections of the exposition:</p>

<ul>	<li>Technical means (autosnowsledges; launches, boats, canoes, kayaks, rafts; bicycles, motorcycles, ski-sledges).</li>	<li>Hunting stock (guns and accessories for weapons cleaning; sport accoutrement for firing practice; items for guns and ammunition storage).</li>	<li>Fishing stock (nets, drag-nets, spinning and float rods; reels, seines, spoon-baits, fishing line, etc.; sport equipment for submarine hunting).</li>	<li>Accompanying equipment and products (tents, awnings, umbrellas; means of fish and meat processing and storage; tourist and sport stock; cartographic and other polygraphic production; sport and special clothes for hunters and fishers).</li>	<li>Equipment for Fish-breeding.</li>	<li>Fishing and Hunting Tours.</li></ul>
