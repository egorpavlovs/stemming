                    Agency of Museum Communications
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Agency of Museum Communications creates complex of events for effective work of museums during the whole year round, supervises special museum projects of Russian Union of Travel Industry on N-W of Russian Federation and Craft House, coordinator «Management in cultural tourism» of the Nordic Council of Ministers in St.Petersburg. Our new project- Playspb and audiotours for iOs and Android.

Main directions of activities:Organization and realization of cultural and entertainment programs, days of national cultures, business and ceremonial events;Creation of new cultural programs, projects and itineraries in field of museums in the city and Leningrad region with help of new technologies and methodsIntroduction of innovative developments to the museumOrganization exhibitions of city hotels
