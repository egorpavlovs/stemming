                    26th International exhibition of security and fire protection equipment and products "Securika St. Petersburg. 2017" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            7 - 9 nov 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Security, Fire and Occupational Safety
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://securika-spb.ru/en-GB/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>7-9 of November&nbsp;2017&nbsp;in EXPOFORUM Convention and Exhibition Centre will take place <strong>26th&nbsp;International exhibition of security and fire protection equipment and products &quot;Securika St. Petersburg. 2017&quot;</strong>.</p>

<p><strong>Securika St. Petersburg exhibition</strong> is the largest in the Northwest of Russia international exhibition in the ﬁeld of safety, which is recognised as &lsquo;The best regional exhibition in Russia&rsquo; on subject &lsquo;Safety, ﬁre safety, labor protection&rsquo; in all nominations according to the results of the Russian National Exhibition Rating.&nbsp;<br />&nbsp;<br />Exhibitors of <strong>Securika St. Petersburg </strong>are local and foreign companies-developers, manufacturers and resellers of security and fire protection equipment and products, interested in active promotion of their products and successful output of new products, which meet modern safety requirements, on the market of Northwest Russia.<br />&nbsp;<br />Among exhibitors there are leading producers and suppliers of world famous brands: Bolid, Garant, Armo systems, LUIS+, GEUTEBRUCK, RVI, Beward, Ritm, Altonika, Omnixk, EVS, Soft Tronik, Decima, Arstel, ISTA, Eternis, Rielta and others.&nbsp;</p>
