                    11th MICE- industry Specialised Exhibition
                
                
                    
                        
                            12 - 14 oct 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://travelexhibition.ru/en/calendar/mice2016.html
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>One of the priority tasks of Saint Petersburg is rapid development of congress and business travel industry that fosters economic growth of the region, expansion of political and cultural relations between the participants of the events, as well as entrance of the city to the international MICE industry market.</p>

<p><strong>The 11th MICE Industry Specialised Exhibition</strong> held within the framework of the 25th&nbsp;INWETEX-CIS Travel Market International Travel Trade Fair brings together the leaders of congress services in Saint Petersburg and creates its own platform for communication with the potential customers, as well as for presentation of the potential of the market players. The exhibitors will have the perfect opportunity to take part in MICE-industry in Russia, the Annual International Conference.</p>

<p>The exhibition main idea is to demonstrate the role of meetings industry in the region economy and culture development and its potential in interregional and international business networking, as well as investments attraction.</p>
