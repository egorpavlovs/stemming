                Saint Petersburg International Economic Forum: three days of business and investments 
            
            June 18 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg International Economic Forum has opened today. Over 7500 delegates from 114 countries participate at one of the most reputable economic events in the world. Among them are 900 heads of Russian and international corporations.

SCO and BRICS forums, G20 consultative forum are on the agenda. Events with the participation of Vladimir Putin become the program’s key moment. Today the president hold a meeting with the chiefs of the biggest investment and sovereign wealth funds from France, Great Britain, United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Brazil and India.

Founder and Executive Chairman of Alibaba Group Jack Ma, CEO of BP Robert Dudley, CEO of Total Patrick Pouyanné, Chairman of the Board of Management at Metro Group Olaf Koch, Chairman of Board of Directors of Boston Consulting Group Hans-Paul Bürkner are among the participants of the forum. Professor of Harvard University Kenneth Rogoff and Nobel laureate in Economic Sciences, Professor of London School of Economics and Political Science Christopher Pissarides invited as star economists. Over 20 ministers from 18 non-CIS countries and 7 ministers and members of the boards from CIS-countries plan to participate at SPIEF. Prince of Saudi Arabia Mohammad bin Salman and Prime Minister of Greece Alexis Tsipras are expected in Saint Petersburg. Experts suppose that over 70 agreements would be signed with representatives of business and authorities at forum.
