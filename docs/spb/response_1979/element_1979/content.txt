                    IX International exhibition "Architecture. Town planning. Restoration"
                
                
                    
                        
                            26 - 28 jun 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Construction, Infrastructure
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.institut1.ru/component/eventlist/details/137
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>International specialized exhibition &quot;Architecture. Town planning. Restoration&quot; represents achievements in the field of restoration, modern restoration technologies and methods of design and reconstruction, new architectural concepts of buildings and constructions of different function.</p>

<p>More than 60 exhibitors from St. Petersburg, Moscow, Belgorod, Rostov-on-Don and other regions of Russia participate in an exhibition.
