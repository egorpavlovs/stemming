                Buying methods in convention bureau have been presented at the conference 
            
            September 4 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The conference Buying in Business Travel & MICE has been held in the Grand Hotel Emerald, Saint Petersburg on August 27. Corporate buyers have shared their experience of preparing and holding tenders for services of transport companies, MICE agencies, hotels and another suppliers.

Head of sales of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Elena Yazeva has told about specificity of buying in dependence on contractors. Corporate clients prefer to use the request for proposal, such form is placed on the website of the bureau. But this form is not quite convenient for multilevel demands of non-corporate sector. Professional associations request the service and the operator, which will undertake sales and search of a sponsor.

Elena Yazeva has urged industry companies to be more active because operators registered on the website of convention bureau receive request first of all. Many questions of delegates about principles of the work of the bureau demonstrate that the market is interested in the cooperation. It means that there are good conditions to increase the number of business events in Saint Petersburg by combined efforts.

The congress program has been continued reports on the organization of business charters, shows case on the search of MICE agency, panel discussion dedicated to the problems of cooperation during the tender.
