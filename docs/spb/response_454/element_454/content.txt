                                  Lotte Hotel St.Petersburg is a place of history. The hotel offers 150 guest rooms including 7 Suites. An impressive restaurant The Lounge is complemented with the elegant interior. The majestic stained glass dome decorated with Oriental lilies made by Italian masters creates a special atmosphere. MEGUmi – is the first premium class Japanese restaurant in Saint Petersburg. For those who fancy champagne with a background of astounding panoramic views, L Terrasa bar was created on the sixth floor of the hotel. The bar’s terrace rightfully claims the top rank among the city’s best viewpoints.Lotte offers two banquet halls for family celebration, wedding or business event.
