                Tourism peculiarities of Saint Petersburg were presented to regional journalists
            
            April 27 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The seminar-presentation of Saint Petersburg’s tourism peculiarities was held within press tour of regional journalists on April 23. General Director of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Anna Chetvergova spoke as moderator of the event.

Chairman of the Committee for Tourism Development Inna Shalyto gave an opening speech, after which invited speakers discovered key aspects of tourism attraction of Saint Petersburg to regional journalists. The State Hermitage, Erarta Museum and Galleries of Contemporary Art, Faberge Museum presented the cultural potential of the city. The representatives of the Northern Capital Gateway told about the development of regional airways from the Pulkovo Airport, St. Peter Line reported on the launch of new ferry destinations. The development of convenient services and internet-resources for tourists and business travellers, tools of increasing of tourism attraction, in particular, the organization of music festivals and original tours, the role of hotels in positive positioning of the city were discussed in the event too.

Consequently the seminar-presentation allowed regional journalists to receive a complex view of perspectives of city development as tourism and business destination.
