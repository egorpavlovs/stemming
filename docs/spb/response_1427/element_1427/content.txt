                        Law
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.spblegalforum.ru/en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The St. Petersburg International Legal Forum (SPBILF)</strong>, hosted by the Ministry of Justice of the Russian Federation, has been held annually since 2011. Support for SPBILF comes from the President of the Russian Federation.</p>

<p>The first forum of its kind, SPBILF offers a unique opportunity of face-to-face communication for top government officials, international lawyers, experts in law and international relations, and heads of the legal departments of major corporations.</p>

<p>The overarching mission of the St. Petersburg International Legal Forum is to promote ideas for the modernization of jurisprudence in a time of global change.</p>
