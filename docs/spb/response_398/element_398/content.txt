                                  The Herzen State Pedagogical University of Russia was founded in 1797. It’s unic architectural masterpiece, located in the center of St.Petersburg. Several educational and administrative buildings were built according to design of the great architect Rastrelli. The President of Russian Federation included the University in the list of the particularly valuable objects of the cultural heritage.Today the Herzen University is a center of the fundamental and applied researches in various fields of science. More than 1600 specialists work in the university, among them there are 800 specialists with PhD degree, 310 doctors of sciences, 6 members and 9 correspondents of Russian Academy of Science and Russian Academy of Education, in addition to that  23 Honored Scientists of the Russian Federation. Every year the University holds over 400 different events of international, national and regional levels.
