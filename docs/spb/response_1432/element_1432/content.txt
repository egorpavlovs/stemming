                     24th Saint Petersburg International Conference on Integrated Navigation Systems (24ICINS2017)
                
                
                    
                        
                            29 - 31 may 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.elektropribor.spb.ru/icins2017/eindex
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The 24th Saint Petersburg International Conference on Integrated Navigation Systems (24 ICINS 2017) &nbsp;will be held by the State Research Center of the Russian Federation &ndash; Concern Central Scientific and Research Institute Elektropribor, JSC on 29 - 31 May 2017.&nbsp;</p>

<p>This annual Conference has become a traditional event for scientists and engineers from all over the world involved in navigation and motion control to share the results of their investigations and discuss prospects for the future.</p>

<p><br /><strong>CONFERENCE TOPICS:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Inertial Sensors, Navigation and Orientation Systems</li>	<li>MEMS Sensors and MEMS-based systems</li>	<li>Global Navigation Satellite Systems</li>	<li>Alternative Navigation Systems and Sensors</li>	<li>Guidance and Control Systems and their Components</li>	<li>Integrated Navigation and Motion Control Systems</li></ul>
