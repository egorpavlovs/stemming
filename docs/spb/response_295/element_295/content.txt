                Sinology Days in Saint Petersburg
            
            August 26 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                21st Conference of the European Association for Chinese Studie is held in St. Petersburg State University on August 23-28, 2016. It is the largest European Sinology meeting.

The biennial conferences of the EACS traditionally take place in the world leading science centers; this year it is organized in Saint Petersburg for the first time. The conference aroused a great interest among sinology scientists from all over the world: there are more than 500 professionals from the leading universities  from 42 countries, including  experts from Universities of Oxford, Cambridge, Harvard, Heidelberg, Singapore, Hong Kong, Beijing and others.

Delegates prepared scientific reports devoted to varied fields of traditional and modern China study, such as history, culture, economics, religion, politics, and foreign affairs.  The section about Chinese gender relations was especially popular. 

21st Conference of the EACS is jointly organized by St. Petersburg State University, the Institute of Oriental Manuscripts RAS and the State Hermitage Museum.
