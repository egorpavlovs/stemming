                Saint Petersburg has been nominated as World's Leading Destination 
            
            October 12 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

World Travel Awards have nominated Saint Petersburg as World's Leading Destination. Such famous tourism centres as London, Las Vegas, New York, Rio de Janeiro compete for this prestigious prize along with the city on the Neva River. Exotic destinations like Jamaica, Seychelles, Maldives are among challengers. The full list of the nominees is placed here.

Voting is available online on the website of World Travel Awards. Votes cast by travel trade professional counted as two points, and those by the public as one. Over 6 million tourists visit Saint Petersburg annually, and their opinion is especially important. The voting continues until November 8.

World Travel Awards are the admitted quality label, "Oscars" of the travel industry. The event is held annually to reward the best of the best in different categories such as hotel, airline, travel agency, car-hire company, and destination. It is a reminder that World Travel Awards named Saint Petersburg as Europe’s Leading Destination for the first time on September 5.
