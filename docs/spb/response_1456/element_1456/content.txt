                Saint Petersburg has got the tourism Oscar
            
            September 6 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg has got the trophy World Travel Awards as Europe’s Leading Destination. The awarding ceremony was held in the evening the 5th of September on Italian island Sardinia in the hotel Forte Village Resort. CEO of Convention Bureau Anna Chetvergova has got the prestigious prize on behalf of Saint Petersburg.

Along with the city on the Neva River Amsterdam, Barcelona, Berlin, Edinburgh, Florence, Istanbul, Lisbon, London, Paris, Porto, Rome, Venice, and Yorkshire has competed for Europe’s Leading Destination rank. Previously Saint Petersburg has never received World Travel Awards. Such famous tourism centres as London, Istanbul, and Edinburgh became the winners in this nomination during last years. Voting was available online at the awards official website. Votes cast by travel trade professional counted as two points, and those by the public as one.

During a glittering ceremony, Saint Petersburg has won World Travel Awards for its exceptionally rich history, centuries-old traditions and bright future. The prize 2015 demonstrates the world professional recognition the contribution of the city to the development of individual and business tourism, the creation of the comfortable environment for its guests, the improvement of the infrastructure.

Lufthansa has got one more prize. It was recognized as leading European airline for the fifth time in a row. Forte Village Resort has got four awards and was named as the best resort in Italy and Europe. Europcar and the Turkish resort Cornelia Diamond Golf Resort & Spa has won the prizes.

World Travel Awards were founded in 1993. They are considered as the top achievement in the tourism sphere. The awards have been called the "Oscars" of the travel industry by The Wall Street Journal. Prizes are given both on a world level and international region level in different categories such as hotel, airline, travel agency, car-hire company, and destination.
