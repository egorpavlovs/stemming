                    MEDICAL TECHNOLOGIES. TRENDS IN DESIGNING HEALTH CARE FACILITIES
                
                
                    
                        
                            7 - 8 apr 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://medtechconf.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The conference was attended by representatives of the Committee, the chief doctors and heads of departments of hospitals and specialists design organizations and manufacturers of medical equipment.</p>

<p>Makers and business leaders to discuss trends in the design of emergency medical care centers, children&#39;s health facilities, radiology department and endoscopy to meet modern requirements of healthcare facilities.</p>

<p>Today in Russia is growing rapidly as the number of construction of new objects of medical health, and the number of projects for the reconstruction of the existing ones. This rapid development entails changing patterns of thinking and approach to the project as a whole.</p>
