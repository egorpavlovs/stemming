                                  Restaurant "The Tsar’s Village Present" is the official partner of the State Museum-Preserve "Tsarskoye Selo". We are located at a distance of about 10 km from St. Petersburg in one of the historic buildings of The Catherine Palace complex. The restaurant has two beautiful halls for 40 and 120 guests, which are suitable for business meetings, presentations and seminars as well as for organizing gala-dinners, buffets, meetings "without ties". We will be glad to organize catering service in the palace halls and parks.   Moreover we and our partners will help you in the creation of cultural and entertainment part of your event. Unique atmosphere, high quality service and excellent cuisine - our distinctive features for over 15 years.
