                    Automechanika St. Petersburg
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Cars, Bikes, and Motorbikes
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://automechanika-spb.com/visitors/about/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Automechanika St. Petersburg </strong>- leading Regional Trade Fair for the Automotive Industry targeting Trade Visitors from North West Russia and the Baltic States.</p>

<p><strong>Automechanika St. Petersburg</strong> - is part of a global brand exhibition Automechanika, created one of the world&#39;s business leaders - Messe Frankfurt Exhibition GmbH.</p>

<p>The exhibition will host Russian and foreign manufacturers and suppliers of car parts, electric equipment, accessories, equipment for service stations and car wash centers.</p>

<p><strong>Automechanika St. Petersburg </strong>will first of all attract exhibitors looking forward to developing their businesses in the Russian North-West and aimed at interregional cooperation.</p>
