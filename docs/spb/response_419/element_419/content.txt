                                  The University activity is built on close collaboration of educational clinical and research activities. That makes possible to train competent and highly-professional specialists owning present knowledge and capable to apply them practically. University research work is carried out in accordance with the relevant and current areas of biomedical development. Considerable attention is paid to researches in the field of health protection and sanitary and epidemiological welfare of the population. Long-term strategy is focused on effective spread of fundamental and applied research activities and organizing favorable scientific and educational environment providing continuous.
