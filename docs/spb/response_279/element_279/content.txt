                Saint Petersburg will host the UFI Global Congress in 2018
            
            November 16 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                Over 600 event professionals from more than 50 countries gathered in Shanghai for the 83rd UFI Global Congress. Within the program of the UFI Global Congress it was announced that St. Petersburg will host the key event of exhibition industry in 2018 – UFI Global Congress.

UFI is the leading global association of the world’s tradeshow organisers and exhibition centre operators, as well as the major national and international exhibition associations, and selected partners of the exhibition industry. UFI directly represents around 50,000 exhibition industry employees globally, and also works closely with its 55 national and regional associations members. 700 member organisations in 84 countries around the world are presently signed up as members.

The UFI Annual Congress is the largest UFI event. It provides excellent networking opportunities for UFI members from around the world. The Congress is an ideal opportunity for members to gain insight into topics of strategic interest, on the current industry trends and challenges that the exhibition industry is currently facing.

"The decision to held the UFI Global Congress-2018 in Saint-Petersburg is not only a recognition of the city’s achievements in the development of exhibition industry, it is also the result of focus efforts made by the St. Petersburg Committee for tourism development and Convention bureau for the development of congress and exhibition infrastructure and attracting the leading business events. Significantly to obtain the recognition from leading professionals of the exhibition industry," – said Andrew Mushkarev, Chairman of the St. Petersburg Committee for tourism development.

According to Sergei Alexeyev, UFI  President, RUEF President, Vice-President of LLC "Expoforum-international",–  "exhibition industry today is  85 countries and 700 UFI members from all over the world. The turnover of the industry is 100 billion euros per year, there are 700 thousand employees, the exhibition space equals to 30 thousand football fields – 260 million square meters. The number of exhibition and congresses visitors annually reaches 260 million a year”.

It is already known that within the UFI Global Congress in Saint Petersburg two thematic sessions will be organized: the first will focus on the management of large complexes, the second – on the marketing of exhibitions and IT-business. The event will be hosted by one of the largest exhibition and congress centers in Europe, Expoforum. Saint Petersburg Convention bureau and the Committee for tourism development will take an active role in the preparations for the key event of the exhibition industry.
