                Convention Bureau deals with inquires following the results of IMEX Frankfurt
            
            May 5 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Representatives of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau have participated in the Worldwide Exhibition for Incentive Travel, Meetings and Events in Frankfurt on April 19-21.

30 target business negotiations were conducted in three days at IMEX. Organizers from USA, India, United Kingdom showed the highest interest in Saint Petersburg as venue for holding congresses, conferences and incentive tours. Destination presentations were held at the booth. The average number of listeners amounted 15 people, the geo coverage included Latin America, Benelux countries, Germany, India and United Kingdom.

Alongside with Convention Bureau, Baltic Travel, Belmond Grand Hotel Europe, ExpoForum International, GALAKTIKA Co Ltd, GLOBAL RUSSIA DMC & PCO, Kalinka International DMC, OLTA Travel, RUSMICE, Events & Motivation и Russian Empire DMC worked at Saint Petersburg booth. This allowed event organizers from corporate and non-corporate sectors to discuss specific working issues with operators and venues. Cinema hall organized in support of the Year of the Movie in Russia adorned the exposition of IMEX Frankfurt.
