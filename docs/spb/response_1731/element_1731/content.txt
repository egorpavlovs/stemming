        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    11th International Conference and school «Problems of Geocosmos»
                
                
                    
                        
                            3 - 7 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://onlinereg.ru/site.php?go=236&page=5854&lang=ENG
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The following sections will be organized during the conference<br /><strong>&nbsp;Section EG. Exploration Geophysics and Earth Conductivity</strong><br />&rarr; Exploration geophysics;<br />&rarr; Conductivity of the crust and upper mantle;<br />&rarr; Electrical properties of the rocks;<br />&rarr; Electromagnetic soundings;</p>

<p>Conveners - Prof. V.N. Troyan, Prof. B.M. Kashtan, Dr. S.S. Krylov, Dr. N.Y.Bobrov</p>

<p><strong>&nbsp;Section P. Paleomagnetism and Rock Magnetism</strong><br />&rarr; Paleomagnetic reconstructions;<br />&rarr; Paleointensity;<br />&rarr; Rock Magnetism as Physical Basis of Paleomagnetism;</p>

<p>Conveners - Dr. A.A. Kosterov, Dr. E.S. Sergienko</p>

<p><strong>&nbsp;Section S. Seismology</strong><br />&rarr; Theory of seismic wave propagation;<br />&rarr; Earth&#39;s structure from seismic data;<br />&rarr; Global and regional seismicity and earthquakes sources;<br />&rarr; Methods for seismic data processing;</p>

<p>Conveners - Prof. T.B. Yanovskaya, Prof. V.N. Troyan</p>

<p><strong>&nbsp;Section STP. Solar-Terrestrial physics</strong><br />&rarr; Space climate and space weather;<br />&rarr; Magnetospheric phenomena;</p>

<p>Conveners - Dr. A.A. Samsonov, Prof. V.S. Semenov, Prof. V.A. Sergeev, Dr. N.A. Tsyganenko</p>

<p>&nbsp;<strong>Section SEMP. Seismic-electromagnetic phenomena</strong><br />&rarr; Satellite and ground observations (in different frequency ranges) of electromagnetic anomalies, possibly related to earthquakes (EQ) and other natural hazards (NH);<br />&rarr; Extraction of the EQ-NH precursory signatures from electromagnetic time series - development of mathematical methods of data processing;<br />&rarr; Modeling of seismic-electromagnetic processes<br />&rarr;Theory of the EQ-NH precursors</p>

<p>Conveners - Dr. N.A. Smirnova, Prof. M. Hayakawa, Prof. Yu.A. Kopytenko, Prof. A.A. Namgaladze</p>
