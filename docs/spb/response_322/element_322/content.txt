                    GRAND HOTEL EMERALD
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    The Grand Hotel Emerald is conveniently located in the historical part of St. Petersburg where the tranquil charm of the old city blends with business and greatest attractions. The classical interiors have absorbed the fascinating controversy of the city on the Neva river and they are in perfect harmony with the modern building executed in the late rational art nouveau style.Since 2003 the hotel has been welcoming its guests who appreciate luxury and thoughtfulness in detail. Few St. Petersburg hotels can offer such level of comfort to those who want to enjoy historical and cultural heritage of the city and to business travelers. The 90 rooms and suites of different kinds, from Standard to King DeLuxe Executive suites, prove that the hotel is one of the best in St. Petersburg.
