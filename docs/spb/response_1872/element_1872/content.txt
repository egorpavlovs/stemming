                    Exhibition-Fair "Sports. Hiking. Fishing. Rest"
                
                
                    
                        
                            6 - 10 apr 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://sivel.spb.ru/calendar/sport/about/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Exhibition-Fair &quot;Sports. Hiking. Fishing. Rest&quot;</strong> consists of several sections.</p>

<p>In the <strong>section of sports</strong> and recreation will be presented: a wellness centers and clubs services; sports clothing, footwear, cosmetics and food; Trainers and special equipment for sports and relaxation.</p>

<p>In the <strong>section of tourism</strong> and recreation will be presented: a sports and eco-tourism tourism; proposals for the sanatorium rest; equipment and accessories for travel; Special tourist centers, health tourism; Nordic walking clubs; home gardening and construction.</p>

<p>The<strong> fishing section</strong> will include: water transport (launchers, boats, motors); equipment for recreational fishing; fishing equipment; equipment for underwater hunting; means of processing, cutting and storaging of fish; consultation of public protection and supervision; Equipment for the preparation of fish after the catch.</p>

<p>In the exhibition program: roundtables, presentations of products and services of sports-hunting companies, meetings with professionals.</p>

<p>The event is officially supported by the committee on tourism development of St. Petersburg and the Leningrad&#39;s society of hunters and anglers.</p>
