                    <p><strong>The St. Petersburg International Health Forum</strong> is a united platform for professionals in the sphere of health care, consolidating five specialised exhibitions in one exhibition brand. The forum is aimed at creation of the main presentation platform for innovative solutions developping an effective health system and improving the quality of life in the Northwest of Russia.<br />&nbsp;<br />The Forum programme covers such directions as medical equipment, pharmaceutics, health and medical services, optics and bio-technologies.<br />&nbsp;<br />Execution of topically similar events at the same venue will provide comprehensive approach to development of the wellness industry.</p>
