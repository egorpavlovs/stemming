                Tourist business in the era of Information Technology was discussed within the “Modern Tourism – Digital Environment” conference
            
            October 19 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                The conference “Modern Tourism – Digital Environment” initiated by St. Petersburg Committee for Tourism Development was held within the business program of the international tourist tradeshow INWETEX-CIS Travel Market on October, 14. Leading experts and professionals, working at the joint of tourism, IT and "future technologies", discussed the global industry trends, presented high-tech solutions for tourists service and shared their experiences on "Digital-approaches" in the business.

 

Olga Mugatina, Saint Petersburg Convention bureau CEO, was a speaker at the conference. She noted that the theme of the digital technologies application has become the leading in the events of international MICE-tourism organizations. The event organizers mark three areas where the "Digital" play a key part: the development of business segments (marketing strategies and tactics modernization), communication with customers and feedback, and the monetization of all forms of content.

 

“High-tech are used at all stages of events preparation and organizing. Crowd funding and sale of tickets through a special platform helps companies and organizations to collect funds for sponsoring events and estimate its efficiency in advance. A new type of badges allows organizers to track the movement of visitors on the venue and send recommendations to visit a particular stand or a work session by means of push-notifications on the smart phone. Aggregators in social media handle photos with the relevant hashtags, geolocation, etc. New gadgets and mobile applications are used even for entertainment, "- said Olga Mugatina.

She stressed that Saint Petersburg Convention bureau successfully use High technologies, in particular, there  is an online register of MICE suppliers. In the long view there is a creation of tourism complex information and analytical base, which could analyze with the help of advanced algorithms industry statistics, attendance of the tourist facilities and identify patterns of tourist behavior. This information could be used both by  City Government  and tourism enterprises.

Michael Ushakov, CEO IT-company "Nota Bena" and director of the National Association of tourist information organizations, Artem Ovchinnikov, a leading internet marketer "Sletat.ru" Tatiana Gedzberg, Managing Director Abrikos Media communications agency and Wild Jam and others were among the speakers. The conference was moderated by Alexander Gorokhov, project manager Visit Russia (Rosturizm).

Within the Conference practice session “Digital technology – a driver of development and competitive advantage of tourism business” and case-session “Russian tourism innovation technologies” were held. Tourism and IT industry experts presented the latest service inventions.

The conference “Modern Tourism – Digital Environment” was attended by over 150 participants – leader experts and professionals of tourist companies, hoteliers, online marketers, IT-specialists, representatives of the academic community and the media.
