                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has entered into the Union of International Associations
            
            August 13 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has entered into the Union of International Associations. It allows to develop worldwide networking of business contacts and to attract international events to the city.

The Union membership opened the access to the database, which includes detailed information on over 68,000 International Intergovernmental and Non-governmental organizations from 300 countries and territories. Approximately 1,200 new organizations are added each year. Now representatives of Convention Bureau can work with the international congress calendar of UIA. The calendar includes over 428,000 meetings and events organized or sponsored by Intergovernmental Organizations and International Nongovernmental Organizations working worldwide, covers meetings from 1851 to 2030, over 10,000 of which are future meetings. It is worth noting the importance of the Union as an analytical resource. Since 1960 the UIA has prepared statistics on the international meetings that have taken place worldwide in the preceding year. The reports provide a comprehensive overview of trends in international organizations' events. Managers of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau can explore all this materials.

The Union of International Associations (UIA) is a research institute and documentation centre, based in Brussels. It was founded over one hundred years ago, in 1907. The UIA has consultative status with ECOSOC and associate status with UNESCO.
