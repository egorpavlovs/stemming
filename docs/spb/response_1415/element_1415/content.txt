                    XI International Forum "Green Economics"
                
                
                    
                        
                            17 - 19 aug 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        The National Congress Palace
                    
                    
                        Ecology and Environment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://bio-nanoteh.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>XI International Forum &quot;Green Economics&quot; </strong>will be held in the State complex &quot;Congress Palace&quot;. The organizers of the Forum are Committee on Natural Resources, Environment and Ecology of the State Duma, the Public Chamber of the Russian Federation and the Office of the Plenipotentiary of the RF President in the North-West Federal District. Forum&nbsp;helps to identify the key challenges in the concept of sustainable development.&nbsp;</p>

<p>The event includes: exhibition and presentation of modern technologies, goods and services, a plenary and closing sessions, round tables on the technological and economic solutions in the area of waste recycling, the problems of water spaces and air pollution.<br /><br />&nbsp;</p>
