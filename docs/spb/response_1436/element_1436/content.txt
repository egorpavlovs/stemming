                    <p><strong>The St. Petersburg International Economic Forum (SPIEF)</strong> is an annual international conference dedicated to economic and business issues. Over the last decade, SPIEF has become a leading international platform for the discussion of the key economic issues facing Russia, emerging markets, and the world as a whole.</p>

<p><strong>The St. Petersburg International Economic Forum 2016 </strong>was attended by over 12,000 participants from 133 countries, including heads of state and government from emerging economies, heads of major corporations, and the world&rsquo;s leading experts in the fields of science, the media, and civil society.</p>

<p><strong>The St. Petersburg International Economic Forum</strong> first took place in 1997 and since 2006, it has been held under the auspices of the President of the Russian Federation, who has also attended each event.</p>
