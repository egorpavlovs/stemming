                Engine roar at the Auto World exhibition
            
            April 5 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

25th Auto World International Show will be held in Saint Petersburg Sports and Concert Complex from 6th till 10th April. The jubilee event presents a joy to all automobilists, both professionals and drivers. The show is a unique chance to take the most popular cars to test drive and to choice own car in a couple of hours.

The exposition presents leading worldwide brands. The electromobile Tesla will be showed for the first time in Saint Petersburg.

The sport program of the Auto World exhibition is the festival of adrenalin and drive. Guests will see Rally-Sprint Competitions. Stuntriders and drifter will show their talents. The performances of young rock stars will be held every day during the show.
