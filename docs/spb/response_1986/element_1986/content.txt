                    International Maritime Defence Show 2015
                
                
                    
                        
                            1 - 5 jul 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Security, Fire and Occupational Safety
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.navalshow.ru/eng/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>IMDS performs the stable dynamics of development and ranks among the three world leading naval equipment and weapons exhibitions.</p>

<p>The Show&rsquo;s format is unique and unites in one display area:</p>

<ul>	<li>exposition of military products presented by Russian and foreign enterprises;</li>	<li>demonstration of armaments and naval equipment;</li>	<li>congress and business events;</li>	<li>visit to defence industry enterprises and VIP negotiations.</li></ul>

<p>The exhibits profile includes: naval and commercial shipbuilding, arms and weapons, complexes of naval armament and weapon, navigation, communication and control, power plants, naval aviation, location and logistic infrastructure, new materials and technologies. Significant areas of the exposition are occupied by the companies which supply marine hardware and materials, devices and electronics, information technologies, consulting, financial and insurance services in the field of design, shipbuilding and development of armament.</p>
