                    International specialised exhibition "Fasteners. Hardware. Tools" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.ptfair.ru/exhibition/fasteners/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The exhibition &quot;Fasteners. Hardware. Tool &quot;</strong> &ndash; this is a great opportunity for stand exhibitors to present their products to all visitors and participants of the St. Petersburg Technical Fair.</p>

<p>The exhibition is part of the<strong> Petersburg Technical Fair.</strong></p>

<p>Among the participants of the exhibition there are manufacturers and suppliers of fasteners, hardware, materials and components for tool production.</p>

<p><strong>&quot;Fasteners. Hardware &quot; section:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Fasteners: industrial, construction, automotive, etc.</li>	<li>Systems and equipment for assembly and installation</li>	<li>Technology of manufacturing fasteners</li>	<li>Materials and equipment for the manufacture of fasteners</li>	<li>Packaging and storage of fasteners</li>	<li>Services in the market of fasteners</li>	<li>Hardware</li></ul>

<p><strong>&quot;Tools&quot; section:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Power tools</li>	<li>Pneumatic Tools</li>	<li>Hydraulic tools</li>	<li>Tools: metalwork, carpentry, assembly, construction</li>	<li>Equipment, devices for warehousing, storage and tools transfer</li></ul>
