                    International congress "Actual trends of modern cardio-thoracic surgery" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            9 - 11 jun 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://thoracic.ru/?page_id=38
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The program of the congress &quot;Actual trends of modern cardio-thoracic surgery&quot; touches upon&nbsp;the most pressing issues of the heart, vascular, thoracic surgery, transplantation, anesthesia, the use of modern imaging techniques in cardio-thoracic practice. In 2016, following&nbsp;topics will be covered:</p>

<p>&bull; Surgery of the pleura;<br />&bull; regenerative medicine;<br />&bull; pulmonary embolism;<br />&bull; Surgery deformities of the chest;<br />&bull; mechanical circulatory support with the terminal chronic heart failure;<br />&bull; seamless and classic heart valves;<br />&bull; Surgery of the aorta;<br />&bull; lung and heart transplantation;<br />&bull; intensive care in cardio-thoracic surgery;<br />&bull; minimally invasive thoracic surgery;<br />&bull; Combined operation for lung cancer;<br />&bull; pulmonary tuberculosis;<br />&bull; Surgery for esophageal cancer;<br />&bull; Surgery emphysema and spontaneous pneumothorax.</p>
