                Beauty competitions on the banks of the Neva River
            
            February 18 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

The Beauty Festival Nevskie Berega will be held in Saint Petersburg on February 18-21.

The aim of the Festival is to develop, promote and innovate the Beauty Industry as well as to support young professionals, ensuring their growth and development. The event format is unique, since it combines International Beauty Exhibition and the Open Championship on Hairdressing, Nail Design and Make-up. It is one of the most prestigious competitions in hairdressing art, nail-design, make-up and decorative cosmetics that requires extensive training and high level of professionalism. Qualifying rounds for Championships are held in almost every major city in Russia.

The prize fund of the Beauty Festival Nevskie Berega amounts over 100 thousand euros. According to estimates of the organizers, about 10 thousand people will visit Nevskie Berega.
