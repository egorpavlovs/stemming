                                  Located in the heart of Saint Petersburg, the 4-star Novotel Saint Petersburg Centre hotel offers everything you need for your business meetings or your trip to discover the city. Free high-speed Internet and satellite TV are available in every room. The hotel also offers its guests a restaurant, lobby bar, bakery, fitness room, free parking, tour desk and gift shop. You will receive a warm welcome and first-class service with Novotel.
