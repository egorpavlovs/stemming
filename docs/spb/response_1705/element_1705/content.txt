                    47th World Congress on Dance Research
                
                
                    
                        
                            1 - 5 nov 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Culture and art
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://cidcongress-spb.com/news/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The World Congress on Dance Research&nbsp;</strong>is organized by the Saint-Petersburg Section of CID&ndash;UNESCO.</p>

<p>The program includes: scientific papers, lectures, workshops, video presentations, exhibitions, performances.</p>

<p>We welcome specialists in cultural studies, art historians, psychologists, sociologists, anthropologists, philosophers, theologists, medics, choreographers, dance teachers, dance therapists, dance film directors and professionals representing all forms of dance from all over the world.</p>

<p>The World Congress on Dance Research is a best opportunity for professionals and choreography amateurs to show their high level and share their research and methodology in dance.</p>

<p><strong>Congress Topics:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>The preservation of intangible cultural heritage. The problems of dance notion</li>	<li>The philosophy of dance</li>	<li>Creative projects presentation</li>	<li>Amateurs and professionals. Forms of interaction</li>	<li>Dance &ndash; harmony of music, motion and image</li>	<li>Artistic education or art-therapy?&nbsp; Innovative methods</li>	<li>Patronage of art. History and the present</li>	<li>Health-keeping technologies</li>	<li>Students &ndash; power of art</li>	<li>Semiotics of dance culture</li>	<li>Lingvo &ndash; dance</li></ul>
