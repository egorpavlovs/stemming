                    4th International exhibition of products and services for aesthetic and cosmetic medicine 'Aesthetic medicine' 
                
                
                    
                        
                            13 - 15 oct 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.aestheticmed.ru/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>On October 13-15, 2017 the <strong>International exhibition of products and services for aesthetic and cosmetic medicine &#39;Aesthetic medicine&#39; </strong>will take place at the CEC EXPOFORUM in St. Petersburg. &nbsp;<br />&nbsp;<br />International exhibition of products and services for aesthetic and cosmetic medicine &quot;Aesthetic medicine&rdquo; is the important industry events for professionals in the field of cosmetology and aesthetic medicine in the North-West of Russia.<br />&nbsp;<br /><strong>Exhibition profile:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Skincare products for face and body</li>	<li>Preparations of medical cosmetology</li>	<li>Injectables</li>	<li>Tools and equipment for aesthetic medicine and cosmetology</li>	<li>Medical laser systems</li>	<li>Body correction devices</li>	<li>Suture and expendable materials for plastic surgery and cosmetology</li></ul>

<p>The exhibition is held within the St. Petersburg International Health Forum, unique platform for the health professionals of the North-West of Russia.</p>
