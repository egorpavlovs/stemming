        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    International congress "St. Petersburg and the Slavic world"
                
                
                    
                        
                            6 - 9 dec 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Culture and art
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The international congress &quot;St. Petersburg and the Slavic world&quot; traditionally will take place in St. Petersburg in&nbsp;the beginning of December, 2015.</p>

<p>The congress will pass under the auspices of celebration of the 70th&nbsp;anniversary of UNESCO and the 25th anniversary of inclusion the&nbsp;object of the world heritage &quot;Historic center of St. Petersburg and the related groups of monuments&quot; in the List of the world heritage of UNESCO.</p>

<p>Within the III Congress the&nbsp;meeting among&nbsp;representatives of Commerce and Industry&nbsp;Chambers of the Slavic participating cities will be held, there will be&nbsp;a festival of Slavic cuisine and the&nbsp;exhibition of Slavic artists&nbsp;works and masters of applied arts.</p>
