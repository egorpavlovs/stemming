                    Nevsky Forum Hotel
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Nevsky Forum Hotel is a business hotel perfectly located in the center of Saint Petersburg, on Nevsky Prospect. In a walking distance there is Mayakovskaya metro station, 10-minute walk from Moscow railway station, in the vicinity of big companies’ offices, scientific and health care centers, consulates and visa centers, restaurants and city malls.

There are 29 rooms at Nevsky Forum hotel – of different categories, up to international standards, for the most fastidious and demanding travelers.
