                                  Vertical Aparthotel in St. Petersburg opened its doors to guests in August 2014. On 12 floors of the hotel there are 126 stylish rooms with furniture and full interior equipment, combining the severity of the business class and the comfort of home comfort. The infrastructure of the Vertical Aparthotel is a combination of modern technologies, reliable security systems and a wide range of hotel services.
