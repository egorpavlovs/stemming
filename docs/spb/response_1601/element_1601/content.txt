                Large food festival in Saint Petersburg
            
            July 24 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Large food festival Oh yes! Food! (Oda! Eda!) will be held on Elagin Island on July 25-26. It promises to become a greatest gastronomic event in Eastern Europe this year.

The main theme of the festival sounds as Saint Petersburg cuisine. Each restaurant-participant will represent one dish on the topic. In such a way Saint Petersburg will make its unique menu card. Guests will be able to taste any dish, vote for it and study out a phenomenon of Saint Petersburg cuisine.

The territory of the festival will include such special zones as three food-courts with restaurants, market, cooking infant school, gastronomic theatre, open-air lyceum.

One of the festival missions is the promotion of healthy lifestyles. The open office of the nutritionist will be worked. Running, yoga and pilates activities will be held on the sports ground.

The festival is supported by Committee for Tourism Development and Committee for Entrepreneurship and Consumer Market Development.
