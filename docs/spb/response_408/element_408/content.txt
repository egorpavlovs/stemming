                                  Buff is a unique theatre in its essence. At the main stage they play classics, while entertaining the spectators by music programmes at the other stage, in one evening. They are planning, in addition to scheduled repertoire performances, to hold music soirées, to organize exhibitions of modern artists and festivals in the new building. Currently Buff has many large-scale interesting plans that will enable us to extend the circle of our communication and make our joint soirées more impressive and unique. Theater offers spacious new rooms that accept a cultural event or a meeting at the highest level.
