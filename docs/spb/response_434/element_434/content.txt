                                  Repino Cronwell Park Hotel, situated on the shores of the Finnish Gulf in the Repino settlement, Kurortniy district of Saint Petersburg, is intended for active corporate or family rest. We offer 118 cozy rooms, restaurant and summer terrace, health camp and swimming pool, entertainment centre and bowling, billiards, conference halls. You won`t find more pleasant place for conference, corporate parties and holidays in the Kurortniy district than our Repino Cronwell Park Hotel. Our hotel has everything to make your holiday unforgettable – on the one hand there`s warm climate, magnificent pine park, clean forest air, on the other hand you get up-to-date hotel service for a good rest.
