                                  This boutique hotel is centrally located in St. Petersburg, close to the Kazan Cathedral and Nevsky Prospect. Elegant rooms with antique furniture, free parking and free Wi-Fi are featured here.Antique Hotel Rachmaninov’s rooms feature classical design, and the doors of each room feature unique art from local artists.Guests can enjoy a continental breakfast every morning and read free newspapers.Nevsky Prospekt, the city's main avenue lies just a 5-minute walk away. The State Hermitage, Saint Isaac's Cathedral and the Mariinsky Theater area are also nearby.Hotel is a great choice for travelers interested in city walks, ambiance and museums.
