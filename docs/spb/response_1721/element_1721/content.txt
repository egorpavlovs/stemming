                    5th International Conference on Russian Ports and Shipping
                
                
                    
                        
                            4 - 6 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Transport, Shipment, Warehouse, and Logistics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://dolphin-uk.cergis.com/home/transtec/about/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The Organisers of the NEVA Exhibition and Conference Programmes are pleased to announce the outline Programme and Contents for <strong>TRANSTEC 2016, The Future of Russian Ports and Shipping Event</strong>, 4 &ndash; 6 October 2016 at the Lenexpo Exhibition Complex in central St Petersburg.</p>

<p>Key topics of the ROSMORPORT FSUE &ldquo;Strategy for Development of Marine Ports of Russia to 2030&rdquo; will also richly support the Port Programme, focussing on:</p>

<ul>	<li>Combining the interests of Ports, Shipping and Shippers</li>	<li>Port Users perspectives on national and global shipping</li>	<li>Development of the Cruise and Ferry Market for the Sea and Inland Waterways of Russia</li></ul>

<p>The Shipping Programme at TRANSTEC 2016 also promises to provide an excellent platform for debate, supported by the following topics:</p>

<ul>	<li>Shipping for the development of the RF Inland Waterway Systems</li>	<li>Shipping Logistics to develop the Northern Sea Route as Transit Route and Destination Route</li>	<li>Increased cooperation with foreign partners, manufacturers and service</li></ul>
