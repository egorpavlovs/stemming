                    St. Petersburg Card
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Saint Petersburg Card is a smart way to explore the northern capital of Russia on your own. It provides a vast range of tourist opportunities and helps discover the city’s unique cultural heritage in all its splendor.

This is a sightseeing smart card designed on the basis of City Pass system, successfully operatingworldwide. The all-in-one package makes sightseeing easier, helps cut travel expenses and providesconvenience of cashless payments. Since 2012, Saint Petersburg Card has been represented in the EuropeanCity Cards group along with 40 other City Card projects.

What's Included: free admission to over 60 museums in St. Petersburg and its suburbs; over 10 various tours - hop-on hop-off double-decker buses, river cruises and more; a rechargeable transport pass; cost-saving special offers and discounts on accomodation, restaurants, cafes, bike rental and other tourist services.

Saint Petersburg Card is available in 2, 3, 5, 7 day options (validity counts in hours). It is activated afteryour 1st visit to a museum or a tour. The Card comes with a guidebook packed with useful visitor information -description and open hours of attractions, recommended routes and other tips to enrich your travel experience.

Purchase online and pick-up your order at tourist information offices or other redemption points, stated onthe official website. Delivery to hotel upon request. Otherwise, choose a convenient sales point from amonghotels in the city centre, several museums or visit the Operator's office directly.
