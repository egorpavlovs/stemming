                Convention Bureau is in the structure of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Russian Federation
            
            November 6 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has become a member of the Committee for Exhibitions and Fairs and Support of Producers and Exporters of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry of Russian Federation. The event was held at the sheduled meeting of the Committee on October 30.

Convention Bureau as the member of the RF CCI receives the access to numerous database of business contacts in Russia and abroad. The Chamber includes Foreign representative offices in Europe, Asia, Australia, Oceania, America and Africa, Russian business Councils with foreign countries, Expert and Coordination Councils. Through the accession to the RF CCI Convention Bureau can take part in the lawmaking actively. At present the Committee for Exhibitions and Fairs and Support of Producers and Exporters elaborates the project of the Federal Law on Exhibition, Fair and Congress Activities in the Russian Federation. Delegates has discussed its drafting at the meeting. The law is elaborated for the first time. This legal act enlarges the current concept on the industry development.
