                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau is armed with DMAI marketing tools
            
            October 28 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has entered into Destination Marketing Association International in October.

Now Bureau as the DMAI member can use progressive marketing tools for the strategic development of Saint Petersburg.  The program DestinationNext is among the most interesting services. This online diagnostic tool is designed to assist DMOs complete an objective self-assessment which can help them determine priorities and strategies for the future. Destination Arena allows countries and cities to compare its performance against other regions across more than 100 different metrics. By using the Event Impact Calculator planners can measure the economic value of an event and calculate its return on investment to local taxes. EmpowerMINT set a mission to connect planners to DMOs.  

Destination Marketing Association International is the oldest professional union. It was established in 1914. Its members are over 600 official DMOs with more than 4,100 staff. The association gained a colossal experience and a wide analytical data, which Convention Bureau will use to increase the effectiveness of its work and develop meeting industry and business tourism in Saint Petersburg.
