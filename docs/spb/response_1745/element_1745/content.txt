                Convention Bureau is a part of ICCA family
            
            June 10 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau entered into the International Congress and Convention Association.

The Association was founded in 1963. The headquarters are located in Amsterdam. Today the ICCA is one of the biggest and most important professional communities in the world uniting 1038 companies and organizations from 95 countries. Congress and tourism bureaus, convention venues, hotels, travel and incentive agencies, event providers, service companies, airlines recruit to the association annually.

The ICCA membership of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau is first of all the access to an extensive informative resource. This platform allows to keep informed about concourses of international association constantly and to become a participant of tender procedures.

The active promotion of Saint Petersburg as a challenger for hosting international business events lets to enhance city image at the global event market.
