                                  Hotel complex Staraya Melnitsa offers to spend a good rest away from the city, on the shores of the Gulf of Finland. Finding yourself on the territory of the complex, the guests will feel like in an old town, where for some time you can forget about the bustle of the city, fully immersed in the atmosphere of the Middle Ages.
