                    Offshore Marintec Russia

                        Shipbuilding
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://offshoremarintec-russia.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    Offshore Marintec Russia is a unique project for the Russian exhibition market, which is the conceptual continuation of the most famous national forum on the development of the RAO / CIS Offshore shelf and the international marine technology and shipbuilding exhibition Marintec.

Offshore Marintec Russia demonstrates the potential of the Russian and world shipbuilding industry by presenting the best practices of maritime and transport construction as an important segment of shelf infrastructure development.
