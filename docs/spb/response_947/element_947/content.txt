                                  The hotel was opened at the end of April 2003, on the eve of the 300th St. Petersburg’s anniversary. Our guests are offered to stay in 35 comfortable rooms. The hotel is conveniently located in a quiet green Rizhskaya street, 10 minute ride away from Nevsky Prospect, Moskovsky railway terminal, Ladozhsky station, the Saint Alexander Nevsky Lavra and the Ice Palace. The nearest metro station is «Novocherkasskaya».
