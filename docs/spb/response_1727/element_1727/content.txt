        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    17th international specialised exhibition for design, construction and operation of transport infrastructure facilities  "Roads. Bridges. Tunnels"
                
                
                    
                        
                            28 - 30 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Transport, Shipment, Warehouse, and Logistics
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.mostdor.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>&quot;The Roads. Bridges. Tunnels&quot;</strong>&nbsp;exhibition is the biggest project dedicated to transport construction in the North-Western region of Russia. It is designed to help meet the development and implementation of promising new technologies, materials, and equipment.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<ul>	<li>A successful local event for advanced machines and technologies, materials and equipment</li>	<li>A contribution to road construction industry development by demonstration of the latest industry developments and organisation of a rich business program</li>	<li>An opportunity of effective goods and services promotion at the industry market in the following sections: mechanical engineering, roads and bridges design and construction, tunnel management, science and research, software and other related fields</li>	<li>A unique outdoor site for demonstration of the industry machines in the historic city center (the Manege square). The site exhibits loaders, excavators, plate compactors, snow ploughs, compressor equipment and other foreign and Russian machines</li>	<li>High interest of trade visitors, including the industry representatives from over 40 Russian regions and numerous foreign countries</li></ul>
