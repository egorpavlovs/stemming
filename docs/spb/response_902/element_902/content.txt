                    Bioinformatics: from Algorithms to Applications
                
                
                    
                        
                            16 - 19 jul 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://biata2018.spbu.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    II International conference «Bioinformatics: from Algorithms to Applications».Modern life science studies involve both biological and computational methods. Breakthrough results in these fields can only be achieved by tight communication and knowledge exchange between the two sides. The ultimate goal of the conference is to bring together bioinformaticians and biologists to share experience in development and practical application of bioinformatics algorithms.
