                                  Domina St. Petersburg  is the first property of Domina Hotel Group in Russia. 109 rooms belong to the Domina’s collection of 5 star hotels and are designed for comfort and unforgettable impressions while visiting one of the most beautiful cities in the world.  Located on the Moika River Embankment, in the noble mansion of XIX century, the Hotel harmoniously combines classic with original eclectic  style. A step away from Saint Isaac’s Cathedral, the Hermitage Museum and the Mariinsky Theatre Domina St. Petersburg will welcome you with home comfort and high standards of European hospitality.  The unique design concept of living 5D Lifestyle created by architect Gianmarco Cavagnino distinquishes the Hotel. It is the five facets of life which every person holds dear: Family, Romance, Nature, Adventure and Fantasy.
