                    Scale model museum «Petrovskaya Akvatoria»
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Scale model museum «Petrovskaya Akvatoria» - is the first large historical scale modelin Russia at a scale of 1 in 87, 500 sq. m. in area, opened in September, 2014. It is located in thecenter of Saint Petersburg, a 10-minute walk from the Hermitage and St. Isaac's Cathedral,within a 5-minute walk from Palace square, near the metro station "Admiralteyskaya". TheMuseum is opened from 10-00 to 22-00 seven days a week, by prior arrangement it is possible toopen the Museum at another time.Permanent exhibition represents reconstruction of the most significant sights of SaintPetersburg and suburbs, related to the city and Russian fleet origin, from Peter the Great’s tillCatherine the Great’s era, recreated according to engravings and archive sources. Unusual formatof the museum – scale model – makes it possible to get a bird’s eye view of Saint-Petersburg of18th century.The main peculiarity of the scale model is over 20 tons of real water, representing thewaters of Neva and the Gulf of Finland. That is why the scale model got the name «PetrovskayaAkvatoria», as a tribute to the city creator, Peter the Great, who saw Petersburg as maritimecapital of the Russian state.For the first time the main sights of Saint-Petersburg and suburbs are collected in oneplace, they may be admired at any weather, and the fountains are in operation for the visitors inall seasons. It is the only place where it is possible to see how Saint-Petersburg and the mainimperial residences looked like three centuries ago and what located in the place of nowadaysfamous buildings and architectural complexes.Thanks to the latest technologies, modern developments, work of close-knit team ofprofessionals: historians, architects, museum employees, engineers who managed to implementan idea of reconstruction of Petersburg of the 18th century, an ancient city come to life. It ispossible to control movement of carriages, ships, models and «activate» domestic scenes withthe help of special interactive buttons. Here you may see smooth change of seasons, day andnight, how model ships of the Petrine era float on water, visitors may see scenes of enormousfloods and fires, graceful carriages move along channels, and gorgeous ladies with elegantcavaliers whirl in the fascinating dance… A true tale that will make everyone happy and wonderas a child!Scale model in numbers: scale layout 1:87, area – 500 sq. m., more than 1000 buildings, 305meters of roads, 1,000 vehicles, including a moving carriages, 20 tons of water, more than 100vehicles, 25 000 characters, 20 000 LEDs.Welcome to Petersburg of the 18th century, welcome to "Petrovskaya Akvatoria"!Acquaintance with St. Petersburg begins here!
