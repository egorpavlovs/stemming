                    Hipposphere. 2018
                
                
                    
                        
                            9 - 12 aug 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Agriculture
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://horses.expoforum.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    Hipposphere – St. Petersburg International Exhibition of horses and ponies, goods and services for equestrian sport and riding has been held in St. Petersburg since 1999 and is the leading, most representative and popular yearly trade fair in Russia.

Organizers: ExpoForum jsc, Rosplemkonzavod Association, All-Russian Scientific Research Institute of Horse Breeding, St. Petersburg Equestrian Sport Federation, Leningrad Region Equestrian Sport Federation

Official Support: Ministry of Agriculture of the Russian Federation, St. Petersburg Government, Government of the Leningrad region, Veterinary Authority of the St. Petersburg Administration, Russian Equestrian Sport Federation Pony Sport Committee.

Participants: St. Petersburg State Academy of Veterinary Medicine, Horse Veterinary Society.

Exhibitors: Leading Russian scientific and education centers, studs and horse breeding farms, private owners, equestrian associations and clubs, the leading Russian scientific and education centers, equestrian industry and trade companies, equestrian sport and horse breeding enterprises, the specialized mass media.

The representatives of modern equestrian market industry introduce the newest accomplishments in this rapidly developing field! It is here that all those who wish have every opportunity to discover new Russian and foreign horse and rider products and services.

The Hipposphere agenda is diverse and is filled with fascinating events and contests, competitions and study seminars. Conferences and ring dressage tests, art work expositions and guided tours – everything is dedicated to horses at the Hipposphere trade fair.
