                    The 85th UFI Global congress 2018 will be held in St. Petersburg, Russia.

The international congress of The Global Association of the Exhibition Industry (UFI) is the key event in the exhibition industry in the world, during intense business program will be made the biggest decisions affecting to the direction of sector development for a nearest year. The congress brings together about 700 professionals of exhibition industry out of 85 countries annually.
