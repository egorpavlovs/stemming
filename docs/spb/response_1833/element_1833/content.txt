                    XV International forum Baltic Weekend
                
                
                    
                        
                            8 - 9 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        Corinthia St. Petersburg
                    
                    
                        Business
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.balticweekend.com/en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Baltic Weekend</strong>&nbsp;is the biggest international&nbsp;forum on communications in Сentral and&nbsp;Eastern Europe. The conference assembles PR and marketing experts from Russia and other countries, state authorities, representatives of the business community, the mass media and educational and non-profit organizations interested in the development of professional and ethical communications.</p>

<p>The concept of the conference is <strong>&quot;Faces &amp; Cases&quot;</strong>: key persons in the communications industry, latest know-hows, bright personalities and success stories. Among speakers of Baltic Weekend are the most famous Russian and international communications practitioners, leaders in the media business and related industries, top managers of the largest companies, political analysts and journalists.<br /><br />Over 600 specialists from 25 countries have taken part in the conference since 2001. During the fourteen conferences over&nbsp;250 experts have delivered their reports and 95 round tables dedicated to global and national trends in the development of &nbsp;PR and marketing communications have been carried out.</p>
