                                  The hotel "Tavricheskaya" is located in historical and business centre of St. Petersburg. Hotel is located near Smolny Cathedral, Kikin house, the Administration of St. Petersburg and Government of Leningrad region, a five minute walk from Tavrichesky Palace and Tavrichesky garden. The hotel "Tavrichesky" is ideal for holding business meetings, international symposia, conferences, forums, business meetings. Conference rooms are equipped the latest technology.
