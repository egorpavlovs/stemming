                    St. Petersburg Mining University
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    October 21 (November 1) 1773 - Catherine II inscribed on the decree on the establishment of the engineering school on the mountain "to be this." This date was the birthday of all higher technical education in Russia.Today, the Mining University implements all levels of higher education with the qualification of bachelor, master, graduate for the largest Russian and foreign companies involved in the forecast, search, exploration, development and processing of the most important types of minerals - oil, gas, ores of precious, non-ferrous and rare metals, diamonds, precious stones.
