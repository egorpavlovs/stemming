                    Peterfood. 2018
                
                
                    
                        
                            13 - 15 nov 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Food Processing, Beverages, Tobacco. Commercial, Refrigeration, and Exhibition Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://peterfood.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    “Peterfood” Exhibition is the main event in the North-West Region of Russia in the sphere of food retail. It's the best opportunity to establish business contacts with retail chains in St. Petersburg and the Northwest of Russia and to present food products on the growing market.

The exhibition is supported by:

St. Petersburg Government;Association of Retail Companies;Union Of Russian Independent Chains;Consumer Association“Rost”;St. Petersburg Quality Control Centre;Association of Consumer Goods Producers And Suppliers “Rusprodsoiuz”.

"Peterfood" is the only exhibition in the world where the Exhibitors are provided with guaranteed contacts with buyers of the retail chains right at their Booths. Buyers of 40 leading federal and regional retail chains of the North West region of Russia will visit your Booth to negotiate for delivery. The decision-makers of the retail chains will explain how they make their choice while performing assortment.
