                    Saint-Petersburg State University of Economics. Faculty of Tourism and Hospitality
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Faculty of Tourism and Hospitality is the largest in St. Petersburg and the Northwest region of Russia, which is carrying out specialized training for the industry of tourism, hospitality, the congress and exhibition industry and the sphere of service in general.

Faculty of Tourism and Hospitality actively interacts with the leading industry enterprises in thematic lectures organization, master-classes of leading practitioners, practices and internships organization, employment of graduates.

Faculty of Tourism and Hospitality provides the following bachelor programs:

43.03.01 BA Service (profile “Congress and exhibition activity”)

43.03.02 BA Tourism (profile “Organization and tourism industry management”)

43.03.03 BA Hotel business (profile “Organization and hotel and restaurant industry management”)

Faculty of tourism and hospitality also provides MA and postgraduate programs:

43.04.01 MA Service, program “Service economy: international services markets”, educational trajectory “Service organizations management in the international event services markets”

43.04.02 MA Tourism, programs: “Organization of tourism destinations management”, “Management of the company and tourist markets”

43.04.03 MA Hotel business, program “Hotel business management”

Postgraduate program:

38.06.01 Economics

Educational programs of all levels of higher education are provided on the basis of the following faculty departments: service and congress and exhibitions activities department, hotel and restaurant business department, organization and management of the services sector. In the faculty structure are the following scientific and educational centers: “Center of youth tourism”, “Higher school of culinary art”, which are carrying out scientific research on current topics and training and production activities for the practical training organization.

Target training of personnel for the congress and exhibition industry is carried out within the framework of the BA program (43.03.01 BA Service, profile “Congress and exhibition activity”) and MA program (43.04.01 MA “Service economy: international services markets”, educational trajectory “Service organizations management in the international event services markets”). Educational programs are updated in accordance with the requirements of the professional standards, incl. standard “Specialist in exhibition activities in the sphere of trade and industrial exhibitions”. The educational process is focused on the project approach and practical orientation with involving students to the creation of concepts of new events, preparation and organization of the complete cycle of own projects, formation of personal portfolio of the graduate as event organizer based on the results of educational program.

Faculty of tourism and hospitality is interested in expanding cooperation with the leading professionals of the congress and exhibition industry, tourism and hospitality on a wide range of issues to improve the quality of training and implementation of partner projects.
