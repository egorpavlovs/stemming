                    8th International Congress «Current trends of modern cardiothoracic surgery»
                
                
                    
                        
                            21 - 23 jun 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                        Crowne Plaza St.Petersburg Airport
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://thoracic.ru/?page_id=2340&lang=en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    The Congress is supported by the Russian Ministry of Health, the Government of St.Petersburg, St.Petersburg State University, St.Petersburg Research Institute of Phthisiopulmonology, Pirogov’s Surgical Society, Federal Almazov North-West Medical Research Centre, Russian Association of Thoracic Surgeons, National Association of TB specialists, Research Institute of Circulation Pathology named by E. Meshalkin (Novosibirsk), European Society of Thoracic Surgery (ESTS) and Hannover Medical School (MHH).The program will be composed of the presentations devoted to the treatment of common pulmonary and esophageal diseases, incl. rare unusual cases, cardiothoracic (heart and lung) surgery, heart and blood vessel diseases, risk assessment in cardiothoracic surgery. The main issues will concern the chest organ transplantation, anesthesiological support and modern imaging methods in cardiothoracic surgery.
