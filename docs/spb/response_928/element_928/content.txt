                Saint Petersburg Technology Fair will present industrial future of Russia
            
            March 14 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Technology Fair will be held in CEC Expoforum on March 15-17. PTF is the forum for leading Russian and foreign businesses for past 10 years. Traditionally, the event comprises specialized international exhibitions in metallurgy, metal working, machinery, machine tool manufacture, conductor, mounting, fastenings, metalware, and compressor equipment and industrial innovations. Industrial companies from Russia, Germany, Belarus, Hungary, Germany, Italy, China, Latvia, Taiwan, Finland and Czech Republic will be presented here.

In 2016, the expo will again become a part of the busy schedule of the Saint Petersburg Industrial Congress, an event that aims to initiate a discussion about the issues of the economic sector between the Government and the business and to achieve practical and efficient solutions. Participants of the Congress will have the opportunity to find new partners and to sign long-term contracts for business development.

Each PTF participant will not only be able to present its innovations, but also compete for the Grand Prix as the Best Innovative Project and Best Scientific and Technological Product of the Year. The expert evaluation of the projects is performed by SRI FRCEC, one of the leading scientific and technological institutions in Russia.
