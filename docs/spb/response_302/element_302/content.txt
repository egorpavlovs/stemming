                We invite you to visit Vedomosti Event Show
            
            August 16 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                The Vedomosti Event Show  will take place in Moscow on September, 8– 9 under support of Saint Petersburg convention bureau. The Conference will gather professional organizers, and service providers of the event industry. 

Within  two days of the Conference the experts will share the best international and Russian practices of entertainment and business event’s organization.

This year the project will combine 2 parallel tracks of the program: the Event Board and the Conference Board, business and non-business events. Key market players will discuss the event market changes for recent years in both sectors: unique formats, approaches, technologies and services: what will allow becoming a leader in the changing world and what will events look like in the near future?

The 2-day conference program includes:

• Foresight session 2016-2020 on the event market in Russia.

• Myths and reality of the event business. Key trends of the event business as seen by Russian and international experts.

• The significance of mega events for the development of urban economy and creation of a comfortable urban environment.

• The most relevant projects and the best cases of 2015-2016. Leaders and success stories.

• Digital evolution: new services and applications for event organizers.

• Event project management methodology and the latest tools in an event manager's toolkit.

• EffectUp conference dedicated to enhancing event efficiency.
