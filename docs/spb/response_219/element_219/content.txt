                                  The Park Inn by Radisson Pulkovo Airport St. Petersburg, a premier airport hotel, has a direct connection with Terminal 1 and welcomes guests to one of Russia’s most exciting cities. Access Terminal 1 via a covered walkway, and also enjoys convenience to business and cultural destinations. Rest well in comfortable rooms, and dine on delectable cuisine at the convenient on-site restaurant. A lobby bar serves drinks 24 hours a day, and travellers can utilise the fitness centre to maintain workout regimens while travelling. With an airport location, the hotel’s meeting rooms are perfect for hosting smooth-running events that are convenient for delegates.
