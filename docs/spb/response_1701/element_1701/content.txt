                    Russian scientific-practical Conference "Physical training and sport in education in Russia: innovations and perspectives of development»
                
                
                    
                        
                            1 - 30 nov 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The basic directions of work of the Conference:</p>

<p>1. Physical education and sport in the modern Russia in the context of the strategy for the development of physical culture and sport in the Russian Federation in the period up to the year 2020.</p>

<p>&nbsp;2. Problems and experiences in the implementation of the Federal State educational standards for a new generation of physical culture.</p>

<p>3. Actual problems of organization, management and efficiency of physical recreational and sports activities and the formation of the healthy way of life of students in educational organizations that implement programs of higher education and secondary vocational education.</p>

<p>4. Development and practical use of innovative teaching methods in sports training system.</p>

<p>5. Modern methods of organizing educational and training processes for mass sports and physical training and sports activity.</p>

<p>6. Student sports clubs. Student sports and volunteer movement in the context of international competitions.</p>

<p>7. Sports complex GTO-the basis of athletic activities of educational organizations.</p>

<p>8. History of development of physical culture and sports in universities.</p>
