                    20th INTERNATIONAL SPECIALIZED EXHIBITION OF NATURAL GAS INDUSTRY  AND TECHNOLOGY FOR GAS FACILITY “ROS-GAS-EXPO”
                
                
                    
                        
                            4 - 7 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Energy. Energy efficiency
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.en.farexpo.ru/fuel/exhibition/about/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>ROS-GAS-EXPO</strong> &ndash; is the biggest event of the gas sector in Russia, devoted to demonstration of achievements in the sphere of construction, maintenance and reconstruction of gas pipeline system and gas consumption.</p>

<p><strong>Exhibition profile:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Gasfication of residential buildings, industrial, municipal and agricultural buildings, industrial zones and industrial parks</li>	<li>Magistral gas-supply&nbsp;</li>	<li>Autonomus and reserve gas-supply&nbsp;</li>	<li>Purification of associated petroleum gas for gas utilities&nbsp;</li>	<li>Operation of gas distribution systems</li>	<li>Gas assessment</li>	<li>Diagnostics&nbsp;</li>	<li>Industrial security and ecology of gas complex&nbsp;</li></ul>
