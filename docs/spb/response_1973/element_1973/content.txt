                    Congress of physicians in St. Petersburg and North-West Russia
                
                
                    
                        
                            15 - 16 mar 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.spbto.info/events/regionalnye-konferentsii/xv-kongress-terapevtov-sankt-peterburga-i-szfo
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Congress of physicians in St. Petersburg and NORTH-WEST RUSSIA is by far the largest educational medical event of Northwest region of Russia.</p>

<p>The Congress is expected to involve the Vice Governor of St. Petersburg, Kazan O.a., Chairman of the Health Committee, Kolabutina v.m., Chief Therapist Russia academician RAS Čučalina a.g., Chief Therapist St. Petersburg RAS academician V.i. Mazurova, Chief cardiologist of Russia academician RAS &Scaron;l&acirc;hto e.v., the President of the Russian scientific medical society physicians academician RAS Martynova a.i., Chairman of the Northwest Branch of the medical sciences academician RAS Sofronova G.a., Chief therapeutists regions NORTH-WEST Russia.</p>

<p>The scientific program will be made by academicians and corresponding members of the Russian Academy of Sciences, professors, associate professors, research associates from St. Petersburg, Moscow and other Russian cities. In his lectures, they will illuminate current issues of internal Pathology: cardiology, Pulmonology, Gastroenterology, rheumatology, Neurology, Endocrinology, hematology, Nephrology, immunopathology and homeopathy. It is expected that the Congress will be attended by more than 2000 physicians.</p>
