                                  Hotel in St. Petersburg convenient to airport and downtownThe Park Inn by Radisson Pulkovskaya, positioned on tree-lined Moskovsk Avenue, offers guests convenient access to both downtown and the airport. This hotel in St. Petersburg is nestled among elegant historical homes, charming parks, stylish shopping centres and vibrant restaurants. Guests can stay in one of the 841 well-appointed guest rooms, including 83 Business Friendly Rooms and 27 Suites. Travellers can enjoy one of the hotel’s 4 restaurants, work out in the on-site fitness centre, or host a business or social gathering in the modern meeting facilities. This hotel is 100% non-smoking.
