                    The First Electrotechnical University
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Saint-Petersburg Electrotechnical University ETU "LETI" is one of the world's largest education centers specializing in radio engineering, electrical engineering, electronics and computer science. University graduates get Bachelor's and Master's degree ог specialist qualification. Today the University trains around 9000 students. Over 200 professors and PhDs work in the University. ETU «LETI» provides education in 19 scientific fields for Bachelors and 53 educational programs for Masters.
