                    St. Petersburg Design Week 2017
                
                
                    
                        
                            24 - 31 may 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Culture and art
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.spbdesignweek.ru/en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>St. Petersburg Design Week 2017 </strong>will&nbsp;present creative projects, show Russian and European design in order to attract attention of the international professional community and media to new design resources in Saint-Petersburg.</p>

<p>The program unites the best city places: museums, galleries, interior and architectural showrooms, hotels and lofts, open architectural spaces. It makes&nbsp; possible to learn architecture, industrial design and culture of the participating countries.&nbsp;</p>

<p>Traditionally the organizer of the Saint-Petersburg Design Week is&nbsp;<strong>FineStreet Media Group</strong>.</p>
