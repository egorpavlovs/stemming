                    Country Living - autumn
                
                
                    
                        
                            25 - 27 nov 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Agriculture
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.zagorodom-expo.com/autumn/index.aspx?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Country Living exhibition is an effective instrument for promotion of the companies in the market of low-rise and country housing construction.</p>

<p>Exhibitors include leading Russian manufacturers and distributors of pre-fabricated houses and products and materials for building and installing interior fittings in country houses.</p>

<p>The show advantages:</p>

<p>​&nbsp;The season successful beginning. The show opens a construction season simultaneously with active search of customers and conclusion of cottage construction contracts</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Unique audience of visitors. According to the poll the majority (53%) of Country Living visitors don&rsquo;t attend other exhibitions on similar subject</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Efficient instrument of products and services promotion. The exhibition allows companies to enter the low-rise and country housing construction in the Northwest of Russia, to expand the client base, to improve the brand recognition, to strengthen the successful company image, to test novelties.</p>

<p>Exhibition profile</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Designing and building country houses and garden structures</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Building materials and tools</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Finishing materials</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Engineering systems for country houses</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Fences, gates and enclosure systems</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Design and interiors for country houses</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Countryside products</p>

<p>​&nbsp;Landscape design</p>

<p>​&nbsp;NEW! Countryside real estate</p>
