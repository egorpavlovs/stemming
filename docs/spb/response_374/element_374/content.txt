                                  Shlisselburg Guest House is located in the merchant house of the second part of the 19th century in the historical centre of Shlisselburg at the source of Neva River, only 40 km to the centre of St.Petersburg. Guest House has full infrastructure for accommodation top managers during the periods of business trips, business events.  Guest House recreates the everyday-life culture belonging to the town of district of the 19th-20th century that allows our guests to enrich their knownledge of Shlisselburg, St.Petersburg and Russia.
