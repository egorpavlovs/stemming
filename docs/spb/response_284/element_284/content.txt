                Saint Petersburg hosts III International Exhibition of Contemporary Art  ArtExpoSpb
            
            October 18 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                III International Exhibition of Contemporary Art ArtExpoSPb will take place on October, 21-23, 2016. This large-scale event will gather diverse works of contemporary art on 3,500 sq.m. in LenExpo exhibition hall.

Annually hundreds of artists and galleries  from across the globe showcase an exciting original artwork, prints, paintings, drawings, sculpture, photography, ceramics, giclee, glass works within the  ArtExpoSPb. 

This year the Exhibition will be held in a new format. The official opening scheduled for 12pm - 4pm will welcome renowned members of art and culture community, museum specialists, foreign diplomats, representatives of private and state organizations, collectors, and art dealers on October, 21. Furthermore, the first exhibition day will have a special program aiming to reveal more about the work of contemporary artists in all its diversity and create favorable environment for sharing experience and building partner relationships between participants and visitors of the exhibition.

All visitors will be able to get acquainted with the authors, inspire by the diversity of presented works, and  purchase any of the exhibits from authors, but also to participate in a program of events: workshops, exhibition tours, lectures on art.
