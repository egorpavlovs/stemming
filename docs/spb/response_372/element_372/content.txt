                                  No matter what the purpose of your trip, you should know that doors of hotel "1913" are open for our guests 365 days a year and 24 hours a day.Hotel "1913" is glad to offer you variety  accomodation: from simple room category "economic" to  luxury two-room suite with original pictures from private collection. Regardless of your choice in each of them you’ll  feel the cosiness and warmth with which it was created.

Comfortable mini-hotel "1913 year"  is located in the heart of the historic center of St.Petersburg. You can reach on foot the city’s main attractions, such as: St. Isaac’s Cathedral, Palace Square, Admiralty. Metro station "Sadovaya" and "Sennaya ploschad’" are also in close proximity. Convenient transport connections allow easy access to the airport and railway stations.Fans of opera and ballet will easily get to the Mariinsky Theatre, because it located just 600 meters from the mini-hotel "1913".Author’s interior design rooms, modern facilities, convenient location and superior service make the mini-hotel "1913 year" an ideal place in Saint-Petersburg for travelers and business people.
