                                  The State Hermitage Museum Official Hotel in Saint Petersburg offers to its guests the luxuriance of the historical interiors and high art of hospitality.  As well as the unique gateway to experience The State Hermitage Museum in St Petersburg. The atmosphere of the residence of Russian Emperors and world-known museum finds its extension in the hotel rooms. The main goal of the concept was to create a link between the Hotel and the Museum, inviting the Guests into the twisted adventures.The State Hermitage Museum Official Hotel is located on Pravda street, 10- in a quiet historical center within the walking distance of the nearest subway stations “Vladimirskaya” and “Zvenigorodskaya” and numerous cultural and historical sites.Event halls and conference rooms- Rossi, Von Klenze, Stasov, Stackenshneider and others are distinguished by the diversity and exquisiteness of the interiors herewith meeting all the modern technical requirements.  The restaurant Catherine the Great and Michelangelo together with independent kitchens close to each events hall ensure the highest level of the service of the banquets and conferences. In Autumn 2015 The State Hermitage Museum Official Hotel will celebrate its two-year anniversary. Meanwhile a number of important events of the international and Russian level with the participants of the professionals of the top ranked industries, representatives of business and culture were conducted in the hotel. For example session of the International Advisory Council of the museum’s directors, the 2nd International Scientific and educational conference «Biglyev Readings», «Islamic thought in the 21st century: the unity of tradition and renewal», the event of the SAMSUNG company, annual bank meetings, anniversary celebrations of corporations and others took place in the hotel.
