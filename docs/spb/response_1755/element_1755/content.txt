                Saint Petersburg’s brand was discussed at the conference
            
            April 24 2015
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                The conference “Saint Petersburg – city-brand” organized by the newspaper “Vedomosti” was held in the State Hermitage Museum Official Hotel on April 22. Representatives of the Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau visited the event.

The city-brand always promotes an idea, which unites citizens and becomes a center of tourist attraction. The brand is the foundation of city development’s strategy, its positioning in Russia and abroad.

Chairman of the Committee for Tourism Development Inna Shalyto told that Saint Petersburg took up 1st rating position in Russia and 9st in Europe according to the tourist attraction. General director of the ExpoForum International Sergey Voronkov highlighted the importance of the increasing business tourist flow. Even now one of the most large exhibition centre in Europe plans to host 7-8 global international congresses per year. Activity of the Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau will contribute to the realization of this plan.

Chief of the excursion and lecture department of the Russian Museum Valery Achunov, Director of the Museum Complex the State Museum St Issaac’s Cathedral Nikolai Burov, President of the PMI Corporation Evgeniy Finkelshteyn, Executive Director of the Lakhta Center Alexander Bobkov, Public Relations Director of the FC Zenit Zhanna Dembo, General Director of the Imperial Porcelain Tatyana Tyilevich and many others spoke in the framework of the conference.

Speakers and delegates arrived at a conclusion toward the end of the conference that Saint Petersburg’s brand was already created. Importantly, the city should be open and friendly. In this case tourists will arrive and will be glad to return here.
