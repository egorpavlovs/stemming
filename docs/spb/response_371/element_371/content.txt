                                  Hotel LDM-hotel 3* - star hotel in the centre of Saint Petersburg, located in the historic city on the banks of the river Malaya Nevka. The hotel offers views of the Petrograd side, Krestovsky island and the Gulf of Finland.

The hotel is located a 10-minute walk from the metro station "Chkalovskaya" and a 15-minute walk from the metro station "Petrogradskaya". The hotel has 232 rooms of different categories: from economy to luxurious Junior Suite.
