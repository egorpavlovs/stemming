                Dentist’s office at the exhibition Dental-Expo St. Petersburg
            
            October 28 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

The International Exhibition of Equipment, Instruments, Materials and Services for Dentistry Dental-Expo St. Petersburg is held in CEC Expoforum on 27-29 October.  

Companies from Russia and Finland, Sweden and Denmark, Brazil and the USA, Germany, South Korea, Israel and other countries take part in the event. Modern dental equipment, tools, materials and services for dentistry are collected on one venue. More than 3000 specialists visited the exhibition last year. According to the statistics of the organizers, 66% of visitors purchased products or services, 54% of exhibitors found new business partners or clients. Traditionally the exhibition is accompanied by saturated business program: conferences, symposia, master-classes, presentations.
