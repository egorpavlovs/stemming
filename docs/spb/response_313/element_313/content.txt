                Global Exhibitions Day 2016
            
            June 2 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

8 June 2016 was announced as Global Exhibitions Day.  ‪#‎GED16, ‪#‎globalExhibitionsday

“The project aims at drawing public attention to the exhibition industry, and showing the key support role of exhibitions for business development and international cooperation. I invite every player of our industry to get involved in the campaign”, says Sergey Alexeev, UFI President, RUEF President.

At the same time, Global Exhibitions Day is also meant to be a celebration of the people working in the exhibitions industry, showing the excitement and career opportunities that the industry has to offer.
