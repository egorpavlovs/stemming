                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau will tell about the results of the work for 2015
            
            December 2 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau will hold the annual event in Renessans Saint Petersburg Baltic Hotel on December 4. 100 top managers of industry companies will take part in the meeting.

The aims of the event are summarizing the results of the meeting industry in Saint Petersburg for 2015, searching of points for business growth, discussing an effective cooperation. The Chairwoman of the Committee for Tourism Development Inna Shalyto and CEO of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Anna Chetvergova will tell about the results of the work for 2015 and the planned projects for the next year.

Managing director of TMF Dialogue Мarketing Germany & India Johanna Fisher will become the special guest and the speaker. Her company is the official partner of the International Congress and Conference Association. Mrs. Fisher is admitted as the high-level expert in marketing and meetings industry. She is the honorary representative of European City Marketing Academy, the international consultant of convention and tourism bureaus, the headliner of business programs of IMEX, IBTM World, Convene. Her speeches are characterized by the big emotional impact and interactive way of the work with the audience. She will hold the workshop “Creating a “smart city” Convention Bureau” on December 4. The role of convention bureau and effective practices for attracting of events, coopetition and international relations, target groups and ambassador programs, communication strategy of destinations will be included into the program of the workshop.

Matchmaking will be at disposal of the participants of the meeting. Renessans Saint Petersburg Baltic Hotel, Domina Prestige St. Petersburg Hotel, Sunny Plantation, R&C Market Research Company, Mikenopa и EGO Translating Company are among partners of the event.
