                    Park Inn by Radisson Pribaltiyskaya
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Park Inn by Radisson Pribaltiyskaya is the largest congress hotel in Saint-Petersburg. The hotel is conveniently located on the Gulf of Finland. Our hotel is about 5 kilometres from historic and cultural attractions that include the Hermitage Museum and the Peter and Paul Fortress. It takes about 25 minutes via public transport from the city centre.

Pulkovo Airport is located 25 kilometres away (30 minutes using the new highway – Western Rapid Diameter/ZSD), and other nearby transportation options include the Primorskaya Metro Station and the Moskovsky Railway Station. Business travelers enjoy close access to LenExpo for conventions and business events. The hotel is located near the north of the city, close to many business and industrial centers.Enjoy your stay in one of our 1184 rooms, including 268 Superior Rooms, 40 Junior Suites and 4 Duplex Suites. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, satellite TV and free wireless high-speed internet.

The hotel offers a wide range of conference facilities designed for events of any format and scale. 26 conference rooms that can accommodate from 10 to 1500 people with a spacious foyer.Experience new culinary discoveries in our restaurants! Choose to eat at the Park Inn restaurant with 800 seats (European cuisine), a cozy lounge bar where you can enjoy a dessert or a cup of coffee at any time of the day or night. The Park Inn Restaurant offers a buffet of hot and cold items and juice for group tourists.Your wedding is one of the most memorable experiences in your life. Whether you're looking to hold a magnificent banquet, an elegant wedding celebration with friends and family or an extravagant soiree - your wedding is in good hands with our professional event staff. We can also help you organize your own beautiful and unique outdoor ceremony to register your marriage.

We are happy to offer a wonderful wedding package for the perfect day: a banquet menu, hotel room for the bride and groom, breakfast for two people, a fruit basket in the room and a bottle of Sparkling Russian wine as a compliment from the hotel.Park Inn by Radisson Pribaltiyskaya offers a wide range of conference facilities designed for events of any format and scale.

The facility features:

• 26 conference rooms that can accommodate from 10 to 1500 people

• over 2000 square meters of exhibition space

• the Blue Conference Room is a unique, multi-functional space that can accommodate up to 1500 guests

• mobile, soundproof walls can separate the Blue Room into five completely different rooms. Up to five events can be held in the room at once

• a spacious foyer on the second floor to welcome guests, have cocktails and coffee breaks

• restaurants and banquet rooms for business lunches and dinners

• individual planning for every event

• conference equipment and wireless internet

• new, comfortable and ergonomic furniture in all conference rooms

• outdoor and indoor parking
