                    White nights of hepatology 2018
                
                
                    
                        
                            31 may - 1 jun 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                        Crowne Plaza St.Petersburg Airport
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://congress-ph.ru/event/WNH2018
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    The X Jubilee international conference "White nights of hepatology 2018"Traditionally, the leading Russian and foreign experts in hepatology will take part in the conference. Invited speakers, world-famous scientists, professors will consider a wide range of issues related to antiviral therapy of chronic viral hepatitis, as well as diagnosis and treatment of fatty liver disease.The speakers and guests will have an opportunity to attend a specialized exhibition at the conference.Official language of conference - English
