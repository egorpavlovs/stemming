                    Specialized exhibition of hunting weapons, tackle, equipment, and appliances for hunting and fishing. Hunting and fishing. Spring 2017
                
                
                    
                        
                            23 - 26 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://expoforum-center.ru/en/calendar/ohota-i-rybolovstvo-2017/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Specialized exhibition of hunting weapons, tackle, equipment, and appliances for hunting and fishing. Hunting and fishing. Spring 2017 </strong>will &nbsp;held at CEC EXPOFORUM. &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong>Exhibition profile:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>technical means (autosnowsledges; launches, boats, canoes, kayaks, rafts; bicycles, motorcycles, ski-sledges);</li>	<li>unting stock (guns and accessories for weapons cleaning; sport accoutrement for firing practice; items for guns and ammunition storage);</li>	<li>fishing stock (nets, drag-nets, spinning and float rods; reels, seines, spoon-baits, fishing line, etc.; sport equipment for submarine hunting);</li>	<li>accompanying equipment and products (tents, awnings, umbrellas; means of fish and meat processing and storage; tourist and sport stock; cartographic and other polygraphic production; sport and special clothes for hunters and fishers);</li>	<li>equipment for Fish-breeding;</li>	<li>fishing and Hunting Tours.</li></ul>
