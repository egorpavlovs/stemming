                Ecology of big city is discussed in Saint Petersburg 
            
            April 6 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

International forum Ecology of Big City and the exhibition and conference Municipal housing complex of Russia are held in CEC Expoforum in Saint Petersburg on April 6-8.

About 120 companies from Russia, Belorussia, Czech Republic, Germany, France present innovative facilities and equipment in the sphere of waste management, rational water use, environmental monitoring, information support of nature conservation activities, environmental technologies used in construction. Within the events specialists will discuss topics on repair and maintenance of roads and road facilities and techniques greenery and land improvement of city territories.

International forum Ecology of Big City is the largest convention and exhibition event in the end of environmental protection, nature conservation, as well as environmental protection equipment, technologies and services in the North-West of Russia. The Ecology of Big City International Forum includes a business program, an exhibition program and business tours to nature conservation facilities of Saint Petersburg.
