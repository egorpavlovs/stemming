                                  The Aliot hotel *** (72 rooms - one Junior suit, 71 standarts) was opened in 1994 in Kirovsky district of Saint Petersburg. This respectable place successfully combines business rhythm and cosy atmosphere. Architectural design and finishing work are made by Austrian and Italian specialists. Our guests can enjoy a beautiful view of the Northern capital out of the hotel windows: golden domes of St. Isaac Cathedral, magnificent blue of the Gulf of Finland, ship masts and crane arms in the Sea Port. The hotel is 30 minute drive from Pulkovo airport, and it takes the same time to get to the centre of the city. Close location to the airport, trade and timber ports and at the same time lack of rush and noise on the roads is one of the advantages of Aliot hotel.
