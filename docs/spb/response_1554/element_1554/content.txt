                    Spring Flora
                
                
                    
                        
                            29 mar - 2 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Landscape design
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.sivel.spb.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The exhibition is supported by the Office for the development of horticulture and gardening of St. Petersburg and the Union of gardeners of Russia.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>

<p>Spring-time to prepare for the long-awaited season, is realized as when it is necessary to spend serious efforts to fall into specific material values-the gifts of nature. All this and presented at the exhibition &quot;Spring Flora&quot;. The exhibition presents a wide range of products for gardeners: seeds, seedlings, flower products, fertilizers, greenhouses, gardening tools, honey and products of farms, comfortable clothing and footwear for gardeners, consumer goods.</p>

<p>The bulk of the exhibitors represent private nurseries and growers-fans (about 70%) are also involved farms (up to 15%), companies offering services in landscape design and landscaping of the lot (up to 10%), as well as research institutes, specialists, in addition to selling a selection of planting material, conduct seminars and give everyone advice on machinery exhibition exhibitors are present from St. Petersburg, Novgorod, Moscow, Pskov, Orel, Leningrad oblast (up to 80%) , Tula, Tver, Moscow regions. This is due to the so-called &quot;zone of risky agriculture&quot;, our Northwest. Geography of firms selling honey, presented much wider: Altai, Bashkortostan, Karelia, Novgorod and Voronezh area.</p>

<p>The exhibition enjoys deserved demand in and gathers around 150 exhibitors.</p>
