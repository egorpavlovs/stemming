                                  The Taleon Imperial Hotel is the only privately owned palace hotel in St Petersburg located in the palace of 18th century. The hotel offers 89 guestrooms of different categories, 2 restaurants, 2 bars, a café, a spa center with and 7 gala halls and lounges for banquets and conferences.What makes the Taleon Imperial Hotel in St Petersburg so different from anything else in the city is the remarkable variety of our historic interiors; and a service as individual as we are.We have rooms for every occasion: a lavish wedding, a large-scale conference, a corporate seminar, product presentation, board meeting or celebratory banquet.Taleon Imperial Hotel is a member of the Leading Hotels of the World and Virtuoso. In year 2011 the Condé Nast Traveler Magazine named the Taleon Imperial Hotel among 100 best hotels in the world.
