                General director of Convention Bureau spoke at the conference “Actual problems of MICE industry’s development in modern times”
            
            March 30 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                On March 26 and 27 XI International Research and Practice Conference “Actual problems of MICE industry’s development in modern times” was held in Saint Petersburg.

This professional discussion about the major problems of MICE industry’s development was traditionally initiated by Saint Petersburg State University of Economics.

Heads of international hotel chains, companies and booking systems were invited as speakers. Among them there were Chief of Strategy Development of Intercontinental Hotels Group in Russia, CIS, Baltic States and Scandinavia Alexey Korobkin, HR-Director of cluster Hilton Saint Petersburg Expoforum and Hampton by Hilton Saint Petersburg Expoforum Anna Kistochkina, Regional manager Russia, Ukraine and CIS Expedia Anton Kuznetsov and others.

General director of Convention Bureau Anna Chetvergova spoke in the framework of the session “Hotel business: actual issues of adaptation under conditions of economic instability”. She defined main functions of Convention Bureau and denoted key strategies of cooperation with the hotel industry in her presentation.

Formed discussion allowed participants to consider opportunities of MICE industry’s support on the part of Convention Bureau.

According to participants reviews this kind of communication format is very effective and brings practical usefulness.
