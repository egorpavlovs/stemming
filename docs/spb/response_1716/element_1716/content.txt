                    The 3rd International Specialised Exhibition «GAS ENGINE FUEL»
                
                
                    
                        
                            4 - 7 oct 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Energy. Energy efficiency
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.en.farexpo.ru/fuel/exhibition/about/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Exposition &laquo;Gas Engine Fuel&raquo;&nbsp;</strong>&mdash; is a unique project, realized in partnership with OOO &laquo;Gazprom Gazomotornoe toplivo&raquo;. The goal of this event is not only to demonstrate technological advances in gas engine fuel and the market development trends in Russia, but also to form strategies of this development in the open dialog of all stake holders.&nbsp;The exceptional advantage of this event is that the exhibitors will get an opportunity of getting the expert evaluation of the key figures of the gas engine fuel market in the Russian Federation.</p>

<p><strong>Thematic areas of the Exhibition:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Designing, constructing and equipping gas filling stations and automatic compressor station gas fillers</li>	<li>Gas Filling Stations and Columns</li>	<li>Equipment for fuel filling stations and gas filling stations</li>	<li>PC hardware and software of filling stations, modern communication and payment systems of filling stations</li>	<li>Travelling automotive gas filling stations</li>	<li>Conversion kits</li>	<li>Machinery and equipment using liquefied hydrocarbon gases, compressed natural gas and liquefied natural gas as engine fuels.</li>	<li>Automobile gas machinery diagnostic systems and instruments</li>	<li>Equipment for conversion centres reconstructing vehicles to use gas as an engine fuel, equipment for providing maintenance and service support</li></ul>
