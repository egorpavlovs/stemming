                Saint Petersburg International Gas Forum hosts 1500 delegates
            
            October 6 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg International Gas Forum is held in Expoforum on October 6-9. It is the high-level and UFI approved event.

1500 delegates from 27 countries take part in the forum. Top managers of the biggest oil and gas companies, representatives of the innovation centres and the projects institutes, experts, academics, heads of the specialized universities are among them. The results of the event will influence the formation of the global gas market. The program of the forum will cover global trends and state policy in the industry, priority gas projects. Delegates will pay special attention to the international experience in the development of offshore in the Arctic, ecological issues, the latest scientific elaborations in the field of gas processing.

The exposition unites on the area of 25 thousand sq.m. over 300 exhibitors and demonstrates perspective technologies, products, services, technics required for the gas industry. The forum is held jointly with the International Energy Forum which is dedicated to investments into energy industry, issues of safe production of high quality oil products, protection of pipelines from corrosion, hard-to- recover oil and gas resources development and other important topics.
