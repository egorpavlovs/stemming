                International Society for Intelligence Research Conference met in Saint Petersburg for the first time
            
            July 18 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                The  17th Annual Conference of   International Society for Intelligence Research was held in Saint Petersburg on the 15th-17th of July, where the leading experts in experimental psychology, neurosciences, genetics, pedagogics and other branches of science discussed problems of intellect andinterrelated topics. 120 participants from 20 countries took part in the Conference.

The Conference of   International Society for Intelligence Research is the key event for scientists all over the world, which research are about intelligence and its applications. In 2016, this event was held in Russia for the first time “Saint-Petersburg is the most fabulous city, which is very interesting for foreign tourists, including ISIR Conference participants, this fact doesn’t surprise, because Saint Petersburg is the most beautiful city in Russia”, – said one of the Conference organizers Sergey Malykh,  academcian secretary of Russian Academy of Education.

Also  this year The International Summer School was firstly organized before the Conference (on the 12th-14th of July),  due to  the Russian Science Foundation students and young scientists from different countries was able to participate for free.
