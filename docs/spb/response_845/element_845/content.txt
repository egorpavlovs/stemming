                    Dental-Expo St. Petersburg. 2018

                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.dentalexpo-spb.ru/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    Dental-Expo St. Petersburg is the largest on the number of participants and visitors autumn dental exhibition in the Northwest Russia. Over the years the exhibition gained recognition in the professional community as the event with the wide exposition, where experts exchange experience and information about updates.

Dental-Expo St. Petersburg is an effective platform for showcasing products and services to a wide range of prospective clients with the purpose of sales development, entering into the market of Northwest Russia, reinforcement of the corporate image, and building of brand awareness among stomatology specialists.

Traditionally a saturated business programme is held alongside the exhibition: conferences, symposia, master-classes of participating companies.
