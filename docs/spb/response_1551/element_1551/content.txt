                    6th Exhibition on country house construction Country Living
                
                
                    
                        
                            6 - 8 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Construction, Infrastructure
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.zagorodom-expo.com/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Country Living exhibition</strong> opens a construction season simultaneously with active search of customers and conclusion of cottage construction contracts.</p>

<p>Exhibitors include leading Russian manufacturers and distributors of pre-fabricated houses and products and materials for building and installing interior fittings in country houses. The exhibition provides participants with the opportunity for direct contact with private customers who are interested in solving practical problems in the construction and arrangement of a country house.</p>

<p>Exhibition profile:</p>

<ul>	<li>Designing and building country houses and garden structures</li>	<li>Building materials and tools</li>	<li>Finishing materials</li>	<li>Engineering systems for country houses</li>	<li>Fences, gates and enclosure systems</li>	<li>Design and interiors for country houses</li>	<li>Products for country rest</li>	<li>Landscape design</li>	<li>Countryside real estate</li></ul>
