                                  Four Seasons Hotel Lion Palace St. Petersburg is the city’s true luxury hotel.Just two blocks from Hermitage Museum, the House with the Lions began life as the palace of Princess Lobanova-Rostovskaya in 1820. Its distinctive layout adjoins St. Isaac’s Square. The palace has been carefully restored, and boasts several heritage protected areas.Four Seasons Hotel Lion Palace St. Petersburg offers 183 spacious and comfortable guest rooms, including 26 suites.Percorso, restaurant of authentic Italian cuisine, features the show kitchen and great wine collection. Sintoho, Asian restaurant, features a sushi-bar and a dining room with a teppanyaki table.One of the palace’s courtyards has become a glass-roofed winter garden – home to the Tea Lounge. Xander Bar, named after Russian Tsar Alexander I, boasts a double-faced fire place and amazing views.The hotel also features a unique Luceo SPA, with 8 treatment rooms, 24-hour fitness center, and spectacular pool under a sky lit glass dome.

                                                The Hermitage is a 3-minute walk from the Four Seasons Lion Palace Hotel, and it is 400 meters from the lively Nevsky Prospekt. A walk to the Mariinsky Theater takes 15 minutes.Moskovsky Train Station is 3 km from the hotel, and Pulkovo International Airport is 15 km away.
