                    6th exhibition-fair of the goods and services for people of elder age "GOLDEN AGE. SECRETS OF LONGEVITY"
                
                
                    
                        
                            27 - 31 jan 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Consumer goods
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://sivel.spb.ru/?page_id=29/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>6th exhibition-fair of the goods and services for people of elder&nbsp;</strong><strong>age &quot;GOLDEN AGE. SECRETS OF LONGEVITY&quot; </strong>will take place at&nbsp;Cultural and exhibition center &quot;EURASIA&quot; from 27 to 31d of January 2017.&nbsp;</p>

<p>This exhibition social project designed for senior citizens, this project includes an extensive exhibition, cultural and educational programs .. Exhibiting companies and experts will present their products and share their experiences. In terms of business events: talks and workshops with the heads of the Department of Pension Fund of Russia in St. Petersburg and the Leningrad Region, the City medical insurance company, consultations with leading oncologists, homeopaths, herbalists.</p>

<p><strong>MAIN SECTIONS OF THE EXHIBITION:</strong></p>

<p>Health products;<br />Fashion for men age sensitive;<br />Products to create a home-like atmosphere;<br />Masterclasses from designers, stylists, makeup artists, hairdressers for the delicate age</p>
