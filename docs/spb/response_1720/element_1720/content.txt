                    St. Petersburg Fashion Week

                        Culture and art
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://spbfashionweek.com/about
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Launched by Fashion Syndicate St.Petersburg initiative, the official<strong> St. Petersburg Fashion Week </strong>encompasses the most dynamic and professional resources of St. Petersburg fashion industry segment. St. Petersburg Fashion Week (SPbFW) is a unique project that brings together:</p>

<ul>	<li>worldwide principles of organization and arrangement of professional fashion week</li>	<li>powerful and most up-to-date Internet resource - social networking with loyal audience exceeding half a million people</li>	<li>professional media</li>	<li>mobile apps developed with Fashion Syndicate St.Petersburg support.</li></ul>

<p>The outstanding information coverage by social networks is provided by SPBFW team whose experience &nbsp;has been demonstrated during previous fashion events. Total social media audience exceeds 500 thousand people.<br />Fashion Syndicate St.Petersburg active support allows arranging professional event and provides access to reputable thematic editions such as DRESS CODE, whose readership today exceed 170 000 people. The mobile applications developed by IT Department of SpbFW have no analogs in St. Petersburg.</p>
