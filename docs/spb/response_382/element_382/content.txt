                                  Hotel "Moscow" is situated in the historical center of Saint-Petersburg, near Nevsky Prospekt, on the Neva embankment.

Metro station is in the hotel bulding. In 20-25 minutes you will be able to reach major historical attractions: Hermitage, Admiralty, Russian museum.

The rooms overlooking the architectural ensemble of Alexander Nevsky Lavra and the Neva River. During the summer white nights you can observe retraction of the Alexander Nevsky bridge.
