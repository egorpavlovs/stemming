                    Petersburg International Automotive Conference (PIAC)
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Cars, Bikes, and Motorbikes
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://autopromrussia.ru/en/index/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>Petersburg International Automotive Conference (PIAC)</strong>&nbsp;is a business platform for improving the automotive industry and market.</p>

<p>The conference concerns the matters of international experience in the formation and development of clusters, professional training, legislative framework, certification, logistics, component supply-chain management and customs regulation. Participants will also consider the major trends on the global automotive market, strategies of leading OES, and the requirements imposed on OEM market players, etc.</p>
