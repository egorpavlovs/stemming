                    10th International exhibition of equipment, instruments, materials and services for dentistry Dental-Expo St. Petersburg
                
                
                    
                        
                            24 - 26 oct 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.dentalexpo-spb.ru/?lang=en-GB
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>&quot;Dental-Expo St. Petersburg&quot;</strong>&nbsp;is the main autumn event for stomatologists, the deserved recognition in professional community as action with the extensive exposition,&nbsp;rich with technical novelties and innovative ideas. Each year&nbsp;the exhibition shows the high level of the organization and representative structure of participants. The companies will present wide&nbsp;range of the innovative stomatologic equipment, stomatologic materials, medicines, means of hygiene in&nbsp;oral cavity.</p>

<p><strong>Sections of the&nbsp;exhibition:</strong></p>

<p>&bull; Stomatologic equipment and tools<br />&bull; Stomatologic materials<br />&bull; Dental equipment and tools<br />&bull; Auxiliary materials for dental laboratories<br />&bull; Systems and tools for dentalny implantology<br />&bull; Materials and tools for bone plasticity and maxillofacial surgery<br />&bull; Orthodontic production<br />&bull; Surgical equipment, tools<br />&bull; Stomatologic medicines<br />&bull; The equipment, tools, materials for disinfection and sterilization<br />&bull; Dentalny anesthesiology<br />&bull; Disposable materials, expendables<br />&bull; Medical clothes<br />&bull; Furniture medical<br />&bull; Means of hygiene of an oral cavity<br />&bull; Stomatologic services<br />&bull; Information technologies in stomatology<br />&bull; Diagnostic equipment, accessories</p>
