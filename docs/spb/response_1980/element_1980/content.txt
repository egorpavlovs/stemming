                    All-Russian specialized exhibition forum "Ecological Safety"
                
                
                    
                        
                            18 - 21 nov 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Ecology and Environment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The All-Russian forum &quot;Ecological Safety&quot;&nbsp;is the complex project uniting intellectual potential, organizational opportunities, appliances for drawing attention to environmental problems. Action purpose: studying the best practices in area of ecology,&nbsp;providing advance of effective innovative technologies and increase of ecological safety of the population.</p>

<p>Within action there takes place the interregional specialized exhibition of the equipment, technologies of water purification, air, processing of waste.</p>
