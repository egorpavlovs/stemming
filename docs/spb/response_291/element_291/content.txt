                World experts in the field of underground urbanism for the first time gathered in St. Petersburg 
            
            September 19 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                For the first time St. Petersburg hosted the 15th World Conference of Associated research Centers for the Urban Underground Space ACUUS. Dmitry Medvedev, Chairman of the Government, sent greeting to the participants, Georgy Poltavchenko, St. Petersburg Governor pronounced welcoming speech at the opening of the forum. The event took place in the Park Inn by Radisson Pribaltiyskaya hotel on September, 12-15.  More than 500 representatives from 38 countries and 17 Russian regions attended the Conference, including 11 official delegations.

The ACUUS has been held for more than 30 years in different cities around the world and supported at a high government level. The event has been held successfully in such cities as Seoul, Singapore, Shenzhen, Athens, Turin, Montreal, Paris and others.

The conference ACUUS 2016 is a discussion platform for world-renowned experts in the field of underground urbanism, investors, representatives of authorities and social agencies. Key topic of the conference is ‘Underground urbanization as a necessary condition for sustainable development of cities’. The experts discussed a wide range of issues relating to the planning, design, construction and using of urban underground space within the panel sessions.

More than 117 reports of underground space development leading experts were presented at the Conference. The specialized exhibition for underground construction and design and poster exhibition allowed specialists to acquaint with new materials, equipment and technologies used in the construction and equipping of underground structures, and with underground construction projects, including a number of existing and planned facilities of underground construction.

The 15th World Conference ACUUS in Russia focused the attention of the authorities and public in general to the problems of development of urban underground infrastructure. As Sergey Alpatov, the CEO, Association of Underground Builders, member of the Board of ACUUS  said, “exchange of experience and demonstration of Russian and foreign specialists achievements in the field of underground space development allowed to demonstrate the advantages of underground urbanization and new opportunities to ensure the comfort and security of residence in modern cities.
