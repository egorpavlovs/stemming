                    International exhibition “SPRING-SUMMER FAIR”
                
                
                    
                        
                            20 - 23 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Consumer goods
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://exposummer.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>International exhibition&nbsp;<strong>&ldquo;SPRING-SUMMER FAIR&rdquo;</strong>&nbsp;is a spectacular event, timed to the beginning of summer season. At the exhibition, visitors can not only see, but also to buy the actual novelties of the spring-summer 2016&nbsp;season.</p>

<p><br />Every year the exhibition is visited by more than&nbsp;<strong>35 thousand customers</strong>&nbsp;wishing to purchase goods of summer range, renew wardrobe, decorate houses and buy food.</p>

<p><strong>Expositions:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>	<p><strong>Main Arena</strong>&nbsp;&ndash; clothes, shoes, hats, leather goods, articles of linen and cotton, designer items, souvenirs and handmade accessories, products for children, educational games, books, jewelry, ornaments, jewelry, household goods, kitchenware, health products.</p>	</li>	<li>	<p><strong>Foyer</strong>&nbsp;&ndash; farm products, meat and dairy products, meat and fish specialties, pastries, cakes, soft drinks, tea, honey, honey products, dried fruits, nuts, spices, foods for youth, beauty and health</p>	</li></ul>

<p><strong>Special exposition &ldquo;LINEN PALETTE OF SUMMER&quot;</strong>:&nbsp;</p>

<ul>	<li>linen clothes for the whole family</li>	<li>headwear of linen</li>	<li>linen in the interior</li></ul>
