                    The Fifth International Conference of the CIS Countries will be held at the Institute of Silicate Chemistry of  RAS, St. Petersburg (ISChem RAS).Sections of the conference:

Theoretical aspects of sol-gel process.Sol-gel synthesis and research of nanoparticles.Sol-gel films, coatings and membranes.Hybrid inorganic and organic-inorganic sol-gel materials.Xerogels, glass and bulk ceramic materials synthesized by sol-gel method.Sol-gel materials for environmental protection, agriculture and biomedicine applications.Applications of sol-gel science and technology.
