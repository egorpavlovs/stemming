                Convention Bureau will participate in IBTM World
            
            November 13 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau will participate in the leading global event for the meetings and events industry IBTM World. The exhibition will take place in Barcelona on November 17-19.  

The main goal of the participation is holding meetings with potential event organizers from corporate and non-corporate sector. At present the packed calendar of conversations is composed with the system of pre-scheduled business appointments. 10 presentations will be held during the exhibition. Their listeners will receive fresh information about ample opportunities of hosting business events and holding incentive tours in Saint Petersburg. The special focus will be on support program generated by Convention Bureau for organizers. The themed party in favor of the World Hockey Championship will be held on November 17.  In 2016 Saint Petersburg will be one of the host cities for this sport tournament.  

Leading Saint Petersburg companies covering all sectors of MICE-industry will work at the booth of the city, too. In total 3,000 exhibitors representing more than 150 countries will take part in IBTM World. List of participants includes destinations, venues, event services and other industry suppliers.
