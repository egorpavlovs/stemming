                                  "Nevsky Forum" hotel is a combination of traditions and modern business life of St. Petersburg. Unique location of the hotel is convenient both for travelers and businessmen (who come to St. Petersburg for business meetings, conferences, and seminars). The hotel is situated in the very heart of the city, on Nevsky prospect, only a few steps away from the metro station "Mayakovskaya".
