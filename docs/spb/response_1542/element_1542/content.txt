                    Exhibition "Planet of rest. World of outdoor"
                
                
                    
                        
                            22 - 24 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://travelexhibition.ru/calendar/adventureplanet2016.html
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Exhibition &laquo;Adventure Planet. Outdoor Universe&raquo;, devoted to outdoor tourism and active rest. Customers a unique event for the city will be able to familiarize yourself with the types of active leisure (outdoor-tourism).</p>

<p>&nbsp;In fact, the exhibition presents all the existing at the moment, the kinds of outdoor recreation, affecting even sports and tourist fashion, as well as paying attention to the latest technology in the form of useful gadgets for travelers.</p>

<p>Experts participating in the exhibition will help interested visitors to understand the selection of necessary equipment and gear for extreme and active holidays, clothes and shoes for travel.</p>
