                                  Radisson Royal Hotel, perfectly located on the Nevsky Prospect, the very centre of St. Petersburg, is only a short distance from all major cultural and historical world famous attractions.  Dating back to 18th century, the building housing the hotel has a colorful history. The interior of the building was completely reconstructed in 2001, and the original facade has been restored to its former glory, retaining original features, many of which are almost 300 years old.All of our guest rooms meet the highest standards of comfort.Radisson Royal Hotel, with its 5 boardrooms and 3 meeting rooms, offers comfortable meeting facilities in the city centre. Our conference rooms are air-conditioned and equipped according to the highest standards.
