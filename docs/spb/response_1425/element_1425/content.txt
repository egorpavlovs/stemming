                    XIII Saint-Petersburg International Book Salon
                
                
                    
                        
                            25 - 28 may 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Publishing
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.spbbooksalon.ru/en/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>XIII Book Salon</strong> will be hold from 25 to 28 May 2017 &nbsp;Mikhailovsky Manege (Winter Stadium) and surrounding street space - Manezh Square, Malaya Sadovaya &nbsp;and Maple streets.&nbsp;The organizers of the Book Fair are the Government of St. Petersburg and the Russian Book Union. Book Fair is supported by the Federal Agency for Press and Mass Communications. Book Fair&#39;s Operator is &nbsp;ANO &quot;Petersburg media projects&quot;.</p>

<p><strong>Book Salon</strong> consists of two exhibitions: the main one located in the Mikhailovsky Manege, and street, located adjacent to the Manezh Maple Street.&nbsp;</p>

<p>By functional orientation <strong>Book Salon</strong> can be divided into two parts &ndash; the exhibition and fair, cultural and business. In the first publishing and bookselling organizations present visitors the widest range of books and related products, and trade. The cultural and business program of the Book Fair attended by officials, heads of publishing houses, booksellers, networks, Russian and international experts, writers, representatives of various funds supporting publishing projects, representatives of library and education community, cultural figures.</p>
