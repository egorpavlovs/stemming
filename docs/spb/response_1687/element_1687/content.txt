                    The international exhibition-fair "New Year Gift"
                
                
                    
                        
                            15 - 18 dec 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Consumer goods
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.expogift.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The international exhibition-fair &quot;New Year Gift&quot;</strong> is the biggest New Year exhibition in Russia (according to the audit of Russian Union of Exhibitions and Fairs). Traditionally, the exhibition includes consumer goods, gifts, New Year paraphernalia, accessories and jewelry, health and beauty products, household products, VIP gifts, products for the New Year&#39;s feast.</p>

<p><strong>The exhibition &quot;New Year Gift&quot;</strong> is located on two levels of the SCC. Annually over 700 exhibitors and over 60 000 customers. Held since 2004.</p>
