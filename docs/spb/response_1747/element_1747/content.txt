                Ideas of World Congress of Landscape Architects will be realized in Saint Petersburg
            
            June 18 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg hosted 52nd World Congress of the International Federation of Landscape Architects on June 10-12. The event moulded the city into the trendsetter of world landscape architecture.

More than 1200 specialists from 74 countries came to participate at the congress. The main theme sounded as the History of the Future. The most reputable experts spoke about it.

Special events were held within the congress all over the city. Delegates planted a grass and trees and laid out a flower bed with IFLA emblem at the Palace Square. The Imperial Gardens of Russia International Festival showed the exhibition Gardens of the Silk Route. The Flower Festival decorated the Alexander Garden. Participants of the congress laid out the park Parade of Gardens and planted seven elm trees which German nursery garden Lorberg gifted the city. Sport initiatives were included into the congress program. Saint Petersburg became the end stop of the international green bike ride Trees connect through nine European countries.

As expected ideas of World Congress of Landscape Architects would receive the practical realization in the sphere of upgrading, landscape architecture, ecology in Saint Petersburg.
