        Calendar of events
    
    

                
                
                    XVIII International Coal Preparation Congress
                
                
                    
                        
                            28 jun - 1 jul 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://icpc-2016.com/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>The International Coal Preparation Congress is held triennially and it is the place where delegates of all coal producing countries discuss the prospects of the branch technical development.&nbsp; Among its main goals is the experience exchange of the implementation of the latest science achievements at the specialized enterprises, agreement on the necessary environmental measures which enable to solve ecological problems, establishment of new business contacts.</p>

<p>At different times the forum was hosted in such cities as Paris, Liege, Pittsburgh, Tokyo, Johannesburgh and others. In 1979 it was held in Donetsk, in 2013 - in Istanbul. During the solemn closing ceremony the authority of Congress-2016 preparation was transferred to the representatives of the Russian party.&nbsp;</p>
