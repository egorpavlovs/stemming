                     21 International Conference on Photochemical Conversion and Storage of Solar Energy 
                
                
                    
                        
                            25 - 29 jul 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        Holliday Inn and Staybridge Moskovskie vorota
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.ips21-spb.com/ru/ips
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <address>The aim of the conference is to cover recent developments in the areas of the solar energy conversion and storage. The conference program will consist of Plenary Lectures, Key-Lectures, and Oral Communications complemented by Poster Sessions. We also plan to organize the summer school for the students and young researchers whose research activity is devoted to the areas covered by the Conference.<br />This IPS-21 Conference will provide a versatile platform for formal and informal discussions with colleagues both from academia and industry, and foster new collaborations and partnerships.<br />The conference will cover the following topics:&nbsp;<br />Solar to Electricity Conversion (Photovoltaics/DSSCs/Perovskite SCs/Organic polymer SCs )<br />Solar to Chemical Energy Conversion (Solar fuel/Water splitting/CO2 reduction)<br />Artificial Photosynthesis<br />Photocatalysis<br />New Photoactive Materials<br />Mechanistic Studies, Engineering, Modelling</address>
