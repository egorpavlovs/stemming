                The Eurasian Economic Commission supports Russian International Energy Forum 2016
            
            May 17 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Russian International Energy Forum will be held in CEC Expoforum on May 17-20.

Forum is an effective platform for dialogue between the leaders of the fuel and energy complex and equipment manufacturers, service providers and technology developers.

This year the Eurasian Economic Commission participated in the forming of agenda and experts. The membership of the co-organizers of special events is significantly expanded. One of the key events of the business program will be a discussion on "Energy integration of member States of the Eurasian Economic Union: perspectives and conditions for the formation of a common energy market", the initiator and organizer of which will be the Eurasian Economic Commission and subordinate structures.

The unique advantage of RIEF is the consolidation of congress and exhibition programs. Exhibitors as experts are actively involved in specialized congress events that form the basis of the business program - technical discussions, workshops, excursions to the enterprises and objects of fuel and energy complex of the region. Jointly with the Forum, the International Specialized Exhibition ENERGETIKA and ELEKTROTECHNIKA will be held.
