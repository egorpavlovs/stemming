                                  Polo Regatta Hotel is located in the Sea Passenger Terminal on Vasilyevsky Island, near Saint Petersburg’s historical centre. It is just 2 minutes' walk from the main exhibition centre LenExpo.The hotel has 130 rooms of different categories with private bathrooms, telephone and a TV. The hotel's restaurant each morning serves a buffet breakfast.Polo Regatta Hotel is just a 10-minute drive away from the Dvortsovaya Square and the Hermitage Museum.The hotel has free parking.
