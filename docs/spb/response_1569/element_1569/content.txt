                    International specialised exhibition "Services for industrial enterprises" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            14 - 16 mar 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://en.ptfair.ru/exhibition/services/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>A modern industrial enterprise is a complicated mechanism interacting with different servicing organisations and bodies responsible for oversight and supervision. This is the main focus of the &quot;<strong>Services for industrial enterprises&quot;&nbsp;</strong>exhibition. It is aimed at assisting its participants &ndash; i.e. companies rendering services for industrial organizations - in search for new customers. Its objective concerning attendees &ndash; i.e. industrial enterprises &ndash; is to resolve the issue of choosing the most reliable service providers and business partners.</p>

<p>The exhibition is part of the<strong> Petersburg Technical Fair</strong>.</p>

<p><strong>&quot;Services for industrial enterprises&quot; sections:</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Small-scale and piece production. Cusom production of components</li>	<li>Enterprise management and development services</li>	<li>Automation, software, CAD, CAM</li>	<li>Industrial and on-the-job safety, technical control</li>	<li>Recycling of raw materials</li>	<li>Unmarketable products, production residues</li>	<li>Certification, licensing</li>	<li>Industrial cleaning</li>	<li>Storage</li>	<li>Logistics</li>	<li>Leasing</li>	<li>Consulting</li>	<li>R&amp;D</li></ul>
