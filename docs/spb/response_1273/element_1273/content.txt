                                  The hotel offers accommodation for groups of schoolchildren, students and for those who are interested in economical accommodation in St. Petersburg. It offers: standard two, three, four-bed rooms with amenities on the block, rooms with amenities in the room, restaurant and evening cafe on the ground floor of the hotel, equipped conference room for 30 seats.
