                    Exhibition of food products and drinks InterFood St. Petersburg 2017
                
                
                    
                        
                            19 - 21 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Agriculture
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.interfood-expo.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>InterFood St. Petersburg</strong> is an effective business platform for direct contacts of producers and suppliers with representatives of wholesale trade, independent stores, retail chains and public catering enterprises of the Northwest Russia, which are interested in expanding and updating the range of food and drinks.</p>

<p>Exhibition profile:</p>

<ul>	<li>Food products: Confectionary. Meat and poultry. Fish and seafood. Grocery. Fat-and-oil products. Canned and frozen foods. Milk products. Ice cream. Fruit and vegetables. Baby food.</li>	<li>Alcohol and non-alcohol drinks</li></ul>

<p>Specialised expositions:</p>

<ul>	<li>Equipment for HoReCa</li>	<li>Healthy food</li></ul>
