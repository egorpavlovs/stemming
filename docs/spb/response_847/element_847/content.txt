                    International conference Physica.SPb/2018 is held since 1997 and covers all areas of modern physics. The Conference annually attracts more than two hundred participants. The Program Committee of the Conference pays great attention to not only the scientific but also educational significance of the Conference presentations.

Young scientists carrying out research in various fields of physics are invited to participate in the Conference. One plenar lecture will be given on each working day of the Conference.
