                    6th International Symposium on Graphene Devices (ISGD-6)
                
                
                    
                        
                            18 - 21 jul 2018 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Science
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    https://isgd6.ifmo.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    The 6th International Symposium on Graphene Devices (ISGD-6), the principal meeting addressing advanced graphene applications, is offering a unique forum to review the present status, the latest developments, future prospects and related fundamental studies, covering both state-of-the-art experimental and theoretical discoveries.

ISGD-6 will be held at Conference center of ITMO University, St. Petersburg, Russia.
