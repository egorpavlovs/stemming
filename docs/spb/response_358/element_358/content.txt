                    "BALTIYA "is a modern hotel located in the historical and business part of the Vyborg district, not far from the metro station" Vyborgskaya", near the city center.The hotel has 90 rooms of different categories, equipped with everything necessary for rest and work, a restaurant with gourmet cuisine, a conference area for 70 people, a business center, a wide range of services and an individual approach to each guest. The BALTIYA hotel has all the possibilities for organizing business meetings and tourist services.
