                Manager of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has won a trip to the Forum of Young Professionals
            
            August 28 2015
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                The video made by sales manager of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has won a trip to the Forum of Young Professionals. The event will be held in Barcelona on November 15-17.

The Forum of Young Professionals brings together 35 young industry professionals from a variety of demographics and presents them with a challenging conference project to prepare. Over three days, these professionals are given networking opportunities, educational sessions, social activities and guidance from some of the most influential meetings industry practitioners. The event is pitched at young professionals determined to build a successful career in the meetings industry.

The forum will focus on what is actually happening, looking at case studies of meetings and practical examples of new industry practices. Expect in-depth discussions around client expectations, programming, marketing, technology, 21st century venues, and destination positioning. The program will include also team exercise work and group presentations. 

Senior industry practitioners will share their knowledge with young professionals. Meetings technology speaker Corbin Ball, senior Exhibition Director of IBTM World Graeme Barnett, president of Marketing Challenges International Michel Couturier, director of Vienna Convention Bureau Christian Mutschlechner, Chief Executive Officer of ICCA Martin Sirk are among them. 

The participation of Olga Petrenko in the Forum of Young Professionals will strengthen the team of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau and will increase the effectiveness of its work at IBTM World.
