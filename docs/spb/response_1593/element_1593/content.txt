                Pulkovo is ranked as one of the best airports in Europe
            
            August 18 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Pulkovo Saint Petersburg Airport has become fourth in the ranking of European airports by customer satisfaction according to Airport Council International.

95 European airports were explored within the research. Pulkovo has been ranked No. 5 by transfer service and No. 3 by public transport accessibility, operational efficiency, helpfulness of staff at the registration, carefulness of preflight inspection. 

Pulkovo has taken up 2nd position in the ranking by service quality in business salons, amenities at lounge and departure gate zones, cleanness at terminal. Cafes and restaurants of Saint Petersburg airport were highly appreciated by passengers. Pulkovo has become first in the ranking by public catering.

 Airport Council International was founded in 1991. ACI advances the collective interests of, and acts as the voice of the world’s airports and the communities they serve, and promotes professional excellence in airport management and operations.
