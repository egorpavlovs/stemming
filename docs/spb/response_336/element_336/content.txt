                    Mikenopa
                
            

        
            
                
                    
                
            

            
                
                    Mikenopa provides event technical support on European standard all around Russia, CIS and Europe. We have branch offices in Moscow, St. Petersburg and Astana. We offer AV equipment and related services (projecting equipment, LED screens and video walls, displays; sound and lighting equipment; simultaneous interpreting equipment and services; interactive voting; audio and video conferences, online broadcasting; office equipment; venue decoration, stage equipment and rigging; event technicial management). Mikenopa has a wide stock of equipment, renews and increases it regularly.

We guarantee top-quality services for events of any scale. The best asset of company is professional team. From the first ideas to the end of event, your personal manager tracks and resolves all your technical needs: clarification of technical task and equipment package, assessment of venue’s capability and other. AV technicians provide set up and dismantling on time, technical support during all the time of event.

With Mikenopa, your event is in safe hands!
