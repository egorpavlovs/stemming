                                  365 SPb Hotel is perfect for those who appreciate quality service at an affordable price. Having become our guests, you will plunge into the atmosphere of old St. Petersburg. The stylish architecture of the restored inn conveys the spirit of a bygone era with its cobbled streets, original woodcarving elements, elegant benches, and wells. Forged visors and handmade lamps, made by the best masters of St. Petersburg, adorn the entrance to each building. Elements of the old brickwork in combination with high quality finishes create the original style of the hotel’s interior, decorated with two working fireplaces. Friendly administrators at the disposal of hotel guests 24 hours a day in the best traditions of St. Petersburg hospitality.
