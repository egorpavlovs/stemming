                Alexander Rodionov has been recognized as Destination Management Professional of the year
            
            February 20 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

The award ceremony of ADMEI Achievement Award has been held in Oklahoma, OK, USA on February 13. The Managing director of TSAR EVENT DMC & PCO Alexander Rodionov has been recognized as Destination Management Professional of the year. Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has congratulated him on the victory and asked some questions.

Alexander, how you were nominated for ADMEI Achievements Award as Destination Management Professional of the year?

- ADMEI Achievement Destination Management Professionals of the Year Award is presented to the individuals who has made a significant contribution to the DMC industry over the past year. 

TSAR EVENT DMC & PCO is proud member of ADMEI  (The Association of Destination Management Executives International (ADME International) since 2011 and during all this time actively promoted Russia as destination that is ready to provide MICE services with the highest possible quality according to standards of ADMEI. The association establishes DMC standards and best practices, supports excellence and ethical business practices and promotes the value of destination management companies to the meetings industry. It offers the Destination Management Certified Professional® (DMCP) program based on the DMC Competencies, the Accredited Destination Management Company® accreditation, and publishes Best Practices in Destination Management.

I became the first DMCP (Destination Management Certified Professional) outside of North America passing exams in 2011, my colleague Senior Project Manager Tatiana Zabolotskikh passed DMCP exams in 2015. So, we are still the only Destination Management Certified Professionals in Russia. TSAR EVENT DMC & PCO  also became the first (and still the only one) company in Russia who earned Accredited Destination Management Company  (ADMC) Designation. 

My Book “Russia Survival Guide” also helped to be nominated, and then to be selected as finalist and to win ADMEI award because the book became very popular among MICE industry Professionals world around. The goal of my book is to help people understand Russia better and to have a better experience while they are here and to show our country as high-end  destination ready for all type of MICE global events. I wanted to write something that would help people fall in love with Russia, as much as I have, by sharing my experiences and my life in Saint Petersburg as travel professional.

How the winner was selected? 

- On the first stage, all members of the Association of Destination Management Executives International nominate the most active and remarkable members, then the winner is chosen by member voting process. The great role playing the nominee’s active support of international MICE sphere, clients’ feedback and his involvement in promotion of the value of destination management companies to the global meetings industry.  

What do you think how this award will influence to development of TSAR EVENTS DMC & PCO?

It is a huge achievement for our company – as this not just prove that our service are  corresponding to the highest global standards and expectations of demanded and spoiled clients, but also recognition TSAR EVENTS DMC & PCO as leader not only in Russian MICE industry, but also on global level. This awards is quality mark of the company that give us big competitive advantage comparison to other DMCs in Moscow and Saint Petersburg, and show Russia as international leading meeting and incentive destination.

Do you mean that the influence of this award is global? 

Winning the ADMEI Achievement Award Destination Management Professional of the Year by representative of Saint Petersburg DMCP demonstrated to the MICE world the fact that Russian members of meeting and incentive industry have the highest level of professional experience and qualification proved by leading international organizations like The Association of Destination Management Executives International. Also it showed that Russia as destination is not just ready for handling very complicated and high-end MICE event for demanded and experienced client, but also is the leader among other world destinations.

The Association of Destination Management Executives International (ADME International) is the only global non-profit association dedicated to increasing the professionalism and effectiveness of destination management through education, promotion of ethical practices, and availability of information to the meetings, convention, and incentive travel industries, as well as the general public. ADME International represents more than 200 Destination Management Companies (DMC) throughout the world.
