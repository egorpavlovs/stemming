                                  Restaurant "Mikhailovsky" is the official partner of The State Russian Museum. We are located in the centre of St. Petersburg in the historic building of The Benois Wing. The restaurant has beautiful hall for 150 guests This spacious venue in classical style is suitable for business meetings, presentations and seminars as well as for organizing gala-dinners, buffets, meetings "without ties". We will be glad to organize catering service for you.   Moreover we and our partners will help you in the creation of cultural and entertainment part of your event. Unique atmosphere, high quality service and excellent cuisine - our distinctive features during many years.
