                Problems of the elderly people will be discussed within the Senior Generation Forum
            
            March 29 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Over 10 discussion platforms dedicated to the problems of the elderly people will work within the Senior Generation Forum in CEC Expoforum from 30th March till 2nd April.

The Senior Generation Forum is the biggest specialized exhibition and congress project in Russia, aimed at the development of the goods and services market for elderly people for the purpose of creation of the most comfortable living conditions. The event attracted more than 20 000 visitors in 2015.

The business program of the Senior Generation Forum includes international congress «Social adaptation, support and health care of the elderly people in the modern society», conferences, seminars and round tables. The exhibition part of the forum unites specialized exhibition «Care and rehabilitation. Available environment», specialized exposition - showroom «Elderly person's house», «Everything for health» trade fair, «Care, Aid and Assistance» exhibition. Over 100 companies will present their novelties and bestsellers here.
