                                  Hotel "Polustrovo" is  a modern 7-floors hotel complex with 242 rooms near the center of St. Petersburg. It takes only 15-minutes to drive from the city center. It is situated near the banks, museums, theaters, shopping centers. Despite this fact, the hotel is located in a quiet green micro district near the metro station Ploshchad Lenina and the Finland Station. You can quickly reach the city's main attractions -  Nevsky Prospekt, the Hermitage, Summer Garden and others. Hotel "Polustrovo" is a great choice for tourists and business travelers who are looking for comfortable accommodation.
