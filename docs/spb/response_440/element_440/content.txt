                                  Constructed in 1750, the Sheremetev Palace is one of the oldest architectural ensembles of Saint Petersburg which has kept its original shape. The smart halls of the Palace amaze the public with a variety of the decorative furniture and keep up traditions of their former owners. The Palace’s musical collection has more than three thousand instruments and it is one of the five largest in the world and the best in Russia. This collection consists of musical instruments created by the famous Western European and Russian masters in XVI-XX centuries, as well as a unique Russian horn orchestra and musical instruments of all continents of the world.
