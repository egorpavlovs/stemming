                Now It's Official!
            
            December 18 2014
        

        






        
            
                
                Saint Petersburg Government Committee for Tourism Development has announced the official launch of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau on December 18th, 2014.

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau has been established by the City Government to ensure the Committe's activities in the field of development of the meetings industry and infrastructure of Saint Petersburg.

The presentation was organized for meetings industry representatives of the city to deliver information on the main priorities of the Bureau and ways of cooperation within the city business community.

The official program of the presentation started with the welcome notes from Inna Shalyto, the Chairman of the Committee for Tourism Development, and Dmitry Levykin, Deputy Director of the City Tourist Information Bureau. The program continued with the series of educational sessions of the leading professional certification brand - CMP. 

The invited experts from Gaining Edge - Gary Grimmer, CEO, and Milos Milovanovic, consultant, former CEO of Serbia Convention Bureau, made the introduction to the CMP, explained the essentials of association and corporate meetings planning, of successful sales and bidding. In the end of the day the participants had a workshop "Why Saint Petersburg?" where they could find themselves in the shoes of a meeting planner considering the destination-to-be via its winning factors.

More than 180 participants from exhibition and convention centres, congress hotels, historical venues, DMC and PCO companies of Saint Petersburg as well as government officials and associations and corporates representatives were solid in positive feedback about the quality of the program, and showed their committment to collaboration with the Convention Bureau to promote Saint Petersburg as a business meetings destination.
