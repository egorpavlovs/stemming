                Convention Bureau presented Saint Petersburg at IBTM Arabia
            
            February 16 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau took part in the Exhibition for Business and Corporate Tourism IBTM Arabia in Abu Dhabi on February 9-11.  The project was chosen for the city promotion because it’s key in the industry in the Middle East, which is a significant resource market for MICE.

Exhibitors and buyers took the city tour in the first day. They not only explored the tourist attractions and convention venues of the capital of the UAE, but also established business contacts. Thanks to this format the second and the third days were concentrated only on negotiations. 20-minutes meetings were held according to the pre-shedule. The plan of negotiations with representatives of Convention Bureau was fulfilled totally. Over 30 meetings were held. Organizers of incentive tours and corporate clients prevailed among buyers, which visited Saint Petersburg. The geography of buyers wasn’t restricted to the Middle East, they were representatives of Brazil, the UK, Hong Kong, Poland, USA and other countries. Specialists of Convention Bureau highly appreciated the quality of buyers. All negotiations had business-oriented character. In return visitors of Saint Petersburg booth admitted undeniable advantages of the city as MICE destination.
