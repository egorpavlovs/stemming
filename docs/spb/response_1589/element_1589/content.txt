                Saint Petersburg Harvest festival
            
            August 26 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Within the International agro-industrial exhibition and trade fair Agrorus over 1000 farm enterprises and companies demonstrate food products, agricultural machinery and innovative solutions at Lenexpo on August 22-30.

For many years Agrorus is the beloved fest of Saint Petersburg citizens and guests of the city. 110 000 people visit the event annually. Usually they buy natural honey, mushrooms, berries, herbs, craftworks items here.

The business program of Agrorus 2015 includes over 40 congress events, many of them will be held with the participation of representatives of Ministry of Agriculture. Experts from 85 Russian regions will discuss prospective lines of industry development, learning of new technologies and promote them to the inside market.

Separate exhibition pavilion dedicated to the hunting is organized for the first time. Horse mini-parade with hounds, master class of cynologists, photo exhibition, falconry show will be held during Agrorus. Music and dancing performances and horse-ridings await visitors.
