                White Nights International Marathon  “ERGO White Nights“ 
            
            July 1 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                The XXVII White Nights International Marathon “ERGO White Nights“is held on the 3th of July 2016 in St. Petersburg. Traditionally this event is taking place with the aim of promotion healthy life-style, introduction regular physical trainings and sports to people, further development of friendly international relations in sports and tourism, and identification the strongest marathoners of St. Petersburg. This year 12 000 people are expected to be engaged in marathon.

The distance is 27 km of experienced  athletes and 10 km – for newcomers. The Marathon route is laid in one lap along the embankments, avenues and streets in the centre of St. Petersburg, along the well-known architectural and historical monuments such as Admiralty Tower, Peter and Paul Fortress, Spit of Vasilyevsky Island and many others.  Start and finish of the Marathon will be at Dvortsovaya Square.

Please be aware that there will be a traffic restriction in the center of the city.
