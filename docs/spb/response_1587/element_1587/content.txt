                Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau summarizes work undertaken at CIBTM 
            
            August 19 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau participated in CIBTM in Beijing on August 5-6. This event is Asia-Pacific leading exhibition where meetings, events and incentives industry decision makers meet for global business adventures.

Over 50 buyers from China, Australia, New Zealand visited Saint Petersburg stand in two days. They were representatives of companies from corporate and non-corporate sectors. Incentive tours for groups from 40 to 400 people, organization of business events, selection of venues and operators were discussed within negotiations. As the result Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau fixed the list of the companies, which are interested at the city as convention venue. 

Within the congress program head of sales of Convention Bureau Elena Yazeva held the presentation about Saint Petersburg. 70 event professionals from China became its listeners. The presentation allowed to present Saint Petersburg in detail and to tell about cultural heritage, sightseeings, flight connection with China, visa regime, different convention venues, business centres of the city.

Total 380 exhibitors from 28 countries and hosted buyers from 21 states participated in CIBTM. Many of them visited Russian tea party with an authentic samovar, varenije, honey, traditional pastries bubliki and baranki at Saint Petersburg stand. The action aroused great interest of CIBTM exhibitors and visitors and was helpful to the creation of a complex brand of the city in Beijing.
