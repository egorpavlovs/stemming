                                  The private residence of Dukes Baryatinsky is also known as the Palace of the Grand Duchess Olga Romanova, the younger sister of the last Russian emperor Nicholas II. The building was reconstructed from 1837 to 1880. There were many gala events and receptions in “Second Rococo” style halls. After 1917 the building was occupied by the numerous organizations. Nowadays St-Petersburg Chamber of Commerce and Industry is located here. Today different meetings and conferences at the international and regional levels, seminars and presentations of domestic and foreign firms are held here.The building is located in the historic and business center of St. Petersburg (3 minutes from the metro station "Chernyshevskaya"). Complex service of St. Petersburg Chamber of Commerce consists of organization of business events, including attraction of the target audience, invitation of speakers and moderators, PR-support and maintenance activities, technical support of events (professional lighting, sound, video projection equipment, online broadcasts), photo and video shooting , wi-fi.
