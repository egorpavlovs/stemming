                    Renal Week 2015
                
                
                    
                        
                            15 - 19 sep 2015 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Health, Medical Equipment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.renalweek2015.org/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Scientific program will cover broad spectrum of topics, ranging from peritoneal dialysis, hemodiafiltration, therapeutic plasmapheresis and technical innovations in blood purification to complications of chronic kidney disease including mineral bone disease, anemia and quality of life of patients. We will also discuss new hyponatremia guidelines and the use of biosimilars in the treatment of our patients. The faculty will be represented by leading nephrologists from Europe and USA.</p>
