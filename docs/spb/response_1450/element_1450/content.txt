                Pulkovo Airport opened the additional building for handling of domestic flights
            
            September 12 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Pulkovo Airport opened the additional building for handling of domestic flights. The object 3,5 thousand sq.m in area is fitted with two boarding bridges and four bus gates. As Director General of Northern Capital Gateway LLC, the company running Saint Petersburg’s Pulkovo Airport Sergey Emdin commented, the total airport capacity increased from 17 to 18 million people per year with the launch of the object.

The new building borders with the south termanal finger (the old Pulkovo-1 building). The total area of the centralized passenger Pulkovo terminal increased to 170 thousand sq.m, the number of gates amounted to 37 with the opening of the object.

The decision about the building was taken with reference of the sustained growth of the domestic passenger traffic in the summer of 2014.
