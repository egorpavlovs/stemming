                                  Obuhoff hotel is located close to the historical centre of Saint-Petersburg on the bank of the Neva. It will not take you more than 5 minutes to get to Nevsky prospekt – the main city thoroughfare, to Alexander Nevsky Monastery with the Holy Trinity Cathedral – one of the oldest architectural ensembles of the 18th century in Saint-Petersburg, as well as to the “Ploshchad Alexandra Nevskogo” metro station.Obuhoff hotel offers a wide range of accommodation: it has 42 comfortable rooms of different categories (superior one-roomed, standard one-roomed, superior two-roomed, standard two-roomed, junior suites and suites) with all you need for business and recreation.
