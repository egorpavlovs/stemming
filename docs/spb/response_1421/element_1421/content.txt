                    The 8th International Maritime Defence Show (IMDS-2017) 
                
                
                    
                        
                            28 jun - 2 jul 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                    
                        Industry
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://www.navalshow.ru/eng/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The 8th International Maritime Defence Show (IMDS-2017)</strong>&nbsp;will be held &nbsp;from 19 June 2015 in St. Petersburg from 28th of June till the 2nd of July, 2017.</p>

<p>The organizer of IMDS-2017 is the Ministry of Industry and Trade of the Russian Federation. The Exhibition is held with participation of the Ministry of Defence of the Russian Federation, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation, Federal Service for Military-Technical Cooperation, the Government of St. Petersburg and Rosoboronexport JSC. The Exhibition Operator is &quot;Morskoy Salon&quot; Co. Ltd.&nbsp;</p>

<p><strong><u>Exhibits profile:</u></strong></p>

<p>1. &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Naval&nbsp;and Commercial Shipbuilding</p>

<p>2. &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Weapons, Armaments, Weapon Control Complexes and Systems</p>

<p>3. &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;Combat Direction Systems, Navigation Sets and&nbsp; Systems, Communications, Radio-Electronics, Radio-technical and Hydroacoustic Armament</p>

<p>4.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Marine Power Plants, Ship Systems and Facilities, Auxiliary Machinery</p>

<p>5.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Naval Aviation (Deck&nbsp;and Shore-Based)</p>

<p>6.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Navy Coastal Forces Arming</p>

<p>7.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;High-speed, passenger and small-size motorboats, vessels, yachts.</p>

<p>8.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Fleet Supply and Services,&nbsp;Logistics&rsquo; Infrastructure</p>

<p>9.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Search and Rescue Equipment, Techniques and Technologies of Deep-sea and Rescue Operations</p>

<p>10. &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;Navigation Security</p>

<p>11. &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;Anti-terrorism Effort, Anti-piracy Campaign, Security Facilities Protection</p>

<p>12. &nbsp; &nbsp; Ocean Development for the Benefit of National Defence and Economy</p>

<p>&nbsp;13.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Development of the Arctic shelf, protection of national interests and state security in the Arctic zone</p>

<p>14.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Harbour and Hydraulic Engineering</p>

<p>15.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Industrial Equipment in Shipbuilding Complex and Related Sectors</p>

<p>16.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Information Technologies in Shipbuilding Industry</p>

<p>17.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Industrial and Raw Materials, Hardware for Shipbuilding Industry</p>

<p>18.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Innovations and Modern Solutions in Shipbuilding&nbsp;Industry</p>

<p>19.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Personnel Training</p>

<p>20.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Project Financing, Management, Insurance and Consulting Services</p>

<p>21.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Press and Electronic Mass Media</p>

<p>22.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Naval History</p>
