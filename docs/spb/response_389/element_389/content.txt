                                  Congress Centre "PetroCongress" is modern venue in St. Petersburg that offers a full range of services for different types of events The main target of PetroCongress is to support the business community in the development of business contacts as well as to encourage development of efficient partnerships. Main principles: customer-oriented solutions; efficiency; innovations and development; flexible collaboration; professional staff.                                              Benefits of cooperation: transforming area, hi-tech equipment, convenient location, wide range of services, transforming conference hall with seating capacity up to 300 persons; 8 multi-purpose conference rooms with seating capacity up to 150 persons; 4 meeting rooms with seating capacity up to 6 persons; videoconferencing facilities; VIP hall with individual catering services, seating capacity up to 60 persons.
