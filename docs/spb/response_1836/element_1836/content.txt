                    XI public action "Choose sport"
                
                
                    
                        
                            10 - 11 sep 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Tourism, Sports, Leisure, and Hobby
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://kfis.gov.spb.ru/razvitie-otrasli-fkis/obshestveenaya-akciya-vybirayu-sport/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p>Visitors and participants of the <strong>XI Public campaign &quot;Choose sport!&quot;</strong> awaits a bright sports show-performances of young athletes, sports competitions and Championships, interactive work sites, workshops, and meetings with famous athletes. &nbsp;In the Pavilion and outdoors places of &quot;EXPOforum&quot; everyone can try desktop hockey, fencing, athletics, basketball, volleyball, armwrestling, florbole, field hockey and other sports. Traditionally, the rally will be held simultaneously with the St. Petersburg stage all-Russian competitions of street basketball &quot;orange ball&quot;. This year will feature more than 80 types of sports.</p>
