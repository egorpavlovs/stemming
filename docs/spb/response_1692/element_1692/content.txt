                    ZOO Show 
                
                
                    
                        
                            26 - 27 nov 2016 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Other
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://zooshow.expoforum.ru/
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>ZOO Show</strong> is a specialized exhibition of dog, felinological clubs and pets, as well as competitions, contests and entertainment. The event takes place from 1993 onwards and is a project of the international exhibition of goods and services for pets<strong> &quot;Zoosphere&rdquo;</strong>.</p>

<p>Within the ZOO Show will feature thousands of pets: dogs and cats of different breeds, birds, reptiles, ferrets, rodents, insects, and other pets. At the exhibition you will be able to select or order the desired animal breeders directly, to purchase food and accessories for your pets, as well as get acquainted with novelties in the pet format presentations and tastings.</p>
