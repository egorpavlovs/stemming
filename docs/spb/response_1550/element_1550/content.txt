                    XVI International Forum "Ecology of a Big City" 
                
                
                    
                        
                            5 - 7 apr 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Ecology and Environment
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://ecology.expoforum.ru/en
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The Forum </strong>is the largest convention and exhibition event in the end of environmental protection, nature conservation, as well as environmental protection equipment, technologies and services in the North-West.</p>

<p>The forum is aimed at promotion and implementation of innovative environmental protection equipment and resource saving technologies in Russia, which contribute to the development of high-tech environmentally friendly and safe production, nature conservation and improvement of the quality of life of the population in big cities.</p>

<p><strong>Exhibition Program</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Waste management: technologies and equipment;</li>	<li>Rational water use, waste water treatment, water production, water supply and sanitation;</li>	<li>Environmental rehabilitation of territories and water bodies;</li>	<li>Environmental monitoring;</li>	<li>Information support of nature conservation activities;</li>	<li>Environmental technologies used in construction.</li></ul>
