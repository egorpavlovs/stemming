                St. Petersburg is the Best City for business and event tourism in Russia and CIS
            
            September 27 2016
        

        




    
        
            
                
                
            
                
                
            
        
    



        
            
                
                St. Petersburg was named as “the Best City for business and event tourism in Russia and CIS” according to Business Traveller magazine. City left behind Moscow, Ekaterinburg, Kazan, Yerevan, Vladivostok, Ufa and Nizhny Novgorod in this competition. This award proved how attractive and convenient St. Petersburg is for business tourists. 

Business Traveller Awards is an annual international award that recognizes extraordinary achievements and innovations in the field of business travel entirely based on reader’s votes. Throughout its existence, Business Traveller Awards has won the reputation of a distinguished award.

"The business and event tourism development is  strategically important task for St. Petersburg, and I am proud of the fact, that we have achieved great progress in this process", - commented Andrey Mushkarev, Chairman of Committee for Tourism Development of St. Petersburg.

For the first time ever, in September 2016 the Russian edition of Business Traveller held the Business Traveller Russia and CIS Awards ceremony, Award nominees included well-known international brands, as well as regional companies from Russia, the CIS and neighbouring countries. According to the voting results the St. Petersburg win the statuette as a nominee "Best city business and event in Russia and CIS Tourism". Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau represented our city in the competition.

Just as nominees Business Traveller Russia and CIS Awards Award registered more than 300 exhibiting companies, among which were both well-known international brands and destinations, and city and regional companies in Russia, CIS countries and abroad.

Voters included readers of the Russian edition of Business Traveller: frequent business travellers, members of international and Russian business schools, chief executives of large Russian and international companies, representatives of banks, Russian state corporations and individual travellers. The voting procedure for the Business Traveller Awards was one of the most unbiased assessment methods: laureates were chosen by readers, and not by a jury panel or an expert board.  Due to an authoritative audit of the votes and a carefully selected audience of respondents, only the best of the best became laureates of the Business Traveller Russia and CIS Awards.
