                The session of Convention Bureau at the Europe + Asia Event Forum 
            
            January 18 2016
        

        






        
            
                
                

Europe + Asia Event Forum will be held for the fifth time in Saint Petersburg on January 20-22. EFEA 2016 will unite industry representatives from more than 28 countries and 25 regions of Russia.

Events as the effective toll of marketing communications are the main theme of the congress program. The Chair of Saint Petersburg Committee on the Development of Tourism Inna Shalyto, Chair of the RF CCI Committee on Fair and Exhibition Activities, General Director of ZAO Expocentre Sergey Bednov, Chair of Russian Union of Exhibitions and Fairs, Vice President of EXPOFORUM, UFI President for the period 2015/2016 Sergei Alekseev, Chief Executive Officer ExpoForum International Ltd Sergey Voronkov, President of RESTEC Group Serguei Trofimov are among 120 speakers of the forum. Secretary General of Taiwan Exhibition & Convention Association (TECA), Member of Advisory Committee on Organization Development of TAITRA Mr. Kevin Wei, Commercial Director of UBM Olsson David, Senior Vice President of Koelnmesse GmbH Christian Glasmacher will give a speech, too.

The separate part of the program is dedicated to the city marketing. The session «Convention bureau - a bet on cooperation and foreign experience» will take place within Europe + Asia Event Forum in Saint Petersburg on January 21. It will allow to have a look at the promotion of business tourism destination from a perspective of stakeholders – government, convention bureaus, DMC, PCO, professional and scientific community. The participants will discuss the strategical partnership between the city and the industry, the technologies of the attraction of the international events, the quality of the services and many other topics. The Deputy Chair of Saint Petersburg Committee on the Development of Tourism Nana Gvichiya, leading PCO and DMC, convention bureaus of Azerbaijan, Moscow and Sochi are among the participants. CEO of Saint Petersburg Convention Bureau Anna Chetvergova will become the moderator of the session.

Europe + Asia Event Forum will create a convenient communications site for the events industry professionals that provides an opportunity to establish international relations, share experience and innovations, attract investments and expand business networking in the global event. The organizer of the forum is R&C Market Research Company.
