                                  The convenient location in the historical and financial centre close to one of the most beautiful cathedrals – the Transfiguration Cathedral and the famous Summer Garden gives good opportunity for our guests to enjoy pleasant walks. The nearest subway stations are “Chernyshevskaya” and “Mayakovskaya”.The main avenue – the Nevsky prospect is just a few minutes away. The Summer Garden, Field of Mars, Saint Michael's Castle, Russian Museum, Church of the Savior on Blood and numerous museums, theaters, shopping centers and restaurants are within the walking distance from the hotel.
