                Open-air opera 
            
            July 21 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

IV International festival Opera for everyone takes place in Saint Petersburg, supported by Committee on Culture. Project is held in open-air format and presents masterpieces of classic opera in architectural surroundings. All concerts are free of charge.  

Festival’s Musical Director is Fabio Mastrangelo. The best orchestras and leading soloists of Russia and Europe are involved in the festival.

The festival opened with Chaikovsky’s opera Mazepa on the Cathedral Square in Peter and Paul’s fortress on July 12. The Symphonic Orchestra of St. Petersburg and Chamber Choir of Philharmonic Society of St. Petersburg performed Turandot in front of the Music Hall in Alexander’s park on July 15. The Orchestra of St. Petersburg’s theatre Music Hall, North Symphony Orchestra and Choir of Smolny Cathedral represented Mozart’s opera Don Juan on parade square in Ekaterina’s Palace (Tsarskoe Selo) on July 18. Festival ends on July 22 on Elagin Island, where the Choir of Mikhailovsky theatre and Music Hall theatre will perform Donizetti’s opera The Elixir of Love. Concert starts at 7 p.m.
