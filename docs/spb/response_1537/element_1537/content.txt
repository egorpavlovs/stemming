                    The «Energetika & Elektrotechnika» exhibition
                
                
                    
                        
                            16 - 19 may 2017 
                        
                    
                    
                        EXPOFORUM 
                    
                    
                        Energy. Energy efficiency
                    
                
                
                
            
        

        
            
                
            
            
                
                    Website
                    http://rief.expoforum.ru/en/vystavka-energetika-i-elektrotekhnika
                
            
            
            
        

        






        
            
                
                    <p><strong>The &quot;Energetika &amp; Elektrotechnika&quot;&nbsp;exhibition</strong>, being one of&nbsp;the primary exhibitions in&nbsp;Russia has been held in&nbsp;Saint-Petersburgsince 1993.&nbsp;&nbsp;The project is&nbsp;prominent as&nbsp;it&nbsp;presents the whole specter of&nbsp;relevant branch data, and the visitors&nbsp;&ndash; specialists of&nbsp;power engineering, being the real target audience&nbsp;&ndash;&nbsp;become potential purchasers of&nbsp;the presented products.&nbsp;<strong>​</strong></p>

<p><strong>Exhibition subjects:&nbsp;</strong></p>

<ul>	<li>Power engineering</li>	<li>Power machine building</li>	<li>Electrotechnical equipment&nbsp;</li>	<li>Gas-, heat-, and power supply systems</li>	<li>Machinery and equipment for housing and communal services</li>	<li>Automated process control systems</li>	<li>Measurement and control instrumentation and systems</li>	<li>Energy-saving and power-efficient equipment and technologies</li>	<li>Safety of the power facilities and environmental safety</li>	<li>Development and investigation</li>	<li>Software</li></ul>
