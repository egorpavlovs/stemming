                                  The hotel "Repinskay" is an ideal venue for business and corporate events.The hotel is located on the shore of the Gulf of Finland. Has a unique microclimate and a private sandy beach. The hotel has modern conference venue for business events. Conference rooms with necessary equipment, flexible meeting rooms, restaurant "Repinskay" and a charming terrace with a beautiful kitchen and panoramic views of the Gulf of Finland. It offers accommodation in comfortable rooms of various categories. Each room is equipped with everything necessary for a pleasant stay and fruitful work.
