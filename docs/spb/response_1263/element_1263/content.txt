                Corporations from USA are interested in incentive tours to Saint Petersburg 
            
            October 26 2015
        

        






        
            
                
                

Convetion Bureau has presented Saint Petersburg at the Worldwide Exhibition for Incentive Travel, Meetings & Events IMEX America in Las Vegas on October 13-15.

The exhibition has been held at an intensive rate. Business tourism is developed at the American continent. Professional associations and big corporations – potential organizers of events – are concentrated here. Totally 66,000 business appointments took place in Las Vegas.

Representatives of corporate and non-corporate sector of business tourism from USA, Brazil, Latin America, Australia and China visited the Saint Petersburg booth. About 30 face-to-face meetings  have been held, 80% among them were appointed online preliminarily. American companies became a primary audience of negotiations. Most commonly they have raised a question about the organization of incentive tours with a surprising program for groups from 30 to 100 persons.

The Association Evening became the effective venue for the establishment of business contacts in the framework of IMEX America. Chief executives of over 200 professional unions, big PCO, convention bureaus have met at this event to discuss perspective business events and share accumulated experience.  

4 presentations have taken place at the Saint Petersburg booth and have attracted about 50 specialists. CEO of Convetion Bureau Anna Chetvergova and Head of sales Elena Yazeva told in details about opportunities for the organization of different business events in Saint Petersburg and original cultural and entertainment programs for MICE tours. The special event dedicated to ICE Hockey World Championship has given strong positive emotions to exhibitors and visitors of IMEX America. Saint Petersburg will host this big sport fest next year and will attract a numerous armee of North American fans.
