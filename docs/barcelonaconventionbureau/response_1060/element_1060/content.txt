                    DESCRIPTION
                    Situated in the very heart of Barcelona, the abba Balmoral Hotel 4**** is the ideal place to stay whether you have come to the city on holiday or on business. The hotel is centrally located near Avinguda Diagonal, just a few minutes from Gaudí's La Pedrera, and very close to Passeig de Gràcia and Rambla de Catalunya.The abba Balmoral Hotel 4* offers 103 recently renovated rooms, equipped with every comfort to ensure a comfortable stay in Barcelona.We offer a very wide range of services, taking care of every last detail to guarantee that our guests are comfortable and have everything they need. These services include free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel, meeting rooms with a capacity of up to 180 people, a cafeteria, restaurant, garage, exclusive Business Centre, gymnasium, sauna and massage room.
