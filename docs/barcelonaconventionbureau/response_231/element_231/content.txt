                    DESCRIPTION
                    Opened in November 2015, H10 Cubik is an exclusive 4-star superior hotel. It is situated in a fully refurbished modern building in the heart of Barcelona. Prestigious interior designer Lázaro Rosa-Violán is responsible for the avant-garde interior design, which combines geometric shapes and colours to create a warm, original ambience. The hotel offers bright, spacious rooms, 4 meeting rooms with natural light, a restaurant, a bar and a spectacular 8th-floor terrace with a plunge pool and magnificent views of the city.
