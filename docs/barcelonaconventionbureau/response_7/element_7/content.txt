        Conference services
             
                There are a lot of factors involved in organizing an event. Find everything from technical solutions for visuals, audio or lighting to complementary services and qualified support staff. We offer you all the resources you need to make your event a success.
            
        
            
                    
                        Computer & audio-visual equipment
                    
                    Do you need a computer projection, video or broadcast streaming? Find a skilled technician.
                        
            
                    
                        Consulting
                    
                    The environment, competition and trends are key factors. For the analysis you need, get consulting services.
                        
            
                    
                        Gifts
                    
                    From the most prestigious to unique boutiques with curious objects, find exclusive gifts.
                        
            
                    
                        Official Guides
                    
                    Find out about Barcelona's secrets and curiosities via a guided tour led by official guides in various languages.
                        
            
                    
                        Hostesses
                    
                    Guarantee excellent service by getting professionals to hire the front line staff delivering your event.
                        
            
                    
                        Light & Sound
                    
                    Find sound and lighting equipment directed and controlled by a team of qualified technicians.
                        
            
                    
                        Luggage transfer
                    
                    Do you want to travel without having to worry about your luggage? Find the company you need.
                        
            
                    
                        Printers
                    
                    ID tags, banners and promotional materials. Choose the digital printing solutions you need.
                        
            
                    
                        Publication services
                    
                    Tell the story and show the spirit of your project or company with the highest quality, carefully produced publications.
                        
            
                    
                        Signage & Communication
                    
                    Street posters, huge LED screens, signs ... Choose the signage you need.
                        
            
                    
                        Technical & Artistic Productions
                    
                    Bring exclusivity to your event through original performances, artistic shows or hiring artists.
                        
            
                    
                        Simultaneous translation
                    
                    If you need language assistance, like simultaneous interpreters or translators, find the right professionals.
                        
            
                    
                        Design, building and decoration
                    
                    Stands, furniture and everything you’ll need to set yourself apart in the design and layout of your space.
                        
            
                    
                        Office Supplies & Promotion Merchandise for Events
                    
                    Wide range of products that addapt to your event. 

                        
            
                    
                        Design studio
                    
                    Concept, design, content and the production of any project.
