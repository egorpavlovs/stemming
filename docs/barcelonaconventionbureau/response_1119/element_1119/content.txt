                    DESCRIPTION
                    Melià Sitges is located in one of Catalonia’s most charismatic and cosmopolitan coastal towns, in the Aigüadolç marina area. Just 20 minutes from Barcelona’s international airport, the hotel’s 307 rooms are spacious and bright, with a terrace overlooking the Mediterranean and the Garraf Natural Park. Its excellent facilities and services include an outdoor pool, landscaped areas, a fully equipped gym and a heated pool. Its 4,000 m2-Convention Centre is equipped with the latest technologies, 19 meeting rooms and an auditorium for 1,380 people. It is a hotel that combines the best in vacation experiences, and, at the time, excellent services for the organization of events and meetings.
