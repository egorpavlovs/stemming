                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Barceló Raval is situated in the city centre in the city's coolest, multicultural neighbourhood just a short walk from La Rambla, the Plaça de Catalunya and the Gothic Quarter and is conveniently located near museums, shops, fashionable bars and restaurants. The hotel has 186 guest rooms, all of them exterior. They have panoramic views, plasma TVs, iPod docks, Wi-Fi and raindance showers. The four matchless suites offer all the home comforts of a small apartment.The BLounge terrace, bar and restaurant serves creative cuisine. You can enjoy a leisurely breakfast, listen to music, have lunch, dinner or read the papers in this unique space.Four very special private rooms for meetings or small conventions with natural light and equipped with state-of-the-art audiovisual technology.Fitness centre with sauna and cardiovascular room.Roof terrace offering unique 360º views of Barcelona.Chill-out terrace with a swimming pool and cocktails in summer.
