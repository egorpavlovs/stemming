                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Condes de Barcelona is housed in a magnificent 19th-century building which has been transformed into a modern hotel where the uniqueness of the past coexists with the latest amenities. It stands on a majestic corner and offers 126 spacious, light-filled guest rooms which ensure the maximum comfort. This iconic building is a vibrant social hub and a gastronomic landmark where you can enjoy dishes prepared by the chef Martín Berasategui at his modern, informal restaurant LOIDI which serves market-fresh cuisine. The hotel has a wide range of rooms for a wide variety of events, an open-air swimming pool and the spectacular ALAIRE terrace, one of the city?s most fashionable haunts which boasts fantastic views and is the perfect place for an informal al fresco dinner or cocktail.
