                    DESCRIPTION
                    We are an audiovisual company with a long history of pioneering productions.We seek out the most advanced and innovative technology with the aim of creating unique solutions that deliver optimum results.Technological innovation - The best of the future for events of today:Of all the fields of operation in audiovisuals, Ditec is an expert in large productions, in which it has experience and its own equipment, including projection, video walls, LED screens, multi-camera production, sound, lighting, augmented reality, webcast and videostreaming, simultaneous translation and interactive voting systems.We accompany our clients from concept to completion, designing unforgettable experiences. We understand the special nature and the needs of different sectors, from corporate events, congresses and conferences, institutions, medical and pharma, to trade fairs, shows and festivals.We have proven experience, talent and creativity.
