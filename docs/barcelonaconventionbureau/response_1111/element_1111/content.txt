                    DESCRIPTION
                    Hilton Barcelona enjoys a superb uptown location just 10 minutes from the city centre and is well served by public transport. The hotel is just a 15-minute drive from Barcelona International Airport and 5 minutes from Sants StationIt has 18 open-plan meeting rooms offering seven dividable spaces which can host up to 580 people. They have ceiling heights of up to 4.70m and adjustable LED lighting. Business centre with personalised assistance.289 contemporary guest rooms inspired by the works of Antoni Gaudí with panoramic views of the city. Our superior rooms offer maximum comfortand provide access to the executive floor.The Vibe Bar and Mosaic Restaurant are particularly recommended.
