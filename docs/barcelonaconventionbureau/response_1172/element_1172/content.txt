                    DESCRIPTION
                    We are ITB events, one of Spain’s leading destination management companies working in the MICE sector and the first agency in the country to receive the Site Crystal Award.As a specialised events agency we take care of the preparation and organisation of all the essentials for tailor-made events. We act as a bridge between the destination and everything it has to offer to help you find everything you are looking for and best suits your needs. Since 1984, our main purpose has been helping our clients reach their targets. This means that our experience guarantees your event will be a success.We work across Spain and have branch offices in Barcelona, Madrid and on the Costa del Sol.
