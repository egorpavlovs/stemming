                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Estela Barcelona, also referred to as Art Hotel, marvels the visitors with its interior and outer design. The Hotel ideal location, just in front of the Marina Bay, offers everything the sea and nature lovers dream about. Once inside, the visitor is totally impressed by the deep artistic atmosphere that reigns here. Exhibitions and famous artists´ collections, such as the ones of Josep M Subirachs, Salvador Dali, Antoni Tapies, Joan Abello, Lorenzo Quinn, Pablo Atchugarry and of Josep Puigmarti, the genius who has been residing in the hotel for about 20 years, are exposed here for the guests ?delight. The Hotel Estela Barcelona is the perfect place where one can breathe of Sea and Art joining together in unison.
