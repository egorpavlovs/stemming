                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Barcelona Golf is a modern and comfortable hotel with 150 rooms of 34 square meters, which could be standard, premium, superior rooms with terrace, family rooms and a Suite of 70 square meters.The Hotel also has a Restaurant Xarel.lo, Cafeteria Bar Hoyo 19, Spa, gym and outdoor pool surrounded by gardens.The Hotel Barcelona Golf is strategically located next to the golf course of the Barcelona Golf Club (old Masia Bach) and with views to Montserrat.The Hotel has also ADSL and free WIFI in the whole Hotel.All rooms have a full bathroom, separate shower and bath, and all the services you may need to make you feel at home, such as ADSL, air conditioning, desk, minibar, hairdryer, TV and telephone.Our Restaurant Xarel.lo is a restaurant dedicated to Mediterranean cuisine, open every day.Xarel.lo Restaurant is an establishment with traditional and gastronomic restoration. In it, we offer our clients a varied, balanced daily buffet with seasonal products. You can enjoy a full buffet and free parking, at a very competitive price.The Hotel has several private rooms for all kinds of celebrations, meetings, conferences or family events with capacities of up to 300 people.Our Wellness Center & SPA is an oasis for wellness, health and relaxation. Our Wellness Center & SPA is composed by three independent and interconnected areas: SPA, fitness area and massage and treatment cabins.In the SPA you will enjoy the Wellness route that is composed of a thematic pool, Finnish sauna, Turkish bath and three types of hydromassage showers.Our hotel has 5 rooms for meetings and conventions with a capacity of up to 300 delegates, equipped with all the infrastructure and resources necessary for your convention to be a success.Our meeting rooms, spacious and diaphanous, have natural light and direct access to the garden, where you can make your breaks with a delicious coffee break, or enjoy a wonderful gala dinner with a cocktail in the pool garden.
