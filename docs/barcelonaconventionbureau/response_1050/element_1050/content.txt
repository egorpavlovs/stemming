                    DESCRIPTION
                    Opened in 2014, the H10 Metropolitan is an exclusive hotel set in a historic building in the city centre. It has been completely renovated and decorated by the prestigious interior designer Lázaro Rosa-Violán in a style inspired by industrial Barcelona of the 19th century.  As well as modern rooms, this four-star superior hotel offers guests the Kitchen Restaurant, the Eden Bar with access to a delightful terrace with a swimming pool, and a top-floor terrace with a plunge pool and views of Plaça de Catalunya and Tibidabo. The hotel has two meeting rooms with a capacity of up to 80 persons in a theatre style. These rooms are equipped with a screen, projector, basic sound system and flipchart.
