                    DESCRIPTION
                    An international company with more than 25 years of experience in the interpreting and simultaneous interpreting and audiovisual equipment sector. We are a multidisciplinary team made up of project managers, translators, sound technicians and audiovisual technicians, as a result of which we can offer a comprehensive service to meet the requirements of each individual client. We aim to offer a personalised service that pays the greatest attention to detail. We are members of the Audiovisual Professionals Association (APAC) and exclusive representatives of Shure Conference Network in Spain and Portugal. We can cover your simultaneous translation equipment needs at large congresses (from 50 to 10,000 receivers and from 1 to more than 20 cabins).Services offered:-Simultaneous, consecutive and liaison interpreters for international conferences.-Technical assistance.-Simultaneous interpretation equipment: ISO 4043 cabins, Integrus Bosch digital receivers, Sennheiser digital infoports, public address systems and microphones.-Audiovisual material.-Translation and revision of texts. Sworn translation.
