                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Evenia Rosselló is located in the centre of Barcelona's main shopping district, just 350 metres from the Passeig de Gràcia and the Diagonal. It offers 94 modern, fully equipped guest rooms with air-conditioning and central heating, 50" flat-screen smart televisions, free internet and Wi-Fi and a writing desk among other services. Its spacious bathrooms have a bath or shower, hairdryer, magnifying mirror and complementary toiletries.The restaurant "L'Illa de Rosselló" serves typical Mediterranean cuisine and signature dishes. It offers a prix fixe and à la carte menu. The hotel also has meeting rooms, a gym, on-site parking and a bar.
