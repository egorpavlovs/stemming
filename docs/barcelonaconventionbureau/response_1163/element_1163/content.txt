                    DESCRIPTION
                    Just 250 metres away from Plaça Francesc Macià, just off Barcelona’s Avinguda Diagonal, this modern hotel offers elegant, soundproofed rooms with air-conditioning, free Wi-Fi and a pillow menu. The Zenit is a 15-minute walk from Passeig Gràcia, where you’ll find Gaudí’s iconic buildings: La Pedrera and the Casa Batlló. Plaça Catalunya and La Rambla are just a 15-minute walk away too.The guest rooms at the Zenit Barcelona have satellite TV, a safe, minibar, writing desk and private bathroom with hair dryer.The restaurant at the Zenit serves gourmet Spanish and world cuisine. It also serves a special gluten-free breakfast and there are children’s menus too. Take-away lunches are available to order and room service is available.The reception is open 24 hours a day and there is a tourist information desk. A laundry, ironing service and dry cleaners are available. There is a paying car park 1 minute away from the hotel.Sarrià-Sant Gervasi is a fantastic choice for visitors interested in landmarks, culture and exploring the city on foot.We speak your language!
