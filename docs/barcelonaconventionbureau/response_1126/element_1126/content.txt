                    DESCRIPTION
                    The hotel NH Constanza is a new building designed by the architect Rafael Moneo with spacious and bright cutting-edge, contemporary interiors.Located in the heart of the business district, right by the Illa Diagonal shopping complex, it has excellent transport links and is conveniently located for the shops, restaurants and nightlife.The hotel has 308 guest rooms, including seven junior suites and one presidential suite, and offers all kinds of services to ensure its guests have an enjoyable stay: the “Nhube” restaurant, Elysium spa, sun deck, swimming pool… it also has six extendable, independent function spaces which are the perfect setting for any kind of celebration or events.
