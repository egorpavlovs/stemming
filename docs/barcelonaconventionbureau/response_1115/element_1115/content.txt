                    DESCRIPTION
                    Located in the centre of Barcelona, close to Passeig de Gràcia and Plaça de Catalunya, and just a stone´s throw from the Ramblas, the Hotel Jazz makes an excellent base from which to explore Barcelona.A lively, modern, cosmopolitan hotel in the very heart of Barcelona.Car park (additional charge), room service until 10.30 pm, laundry service, daily newspapers. Rooms and public areas offer easy access.The JAZZ BAR has a pleasant atmosphere and is modern in design, with a large flat screen LCD TV and free Internet access.WI-FI INTERNET ACCESS: for all guests and in all areas of the hotel.The terrace is open all year round, with a bar service from May to September.108 rooms: All JAZZ ROOMS & JUNIOR SUITES have individual air conditioning, free Wi-Fi, LCD TV with international channels, private bathroom, hair dryer, minibar (additional charge), telephone, background music, safe deposit box.
