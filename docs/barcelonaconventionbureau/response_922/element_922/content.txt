                    DESCRIPTION
                    Since 1989, we have organised more than 700 events for over 100,000 delegates. Reunions i Ciència is currently the leading organiser of meetings and congresses for tens to thousands of participants. Our professional and enthusiastic team has been working at the company for more than ten years and will ensure your congress is a resounding success. We are leading experts in scientific congresses on every kind of subject and our customers’ satisfaction is our main objective. This explains our commitment to quality. In 2002, we were awarded ISO 9001:2008 certification for the Organisation and Integrated Management of  Meetings and Congresses.
