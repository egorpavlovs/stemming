                    DESCRIPTION
                    Hotel Miramar Barcelona 5* G.L. has 75 spacious rooms, 5 meeting rooms and 2 banquet halls with gardens and terraces to host corporate and private events in a unique setting, boasting spaces with natural light and panoramic views of Barcelona and the Mediterranean Sea. In addition, the hotel has a very complete Spa with indoor heated pool, the Forestier restaurant serving delicious Mediterranean cuisine, a modern gym with natural light, free WIFI access throughout the facilities and a large outdoor pool overlooking the sea.
