                    DESCRIPTION
                    A contemporary hotel with innovative design, Meliá Barcelona Sky has become one of the city’s most recognisable landmarks. Standing 120 metres high, it has 29 floors and 258 guest rooms that make it one of the tallest and most iconic buildings on the Barcelona skyline.The impressive facilities include an outdoor pool, luxury spa and wellness centre, Michelin-star fine dining at the “Dos Cielos” restaurant, spectacular terraces looking out to sea, the Le Pool area for private celebrations, 1,200 m2 for events and meetings and exquisitely designed guest rooms which boast spectacular views of the Mediterranean and the city.
