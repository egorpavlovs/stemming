                    DESCRIPTION
                    Les Comes, a restored farmhouse respecting the style of the time, has 518ha of land, more than 60km of tracks and 20km of trails.  A space at the service of companies to carry out meetings and incentives, launch new vehicles and product presentations, training programs and events.  To offer all these services, Les Comes has several multipurpose rooms and charming corners, a catering service with proximity products and a great natural environment with total exclusivity.An ideal space for all kinds of motor activities, physical activities and Team Building, unique experiences and many other options and ideas that our team offers with a totally personalised treatment.  More than 20 years of experience developing customized programs.
