                    DESCRIPTION
                    Situated in the Eixample district, the Hotel Viladomat is just a 10-minute walk from the main-line metro stations and under 1 km from Sants Station: the high-speed train terminal which also offers direct connections to Barcelona Airport terminal 2 and the city centre. Its location in the residential and shopping area of the Eixample makes the Hotel Viladomat the perfect choice for holidaymakers who don’t wish to stay in the crowded city centre but still want to explore the city on foot. The hotel is less than 30 minutes away from the city’s main tourist spots, such as Passeig de Gràcia, La Rambla, Plaça Catalunya, Avinguda Paral·lel… The hotel has spacious guest rooms with air-conditioning and satellite TV. There is free Wi-Fi throughout the building.
