                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Santos Porta Fira has been named the world’s best skyscraper and was the recipient of the prestigious architecture prize “EMPORIS AWARD 2010”. It is located in one of Barcelona’s most rapidly growing financial areas in L'Hospitalet in front of the Gran Via II Trade Fair site, with excellent access to the city centre and airport. It is the perfect choice for business clients as it has 320 guest rooms and suites equipped with state-of-the-art technology, a restaurant, wellness centre and several meeting rooms which include an extendable plenary meeting room of 800 m2 with a maximum ceiling height of 5 metres which makes it suitable for any kind of event.
