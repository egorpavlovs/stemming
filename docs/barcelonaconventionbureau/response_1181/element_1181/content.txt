                    DESCRIPTION
                    Framed in the restored Palau del Mar building since 1993, at the foot of the Mediterranean Sea, but with history and experience accumulated since the 1930's. Mediterranean and seafood cuisine, with a large terrace thathas a capacity for 300 guests. New fully equipped indoor dining rooms where, as well as tasting our gastronomy, to hold meetings, presentations or conferences. We evolve respecting the tradition and the flavor of our dishes.“The Barceloneta, a meeting point”
