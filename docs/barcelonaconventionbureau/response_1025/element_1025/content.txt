                    DESCRIPTION
                    At Alexandra Barcelona Hotel, Curio Collection by Hilton merges quality and efficiency in an environment where avant-garde and traditional meet. In this boutique design hotel, you will find spaces of different styles, with the latest technology and facilities of great comfort, which adapt to the needs of each person and circumstances.The building dates from the 19th century and it is a few steps from Passeig de Gràcia, the fashionable, cultural and commercial heart of Barcelona. It reflects the charm, elegance and spirit of our Barcelona surroundings: you will love the local photography that covers our walls, the design furniture of the 50s and 60s, the original mosaic floors, the framed ceilings and the natural oak - A wide selection of details that will make your stay a simple and pleasant moment. Excellent gastronomic experiences are offered, highlighting the Solomillo Restaurant - a modern and sophisticated brasserie where you can eat meat by weight according to the taste of the diner -, Charcuteria where you can taste traditional Catalan products and an urban oasis converted into Patio, a terrace with great landscaping care with autochthonous plants of the region, which together with the swimming pool makes of this a redoubt of tranquility.
