                    DESCRIPTION
                    Royal Passeig de Gràcia is a design hotel housed in one of the most iconic and architecturally valuable buildings on the avenue.Royal Hotels have refurbished the entire building, preserving original features like the façade with its parabolic forms, the Warren space with its peculiar stacked beams and the spectacular 83,3 Terrace Bar on the top floor which is the only one in the city that offers impressive 300- degree views over Barcelona.Royal Passeig de Gràcia has 124 rooms set out on nine floors. They cater to all types of guests and are decorated in an elegant and cosy style. You can choose from more basic options in the comfort, premium or superior rooms, as well as more luxurious and spacious rooms on the 8th and 9th floor, superior rooms with a terrace, deluxe rooms with a terrace, junior suites and royal deluxe rooms, which all have a private terrace/sun deck and spectacular views of the city.
