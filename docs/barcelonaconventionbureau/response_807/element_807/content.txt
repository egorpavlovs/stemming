                    DESCRIPTION
                    Faircom is a leading company in the production and installation of graphic material for conferences and events in Barcelona. We are a company of Dutch origin with nearly 20 years of experience in the sector. In Barcelona, we work in all the city's venues, always striving to be swift, reliable and flexible, delivering the highest possible level of quality.We work for many well-known and highly demanding international brands at top-notch events like Mobile World Congress, but also for specific events at hotels, the Maritime Museum and La Llotga, for example.Depending on your needs, Faircom can produce and install anything from small vinyl graphics to printed backdrops of hundreds of square metres, signage for hotels and the exteriors of trade fairs, flags, vehicles, etc. We speak 5 languages fluently, so perfect international communication is guaranteed.
