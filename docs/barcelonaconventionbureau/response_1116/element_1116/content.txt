                    DESCRIPTION
                    231 guest rooms and suites awaken your senses with the atmosphere of the city’s most famous boulevard, La Rambla. The rich artistic heritage, represented by Barcelona’s museums, music and architecture, inspires your experience while you enjoy the local culture with all the main attractions within walking distance.The Palau Güell is one of the landmarks that are not to be missed on any visit to the city. Guests can gain access with Le Méridien’s Unlock Art programme. Le Pop cocktail bar stimulates your senses with an exciting blend of cocktails, music and vintage savoir vivre with a modern twist.
