                    DESCRIPTION
                    If you are looking for hotels with gymnasium in Barcelona, ILUNION Barcelona is your Hotel. Located in the most modern, avant-garde area of the city, it is ideal for leisure and business. This four-star hotel stands out for its swimming pool located in a strategic position, and for its careful design, which is suitable for the most demanding guest. It has 244 rooms, free Wi-Fi, and all the services to make your stay an unforgettable experience. The ILUNION Barcelona is ten minutes on foot from the beach, which allows you to get to know the most innovative, irresistible side of Barcelona, and visit those places which have made the Catalonian capital one of the most cosmopolitan places in the world.
