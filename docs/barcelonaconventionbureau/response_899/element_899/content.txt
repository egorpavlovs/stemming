                    DESCRIPTION
                    At d-company we have specialised in the design and management of events since 1996. We organise product launches, exhibitions, incentives, cultural tours and anything our clients may require. We bring a personal and creative touch to MICE (meetings, incentives) services and always offer unique, tailor-made programmes. Our mission is to deliver experiences that appeal to all the senses with initiatives guided by originality and humour, inspiration, flexibility, commitment and innovation. Personalised attention and quality service are our trademarks. Helena Holmström, d-company general manager and partner, works every day to ensure the company is recognised by its good practices as a key player in the destination management companies sector.
