                    DESCRIPTION
                    The hcc Montblanc is situated in the heart of Barcelona, just a few minutes from Plaça de Catalunya, Passeig de Gràcia and the Gothic Quarter. It has 157 modern rooms with air conditioning, heating, LCD satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, minibar, safe deposit box, telephone and a fully fitted bathroom with a bath, hair dryer and auxiliary telephone. On request, the hotel also offers specially adapted rooms for guests with limited mobility.Complementary services at the hotel include the restaurant El Racó del Montblanc, which serves a varied buffet breakfast and Mediterranean dishes à la carte, a car park in the same building, spacious rooms for all kinds of meetings, conferences and congresses, and in the summer season an open-air swimming pool with a terrace, solarium and a bar with live music and a pianist on Fridays and Saturdays.
