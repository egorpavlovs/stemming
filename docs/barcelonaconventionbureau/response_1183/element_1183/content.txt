Hold your event in one of our innovative indoor spaces perfect for meetings, presentations and seminars, or take advantage of the sun outside and enjoy spectacular views of the Mediterranean. We offer the opportunity to book Le Méridien Ra Beach Hotel & Spa exclusively and hold your events with complete comfort and privacy.
