                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Barcelona Universal is just a 10-minute walk from the Ramblas in a location where all points in the city are within easy reach. Just opposite the entrance to the hotel, guests will find the "Paral.lel" metro station (lines L2 and L3) and several bus routes, including direct lines to the beaches and Park Güell. Based at our hotel near the Ramblas, you will be able to enjoy all the attractions of Barcelona.Complimentary Wi-Fi / Two free Business Centres / Outdoor swimming pool (open April - October) / Terrace / Solarium / Fitness Centre / Lobby and bar / Safe deposit box / Daily newspapers / Private car park (additional charge) / Laundry service (additional charge).One of the most attractive features of the Hotel Barcelona Universal can be found on the 10th floor: our swimming pool is surrounded by 2 superb terraces that afford magnificent views of the Barcelona skyline.165 standard rooms + 2 suites with jacuzzi and private terrace
