                    DESCRIPTION
                    Boutique agency headed by owner Hannelore Carzon, a corporate events specialist with more than 25 years’ experience with international organizations.In-depth understanding of the brand experience which has to be delivered with consistent levels of excellence for meetings, conferences, incentives and events - no matter how big or small the audience may be.
