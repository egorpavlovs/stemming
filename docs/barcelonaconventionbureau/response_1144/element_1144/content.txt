                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Roger de Lluria hotel is located in one of the most iconic areas of Barcelona, the Eixample, famous for its cultural and commercial attractions. You are in the heart of modernist Barcelona. The Hotel is surrounded by the city's shopping and cultural districts. Nearby are Passeig de Gracia, Ramblas, Catalunya Square, the Gothic Quarter and Cathedral.The Hotel itself has just 48 rooms, large enough to offer all modern services and facilities including adjoined parking and complimentary Premium wifi. Yet small enough that you feel like a valued guest. Tradition and modern comfort are the essence of Hotel Roger de Llúria, as well as its excellent service. Our Mirall Restaurant offers exquisite Catalan and International cuisine for private dinners and other celebrations, while our Meeting facilities can accommodate up to 350 clients.
