                    DESCRIPTION
                    Located just 30 minutes from the centre of Barcelona, La Roca Village offers more than 130 boutiques of leading international and Spanish brands with savings of up to 60% on the recommended retail price, as well as a wide range of restaurants and cafés to suit every taste, featuring Catalan, Spanish, Asian, Italian, American and continental cuisine. Our Village offers a selection of Premium services including: Tourist Information Centre, free Wi-Fi, multilingual staff, Hands-free Shopping, personal shoppers, gift-cards, the Shopping Express® (the daily coach service which connects Barcelona and the Village), frequent flyer programme and an exclusive VIP room. Recently, we have been awarded with the “Halal” certificate for the excellence in our services.La Roca Village has a dedicated team for incentives and events, offering new experiences “outdoor” with branding, VIP parking and activities up to 4,000 guests, as well as “indoor” in our VIP room with workshops on trends, colour session, enotourism and cocktails to name a few, up to 135 guests.
