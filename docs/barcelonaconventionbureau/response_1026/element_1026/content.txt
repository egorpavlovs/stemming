                    DESCRIPTION
                    Ambiance Incentives is a leading, privately owned Destination Management Company (DMC) and the only Accredited DMC (ADMC) in Spain, with offices in Barcelona and Seville. We offer qualified DMC services for the global meetings and incentives industry throughout Spain, Andorra and Gibraltar.Founded in 1998, we form part of a select club of only 68 DMCs worldwide that have been recognised for meeting the quality standards of ADMEI with regard to insurance coverage, financial stability, business licenses, physical office space, internal organisation, client and supplier references, and which are members of Tourist Offices, Convention Bureaux and international incentive industry associations.Our clients choose Ambiance Incentives because of our:•	Commitment to exceptional customer service•	Constant availability•	Experience and proven local knowledge•	Reliability and strong work ethics•	Willingness to go the extra mile, always
