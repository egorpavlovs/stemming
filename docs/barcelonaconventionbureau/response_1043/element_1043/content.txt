                    DESCRIPTION
                    Grup Limousines is a transport company founded in Barcelona in 1990 and specialising in chauffeured vehicle hire in Barcelona, Spain and throughout the world. Since our beginnings, our key day-to-day objectives have been personalised service and the quest for perfection. As a result, we have become the leading company in the sector. We currently have a team of 75 staff at your service and a fleet of more than 50 vehicles, which include Mercedes E-class, Mercedes S-class, Mercedes Vans, Minibuses, buses and Classic vehicles.Comfort, quality and exclusivity. Let these three words accompany you on your business trips or while you enjoy your holidays in Spain. At Grup Limousines we offer a personalised service of the very highest quality at very competitive rates.Among the services we offer, we would highlight transfers, tailored services and the celebration of special events.
