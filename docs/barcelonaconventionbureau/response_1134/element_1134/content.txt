                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Pestana Arena Barcelona has 84 spacious and modern guest rooms, including junior suites with a terrace and jacuzzi with views of the Olympic mountain of Montjuïc. This boutique hotel also has a fitness room, with sauna and Turkish bath, and an attractive dining area, Lorenzo’s Bar & Restaurant, where you can sample an extensive menu of tapas and a great selection of wines, beers and cocktails. Two well-appointed meetings rooms cater to meetings for up to 30 delegates.The Hotel Pestana Arena is close to the Fira de Barcelona trade fair site and within easy access of the high-speed rail terminal, Sants Estació, as well as metro lines and bus routes, making it the perfect choice for external congresses and your business meetings.
