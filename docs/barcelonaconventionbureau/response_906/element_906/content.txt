                    DESCRIPTION
                    Viajes Munditravel, SL is one of the most important Destination Management Companies in Spain, with more than 50 years' operational experience in the MICE sector. We can point to highly satisfied clients, thanks to the professionalism, experience, flexibility and creativity of our staff.Our products are tailor-made to meet the needs, objectives and budget of our clients, and we pay the greatest attention to the smallest detail.We offer all the services you need to organise your event, both in Spain and in Portugal, drawing on an extensive network of collaborators of proven experience and professionalism. We have organised more than 1,000 events for different groups numbering from 10 to more than 4,000 people.From the outset, you will have a product manager from our organisation at your disposal, who will assist you with all your needs, monitoring operations from hiring through to final billing, thereby avoiding various intermediaries and facilitating communication.
