                    DESCRIPTION
                    The SB Diagonal Zero (4* Sup., 262 rooms of different categories) is situated in front of the Barcelona International Convention Centre (CCIB), with fantastic views of the Mediterranean. The principal attractions of the city are within easy reach by public transport. The hotel has 7 meeting rooms with a capacity of up to 300 people in a theatre style (plenary style with a height of 5 m), all with natural light. Spacious terraces and a large cocktail bar are also available for staging all kinds of events. As in all the hotels in the SB chain, guests are offered free Wi-Fi. Our Spazio Wellness spa provides all kinds of personalised treatments, with a large area devoted to thermal pools and relaxation, including a sauna and a Turkish bath. SB Hotels is a family business which is growing every day as it adapts to the needs of a demanding market. We work together with our clients with the aim of exceeding their expectations.
