                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Soho Barcelona is situated at Gran Via, 543, close to Plaça Universitat, in the heart of the Eixample district.From the hotel, you can walk to Plaça Universitat, Plaça de Catalunya, Passeig de Gràcia, the Ramblas or Plaça Espanya and the Barcelona Trade Fair if you are visiting the city on business.Key tourist sites of interest close to the hotel include the Boqueria market, the Gran Teatre del Liceu, MACBA, CCCB and the Gothic Quarter with the Cathedral, the Picasso Museum and the Palau de la Música Catalana.The Hotel Soho has 51 rooms decorated in a modern, urban style:Basic Room; Standard Room; Superior Room and Superior Room with Terrace.Safe deposit box, minibar, LED television, writing desk, air conditioning, international TV channels, private bathroom, hair dryer, telephone, radio and free Wi-Fi.The Hotel Soho Barcelona offers you all the services of a 3-star hotel in Barcelona.
