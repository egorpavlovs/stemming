                    DESCRIPTION
                    The NH Sants Barcelona is located just 400 metres from Barcelona Sants Station and has modern guest rooms with air-conditioning and central heating, free Wi-Fi, minibar, flat-screen TV and fully equipped private bathroom with complementary toiletries.The hotel serves a full buffet breakfast and has a restaurant serving contemporary Mediterranean cuisine. There is a relaxing bar and café in the hotel lobby. The NH Sants Barcelona, formerly known as the NH Numancia, is located in the heart of Barcelona’s financial district, just a few steps from the trade fair, Fira de Barcelona, and Sants Station. The city centre is just a 15-minute metro ride away and Camp Nou, the home ground of Barcelona football team, is a 20-minute walk. Les Corts is a great option for travellers interested in city breaks, exploring the city on foot and sightseeing.
