                    DESCRIPTION
                    Built in 1919 as an initiative of the Catalan politician Francesc Cambó, and the Swiss hotelier César Ritz, the hotel has fascinated Barcelona locals and visitors alike with its architectural treasures. It is a living symbol of the luxury and glamour that defined 1920s Barcelona and its classical aesthetic, drawing on the splendour of the Belle Époque and including the latest comforts, is the badge of its identity.Salvador Dalí, Xavier Cugat, Elton John, Frank Sinatra, Paul McCartney, Camilo José Cela, and many other illustrious and influential guests of the past century have made their mark on the hotel, captivated by its atmosphere, luxury and distinction. Elegance and sophistication right in the centre of Barcelona.We wish you a warm welcome.Accommodation: 120 guest rooms including 6 Classic Arts Suites.Guest services: Private chauffeur experienced in driving Maybach cars, family friendly, wedding and events planners, guest relations, Clef d’Or concierges, parking and valet parking, 24-hour doorman and desk service, 24-hour room service, gym and spa centre, business centre with Wi-Fi, Chopard toiletries, disabled-accessible hotel and rooms, pet friendly, pillow menus, florist service.
