                    DESCRIPTION
                    The SB Icaria Barcelona (4*, 180 rooms of different categories), is situated just two blocks from the beach and two metro stops from the city centre. It has meeting rooms with capacity for up to 300 people in a theatre style (height of 4.75 m) and various indoor and outdoor areas for staging events of all kinds. There are six meeting rooms which can easily be adapted to meet the needs of the client. As in all the hotels in the SB chain, services include free Wi-Fi connection, a swimming pool, gymnasium, sauna and Turkish bath. SB Hotels is a family business which is growing every day as it adapts to the needs of a demanding market. We work together with our clients with the aim of exceeding their expectations.
