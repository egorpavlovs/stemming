                    DESCRIPTION
                    Hilton Diagonal Mar Barcelona overlooks the Mediterranean Sea, at the center of Barcelona’s shopping and business district, just a few meters away from the International Convention Centre of Barcelona (CCIB). It has 433 rooms, including 110 newly renovated rooms, styled in cool, calm tones inspired by nature that allows to quickly disconnecting from the tiring day.The hotel has 22 flexible meeting and conference rooms with natural light including a Ballroom with capacity for 1,000 people, and a large Hilton Pool Terrace area with a versatile floor space for events of up to 780 people for cocktails or private poolside celebrations. The hotel also boasts a fully equipped Business Center. It also has a gym, and an exclusive restaurant to enjoy an exquisite Mediterranean food, as well as a seasonal Purobeach Barcelona - an urban oasis of happiness and an authentic world of sensations. Purobeach Barcelona was voted one of the best beach clubs in the world by Conde Nast Traveler. Spend the day at the pool, add the pleasure of a relaxing treatment at the spa while listening to the unique Puro Music with the resident DJ’s, and discover the M3 cuisine concept with influences of Miami, Marrakech and Melbourne. Hotel guests will be able to enjoy Purobeach Barcelona sunbeds at special rates. The hotel is located 25 minutes from Barcelona airport and 15 minutes from the city center.
