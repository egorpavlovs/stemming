                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel TRYP Barcelona Condal Mar has 178 rooms and 10 meeting rooms for conventions and business events. The hotel enjoys a perfect location just 800 metres from the International Convention and Congress Centre (CCIB), next to Diagonal Mar shopping mall and the 22@ innovation district, Diagonal Mar (Fórum). The tram and metro stations are just 100 metres away, meaning guests can easily reach any part of the city of Barcelona. In just 15 minutes you can get to downtown Barcelona, the Gothic Quarter and the Borne. Other facilities: free Wifi in the entire hotel; hotel with an innovative design; a few metres from the beach of Mar Bella, in the district of Diagonal Mar; outdoor pool bar with a terrace overlooking the city of Barcelona with panoramic views; gym; breakfast buffet; lobby bar; 10 events and meeting rooms with capacity for 200 people; car park.
