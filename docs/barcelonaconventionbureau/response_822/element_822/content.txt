                    DESCRIPTION
                    Who are we?AP Productions is a company that specialises in providing artistic and technical support for the production and organisation of all kinds of events.Organisers of events, incentives, congresses, product launches, company dinners and all kinds of celebrations can rely on us to help them with our creativity, experience, and our extensive portfolio of artists and technical support.We offer:• ARTISTS AND SHOWS	• Artists	• Thematic Parties (African, Brazilian, Catalan, Medieval, Mediterranean…)	• Music (for dancing, Jazz, Blues, Gospel, Classical, Ethnic, Traditional…)	• Dance (Flamenco, Catalan, dances of the world, modern dance…)	• Theatre (actors, clowns, presenters…)	• Entertainment and variety acts (circus, parades, magic and handicrafts, make-up…)	• Exclusive shows (equestrian…)• TECHNICAL PRODUCTION	• Audiovisuals (sound, lighting, video…)	• Stages, marquees, dance floors, platforms, tiered seating, showcases…
