                    DESCRIPTION
                    Our mission is to work together with our clients on their projects to exceed their expectations and provide them with the very greatest satisfaction.As a PCO, Atlanta travel & corporate events consultants place a team of 70 reliable professionals at your disposal. Our consultancy services go beyond the logistics of organising a congress – technological, financial and communication needs, content management, promotion, marketing, etc. – to apply criteria of sustainability and measure return on investment.We would like you to benefit from the experience we have gained over the 35 years we have served Associations, Corporations and Institutions. We have organised more than 15,000 Congresses and Events with hundreds of thousands of satisfied participants, and this, together with our ISO quality certification, guarantees the success of your event.
