                    DESCRIPTION
                    AC Hotel Gava Mar is an urban hotel with 69 fully-equipped rooms. Hold events, business meetings, or private ceremonies in one of our five beautifully appointed meeting rooms at an unbeatable location next to the beach. Take a dip in our outside swimming pool to relax and find peace from the stresses of the day. The hotel allows for easy access to the historic center of the city, the Gava Museum and Botanic garden, the Minas de Gava Archaeological Park, the financial center of the city, and Barcelona Airport.
