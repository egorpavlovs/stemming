                    DESCRIPTION
                    Hotel Can Cuch is tucked away in a quiet spot in the Montseny Natural Park and offers 11 charming guest rooms. This magical hotel  is housed in a Catalan country house dating back more than a thousand years.We specialise in organising and planning events, conventions, congresses, meetings, incentive programmes and team building exercises. We have three fully equipped meeting rooms.Our restaurant boast spectacular views and serves delicate, creative cuisine drawing on the excellent quality of locally sourced raw ingredients. In addition to the restaurant menu, we have a wide variety of menus for groups and businesses. We offer a wide range of activities, including hot-air balloon rides, hiking trails, treasure hunts, wine tastings and gin and tonic classes.
