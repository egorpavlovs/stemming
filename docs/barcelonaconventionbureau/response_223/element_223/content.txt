                    DESCRIPTION
                    This four star hotel is surronded by gardens and upscale boutiques, making it ideal for those who love city lifestyles and shooping. It stands within one the biggest malls in Barcelona, just a few minutes walk from Palacio de Congresos de Cataluña, AXA Auditorium and the Camp Nou stadium. Excellent connections to the city centre. The ideal hotel for a great experience in Barcelona city.Large rooms in standard, superior, family and suites. Special features like Nespresso coffee machine in all rooms. The majority of rooms have sofa bed, kettel, ironing board kit, bathrobes and slippers and special amenities.
