                    DESCRIPTION
                    The HCCLugano is situated just a few metres from the Fira exhibition centre in Plaça Espanya, with metro and train stations nearby. The airport bus stop is also just a short walk from the hotel.There are 70 bright, modern rooms, with LCD satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, minibar, writing desk, telephone, free safe deposit box, air conditioning, heating and a fully fitted bathroom with a hair dryer, auxiliary telephone and courtesy tray. The hotel has a 24-hour reception service.On request, the hotel also offers specially adapted rooms for guests with limited mobility.The range of services is completed by the bar/cafeteria where a buffet breakfast is served, meeting rooms suitable for all kinds of events, and a car park in the same building.
