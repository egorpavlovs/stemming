                    DESCRIPTION
                    Acta Atrium Palace* * * * is a 4-star boutique hotel located on the famous Gran Via. Its excellent location right in the centre of Barcelona makes it the perfect place to stay both for business travel and holidays as it is within walking distance of all the main attractions.The hotel is housed inside a 19th-century neo-classical building and has 67 guest rooms, fully equipped with everything you’ll need to ensure total comfort.The Hotel Acta Atrium Palace* * * * has several meeting rooms, free WiFi connection throughout the building and a restaurant to ensure you enjoy your stay in Barcelona to the full.
