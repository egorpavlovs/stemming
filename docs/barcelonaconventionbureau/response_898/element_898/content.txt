                    DESCRIPTION
                    Cititravel was founded in 1986 and is a DMC covering Spain and Portugal specialising in top-quality tailor-made programmes for the MICE industry. It has seven strategically located branch offices, with Barcelona being the biggest. Our services include hotel and restaurant bookings, transport, cultural trips and tours, leisure and team-building activities, special-venue hire, arranging entertainments and decorations and fulfilling any audiovisual equipment requirements for meetings and events.Cititravel has won many tourism industry awards. These, together with its quality management certification and multilingual team, make it your ideal partner to work on any project in Catalonia and the rest of Spain.
