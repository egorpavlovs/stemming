                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Acta City47 is a 4-star hotel located just a few minutes away from Sants Station, which provides easy transport links to the city’s main points of interest. Its strategic location makes it ideal for tourists and business travellers.The hotel offers 80 comfortable guest rooms where you’ll find everything you’ll need to enjoy your stay to the full. It also has private parking for its guests, a gym, restaurant and free Wi-Fi throughout the building.The Hotel Acta City47 has a number of rooms for meetings and events and a team of professionals who will ensure they are tailored to your needs.
