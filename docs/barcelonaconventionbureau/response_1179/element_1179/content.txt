                    DESCRIPTION
                    L’Aquàrium de Barcelona gives you the opportunity to host a unique event in a lively setting. In addition to discovering the depths of the sea, your guests will enjoy an unforgettable evening. Business, pleasure and culture come together in a different, elegant and special way.Situated in the old harbour, the Port Vell, L’Aquàrium de Barcelona is the perfect place for a wide range of events. The auditorium is the perfect convention venue which is fortunate to have as its backdrop a Mediterranean aquarium that makes it unique. Planeta Aqua is a multipurpose space surrounded by different tanks. Penguins, rays, tropical fish and piranhas are part of the setting. The terrace boasts spectacular views of Barcelona and is a unique setting for cocktail parties and receptions with appetizers. These are exclusive spaces where you can enjoy unique experiences in the city: the perfect combination for the success of every kind of event.
