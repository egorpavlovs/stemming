                    DESCRIPTION
                    The AC Barcelona Fórum is situated in Barcelona?s new business district. It offers 368 guest rooms, 17 meeting rooms and two exhibition areas and is at the cutting edge of urban hotels designed for events and conventions.The hotel is ideally located for business trips or city breaks, near the CCIB (Barcelona International Convention Centre) opposite the Diagonal Mar Shopping Centre and just 50 metres from the beach.Its 368 guest rooms include 240 standard rooms (four of them adapted for guests with reduced mobility), 80 superior rooms, 40 junior suites and eight suites, meaning that the hotel offers accommodation choices to suit everybody.The hotel has a restaurant, ?Dia i Nit?, which specialises in Mediterranean cuisine,abar?El Café? and 24-hour room service offering all your favourite dishes. The 13th floor covers an area of 700 m2 and has the outdoor swimming pool with a sun deck and the ?Natura Café? which opens during the summer months to offer guests a variety of fresh, light dishes as well as refreshing shakes and cocktails.The hotel?s 17 meeting rooms are distributed over three floors. Most of them have natural light and can host small meetings for ten people as well as large events for up to 560 people.The third floor has seven meeting rooms and two spectacular exhibition areas; the 14th floorhas nine more meeting rooms with uninterrupted views of the sea and city and its own private reception area. The largest-capacity meeting room (up to 560 people approx.) is on the fourth floor, opposite the restaurant.All the meeting rooms have modern, functional furniture,Wi-Fi and can provide all the necessary audiovisual equipment for your event.The AC Barcelona Forum offers everything you?ll need to ensure your events are a resounding success.
