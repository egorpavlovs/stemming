                    DESCRIPTION
                    Set in an enviable location on a hill, surrounded by pine trees and with views of the Mediterranean, just 20 minutes from Barcelona and 10 minutes from the airport, the Gran Hotel Rey Don Jaime is the ideal venue for organising all kinds of professional events (training sessions, conventions, kick-offs, product presentations, etc.).It has 220 double rooms, 20 meeting rooms with natural light, and a Conference Centre equipped with cutting-edge audiovisual technology, with a capacity of up to 350 people.The hotel offers guests free indoor and outdoor parking, free Wi-Fi and free access to a wellness-sport area, in addition to many outdoor spaces which are perfect for organising catering services, teambuilding activities and outdoor training.
