                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Casa Fuster, which has a 5-star Luxury Grand Monument category, is located in the heart of Barcelona, on Passeig de Gràcia. This gem of a building was designed by the Catalan architect Lluís Domènech i Montaner and forms part of the European Modernisme Route.It is a member of The Leading Hotels of the World and offers superior service that is meticulous down to the very last detail, ideal for both leisure and business travellers. The hotel has several spaces for events and meetings; a restaurant serving avant-garde Mediterranean cuisine, the Restaurant Galaxó; a historic café, the Café Vienés, where cultural events are frequently staged; and a panoramic terrace, with a fitness centre, sauna, solarium and swimming pool ? a unique setting with superb views of the city. Guests will also find a reading room which makes the perfect place to relax with a good book.
