                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hesperia Presidente hotel made an impressive addition to the Barcelona skyline when it opened its doors in 1964. Located in the geographical heart of the city, it is still a significant landmark, with easy access to the city?s business district, stores, restaurants, nightlife and tourist attractions, including Passeig de Gràcia.Our spacious rooms have been designed by the renowned interior designer Pascua Ortega. They all include air conditioning and free Wi-Fi, among other features. Reserve a room on the 11th floor or higher for breathtaking views of the city.The Essence restaurant serves beautifully prepared local and international dishes. Additional services include an elegant bar, a solarium on the 9th-floor terrace, and a gymnasium in the same building.
