                    DESCRIPTION
                    Situated on the seafront in the centre of Sitges, the Hotel Calipolis has the perfect location for an unforgettable stay.Each of its 170 rooms and suites is spacious, comfortable and bathed in natural light. The rooms facing the sea afford spectacular views of the Mediterranean and the esplanade.  Conventions play an important role at the hotel, thanks to the 7 conference rooms equipped with the latest technology and a team of events specialists to guarantee the success of every meeting.The Mirador and Infinity restaurants serve the very finest Mediterranean cuisine. Enjoy dishes prepared with products of the highest quality in this wonderful setting by the sea.Sitges is one of the jewels of the Catalan coastline, situated just 35 km from the airport. This is a dream location for a corporate convention or for any celebration close to the sea.
