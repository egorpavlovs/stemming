                    DESCRIPTION
                    The HCC Taber hotel is set in a modernista building designed by the famous Barcelona architect Domènech i Montaner. It is just 8 minutes' walk from the central square Plaça de Catalunya.It has 92 spacious rooms with parquet flooring, flat-screen satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, safe deposit box, telephone, air conditioning, heating, and a fully fitted bathroom with a bath, hair dryer and well-stocked hospitality tray. Our rooms with a terrace retain the characteristic forged iron balconies and large windows to enhance the enjoyment of your stay in Barcelona.Guests will also find a restaurant where buffet breakfasts are served, a bar, and meeting rooms for staging business or family events.The Ramblas and Gaudí's masterpieces Casa Batlló and La Pedrera are just a 10-minute walk from the hotel.
