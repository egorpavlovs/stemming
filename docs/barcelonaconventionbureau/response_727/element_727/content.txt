                    DESCRIPTION
                    Eikonos is an audiovisual service company established in 1985, specialized in assembling and audiovisual support: projection, video walls, LED screens, interactive, HD live production, sound, lighting, and so on; supply and rental of audiovisual equipment with a stock of over 2,000 references and the production and realization of content, editing, post-production audio / video, and 3D graphics effects mappings.We handle the production, implementation, integration and automation control of exhibition spaces such as museums, exhibitions and other fixed installations for all kinds of national and international events with technical competence guaranteed in conventions, conferences, shareholder meetings, presentations, marketing campaigns, fairs , etc.Since its inception, Eikonos constantly incorporates new audiovisual and multimedia technologies, offering advanced solutions that anticipate the needs of its customers. Undoubtedly, the key to success is its team, a group of passionate professionals about image, constantly training, offering sensitivity and accuracy in all his works.
