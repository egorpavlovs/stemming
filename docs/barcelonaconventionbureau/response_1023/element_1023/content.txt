                    DESCRIPTION
                    Almanac Barcelona offers 91 luxurious accommodations including 30 suites.In a prime location, the hotel is steps from Passeig de Gràcia and Plaça Catalunya, where guests can find stylish shopping, restaurants, bars and museums.Almanac Barcelona offers a level of service that is high on engagement, relaxed, personalized and consistent, while a wide range of facilities cater to guests comfort.A Mediterranean restaurant offering an all-day dining and sharing menu where locals and traveler’s lives intersect from morning to night.Azimuth, our captivating panoramic rooftop terrace and lounge. The perfect place to relax and enjoy light bites.Almanac Barcelona offers a choice of five venues for meetings, conferences or special events that can accommodate from 10 to 100 people.
