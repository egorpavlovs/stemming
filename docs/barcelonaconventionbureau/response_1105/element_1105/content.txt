                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Granados 83 is situated on a semi-pedestrianised street just a few minutes away from the Passeig de Gràcia. Elegant and modern with Zen-inspired décor, it has 77 guest rooms, some of them with their own semi-private terrace and swimming pool. Guests can use the business centre, sun deck and gymnasium. Granados 83 offers its guests impeccable service, a relaxed atmosphere and all-pervading peace and quiet. They will also find exceptional works of art in every corner of the hotel. A perfect combination of luxury and culture, it has Buddhist and Hindu artworks on show spanning the 5th to the 19th centuries. The hotel conference room is perfect for meetings, parties and private events and features Roman mosaics from the 2nd to the 4th centuries AD.
