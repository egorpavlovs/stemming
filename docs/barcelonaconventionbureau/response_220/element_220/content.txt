                    DESCRIPTION
                    With 142 rooms, Hotel 4 Barcelona was opened at the beginning of 2010. It is located on the coast in 22@, Barcelona's technological district, just a few minutes away from the CCIB convention centre, the zoo and the Port Olímpic, one of the city's principal leisure areas. The hotel offers a fitness centre, sauna and steam bath. There is also a business centre, which can be used free of charge. The restaurant serves a wide variety of Mediterranean cuisine. The hotel has a car park in the same building and four conference rooms, three of which have natural light.
