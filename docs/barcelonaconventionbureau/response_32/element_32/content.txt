                    WHAT IS BCB?
                    Barcelona Convention Bureau (BCB) is a specialized Turisme de Barcelona programme for business tourism.
                   
        
            
                    HOW CAN WE HELP YOU?
                    We offer support and advisory services for organising and promoting your event. 
                   
        
            
                    BCB TEAM
                    Qualified professionals that will guide you on the organisation of your event. Get in touch with us!
                   
        
            
                    LET'S MEET
                    BCB takes part in the main industry meetings in the world. Find out which events you can find us at! 
                   
        
            
                    BCB MEMBERS
                    More than 300 associate members guarantee our work. Discover the advantages of being a BCB member.
