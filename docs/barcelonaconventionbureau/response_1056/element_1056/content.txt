                    DESCRIPTION
                    A modern, welcoming hotel which is perfect for city breaks or business trips to Barcelona. Ideally located for sightseeing, close to Plaça Espanya, line 3 of the Barcelona Metro, which takes you to the city centre in just 10 minutes, near museums, Montjuïc, the Fútbol Club Barcelona stadium and the Olympic Stadium. This light, bright hotel is located alongside public gardens and offers very convenient access to Barcelona airport and the Barcelona Trade Fair site. It offers a wide range of facilities and has a magnificent restaurant, ?Amalur?, serving signature Basque cuisine.140 guest rooms.Meeting rooms for up to 200 people. ?Amalur? restaurant, executive floor, business centre and garage.
