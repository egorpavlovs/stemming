                    DESCRIPTION
                    SIC is a company directed by:Maite Bouyat: Graduate in Philosophy and Arts, University of Barcelona. More than thirty years of passionate dedication to translation and interpreting.Milagros Cantarell: Graduate in English holding a Diploma in Translating and Interpreting. Extensive experience in translating and interpreting, specialising in Medicine. Professional interpreters who have graduated from the best translation and interpreting schools collaborate with SIC.We have provided language services specialising in conferences and meetings since 1985.Over the years, we have successfully established an impressive portfolio of clients that include Trade Fairs and Exhibitions, national and international companies, pharmaceutical companies, universities, business schools, professional associations, radio and television, professionals from a variety of sectors and a large number of PCOs.Our services:Simultaneous interpretingConsecutive interpretingLiaison interpretingInterpreters for business trips, top-level meetings, visits to centres, factories and installations, both in Spain and abroad.Hire of technical equipment General, specialist and sworn written translations
