                    DESCRIPTION
                    REC HOTEL BARCELONA (ADULTS ONLY)  is a different hotel concept designed for people who love city breaks and want to enjoy the city by mingling with its people, exploring the hidden nooks and crannies of the Born, discovering the food and charm of small bars and restaurants. The hotel has 99 guest rooms in 5 different categories. However, the true heart of the REC HOTEL BARCELONA is the LOUNGE on the 7th floor. An open, outdoor space with magnificent views where our guests are welcomed as friends. At the  REC LOUNGE you can start off the day with breakfast and take part in a number of tailor-made activities, or round off the day with tapas and cocktails and, in the warmer weather, relax in the swimming pool and on the sundeck.
