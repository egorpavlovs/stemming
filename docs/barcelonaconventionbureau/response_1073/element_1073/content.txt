                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Barcelona Catedral has been rated excellent by 95 % of its guests. It enjoys an unbeatable location in the Gothic Quarter near the cathedral, the PlaçaCatalunya and La Rambla.The Barcelona Catedral is a modern and comfortable hotel which was redecorated in 2014. It has all the services you would expect from a boutique hotel as well as a swimming pool, gym, chill-out terrace, car park, free bicycles, a business centre, free Wi-Fi and a meeting room.Its restaurant, ?4 Capellans?, offers à la carte Mediterranean cuisine, a set lunch menu, banqueting facilities, cocktail receptions and catering service. It has a spectacular outdoor terrace which is ideal for events and celebrations.Cultural activities: Wednesday evenings and Sunday mornings free walking tours of the Gothic Quarter, ?Secrets and Legends?.Cooking lessons, Wine tasting dinner and Cooking Team Building for companies and incentives.
