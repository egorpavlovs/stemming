                    DESCRIPTION
                    The 4-star luxury Hotel 1898 is situated on the Ramblas, close to Plaça de Catalunya, in the very heart of Barcelona.This is a hotel whose charming luxurious decoration will transport you back in time to the 19th century and the Spanish colonies of Cuba and the Philippines. A veritable oasis in the heart of Barcelona.Services at the hotel include free Wi-Fi in the rooms and public areas, the Library, Business Centre, 1898 Shop, silent film classics, complimentary Tea Time every day at 5 pm, free access to the spa and gymnasium (pools, sauna and steam bath), a swimming pool (heated in the winter) and a solarium on the top floor of the hotel. LATE BREAKFAST; from 11 am to 1 pm in the Bar H1898 in the lobby.The hotel has 169 rooms, a terrace, a private swimming pool and a relaxation area.
