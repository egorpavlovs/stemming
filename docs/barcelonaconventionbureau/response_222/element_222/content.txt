                    DESCRIPTION
                    The best SteakHouse in Barcelona. A piece of Argentina located in the heart of Barcelona, very close to the most emblematic places in the city. 9Reinas Barcelona was born along with our indisputable values of quality and service. It has a welcoming, intimate atmosphere with private rooms. Our grill, crafted by the hands of our expert 9Reinas Barcelona chefs, brings a special flavour and aroma to our cuts of meat.9Reinas Barcelona is divided into two floors and has a private room, the 9Reinas room, with a capacity of 12 people. The entire restaurant envelops you in a warm and modern, perfect for any occasion. We adapt to our customers’ tastes, whether you want to have a romantic, candlelit dinner for two or hold business or group meals.
