                    DESCRIPTION
                    The fully renovated Meliá Barcelona Sarrià is a luxury city hotel, strategically located in the financial, cultural and commercial heart of Barcelona.Its333 elegant guest rooms, which have all the comforts of a luxury hotel, excellent dining options and spa and fitness area are just some of the reasons why Meliá Barcelona Sarrià is one of the city’s hotels of choice.The hotel also has a modern convention centre, the "Espai Sarrià", which offers 1,900 m2for meetings and events. It has two plenary multi-purpose rooms and 16 breakout rooms fitted with state-of-the-art technology.
