                    DESCRIPTION
                    The H10 Casanova hotel offers a wide range of excellent services and facilities: a welcome drink on arrival, 24/7 reception, luggage storage, a free safe deposit box, elegant, light-filled rooms and a car park. The Ona Lounge Bar features music sessions on Friday and Saturday nights, while the Restaurant Mediterrani serves a delicious buffet breakfast and is also open for private events for groups of 10 or more. There are 7 meeting rooms and free Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel. The Despacio Beauty Centre (open from Tuesday to Saturday) can be found on the second floor, and the 8th-floor Sky Bar terrace has a free solarium, gymnasium and open air plunge pool.The H10 Casanovas offers all the comfort and facilities to be expected of a 4-star hotel, and it makes an excellent choice for leisure and business travellers alike.
