                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Best Western Premier Hotel Dante enjoys a superb location in the heart of the city, just three blocks from majestic Passeig de Gràcia, close to the area of modernista architecture and Gaudí's masterpieces, and a 10-minute walk from Plaça de Catalunya. Whether you are in Barcelona on business or on holiday, the Best Western Premier Hotel Dante is the ideal choice for your stay. If you are looking to shop, you are in the right place. Within a radius of 100 metres, you will find the most elegant and fashionable stores on Passeig de Gràcia and a wide range of restaurants.
