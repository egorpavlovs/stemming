                    DESCRIPTION
                    This hotel is situated in Montbrió del Camp, just a few minutes from the finest beaches on the Costa Daurada and from towns and cities such as Cambrils, Salou, Tarragona and Reus. A notable feature of this location is the peaceful atmosphere of the town, but at the same time various tourist resorts are just a few kilometres away. Our Thermal Spa is oriented towards relaxation and wellness programmes, supervised by a team of therapists. All hydrotherapy techniques are offered, in addition to a wide range of complementary techniques.Guests will find the most precious jewel in the complex surrounded by botanical gardens with century-old plants and sculptures: Aquatonic.With thermal waters covering an area of more than 1,000 square metres, the Aquatonic spa offers a complete recreational thermal circuit: laminar waterfalls, rapid circular hydromassage jets, micro-bubbles, and caves with Greek and Roman baths.Aquatonic is fun, health and relaxation rolled into one; the beautiful surroundings of the complex make this the ideal setting in which to enjoy a highly original experience.
