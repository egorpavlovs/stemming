                    DESCRIPTION
                    The SB Plaza Europa (4*, 243 rooms of different categories) is situated in front of the Fira Gran Via exhibition centre, providing easy access to both the north and the south entrances. The hotel is just two stops from Plaça Espanya on the Ferrocarrils Catalans rail network and it is also on the metro route to the airport. There are eight meeting rooms with natural light for a maximum of 170 people in theatre style (plenary style with a height of 4 m), and large terraces for the organisation of all kinds of events. As in all the hotels in the SB chain, services include free Wi-Fi connection, a swimming pool, gymnasium, sauna and Turkish bath. SB Hotels is a family business which is growing every day as it adapts to the needs of a demanding market. We work together with our clients with the aim of exceeding their expectations.
