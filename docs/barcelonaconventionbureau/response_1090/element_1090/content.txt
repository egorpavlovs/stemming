                    DESCRIPTION
                    A charming boutique hotel with 59 exclusive rooms, located in front of the port of Barcelona, just a few metres from the Ramblas, the Gothic Quarter and the district of Barceloneta. This recently refurbished 19th-century mansion house has a terrace with a solarium, swimming pool, cocktail bar and restaurant on the top floor of the hotel, with wonderful panoramic views over the city's marina. The hotel offers double or twin rooms, as well as family and connecting rooms.
