                    DESCRIPTION
                    Within our hotels in Barcelona Spain, the Vincci Maritimo HotelOffers an impressive sea view and next to the new Conference Palace (CCIB). This hotel is located in a new district called “Diagonal Mar”.Only a few miles from Mar-Bella Beach its all-round glass-front brings the light in to create a unique effect on the architecture and deco. Every bedroom offers plenty of space and natural daylight. The Hotel´s meeting rooms are the ideal place for business lunches and other events. And after a long work day, unwind in our Japanese garden.Very close to “Diagonal Mar” Shopping & Leisure Centre, you will find all high street labels, bars and restaurants at our doorstep.
