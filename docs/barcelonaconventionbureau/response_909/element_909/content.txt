                    DESCRIPTION
                    TerraEvents Spain is the DMC of choice for organisers of meetings and events in Europe, America, Pacific Asia and Africa, with international offices in Italy, Portugal and Francel.For more than a decade now, TerraEvents Spain has organised the most authentic experiences in all parts of Spain. You will be working with accredited local experts who are highly creative, 100% committed and full of passion. They will help you select the ideal components.Based in Barcelona, Madrid and Mallorca, our team (which has formed part of one of the leading DMCs in Spain) knows every corner of the country and will share Spain's secrets and treasures to create memorable experiences.Their professionalism, ethical approach and that special human touch will make planning your event easy and fun!We hope to have the pleasure and privilege of welcoming you as a client of TerraEvents Spain.
