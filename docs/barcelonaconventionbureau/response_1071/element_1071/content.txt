                    DESCRIPTION
                    Hotel Attica21 Barcelona Mar is an urban hotel with 75 guest rooms equipped with state-of-the-art facilities. It offers round-the-clock personalised service guaranteeing convenience and comfort: a perfect and balanced combination that caters to the needs of our guests.Hotel Attica21 Barcelona Mar offers a world of comfort and elegance, with fantastic rooms, a swimming pool and sun terrace, as well as unbeatable views of the Catalan capital. It also has excellent amenities to meet the demands of the business community and its events, convention tourism and, of course, family breaks at any time of the year.
