                    DESCRIPTION
                    The 4-star Hotel Barcelona Center is situated in the heart of the Eixample district. Its unbeatable central location is complemented by top-quality accommodation, excellent gastronomy and cutting-edge technology. With 132 rooms, this hotel was destined to become a new benchmark in its category. In addition to the superb accommodation, there is an excellent choice of spacious rooms for events and banquets, equipped with the very latest technology to ensure that every celebration is a success. Close to elegant Passeig de Gràcia, exclusive stores and the city's business hub, this hotel makes an ideal choice for your stay in Barcelona.
