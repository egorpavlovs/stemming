                    DESCRIPTION
                    Are you looking for an audiovisual partner in Barcelona? We help you make your event a memorable experience. We are specialists in audiovisual integration for all types of corporate events, meetings and congresses.Since 1972, we have combined creativity and technology to provide companies and organizations with tailor-made audiovisual solutions with excellent results. 40 years of experience and a deep knowledge of the technology, always attentive to the innovations of the sector, make us experts in large assemblies.We create innovative solutions and we have an extensive catalogue of audiovisual equipment rental: indoor and outdoor high resolution LED screens, 4K projection, 360 ° projection, video-wall, simultaneous translation, video-mapping, interactive, multi-touch screens, floor screens, streaming, equipment Computing, state-of-the-art sound ...We accompany you in all phases of the event, offering audiovisual consulting, design, technical production, rental, installation, programming, regimentation and assistance.If you are looking for a solid and reliable technology partner, contact us.
