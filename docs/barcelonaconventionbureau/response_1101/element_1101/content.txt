                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Fira Congress Hotel is located just in front of “Fira Gran Via” exhibition center, in L’ Hospitalet de Llobregat, perfectly connects with the city centre (just 100 meters from Metro L9, bus, and FGC train) and with El Prat-BCN Airport with our free 24h shuttle. Coming with your vehicle? No problem, you can use our outside parking spaces or the covered garage. Discover the wide variety of dishes from our daily set menu to our à la carte options from our Mediterranean cuisine in NARANDAM Restaurant, or have a snack and a cocktail at the Bar or the lounge terrace. Fira Congress Hotel offers you modern multifunctional spaces for your event, meeting, banquet, or anything you may imagine, managed by our experimented team, we will lead your event to the perfection in the easiest way for you.In Fira Congress Barcelona Hotel you choose the most suitable room:-	Standard: 23 m2, diaphanous and functional space divided into a large bedroom and bathroom with shower, hairdryer, magnifying mirror, amenities (shampoo, soap, razor and shaving cream, toothbrush and toothpaste), telephone, minibar, flat smart TV with international channels, natural light, nice views towards Barcelona... -	Superior: situated on the upper floors, with great views of Montjuïc and Barcelona, includes Nespresso coffee machine, music equipment, bathrobe, slippers and free late check out at 14:00.-	Junior Suite: 60 m2 gives you the space you need on 2 separable spaces: on one side, king size bed a comfortable desk to work, and a 49 inches smart TV, and on the other side, a sofa, table and another 32 inches TV: relax or have a business meeting, it is your choice. Double bathroom, fitted with a great bathtub with views, as well as the shower. Nespresso coffee machine, water boiler, music equipment, bathrobe, slippers and free late check out at 14:00, are also included.Free services for all our Guests: 2 daily mineral water bottles in the room, WIFI connection in all the spaces, access to the gym, shuttle from and to BCN-El Prat Airport.In brief: Hotel Fira Congress is because of the facilities and the staff team, the perfect place for business trips and relaxing getaways to Barcelona.
