                    DESCRIPTION
                    The NH Collection Barcelona Gran Hotel Calderón is superbly located right in the centre of Barcelona, just 10 minutes’ walk to La Ramblas, and close to the city’s iconic shopping street, Passeig de Gràcia.The hotel reopened in February 2016 following a complete refit and features stunning lighting and an amazing 56m2 video wall that is unique to Barcelona.The hotel is just five minutes’ walk from Passeig de Gràcia metro and has 255 guest rooms spread over 11 floors. The two upper floors boast panoramic views of the city and are decorated in an elegant, modern style.The breakfast features a wide variety of top-quality produce, carefully selected to ensure your day gets off to a healthy, delicious start.The hotel offers 12 fantastic meeting rooms which can accommodate anywhere from four to 300 guests. They are the best choice in the centre of Barcelona for presentations, dinners and meetings.It also has a rooftop swimming pool with spectacular city views.The Don Giovanni restaurant specialises in Italian cuisine and is a wonderful opportunity to sample the dishes by chef Andrea Tumbarello. And you can’t leave the hotel without dropping by the cocktail bar, an eclectic space designed for unique experiences under the expert guidance of one of the world’s top bartenders, Diego Cabrera.
