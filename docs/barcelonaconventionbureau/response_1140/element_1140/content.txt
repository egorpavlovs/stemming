                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Pullman Barcelona Skipper hotel is the ideal port of call for your business trips, since it offers cutting-edge technological resources, every comfort and an excellent location in the city. The hotel has 222 rooms of different categories, including large balconies to enjoy the views of the Mediterranean Sea. The floor dedicated to conventions, with an area of more than 2,000 m2 for meetings and a capacity of up to 600 people, offers the full guarantee of the Pullman Hotels Co-Meeting commitment, including technological support, free Wi-Fi and every assistance from an Event Manager. The two restaurants, two swimming pools, two bars, gymnasium and spa complete the services available to ensure the success of your event.
