                    DESCRIPTION
                    Hotel in the centre of Sant Cugat del Vallés, just 15 km from Barcelona. It has 97 guest rooms and 600 m2 of function rooms for all kinds of events. Particularly attractive are our open spaces, covering an area of more than 500 m2, which are ideal for any kind of al fresco event. We specialise in the MICE market.The Hotel Sant Cugat is modern, comfortable and perfectly combines a practical spirit with warm and stylish decorations. The rationalist design is the work of architect Pep Bonet. The hotel’s extraordinary location in the centre of Sant Cugat makes it the perfect choice for business trips, or visits with family and friends. You can rest and unwind in the centre of town. The Hotel Sant Cugat offers large and well-lit spaces, comfortable rooms, and beautiful gardens and terraces that will make you feel right at home. As we’re located in the centre of Sant Cugat, you can enjoy the historic old town, visit the Romanesque monastery, discover the old market, the Mercat Vell, or browse a wide array of shops and boutiques with all kinds of articles, from design to handcrafted goods. In Sant Cugat you’ll be able to choose from a wide range of bars and restaurants where you’ll be able to unwind.If you want to go at a different pace, we can suggest places to go running, hiking, or cycling.
