                    DESCRIPTION
                    ECIS SL is a registered travel agency (license GC920) based in Barcelona offering services as specialized Destination Management Company for the companies and agencies in the MICE market segment.We provide you with hotel research, room block management,  restaurant reservations,  technical equipment, hostesses & local support,  theme parties, entertainment,  settings and stage design, exhibition (booth-stands),  team building activities, tours, transportation, meet & greet. We have managed several events since 1997, building a widely respected reputation and excellent personalized service to our clients. Our professional team is multilingual, highly experienced and always ready to provide you with creative ideas for your projects!
