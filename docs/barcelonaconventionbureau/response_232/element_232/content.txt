                    DESCRIPTION
                    Inaugurated in 2018, this elegant and historic neoclassical building has an unrivalled location on the fashionable and Modernist Paseo de Gracia where it meets the famous Plaza Cataluña. The establishment has spacious and luminous room with city views.Enjoy the best Mediterranean dishes in our restaurant on the ground floor, with natural daylight and views to Plaça Catalunya. The hotel also has 2 meeting rooms, parking and a gym.Finally, in the spectacular panoramic terrace with a swimming pool and a Sky Bar.
