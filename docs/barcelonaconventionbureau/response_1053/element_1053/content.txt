                    DESCRIPTION
                    This majestic hotel rises up to a height of 107 m and is surmounted by a glass structure which houses the new, exclusive bar and restaurant The Dome. 5.000 m2 of multipurpose function rooms, 20 open-plan rooms and an auditorium for 500 people. An 8-metre-high events room with a floor space of 1,614 m2 that can host up to 1,800 people and has two large terraces.Business centre, conference concierge service, Wi-Fi internet, free shuttle bus to and from the airport and city centre, private parking.280 guest rooms of different categories as well as the personalised service offered by the Tower Club for guests staying in the suites and executive rooms.Our culinary offering caters to banquets, congresses and conventions. The hotel also has the Bouquet restaurant and bar, a private, modern space full of design details. The Azimut restaurant serves breakfasts, lunches and dinners by prior booking only. It offers a Mediterranean-style buffet consisting of light dishes. There is also the chic Axis Bar in the hotel lobby.
