                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Regina is a historic building located in the Eixample district, just 25 m. from Plaça Catalunya and 50 m. from the famous Rambla. The Regina is a 4-star hotel with a timeless elegance and contemporary design and was refurbished in 2016. In the lobby bar, food and drink are served all day. Food and drink are also available from room service. On the ground floor of the hotel you’ll find the recently refurbished El Patio, a light-filled function room which is fully equipped for meetings and events. The guest rooms are soundproofed and have air-conditioning, TV, free Wi-Fi, mini bar, telephone, safe, complementary tea and coffee, and welcome bottle of mineral water. You’ll also find special touches like freshly cut flowers, slippers and bath robes to make you feel at home.
