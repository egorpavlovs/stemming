                    DESCRIPTION
                    Guided bicycle tours and bicycle hire since 1995.In the city of Barcelona: at a relaxed pace, our professional multilingual guides will lead you through and past the most interesting places in the city: the Gothic Quarter, Gaudí's works, parks, beaches, markets... revealing Barcelona's most beautiful nooks and crannies.This is your opportunity to discover Barcelona by bicycle! Our team is ready to help you enjoy an unmissable experience in our city.Outside the city: cycle through vines and take part in workshop activities 30 minutes from Barcelona, in the Penedés region.We love bicycles, nature and wine; so let us invite you to the Penedés to enjoy an unforgettable day out. We will visit wineries and oil mills, sampling the finest wines, caves and olive oils.We also organise workshops: green pruning, olive oil, grape harvest, wine and cava-making, and excursions on foot through the vineyards.
