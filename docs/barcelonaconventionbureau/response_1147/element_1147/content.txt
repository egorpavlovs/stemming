                    DESCRIPTION
                    SB Glow has 235 rooms. Located next to the emblematic Agbar Tower, at plaça de Les Glòries, it is placed in the 22@ district: the innovation district that integrates research, training and transfer of technology centres, with large green areas which areintermingled between Barcelona’s industrial legacy. The Auditorium, Teatre Nacional de Catalunya and the new Mercat dels Encants complete the cultural and leisure offer in the area. SB glow, ideal combination of the latest technology trends and a professional, warm and thoughtful service, has been designed as an advanced, innovative and cosmopolitan hotel.In its two restaurants offer local cuisine based on the diversity of flavours, aromas and Mediterranean colours. A fresh, healthy and close proposal integrated in the city.An innovative Cocktail Bar offer the opportunity to enjoy the privileged Mediterranean weather as well as spectacular 360° views of the city, in a serene, friendly and relaxed atmosphere.Its meeting rooms and common spaces, surrounded by bright interior gardens, offer multiple solutions to hold all kinds of events. Meeting rooms from 35 to 370 square meters, equipped with cutting-edge image and sound technology.
