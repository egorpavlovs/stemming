                    DESCRIPTION
                    Icebarcelona has become an absolute classic and a must-see in the Catalan capital since it opened in November 2007. It is the first ice bar in the world right on the beach designed by world-renowned artists, which offers a cutting-edge and experimental vibe at below-zero temperatures. The perfect place for original events and an experience you’ll remember forever! In addition to the elegant ice lounge, Icebarcelona has a terrace with relaxing views of the Mediterranean and a top-class restaurant and lounge that can host more people.Put in a nutshell, a unique place in an unbeatable location in one of Barcelona’s most popular nightlife hotspots where you can enjoy yourself any time of the day.
