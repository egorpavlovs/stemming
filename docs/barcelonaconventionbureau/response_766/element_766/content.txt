                    DESCRIPTION
                    Expertus Customer Services, part of the Expertus Group, is a business initiative specialising in the customer service sector and in managing teams of staff for events, museums, cultural centres, information points, congresses and trade fairs.We believe every project is unique and deserves personalised attention. Our priority is to pinpoint, understand and anticipate the needs of our clients. Thanks to our extensive professional network, we are able to contract suitable staff and attend to the needs specific to each case.We work on three levels of specialisation:Professional environments	We know that different environments receive different visitors. Giving them the best possible service is our goal.Professional profileWe select unique profiles for unique positions. Suitable profiles for each service and environment.Types of serviceWe cover the whole range of customer services.
