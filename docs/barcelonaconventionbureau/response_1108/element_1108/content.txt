                    DESCRIPTION
                    The HCC Regente hotel is set in a traditional modernista building in the heart of Barcelona. It is decorated in an Art Nouveau style with wrought iron balconies. The hotel is situated in elegant Rambla de Catalunya, just 10 minutes' walk from the Ramblas.It has 79 modern rooms with air conditioning, heating, LCD satellite TV, free Wi-Fi, minibar, safe deposit box, telephone and a fully fitted bathroom with a bath, hair dryer and auxiliary telephone.Guests are offered rooms with a private terrace, meeting rooms, and in the summer season an open-air swimming pool with a bar on the rooftop terrace, which affords spectacular views of the city. The hotel also has a restaurant where a varied buffet breakfast is served.
