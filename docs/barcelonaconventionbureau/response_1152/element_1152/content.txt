                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Silken Ramblas is situated in carrer Pintor Fortuny, just a stone's throw from the world-famous Ramblas. It has 105 rooms, all with parquet flooring, television, telephone, fully fitted bathroom, safe deposit box, minibar and electric kettle. The top floor of the Silken Ramblas has a terrace with a swimming pool. It offers unrivalled views over the centre of the city.The events rooms at the Hotel Silken Ramblas Barcelona are ideal for staging all kinds of functions. Choose between the MACBA room and the LICEO room. Both are equipped with the latest audiovisual resources and their hire is enhanced by excellent, highly qualified service. There is also the additional advantage of the hotel's location close to the Ramblas: your event can be staged in the very heart of Barcelona.
