                    DESCRIPTION
                    H10 Itaca is situated in the Eixample district, very close to the trade fair area in Plaça Espanya and just a few metres from Sants station (AVE high-speed train). Recent renovations at the hotel have included the lobby, with its warm and elegant interior design, and the El Jardí terrace, which offers a bar, a hammock area and a plunge pool.
