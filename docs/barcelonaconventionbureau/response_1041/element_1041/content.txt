                    DESCRIPTION
                    The hotel has a perfect location between the Gothic district with its spectacular Cathedral or the ancient city walls and “el Born”, right beside the commercial neighborhood, various cafés and restaurants.Grand Hotel Central has 147 rooms and a spacious rooftop with breathtaking panoramic views of Barcelona, the Skybar, which offers a relaxed lounge area and one of Europe’s most famous infinity pools with city views, together creating an oasis among the urban setting of the Gothic Quarter. The City Restaurant provides a taste of market fresh Mediterranean cuisine with a cosmopolitan twist and the City Bar offers all day dining including salads, tapas and a selection of signature cocktails created by renown mixologist Manel Vehí, all  surrounded by good music in a relaxing atmosphere. Wellbeing is taken care of with the Wellness Suite and its carefully designed treatments, a Thermal Suite that offers a private steam and sauna experience and a well-equipped Fitness Center.The hotel also has exclusive spacious event and meeting rooms with natural light and spectacular views of the city, which can be personalized for multiple kinds of events.
