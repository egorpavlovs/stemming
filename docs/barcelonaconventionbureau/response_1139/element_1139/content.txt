                    DESCRIPTION
                    You will find Hotel Pulitzer Barcelona in the 8 of Bergara street, right in the heart of the city of Barcelona. You will be able to find us a few steps away from Las Ramblas and Paseo de Gracia.The Pulitzer is a boutique hotel located in a renovated 19th-century building where natural light has been incorporated via a skylight. It is equipped with meeting room, library, private patio, roof top terrace, a brand-new restaurant called The Greenhouse and a lobby bar. The Pulitzer is being certified as a Biosphere hotel since 2015.Hotel Pulitzer has fully equipped rooms decorated with avant-garde materials. In these soundproofed rooms, you will find an European atmosphere, lit by natural light, which make of this space a cozy and unique place. Rooms are soundproofed and equipped with air-conditioning, mini bar, safety box, satellite TV, free coffee and tea set and welcome mineral water, laundry service and free of charge Wi-Fi in the entire hotel.
