                    DESCRIPTION
                    Hotel Arts Barcelona is an urban resort in the heart of the city, with spectacular views of the Mediterranean Sea. It is located just a short distance from Barcelona's historic center and a 20-minute drive from the airport. Counts with a wide range of rooms, suites and duplex penthouses, which are characterized by its luxury, amplitude, and views.Enjoy six restaurants where you can taste the best avant-garde cuisine, from a light and casual breakfast to the most exclusive cuisine awarded with two Michelin stars. Relax by the outdoor pool or in any of two floors with terraces and gardens. Discover 43 The Spa, an urban sanctuary on the top of the highest building in Barcelona, with hydrotherapy pool, sauna, steam bath, rooftop terrace, and a wide range of treatments.Facilities: parking, gym 24/7, shops, hairdresser, iLounge and Club Lounge.
