                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Olivia Plaza Hotel is a magnificent 4-star boutique hotel in Plaça Catalunya, right in the centre of Barcelona. The Olivia Plaza has a garden, restaurant, parking, terraces and meeting rooms as well as free Wi-Fi. Its 113 guest rooms have views of Plaça Catalunya and the Gothic Quarter, to ensure you enjoy an exclusive, unforgettable stay in Barcelona. All rooms are soundproofed to make sure guests get their rest despite being right in the city centre. There are also six spaces for business meetings, celebrations, parties and corporate events. Guests have access to the Holmes Place Urquinaona gym. At the Olivia Plaza Hotel you’ll also find the Nineteen Restaurant, where you’ll be able to enjoy genuine Barcelona cuisine indoors or on the terrace.
