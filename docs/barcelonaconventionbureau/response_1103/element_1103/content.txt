                    DESCRIPTION
                    With its fascinating surroundings and convenient location in one of Barcelona’s coolest neighbourhoods, Four Points by Sheraton Barcelona provides a functional meeting space in the heart of the business district.Its striking, simple and welcoming design and high service standards will assure your next event is a real success.Its three meeting rooms are flexible, fully equipped and soundproofed, with high-speed internet connection and state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment. Our spaces can accommodate up to 75 people.Natural light and the terrace create the perfect atmosphere for inspiration.You can also choose from a wide range of culinary proposals created by our chef. And if you want to add a unique touch to your meeting, you can personalise your menu according to your needs.The hotel also has a gym on the top floor boasting breathtaking views of the city.
