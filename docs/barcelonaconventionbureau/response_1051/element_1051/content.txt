                    DESCRIPTION
                    Hard Rock Café Barcelona blends the local ambience of Catalonia with our inspiring, creative cuisine and celebrated music memorabilia. Located in Plaça de Catalunya beside the renowned Las Ramblas promenade, our restaurant brings its world-famous service to the heart of historic Barcelona.Barcelona is one of the most vibrant, exciting and sophisticated cities in Europe, with its elegant boulevards, brightly coloured shops and cafés, and highly detailed Gothic art and architecture. Our menu fuses the flavours of this iconic city with American dishes to create genuine works of art. Choose from classic American staples like our Legendary® Burger or try something new with our main courses prepared with local ingredients.Our restaurant has an upstairs area, a ground floor with a private room, and an outdoor terrace, where you can people watch and soak up the energy of the Ramblas.
