                    DESCRIPTION
                    Situated in Avenida Paral·lel, the hotel is close to the Ramblas and the port, as well as Montjuïc, the Magic Fountain and the Palau Sant Jordi. From the Hotel Paral·lel, all places of tourist interest are within easy reach, and you can travel by metro to Plaça de Catalunya or the beach.The comfort and convenience of the facilities at this hotel make it the perfect choice for both leisure and business travellers. There is a Business Centre with free Internet access, a TV lounge, laundry service, breakfast service and an outdoor public car park opposite the hotel. Small pets are accepted at an additional cost.The Hotel Paral·lel has 64 modern rooms: CLASSIC INDIVIDUAL / STANDARD DOUBLE-TWIN / FAMILY JUNIOR SUITE, individual air conditioning, background music, free Internet access, satellite TV, private bathroom, hair dryer, minibar (additional charge), laundry service, free safe deposit box, telephone.
