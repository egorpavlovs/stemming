                    DESCRIPTION
                    The HCC St. Moritz is set in an elegant, classical-style, 19th-century building, which was restored as a hotel in 1990. It is a 5-minute walk from two of Gaudí's most famous buildings: Casa Batlló and La Pedrera.There are 91 spacious modern rooms with LCD satellite TV, free Canal+, free Wi-Fi, minibar, telephone, free safe deposit box, air conditioning, heating, and a fully fitted bathroom with a bath, hair dryer, slippers, amenities and auxiliary telephone. On request, the hotel offers specially adapted room for guests with limited mobility, as well as family rooms.  Additional services at the hotel include the ?St Gallen? restaurant, which serves breakfast, lunch and evening meals, a bar, meeting rooms ideal for all kinds of events, a car park in the same building, and a terrace that opens in the summer months.
