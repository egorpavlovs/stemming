                    DESCRIPTION
                    Enjoying a superb location in the Vila Olímpica district, just 25 minutes' walk from the Gothic Quarter, the H10 Marina Barcelona hotel offers an outstanding range of services. It has four spacious meeting rooms with a capacity of up to 220 people and several areas bathed in natural light which are perfect for coffee breaks and/or cocktails. The Dionissos restaurant with its excellent signature cuisine is an ideal setting for working lunches/suppers and banquets. The Skyline terrace with its swimming pool, solarium and bar that opens in the summer makes an excellent option for private cocktails, affording spectacular views over the city and the sea. The Junior Suite rooms and the exclusive Privilege service, in addition to the Despacio Spa Centre, provide exquisite touches to enhance our guests' experience of the hotel.
