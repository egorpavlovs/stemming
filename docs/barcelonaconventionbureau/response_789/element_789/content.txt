                    DESCRIPTION
                    Barcelona Guide Bureau is a travel agency specialising in a wide range of exclusive tours in and around Barcelona and Catalonia. We have our own team of multilingual official guides who are experts in different fields. Our main services are: Private tours. We provide advice and design exclusive themed tours tailored to the needs of each client. We can book fast-track tickets at any museum or landmark.-	Barcelona Daily Sightseeing Tours. Guided tours in English for individual clients, designed to ensure they get the most out of their visit to Barcelona.*** We are committed to sustainability and offer activities on alternative means of transport and guided tours of the new smart and sustainable Barcelona.
