                    DESCRIPTION
                    PUZZLE is a Barcelona-based company that produces a wide range of tourist merchandise, with several collections and different designs.It new collection, HIDRÁULICA BARCELONA, is inspired by the hydraulic tiles typical of the Catalan art nouveau, or modernista, period. The items in the collection spell out the word BARCELONA.This colourful collection has been designed with the discerning tourist in mind.The collection includes a tote bag, mug, 16cm x 16cm tray, salt and pepper set, chocolate bar, purse, A6 note pad, 8x8 wiro notebook, bookmark, magnetic clip, ruler, fridge magnet, fan, drinks mat, etc.
