                    DESCRIPTION
                    LOEWE started as an association of leather craftsmen in the centre of Madrid in 1846. These were the humble beginnings of what is now one of the world's leading luxury brands. A commitment to modernity has been a key factor in the development and expansion of the brand. In October 2013, Jonathan Anderson was appointed Creative Director. Under his leadership, LOEWE is building on a legacy of 170 years of craftsmanship and innovation as it strides into the future.
