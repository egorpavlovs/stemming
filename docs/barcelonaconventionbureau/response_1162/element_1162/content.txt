                    DESCRIPTION
                    This iconic, cutting-edge building stands on the Barcelona beachfront. Designed by renowned architect Ricardo Bofill, W Barcelona sets the scene for a spectacular stay. Check into one of our 473 guest rooms, which include 67 suites, two Wow suites and one Extreme Wow suite and enjoy panoramic views of the city and the sea.Located on the beachfront by the Barceloneta boardwalk, W Barcelona is close to the Mediterranean coast and just a short distance away from the Gothic Quarter and Born district.Since it opened in 2009, W Barcelona has never ceased to amaze. It offers a wide range of restaurants, including WAVE, SALT and Bravo24, where Michelin-starred chef Carles Abellserves up Barcelona-inspired cuisine.
