                    DESCRIPTION
                    Personalized corporate experiences!At Obrint Via we work closely with corporations to provide them with tailor-made solutions for both competency and leisure-based training.Since 1998 we have been inspired by the values and benefits of sport, the thrill of the outdoors, being at one with nature, along with the artistic and cultural heritage of Catalonia.Our Top 10•	Be Catalan for a day!•	Barcelona Mosaic: 4X1•	Gaudi Photo Competition•	Photo Safari by bike: from Barcino to 21st century Barcelona•	1992 Olympic Games•	Adventure Racing in Montjuic or Collserola•	Canoeing and Stand-Up Paddle Boarding: see the Barcelona Skyline from the sea!•	Art Workshops: 4 schools, 4 styles•	Wine experiences•	Quinabarra Cooking WorkshopsWe have a professional, multi-skilled team who is highly-qualified and committed.We guarantee the best results in all your projects.We are ready to take on your challenges!
