                    DESCRIPTION
                    The U232 Hotel is situated in the district of Eixample, a quiet residential neighbourhood planned by Ildefons Cerdá in 1860 and home to Gaudí's most famous works: the Sagrada Familia, La Pedrera and the Casa Batlló, in addition to many other jewels of modernisme.The elegance and convenience of the centrally located U232 Hotel is enhanced by meticulous service and attention to detail, with the ultimate aim of giving guests the greatest satisfaction.Free Wi-Fi throughout the hotel / Business Centre / Laundry service / Daily newspapers / Book-crossing library / Specially adapted access and facilities / Car park (additional charge) / Safe deposit box / Room Service until 10.30 pm / Terrace with solarium / GymnasiumThe U232 Hotel has 102 rooms:Single rooms, standard doubles (interior rooms), doubles, doubles with a balcony, (family) triples and Top rooms with a terrace.Individual air conditioning / Free Wi-Fi / LCD TV / Fully fitted bathroom with hair dryer / Minibar (additional charge) / Safe deposit box (with room for a laptop)
