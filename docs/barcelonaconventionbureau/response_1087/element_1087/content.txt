                    DESCRIPTION
                    The Hotel Colón is located in the Gothic Quarter in the heart of Barcelona's historic old town with its cultural attractions and shops. It stands right opposite the cathedral and is close to the Plaça de Catalunya and the port. Put in a nutshell, it is in one of the city's most vibrant areas. All our 141 guest rooms are spacious and fully equipped and many of them boast wonderful views of the cathedral. The Hotel Colón has meeting rooms with natural light and all the technological features you need to host any event. Our guests can also enjoy a new spa area which offers a variety of different massages and facial and body treatments.
