	    Industry-related Content
	




	
	Singapore Revealed Wallpaper* Supplement
	
	In 2011, Wallpaper* flew into Singapore for a longoverdue and in-depth look at the country’s burgeoning creative scene. As we documented in the subsequent Singapore Revealed (W*146) we found plenty to admire – in particular, the young cohort of designers, chefs and architects who were building on the pioneering works of the previous generation, and who were pushing into new frontiers of space, taste and silhouette.

	
	More details
	




	
	A Historic Landmark Transformed
	
	National Gallery Singapore occupies the premises of the former Supreme Court and City Hall. Opened in November 2015, it is Singapore’s newest museum and houses the largest public collection of modern art in Singapore and Southeast Asia.
Located in the heart of the Civic District and just a three-minute walk from City Hall MRT Station, it is a cultural attraction not to be missed. 

	
	More details
	




	
	Furnishing creative conversations: Singapore Design Week
	
	Presented with the UNESCO City of Design accolade in 2015, Singapore advocates a strong collaborative creative spirit. This is none better demonstrated through Singapore Design Week, an event with an emphasis on the synergy of ideas. Check out last year’s highlights that set the design community abuzz.

	
	More details
	




	
	Showtime in Vegas: Singapore at IMEX America 2015
	
	From serving up local cuisine to displaying the best of Singapore design, the Singapore Exhibition and Convention Bureau (SECB) thrusted the country into the spotlight and yielded business opportunities at last year’s IMEX America. Learn how this was achieved at the largest exhibition for meetings, incentive travel and events in North America.

	
	More details
	




	
	Singapore Pavilion draws crowds at IBTM World 2015
	
	At IBTM world 2015, the Singapore Exhibition and Convention Bureau (SECB) led a delegation of 10 local partners to promote Singapore as a leading events destination. Find out how the SECB booth turned heads with a 360-degree virtual reality station and a vertical garden made of 1,500 orchids.

	
	More details
	




	
	Uncovered: Distinctive Local Souvenirs for the Jet-setting Executive 
	
	Looking for the perfect piece of Singapore to bring home? Nothing says local more than a rich marmalade created after the Singapore Sling cocktail, or wooden blocks modelled after iconic landmarks such as the Singapore Flyer and Esplanade. No matter which souvenir you pick, each is sure to leave a lasting impression.

	
	More details
	




	
	An Upscale Space for Events in the City 
	
	With the opening of The South Beach, Singapore's bustling MICE sector sees the addition of yet another world-class venue. Featuring interiors by renowned French designer Philippe Starck and 19 modernistic meeting rooms, see how the hotel caters to all your professional needs while offering a unique stay in a historical building.

	
	More details
	




	
	The historical, indigenous, and contemporary are all blended in Singapore’s design movement
	
	Singapore has long been known for its thriving economy, a strength that has shaped a largely business-oriented culture. But in recent years, the city has begun a coordinated effort towards fostering a local design, art, and architecture scene. 

	
	More details
	




	
	Designed to  shine: The best of Singapore’s design scene
	
	Singapore has long been considered an up-and-coming destination for design professionals and enthusiasts— its recent UNESCO Creative City of Design accolade bears testimony to its standing and continued ambition. 

	
	More details
	




	
	3 Reasons why Singapore is Destined to be the Next Design Capital of Asia
	
	In a worldwide economy that’s increasingly idea-driven, design has found its place as an important enabler to promote change, solve problems and improve the way we live.

	
	More details
	




	
	Designed in Singapore, Designed for Success
	
	In this iconic year that is Singapore’s 50th birthday, her economic success story has been well related to all and well celebrated. But less familiar are we with the design scene in Singapore which has also grown and flourished in these last fifty years as well. 

	
	More details
	




	
	The Singapore Story, a six-part report
	by DesignSingapore Council
	Singapore is a modern city-state located in Southeast Asia, with a Westminster system of parliamentary democracy. It has a free-market economy, supported by multinational corporations, solid financial institutions and world-class universities.
