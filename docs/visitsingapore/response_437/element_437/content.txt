		Singapore is a city of limitless opportunities; home to traditional artisans, culinary craftsmen, and passionate artists alike. To optimise your experience, maximise your enjoyment, and navigate the city with ease, download the Visit Singapore Travel Guide app (available on iOS), and gain access to endless possibilities in the palm of your hand.
	


        Finding your tribe

		
        

		
        

		
        

		
		Food lovers who revel in the multisensory experiences of gastronomy. Travellers who yearn for new stories and unseen sights. Art lovers with a deep appreciation for culture, craft, and inspiration.
Whatever your passion, you’re bound to find kindred spirits and uncover new possibilities in Singapore. Download the Visit Singapore Travel Guide app, and discover all that you can become in Singapore, whether you’re a Socialiser, Explorer, Foodie, Collector, Action Seeker, or Culture Shaper.
Optimising your in-app experience
	


        Experience Singapore

		
        

		
        
			
                
                
                  
			
		

		
        

		
		To explore new experiences or just to get up to speed on the activities offered in our bustling metropole, check out Discover Singapore on the app’s homescreen. Our articles will clue you in on upcoming events and lifestyle offerings both in the vicinity and around the island, including recommendations for nightlife, accommodation, shopping experiences, tours, and food. You can also discover places of interest according to your tribe.
	


        Gather your essentials

		
        

		
        
			
                
                
                  
			
		

		
        

		
		First time in Singapore? Get your bearings by tapping on the Essentials tab, a nifty, all-in-one feature that answers your travel questions, from currency exchange rates to nearby free Wi-Fi hotspots.
 
 
	


        

		
        

		
        
			
                
                
                  
			
		

		
        

		
		Get contextual recommendations with the app’s smart functions, such as free Wi-Fi hotspots, food recommendations, and places of interest, depending on the time of the day.
 
 
	


        Find directions and more

		
        

		
        
			
                
                
                  
			
		

		
        

		
		Every traveller knows the joy of immersing one’s self in the sensory experiences of a new city.
The journey is just as important as the destination; you can discover other places of interest along your route. Besides getting directions to your destination, the app will recommend a number of interesting locales along the way.
	


        Save your Favourites

		
        

		
        
			
                
                
                  
			
		

		
        

		
		Save places of interest in your Favourites list for easy access on the go.
	


        

		
        

		
        

		
        

		
		Download Visit Singapore Travel Guide now and live out your passions in the city today.
