        Cruise LinesDeparting from Singapore
        As a cruising hub that is ideally located at the heart of Southeast Asia, Singapore is the perfect place from which to take off and explore the old-world charms as well as exciting new world attractions of neighbouring cities and beyond.
Embark on your cruise holiday by booking a package with any of these leading cruise lines today.


        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Star Cruises
	                    
	                    
	                        Set sail to enchanting Asian destinations where the sun, sand and sea converge in perfect harmony; whether it be exploring marine life at Redang or rejuvenating over a relaxing massage in Phuket.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Royal Caribbean International
	                    
	                    
	                        A true destination on its own that WOWs you with first-at-sea on-board features such as an ice skating rink, shopping promenade and DreamWorks Entertainment. Experience Asia in a different way.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Princess Cruises
	                    
	                    
	                        Let Princess be your guide to Southeast Asia and the world. Topping the charts for some of the best itineraries, sail to ports including Bangkok, Bali, Hong Kong and more. Experience travel that transforms and come back new with Princess.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Costa Cruises
	                    
	                    
	                        The Carnival of Venice at Sea – enjoy the sights, sounds and tastes of Italy's famous masked festival. What's more? Children under 13 years travel for free, if they stay in a cabin with two adults, on this family-friendly cruise.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Celebrity Cruises
	                    
	                    
	                        Modern Luxury defines Celebrity Cruises, with cool, contemporary design and warm spaces, dining experiences where the design of the venues is as important as the cuisine, and the amazing service that only Celebrity can provide.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Seabourn
	                    
	                    
	                        The heralded arrival of Seabourn's young and contemporary fleet of ships in early 2017 is the perfect blend of glamour, impeccable service, exquisite cuisine and highly personalised experiences in the ultra-luxury market.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Azamara Club Cruises
	                    
	                    
	                        Destination-immersive experience with Longer Stays, More Overnights and Night Touring, boutique ships, extraordinary service and fine cuisine. All while sailing to a host of destinations larger ships cannot reach.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Holland America Line
	                    
	                    
	                        Discover Asia onboard Holland America Line’s mid-sized ship with elegant lounges adorned with art and antiques, spacious staterooms with private balconies, delicious dining and state of the art leisure amenities.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Silversea
	                    
	                    
	                        Equivalent to a 5-star hotel, sail around the world with Silversea, with long haul cruises across Asia, Europe, the Americas and even Antartica.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        Coral Expeditions
	                    
	                    
	                        Linger in historical towns, pristine marine parks and postcard perfect islands as Coral Expeditions brings the same immersive style, Australian hospitality and expedition team to guests in experiencing new Asian voyages.

	                    
	                
	            
	        
        
        
        
	        
	            
	                
	                
	                    
	                        TUI Cruises
	                    
	                    
	                        Unwind aboard TUI Cruises’ premium, well-being concept. Guests will enjoy an all-inclusive array of dining, spa, and entertainment, and top-notch service on board.
