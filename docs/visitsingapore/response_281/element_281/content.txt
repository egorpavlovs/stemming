	    Overview
	




	
	    
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
    
            
		
		
            
		








	
	
	
	Make your event a memorable one with W Singapore - Sentosa Cove. This designer hotel is situated on Singapore's resort island, Sentosa, where you can mix your event with business and pleasure.

The hotel has more than 1,500 sqm of meeting venues, including a 720 sqm Great Room, a grand space dominated by dazzling chandeliers. All 12 spaces are well-equipped with the most comprehensive meeting facilities on the island, which includes fully adaptable conference equipment and world-class audio-visual facilities.

W Singapore is the only BCA Green Mark Platinum awarded-hotel in Sentosa. Among its initiatives is having Sustainable Meeting Practices (SMP) in place. SMP is designed to enhance meetings while remaining cost-effective and operationally efficient. This can be seen from their sustainable menu choices, bottled water alternatives, and energy-efficient digital signages. Throughout the hotel, the sustainability techniques developed have resulted in a reduction of 11mil kWh in energy expenditure and 140,000m3 of water yearly.
