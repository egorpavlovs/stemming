	    Overview
	




	
	    
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
    
            
		
		
            
		








	
	
	
	Singapore's newest, largest and most flexible conference and meeting venue is located in the heart of the Central Business District, and is nothing short of spectacular. Sands Expo® and Convention Centre at Marina Bay Sands is an award-winning event venue and the perfect setting for high-profile MICE events such as regional and international conventions, trade shows and exhibitions. 
With 120,000 square metres of event space, the venue can host conventions of up to 45,000 delegates, support 2,000 exhibition booths and accommodate 250 meeting rooms. Sands Expo and Convention Centre has the largest ballroom in Southeast Asia, able to host 11,000 guests at a time. Be it intimate meetings or huge conventions, the meeting space is highly flexible and can be tailored to your event's needs.
Located at the heart of Singapore's Central Business District area, visitors and delegates can enjoy world-class entertainment, shopping, dining, arts and culture, and accommodation options under one roof. Singapore’s many other attractions are all within easy reach.
Marina Bay Sands champions green meeting goals and Sands Expo is the first green venue in Southeast Asia to attain ISO 20121 certification for event sustainability management. The integrated resort is also the first facility in Singapore to adopt the Singapore Tourism Board’s new sustainability guidelines for the MICE industry in 2013.
