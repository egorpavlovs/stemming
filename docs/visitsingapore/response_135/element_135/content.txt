	    Industry-related Content
	




	
	Experience Creation in the Tourism Industry
	
	The Singapore Tourism Board has created two videos to help tour operators drive more value for today’s discerning tourists. Through these videos, operators can learn how to develop innovative and immersive experiences in order to boost tourist spend and repeat visitorship.

	
	More details
	




	
	Staying Ahead of the Competition
	
	With Asian cities in the region fast catching up with Singapore as global business hubs, MICE industry veteran Edward Liu sheds light on what this tiny city-state needs to do to maintain an edge over its competitors.

	
	More details
	




	
	Harnessing the here and now: Tax Free World Association (TFWA) Asia Pacific Conference & Exhibition
	
	Tax Free World Association (TFWA) Asia Pacific Conference & Exhibition, a prestigious duty-free travel retail trade show held in Singapore, saw yet another fruitful edition in 2015. Find out what drew in crowds at last year’s event.

	
	More details
	




	
	TravelRave 2015 Report: Catalysing Travel and Tourism in Asia 
	
	Get the key insights shared at TravelRave 2015 and learn more about Asia’s burgeoning travel sector in this report. Find out about the latest online travel and technology trends, the growing potential of business travel in Asia, and the state of human capital within the industry.

	
	More details
	




	
	Asian Business Travellers: Five things you need to know
	
	The report presented at the Asia Travel Leaders Summit explores the consumer habits of Asian Business Travellers (ABTs) amidst an ever-changing landscape of digital platforms and flexible travel policies. It also examines its significance within Asia, a region that’s fast becoming the epicentre of business travel.

	
	More details
	




	
	Expansion and Evaluation in Asia's Travel and Tourism Industry: Discussions and Discourse at TravelRave 2014
	
	Since its inception in 2009, TravelRave has grown to become Asia’s premier travel and tourism event. 2014 saw a line-up of quality conferences and exhibitions, including inaugural events such as Hotel Management Summit and RaveOn! Tours. Learn about the year’s insights and findings, as well as megatrends that paved the way for the industry.

	
	More details
	




	
	The Asian Millennial Workforce and the Travel Industry
	
	With the Asian travel industry driven largely by millennials, managers need to fine-tune their approaches to accommodate the diverse values and expectations of this new generation workforce. Through this report, learn how understanding, developing and retaining millennials within your organisation can help you gain a competitive advantage.

	
	More details
	




	
	Navigating the Next Phase of Asia's Tourism
	
	In the 2013 edition of TravelRave, thought-provoking content and influential speakers cast the spotlight on the future of Asian travel. With Asia driving international tourist growth and new technologies fuelling change, find out steps that were taken to successfully manage these prospects.

	
	More details
	




	
	Capturing the Asian Millennial Traveller
	
	What are the ways to target Asian Millennial Travellers (AMTs) - discerning individuals who desire flexible travel, value research yet exhibit a lack of brand loyalty? Through this report, learn how to capitalise on this up-and-coming target segment and in turn, create tremendous opportunities.

	
	More details
	




	
	Opening Borders & Possibilities
	
	Home to the world's best airport, Singapore Changi Airport, which is situated in the heart of the ASEAN region, millions of business and leisure travelers benefit each year from Singapore's wide-reaching connectivity as they transit to neighboring countries and greater parts of Asia and the world. That reputation as an established travel hub appears to be strengthening as the global travel industry undergoes a digital transformation.

	
	More details
