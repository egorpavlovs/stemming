	The Conference Ambassador Programme (CAP) is an initiative by the Singapore Exhibition and Convention Bureau™ (SECB). The main objective of CAP is to identify and recognise the contributions and efforts of notable professionals and key opinion leaders that champion Singapore’s Meetings, Incentives, Conventions and Exhibitions (MICE) industry.
We invite CAP members to partner us to bid for events. For assistance or information, please call us at +65 6736 6622.

	    CAP Success Stories
	




	
	World Congress of the International Union of Microbiological Societies 2017
	
	A combination of three major co-located events, it is the largest gathering of top researchers and experts in microbiology. Top attendance numbers is 6,000 delegates. Singapore will host the event in 2017.

	
	More details
	

	
	Conference on Lasers and Electro-Optics Pacific Rim 2017
	
	The leading international forum for scientific and technical optics, Singapore will host the event in 2017. Estimated attendance by 1,000 delegates, among them scientists and experts in laser technology.
