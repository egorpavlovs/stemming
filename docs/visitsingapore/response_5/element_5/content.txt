	    We are Here to Help
	




	
	
	
	SECB is a full service bureau with a team of dedicated staff working with you to facilitate the delivery of high quality events. There are many ways in which SECB can assist you:

	
	
	

	
	Bidding and hosting international association conferences
	
	SECB will partner relevant industry stakeholders to bid for international renowned events, collating information on bid requirements and in compiling bid materials. When Singapore is confirmed as the event destination, SECB will work hand in hand with organisers in boosting delegate numbers at related platforms, such as other relevant events, as well as enhancing the delegate experience during the trip.

	
	
	

	
	Providing government support
	
	SECB can facilitate link-ups with other government agencies and regulatory bodies for inquiries and applications of permits and licenses. Check on licensing requirements here.

	
	
	

	
	Grants and incentives
	
	Find out how SECB can help with financial grants and tax savings. As a key driver of the development and growth of new business events in Singapore, SECB provides grants and incentives for event proposals that meet its criteria for fresh and high caliber events with innovative content.

Business Events in Singapore (BEiS)
Double Tax Deduction for Approved International Fair (AIF)


	
	
	

	
	Familiarisation trips
	
	Find out firsthand why Singapore is the ideal conference and event destination. Together with its industry partners, SECB can organise familiarisation trips to give you an insider's guide to the very best of Singapore's venues, attractions, accommodation, dining and entertainment options.

	
	
	

	
	Local business events suppliers
	
	No one knows Singapore like the SECB team, and they can help you access the best venues, accommodation, business events products and services in Singapore to meet your needs. 
 

	
	
	

	
	Event promotion and marketing
	
	SECB assists your event's promotion by having your event listed on its website's events calendar or promoted through street banner advertising in high traffic areas such as the Central Business District, Arts & Heritage District or Singapore's shopping mecca, Orchard Road.

Events calendar 
Street banner advertising 


	
	
	

	
	Strategic partnerships
	
	SECB connects you with experts and leaders in Singapore from across multiple key industries such as business, manufacturing, health, trade, IT and knowledge, to help you create an even more enriching experience for your delegates.
