	    Overview
	




	
	    
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
    








	
	
	
	Strategically located on the world renowned Orchard Road and within the commercial and shopping district of Singapore, Concorde Hotel Singapore is just minutes away from Suntec City, Marina South, The Esplanade, Boat Quay, Clarke Quay, Chinatown and other major tourist attractions. Offering business and family travellers to Singapore the finest with unmatched hospitality and service in a modern and relaxed atmosphere, this 4 star hotel has 407 rooms and suites designed to meet their needs.
After a recent nine-month long refurbishment, modern changes include completely renovated bathrooms, improved in-room lightings, bedside USB charging points, brand new carpets and a range of other upgraded amenities. Complimentary Wi-Fi connectivity is also available to Hotel Guests in their rooms, the lobby, by the pool, and other selected public areas. Whatever the occasion, Concorde Hotel Singapore ensures a unique location for your event. With 11 function spaces such as a pillarless Concorde Ballroom that can accommodate up to 450 guests, any sized event is in the best of hands, fully supported with equipment rental including wired internet access, LCD projector, Laptop, Cordless and Clip-On Microphones, TV and DVD, CD Player and Wi-Fi internet access for multiple devices.
As part of its efforts in staying committed to a sustainable future by implementing good environmental practices that focuses on the 3Rs (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle), energy conservation and water efficiency, Concorde Hotel Singapore provides guests with value-added services in the easiest and most convenient way possible, hence offering PressReader that allows them to have unlimited access to worldwide publications in an environmentally friendly and sustainable way. Other green measures include installation of energy saving light bulbs in all guest rooms and approximately 60% throughout the hotel. As of 2017, Concorde Hotel Singapore had also saved 5% of water usage throughout its premises and will continue its conservation and recycling efforts.
