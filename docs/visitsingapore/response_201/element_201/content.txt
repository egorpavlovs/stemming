	    INSPIRE Europe & North America
	




	
	
	
	The Singapore Exhibition and Convention Bureau™ (SECB) launched the In Singapore Incentives & Rewards (INSPIRE) programme for the Europe and North America markets in April 2016. Conceptualised in partnership with Singapore Airlines and Changi Airport Group, the pilot Western edition of the rewards programme will feature cocktail experiences and thematic business tours alongside attractive group airfares to complement business trips.

Criteria:
• Groups of 20 or more
• Originating from Europe or North America
• Minimum stay of 3 days in Singapore
Eligible corporate meetings and incentive travel groups will enjoy attractive offers from Singapore Airlines and be able to choose 1 of 14 complimentary experiences (from a or b) during their meeting or incentive travel to Singapore.
 

	
	
	

	
	Attractive offers from Singapore Airlines
	
	Eligible corporate meetings and incentive travel groups can enjoy attractive group airfares and other in-flight benefits on Singapore Airlines when travelling to Singapore and beyond, from points in Europe and North America served by the Singapore Airlines network.
• Attractive group rates
• Complimentary air tickets for INSPIRE groups:
 - 1 x economy ticket for every S$25,000 spend
 - 1 x premium economy ticket (where available) for every S$40,000 spend
 - 1 x business class ticket for every S$90,000 spend
• Attractive airfares from Singapore to Southeast Asia
• Excess baggage allowance of 10kg per person (for itineraries originating from Europe)
• Facilitation of on-board seating arrangements - on a best-effort basis and on SIA flights only 

	
	
	

	
	Complimentary activities to further enrich travel itineraries
	
	Eligible Meetings and Incentive Travel groups can choose one out of the following 14 experiences (from a or b) to complement their itineraries.

	
	
	






	    (a) Cocktail experiences hosted in historic venues or rooftop bars helmed by rising Singapore mixologists
	






	    For qualified groups of 20 pax or more staying in Singapore for 4 days or more:
	




	
	
	Supertree by Indochine, Gardens by the Bay
	Set atop a “Supertree” at Gardens by the Bay, guests would be able to enjoy drinks under the starlit skies and enjoy unobstructed views of the Singapore skyline. 

	
	
	

	
	
	1-Altitude, Raffles City
	Elevate yourself up and away from the bustle of the Central Business District at the world’s talletst alfresco bar. Standing at 282 metres above sea level, guests can enjoy spectacular 360-degree views of Singapore.

	
	
	

	
	
	Lantern, Fullerton Bay Hotel
	A laidback rooftop bar and pool with enthralling panoramic views of the Marina Bay waterfront and the Singapore skyline, this urban oasis framed by luxuriant backdrop of greenery captivates with its sensual vibe.

	
	
	

	
	
	Botanico, Singapore Botanic Gardens
	Situated in the UNESCO World Heritage listed Singapore Botanic Gardens, the bar offers a sanctuary in a heritage building surrounded by lush greenery.

	
	
	






	    For qualified groups of 20 pax or more staying in Singapore for 3 days or more:
	




	
	
	1919, Waterboat House
	Housed in a colonial heritage building and perched over the quay canals, guests can enjoy signature cocktails overlooking the iconic Fullerton Hotel Singapore.

	
	
	

	
	
	Loof, Odeon Towers
	Loof serves up quality whimsy, fresh nostalgia and unbridled playfulness in an urban rooftop garden in downtown Singapore. Enjoy carefully crafted Southeast Asian inspired cocktails with bar snacks that have a local twist.

	
	
	

	
	
	Potato Head Folk, Chinatown
	A rooftop bar situated in an iconic pre-war shophouse in Chinatown, Potato Head Folk offers a trendy and whimsical vibe. A retreat for the Singapore creative crowd in Singapore, the bar showcases the contrast between the old and new Singapore.

	
	
	

	
	
	Smoke & Mirrors, National Gallery Singapore
	Located on the rooftop of an iconic building that seamlessly combines the former Supreme Court and City Hall, this stylish bar offers unobstructed views of the Marina Bay.

	
	
	






	    (b) Thematic business tours on the Singapore story
	




	
	
	Pulse of a Nation
	Witness Singapore’s physical transformation to one of the most liveable cities in the world today. Understand how forward-looking, long term and integrated land use planning is done in Singapore. 

	
	
	

	
	
	“Makan Makan” – Let’s Eat
	Go on this food tour to sample a wide variety of cuisines and visit small food factories in Singapore. Understand the role these Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) play in the global food processing and supply chain. 

	
	
	

	
	
	Unity in Diversity
	Find four of the world’s major religions along one short stretch of road. See the differences and the commonalities that make up Singapore’s approach towards peace, progress and prosperity as a multi-racial and multi-religious society.

	
	
	

	
	
	The Peranakans
	Unveil the wonders of a unique ethnic group in Singapore – the Peranakans, which means “local-born”. Immerse in the culture, traditions and rituals of the Peranakans and visit the Joo Chiat & Katong precincts where many of the shophouses still stand.

	
	
	

	
	
	Of Feng Shui and Architecture
	Embark on this tour with a trained Feng Shui master and guide to learn how the elements and thinking of this ancient art are incorporated in the designs of some of Singapore’s prominent buildings and modern architecture. 

	
	
	

	
	
	The Way We Live
	Take a journey to a public housing estate to see the way most Singaporeans live. Visit a typical local neighbourhood and a Singaporean home, and understand how public housing and infrastructure are planned and integrated in Singapore.
 

	
	
	

	
	
	
	Register your interest and sign up by 30 June 2019 to qualify for this offer.
Groups must travel between 15 May 2017 and 31 December 2019. Other terms & conditions apply.
