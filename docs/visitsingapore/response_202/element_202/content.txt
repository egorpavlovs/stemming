	    INSPIRE SAMEA
	




	
	
	
	The INSPIRE SAMEA programme has specially curated itineraries that cater specifically for the Indian and Middle Eastern market. SECB partnered with Focus Adventure, Gardens by the Bay, Singapore Turf Club, Sentosa Development Corporation, Wildlife Reserves Singapore and Singapore Flyer to put together packages that will appeal to the SAMEA market incentive travellers.
Criteria
To be eligible for the INSPIRE SAMEA programme, groups must have a minimum of 50 pax and a maximum of 200 pax. Groups must also stay for a minimum of 2 nights at a premium hotel. Eligible incentive groups will enjoy a range of benefits and value-added experiences from each of the programme's seven major tourism partners.

The INSPIRE SAMEA programme is valid from 1 March 2017 to 31 December 2018.

	
	
	

	
	Focus Adventure
	1. The Dragon Boat Challenge
	Criteria

25 to 150 Visitor Arrivals
 
Description of Experience


Half-day team building event
Inclusive of design and delivery of customised programme, provision of facilitator(s) and instructor(s)
Complimentary use of programme equipment and set up
Inclusive of use of Dragon Boats
Provision of floating device (life vests) and paddles
Complimentary two-way coach transfer to and from Raffles Marina before 1900hrs


	
	
	

	
	
	2. The Incredible City Race (Clarke Quay / Chinatown) OR The Heritage Challenge at Pulau Ubin
	Criteria

50 to 200 pax
 
Description of Experience

Half-day team building event
Inclusive of design and delivery of customised programme, provision of facilitator(s) and instructors(s)
Complimentary use of programme equipment and set up 
Complimentary race pack (race booklet, writing materials and disposable ponchos)
Inclusive of budget for MRT transport to & from Chinatown and Clarke Quay or bumboat rides to and from Pulau Ubin


	
	
	

	
	Gardens By the Bay
	1. Evening Drink at OCBC Skyway
	Criteria

25 to 50 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Complimentary access to OCBC Skyway from 9pm to 11.30pm
Complimentary 2-way Garden Cruiser services
Exclusive Garden Rhapsody light and sound show after 9pm


	
	
	

	
	
	2. Standing Cocktail Reception at the Secret Life of Trees
	Criteria

100 to 200 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

3-hour free flow light snacks and cordial drinks
Existing Garden Rhapsody light and sound show at 7.45pm & 8.45pm
Option of S$10 Satay by the Bay food voucher or an upgraded cocktail menu


	
	
	

	
	Singapore Turf Club
	1. “Live Action” @ Singapore Racecourse
	Criteria

25 to 200 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Free Admission to Singapore Turf Club
Buffet Dinner (exclude alcoholic drinks and fruit juices)
Guided Tour to Parade Ring
Special Talk on “How to Pick Winners”
Exclusive Club’s Memento
A Race Guide


	
	
	

	
	Sentosa Development Corporation
	1. Dinner at Private Dining Room, Faber Peak Singapore
	Criteria

25 to 50 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Happy Drill
Cable Car Experience with a complimentary welcome drink 
Wings of Time Show with complimentary customised water screen welcome message


	
	
	

	
	
	2. Dinner at The Ballroom, Faber Peak Singapore
	Criteria

50 to 200 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Happy Drill
Cable Car Experience with a complimentary welcome drink 
Wings of Time Show with complimentary customised water screen welcome message


	
	
	

	
	
	3. Dinner at Emerald / Sapphire Pavilion or Palawan Green
	Criteria

100 to 200 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Happy Drill
Cable Car Experience with a complimentary welcome drink 
Wings of Time Show with complimentary customised water screen welcome message


	
	
	

	
	Wildlife Reserves Singapore
	1. Enchanted Evening at Night Safari
	Criteria

25 to 50 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Night Safari tour with Indian buffet dinner at Tipi Tent, Night Safari
Animal appearance at the Tipi Tent
Thumbuakar performance
Creatures of the Night Show


	
	
	

	
	
	2. Mystical Evening at Night Safari
	Criteria

50 to 200 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Night Safari tour with Indian buffet dinner at Forest Lodge, Singapore Zoo
Pre-dinner cocktail and animal appearance
Thumbuakar performance
Creatures of the Night Show


	
	
	

	
	
	3. Colours of Paradise at Jurong Bird Park
	Criteria

25 to 200 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Jurong Bird Park tour with Indian buffet lunch at The Lodge or Lakeview Room
Flamingo appearance for group photography
High Flyer Show with special welcome greetings
Complimentary Tram ride
Lory Loft Feeding


	
	
	

	
	Singapore Flyer
	1. Cocktail Reception at Singapore Flyer
	Criteria

25 to 50 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Priority boarding on a private capsule
30-min flight experience with a dedicated in-flight host
Choice of a glass of whiskey on the rocks or mocktail and an assortment of canapés during the flight
Merchandise discount offer


	
	
	

	
	
	2. Cocktail Reception at Singapore Flyer
	Criteria

51 to 125 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Welcome drink served with dried fruit and assorted nuts at Flyer Lounge prior to boarding
Priority boarding on a private capsule
30-min flight experience with a dedicated in-flight host
Choice of a glass of whiskey on the rocks or mocktail and an assortment of canapés during the flight
Merchandise discount offer


	
	
	

	
	
	3. Cocktail Reception at Singapore Flyer
	Criteria

126 to 200 Visitor Arrivals


Description of Experience

Welcome drink served with dried fruit and assorted nuts at Flyer Hall prior to boarding
Singapore Flyer souvenir
Priority boarding on a private capsule
30-min flight experience with a dedicated in-flight host
Choice of a glass of whiskey on the rocks or mocktail and an assortment of canapés during the flight
Merchandise discount offer
