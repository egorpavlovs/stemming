	    Holiday Calendars
	




	
	
	
	Singapore's rich and colourful multicultural society means that there are numerous important cultural and religious events and festivals throughout the year. There are 11 public holidays per annum, 6 of which have changeable dates. 

	
	
	







	
	    
		2018
			
				Mon, 1 Jan New Year’s DayFri, 16 Feb – Sat, 17 FebLunar New YearFri, 30 MarGood FridayTue, 1 MayLabour DayTue, 29 MayVesak DayFri, 15 JunHari Raya PuasaThu, 9 AugNational DayWed, 22 AugHari Raya HajiWed, 7 NovDeepavaliTue, 25 DecChristmas Day
			
		
    
            If a public holiday falls on a Sunday, the next working day will be the replacement holiday. Please click here for the official public holiday dates.

				Tue, 1 JanNew Year’s DayTue, 5 Feb – Wed, 6 FebLunar New YearFri, 19 AprGood FridayWed, 1 MayLabour DaySun, 12 MayVesak Day*Wed, Jun 5Hari Raya PuasaFri, 9 AugNational DayMon, 12 AugHari Raya HajiSun, 27 OctDeepavali*Wed, 25 DecChristmas Day
			
		
    
            If a public holiday falls on a Sunday, the next working day will be the replacement holiday. Please click here for the official public holiday dates.
        
	










	
	    
		2020
			
				Wed, 1 Jan New Year’s DaySat, 25 Jan - Sun, 26 JanLunar New Year*Fri, 10 AprGood FridayThu, 30 AprVesak DayFri, 1 MayLabour DaySun, 10 MayHari Raya Puasa*Fri, 31 JulHari Raya HajiSun, 9 AugNational Day*Sat, 14 NovDeepavali*Fri, 25 DecChristmas Day
