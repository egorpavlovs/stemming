	    Overview
	




	
	    
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
	        
                
                
	            
	            
	        
    
            
		
		
            
		








	
	
	
	Define your next event with unique and inspiring spaces in downtown Singapore. Our standalone heritage buildings offer 15 modern function rooms and an elegant Grand Ballroom.
Unveil our Forest of Lights or set your event outdoors to create an exciting and memorable occasion. Perfect for corporate events, weddings and social gatherings, our iconic 1930s heritage Grand Ballroom has over 8,000 square feet of space, with a pre-function foyer on the first level and the main ballroom and mezzanine on the second level. Featuring the largest LED Wall in a hotel ballroom in Singapore, the venue is ideal for receptions, banquets, conferences, product launches and more.
Our 15 function rooms are flexible and ideal for small and medium events, offering modern meeting and conference space to meet those needs. Located in a standalone two-level heritage building with direct driveway access, these venues are neatly situated and fully equipped for your events. Our hotel also provides convenient direct access to the Suntec Singapore Convention and Exhibition Centre.
Plan a reception unlike any other in the stylish outdoor rooftop space of our East Tower. With panoramic views of the Singapore city skyline, our sky garden and infinity pool set the stage for an inspired occasion. Measuring over 2,000 square feet, this space has the capacity for hosting a reception for up to 100 guests.
