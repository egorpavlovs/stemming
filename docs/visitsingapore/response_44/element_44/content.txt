	5 things to know about planning a sustainable event
	
	
Implementing smart, sustainable practices can help to minimise cost, conserve energy and reduce waste 
A green event enhances your brand's reputation and commitment to sustainability 
Sustainability efforts drive innovation in resource and event management 
An effective green event can lead to better communities in the host city 
Going green is a chance to give back to Mother Earth

If you're looking to go green while planning an event in Singapore, check out our green venues and services.

	
	
	



			
	    
	







	    
	        
				






	    
			    Going Green in Singapore
			    As Asia's greenest city, Singapore sets the standard amongst other cities with the Sustainable Singapore Blueprint 2015. It outlines the country’s national vision and plans for a more liveable and sustainable Singapore.

	Public transport in Singapore is clean, efficient, safe and sustainably developed. With extensive and comprehensive networks, it is quick and easy to get around by bus or train while reducing your carbon footprint.
	
	
	
	

	
	By train
	
	Singapore has one of the cleanest transport systems in the world, with an extremely reliable and energy-efficient train system.

	
	
	

	
	By buses
	
	SMRT runs a fleet of fuel-efficient and eco-friendly buses on a comprehensive island-wide network.

	
	
	

	
	By taxi
	
	A third of SMRT's taxis are eco-friendly hybrid cars that provide cleaner and greener rides.
