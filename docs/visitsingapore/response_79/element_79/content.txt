	Why use street banners? 
	
	Street banners are strong visual advertising tools for your event and activities. When located in prominent locations such as the Central Business District or Arts & Heritage District, they are cost-effective promotions with high-impact visibility. In the Orchard area, the city's most popular shopping strip, street banners attract an estimated 1 million views per month.
What we offer: 

43 prime locations for banner placement 
More than 1,300 banner spaces throughout the island

        Types of lamp posts
        There are 2 types of lamp posts; decorative and non-decorative with different requirements for banner dimensions. To see what type of lamp posts are in the area, please refer to the ‘Banner location map’. 
        
        
			
					
				Non-decorative lamp posts are lamp posts with space for only one banner. Banners should be 3m x 1m. 
				
			
	                
	            Decorative lamp posts in selected areas can consist of 1 or 2 banner spaces. Banners should be 3.5m x 1.2m.

	    Arts & Heritage District
	






    
        
		
				
					
					
					
				
				
					
					
					
				
	        
        
        
        
        
        
			
					
				
				
			
	            Banner spaces for advertisement
	            The lively Arts & Heritage District in the heart of the city is a must-visit for any arts and culture lover in Singapore. It holds a vibrant collection of national institutions, private galleries and art spaces, including the Singapore Art Museum, Lasalle College of the Arts and Art Plural Gallery. Take advantage of its impeccable position with the National Publicity Platform, which gives free outdoor banner space for the promotion of arts and arts-related events. 
	            
			
	



        
            
            When to apply 
            Banner spaces are open for booking on a half yearly basis.
            
            
        
        
            
            How to apply 
            Email here


Applications must be submitted at least 3 months before the commencement of the banner display.

	            Banner spaces for advertisement

A hub for business, entertainment and shopping, the stunning area with its myriad of attractions is top draw for Singaporeans and visitors alike at all hours of the day, giving your street banners maximum visual impact.
