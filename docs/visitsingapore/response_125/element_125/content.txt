	Like event websites, mobile apps can be used as the springboard for all your event content – from the agenda, speakers’ bios and programme information to exhibitor listing, floor plans, social events and more. Mr Lee Yoong Foo, MICEapps Strategist at Ace:Daytons Direct, shares his insights on using this technology at your next event.

	Taking AIME to win the best innovative stand
	
	Held from 23 to 24 February 2016 at the Melbourne Convention and Exhibitions Centre, the Asia Pacific Incentives and Meetings Expo (AIME) is the largest international event of its kind in Australia. This annual trade show brings together a community of corporates, meetings, events and incentives industry decision makers to network and meet new business leads.

	Facilitating conversations from the ground up: Science Café SG
	
	With Singapore’s growing number of professionals and well-educated individuals, grassroots organisations are flourishing across the country and gathering like-minded individuals for discussion and learning. Find out about one such example – Science Café SG, a forum that seeks to foster scientific dialogue amongst enthusiasts.

	Turning Passion Into Progress
	
	Singapore is renowned among international business leaders and decision makers for its model economy built on sound laws and business-friendly policies. Multinational companies have for decades chosen Singapore as their regional base, investing heavily in manufacturing facilities, R&D labs, innovation hubs and talent centers.
