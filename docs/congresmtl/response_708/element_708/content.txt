	Espace 1001 presents : Michelangelo’s Sistine Chapel

	 
		communications
		Destination Montréal, Expositions publiques et commerciales, Tourisme
					
    
    Michelangelo’s Sistine Chapel: The Exhibition is in full swing until October 12, 2015 at Espace 1001 in the Palais des congrès de Montréal. In its world premiere, this event features life-size photographic reproductions of the ceiling paintings from the famous Vatican chapel.
This exhibition allows visitors to appreciate and admire these Italian Renaissance masterpieces up close and at their own pace. Thanks to an audio-guide, visitors can immerse themselves in the frescoes and cultivate a deeper understanding of the technical and aesthetic details therein. A must-see as much for those who have already visited the Sistine Chapel as for those who wish to discover its beauty!
Michelangelo’s Sistine Chapel: The Exhibition is being held at Espace 1001, a downtown venue ideally designed for public events and dedicated to extended-run thematic exhibitions.
