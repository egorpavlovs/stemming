                            OUR PROMOTION IS BACK!

                        

                        
Experience Montréal shopping at Les Galeries du Palais


                        


Until March 31, 2019, earn a reward worth up to $10 when you spend $5 or more (before taxes) in two separate Galeries du Palais stores. Once your loyalty card is stamped and filled out, simply drop it off in the appropriate box at a participating store. You will receive your reward in the mail.

Contest Rules (in french only)

A mall in the heart of the Palais
Visitors can find everything they need right in the heart of the Palais des congrès! Les Galeries du Palais is a commercial mall home to approximately 20 service locations and stores, including restaurants and boutiques, which is why locals and tourists alike converge at Les Galeries du Palais for a bit of shopping.
Les Galeries du Palais is a commercial mall home to approximately 20 stores including restaurants, boutiques, and service locations
Some retailers can also be accessed from outside:

Presse Café at 904, Saint-Urbain
Restaurant La Popessa at 115, Saint-Antoine Ouest
Restaurant MELK, 908 Saint-Urbain

Map of Les Galeries du Palais

Free Wi-Fi
Event visitors and participants at the Palais can enjoy free wi-fi along the Galeries du Palais shopping concourse, on Level 1. Simply join “Free zone gratuite Palais” from your list of available networks on your mobile device.
Comprehensive Wi-Fi
Need wi-fi everywhere in the Palais? Select the comprehensive wi-fi package and enjoy wi-fi designed for professionals like you! Simply join “Palais des congrès” from your list of available networks on your mobile device.
