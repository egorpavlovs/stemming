Frequently Asked Questions


Where are the events listed?
The Events Calendar contains the dates and a link to the organizer‘s website for complete details.



Which hotels are nearest the Palais des congrès de Montréal?
See the Accommodations page to learn about the hotels located near the Palais.



How do you get to the Palais?
The Access page lists every possible commuting option for getting to the Palais, regardless of the means of transportation you use.



What are the parking fees?
The Access page lists every possible commuting option for getting to the Palais, including information on parking rates.



Where are the designated disabled parking spaces?
The Access page lists every possible commuting option for getting to the Palais, including information on adapted services.



Can we take pictures of the Palais des congrès?
We require photo and film shoots at the Palais des congrès de Montréal to be scheduled in advance, to keep events in progress from being disrupted. To learn more, please see the Film and TV Production page.



I will be attending an event at the Palais des congrès. Where can I find the information I need to plan my stay?

Palais area: The Near the Palais page is full of information on the city’s nearby popular districts and attractions.
Getting to the Palais: The Access page informs you on the best way to get to the Palais based on the mode of transportation you choose. Includes maps (Montréal’s underground city, metro, parking, self-serve bike stations, directions from the bus terminal).
Lodging: 4,000 hotels rooms linked to the Palais, and over 15,300 more within a 10-minute walk. Go to the Accommodations page for complete details.
Tourism: You’ll find a host of useful links to help you plan everything from sightseeing and shopping to dining out in the Destination Montréal section and on the Useful Links page.
Onsite services: The Palais houses a shopping promenade lined with numerous restaurants and shops. Discover them on our Commercial Mall page.
Wi-Fi: For your convenience, the Palais is pleased offer free wi-fi in public areas (shopping promenade). For wireless internet Palais-wide (including the meeting rooms and exhibition hall), simply go to our Internet login page and purchase a 24h individual package appropriately designed for personal use (credit card required). Exhibitors can go to the For Exhibitors page or contact their event promoter to learn about the range of packages available for professional use.



I am doing a school project on tourism management/event planning. Where can I find information on the subject matter?
We invite you to download this document, which helps evaluate the cost of hosting a typical event, including an exhibition, a plenary session and a gala, and also provides a detailed list of what you need to plan for.



What restaurants can be found in and around the Palais des congrès?
The Commercial Mall page lists the restaurants located inside the building and nearby.



What address do we use for shipments and deliveries?
The Access page specifies the delivery address and provides information on how to get to the Palais des congrès de Montréal.



Do you provide wheelchairs during events?
It is possible to borrow a wheelchair when attending consumer shows. This service is available free of charge but must be booked in advance. Fill the application form for Complimentary Wheelchairs.



What booth services are available for exhibitors?
Visit the For Exhibitors page for an overview of the services the Palais des congrès offers.
