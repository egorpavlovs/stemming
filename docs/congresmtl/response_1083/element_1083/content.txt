	20 décembre 2017
	
	Your hosts for 2017

	 
		communications
		Non classé
					
    
    This year, 10 Appointed Ambassadors welcomed the world by hosting their major event at the Palais des congrès de Montréal. Their involvement made it possible for over 15,000 delegates to visit Montréal and book 42,300 room nights in our city’s hotels, generating in the process more than $41 M in tourism revenues for Montréal and Québec.



WorldMUN 2017 – Harvard World Model United Nations
Jonathan Sasson


Global Public Transport Summit – UITP Montréal 2017
Carl Desrosiers
Nicolas Girard
Michel Labrecque


12th Metropolis World Congress
Denis Coderre


URSI XXXII General Assembly and Scientific Symposium
Fabrice Labeau


Conference of the Americas on International Education
Francis Brown


World Design Summit
Sheila Copps
Alain Dufour


24th Intelligent Transportation Systems World Congress – ITS 2017
Claude Carette
Michael De Santis



Thanks to their drive and determination, these hosts actively contributed to generating significant intellectual wealth for Montréal and their respective fields.
