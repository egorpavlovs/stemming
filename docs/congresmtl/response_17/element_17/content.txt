                        The right space for any event
The Palais des congrès has the right type of space for all events! Incredibly versatile, our different rooms cater to any activity. Eight levels can accommodate special events,
congresses, conferences, exhibitions, and the list goes on. Whatever you’re looking for, you’ll find it here.
Infinite options
The possibilities are limitless, thanks to 551,520 sq. ft. (51,280 m²) of rental space including 113 rooms and venues, that can accommodate up to 1,950 booths. This includes one of Canada’s largest columnless halls and exhibition venues. The Palais also rents meeting rooms, reception rooms, VIP and Board rooms, ballrooms, terraces, and offices.
Designed to meet your needs
Modern, sophisticated, and flexible, our installations are set up in a contemporary architecture featuring abundant natural light. All venues are fully accessible and equipped with the latest state-of-the-art technology. A staff of creative and passionate experts is also on hand to make sure any event is a memorable success. Since 1983 more than 7,700 events have taken place at the Palais des congrès and we’ve earned a near 93% level of customer satisfaction.
