                                            A Word From the President
                                        
                                        
                                            
                                              
                                              Dear Ambassadors,
                                              It is the first of issue of The Ambassador of 2018, so please allow me to wish you health, wonderful times with your families, and success throughout the year.
                                              Key influential events abounded in 2017. During the course of the Exchange Forum we jointly organized last November with the Fonds de recherche du Québec, and also the Forum on the Socially Responsible Development of Artificial Intelligence, I saw the role strategic alliances play in attracting and developing events able to generate significant intellectual wealth. That synergy can be harnessed toward every activity sector, with Ambassadors at the centre of this collaborative process that benefits Montréal and the province.
                                              Let us continue to host our peers through major events in our respective fields and showcase our city at every opportunity!
                                              Happy New Year!
