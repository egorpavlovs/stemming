                            Accommodations
                        

                        
Thousands of hotel rooms conveniently located near the Palais des congrès



                        Staying overnight
Linked to Montréal’s indoor pedestrian network and strategically nestled at the crossroads of the city’s business centre, international district, Chinatown and Old Montréal, the Palais des congrès is surrounded by numerous and easily accessible hotels.
Plenty of options
There are 4,000 rooms linked to the Palais and about 16,000 within a 10-minute walk. Accommodations are easy to find, and easy to get to!
