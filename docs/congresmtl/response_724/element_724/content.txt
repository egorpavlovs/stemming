	Testimonial : The Motorcycle Show – 2016

	 
		communications
		Témoignages
					
    
    
Ms. Bianca Kennedy shares her experience at the Palais des congrès de Montreal just after the 2016 Motorcycle Show welcomed 34,000 visitors.
This public show, which attracted  34,000 visitors in 2016, takes place in Febuary at the Palais des congrès every year since 2008. Over 150 exhibitors participate in this must-see event for motorcycle enthusiasts in Québec.
