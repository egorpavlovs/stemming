																																																			The Palais des 
congrès de Montréal and its exclusive caterer, Capital Catering, were 
very pleased to introduce recently their new executive chef: Simon 
Devost-Dulude.
																																																		
																																																			A Montréal native and
 a graduate of the ITHQ hotel management school, Chef Devost-Dulude has 
taken over as head of the seasoned staff at Montréal’s largest 
institutional kitchen. The creative and passionate young chef plans to 
seize this extraordinary opportunity to add his own unique flavour to 
renewing the Palais culinary experience, a process that began in 2010.
																																																		
																																																			“Selecting a local 
chef demonstrates how much we appreciate Québec cuisine and our 
eagerness to widely promote it among our regional and international 
customers,” says Marc Tremblay, President and CEO of the Palais des 
congrès de Montréal.
																																																		
																																																			“With the support of 
his experienced kitchen staff, Simon Devost-Dulude is determined to 
showcase fresh-picked Québec products, such as the vegetables and fine 
herbs on the Palais rooftop garden, grown literally just steps away from
 the guests,” mentions Sébastien Hébert, Capital Catering’s Director of 
Sales and Development.
