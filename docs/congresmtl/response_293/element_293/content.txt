                            For your 2019 Holiday reception…
                        

                        



                        Come celebrate in venues with limitless possibilities Located in the heart of the city, easy to access by car or public transit, the Palais offers an unbeatable selection of versatile and modern venues that adapt to all types of events. Whether you have a glamourous “red carpet” theme in mind, or a “high-voltage” evening with acrobats perched 10 metres above you and your guests, our team will help you find the perfect solution to make your Holiday reception an outstanding success.

113 rooms and spaces
543,954 sq. ft. (50,535 m2) in reception hall venues
115,040 sq. ft. (10,688 m2) in ballroom venues, including one of the largest column-less ballrooms in Canada, capable of accommodating 3,240 guests banquet-style
5,705 sq. ft. (530 m2) in VIP rooms




















Offer your guests superior qualityThanks to the creativity and know-how of our qualified personnel, nothing is left to chance with a view toward exceeding your expectations and ensuring the success of your big gala or event.
Treat your guests to an exclusive culinary experienceThe food plays a key role in the success of a Holiday reception. Which is why you can count on our chef Simon Devost-Dulude and his Capital Catering kitchen staff to turn every dish into an event.
NEW!
The Palais and Capital Traiteur have teamed up with Québec-based Made With Love to provide an amazing mixology experience for your event. Learn more about the different Thematic Experiences available to you and give your guests an unforgettable cocktail experience!

Enjoy reliable technology and peace of mindBenefit from having the Canadian leader in high-tech presentation solutions on your team. Freeman Audio Visual are the Palais’ official supplier of audiovisual services, and as such serve as an extension of our own technological services. It’s a synergy you can bank on to deliver expert advice and dazzle your guests.
Let our team of experts advise you through every phase of your Holiday reception’s planning and staging process. In 2019, go for a touch of prestige and pick the Palais for your end-of-year celebrations, pure and simple!
