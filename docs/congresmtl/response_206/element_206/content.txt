                            Destination Montréal
                        

                        
Ranked top convention city in the Americas



                        Worth visiting
Montréal is a beautiful city renowned for its international flavour and inspiring diversity. Located on an island in the St. Lawrence River, it is home to approximately 1.6 million residents and the largest bilingual workforce in Canada. It is the largest French-speaking city outside France and a perfect blend of European energy and North American efficiency. One of the world’s top convention cities and a certified destination for sustainable events, there’s something for everyone here. You’ll find a friendly population, world-famous gastronomy, and an atmosphere of celebration!
