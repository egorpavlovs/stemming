                                          The Palais des congrès de Montréal presents its exclusive Casino Package, a new feature in 2011 for planning unforgettable Christmas parties. Picture yourself in Las Vegas and celebrate with your guests in a sumptuously plush casino-style décor! Take advantage of this turnkey themed night complete with live performances, created in collaboration with Les productions Éric Quentin. 
                                        Also, our newly designed 50,000 sq. ft. ballroom will be ready in November in time for your banquets and receptions. The experience will be uniquely magical.
