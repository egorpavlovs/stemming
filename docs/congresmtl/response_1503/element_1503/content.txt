	A Palais des congrès first: Destination Health – Canadian Medical Tourism Trade Show

	 
		communications
		Expositions publiques et commerciales, Créneaux d’excellence, Tourisme
					
    
    The Palais des congrès de Montréal will host the first trade show devoted to medical tourism for Canadians: Destination Health, to be held August 14 and 15, 2015. This major event will bring together hundreds of participants from the international medical tourism and travel insurance industries who will unite their efforts to develop medical tourism jointly with Latin America, Europe, Asia, the U.S. and the Caribbean. According to the Fraser Institute, last year over 52,000 Canadians travelled abroad for medical care. For the first time in Canada, Destination Health will draw health, dental and wellness providers from countries such as the United States, Mexico, Dominican Republic, Turkey, Poland, Germany, Panama, Guatemala, Turks & Caicos, Argentina and Costa Rica to showcase their services to Canadians. Montrealers and other visitors from the canadian public will enjoy free entrance to the exhibition and will find information and services to plan their upcoming medical trips to other countries, or simply to ensure their access to health care during their next stay abroad. For more information, please visit: www.destinationhealth.ca
Hosting the biggest life science events
In 2014 and 2015, the Palais des congrès will host over 55 events related to the life sciences, including the prestigious World Congress on Brain, Behavior and Emotions, and the 25th World Congress on Ultrasound in Obstetrics and Gynecology. These events will attract nearly 85,000 business tourists and visitors to the city, which will generate over $118M in economic benefits for the province, namely through the booking of more than 83,000 room nights in Montréal hotels. Overall, the healthcare sector accounts for over 37% of the events hosted by the province’s biggest and largest convention centre.
