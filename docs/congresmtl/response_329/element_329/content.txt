  Our online ordering service simplifies and speeds up the process of booking your services. Use it to place orders and pay online at your convenience, and to do so much more.
In this Portal, you will find all the services available to you for your exhibition space and booth based on the type of event, plus the freedom to book them online at any time.
Accessible 24-7, the Client Portal also delivers exclusive packages and preferred rates.
