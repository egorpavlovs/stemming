                                                                                    Installations                                        
                                                                            

                                                                            
                                            The Palais des congrès has the right type of space for all events! Incredibly versatile, our different rooms cater to all activities. Eight levels can accommodate special events, receptions, congresses, conventions, exhibitions, and the list goes on.

                                                                                    Initiatives                                        
                                                                            

                                                                            
                                            Over a decade ago the Palais des congrès pledged to do its part to become environmentally sustainable. It has since developed a plan that calls for environmental protection as well as social commitment. Moreover, to remain an industry leader when it comes to meetings and congresses, the Palais des congrès de Montréal is in the process of a major hi-tech modernization project.
