                        TOOLS AND RESOURCES AT YOUR FINGERTIPS
The Palais des congrès de Montréal attracts and hosts conventions and other events for the purpose of generating tourism revenues and intellectual wealth for Montréal and Québec, thus contributing to Montréal’s international reputation as a premier host city
The Palais des congrès de Montréal has a team of specialists to support you along the way as well as material and financial resources to help you put your project together.

Drafting and presenting the bid1
Producing the bid book, rated among the best worldwide
Determining lobbying strategies intended for decision-making and voting parties
Negotiating and building partnerships
Securing logistical support and funding
Providing promotional support
Support for event planning
Drafting a convention blueprint and budget

1 Upon reviewing the project, we may absorb the influencer’s travel expenses.
