Enjoy state-of-the-art technology
A canadian leader specialists and the Palais' official supplier, Freeman Audio Visual is home to hi-tech innovation experts who consistently strive to deliver a successful and out-of-the-ordinary audiovisual experience for your conventions, exhibitions, tradeshows and corporate events.
It has offices and inventory onsite and knows every square inch of the building. Freeman Audio Visual's team of technical experts are specialized in presentation technology messaging and providing quality-driven solutions.
