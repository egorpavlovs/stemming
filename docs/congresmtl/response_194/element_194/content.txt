                            The Palais
                        

                        
State-of-the-art venues and outstanding planning services 



                        
Reinvent Event from Palais des congrès de Montréal on Vimeo.
World-class venues
The Palais des congrès is one of North America’s most renowned convention and exhibition centres. Since 1983, the Palais in Montréal has welcomed more than 7,700 events in its accessible and innovative facilities. Seasoned staff and superior planning services have earned us an international reputation for excellence and sophistication, and a near 93% level of customer satisfaction.
The perfect site for any event
Located in the core of one of the world’s most vibrant cities, the Palais offers high-performance spaces for a wide variety of events, from corporate meetings to international conferences. No matter what you are planning, our professional team will work together with you every step of the way to meet your every need and ensure that your event is a memorable success. Our goal is to go above and beyond your expectations to make your activity an extraordinary one.
At the heart of Montréal
In the heart of downtown Montréal and near historic and cultural areas such as Old Montréal, Chinatown and the arts and entertainment district, the Palais is right next to the action! More than 16,000 hotel rooms are located a short ten-minute walk away, including approximately 4,000 accessible by the underground pedestrian network. We’re also only 20 minutes away from Montréal-Trudeau airport.
Everything for an outstanding event
Premium location, state-of-the-art technology, an elegant atmosphere, and qualified experts will make any event a truly unique experience!
