                            Level 7
                        

                        
Outdoors or inside, fully equipped and ready to host your next event



                        Outdoor facilities
A spectacular view awaits you on level 7. Divided into outdoor and indoor venues, the seventh floor features an atrium, VIP lounge along with outdoor belvedere, and terrace with marquee. Totalling 79,970 sq. ft. (7,455 m²), this floor can be used for a variety of purposes, including banquets and receptions.

Ambassadors Lounge
The seventh floor is also home to the Ambassadors Lounge, named in honor of the Ambassadors' Club members, a private lounge that connects to the terrace, ideal for VIP meetings and receptions.
Soak in the sights
The lovely Palais terrace of the seventh floor is strategically designed to give everyone a beautiful view of Montréal. Its surface is 19,575 sq. ft. in and can welcome more than 500 guests for an outdoor cocktail.
Level 8
The Mezzanine on the eighth floor includes two dressing rooms, close to the action on level 7.
