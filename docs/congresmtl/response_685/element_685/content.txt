	23 juin 2014
	
	2014 Real Estate Awards of Excellence

	 
		communications
		Développement durable
					
    
    The Palais des congrès de Montréal and the Centre de recherche du CHUM were both shortlisted for a Real Estate Award of Excellence, in two separate categories: Marketing Communication and Best Real Estate Project – Commercial, respectively.

The Palais ultimately finished second in the marcom category for its Child’s Play campaign, whose centrepiece was a playful yet poshly designed print piece that conveyed the uniqueness of the Palais experience simply and directly!

Dr. Jacques Turgeon, a Palais des congrès de Montréal partner, had the honour of attending the gala ceremony and accepting the award, which recognizes, among other things:

How harmoniously the building integrates the urban architecture;
The colossal effort required to render the building accessible by way of public transit, and including works of art and open spaces for the general public;
The teamwork behind integrating the building to its surroundings and enabling it to generate economic and social benefits for an area in need of both;
Imminent LEED Silver certification, and for completing the energy and environmental improvement work on schedule and within budget!

Since 2000, the Real Estate Awards of Excellence of the Urban Development Institute of Québec (UDI) have recognized the outstanding work of individuals responsible for the success of remarkable Québec construction projects, and have also helped promote leading Québec real estate industry accomplishments.
