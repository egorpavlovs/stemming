	A mexican restaurant at the Galeries du Palais

	 
		communications
		Destination Montréal, Galeries du Palais
					
    
    Today is opening day for the Galeries du Palais’ newest restaurant: Resto-Bar 3 Amigos. 3 Amigos is a must in Montréal! Try their famous burritos, tacos, enchiladas, fajitas or any other Mexican and Tex-Mex specialty available on the menu.

3 amigos is located on the west side of the shopping mall, near 301 Saint-Antoine West, offering a magnificent view of the Palais surround glass!
Perfect for drinks with friends or a bite between meetings!
