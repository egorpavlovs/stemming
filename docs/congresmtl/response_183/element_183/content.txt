                            Terraces
                        

                        
A beautiful outdoor setting will make your next special event unforgettable



                        Take your event outside
The perfect way to enhance business meetings, receptions, press conferences, or any other promotional activity, the four terraces of the Palais des congrès will definitely help set your next event apart.
Download the complete brochure (PDF, 1.4Mb)

Palais Terrace
The Palais Terrace totals 19,575 sq. ft. (1,818 m²) and can host 630 people. It includes a Marquee and Reception area and gives a view of the Jacques-Cartier bridge and the St. Lawrence River, perfect for firework nights!

See the Palais Terrace on our Flickr.

Belvedere
The Belvedere overlooks the city’s business district and can welcome 751 people on its 6,688 sq. ft. (621 m²).

Terrace Viger
The Terrace Viger is 834 sq. ft. (77 m²) and is perfect for smaller get-togethers. An ideal way to add a little flair to your next meeting!

Esplanade
The Esplanade is the largest of the four terraces, located in a gorgeous courtyard filled with crab apple trees. It is the ultimate blend of urban landscape and soothing nature. It has a capacity of 1,400 persons on a 21,440 sq. ft. (1,992 m²) surface. For those times when you want to add elegant natural beauty to your activities!
Top-notch services
Events will be outfitted with flexible and first-rate infrastructures while professional staff will be on hand to help you and your guests get the most out of your spectacular event.
