	More tech events than ever

	 
		communications
		Non classifié(e)
					
    
    In the wake of its sweeping IT modernization project, the Palais des congrès has been hosting more and more major events making the most of event technologies.
Events like the April 2014 Pycon convention, attended by 2,400 programming specialists, which took advantage of the wifi network’s coverage and stability to hold interactive workshops and conferences. The Cisco Partners Summit, which is welcoming 3,000 delegates from April 27 to 30, will be able to provide their ultra connected users looking to remain productive with a network capable of handling numerous simultaneous connections.
Also, events like those of the American College of Chest Physicians and the Journées dentaires internationales will be fully capitalizing on the possibilities made available by the award-winning signage network comprising 90 HD displays and a 90 sq. ft. video wall, to inform and advertise to their participants while generating revenues for their organizations.
Remember that the keys to using event technologies successfully are ascertaining the delegates’ and organizer’s needs, and incorporating the technologies in a sponsorship program.
