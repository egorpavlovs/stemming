	11 décembre 2013
	
	Inaugural edition of the Palais Luncheon « Enrich Québec province through research and innovation »

	 
		communications
		Conférences, réunions et galas, Créneaux d’excellence
					
    
    The Palais des congrès de Montréal developed once again another innovative way to promote the city! December 9, over 350 participants attended the very 1st edition of a luncheon organized in collaboration with the Board of Trade of Metropolitan Montréal.

This major event marked the important collaborative work the business and university communities undertook in 2013. It has enabled to further bolster ties between universities, researchers and corporations!
As you know, the Palais des congrès is actively involved in generating economic and intellectual spinoffs for Montréal. This new event will now be part of that process. Innovation, research and the economy will be front and centre!
The luncheon was highly informative, and the keynote speaker was Mr. Pierre Duchesne, Québec Minister of Higher Education, Research, Science and Technology, who will talk about the government’s new innovation policy.

December 9 was therefore another opportunity to participate, share and contribute to the creation of intellectual wealth for the city of Montreal! This is a rendez-vous for 2014!
	
		


		
				 
	« 1re édition du déjeuner-causerie du Palais  « Enrichir le Québec par la recherche et l’innovation »	» Raymond Larivée nommé à la tête de la Société du Palais des congrès de Montréal
