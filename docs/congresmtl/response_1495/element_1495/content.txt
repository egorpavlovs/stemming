	Palais in Pictures Contest: Announcing the winners!

	 
		adminJS
		Non classifié(e)
					
    
    The winners of the “Palais in Pictures” contest are Saint-Léonard’s Christiane Casavant, Montréal’s Martin New and Sainte-Catherine’s Cristiano Carvalho. Ms. Casavant wins an iPad, while Mr. New and Mr. Carvalho each take home a $100 Galeries du Palais gift card.
Christiane Casavant
Martin New
Cristiano Carvalho
The winning shots were selected from among all the pictures received, by a five-person jury that scored the entries based on their originality, visual composition, lighting and framing. What really stood out from the entries was the drawing power of the Palais’ iconic colourful glass exterior and its contribution to the city’s dynamic energy!
Congratulations to our winners, and to everyone who entered, thank you!
