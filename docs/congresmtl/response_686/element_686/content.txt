	23 juin 2014
	
	Ambassadors’ Club Partner Jacques Turgeon appointed Executive Director of the CHUM

	 
		communications
		Club des Ambassadeurs
					
    
     

The Palais des congrès de Montréal would like to congratulate Mr. Jacques Turgeon on his appointment as Executive Director of the Centre hospitalier de l’Université de Montréal (CHUM), one of Montréal’s two new superhospitals. Mr. Turgeon, a Partner of the Palais Ambassadors’ Club, will make the transition from his current position as Director of the CHUM Research Centre to begin his four-year mandate on June 19, 2014.
A tenured professor in the Université de Montréal’s faculty of pharmacology, Mr. Turgeon has an impressive academic background and has supervised the construction of several research institutions.
He is an outstanding example of the calibre of professionals who are members of the Palais Ambassador’s Club, a network of almost 300 professors, researchers and business leaders who have made important contributions in their respective fields of activity.
The Palais des congrès de Montréal wishes Mr. Turgeon much success in his new position.
