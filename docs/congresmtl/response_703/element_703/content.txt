	Ambassador News

	 
		communications
		Club des Ambassadeurs
					
    
    Serge Gauthier (2006), was named member of the Order of Canada, a distinction which recognizes outstanding achievement and dedication to the community and service to the nation.
Michel Wong Kee Song (2003), received the Queen Elizabeth II Diamond Jubilee Medal in 2014 and the Medal of the Order of Merit of Brossard in 2014.
On a sadder note, Roland Proulx (2005) suddenly passed away last month. Our condolences to his family, relatives and colleagues of the University of Montreal, where he worked as Professor and Director of planning.
