	2012-13 Annual Report: Planning for the future with determination

	 
		communications
		Club des Ambassadeurs, Congrès, Créneaux d’excellence
					
    
    The Société du Palais des congrès de Montréal 2012-13 Annual Report submitted in October to the National Assembly is now available.
Excellent results
The Palais ended the year on a positive note, with over $19.7 million in own-source revenues, thus making 2012-2013 the second best year in that department in its 30-year history. The 359 events the Palais hosted during the year attracted over 642,000 visitors and generated $181.9 million for the city of Montréal and Québec as a whole.
Some of these events were made possible thanks to the commitment and dedication of Palais Ambassadors.
• Michel Venne: 11th CIVICUS World Assembly
• Dr. Eduardo L. Franco and Dr. Michel Gélinas: 2012 UICC World Cancer Congress
• Ke Wu: IEEE MTT-S International Microwave Symposium
• Bernard Courtois: 18th World Congress on Information Technology
• Emile Bouchard and Pierre Labrosse: 45th Annual Convention of the American Association of Bovine Practitioners
Exceptional business performance
For a third consecutive year, the Palais teams did an exceptional job securing events for the coming years. Thanks to an impressive bid book, the Palais truly stood out from the competition on the international stage, as evidenced by the results. Over 388 agreements were concluded across all markets for future events!
Collaborative effort
The Palais des congrès continued to actively develop solid partnerships within the hubs of excellence that help drive the Montréal and Québec economy. This resulted in the creation, in 2012, of a new Healthcare Sector Committee, modeled after the Aerospace Major Events Committee, which has been yielding tangible outcomes.
As always, the Palais was able to count on its longstanding partners, the Palais Ambassadors, who make it possible to enjoy privileged access to universities here and abroad through their tireless work and their contribution to the edification of Québec society.
