	1 octobre 2013
	
	Dr. Hany Moustapha receives prestigious honour

	 
		adminJS
		Club des Ambassadeurs
					
    
    The Palais des congrès de Montréal is extremely proud to count Dr. Moustapha among its Ambassadors! Not only is he an emblematic figure  in his field, he is also an inspiration to emerging researchers through his dedication, involvement and achievements.
Dr. Hany Moustapha, an eminent propulsion expert, has been playing a leading role within Québec’s aerospace community for the last 30 years, performing research, developing technologies and influencing policy.

 
Thanks to his work and substantial contribution, Dr. Moustapha, an Emeritus Ambassador of the Palais, is among the remarkable luminaries who have helped enlighten Québec society. For this reason, Hany Moustapha was recently awarded the distinguished National Order of Québec.
The Québec Premier was among those who had nothing but praise for Dr. Moustapha!
“Your exceptional work, determination and dedication have opened new horizons.”
Pauline Marois, Premier of Québec
