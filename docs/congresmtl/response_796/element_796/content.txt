	13 juin 2013
	
	Yet another victory for Montréal !

	 
		communications
		Montréal international
					
    
    
Montréal ranked top host city in America for international events
Business tourists from all over the world are simply drawn to the city’s unique charm and the “joie de vivre” of Montréalers! A fact confirmed by the Union of International Associations’ (UIA) official rankings for 2012, which clearly establish Montréal as the most attractive and most popular host city on the continent for convention or exhibition organizers.
This is the second year in a row Montréal rates as the leading host destination in America for international events, much to the delight of everyone at the city’s convention centre, the Palais des congrès.
Finishing ahead of cities such as Washington, New York, Boston, San Diego, Rio de Janeiro, Buenos Aires, Toronto or even Vancouver demonstrates that Montréal can deliver the unique and particularly appealing assets that are sought after by major international organizations.
And all of this is in addition to Montréal also having been ranked first in North America, by the International Congress and Convention Association (ICCA), for number of conventions hosted.
The Palais will continue to lead the way in order to attract even bigger events and thus actively contribute, as it has for 30 years, to Montréal’s vitality and dynamism!
