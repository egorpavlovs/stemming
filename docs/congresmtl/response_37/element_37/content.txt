                        Welcome to the Palais des congrès de Montréal
We are pleased to offer you the ideal setting in Montréal to hold consumer and trade shows.
The Palais is located in the heart of the city, is easily accessible and has many logistical advantages that facilitate the installation of booths:

On-site storage space
An official customs clearance point recognized by the Canada Border Services Agency
18 loading docks and an indoor waiting area capable of accommodating ten 53-foot trailer trucks at once
High load capacity

Many opportunities to show your colors
Do you want to increase your visibility at events? Ask your promoter about the range of visibility options now available, thanks to the new technological environment at the Palais.
Customer Portal: All your orders in a single point of access
Introduced in the fall of 2013, the Palais Customer Portal provides an electronic interface that is unique in Canada, where you can place your orders online 24/7. Fast, user-friendly and secure, the Portal makes it easy to participate in a show at the Palais.
