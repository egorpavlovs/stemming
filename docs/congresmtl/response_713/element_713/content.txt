	28 août 2015
	
	Smart kitchen!

	 
		communications
		Conférences, réunions et galas, Expérience culinaire
					
    
    Since Spring 2015, the Palais kitchen has been operating with the very latest event technology. That’s because the Palais’ exclusive caterer, Capital Catering, invested nearly one million dollars on 12 new smart ovens and complementary accessories. The new equipment makes it possible to cook several different items at once by individually setting cooking options, humidity levels, as well as cooling and warming sequences for impeccable plating.

The kitchen has tripled its plating capacity for large banquets of up to 1,500 guests, while halving the overall time required to serve them. Above all, the new tools give the chef and his team the opportunity to really showcase their creativity and inventiveness thanks to new cooking methods that can be electronically programmed and new deliciously attractive presentation options!

Capital Catering Executive Chef Simon Devost-Dulude says: “It’s really exciting to revisit your recipes with new technology to extract all of the flavour from our ingredients. The process allows us to serve eye-pleasing dishes that are guaranteed to be warm and flavourful.”
