	Contact MTL launches with verve!

	 
		communications
		Club des Ambassadeurs, Montréal international, Créneaux d’excellence
					
    
    On September 30, 2015, Montréal International and its partners launched the Greater Montréal Ambassadors Network’s Contact MTL. The evening brought together over 800 dedicated Montrealers for a celebration of this unprecedented drive to shine the spotlight on the city’s achievements.
Active since September 2014, the network aims to raise the international profile, competitiveness and prosperity of Greater Montréal, by mobilizing citizens from all backgrounds as well as their respective contacts and networks.
Present on social media, including via @GreaterMTL, the news and information shared showcases the expertise, knowhow and talent of Montrealers that contribute to making the city such an attractive destination.
Anyone who is passionate about Greater Montréal, who loves learning about its achievements and sharing them with others is invited to join the Greater Montréal Ambassadors Network. Will you be the next ambassador?
