                            Conference and meeting
                        

                        



                        
Access multifunctional spaces
Located at the heart of the city, easy to access by car, public transport, and on foot, the Palais offers an unbeatable selection of versatile and ultramodern spaces that adapt to all types of events. Whether you need VIP salons, spacious conference halls, or several adjacent rooms on the same floor, our capable team can help you find the ideal solution for your event.

113 rooms and spaces
362,077 ft2 (33,638 m2) of meeting rooms
543,954 ft2 (50,535 m2) of reception spaces
5,705 ft2 (530 m2) of VIP rooms

Organize an outdoor event (May to October)
A winning option to add a twist to your business meetings, receptions, press conferences or any other promotional activity, the terraces will make your next event an unforgettable moment.

Terrasse du Palais 19,575 ft2 (1,818 m2) partially covered with an air-conditioned/heated marquee and offering a breathtaking view of the Saint Lawrence River and the Jacques-Cartier Bridge.
Belvédère 6,688 ft2 (621 m2)


Offer your guests superior quality 
Thanks to the creativity and know-how of our qualified personnel, nothing is left to chance in view of exceeding your expectations and ensuring the success of your conference or meeting.

Finalist for the title of World's Best Congress Centre, the Palais holds the most prestigious international certification, the Quality Standards of the International Association of Convention Centres.

 See the testimonials from our satisfied customers
Treat your guests to a gastronomic experience exclusive to the Palais
The success of a conference or meeting also rests on the gastronomic experience. Our chef Simon Devost-Dulude and his brigade will delight even the most delicate palates. In season, you can even enjoy produce harvested from the Palais' own gardens.
Enjoy reliable technology and peace of mind
You can rely on the Canadian leader in matters of technological presentation solutions and official supplier of the Palais. Freeman Audio Visual acts as an extension of the Palais' technological services and offers clients many advantages.
To successfully plan all aspects of your event, let our Conferences, Meetings and Galas advisors guide you every step of the way.
