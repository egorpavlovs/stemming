	New Board Members

	 
		communications
		Société du Palais des congrès
					
    
    The Société du Palais des congrès de Montréal welcomed two new members on its Board of Directors in November of 2015.
Céline Gamache
 Céline Gamache is a member of the Prud’homme Consulting Group management team. She is primarily in charge of recruitment for professional, managerial and executive positions in corporate management, production, engineering and IT fields. She is also on the firm’s executive coaching and career transition teams. Her HR and marketing experience at Building Products of Canada Corp and Sodisco-Howden serve her well. She holds a Bachelor of Industrial Relations from Université de Montréal and an MBA from HEC Montréal.
 
Annie Tremblay
 Annie Tremblay is the President of the ESSENCE advisory services. A senior consultant in the area of managing strategic performance, she helps organizations harness the tools they need to rigorously monitor their strategies’ rollout. Her tenures at reputable firms such as Ernst & Young, National Bank Financial and Teleglobe Canada, combined with her advisory work, afford her a 360-degree insight into the operational and corporate governance dynamics and challenges associated with managing change. She earned her ASC designation from the Collège des Administrateurs de Sociétés and her Bachelor of Business Administration from HEC Montréal. She serves on the Board of Governors of the Université du Québec
