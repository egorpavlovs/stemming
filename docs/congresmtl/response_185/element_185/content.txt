                            Complimentary Wheelchairs
                        

                        



                        
                
                        Wheelchairs may be borrowed for consumer shows. They are available for loan free of charge and must be reserved in advance. A $25 deposit and ID will be required, both of which will be returned to you once you bring back the wheelchair.If the event is already in progress, it might be possible, subject to availability, to borrow a wheelchair without a reservation at the coat check located in Hall Viger.
