	Consumer shows: What’s new for 2016

	 
		communications
		Expositions publiques et commerciales
					
    
    

Lots of new and interesting events among the consumer shows coming to the Palais des congrès de Montréal in 2016. And plenty of variety too, including cars, motorcycles, weddings, food, jobs and the outdoors.
What’s new in 2016:

The National Wedding Show, January 30 and 31, 2016
The RV Show, February 4 to 7, 2016
The Salon de l’Homme, March 10 to 13, 2016
The Salon de la course à pieds, March 12 and 13, 2016
The Outdoor Adventure Show, April 2 and 3, 2016

Just like the Montréal International Auto Show, which recently attracted over 196,000 visitors, many consumer favourites are back this year, including:

 The Salon national de la pourvoirie et du bateau de pêche, January 28 to 31, 2016


The Motorcycle Show, February 26 to 28, 2016
Expo Manger santé et vivre vert, March 11 to 13, 2016
The National Women’s Show, April 29 to May 1, 2016

Check out our calendar for the full listing of events.
