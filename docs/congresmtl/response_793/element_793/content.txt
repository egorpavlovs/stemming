	State-of-the-art technology and revenue-generating opportunities

	 
		communications
		Conférences, réunions et galas, Congrès, Projet technologique, Environnement technologique
					
    
    A tireless innovation leader, the Palais des congrès undertook an ambitious IT modernization project that includes a powerful and highly dependable Wi-Fi network, as well as a topnotch digital signage service.
Event planners can now use this new technology to host greener events, enhance the participant experience, and best of all, generate more money!

Technology serving events
The Palais des congrès Wi-Fi network can handle up to 20,000 wireless connections simultaneously and provide event planners with two sizeable advantages:

Staying connected: Offering participants fast, secure and free Wi-Fi so they can continue to be productive, and connected, throughout the event.
Generating sponsorship revenues: Offering partners and sponsors the opportunity to offer free wireless internet to participants throughout the convention centre, in exchange for prime recognition on the login page, giving them exclusive visibility each time a user connects to the Palais Wi-Fi network.

As for the new digital signage network, it comprises 88 55” HD screens and an impressive 90 sq. ft. video wall in the Palais’ busiest area. This represents even more benefits for event planners:

Greener events: The network eliminates the need for printed signs, thus reducing the event’s environmental footprint while maximizing its visibility.
Economical: Lower printing and handling/shipping costs.
Enhanced experience for participants: Tailored messages and venue location information complete with directions. Plus, last-minute changes to keep visitors updated with the very latest information.
Added revenues: The ad space on the digital sign network may be resold to sponsors or exhibitors, so they enjoy remarkable visibility while event planners generate more revenues for their event!

More new features coming in 2014:

Client portal: Launch of an e-commerce portal that will enable exhibitors to order their services directly online 24-7.
ZAP: Spanning the hotels and major attractions within a 3 km radius (final phase) around the Palais, ZAP will allow users to enjoy an unparalleled integrated browsing experience even outside the Palais des congrès de Montréal.

Contact the Palais des congrès de Montréal team to find out how they can help you propel your event to new levels of success and profits!
