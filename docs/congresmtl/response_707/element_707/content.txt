	The Ambassadors Lounge: A prestigious venue accessible to all members

	 
		communications
		Club des Ambassadeurs
					
    
    The Palais des congrès de Montréal inaugurated its Ambassadors Lounge in 2008. This VIP meeting room offering a spectacular view of Montréal and the Jacques-Cartier bridge is named after the members of the Ambassadors Club in recognition of their work in helping maintain Montréal among the world’s top convention cities. All duly accredited members enjoy free access. They may book the room to host meetings for committees working on development projects or preparing bids for events, or even for private business functions. The Lounge is a unique gathering place housing a sitting room, a cloakroom, a boardroom and access to the outdoor terrace.

To learn more about the Ambassadors Lounge or to reserve it, Club members simply need to contact Amélie Asselin, the advisor in charge of the Ambassadors Lounge, at clubdesambassadeurs@congresmtl.com or 514 871-5897.
