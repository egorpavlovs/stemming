                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Located on the Seine, on the Ile de la Cite, les Jardins du Pont Neuf offers more than 500 m2 of reception space, where modularity, comfort and the art of receiving have been taken as the thread of this new concept in the most Pure tradition of Parisian elegance. Our terraces, covered and heated, are fully equipped to receive your guests summer and winter.
