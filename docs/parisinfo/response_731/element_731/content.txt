                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Perfectly situated in the heart of Golf Disneyland®, the Radisson Blu Hotel Paris, Marne-la-Vallée offers you the opportunity to stay near Disneyland® Paris in a beautifully green setting.
The hotel's 250 calm, comfortable rooms, as well as the wellness area where you can relax and take a dip in the heated indoor pool, are just a part of what makes this hotel truly exceptional. With easy access by plane (45 minutes from the Orly and Charles de Gaulle airports), by train (5 minutes from the Chessy Marne-La-Vallée TGV and Eurostar station) and by car (close to the A4 motorway), the hotel also has 270 free parking spaces as well as our shuttle service to the train station operating every 30 minutes. 
The Chardon Bar's outdoor terrace and cozy indoor space with an impressive fireplace, along with the hotel's two restaurants, offer a wide range of dishes and cocktails to satisfy your tastes. With 1,200 m² of meeting space spread across 27 rooms, you can hold large private conferences and receptions or book a private space at your convenience. These rooms can be rearranged and their lighting reconfigured to become a theatre seating up to 300 people. Our Meeting and Events coordinators can help you choose your event's menu with refined and inventive cuisine elegantly prepared by our chef, as well as ensuring that you have access to all the latest audiovisual equipment to help your event run smoothly. Free high-speed Wi-Fi and plenty of natural light complete this perfect setting.
The hotel's Grand Foyer is the ideal place for receptions and coffee breaks. The Chardon Bar's outdoor terrace and cozy indoor space complete with its impressive fireplace, as well as the hotel's two restaurants, are available for hosting your special event.
