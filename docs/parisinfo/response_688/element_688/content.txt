                        Description
                        
                                                    
        A unique experience in Paris! A stone's throw from the Eiffel Tower, the Mercure Paris Centre Tour Eiffel offers a welcoming environment for all of your events right in the heart of Paris. With 6 adaptable meeting rooms on one level, including 5 which are naturally lit, this hotel and its 405 guest rooms is an ideal place to combine work with pleasure in a peaceful atmosphere.
    
                                                

                                                    
                                
                                                                            Number of guest rooms : 405
                                                                                                                                            
                            
                        
                                                    
                                Clientele
                                
                                                                            Professionals,                                                                             Private individuals                                                                    
                            
                                                            Events held
                                Cocktail, Conférence de presse, Dîner, Lancement de produit, Réunion / conférence / journée d'étude, Séminaire résidentiel, Show room, Soirée dansante
