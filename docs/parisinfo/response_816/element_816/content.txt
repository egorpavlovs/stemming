               
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The restaurant Dans Le Noir? offers you to enjoy a unifying, convivial and disinhibiting moment in team.
You will have a unique sensory experience around the tasting of a surprise Menu in the total darkness. A moment that will delight your taste buds.
Around the surprise Menu, the restaurant offers various formulas allowing you to customize your order according to your expectations. Of course all restrictions and allergies are collected at the time of your coming, and the Menu is adapted accordingly.
During the meal, the roles will reverse. You will be served by unexpected guides who will reassure you and make you live a beautiful moment.
You can privatize the restaurant for your special events around lunch or dinner.
We can also organize your conferences, meetings, team building or product launches Dans Le Noir? Which is a source of multiple opportunities for a group!
You can also educate your employees about disability in a fun way. An original moment that will challenge you and mark the spirits.
You can enjoy our light bar space and our lounge area in the basement with about 20 seats to start the experience around an aperitif or a meeting.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Visual impairment: the restaurant is in total darkness with service carried out by blind or partially-sighted servers. no obstacles at eye-level (wall lamps, displays, underneath the stairs, etc.), secured stairs with tactile and visual contrast 50cm before the first step down, continuous hand rail and contrasting edge of steps, information and signs in large print, signs at an appropriate height.
