                            Classic restaurants, bistros and brasseries 
                            Looking for a good restaurant for a group, or a place to celebrate your birthday or a loved one’s, with help from an experienced event planner? Planning a small get-together to celebrate your wedding in a typically Parisian restaurant? Have a business dinner to organize? Why not opt for private dining at a bistro or brasserie in Paris? 
                        

                         Find the ideal restaurant for your private group and hand the task of organizing everything to a professional planner, who will go all out to make your event a success.Private dining at a restaurant in Paris is an opportunity to experiment with new options. Some current trends are well worth trying, like the ‘bistronomy’ movement (a blend of ‘bistro’ and ‘gastronomy’): top chefs cooking in an informal bistro atmosphere. You can also sample a fresh twist on traditional French cooking at brasseries and restaurants with an inventive approach to food, where you can taste delicious dishes in an innovative setting.There is no lack of bistros for private hire in Paris: places where you can enjoy top-quality food in a calm atmosphere, with experienced service providers handling all the arrangements for your lunch, cocktail party or dinner.  See our selection of classic restaurants, brasseries and bistros in Paris to find the provider best suited to your requirements.
