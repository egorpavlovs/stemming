                            What's on?
                            
        Hotel opening: 12 July 2018
    
                        
                    
                    
                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Lutetia, an iconic hotel in the heart of Saint-Germain-des-Prés, is the Left Bank's only Grand Hotel. With modern, contemporary decor, the hotel offers all the services and comforts of today's Palace hotels.
Of the hotel's 184 stylishly-decorated rooms, 47 are suites. The banquet halls, including the the iconic Salon Cristal ballroom, have a total area of 700 m² and have been entirely renovated with modern equipment for all kinds of private and professional functions.
The Lutetia, an emminent Parisian luxury hotel, has started a new chapter in its rich history.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Reduced mobility: car park with specifc disabled spaces, floor-level entrance or ramp, lowered reception desk with leg room underneath, adapted toilet stalls with grab rail and sink with leg room underneath, 5 adapted rooms including en-suite with floor-level shower (with foldable or moveable seat) and sink with leg room underneath, restaurant with aisles wider than 1.4 m and tables with room for a wheelchair.
- Reduced visibility: glass doors with contrasting designs, no head-level obstacles in the corridors, stairs with tactile and visual cues before the first step down and consistent hand rails, lift with adapted keypad (buttons with raised text or braille) and speakers to announce floor numbers, signs in large font at reasonable reading height.
- Reduced hearing: online booking and payment on the website, fire alarm accompanied by flashing lights.
- Mental disability: trainsed staff, easily-understandable and -readable signs with accompanying images, entrance monitored with CCTV and visible from the main reception.
