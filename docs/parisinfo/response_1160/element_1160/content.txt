                            Fashion Freak Show: the whacky show by Jean-Paul Gaultier
                            From October 2nd, discover Fashion Freak Show at the Folies Bergère, a revue and fashion show rolled into one by the provocative fashion designer. 
                        

                        Spend memorable moments with your colleagues or during a group stay in Paris, thanks to this offbeat show mixing fashion and pop culture!This autumn, the enfant terrible of fashion takes you back through his career. In an eccentric and provocative manner, he recounts his early days in the industry, his greatest fashion parades, his evenings at the Palace, and London party nights. Dancers, actors, and artists perform a 2 hr 30 burlesque and breathtaking show, made even more sublime by the mythical stage of the Folies Bergère. For the occasion, new exclusive creations will be added to cult pieces like the iconic sailor’s top or the famous conical corset worn by Madonna! A new kind of show, running until December 30th. Get your tickets here!
