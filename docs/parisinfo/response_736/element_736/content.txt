                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The latest generation Courtyard Paris Arcueil hotel is designed for the comfort of today's business travellers. It offers 170 guest rooms, each with a surface area of 25 m², enabling you to get the most out of your stay in Paris. A few metres from the Laplace RER B station, the hotel is directly connected to Orly and Charles de Gaulle airports.
