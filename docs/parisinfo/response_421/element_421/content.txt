                            Maison Astor Paris: Curio Collection by Hilton undergoes a true metamorphosis
                            In the capital’s 8th arrondissement, Maison Astor - a 1907 historic residence – re-emerges in April under the Curio by Hilton brand, after a complete makeover.
                        

                        The hotel is ideally situated for a business trip or leisure break, just a few minutes’ walk from the Champs Elysées, the Opera and major department stores, at the intersection of a vibrant district for business and Paris haute couture.Its 131 rooms and 4 suites have been fully refurbished in a contemporary style inspired by Parisian apartments and feature large windows and high ceilings decorated with mouldings. The suites on the 7th floor are veritable works of art, signed Hermes, and offer a panoramic view over the Paris rooftops.Maison Astor Paris, Curio Collection by Hilton has two meeting spaces: the 34 m²-library Kitty for meetings of up to 18 people in U configuration and 30 people in theatre configuration, and the 10 m²-Cabinet de Voyage for a private cocktail reception, lunch or private dinner for up to 10 people.The restaurant and bar, drenched in natural light through the glass ceiling, offer quality local products, beautifully cooked and delicious cocktails. The restaurant can be privately hired for dinner or lunch for up to 40 guests or for a cocktail reception for up to 150 guests.
