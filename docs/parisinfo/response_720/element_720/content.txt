                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Entirely renovated in 2017, the hotel offers 240 spacious, soundproofed and air-conditioned rooms, 12 meeting/seminar rooms, 2 restaurants, a bar and a terrace for enjoying the sun. 
You can also enjoy the fitness centre to get your blood pumping and the freely-accessible mini-market in the foyer.
The hotel offers 12 meeting rooms equipped with state of the art visual and sound systems and private entrance for conferences.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: specific disabled parking spaces, floor-level entrance or ramp, lowered and adapted reception desk, communal toilets with grab rail and adapted sink, 6 adapted rooms with en-suite equipped with walk-in shower, adapted sink and toilet with surrounding space and grab rail.
- Visual impairment: no head-level obstacles in the corridors, stairs with tactile and visual contrast before first step down, with continuous hand rail and contrasting edges, lift with speaker to announce floor numbers, signs with large font at a reasonable reading height.
- Hearing impairment: online booking and/payment on the website, lift with visual display and/or magnetic induction loop, fire alarm accompanied by flashing lights in adapted rooms.
- Learning disability: trained and educated staff, easy to read and understand signs with pictograms, entrance monitored with CCTV and visible from the reception.
