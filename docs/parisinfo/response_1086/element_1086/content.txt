                            The newly revamped L’Arc Paris at L’Etoile
                            After 18 months of renovation work by Lenny Kravitz’s design studio, L’Arc Paris has reopened its doors
                        

                        The Arc Paris, one of the most iconic clubs in Paris has reopened after an 18-month refit. Its transformation was entrusted to Lenny Kravitz, as his first project in France and in Europe.Facing the Place de l’Etoile, the garden offers a unique view of the lively scene around the majestic Arc de Triomphe. Kravitz Design have kept the white marble counter of the bar. Marble has been used throughout, including for the customized tables. There is a new cocktail bar at the entrance and a newly-designed smoking area.Open at weekends for exclusive events, L’Arc Paris is the ideal venue for prestigious functions and private or business events: upscale with a festive atmosphere.Up to 400 people for a cocktail party or 120 for a dinner. For these events, the venue provides the kind of technical package to be expected from an international club.For further details :Please contact Gregory Areinx :Mail : g.areinx@larc-paris.com
