                            The Terrass″ Hotel unveils its new event space! 
                            Ideally located in the lively and historic Montmartre district, the Terrass″ Hotel unveils its new event space ‘STAGE’. 
                        

                        This trendy 400 m²-plus studio features a glass roof, plant wall and urban furniture. The idea is to break with conventional study days and provide an energizing venue for original team-building events and seminars! Five meeting rooms (from 32 m² to 92 m²), equipped with the latest technology, can host up to 90 people.  For lunches and breaks, there is lots of room for conviviality! The open kitchen, lounge areas and different dining rooms offer ‘home-made’ dishes, and drinks throughout the day.The Terrass” Hotel Montmartre offers a panoramic view over Paris and guest rooms with beautiful artistic decor and furnishings.  Not to be missed!

            Events venues in Paris
        
        Many Paris museums, monuments and chateaux are available to hire for daytime or evening events. Rent a private room to hold your party, catwalk show or conference in Paris.  

    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            Unusual venues for an event ‘just like at home’
        
        Looking to organize your next professional event at a Parisian venue with an intimate and welcoming atmosphere ‘just like at home’, a private evening function in a magnificent Parisian apartment, or are seeking an unusual rental in the heart of Paris for a group trip? Then, hurry and check out our selection of venues that combine modernity and originality, tailor-made services, and upscale amenities in an atmosphere that’s just like home. With choices like New York style lofts with roof-top terraces, chic and sobre apartments with views over the rooftops and famous Paris landmarks, and vaulted cellars, you are sure to find the perfect venue!
