                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Hotel Ibis Paris Bastille Opéra 11th is located in the historic city centre, near the Seine quaysides and the Marais district. Your clients will stay just 10 min. from the Gare de Lyon, the Gare du Nord and the Bercy business district. We offer you the best price-quality ratio in the district for the planning of an effective working day where you will find all the facilities to satisfy your customers. Our 6 flexible meeting rooms with daylight host from 7 to 80 people. The establishment has 305 guest rooms, a traditional brasserie and a sunny terrace.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Motor disability: car park with reserved adapted spaces, ground level entrance or inclined surface, adapted reception/checkout with hollowed-out lower part, common area toilet equipped with a transfer bar and adapted washbasin with lower part hollowed-out, 7 guest rooms with bathrooms equipped with a shower on the same level (with folding seat or mobile seat), a sink with hollowed-out lower part, toilet with lateral space and transfer bar, dining room with alleys more than 1.40 m wide, with tables adapted for people in wheelchairs. 
-Visual impairment: presence of transparent doors/windows walls indicated by contrasting elements, absence of obstacles at face level in airs of circulation, staircase with tactile and visual contrasts before the first step down with continuous handrail, with contrasting edges of each step, lift with adapted controls (raised buttons and/or in Braille), with cabin with floors announced, information in large letters, displayed at a reasonable height.
-Hearing impairment: booking and/or payment possible on the website, reception/checkout equipped with a magnetic induction loop, light flash coupled with sound fire alarm (where a deaf person may find themselves alone). 
-Learning disability: trained/informed staff, simple to understand signage, easy to read, including pictograms, monitored entrance door, visible from the front desk/checkout.
