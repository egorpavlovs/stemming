                            The Roch Hotel & Spa, a new chic and confidential address
                            Tucked away in the heart of the capital’s 1st arrondissement, between Place Vendôme and Place de l’Opéra, the Roch Hotel & Spa is a smart new 5-stars boutique hotel.

                        

                        The Parisian interior designer Sarah Lavoine has imagined the stylish decor of the hotel perfectly with choice colours, lighting and materials to make harmonious and elegant living spaces that are both simple and relaxing. The 32 rooms and 5 suites offer impressive comfort and modernity.  Cutting-edge high-tech facilities, particularly adapted to business clientele, are at your disposal and include flat screen TV, interactive multi-service tablets (web access, room-service remote control, alarm clock, luggage service ...), and Bluetooth speakers. All suites have a private hammam.Indulge in a festival of culinary creations for a quiet business meal at any time of day, either in the restaurant with its soft velvet upholstery and hushed atmosphere, outside in the courtyard garden, on the terrace, or by the fireplace. At lunchtime and in the evening, the starred chef Arnaud Faye offers inventive dishes, unveiling a mix of new flavours, such as sea bream with fennel/tomatoes confit/ taggiasca olives. In the afternoon, tea-time is a must, and in the evening, there is a remarkable selection of wines, champagne, and cocktails to sample.To indulge your staff or clients, you can organize an exclusive event at the Suite Saint Roch (47 m²), which has a private terrace overlooking the courtyard garden. For a 'Paris apartment’ configuration, the 6th floor is available for full private hire, including the Suite Saint Roch and the adjacent ‘Deluxe Indulgence guest room with hammam’.For a moment of sheer relaxation, head to the pool & spa: a pool made of black lava rock, a modern gym, a hammam and a spa with numerous upscale face and body treatments.
