                                                    Description
                            
                                
        La Cité internationale universitaire: Organise your event in the heart of a unique Parisian location.
More than 30 lounges and 4 amphitheatres at your disposal.
La Maison Internationale, built in the 1930s thanks to the patronage of John D. Rockefeller Jr., was designed to encourage networking between students of all nationalities.
The style, inspired by the Chateau de Fontainbleau, which has the appearance of a great monument.
La Maison Internationale offers catering services for different needs: snacks and coffee breaks, lunch and dinner, buffets and cocktail parties, etc.
We welcome your guests in our different restaurant spaces: Le Restaurant La Terrasse, Le Restaurant Universitaire, and the Cafétéria.
We offer a technical service packaged or tailor-made for our different spaces.
Our logistic and technical team is at your disposal.
It takes care of all your needs: audio, video, simultaneous translation, security agents, hostesses ...
Find in our living rooms: flipchart, WiFi and signage included.
Guided tours and team-building: go for a world tour in the heart of Paris. Quotation: request online.
