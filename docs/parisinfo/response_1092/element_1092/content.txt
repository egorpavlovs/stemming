                            Hollywood on Seine by Marriott
                            The Hôtel Courtyard by Marriott arranges your event for you at the Cité du Cinéma for a unique inside view of the world of cinema.
                        

                        The Cité du Cinéma, a veritable temple of cinema, offers numerous spaces for your events, whether it be a simple meeting, a cocktail reception or a team building event.Discover the various seminar spaces (auditoriums, meeting rooms, boardrooms) available to host groups of 10 to 428 people. Enjoy a business lunch or dinner elaborated by the chef of the Hôtel Marriott! You can combine this with a team building event. Make Up For Ever offers you a team building event on the theme of special effects make-up at the cinema. Take part in the team-building movie quiz, which is made up of 10 activities on the theme of cinema. The Cité du Cinéma also welcomes groups for a guided tour of the different spaces (1 hr 45).Finally, take advantage of the accommodation facilities (150 bedrooms) and restaurant facilities at the Hôtel Marriott Paris Saint-Denis, situated close to the Cité du Cinéma. As well as meeting rooms, the hotel offers you and your clients or colleagues places to get together and relax (garden, terrace, and restaurant).
