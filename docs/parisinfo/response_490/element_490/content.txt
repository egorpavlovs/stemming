                                                    Description
                            
                                
        If you need safe and sophisticated transport for your guests, without the stress of organizing it;  STANDING EURO TOURS is there for you!
With 30 years of experience, Standing Euro Tours is the first 'top-of-the-range' transport in Paris, specializing in minibuses and coaches. Ever since it began, Standing Euro Tours continues to innovate and assert itself in this sector, with a team of experienced professionals as well as a fleet of vehicles always at the cutting edge of technology.
    
                            
                                            

                    
                    



    
        Services 
    
    
        
            
                                
                    Description of fleet
                    
        Standing Euro Tours has the latest generation of 'luxury' Berlines, minibuses and coaches, secure, respectful to the environment and equipped with: Wi-Fi, individual USB plugs, leather upholstery with small lounge, etc.
    
                
                
                                
                    Type of vehicle
                    
                                                    Buses, cars
                                                    Minibuses, people carriers (7+ seater)
