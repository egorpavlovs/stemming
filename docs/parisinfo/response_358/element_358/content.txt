                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Paris le Bourget is the Greater Paris region's third exhibition complex and a very international venue. It offers welcoming green spaces and light-filled halls that are very visitor-friendly. The complex is an excellent location for large-scale events, with 250,000 sq. m of grounds and 80,000 sq. m of interior spaces. Paris le Bourget has five halls, an auditorium and six meeting rooms.
