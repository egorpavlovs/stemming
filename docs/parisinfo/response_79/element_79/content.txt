                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Marin d'Eau Douce's marina hosts all kind of business events, whether seminars, team building or incentive days on the water with a treasure hunt, orienteering course or race.
These challenges on our electric boats, requiring no license, put employees in the heart of the action in an exciting situation with their colleagues.
If you'd like to organize a different kind of business seminar in a pleasant work setting, we are here to help.
Your day can be planned to combine work with pleasure. Work sessions in the morning then time to relax in the afternoon with our electric boats (or the other way round.)
Set off on a treasure hunt or for some nautical orienteering.
The combination of work sessions and fun activities will make your business seminars a success and please your employees.
They won't forget the unique experience of sailing an electric boat in Paris.
