                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Cook'n with Class Paris cookery school was created in 2007 in Montmartre (18th arronissement) by Eric Fradeau, a skilled French chef.
The team of professional chefs welcomes you with three workshops dedicated to "at home" French food and wine. The school can welcome groups of between 8 and 20 people for friendly classes that are accessible to all: from experienced cooks to beginners to those who just want to taste.
We offer themed cookery classes in a laid-back, happy atmosphere.
