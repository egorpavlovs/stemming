                                                    Description
                            
                                
        An historical, prestigious place in the heart of the 7th arrondissement, the Maison de la Chimie provides visitors with a modern house and a genuine soul. All types of events can be held here, with their Amphithéâtre Lavoisier (854 seats), Petit Amphithéâtre (220 seats), 18 bright and naturally-lit conference rooms, and the private garden (900m²).
A unique place with different sides to it, combining the charm of the 18th century with the simplicity of the 1930s, the Maison de la Chimie is definitely an important congress and conference centre in France. 
The Maison de la Chimie welcomes congresses, exhibitions, forums, shows, debates, conferences, study days, general estates, general assemblies, seminars, meetings, administration councils, products launches, press conferences, and events.
