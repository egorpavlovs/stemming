                        Description
                        
                                                    
        We are currently carrying out maintenance and renovation works on all of our seminar rooms - Spacious and bright, our 94 air-conditioned guest rooms, with refined decor, are all equipped with a mini-bar, Wifi and satellite TV for your comfort. 24hr bar and room service. Musical entertainment for your lunches and dinners at the Bistrot Mirabeau, which welcomes you from 12.30pm to 3pm, and from 7pm to 11pm. Organize your business meals, cocktail dinners and much more at the Hôtel Auteuil Tour Eiffel. We look forward to using our expertise to best meet your needs.
