                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Palais des Congrès d’Issy's unique setting is a favourite with guests. Rich in architectural detail from the golden age of Art Deco, its 3,000 sq. m provide the ideal setting for unforgettable events. It is comprised of two multi-purposes spaces, a spectacular amphitheatre and eleven meeting rooms.
