                            Business meals in an original setting
                            Looking for a restaurant with an original theme for a get-together with your staff or customers?  There are so many offbeat ideas to bring a touch of fantasy to your next business lunch or dinner in Paris!
                        

                        Why not climb aboard a special kind of bus to discover the French capital as you've never seen it before, go back to the time of the kings and queens of France for a memorable dinner show, enjoy a bird’s eye view of the horse races ...Dans le noir: Blind tastingAn amazing experience for your senses awaits you in the heart of the 4th arrondissement of Paris. The concept of this unusual restaurant called Dans le Noir? is to enjoy surprise menus of 2 to 5 dishes in total darkness. Traditional French cuisine is served (vegan menu available) accompanied by delicious wines. To guide you and answer your questions, staff can be present throughout your meal. All you have to do is relax and enjoy this awakening of the 5 senses!Get a proposalBus Burger: gourmet trip Bus Burger, a travelling bus restaurant kitted out like a 1970s American diner, lets you combine a meal with a sightseeing tour of the French capital! Comfortably seated, enjoy tucking into a 100% French burger on a bus tour lasting 1 hour 15 minutes. Relax and admire the districts and monuments of Paris: Place de la Bastille, the Pont Neuf, Arc de Triomphe, Louvre Museum ... The bus, which is available for full private hire, can accommodate up to 31 people seated or up to 50 people standing.Les Belles Plantes: bucolic breakThe Belles Plantes opened in 2018 within the grounds of the Jardin des Plantes. Open all day, this bright and modern restaurant hosts after-work,drinks or business lunches in an idyllic setting. Savour original and delicious cocktails, and seasonal cooking including Parisian brasserie classics. The terrace on the garden side is perfectly suited to your business meals (up to 48 people), while the larger courtyard terrace can host up to 80 people for a cocktail party. The restaurant is available for full private hire for up to 230 people.Get a proposalRural By Marc Veyrat: Mountain Chalet Rural by Marc Veyrat, at the Palais des Congrès de Paris, is a bright and convivial eatery that takes its inspiration from a traditional mountain inn. With wooden chairs, tables and beams, and sheepskin fabrics, it has the look of an authentic mountain chalet – ideal for meals for convivial group get-togethers (300 people seated and 440 for a cocktail party) meals. On the menu: local cooking with casserole dishes, an irresistible selection of cheeses, and a choice of traditional desserts from the buffet.Get a proposalReminisens: travel back to royal times How about a business meal that takes you back to the time of French royalty, from Louis XIV to Louis XVI?  This is the concept of Reminisens, a restaurant theatrein the heart of Versailles. Menus are inspired by gourmet recipes from the 17th and 18th centuries and the waiters and waitresses are dressed in the costume of the fashion of the French Court of the time. And for an extra touch, you can become a spectator by attending a play while enjoying a meal in a private room!Get a proposalLes Bains du Marais: sheer relaxationIf you wish to offer your guests a haven of peace then head to the Marais to the elegant and spacious Bains des Marais, where the emphasis is on relaxation. The spa has a hammam, a sauna, a hairdressing salon, a treatment room...  as well as a restaurant, the Café des Bains (70 covers). Get your staff together for a dinner in the chic and modern restaurant with its lovely glass roof. Menu features Italian cooking and Middle Eastern flavours. Get a proposalLe Buddha Bar: exotic world tour  The Buddha bar is a  bar restaurant, with atypical decoration including a statue of Buddha overlooking the main room, is spread over 2 levels. The mezzanine invites you to enjoy an exotic cocktail and the downstairs room is ideal for your business meals. The menu revolves around world food, with Californian, Hawaiian, Asian and Indian influences. Playing a lively eclectic music selection that gets more potent as the night goes on, this is a great place to experience Parisian nightlife. To round off your evening, go to the Buddha Bar’s intimate new bar, the Secret 8 – available for private hire for up to 25 people!Get a proposalManko: burlesque cabaret Step through the doors of Manko, a hybrid nightspot on the prestigious Avenue Montaigne. Combining fine dining and theatre, this unique 400 m² venue offers new a festive experience in the heart of Paris! Globally renowned chef Gaston Acurio serves up dishes with Peruvian flavours. The entertainment line-up includes astonishing and burlesque numbers, interspersed with DJ sets. This new hip night spot is available to hire according to your needs: either partially, the restaurant or the 200 m² mezzanine, which offers a bird's eye view of the cabaret, or in its entirety to surprise your guests with a sumptuous setting.Get a proposalLe panoramique: dinner at the racesFor a memorable dinner and spectacle, the Vincennes Hippodrome de Paris is the place to go. The restaurant, Le Panoramique affords a privileged view of the racetrack. And for a special business dinner, a private box is available to you. If you wish, you can also take a peep behind the scenes on a guided visit! It is worth noting that the Vincennes racecourse in Paris has numerous modular spaces for your professional events such as conventions, workshops and cocktail receptions.Get a proposalNoto Salle Pleyel: art-deco settingSituated above the Salle Pleyel, the recently-renovated famous concert hall ‘Noto Salle Pleyel’honours Italy and the seasons through delicious food – an invitation to travel to the Mediterranean! The interior decor was inspired by images of Visconti’s epic movie The Leopard, but still retains an art deco spirit with crystal chandeliers, thick carpeting and red velvet banquettes. The place can be privately hired from Sunday to Wednesday for up to 120 people for a cocktail reception and for 80 people for a sit-down dinner.Get a proposal
