                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Pleasantly located in the La Défense Faubourg de l'Arche business district, on the edge of the Millennium Park, the Apparthôtel Pythagore Grande Arche offers "ready to live in" studios with a kitchenette, a private bathroom, flat screen, Wifi. The aparthotel Pythagore Grande Arche welcomes both professionals on the move and tourists visiting Paris. 
For a carefree stay right near Paris. 
It is ideally located: 
- close to the business district of La Défense, the CNIT and the Esplanade de la Défense 
- 10 minutes from the new U Arena 
- RER station in the center of Paris and Champs Elysees 4 
- close to all transports (RER A, metro line 1, bus n ° 43 and Tram T2)
