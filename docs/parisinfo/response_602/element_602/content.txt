                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Close to the Charles de Gaulle Airport, in a space specially designed to respect the environment, Les Fontaines offers an exceptional venue for your events which combines the history and splendour of a 19th century Rothschild Palace with modern equipment and campus technology. As well as its unique infrastructures, the venue provides a high quality support service.
