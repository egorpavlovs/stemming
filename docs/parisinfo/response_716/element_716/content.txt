                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Hôtel Renaissance Paris Hippodrome de St-Cloud also has an events space which includes 540 m² of naturally-lit meeting rooms, equipped with the latest technology. There are 2 large rooms measuring 220 m² and 110 m² and 4 break-out rooms. Their sun-filled terraces can welcome seminars, exhibitions and cocktail events and the 1,500-m² garden offers a dream setting for any type of event.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        -Physical disability: car park with adapted parking spaces, ground-level entrance with access ramp, adapted reception desk, adapted communal toilets with transfer bar and adapted sink, 1.40-m wide passages in the restaurant. 
-Visual impairment: glass doors and windows are made obvious with contrasting elements, no obstacles at eye level, tactile and visual contrasts in staircases 50cm before the first step, continuous hand rails on stairs, adapted controls in the lifts and each floor has an audio announcement, signs in large writing at a reasonable height. 
-Audio impairment: reservation and/or payment available online, hearing loop available at reception desk, bright flashing and alarm in case of a fire (where the hard-of-hearing guest may be alone).
