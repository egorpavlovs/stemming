                            Tasting and gastronomy - teambuilding based on gastronomy
                            If you’d like to organize something truly unique, why not invite your guests to a food or wine-related team-building event? Paris hosts many renowned workshops focusing on a wide variety of themes that your teams will enjoy getting their teeth into (bistronomy cooking, quick and easy lunch recipes, pastry making, cocktail classes and so on).
                        

                        Sign up for a cookery workshop in Paris and select the concept best suited to your tastes. Pastry making classes, learning to make international dishes and cooking challenges in teams to build closer ties with co-workers are just some of the options available. See our list of cookery schools in Paris and make your choice on the basis of your needs and expectations.Wine and champagne lovers can head to big-name French wine cellars and retailers to learn about wines from around the world and enjoy tasting sessions featuring selections of classic wines and great vintages: a treat for the taste buds!Here is a list of our cookery school partners, who provide an array of tailormade services. They include a wide range of top-flight chefs and wine specialists who can provide a fascinating introduction to the world of food and wine in a friendly and relaxed atmosphere.
