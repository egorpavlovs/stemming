                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The hotel, situated between the three terminals of Roissy Charles de Gaulle airport, at the foot of the RER station and 5 minutes from the Roissy Charles de Gaulle 2 TGV station, offers 385 spacious and well soundproofed guest rooms. The 24 meeting rooms can accommodate up to 1,200 people and were completely renovated in 2009. Benefiting from the natural daylight, these rooms offer an ideal setting for your seminars, banquets or business meetings.
