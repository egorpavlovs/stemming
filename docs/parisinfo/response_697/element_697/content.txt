                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Create an event in a legendary place. Full of elegance and nestled in the heart of the 16th arrondissement, Studio Harcourt is opening up its building for exceptional events. From the photographic plates in the Café Harcourt, passing through the Curiotheque, the Exhibition Room, and the Showroom, Studio Harcourt is unveiling its hidden treasures, and its precious heritage that classes it as an 'Entreprise du Patrimoine Vivant' (Business of Living History). 
From the intimacy of a small committee meeting to the splendour of a large reception, from a conference to an evening of dancing, from a breakfast in Café Harcourt to a dinner in the heart of the exhibition space, from the darkness of a photo tray to the light of our garden terraces ... Studio Harcourt is ideal for any event of 10 to 250 guests. A made-to-measure photo showing enables your guests to live a unique experience, and to create an eternal memory of your event.
Push through the doors of Studio Harcourt, beyond is the promise of an unforgettable moment for your guests!
