                            Guided visits
                            Paris visits, with tour guides or specialized professional guides.
                        

                        Specialized professional guidesSpecialized professional guides are qualified professionals who introduce Paris and its region to French and foreign visitors: museums, monuments, exhibitions and cultural sites, local walks, etc.They have completed a professional training program, which gives them the knowledge and background to make every visit special. For guided tours in museums and national monuments, travel operators are required by law to use the services of State licensed tour guides.For a complete list of specialized professional guides members of the Paris Convention and Visitors Bureau, click here.Guided toursMany guided tours are available to discover Paris, with classic or more original itineraries and themes:Paris International VIP Services offers private visits with specialized professional guides in Paris and across France, with or without driver, for FITs and for groups.Available languages: French, English, Chinese, Polish, German, Spanish, Italian, Japanese, Arabic, Hindi, Russian, Swedish, Dutch, Finnish, Korean, Portuguese, Turkish and more.Classic Paris tours, excursions to Versailles, Malmaison, Giverny, Auvers sur Oise, Fontainebleau & Barbizon, Chartres, Champagne ...Excursions in France: D-Day landing beaches, Loire castles, Mont Saint-Michel, Provence, Riviera ...Theme visits: architecture, literature, wine, photography, Da Vinci Code ...Elytour offers a wide range of exclusive and bespoke visits for FITs  and for groups.Available languages: French, English, German, Spanish, Italian, Russian and more.Local visits: Marais, Saint-Germain des Prés, Montmartre, la Villette, la Défense ...Theme visits: fashion, gastronomy, covered passages ...Excursions: Versailles, Champagne, wine route of Burgundy, Loire castles, D-Day landing beaches, Bordeaux...RobertPINK agency offers personalized walks across Paris.Available languages: French, English, Spanish, German and more.Walks and tastings: art and pastry, art and chocolate, art and wine ...Art walks: street art, Montmartre ...Promenade des Sens offers guided visits for FITs and groups up to 300 persons.Available languages: French, English, German, Arabic, Chinese, Spanish, Greek, Italian, Dutch, Polish, Portuguese, Russian and more.Classic or unusual guided visits, gourmet tours, shopping tours, treasure hunts ...For further information on theme visits or tours with a specialized professional guide, contact us.
