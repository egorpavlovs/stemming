                             Top 10 addresses for brunch in Paris
                            Relaxing and sociable occasions, brunches are one of the things that makes a stay in Paris memorable! Searching for a place to brunch with your customers, colleagues, or friends?  Check out our selection of addresses that include everything from luxury hotel, to new restaurant, mythical brewery and more!
                        

                        Les Bains du MaraisSeeking complete relaxation? Head to the Bains du Marais, in the Marais district, an address for total well-being. In addition to its hammam, sauna, and hair salon, the establishment houses the Café des Bains, a 70-cover restaurant. Get your staff together for a yummy brunch! All you have to do is choose your drink, your savoury main course and your dessert.  The interiors have a Mediterranean feel with suspended lights of palm leaves/fronds and plants on the walls.Get a proposalSpoonHoused in the Palais Brongniart, Spoon(available for private hire from 50 people) invites you to take a gastronomic world tour with its original and generous brunch. Help yourself to your starter and dessert from the counter, and choose your main dish from a menu inspired by the travels of the starred chef Alain Ducasse. The atmosphere is warm and friendly, with a large U-shaped zinc bar, a glass kitchen, and soundtrack by Armand Amar. A perfect setting for your business meals (110 people) or cocktail parties (130 people)!Get a proposalBateaux ParisiensBistro Parisien is the name of the quayside restaurant of the Bateaux Parisiens company. Enjoy a wonderful all-you-can-eat buffet brunch (a selection of drinks, starters, hot dishes and gourmet desserts). There is also a package with champagne! The icing on the cake: a stunning view of the Eiffel Tower ... Memorable moments by the Seine. In summer, the terrace is an idyllic setting.Get a proposalChalet du Lac Designed as a beautiful country house, at the edge of the Bois de Vincennes, the Chalet du Lac welcomes your cocktail parties up to 2,000 people. From Friday to Sunday, the venue turns into a veritable Trattoria and hosts your brunches in front of the lake. The buffet menu makes for a relaxed atmosphere. On the menu: mini-viennoiseries, cold salmon, carpaccio of roast beef, an assortment of cheeses and charcuterie,  and chicken marinated with citrus fruit. For dessert: panna cotta, iles flottantes and verrines of tiramisu will delight those with a sweet tooth!Get a proposalRitz Paris The Ritz Paris, the mythical Parisian palace on Place Vendôme, opens its doors for you to discover its Grand Brunch. This takes place in the Summer Lounge, which opens onto the big garden. From one buffet table to another the accent is on Mediterranean flavours: direction Italy and its antipasti, the Lebanon with its mezze, and Spain with its Iberian charcuterie. There is meat and fish, and delicious bite-sized Japanese food for a taste of Asia too. Cakes and desserts, signed François Perret, include iles flottantes, cakes, tarts, pear Belle Hélène, madeleines … Simply irresistible!Get proposalLe MeuriceThe Parisian palace hotel Le Meurice is a perfect example of French art de vivre and splendid 18th century architecture. At its heart, the Dalí restaurant, designed by Philippe Starck, puts on a real feast every Sunday. Starting with a wonderful (sweet) buffet selection of French pastries, fruit salads and yogurt. Followed by a savoury buffet of smoked Iberian ham, gravlax, Caesar salad, tabbouleh quinoa, scrambled eggs, etc. The savoury selection finishes with a selection of cheeses served by the plate. The dessert buffet by famous pastry chef Cédric Grolet adds the perfect finishing touch to your meal. Note that Le Meurice has 5 reception rooms available for private hire for your events.Get a proposalLes Éditeurs Les Éditeurs (200 covers), situated in the Saint-Germain-des-Prés district, exudes the spirit of a literary cafe. Seated comfortably in a large red leather chair, enjoy a brunch served at your table and a book from the bookcases with over 5,000 books. Sweet foods: basket of bread, French viennoiseries and brioches. Savoury foods: scrambled eggs with salmon and Caesar salad. And to finish, a fruit salad and an irresistible cheesecake made with speculoos. The restaurant offers tailor-made packages for your private and professional events (breakfast, cocktail party, press conference, etc.).Get a proposalHôtel du NordIn a trendy area of Paris, close to the République and the Gare de l’Est, the Hôtel du Nord (140 seats), made famous by the eponymous film by Marcel Carne, will give you an appetite with its gourmet brunch. Impossible to resist the poached egg with hollandaise sauce and bacon or salmon served on Parisian brioche, and accompanied by potatoes and crispy lettuce with nuts. The Hôtel du Nord is available for partial or full hire. All configurations are possible! Get a proposalDoobie’s A few steps from the prestigious Avenue des Champs Elysées,Doobie's, a chic restaurant bar and club with intimate decor, offers cuisine with Thai and Italian influences. The tuna marinated with sesame and the penne with arrabiata sauce are a must. Every Sunday, brunchers can enjoy viennoiseries, scrambled eggs, pancakes, crepes, chocolate cake, and fresh fruit salad. The space can be adapted to the occasion and accommodates your cocktail parties (250 people) and sit-down dinners (140 people).Get a proposalYooma Urban Lodge      The Yooma Urban Lodge, a surprising urban hotel, is located at 51 Quai de Grenelle, between the Eiffel Tower and the Beaugrenelle shopping centre. Recognizable by its blue facade, and black and white stripes signed Daniel Buren; the hotel is a lively place that meets the accommodation requirements of small groups with its adapted rooms. The restaurant (130 covers) serves traditional French cuisine, including the Yooma Brunch. The latter consists of a gourmet all-you-can-eat buffet with sweet and savoury suggestions by the chef!Get a proposal
