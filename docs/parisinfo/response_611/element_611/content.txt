                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Novotel Paris Sud Porte de Charenton is situated at 200 metres from Paris, next to the metro station Liberté (ligne 8), going directly to Bastille and Opera. 
The renovated conference centre has a completely equipped amphitheatre for up to 230 people (ProAct Label), with a dedicated AV director. Enjoy the 11 modular meeting rooms with natural daylight from 10 to 220 people. Savour the coffee breaks, appetisers, cocktails in our two cosy and bright forums, or even in our outside terrace on warm days. Ideal for all kind of meetings. Paying public parking.
