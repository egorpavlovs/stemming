                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In the heart of the Champs-Élysées district, a typically Parisian restaurant for a lunch or dinner at any time. Famous for its meat, our star dish is a sirloin steak served on a heated plate, accompanied by its famous sauce, whose recipe is still a well-kept secret. It is served with a copious helping of French fries. You can also choose other dishes, salads and burgers from our menu. To finish off your meal, we offer home-made desserts and a ‘café gourmand’ (a coffee served with a selection of mini desserts). We can create 'Group' menus to suit you, at preferential rates. Contact us by email to request information; we will reply to you within 24 hours.
