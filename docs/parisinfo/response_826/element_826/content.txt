                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Espace Grande Arche's location at the centre of Europe's largest business district means excellent connectivity to Paris and other European capitals. Its 5,005 sq. m provide the perfect venue for meetings of all times. It consists of three remarkable, multi-function spaces as well as an elegant, contemporary reception area.
