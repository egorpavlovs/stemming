                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Renaissance Paris Le Parc Trocadero Hotel is ideally located just between the Eiffel Tower and the Arc de Triomphe, in the city center of Paris. The Hotel is composed of four buildings surrounding an enchanting garden of 13,000 feet¬≤, a haven of peace in Paris.
Let the Renaissance Paris Le Parc Trocadero Hotel set a memorable stage for your next adventure. Our 5-star boutique hotel here in the heart of Paris offers stylish, well-designed accommodations. 
Most guest rooms overlook our scenic private garden, and some hotel suites offer striking views of the Eiffel Tower. Each of our 122 hotel rooms offers luxury amenities, including deluxe bedding, quality in-room entertainment, and elegant marble bathrooms. 
Indulge yourself with an exquisite French meal from Le Relais du Parc, our hotel's signature restaurant, or enjoy complimentary access to our fitness center. If you are interested in hosting a seminar in Paris, with its 5 meeting rooms, our hotel offers sophisticated, adaptable venue space and first-rate planning and catering services. 
Our boutique hotel's perfect placement in central Paris offers convenient access to notable attractions, including the Eiffel Tower and the 16th arrondissement.
