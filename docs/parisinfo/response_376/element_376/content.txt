                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Gustave Eiffel Reception Room, on the 1st floor of the Eiffel Tower, has a breathtaking view of Paris. With a full range of technical equipment (sound, light and video), it can host any kind of event. A catering service is possible in the room or at the 58 Tour Eiffel restaurant next to it.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        Priority queue-jump access is granted to disabled visitors and an accompanying person.
- Physical disability: access to the 1st and 2nd floors by lift. For safety reasons, the top of the Eiffel Tower is not accessible to those in wheelchairs.
- Hearing disability: the tills are fitted with hearing loops.
