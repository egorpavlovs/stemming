                            The Chalet du Lac: an outstanding events venue in the East of Paris!
                            The Chalet du Lac, at the edge of the Bois de Vincennes, was a former residence of Napoleon III and designed as a grand country house.
                        

                        With a surface area of 1,500 m² indoors and 1,500 m² outdoors, the Chalet du Lac offers numerous possibilities for events, from business meals (up to 900 people) to cocktail parties (up to 2,000). For evening functions with dancing, it boasts a huge dance floor (600 m²) overhung with a signature Philippe Starck disco ball. And for an outdoor event, see the 3 lakeside terraces.  From Friday to Sunday, the venue takes on the appearance of a trattoria (500 people) and receives your guests for lunch on the terrace, brunch looking onto the lake, as well as private dinners.A delightful bucolic getaway opposite the Lake of Saint Mandé!
