The prestige of the venue combined with the possibility of a private tour of the collections or the temporary exhibitions contribute to the success of the private events held here. Another advantage of this monument, opened in 1961, is that it is located on the edge of the Seine, between the Champs-Elys√©es and the Eiffel Tower.
