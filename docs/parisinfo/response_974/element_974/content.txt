                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Groups, seminars, events, students, companies, incentives,  trade shows, corporate conferences, etc.
Large groups are possible with 150 bicycles in Paris, 90 in Versailles, 50 in Giverny & 75 Segways.
Our tours :
- Bicycle, walking or by Segway.
- Visits from 2 to 8 hours: Paris, Versailles and Giverny.
- 20 different visits: morning, afternoon and evening.
- Monuments, museums and city discovery tours.
- Skip-the-line access to the Eiffel Tower, Versailles, Louvre, Orsay & the Catacombs.
- All visits are guided and in English (other languages possible on request).
- Safe, fun & physically undemanding. 
- Been running for 15 years!
Our tours can be adapted to your request:
- Longer or shorter,
- Add stops or a specific visit,
- Add in a break (coffee/lunch/picnic/champagne!),
- Start a tour from a different point to our standard start…
Our special group tours:
- Exclusive visits (without the crowds)
- Versailles Palace just for your group.
- The Towers of Notre Dame.
- Breakfast at the Arc de Triomphe.
- Master Chef on foot: rue Montorgueil.
- Chic chocolate in the Marais: Paris still has a few hidden gems. Discover the chocolate making workshops of one of Paris's most popular chocolate makers in Paris, by painting your picture in chocolate with an American artist!
