                                                    Description
                            
                                
        With its full view from its garden-terrasse on the Arc de Triomphe, the Arc Paris will receive your most beautiful events, corporate or private, up to 600 people.
Thanks to its unique viewpoint, its state-of-the-art technic and its modularity, the Arc Paris will allow you to realise all your events : product launch, conferences, dinner cocktail…
For an event, the Arc Paris can be combined with the others venues of the same building : the Pavillon Etoile-Presbourg, or even the Victoria Paris.
Privatisation from Sunday to Wednesday included.
