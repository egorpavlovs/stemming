                            Play in a team on the temporary courts of Paris Ma Belle!
                            With the arrival of summer weather, the agency Paris Ma Belle unveils a new offering of temporary pétanque courts. Find out more about this typically-French game during a fun team-building event!
                        

                        Paris Ma Belle can bring its two boulodromes, known as Caesar and Marius,to your workplace or your event venue. These two bowling courts can be adapted to your space for a playful and original incentive event. The only prerequisites are: level ground (parquet, paving, slabs, lawn, etc.) and the capacity to accommodate a 2 m x 8 m court on your rooftop, in your hotel, in your offices, etc. The essential ingredients for an exciting match are also provided: rental of pétanque balls, team badges, medals, tournament table ...As well as providing all the equipment, the agency guarantees the success of your event with tailor-made support. It helps you to choose your ideal type of competition (a tournament, non-competitive games, introduction to bowling), the format (number of players, number of teams and duration), and it hosts the match.Also worth noting, Paris Ma Belle organizes urban orienteering in the capital to explore it via stories, legends, and clue solving. Pétanque tournaments too can be planned in landmark places in Paris on existing pétanque-playing areas like at Montmartre, in the Bois de Boulogne, or in the Bois de Vincennes. So, on your marks, get set, point or shoot!
