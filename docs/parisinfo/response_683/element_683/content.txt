               
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Housed in a seventeenth century mansion in the Marais, Dessance is a unique fine-dining restaurant in the world: a bold architectural cocoon that received the Paris Design Award in 2015-2014 and a brand new vision of seasonal vegetables, fruits and plants, served with or without fish and meat, giving birth to new culinary emotions. The sculptural plates are prepared under your eyes and served with exceptional alcoholic or homemade drink pairings. The restaurant Dessance can be privatized and host your business meals, up to 44 people of which 30 on the ground floor and 14 in the mezzanine upstairs which operates as a private lounge. 
It is ideally located: 
- in the historic center of Paris, near the Hotel de Ville, République and the Pompidou Center - near Arts et Métiers subway (M3), République (M8), Hôtel de Ville (M1) 
- at 10 minutes walking distance from Châtelet-les-Halles RER
- near n° 75 and n°20 bus station.
More photographies on www.dessance.com.
970 words spaces included.
