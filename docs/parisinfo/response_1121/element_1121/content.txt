                            A new citizenM hotel livens up the Paris Gare de Lyon
                            citizenM, the very ‘connected’ hotel chain, which aims to change the codes of urban travel, has just opened its third establishment in Paris, not far from the bustling Gare de Lyon.
                        

                        After opening a hotel in June in La Défense and near Roissy Charles de Gaulle airport, the hotel chain has cleverly applied the same striking features of its innovative concept to its brand new hotel: a casual and arty atmosphere, affordable luxury amenities, welcoming, well-being, and an uncommon combination of business and pleasure.For business clients, citizenM offers the cloudM concept on the 15th floor. This chic and intimate Parisian apartment has a meeting room and a sky bar with stunning panoramic views of Paris, available for private hire (100 people) until 6pm. If you prefer to keep your feet on the ground, the societyM ground floor space offers 3 meeting rooms. In total, 4 meeting rooms for up to 10 people each, with boards, office supplies, coffee and free Wi-Fi. You can also take advantage of the self-check-in service.As for its 338 guest rooms, the hotel, mixes technology with comfort. Rooms have a king- size bed, Wi-Fi, free movies, a digital ‘Mood Pad’ tablet so that guests can customize and create the atmosphere of their choice (lighting, curtains, temperature, TV), as well as a large bay window to enjoy the view.Finally, the lobby area is a place to socialize, along with a patio, a bar and an open kitchen.
