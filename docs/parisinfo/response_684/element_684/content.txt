                                                    Description
                            
                                
        For more than a century, the Angelina tearoom has been regarded as a top gourmet establishment in Paris. Lovers of pastries and desserts come from all over the world to enjoy the atmosphere at this Parisian tearoom. Serving breakfast, brunch and lunch, Angelina has the perfect savoury or sweet product for any time of the day. Open Monday to Friday from 7.30am to 7pm and on Saturday, Sunday and public holidays from 8.30am to 7pm.
