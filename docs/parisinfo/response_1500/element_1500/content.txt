                                                    Description
                            
                                
        La Grande Crypte is an atypical and modular venue of 1200 m2 of 800 people maximum capacity. It also features a 250 seat-auditorium as well as the 80m2 Point d’Ho Room, which can accommodate a press or VIP meeting. La Grande Crypte offers an extra service of high speed wifi connectivity (dedicated fiber) according to the demanded internet speed per connexion. Supported by referenced caterers, a dedicated technical partner and a digital agency for your online events we can offer you a broad range of services.
