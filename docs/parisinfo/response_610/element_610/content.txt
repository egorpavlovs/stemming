                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Offering premium services in a trendy atmosphere, the hotel boasts modern guest rooms, a 1,350 m² naturally lit convention centre, a sauna, a gym, an indoor pool, a massage service and Le Spirit restaurant and bar. In 2012 the hotel opened a 232 m² auditorium with additional adaptable naturally lit meeting rooms, a function room, a billiards room, 20 bowling lanes, a restaurant with 170 seats, a lively bar, an additional gym.
