                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physically disabled: car park with reserved adapted space(s), street-level entry or via access ramp, adapted reception desk, adapted communal toilets, 8 adapted guest rooms, accessible dining room.
- Visually impaired: transparent glass doors and walls are indicated with contrasting elements, no eye-level obstacles along pathways, lifts with adapted control panel.
- Hearing impaired: booking and/or payment can be made online, reception equipped with induction hearing loop.
- Mentally disabled:easy to understand signage, coupled with pictograms, monitored entrance, visible from reception.
