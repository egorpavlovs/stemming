                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Ideally situated right in the centre of Paris, only 300 m from the foot of the Arc de Triomphe, 10 minutes from La Défense by public transport, and only a 10 minute walk from the renowned Avenue des Champs Elysées. The 5* Renaissance Paris Arc de Triomphe is a stylish establishment. The hotel has an outward-facing view and an impressive curved glass facade designed by famous French architect Christian de Portzamparc. Inside, 118 spacious and comfortable bedrooms and suites all cover at least 30 m².
