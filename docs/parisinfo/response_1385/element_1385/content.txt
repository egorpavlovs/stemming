                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Located in the heart of the authentic district of Bastille, few minutes from the Gare de Lyon and Paris Bercy, this 5 * hotel has taken place in the place full of history that is "Maison Boutet". Importer of exotic wood then chocolate, the building has kept its soul and charm of yesteryear. The 80 rooms and suites, 8 of which have terraces overlooking the rooftops of Paris, are unusual and individual and give the hotel its unique character. All rooms are equipped with a tea / Nespresso machine, a Bluetooth docking station and a Wi-Fi connection. The SPA takes care of you with 2 treatment cabins, swimming pool, sauna, steam room and gym open until 10pm. The bar offers light meals and chocolate breaks to enjoy in a warm atmosphere. The Boutet room is perfect for afterworks and cocktails, they will benefit from the two terraces and French food products. The hotel has car park to accommodate your guests.
