                            What's on?
                            
        The Vedettes de Paris company continues to modernize and launches the construction of two new  pontoons for the public, one for a sightseeing cruise and dining, the other for private use.
    
                        
                    
                    
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Located at the foot of the Eiffel Tower, the Vedettes de Paris is a unique place to oragnise an event. Whether it's for a conference, seminar, study day, dinner or cocktail, you can welcome your guests on the banks of Seine with view of Trocadéro and the Eiffel Tower.
Far from traffic noise, we offer you three spaces:
- The "Salon des Vedettes", a 200m² entirely-glazed, floating room including a 50m² mezzanine offering a view of the Seine and the Eiffel Tower. In a pure and modern style with a luminous bar and lounge in the mezzzanine, it's ideal for use for a seminar with up to 100 people, 120 people for a seated dinner, and 250 for a cocktail party.
More than 60 parking spaces are available for our clients, facing the "Salon des Vedettes", subject to availability.
- Our twin boats Paris Iéna and Paris Trocadéro are fully equipped including an upper deck, a panoramic lounge and bar can accommodate up to 20 people for a seminar, 40 for dinner and 70 for cocktails.
- The Paris Etoile combines tradition and modernity, comfort and performance, modularity and functionality, and has the latest technological advances.
It has a flexible bar that can change according to the type of service offered, allowing you to effectively manage the quality and service for different areas of the ship: main lounge, panoramic lounge and front upper sun deck. Once configurated, it can accommodate up to 80 people for a seminar, 60 for a seated dinner and 110 for cocktails. These large windows offer stunning views of the outside and the boat configuration is ideally suited for a lunch and dinner cruise in an intimate setting.
We offer these three "naked" spaces without a corkage fee, or a bundled offer with catering, decoration, sound, and animation if needed.
