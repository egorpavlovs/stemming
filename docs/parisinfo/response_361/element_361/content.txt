                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Cucina, the latest venture of Ducasse Restaurant and GL events, is ideally located at the heart of the Maison de la Mutualité, known as the city’s Left bank venue where many professional events and meetings are organized. The restaurant can be privatized and will welcome your business meals in a modern setting designed by architect Jean-Michel Wilmotte. A la carte, true Italian cuisine that includes some pizzette and a selection of salumeria while waiting, then comes the antipasti, primi piatti, linguine alle vongole, capellini all’aragosta, Di Pesce, Di carne, and desserts obviously: Tiramisu, Zuppa Inglese, gelato al cioccolato from our Manufacture… Wines are essentially from Italy.
