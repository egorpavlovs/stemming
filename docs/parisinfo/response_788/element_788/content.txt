                            Barges and boats for a wedding in Paris
                            If you’re a romantic at heart, and you’ve always dreamt of a wedding on the water, Paris is the place for you! In the city where anything is possible, you can make your dreams come true by hiring a boat to host your wedding reception. 
                        

                        Take a leisurely cruise along the Seine and see the magnificent historical monuments of the City of Lights through new eyes. It is an enchanting itinerary for this very special occasion, and you will store up precious and unique memories of your wedding day.A wedding on a boat in Paris is an event you and your guests will always remember. You can call on wedding specialists who will indulge all your desires to ensure you have a perfect day. A range of suppliers will be on hand to help you organize your dream wedding and take care of all the practical details so you’re free to enjoy this magical occasion. For your wedding reception, you can choose to simply hire the boat, or opt for a full range of services including hostesses, security staff, catering, DJs and a cloakroom. Whichever option you choose, the staff at the venue will go all out to make your wedding reception a truly unforgettable event for you and your guests.

            Fancy restaurants for a wedding reception in Paris
        
        Paris has any number of original and prestigious venues to host your reception and make it a unique and unforgettable event, whatever your choice of wedding menu: a gastronomic meal, a picnic-style reception, or a medley of flavours from around the world. 
    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            Luxury hotels for a wedding reception in Paris 
        
        It’s always hard to locate the perfect place to hold your wedding reception. The venue you choose will make all the difference when it comes to ensuring it’s an unforgettable occasion. Play it safe and pick the right spot with our selection of wedding venues in Paris hotels. 
    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            Unusual wedding reception venues in Paris 
        
        Paris is a city where anything is possible. If you want to make your wedding extra special and really unique, don’t be afraid to step off the beaten track and book an unusual venue to host your wedding reception in Paris. Your guests will be talking about it for years to come!
