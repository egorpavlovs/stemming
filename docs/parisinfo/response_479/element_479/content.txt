                            Story Bulles: create your own comic strip in a team!
                            Working together in a team, imagine and create your own comic strip with a very realistic look on an IPad! A comic-strip team-building event that is creative, original and 100% digital.
                        

                        Looking for an original teambuilding event for a seminar or a special evening function? Check out ‘Story Bulles’ by For Event, an entertainment agency. After choosing a specific topic, each team (of 5 to 10 people) imagine their own scenario and the various dialogues.Action! Participants now become the actors in their story. Using the IPad app given to you and accompanied by a member of staff from the For Event agency, take photos using the costumes and accessories of your choice.The application transforms them into a real comic strip. Give free rein to your imagination, choose your page layout and add speech bubbles and dialogues, captions and onomatopoeia. The comic strips can be exported immediately and will be given to you in PDF format. It is also possible to print them in order to keep an unforgettable memory of your stay. This teambuilding event lasts for a maximum of 3 hours and can be organized for 6 to 600 people.
