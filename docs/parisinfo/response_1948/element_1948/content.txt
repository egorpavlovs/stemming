                JIB 2019 - Days of Innovation in Medical Biology

                                                                                        to 22 November 2019
                                                


                                            
                    52 route de la Demi-Lune - 92000 Puteaux - La Défense
                
                        
        
                
                                                    
                    
                                                    La Défense
                                                                    
                
                    
        
        



            

                

                
                    
                                                      

           
           
           
               
                

           
                                                       

                    
                                                                                    Description
                        
                            The medical biology is a sector of health which makes its revolution. These Days of Innovation emphasize dissemination of new techniques and new therapies (molecular biology …), arrival of artificial intelligence after the advent of the automating…These Days in medical biology, beyond the annual meeting around conferences and exhibition, will live throughout the year by positioning itself as a dematerialized incubator where innovations and transformations are revealed, an experiment and confront each other.This event takes place at l'Espace Grand Arche, Paris-La Défense - VIPARIS
                        
                    
                                        
                                                                            
                                                    Website
                            
                                https://jib-innovation.com/
                            
                                                                        
                                        

                                        
                                            



    
        Place 
    
    
        
            
                Espace Grande Arche - VIPARIS
                
                    
        At the heart of Europe's number one business district, Espace Grande Arche offers an intimate, contemporary setting. Its unbeatable central location is well served by public transport.
