                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Located on the Seine, this residence is just a few minutes from Paris by tramway or RER. Built in the new Business district west of Paris, this luxury residence offers many services: reception open 24/7, mini-market, bar, gym. For just a night or a longer stay, everything has been taken care of to make your apartment a great living space.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        Motor disability: adapted and reserved parking spaces for wheelchair users, ground floor entrance or entrance with sloping surface, low reception desk adapted for wheelchair users, adapted apartments.
Visual disability: stairs with touch-sensitive and visual contrast before the first descending step, with continuous handrails, lift with announcement of floor.
Hearing disability: booking and/or payments available to do online, reception desk equipped with audio induction loop, lift with visual announcement and/or audio induction loop, flashing lights when fire alarm sounds in the adapted apartments.
Mental disability: attentive staff, signaling system using pictograms, supervised entry and visible from the reception desk.
