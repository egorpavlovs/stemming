                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Ideally situated in front of the Tuileries Gardens, Le Meurice has been one of the most elegant hotels in the world for nearly two centuries. Combining an exceptional decor, style and quality with a contemporary refinement, with new and spectacular interior decorations, designed by Philippe Starck, Le Meurice is the image of a French Palace and is one of the most beautiful locations in Paris.
