                                                    Description
                            
                                
        At the edge of Bois de Boulogne, Pavillon d’Armenonville is an exceptional, prestigious venue for company and private events.
This former royal hunting lodge takes your events to another level by offering eight air-conditioned, modular reception rooms, bathed in natural light, as well as magnificent terraces with views of the landscaped gardens and lake. 
Pavillon d’Armenonville and its 1,800m² of reception space enable you to arrange a select or large-scale reception for up to 2,000 people in an enchanting venue, where Belle Epoque decoration mingles with contemporary inspiration.
