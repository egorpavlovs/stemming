                            The merger of Châteauform’ and Eurosites 
                            Châteauform’ and Eurosites unite to become a key European player on the segment for training, seminars, and special events.
                        

                        Châteauform', created in 1996, is a leader in the organization of seminars in country settings, in the city, in small or large groups. It offers businesses a wide range of user-friendly venues in natural settings with personalized support, for a successful team-building.Eurosites, created in 1992, has a catalogue of 50 venues, 500 rooms, in 41 towns and cities in France, notably Paris, as well as the catering brand Nomad. It offers quality packages and services and functional and exceptional venues for beautiful receptions.The merger of the two companies is the result of a common motivation to enrich and improve the offer to customers in an increasingly competitive environment. This newly-formed Group is now a leader in the MICE sector. With more than 54 venues in Europe – including iconic places like the Salle Wagram or Les Docks de Paris – and a caterer called Nomad, it now offers a complete offer to respond to a wide range of demands for event planning. A portfolio of 10 brands has been put together, among which are: The Home of Seminars, with venues ‘just like home’, Campus, for conferences or seminars for big teams, and Event & Conventions, for tailor-made events.
