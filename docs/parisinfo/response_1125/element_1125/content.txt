                            Enjoy a break at the 5-star Parister hotel in the Faubourg Montmartre neighbourhood
                            The 5-star Parister Hotel opened in September in the 9th arrondissement of Paris, just behind the Folies Bergères, the famous cabaret music hall. An exotic and unusual escapade in a buzzing neighbourhood.
                        

                        The hotel appeals to the mind and body and mixes nature with urbanism to offer a whole new experience to travellers and residents.The 45 bedrooms include suites and duplex suites, all bathed in natural light and with surface areas ranging from 17 to 32 m², and terraces of up to 11 m². The establishment’s 3 duplexes are equipped with a sauna, a steam room, and a private terrace.The ground floor has been designed as a veritable living space, fully open-plan and accessible both to guests and Parisians. Combining brass, velvet, and wood, it is a succession of lounges and bookshelves, a bar/restaurant and a patio with plants. At the bar ‘Les Passerelles’, an expert mixologist serves delicious snacks and original cocktails — an invitation to travel. The restaurant can be privately-hired for a cocktail reception or partially privately-hired for up to 60 people.The well-being area on the lower ground floor is an oasis in the city. The 20-metre-length pool is accessible to hotel guests, but also to visitors for swimming lessons only. The spa offers a range of unique treatments signed Zenology. For professionals, package breaks can be arranged on the theme of sport and food for ten or so people.In 2018, the Parister will be equipped with a meeting room for up to 20 people in class configuration.
