                            The H√¥tel Splendide Royal Paris: a new upscale concept 
                            The H√¥tel Splendide Royal, a new Roberto Naldi Collection hotel, occupies a 19th-century town house, a few steps from the Faubourg St-Honor√©.
                        

                        Designed as a true Parisian residence, with an Italian touch, the Splendide Royal Paris consists of 12 spacious suites, each with its own specific style. Accessed by a sumptuous marble staircase and a padded lift, the suites are subtly perfumed by Florentin Lorenzo Villoresi. Colour tones of beige, almond green and powder blue marry harmoniously with the classic furniture made to measure by Italian craftsmen. The light marble bathrooms boast heated floors and extra-large showers. The personal wine cellars are stocked with delicious Italian and French vintages.¬†Room service is available all round the clock. Plus, guest rooms are equipped with cutting-edge technology including broadband and extra-large television screens.In early 2017, the hotel will open an Italian chic bistro style restaurant (46 seats), on 2 floors. The upstairs dining room (14 seats) is available for¬†private hire for your business meals in total privacy. The starred chef Michelino Gioia promises you exceptional cuisine every day at lunchtime and in the evening.
