                            Parisian venues for a private event
                            Find out our guide of venues available for private hire: wedding, birthday, christening, or any other type of private event in Paris. 
                        

                        Whether you are in search of an ultra-modern and comfortable loft for an ‘at home’ atmosphere, a totally offbeat place, or even a trendy nightclub for an evening with dancing, the capital is packed with rooms available for private hire by private individuals. There is something for all tastes, from the most confidential, to the most spacious, to the most surprising, whether there are 10 or 200 of you! See below our selection of parisian partners and request an estimate to obtain a tailor-made service.
