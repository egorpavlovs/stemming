                                                    Description
                            
                                
        With 6 meeting rooms, the Maison des Métallos welcomes all kinds of business events (up to 400 people for a cocktail party) as well as shows, exhibitions, artistic meetings and public debates throughout the year. One of the City of Paris' many 'Espaces Culturels' (Cultural Spaces) , the Maison des Métallos is situated in a historic industrial district. Popular, lively and close to the city's most fashionable spots, this venue is truly unique.
