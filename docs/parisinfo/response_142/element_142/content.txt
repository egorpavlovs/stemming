                            Guide to hotels with meeting rooms for the organization of your conferences, conventions, seminars and banquets.
                        

                        Guide to hotels with meeting rooms for conferences in ParisSee below the list of conference hotels in Paris and the Paris region for the organization of your conventions, seminars and banquets.Whether it is with a view of the Eiffel Tower, opposite the Paris Palais des Congrès, within the heart of a theme park, close to an airport, with a video conference room, business centre, or with an auditorium … Paris has numerous conference hotels for the organization of your professional event. See the technical fact files of our service providers with a table showing the capacities of their meeting rooms, and request a free estimate by using the button ‘Contact the service provider’. You will receive an estimate tailored to your requirements and budget, for the organization of events capable of hosting up to 4,000 people.
