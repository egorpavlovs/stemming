                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A stone's throw from the National Assembly and Saint Germain des Près, follow in the footsteps of Châteaubriand and Honoré de Balzac and find us on rue Saint Dominique... Two individual hotels welcome you on either side of the Cour d'Honneur. With its 800 m2 of greenery in central Paris, the name of the hotel was obvious: les jardins de Saint Dominique. This third Châteauform’ City allows you to surround yourself with greenery whilst being sheltered from the noise of the city.
