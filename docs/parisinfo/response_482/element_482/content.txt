                                                            Paris La Défense Arena: a brand new experience for the events industry
                                                
                                    



        
            
                
                    
                        
                            Paris La Défense Arena: a brand new experience for the events industry
                            A perfect mix between a modern sports arena and a futuristic auditorium, the Paris La Défense Arena opens on 19 October in the centre of the Paris La Défense business district. 
                        

                        As the biggest modular building in Europe with a capacity for 20,000 to 40,000 spectators, Paris La Défense Arena will host sporting events and concerts with international stars. It is the first auditorium where the stands are in a U-shape configuration to provide a panoramic view for everyone. This lively and accessible place offers visitors and professionals an exciting experience in a grandiose and innovative setting.Equipped with the latest technology like the incredible Emotion Studio (for giant projections in 3D over a 4,500 m² area), Paris La Défense Arena is revolutionizing corporate events. It can accommodate any kind of event, thanks to its fully modular spaces: 95 boxes, for a capacity of 10 to 44 people, with à la carte services (concierge, restaurant ...); 8 rooms (between 93 and 672 m²) can accommodate from 80 to 530 people; a conference room and a multipurpose room; as well as the possibility of holding an event in the pit of the arena (9,000 m²).Note that the building can be hired for a convention (for between 1,500 to 30,000 people) including the use of the arena and stands, the activation of the Emotion Studio and the projection of the event on the screens, or alternatively  the entire Paris La Défense Arena may be hired with all its areas for 40,000 people.It is caterer Butard Enescot that will manage the catering in the hospitality spaces, putting an emphasis on conviviality, quality, fresh products and bespoke services.
