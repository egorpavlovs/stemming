                                                                                    Description
                        
                            For more than twelve years now, Omnivore is devoted to paving the way for the young cuisine by reuniting international chefs around the world. The goal of the OMNIVORE WORLD TOUR is to create events that will help people discover this network of chefs of the Young Cuisine, all over the world in cities like Paris, Moscow, Mexico, Istanbul, Budapest...This year in Paris, the Chef Anne-Sophie Pic is in the place of honour.Do not miss this event at the Maison de la Mutualité, in Paris.
                        
                    
                                        
                                                                            
                                                    Website
                            
                                https://www.omnivore.com/
                            
                                                                        
                                        

                                        
                                            



    
        Place 
    
    
        
            
                Maison de la Mutualité - GL Events
                
                    
        Inaugurated in 1931, La Maison de la Mutualité was built in the Art Deco style right in the heart of the Latin Quarter. Redesigned by Jean Michel Wilmotte for GL Events, it reopened in January 2012 with a congress and exhibition centre for events of between 10 and 2,000 people.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: level or ramped entryway, reception desk with dropped section, adapted bathroom with transfer bar and accessible sink, stairs with alternative means.
- Visual impairment: transparent doors/walls preceded by contrasting signage, no obstacles above head height, stairs with tactile and visual contrasts before first downward step, with continuous handrail, lift with floor announcements and relief and/or Braille buttons, information in large print, signs at a reasonable height. 
- Hearing impairment: lift with clear visual display and/or audible announcements with magnetic hearing loop, flashing light accompanies alarm (where the deaf person may be alone). 
- Intellectual disability: trained staff, easy-to-understand signage with pictograms, monitored entryway visible from cashdesk.

        Sustainable 
    
    
        

                            Sustainable development approach
                Since 2012, the GL Events network of venues in France is certified ISO 14001, system of environmental management. Three large environmental issues are defined: energy, water, and waste. At the next financial year in January 2018, the network of certified sites will pass a surveillance audit, based on the new version of ISO 14001 version 2015.
