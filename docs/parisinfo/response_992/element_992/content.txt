                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Paris Nord Villepinte is France's largest exhibition complex. It is the leading venue for corporate events, with 360,000 sq. m of comfortable, interconnected and fully-equipped spaces, as well as 246,312 sq. m of exhibition areas. It is also an eco-responsible site where comfort and contemporary concerns go hand-in-hand. Paris Nord Villepinte has two amphitheatres, 42 meeting rooms and a conference centre.
