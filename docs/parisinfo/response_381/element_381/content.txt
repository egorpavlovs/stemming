                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A shrine to Parisian partying since 1889, immortalized by Toulouse-Lautrec, the cabaret is world-famous for its French Cancan. The Moulin Rouge presents 'Féerie', a dazzling 2-hour show featuring 60 performers and 1,000 costumes with feathers, rhinestones and sequins, sparkling decor and original music ... You can also enjoy a traditional French gourmet dinner. A legendary and magical place and an unforgettable experience! Correct clothing required. Jacket and tie appreciated. Shorts, bermudas, sports clothes and shoes, flip-flops are not allowed.

                        Services and facilities of the establishment
                        
                                                            Restaurant
                                                    
                    
                
                                                        
                        Restaurant
                                                    In a Belle Epoque style setting, this world-renowned cabaret offers high quality dinners with a show. Large choice of French gastronomics dishes : Dinner & Show formula: Mistinguett menu: from €180 per person - Toulouse-Lautrec menu: from €195 per person - Belle Epoque menu: from €225 per person - Vegetarian or vegan menus (upon request): from €195 per person.
Show only at 9pm and 11pm from €77.
½ bottle of Champagne included in all our formulas.
Dinner and Show at €115 for children from 6 to 11 included.
Children must be aged 6 or above.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        -	Motor disabilities: access through the door next to the main entrance, and lift to the main hall. Due to several steps leading to the room, the Maîtres d'Hôtel can carry the person (with manual wheelchair only), with his agreement. Adapted toilet facilities onsite. Tables are easy to access with clear views. In order to receive you in the best possible way, please indicate that you will be getting around by wheelchair when booking.
