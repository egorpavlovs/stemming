                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Renaissance Paris Vendôme hotel completed a major transformation in May 2018, led by the famous designer Didier Gomez. The contrast between the architectural heritage of the 18th century and the new contemporary urban style of the hotel provides a sense of elegance and femininity in this intimate address of 97 rooms ideally located in Paris' 1st arrondissement, the district fashion and jewelry. The Renaissance Paris Vendôme, a figurehead for the brand, offers guests the opportunity to observe a dialogue between the past and the present – the essence of contemporary Parisian life - through a kaleidoscope of immersive experiences.
Among them, the elegant rooms offer a comfortable and warm atmosphere like a private apartment, with a new and functional design. Twin rooms are available from the third category.
Curious spirits can enjoy the Mediterranean cuisine of the famous Chef Assaf Granit in a creative and modern décor by interior designer Dorothée Meilichzon. Balagan is the place to be, where day and night come together in a never-ending show illustrating both the festive atmosphere of the restaurant and the boudoir atmosphere of the cocktail bar.
The inspiring experiences extend right into The Studio, a veritable laboratory of discoveries where the opposites meet, the interior and the exterior fuse, the frontiers fade and the life of the neighborhood takes place at home. A neighborhood that the "Navigator" staff of the hotel takes pleasure to present through customized itineraries for each client, revealing the latest favorite addresses nearby!
Always nourishing creativity: from co-working to showroom, from meeting to pop-up fashion, from private dinner to pre-dinner parties, the new Atelier space is the ultimate stage to imagine tailor-made events.
Finally, nestled in the heart of the hotel, the pool and spa offer a unique sensory break to refresh and relax away from the surrounding frenzy.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: floor-level entrance or ramp, lowered and adapted reception desk, communal toilets with grab rail and adapted sink, 2 adapted rooms with en-suite equipped with adapted sink and toilet with surrounding space and grab rail.
- Visual impairment: no head-level obstacles in the corridors, stairs with tactile and visual contrast before first step down and with continuous hand rail, lift with adapted keypad (buttons in relief and/or Braille) and speaker to announce floor numbers.
- Hearing impairment: online booking and/or payment available on the website, lift with visual display and/or magnetic induction loop, fire alarm accompanied by flashing lights.
- Learning disability: easy to read and understand signs with pictograms, entrance monitored with CCTV and visible from the reception.
