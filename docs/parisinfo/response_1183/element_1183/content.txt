                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Located at the heart of the 8th arrondissement, between the Opéra Garnier and the Arc de Triomphe and near the historic department stores, the Champs Elysées and the Rue du Faubourg Saint-Honoré, the neighborhood remains the capital’s main economic hub.
La Villa Haussmann offers guests a real tribute to the Second French Empire by combining the interpretation of a sophisticated, refined and contemporary style with all the modern comforts. The hotel has 41 rooms and 2 suites.
Five size categories ranging from 20 to 45 square meters meet the expectations of every guest.
Our restaurant, “Le N3,” offers French cuisine showcasing regional products in a chic and cozy atmosphere that is extended to its landscaped terrace. Our collection of cocktails served with appetizer bites can be enjoyed at the bar. Our chef offers a variety of suggestions (cocktail, finger buffet, three-course lunch or dinner, etc.) based on your needs for private or professional events. 
The hotel also has a meeting room that gives a touch of privacy to business in Paris. A 40-square-meter naturally lit room for meetings, dinners and cocktails. An additional room accommodating four to six guests may be requested. La Villa Haussmann provides an event manager to organize and coordinate your events.
Our quiet and intimate 100-square-meter wellness center is equipped with a hammam, fitness area and heated stainless steel pool. The services of a personal trainer may be requested.
