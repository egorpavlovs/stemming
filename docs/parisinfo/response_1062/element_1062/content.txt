                                                                                    Description
                        
                            Art Paris Art Fair is a springtime art fair focusing on discovery. Art Paris Art Fair brought together for the 2018 edition, at the Galeries nationales du Grand Palais in Paris, 143 galleries from 23 countries to explore European art from the post war period to the present day as well as international emerging art scenes.The coming edition will present a survey of the new, effervescent and highly stimulating artistic environment of four major Latin-American capitals: Bogota, Buenos Aires, Lima and Mexico City.Source : CCI Paris Ile-de-France – Unimev 
                        
                    
                                        
                                                                            
                                                    Website
                            
                                http://www.artparis.com/en
                            
                                                                        
                                        

                                        
                                            



    
        Place 
    
    
        
            
                Galeries nationales du Grand Palais
                
                    
        This giant stone building with a splendid steel-framed glass roof was built for the 1900 Universal Exhibition. Dedicated ‘by the Republic to the glory of French art’, it was classed as a historical monument in 2000. An architectural innovation in its time, the Grand Palais hosts four major temporary exhibitions each year organized by the Réunion des Musées Nationaux and the Association Française d'Action Artistique (AFAA). More than 40 highly eclectic events are held each year, drawing two million visitors annually.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Mobility impairment: car park with reserved adapted parking spaces available, reception/till with adapted counter, adapted public toilets with transfer bar and adapted sink, alternatives to the stairs (lift, stair lift, access ramp). Access by avenue du Général Eisenhower for exhibitions in the Galeries Nationales, and by avenue Winston Churchill for exhibitions in the Nef.
- Visual impairment: transparent glass doors/walls clearly marked with contrasting elements, no obstacles at eye-level, information and signs in large print, signs at an appropriate height.
- Audio impairment: reservation and/or payment possible online, ticket office equipped with hearing magnetic induction loop for the hard of hearing.
- Mental impairment: trained/sensitive staff at reception, easy-to-read and easy-to-understand signs with pictograms, monitored entrance visible from reception/till.
