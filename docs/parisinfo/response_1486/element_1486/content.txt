                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The perfect format for team bonding in a cosmopolitan atmosphere !
Since its inception, more than 200 leading French and international companies have turned to How to Become a Parisian in One Hour? to strengthen links between their staff.
GROUP BOOKINGS AT THE THEATRE DES NOUVEAUTES
For your team building activities, incentives or corporate gifts, please contact us to book tickets for one of our performances and we will send you a custom quote. We will give you preferential rates with a choice of seats to suit your needs.
The Théâtre des Nouveautés is part of the great traditional Parisian theatres and is located on the Grands Boulevards in the heart of the city.
Entirely renovated in 2016, it has a capacity of 600 seats and provides all the services needed to host your event: a greet and seat team at your disposal, a bar (serving alcoholic and soft drinks, light meals and snacks) and a cloakroom for coats and small items.
SHOW PRIVATIZATION
Are you looking to energize your private events by adding a friendly angle to help foster cohesion and team spirit ?
The show How to Become a Parisian in One Hour? can come to you!
Many international companies have already called on us for their private events. Contact us !
