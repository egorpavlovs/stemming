                             Parisian stores all dressed up for winter
                            Christmas is fast approaching, and, as is the custom, Parisian shopping centres and department stores dress up in their winter finery ... The ideal occasion to discover their team-building activities as well as gourmet offerings for delicious corporate gifts, or even to arrange a business lunch, a tea-time snack, or an after-work event with your customers or employees in a magical setting!
                        

                        Galeries Lafayette Paris HaussmannThis year, Galeries Lafayette Paris Haussmann unveils ‘The Dream Factory’. Under the dome, a big Christmas tree in different shades of green is enhanced by little characters made from wire, and the 11 store windows showcase various Christmas traditions. For your corporate gourmet gifts, head to the Lafayette Gourmet/Maison store to discover sublime creations by starred chefs. And for your professional events, the Opéra reception room on the 5th floor can accommodate up to 250 people; the Terasse rooftop with a panoramic view over the rooftops of the capital, up to 400 people; and the winter dome, up to 50 people! Printemps Paris HaussmannUntil 7 January, the windows of the Printemps Paris Haussmann department store will spirit you back to your childhood days. Two young adventurers, Jules and Violette, invite you to travel through magical worlds populated by funny creatures to search for Santa. After this enchanting show, why not warm up at the Brasserie Printemps (450 people for dinner or a cocktail party)? On the menu, dishes worthy of the greatest Parisian brasseries with a modern twist.Le BHV MARAISThe BHV MARAIS is celebrating Christmas in a British and retro style in honour of the new Disney film Mary Poppins Returns. The store windows are the stage for the magical world of this musical. An enchanting interlude for grown-ups and kids! Discover the tea room by Emma's Corner with its 1930s-style decor and enjoy a tea-time break with your customers or colleagues. Looking for a team-building activity for the end of the year? The BHV MARAIS invites you to discover its Do It Yourself workshops!     La Vallée Village  The date has been set for a theatrical and spectacular Christmas at the Vallée Village shopping outlet: A golden sleigh and reindeers, red velvet curtains in the windows, and glittering Christmas trees. Like the Mini Palais restaurant at the Grand Palais, Le Menu Palais — the new chic brasserie at this mecca for shopping — offers refined authentic dishes. A resolutely arty and trendy venue for your business meals. Note: the Vallée Village will be exceptionally open on 1 January 2019.Aéroport de ParisAway for a business trip in Paris?  Not yet found the time to look for your corporate gifts? The Aéroports de Paris have it all wrapped up for you with their typically Parisian boutiques and Christmas gift boxes. For the festive season, the airport becomes a fun and joyful world.  On the programme: decorations, entertainment and a musical atmosphere.Paris Aéroport- OrlyParis Aéroport - Charles de Gaulle
