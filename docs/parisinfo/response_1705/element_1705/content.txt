                                    Accessibility information
                    
        Motor disability: adapted and reserved parking spaces for wheelchair users, ground floor entrance or entrance with sloping surface, low reception desk adapted for wheelchair users, toilet facilities adapted and equipped with transfer bar and sink adapted for wheelchair users, 4 bedrooms adapted with bathroom equipped with floor-level shower (with folding or movable seat), a sink adapted for wheelchair users.
Visual disability: transparent glass doors and walls with contrasting elements, absence of obstacles at face level to impede circulation, stairs with touch-sensitive and visual contrast before the first descending step, with continuous handrails, lift with adapted keypad (raised buttons and/or braille) and with announcement of floor.
Hearing disability: booking and/or payments available to do online, lift with visual announcement and/or audio induction loop, flashing lights when fire alarm sounds.
Mental disability: attentive staff, supervised entry and visible from the reception desk.
