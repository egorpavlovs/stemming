                                                            The Grand Musée du Parfum is opening in the Faubourg Saint-Honoré
                                                
                                    



        
            
                
                    
                        
                            The Grand Musée du Parfum is opening in the Faubourg Saint-Honoré
                            A true symbol of French art de vivre and the former couture house of Christian Lacroix, it will open on 16 December 2016. 
                        

                        The Grand Musée du Parfum has taken up residence at no. 73 on the Rue du Faubourg Saint Honoré within a 1,400 m² Parisian town house with a large garden.This new cultural institution will offer an educational fun and immersive visit to unveil all the mysteries of the world of perfume. Enjoy a unique sensory and artistic experience!Whether you are a novice or fascinated by scents, you will learn more about the process of creating a fragrance, which is partly an art and partly a science. Thanks to advanced technology, the museum traces the history of fragrances in three stages by inviting you to discover a unique olfactory collection of over 60 scents during your visit. The museum’s cultural programme will include numerous events, workshops and exhibitions.The museum is also an ideal venue for your professional events. The 'Suite' hosts 12 people for dinner and 20 people for a cocktail reception. The 3rd floor (50 people) is open to private-hire with 2 rooms and a gallery. The 'White Club' hosts 50 people for dinner and 80 people for a cocktail reception. For an outdoor event, you can receive your guests in the garden.
