                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A place for an event, breath for real !
Located in the heart of Parc Floral de Paris, the events' center offers you a unique venue where nature and city are brought together to give life to your events whatever the season.
Set in a lush and green setting with its original features and architecture, the layout of the venue allows us to host all types of events (professional and general public trade fairs, congresses, conferences, business conventions, galas and cultural events, product launch, fashion shows, team building activities, road shows...).
Both functional and modular, the venue offers 22,000 sq.meters laid out as follows:
-The main Hall with its majestic “Baltard” architecture (10,200 sq.m area) / capacity : 8,500 seated - 12,000  standing) 
-Four pavilions linked by glass corridors (3 pavilions 384 sq.m, 1 pavilion 282 sq.m) including a conference room (310 seated).
-Flexible complementary exterior space such as the Esplanade (800 sq.m), Terre Plein (1,600 sq.m) and a mini golf
-A private parking lot with 300 parking spaces (8,000 sq m)
