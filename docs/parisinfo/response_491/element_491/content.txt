                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Visual Transdev is a specialist in business and leisure tourism transport in Paris and the surrounding region with its fleet of 90 coaches with different capacities (8-16 seats, 49/57 and 63 seats, 78 seats on double decker buses)
Transport upon request, from a single person to the largest possible events
Airport transfers, shuttles for congresses and exhibitions, vehicles provided.
Regular shuttles to Disneyland Paris and Parc Astérix, as well as the Château de Versailles. 7/7 hotline. 
    
                            
                                            

                    
                    



    
        Services 
    
    
        
            
                                
                    Description of fleet
                    
        Visual offers a full range of vehicles with the best equipment for comfort and safety, meeting the latest environmental standards and hiring professional drivers who are trained in eco driving.
                
                
                                
                    Type of vehicle
                    
                                                    Buses, cars
                                                    Minibuses, people carriers (7+ seater)
                                                    Airport shuttles
                                                    Shuttles to Disney, shopping centres
                                            
                
                
                                    
                        Others
                        Minibus, Autocars Grand Tourisme and VIP, Wi-Fi onboard Minivan 7/8 places, Prius Hybrid, Wi-Fi onboard
