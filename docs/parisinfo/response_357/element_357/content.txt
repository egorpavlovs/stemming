                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Disneyland® Paris, an all-in-one venue for all your professional event needs! Just 35 minutes from Paris, with an on-site TGV/RER/Eurostar terminal, Disneyland® Paris offers 2 world-class convention centres, a total of 23,500m² of professional space, 95 meeting rooms for 10 to 4,000 people. 8,200 rooms spread across 14 hotels, all located close to the convention centres. Two theme parks and Disney® Village host evening events for 50 people or more. Complete with a 27-hole golf course, a range of team-building activities, and two adventure courses and a large range of products for personal motivation.
