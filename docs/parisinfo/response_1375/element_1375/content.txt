                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The hotel Alfred Sommier, former Parisian mansion of a big industrialist of the 19th century, preserves the soul of a family house. It was recently restored and transformed by a descendant of the Alfred Sommier family into a luxury establishment. The hotel offers 80 rooms, of which 20 suites, historic lounges as well as a restaurant and a bar giving onto a private garden.
The hotel has 5 meeting rooms, which can accommodate any type of event. The meeting rooms have a private wi-fi connection, daylight and some of the rooms are open on our private garden.
