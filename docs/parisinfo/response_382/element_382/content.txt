                                                    Description
                            
                                
        This exceptional private mansion, listed as a historical monument, is located in the heart of the golden triangle. Neoclassic and authentic French 'Art de Vivre' architecture, the harmony of its landscaped gardens, newly renovated rooms and service worthy of great houses combine to ensure the success of your business events, cocktail parties and gala dinners as well as seminars and conferences. An exceptional venue, adaptable to any kind of event whether corporate, cultural or private, and located a stone's throw away from the Champs-Elysées.
