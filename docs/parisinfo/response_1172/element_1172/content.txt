                            2018, the year of the Paris Motor Show!
                            The Paris motor show (Mondial de l’Automo) is held every two years. In 2018, the Paris Expo Porte de Versailles trade fair grounds will welcome car lovers and trade professionals from around the world from Thursday 4 October to Sunday 14 October. 
                        

                        The Paris motor show is held every two years. In 2016, the Paris Expo Porte de Versailles trade fair grounds will welcome car lovers and trade professionals from around the world from Thursday 4 October to Sunday 14 October. It is the most visited motor show in the world, with more than 1,066,000 visitors in 2016.Dozens of launches and world premieres take place at this world-famous, must-see event packed with eye-catching stands and innovative events. At the last edition of the show, 245 car brands from 19 countries displayed their latest designs, showing 140 novelties, and the possibility to try the autonomous car.Alongside the show proper, there are a host of car-related events, workshops and exhibitions focusing on economic, social and technical issues affecting the automobile sector. The show gets huge international media coverage, with more than 10,000 accredited journalists from 100 countries. It generates hundreds of hours of French and international TV and radio coverage in places as far-flung as Brazil, Mexico and the U.S.The motor show is open to the trade and the general public alike, with a well-organized schedule of press days, trade-only days, general public days and late openings. There are special arrangements for group visits.The motor show will celebrate in 2018 its 120th anniversary.Website: www.mondial-paris.com/en/
