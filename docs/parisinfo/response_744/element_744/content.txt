                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Crowne Plaza® Paris Charles de Gaulle is a hotel situated in Roissy, France, just minutes from the Roissy-Charles de Gaulle airport. The 455 rooms are equipped with a bathroom with bathtub and shower as well as a terrace or a balcony. The hotel also has a large covered car park and high speed Wi-Fi. The conference space offers various meeting rooms, all with natural lighting, accommodating up to 200 people in the main theatre. It offers its guests the fantastic Spa Nuxe with a pool, sauna, and steam room as well as a fitness suite, a French restaurant (l'Infini) and a bar-lounge ideal for meeting colleagues.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: car park with reserved adapted space(s), level or inclined entryway, reception/cashdesk with dropped desk, collective bathroom with transfer bar and accessible sink ; 16 adapted bedrooms with bathrooms equipped with level shower (with folding or mobile seat), accessible sink, toilet with lateral space and transfer bar ; dining room with aisles over 1.40 m wide and with adapted tables allowing wheelchair access.
- Visual impairment: transparent glass doors / walls preceded by contrasting signage, no obstacles above shoulder height, staircase (s) with tactile and visual contrast before the first downward steph, with contrasting steps, elevator with adapted control keypad (raised and / or braille keys) and with sound system with announced floor.
- Hearing impairment: reservation and / or possible payment (s) on the website, reception / cash desk equipped with a magnetic induction loop, lift with visual and / or audible announcement with magnetic induction loop, flash light coupled to the fire alarm.
- Mental disability: trained staff, simple to understand and read signposting with pictograms, supervised entry door, visible from reception / cash desk.
