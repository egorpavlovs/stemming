                            Launching of the new Palais des Congrès at Paris Saclay 
                            At the heart of the vibrant Paris Saclay economic hub, the Châteauform’ group is opening a brand-new Palais des Congrès at Massy, combining comfort, flexibility, environmental sustainability, and modernity.
                        

                        Designed by renowned architect and urban planner Christian de Portzamparc, the Palais des Congrès Paris Saclay accounts for more than 5,500 m² of modular space, equipped with the latest technology. Intended to host large-scale events, it has a 1,000 m² exhibition space, a 600-seat auditorium, eight naturally-lit meeting rooms (from 50 m² to 150 m²), and two foyers of 460 m² and 310 m² respectively. Catering is provided by Nomad, with bespoke packages based on fresh produce. One of Châteauform’s strengths is the personalized welcome it provides, and you will certainly find that at this new, lively Palais des Congrès along with an internal garden decorated with swings and birdcages, and 600 m² of informal gathering areas.Ideally located just 150 m² from the RER and TGV stations, and close to a car park, the complex is also surrounded by luxury accommodation, a cinema, shops and many hotel rooms.
