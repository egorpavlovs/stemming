                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A New Construction In The Service Of Knowledge
3 Mazarine takes place in an area listed as historic monument, on the banks of the Seine, classified as a world heritage site by Unesco.
Achieved by Marc Barani’s atelier, this new contemporay space perfectly stands in a architectural complex. Designed to host meetings and receptions organized by l’Institut de France, 3 Mazarine will also host corporate, institutional and private events.
Accessible from Mazarine’ street and equiped to suit contemporary practices, 3 Mazarine will open in january 2019.
