                            L’Atelier by Yachts de Paris: a new event venue on the front of the Seine
                            Welcome aboard L’Atelier, the brand new private covered terrace from Yachts de Paris: a modern venue where design, comfort, and well-being combine to offer you a most unusual event.
                        

                        Situated just below the Ile Saint Louis with stunning views of Notre Dame de Paris Cathedral, L’Atelier is a contemporary space, on a barge, with a chic and relaxed atmosphere. The place is ideal for enjoying special moments with your customers and colleagues. Furnished with large leather chairs and neo-industrial decor, the space is also equipped for presentations, with two screens, speakers, an amplifier and a cordless microphone.Four packages are available for your receptions: breakfast, cocktail buffet lunch, after-work cocktail party or a cocktail party, with a 30 to 80 people capacity. Catering is provided by renowned caterer Lenôtre, which offers exclusive creations made from exceptional ingredients. Cooking workshops can also be hosted by top Chefs from the Lenôtre School, with a champagne aperitif, followed by a meal with food and wine pairing: ideal for a tasty teambuilding!Remember that Yachts de Paris also has a fleet of 8 yachts, and 3 reception lounges, by the banks of the Seine for upscale events on the water.

            Boats and barges for private hire in Paris 
        
        Our guide to boats and barges available for private hire. Host a workshop, dinner cruise or private event (a birthday party or wedding reception) on the Seine or the Paris canals. 
    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            Paris along the water 
        
        Summer terraces, rooftop bars and barges are springing up along the Seine riverbanks with chairs, tables and sun umbrellas … Inviting places for lunch, drinks after work, a business dinner or an evening of dancing and partying on the water. What’s more, the views of the capital’s iconic and historic monuments are simply breathtaking. The Seine and its boats is also an ideal playground for your teambuilding events! Alongside your event, take advantage of Paris Plages until early September, and the new Parc Rives de Seine pedestrianized quaysides that offer lots of entertainment. Take a look now at our selection of the best places by or on the Seine, for private events, group stays, and ever-more original incentive activities.
