                            VIP stay in Paris - by Agence Tiveria
                            Tiveria Paris agency has created a special programme in Paris for Italian clients. A varied program, mixing work and pleasure, realized in order to make them discover all the wonders of Paris.
                        

                        Choice of theme: VIP stay in ParisNumber of participants:80 peopleLength of programme:3 daysSector(s) of activity of client:Italian Bank and InsuranceProgrammeDAY 1Welcome of the participants at the airport and transfer to their hotel.After check-in in the prestigious hotel Westin Paris-Vendôme, a business meeting was set up, followed by a welcome drink. For the evening and to start this stay on a high note, participants have had access to an emblematic place of Paris: the famous cabaret Lido. The evening ended with a dinner and show followed by the revue “Bonheur”. All the participants got the best seats thanks to our privileged partnership.DAY 2 A first morning in Montmartre, with a private show tour in Italian with anecdotes and stories narrated by actors. To continue this great day, the participants were greeted to the restaurant “Le Moulin de la Galette” fully privatized for lunch, located within the walls of the legendary " Bal Populaire " painted by Renoir.The afternoon and evening took place in one of the iconic places in the Paris region: the magnificent Château de Versailles. For the delight of participants, a VIP tour of the castle followed by a special evening at the Horse Academy of Versailles have been organized with  the famous horse show Bartabas, the visit of the Stables with aperitif cocktail and a dinner served in the “manège” and prepared by the top-end caterer Potel & Chabot.DAY 3To close this stay in Paris, nothing like a little stop in the kitchen of a great French chef with the organization of a cocktail lunch participatory in Alain Ducasse’s Cooking School taught in English.For the last evening, privatization of the Louvre Museum with a night-time tour of the permanent collections. Then, transfer to “l’Escale du Louvre” and dinner cruise on the Seine aboard the Yacht“Excellence”, entertained by a Jazz trio.Presentation of TiveriaTo make sure your event will be a success, Tiveria Paris has developed a real expertise in corporate events, event communication, clients’ relationships, congresses, seminars, incentives and public relations and brings its added value:More than 10 years’ experience in corporate events and in VIP services management.A quick and flexible action in all the steps of your projects.A control on service providers, settings, and speakers adapted to your project and budget.
