                            Major Parisian exhibitions 
                            Paris’ leading museums show exhibitions, some of which come from many different parts of the world. Here is a selection of highlights for the 2017-2018 cultural season and a preview of the line-up for the upcoming years.
                        

                        Current exhibitions in Paris:Fernand Khnopff (1858-1921), master of enigma at Petit Palais until 17 March 2019 Jean-Jacques Lequeu (1757-1826), builder of fantasy at Petit Palais until 17 March 2019 Nara - Treasures of Japanese Buddhism at Musée Guimet until 18 March 2019 Picasso-Rutault at Musée Picasso until 20 March 2019 Alberto Giacometti / Peter Lindbergh - Seize the invisible at Institut Giacometti until 24 March 2019   David Perlov at Musée d'Art et d'histoire du judaïsme until 31 March 2019 Mircea Cantor - Vânătorul de imagini at Musée de la chasse et de la nature until 31 March 2019 Forces de la nature - 3 Norwegian artists (Kari Dyrdal, Torbjørn Kvasbø and Marit Tingleff) at Musée national de céramique in Sèvres until 1 April 2019  Cut-outs at Musée Rodin until 7 April 2019 Japanese bamboo art at Musée du Quai Branly Jacques Chirac until 7 April 2019 Leonardo Da Vinci and Italian Renaissance at Beaux-Arts until 19 April 2019 Doisneau and music at Cité de la musique until 28 April 2019  Passer-by by Atelier E.B at Lafayette Anticipations until 28 April 2019 Tutto Ponti, Gio Ponti archi-designer at the Musée des Arts décoratifs until 5 May 2019 Vasarely at Centre Pompidou until 6 May 2019 Thester Gates, Angelica Mesiti & Julien Creuzet at Palais de Tokyo until 12 May 2019  Eugène Lami, painter and decorator to the Orléans family in the Prints and Drawings Gallery of Chantilly castle until 19 May 2019 Red hair! From Jean-Jacques Henner to Sonia Rykiel at Musée national Jean-Jacques Henner until 20 May 2019 Asian Infinities - Photographs by Jean-Baptiste Huynh at Musée Guimet until 20 May 2019  Love, Ren Hang at Maison européenne de la Photographie until 26 May 2019 Coco Capitán, busy living at Maison européenne de la Photographie until 26 May 2019  Ellsworth Kelly: Windows at Centre Pompidou until 27 May 2019 Sérusier's "The talisman", a prophecy of colour at musée d'Orsay until 2 June 2019 Luigi Ghirri - 'Cartes et territoires' at the Jeu de Paume until 2 June 2019 The Courtauld collection, a look at Impressionnism at Fondation Louis Vuitton until 17 June 2019  Franz Marc / August Macke - The Adventure of the blue rider at musée de l'Orangerie until 17 June 2019 Sebastião Salgado - Declarations at Musée de l'Homme until 30 June 2019  Oceania at Musée du Quai Branly Jacques Chirac until 7 July 2019  From dream to reality: Eastern world of painters at musée Marmottan-Monet until 21 July 2019  Thomas Lawrence (1769-1830), painter of intimacy at Musée de la vie romantique until 21 July 2019  Poison - 30 living species at Palais de la Découverte until 11 August 2019 Calder - Picasso at Musée Picasso until 25 August 2019 Van Gogh - The Starry night at L'Atelier des Lumières until 31 December 2019 Upcoming exhibitions in Paris:Bonnard, Vuillard, Maurice Denis ... Nabis decor at the Musée du Luxembourg from 13 March until 30 June 2019  Hammershøi, master of Danish painting at Jacquemart André museum from 14 March until 22 July 2019 Thomas Schütte - Three acts at Monnaie de Paris from 15 March until 16 June 2019  Thomas Houseago - Almost Human at Musée d'art moderne de la Ville de Paris from 15 March until 14 July 2019  Revolution generation - French drawings from the Fabre museum, 1770-1815 at Musée Cognacq-Jay from 16 March until 14 July 2019 Red. Soviet arts at Grand Palais from 20 March until 1 July 2019 The Bührle collection. Manet, Cézanne, Monet, Van Gogh etc. at Musée Maillol from 20 March until 21 July 2019  Helena Rubinstein, the adventure of beauty at Musée d'Art et d'histoire du judaïsme from 20 March until 25 August 2019  Hebrew manuscripts from Italy at Bibliothèque nationale de France François Mitterrand from 20 March until 22 September 2019 Tutankhamun: treasure of a pharaoh at La Villette from 23 March until 15 September 2019 Black models: from Gericault to Matisse at Musée d'Orsay from 26 March until 21 July 2019  Life lines, an exhibition of legends at MAC/VAL from 30 March until 25 August 2019  The Moon, from reality to imaginary travels at Grand Palais from 3 April until 22 July 2019  Fellini / Picasso at Cinémathèque française from 3 April until 28 July 2019  Oceans at Grande Galerie de l'Évolution from 3 April 2019 until 5 January 2020  Young artists in Europe - Metamorphosis at Fondation Cartier from 4 April until 16 June 2019  Alberto Giacometti / Teresa Hubbard & Alexander Birchler - 'Flora' at Institut Giacometti from 5 April until 9 June 2019  Picasso and war at Musée de l'Armée from 5 April until 28 July 2019  Electro dreams - from Kraftwerk to Daft Punk at Philharmonie de Paris from 9 April until 11 August 2019 The world in spheres at Bibliothèque nationale de France François Mitterrand from 16 April until 21 July 2019 Théo Mercier - Every stone should cry at Musée de la chasse et de la nature from 30 April until 30 June 2019  Forgotten empires: heirs to the Hittite Empire at the Musée du Louvre from 2 May until 12 August 2019  Prehistory - A modern invention at Centre Pompidou from 8 May until 16 September 2019  Romantic Germany 1780-1850 - Drawings from the Weimar collection at Petit Palais from 22 May until 1 September 2019  Romantic Paris 1815-1848 at Petit Palais from 22 May until 15 September 2019  Félix Fénéon (1861-1944) at Musée du Quai Branly - Jacques Chirac from 28 May until 29 September 2019 Bernard Frize at Centre Pompidou from 29 May until 26 August 2019 'The Nude Mona Lisa' at the Jeu de Paume in the Domaine de Chantilly from 1 June until 6 October 2019 De Gaulle - Eisenhower at Musée de l'Armée from 3 June until 1 October 2019 Dora Maar  at Centre Pompidou from 5 June until 29 July 2019 Henry Wessel, a dark thread at Maison européenne de la Photographie from 5 June until 1 September 2019  Hella Jongerlus at Lafayette Anticipations from 7 June until 8 September 2019 Berthe Morisot (1841-1895) at Musée d'Orsay from 18 June until 22 September 2019 City Prince·ss·es at Palais de Tokyo from 21 June until 9 September 2019 Marc Pataut at the Jeu de Paume from 18 June until 22 September 2019 Sally Mann at the Jeu de Paume from 18 June until 22 September 2019 Buddha, the golden legend at Musée Guimet from 19 June until 7 OCtober 2019 Back on fashion at Musée Bourdelle from 1 July until 30 November 2019 Alberto Giacometti - 'Histoire de Corps' at Institut Giacometti from 11 July until 27 October 2019  Carte blanche to Hassan Hajjaj at Maison européenne de la Photographie from 11 September until 24 November 2019  Francis Bacon at Centre Pompidou from 11 September 2019 until 20 January 2020 Golden age in English painting: Gainsborough, Reynolds, Turner ... at the Musée du Luxembourg from 11 September 2019 until 31 January 2020 Figurative Mondrian at musée Marmottan-Monet from 12 September 2019 until 26 January 2020  Degas at the opera at musée d'Orsay from 24 September 2019 until 19 January 2020  The Dreamer in the forest at Musée Zadkine from 26 September 2019 until 23 February 2020 Henri II warrior king at Château de Vincennes from 27 September 2019 until 5 Janvier 2020 Henri de Toulouse-Lautrec at Grand Palais from 9 October 2019 until 27 January 2020  Katinka Bock at Lafayette Anticipations from 10 October 2019 until 5 January 2020 'Les Canons de l'élégance' at Musée de l'Armée from 10 October 2019 until 26 January 2020 'Le Supermarché des images' at the Jeu de Paume from 15 October 2019 until 12 January 2020  Vincenzo Gemito (1852-1929) at Petit Palais from 15 October 2019 until 26 January 2020 French scene at Palais de Tokyo from 16 October 2019 until 5 January 2020 Marie-Antoinette 'métamorphoses d'une image' at the Conciergerie from 16 October 2019 until 26 January 2020 Félix Fénéon (1861-1944) at musée de l'Orangerie from 16 October 2019 until 28 January 2020  Alimentation at Musée de l'Homme from 16 October 2019  J.R.R. Tolkien at Bibliothèque nationale de France François Mitterrand from 21 October 2019 until 4 February 2020  Hans Hartung retrospective at Musée d'art moderne de la Ville de Paris from October 2019 until February 2020  Chosen pieces from the Lafayette Anticipations collection at Musée d'art moderne de la Ville de Paris from October 2019 until February 2020  Leonardo da Vinci at the Musée du Louvre from 24 October 2019 until 24 February 2020  El Greco at Grand Palais from 27 October 2019 until 10 February 2020  Embroidery in the Middle Ages at Musée de Cluny in the fall 2019  23rd Paris Photo Fair at the Grand Palais from 6 until 10 November 2019 'La chaussure, la marche, la démarche' shoes exhibition at Musée des arts décoratifs from 7 November 2019 until 23 February 2020 Boltanski: retrospective at Centre Pompidou from 13 November 2019 until 16 March 2020 Luca Giordano, triumph of Napolitan baroque at Petit Palais from 14 November 2019 until 23 February 2020 Joris-Karl Huysmans Art Critic. From Degas to Grünewald at musée d'Orsay from 3 December 2019 until 2 March 2020 Ursula Schulz-Dornburg at Maison européenne de la Photographie from 4 December 2019 until early 2020 Pierre Soulages at the Musée du Louvre from 11 December 2019 until 9 March 2020  Van Cleef and Arpels at Grande Galerie de l'Évolution in the spring 2020 Koudelka at Bibliothèque nationale de France from 4 April until 4 July 2020  Italian sculpture from Donatello to Michelangelo (1460-1520) at the Musée du Louvre from May until August 2020 Revolutions in France from 1966 to 1970 at La Villette in 2020  The Ivan Morozov collection (Cézanne, Gauguin, Van Gogh, Renoir, Monet, Picasso...) at Fondation Louis Vuitton in the fall 2020  Taharqa, Pharaoh of two lands at the Musée du Louvre from 15 October 2020 until 31 January 2021  Penone at Bibliothèque nationale de France from 17 October 2020 until 30 January 2021  Arts in Toulouse in the 14th century at Musée de Cluny in the fall 2020 Paris-Athens 1800-1900:
