                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In the heart of the only museum in Paris dedicated to Salvador Dalí, the master of surrealism, contact us in order to organize your made-to-measure event, smack in the middle of the bronze sculptures and graphic artworks.
Two options to use separetly or combined : an Art Gallery overlooking the pittoresque, typical Parisian Rue Poulbot, and the exhibition space, making your event unforgettable.

                            Surréalisme (XXe)
