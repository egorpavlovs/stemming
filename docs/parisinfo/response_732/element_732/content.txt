                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Crowne Plaza Paris - Neuilly, ideally located in a leafy and safe district at the Paris’ doors, is minutes away by metro (Line 1 and 3) from the main attractions of Paris like the Arc de Triomphe or the Eiffel Tower.
Refurbished in a modern Parisian cozy style, the building offers 281 rooms and suites. Some of them on the 7th floor have a city view on the Eiffel Tower and Montmartre.
Our 1100 m² modular exhibition space is split up into 19 meeting rooms, 17 of which benefit from natural daylight, and our le Figaro lounges have access to the private garden.
Our restaurant offers a wide upscale French and International Cuisine while our bar offering 80 varieties of Champagne is ideal for enjoying a light meal and a drink at any time of the day.
During summer, the terrace is the perfect place to relax.
