   
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Casual Picnic is a picnic delivery service in all its forms. Our baskets adapt to your needs and your desires: indoors and outdoors, individual, to share and cocktail version. Consisting of gourmet products selected from local and responsible sources (small producers, artisans, groceries and ESAT), they will allow you to taste authentic flavours of the Paris region. True moments of sharing and conviviality, our picnics are perfectly adapted to group meals: team meal, team building operation, seminar, aperitif, catering for events.

                            Sustainable development approach
                Short circuit supply directly from local producers and artisans; partnership with ESAT (Establishment and Service of Help Through Labor); use of packaging made from recycled, recyclable and reusable materials.
