   
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Situated at the heart of the Roissy en France hotel centre, just 10 minutes from the Parcs des Expostions de Villepinte/Le bourget, the Orangerie auditorium can host your events for up to 400 people. In a verdant setting, the venue is equipped with the latest technologies and the different spaces on offer will pleasantly surprise you. The Tourist Office, Roissy Clé de France, will assist you in hiring out the auditorium at L'Orangerie.
