Our sales team guarantees a swift reply to your requests (within 48h).

        Sustainable 
    
    
        

                            Sustainable development approach
                Espace Charenton increases staff awareness by committing to monthly meetings.
The venue is easily accessed by metro, bus or tram.
Eco-friendly building, with optimised energy management: EDF annual study, sorting & collection of "RECY'GO Paper" recycling, abolition of all  non-biodegradable plastics in the convention centre, low-energy lighting "LED" used throughout the building.
Measurement of energy consumption with the aim of continuing reduction.
Providers using a sustainable development approach, Ethics caterer, EDINEWS printer, cleaning products and eco-labeled cleaning company, Green Post postage stamps, using local providers to reduce the CO2 emissions.
80% of space is naturally lit, with a possible concealment for the use of video projection device.
Systematic updating of health and safety rules.
Internal and external communication on eco-friendly aspects.
Hybridisation of forest trees, a landscape-scale project adjacent to the front of the property and covers 75% of the surface of the green roof.
