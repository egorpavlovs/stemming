                        Description
                        
                                                    
        A unique place at the heart of Paris, the Novotel Paris Vaugirard Montparnasse invites you to discover one of the most beautiful views over the rooftops of Paris, the Eiffel Tower, Montmartre, etc. as well as its convention centre which has been entirely renovated and integrates the latest technology. Ideal for hosting meetings of between 6 and 300 people.
After its renovation, the convention centre now welcomes you into a new bright and contemporary decor, with all the latest must-have technology. Perfectly adapted for your conventions, the Novotel Paris Vaugirard Montparnasse boasts 750 m² of meeting spaces which are entirely privatizable, air-conditioned and naturally lit. The plenary room can receive up to 300 people. For your seminars and workshops in small groups, the hotel offers its 'sky-meeting' area composed of 13 meeting rooms with special access to our terrace, offering a panoramic view of the Eiffel Tower. For your summer parties, we offer you the opportunity to hire out our Lounge Bar View terrace.
