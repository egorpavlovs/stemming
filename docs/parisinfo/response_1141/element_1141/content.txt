                            Paris Convention Centre : the largest conference centre in Europe
                            The recent inauguration of the Paris Convention Centre on 22 November is an important step in the ambitious 10-year renovation project of Paris Expo Porte de Versailles, which began in May 2015. 
                        

                        Fully reconfigured by Valode & Pistre, the new Pavilion 7 features a fabulous glazed wall with an undulating surface, creating wide openings and an exceptional view of Paris. Level 3 houses the Paris Convention Centre, the largest conference centre in Europe.With no fixed partition, this new site, which complements the Palais des Congrès de Paris, is able to host the biggest international congresses. Boasting a plenary room, which can accommodate up to 5,200 people seated, connected to 44,000 m² of exhibition space, the Paris Convention Centre is a venue where everything is possible.Level 4 of Pavilion 7 has also the 'Terminal 7' events space capable of accommodating up to 1,000 people. This space offers a panoramic view of Paris from its wooden terrace. The Pavilion’s roof garden is also available for full private hire.A brand new venue for hosting International conferences in Paris – the world’s leading destination in the ICCA 2016 rankings (congress study here)!
