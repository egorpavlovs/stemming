                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Hidden Gems Tours, based in Versailles and Paris, is accredited by the French Ministry of Culture and approved by Atout-France. Hidden Gems Tours is also a member of the travel agents’ associations IATA and ASTA.
The agency provides unique and meaningful experiences, harnesses the power of its networks, and provides a personal service to its clients.
Hidden Gems Tours is led by Xavier Fontanilles, an engineer by trade, a trustworthy and responsible leader who loves travel experiences. His aim is to share the City of Light under a new and unique approach: experience Paris as the royalty in the 16th -18th centuries did.
The agency specializes in private experiences. Grouped tours ONLY. For couples, honeymooners, families with children of all ages, or companies (seminars, team building, trade shows and conferences). For tours of 1 to 400 people, Hidden Gems Tours proposes a wide range of activities as well as exclusive, private, & custom tours of Versailles - they are the specialists of Versailles and the French monarchy -, and of Paris, but also the French chateaux located throughout the country.
Of course, their carefully chosen partners, as well as their own team of skilled guides & professional drivers are able to look after your clients for a visit anywhere in France and especially at the main sights of the city, such as the Louvre, the Musee d’Orsay, or the Eiffel Tower. They can meet your clients at the airport and take care of them throughout their stay in France from the first minute to the last. They can also drop your clients off at the airport, with good memories to share at home.
But the company’s real asset is offering an exclusive, unique look into the world of the French Monarchy. If you wish to offer your clients unique activities, with lasting memories to go along with them, then this company might be a good option. A mixture of lifestyle, history, education, museums, architecture, shopping, food tasting (chocolate, pastries...), their curated activities & private guided tours on the theme of the French monarchy will offer your clients a unique experience. Care to spend the night in a French chateau from the 17th Century? Or perhaps you’d prefer to visit lovely buildings from the 17th Century along the banks of the River Seine? Maybe you would like to sample pastries while walking in the footsteps of Marie Antoinette? Or maybe, your company or group of friends would like to experience a night living like Queens & Kings, in a French masquerade ball decked out in a renaissance look, with wigs, French hats, and made-up “à la Francaise”?  Well then, we believe that you have come to the right place. Hidden Gems Tours will take charge of your clients in an atmosphere of safety and serenity.   Check out their TripAdvisor reviews online if you need further convincing.
