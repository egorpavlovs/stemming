                            Description
                            
                                
        In an open-air village, discover more than 110 fashion and lifestyle boutiques offering the trendiest products from their previous collections at prices reduced by at least 33%* and often more.
You can find collections by ARMANI, COACH, FURLA, JIMMY CHOO, KENZO, MICHAEL KORS, PAUL SMITH, SANDRO, SONIA RYKIEL, SUPERDRY, TOD’S, UGG, ZADIG & VOLTAIRE and many more.
Open 7 days a week, just 40 minutes from Paris and 5 minutes from Disneyland Paris.
Travel Trade partners benefits:
	Online booking platform
	Welcome Center with multilingual staff
	Support for various transportations options
	Complimentary meal voucher for drivers and tour leaders
	Complimentary coffee break vouchers for selected groups
	VIP Card (10% further reduction in 6 boutiques of their choice) for guests
	Bespoke signage
	Incentive hospitality welcome pack
	Dedicated and personalised welcome by our hostess
	Centralised drop-off and pick-up point
	VIP lounge and art gallery for tailor-made hospitality
Guests benefits:
-	Welcome center with multilingual staff
-	Tax refund
-	Free wi-fi
-	Restaurant & cafés
-	Hands-free shopping
-	Air miles redemption when shopping in the village
-	Phone charging station
CONTACT US to organize your tailor-made event at least 48 hours in advance by mail.
