                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The team of All Seasons Paris Bercy***invites you to discover its all-in package (room, breakfast and unlimited Wi-Fi). Our 361 air-conditioned rooms were fully renovated in 2008. Thanks to its ideal location just next to the Parisian sports hall and Bercy village, near to the Gare de Lyon and Gare de Bercy, it will take you only ten minutes by metro to get to the Opera, the Louvre museum and the department stores. The hotel, of which the terrace is just above the Bercy park offers: restaurant, bar, seminars, charged public car park.
