                        Description
                        
                                                    
        In June 2013, the Royal Monceau Raffles Paris recieved its 'palace hotel' status. 
The famous hotel on avenue Hoche was given a new lease of life under the Raffles, hotels & Resorts collection: reopened in October 2010, its transformation was entirely designed and managed by designer Philippe Starck, who revisited traditions of celebration and prestige with a boldness and finesse. 
A genuine place of art and culture, contemporary creation are displayed as an invitation to travel and encounter new things, where the guest can mix with the most popular artists of who count the Royal Monceau as a 'home' and their new hideout. 
The hotel was reborn with a unique spirit from the Roaring Twenties, combining glamour, effervescence, and design. They offer a new alternative to other hotels.
