                            Country getaway at the Hyatt Regency Chantilly
                            In the heart of the state-owned forest of the Château de Chantilly, the new Hyatt Regency Chantilly is the perfect place for an event in a green setting. A real hamlet with eight 18th-century stone buildings, it is located only 20 minutes from Charles de Gaulle airport and 45 minutes from Paris. 
                        

                        Close to historical sites and recreational activities such as the area’s golf courses, this 4-star hotel perfectly combines business and relaxation. With 211 rooms and suites, design furniture in light wood and refined decoration, it also has many living and reception areas. ‘La Fabrique’, a 1,500 m² modular space equipped with state-of-the-art technology, is entirely dedicated to events. It offers 10 bright and contemporary lounges, including the 500 m² Regency Salon, and 2 convivial spaces of 203 and 307 m², for your creative work sessions, seminars, cocktail receptions and dinners.On the catering side, the ‘Nord’ (73 seats), revisits the specialities of the region with bistronomic and generous cuisine. The ‘Craft’ (133 seats) serves healthy and gourmet seasonal cuisine. Both restaurants have a large outdoor terrace.A magnificent spa and beauty centre (1,200 m²) consisting of 5 treatment rooms, a large fitness room, a hammam and an indoor swimming pool round off the wonderful offerings of this luxurious establishment.
