                        Description
                        
                                                    
        With its luxurious meeting rooms, the Paris Marriott Opéra Ambassador hotel represents a must-see place where to organize major events in Paris. The hotel offers 11 meeting rooms decorated tastefully and well equipped all of different size going from the smallest executive room on the very last floor with a surface of 20 square meters and the biggest called Vendôme with a surface of 250 square meters. Air-conditioned, these meeting rooms combine easily a traditional environment with modern technology. The Ambassador meeting rooms suit perfectly to all your event size going from the small meeting to worldwide seminars as well as from intimate lunch to gala dinner.
