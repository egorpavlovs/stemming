                        Description
                        
                                                    
        In the heart of nature, Business Village welcomes you to a preserved location 1 hr 15 mins from Paris, in the middle of Ile-de-France, in a 15 hectare wooded park with 200 wooden cottages.
A congress centre with 17 meeting rooms is available for you to organise team-building, motivation seminars, meetings or international congresses. 
Design your seminars by creating a team spirit and bringing your employees together during a special day!
Team building allows you to analyse the mechanisms related to the functioning of your business and promote exchange between your employees.
Humour, address, creativity, solidarity and interactivity will be developed to meet the challenges.
On site we can organise all the original activities in and out of the village.
