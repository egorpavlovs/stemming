                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Hotel  BANKE***** (Derby Hotels Collection group) is a design and trendy hotel ideally located in the centre of Paris, near Boulevard Haussmann and a few steps away from the Palais Garnier Opera House and Paris’s main department stores (Galeries Lafayette, Printemps, etc.)
Designed by the architects Paul Friesse and Cassien Bernard in the 'Eiffel' style, the luxury property is a beautiful and typically-Parisian 20th-century building that was the former head office of the CCF bank. The hotel magnificently combines a classical facade and an avant-garde interior, with a spectacular panoramic view of the Sacré Coeur and the Butte Montmartre.
Fully equipped with the free HSIA and Wi-Fi systems, the hotel offers 93 rooms including 15 suites, 24/24 room service, one spa area with fitness center & hammam, massages in rooms on request, 2 meeting rooms for up to 90 people in a theatre style, one trendy restaurant offering contemporary Mediterranean food (THE JOSEFIN), a more private restaurant to welcome groups, one lounge bar where you can taste traditional tapas and drink delicious international cocktails (THE LOLABAR). 
The Salle des Coffres is a 250 m² multipurpose function room that preserves the former bank’s safes. The unique room has a separate entrance, can host up to 180 guests and comes with a number of personalized options to fit your taste. A unique and charming venue for any type of event, from cocktails to exhibitions, presentations and private celebrations.
