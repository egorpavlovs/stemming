                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In Saint Germain des Près, Café Louise is the perfect place for all your business events and more.
With a total capacity of 158 pax, the restaurant offers a great space : 
-the ground floor which is open on the terrasse and the main road, Saint Germain.
-the Cave Louise, tipical from Saint Germain des Près with exposed stones. This one is equipped with A.C, a bar and all the material for a dancing event with DJ and can received 120pax cocktail or 80 seated.
About the food, the Chief, Jérôme Markovic cooks with love, French traditionnal and homemade cuisine.
The Café Louise focus on the quality of selected products (Tarama from Petrossian, Pata Negra Bellota, Poulet de Bresse...), the warm welcome and the personalised service and the passion of the Chief who cook for you.
Parking just under the restaurant and bus stop on the front of the restaurant.
