                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The César Palace Cabaret in Paris, which has been in existence for more than 20 years, was originally for French clientele, but for the first time, from April 2014, the new show 'Palace' will be opened to an international clientele. 
At 7.30pm: welcome from the managing director, Mr Stéphane, in his 250-seat restaurant, where your clients will start their evening with an aperitif followed by a traditionally French dinner.
At 10pm: the curtain goes up for a traditionally Parisian show lasting 1hr45, with feathers, glitter, and the French cancan will transport you to a magical universe that is French cabaret.
