                            Saddle up for a new concept of team building in Paris, by Byke & Co!
                            Byke & Co launches a totally new type of tour for discovering Paris in small groups aboard a large 'bike' for a sporty team-spirit experience!
                        

                        On a visit lasting between 1hr30 and 2hrs, 8 to 14 participants ride on a very special vehicle equipped with 10 pedals and 4 passenger seats. They explore the French capital as they pedal along, accompanied by two members of the Byke & Co team (a driver and a host/server)! An unusual activity in which participants must unite their efforts to advance.Several options are available: the Byke Tour, with a choice of two alcohol-free cocktails and savoury or sweet snacks; the Beer Byke with beers and snacks; the Byke Brunch with a hearty brunch, opposite the Eiffel Tower, including fresh fruit juices, hot drinks, cheese and charcuterie, and viennoiseries or fresh fruit, French pastries and savoury treats, to enjoy after the physical effort of the ride and before setting out! Each tour passes in front of Parisian landmarks, but can also be adapted to the wishes of customers. All these options provide an ideal way to surprise your teams with an original team-building event, whose key words are conviviality and ecology.
