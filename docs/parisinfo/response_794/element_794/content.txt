                                                    Description
                            
                                
        More than a typical boat, le Pavillon Seine is an unique and exclusive venue to discover Paris by night, the City of Lights, at a special evening event with 400 guests.
At a dock or on a cruise, le Pavillon Seine is the only boat providing an incredible panoramic view of the most beautiful monuments in Paris.  It proposes 12 stopovers on the Seine river* and can represents the anchor point of a party onboard, or the boarding point of a gourmet cruise.
Le Pavillon Seine offers 700 sqm reception lounges with 3m of ceiling height: 
-	Panoramic indoor lounge : 360 sqm with 34 bay windows providing an incomparably clear sight of Paris and the Seine river,
-	Upper deck area : 325 sqm offering a 360 degree view.
Each lounge is designed with wood and contemporary furnitures, but also without stairs or columns to sublimate the incredible panoramic view.  That way means flexibility and comfort to host seminars, prestigious cocktails and dinners from 50 to 400 guests. 
Really quiet, le Pavillon Seine proposes a sunny and flexible lounge with high technology equipments : reversible air conditioning, 6 portable microphone speakers, podium at the bow end, independent LED lighting with adjustable colour function…
For a gourmet cruise, Saint Clair le Traiteur offers you a refined gastronomy, synonym of daring and authenticity. Event Managers’ team is permanently at your disposal to propose you  tailor-made offers for seminars, press conferences, gala dinners or cocktail receptions. 
* 12 available stopovers: 
•	Sèvres 
•	Javel-Bas Parc André Citroën
•	Grenelle
•	Ile-aux-Cygnes – Bir Hakeim 
•	Debilly Trocadéro
•	Solférino – Musée d’Orsay
•	Saint-Bernard – Jardin des Plantes
•	Austerlitz – Cité de la Mode et du Design
•	Bercy 
•	Ivry-sur-Seine
•	Saint-Denis – Stade de France
•	Asnières-sur-Seine
