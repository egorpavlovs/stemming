                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Entirely dedicated to the success of your stay, the hotel Elysée Val d'Europe offers you the setting of a true Parisian hotel associated with quality services.
The hotel has undergone many renovations over the past two years: the restaurant, bar and reception in 2016 and our meeting rooms and all our rooms early 2018.
Our main salon "Elysée" of 185m ² is in the light of the day and is flexible in 3 separate living rooms from 42 to 72m ². Other possible combinations of 115 and 145m². For VIP meetings we offer our salon Vendôme. You will appreciate its modern furniture, its natural luminosity and its windows opening onto the interior garden.
The hotel Elysée also has four meeting suites of 38m² that can accommodate up to 10 people in U.
All our rooms are equipped with a big screen, video projector, click system share, flipchart with assorted pens, notepad and pencil, bottle of mineral water.
