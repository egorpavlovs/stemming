                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A new venue in the east of Paris, set in lush green surroundings. The Chesnaie du Roy, located in the Bois de Vincennes, opposite the Parc Floral, is ideal for hosting seminars, functions and events (cocktail parties of more than 2000 people).
