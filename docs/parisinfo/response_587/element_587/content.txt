                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In the heart of Paris, the Forum des images offers an architectural identity between elegance and modernity, and a choice of conference and meeting rooms to suit your different needs. Each event is attended by a team at your service. Business evenings, collaborator and/or client meetings, product launches, awards ceremonies, and seminars will all benefit from a big screen. Perfect accessibility and the parking facilities reflect the venue's ideal location. So don't hesitate, come along to the Forum des images.
