                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A new catering experience for your events...
Place party tables along the walls, generously stocked with flavours to share and finished with traditional petits fours, the serving plates overfilled and the buffets lined up... Everything is considered to create a gourmet, privileged, friendly and magical moment!
The recipe of Nomad, la Table de Châteauform':
- The theatricalization of the events... Like actors on stage, the serving staff accompany the guests throughout their meal: a parade of giants boards carried on shoulders, live cooking, advice andsharing with the guests.
- The DNA of La table de Châteauform', a table recognized for its delicacy, its generosity, and its authenticity.
- A party table to please, surprise, and create an unforgettable event!
- Simple, seasonal food made with products selected from the best local producers.
- All-inclusive packqges and tailor-made solutions to fit your needs: responding to your intimate events or major projects.
