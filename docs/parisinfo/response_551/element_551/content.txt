                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In the 18th arrondissement, College L' Ecole de l' Histoire welcomes your teams from 10 to 200 participants. Thanks to its 18 modular rooms, your participants will enjoy their training sessions in complete peace of mind. Its theme will take you through the great dates of history, a recurring source of inspiration for the formators. Finally, give yourself a moment of conviviality around our bar, the ideal place to exchange informally at the end of the day.
