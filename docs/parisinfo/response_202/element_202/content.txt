        Calliop√©e is a D.M.C. based in Paris
Calliop√©e is one of the rare agencies, to be present only in the field of Art and Culture. Which makes her a real expert on the subject.
We are active in the field of events, business travel and, in the realization of private evenings, in the largest Parisian museums or other places symbolic of Art and History.
We are also present, for your incentive actions and, offer you activities such as Murder parties, Cultural Hiking Rallies.
Our mission is to surprise our customers, our goal is to respond as closely as possible to their wishes.
Our strength is our creativity to continually have new ideas, to implement new products, so that your action is innovative and successful.
