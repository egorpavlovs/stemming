                International Conference on Oxidative Stress Reduction, Redox Homeostasis & Antioxidants 
                            

            
                                    June 2019 (subject to modification)
                            


                        
        
        
        



            

                

                
                    
                                                      

           
           
           
               
                

           
                                                       

                    
                                                                                    Description
                        
                            Paris Redox aims to make an important contribution towards a better understanding of redox control in physiological and pathological states that will lead to new therapeutic and disease-preventive agents.Among strategic topics discussed during ISANH Redox:Oxidative Stress, Redox Regulation & Modulation and Redox-Active AgentsOxidative Stress & Biomarkers: Imaging of Oxidative StressOxidative Stress & Stem CellsBeneficial effects of Oxidative Stress vs deleterious effects of antioxidantsNew Players in Redox balanceOxidative Stress, Ageing & Longevity: Where are we now?Oxidative Stress & Chronic Diseases: From Predictive to Preventive MedicineOxidative Stress, Antioxidants & InnovationsThis event takes place at Université Pierre et Marie CurieCampus Jussieu 4 Place Jussieu, 75005 Paris
