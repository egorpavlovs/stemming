                            Unusual venues for an event ‘just like at home’
                            Looking to organize your next professional event at a Parisian venue with an intimate and welcoming atmosphere ‘just like at home’, a private evening function in a magnificent Parisian apartment, or are seeking an unusual rental in the heart of Paris for a group trip? Then, hurry and check out our selection of venues that combine modernity and originality, tailor-made services, and upscale amenities in an atmosphere that’s just like home. With choices like New York style lofts with roof-top terraces, chic and sobre apartments with views over the rooftops and famous Paris landmarks, and vaulted cellars, you are sure to find the perfect venue!
                        

                        101 HaussmannA delightful Parisian venue in the heart of the 8th arrondissement. 101 Haussmann is a private and intimate setting housed in an apartment building. Solid oak herringbone parquet flooring, a bookcase, mouldings and a crystal chandelier decorate the 30 m² reception room, which gives onto a charming 15 m² terrace with greenery. 101 Haussmann hosts meetings and team-building events for up to 25 people in a stylish and welcoming atmosphere.Get a proposalParis ClandestinIn the heart of Saint Paul ‘village’, Paris Clandestin - a historic place that was once the wine cellar of Louis XIV –, now serves as an unusual place for original events. Paris Clandestin comprises 3 different spaces: the lounge area with exposed stonework and alcoves, the club bar in the style of a 1920s speakeasy, and the boudoir with large comfortable seats. Each space can be privately-hired either separately or together for dances, cocktail parties or dinners for up to 200 people. Catering is provided by Les Petits Cuistots which has an eco-responsible and social-solidarity offering.Get a proposalPavillon Etoile PresbourgThe Pavillons Etoiles and Presbourg are two Haussmannian apartments within a former private mansion. Each has a surface area of 200 m² and is fully flexible; they can be privately-hired separately or together, for up to 300 people. In addition to their resolutely elegant and Parisian interiors, these apartments offer a panoramic view of the Arc de Triomphe. It is also possible to combine the Pavilions with the Arc Club or the Victoria 1836 restaurant, located at the same address.Get a proposalLe 6, MandelLe 6, Mandel is a hôtel particulier devoted to contemporary art, located just a few metres from Trocadéro, facing the Eiffel Tower. This haven of peace in the heart of Paris has a surface area of 100 m² spread over 3 floors. A 130 m² garden completes the offer of this exceptional place that has been the residence of eminent people including Christian Dior. Le 6 Mandel hosts dinners and sit-down lunches for up to 20 people, and cocktail parties for up to 60 people. The venue also offers many additional services such as a florist, musicians, opera singers and valet parking.Get a proposalFabrique EvénementielleA stone's throw from the Republic district, the Fabrique Evénementielle has a total surface area of 700 m² spread over 2 levels. This space can be hired together or separately and can be entirely adapted to suit all different types of scenographies. The ground floor, with its industrial spirit, is ideal for dancing evenings due to its soundproofing, or product launches. Upstairs, a superb glass roof inundates the venue with natural light; the space is rounded off by a terrace. On both levels, it is possible to organize a conference (140 people), a meeting (35 people), a sit-down dinner (100 people) or a cocktail party (250 people).Get a proposalComme à la MaisonExposed beams, wooden chairs, leather sofa, walls decorated with plants … Welcome to Comme à la maison ! This atypical loft, designed as a home, is situated just a few minutes from the Grands Boulevards. With its various private rooms, it can be adapted for your photo shoots, exhibitions, conferences (50 people), cocktail parties (120 people), seated dinners (42 people) or evenings with dancing (145 people). The entrance hall opens onto the main living area, which is flooded with light from a large glass roof.Get a proposalLoft BastilleRight in the heart of the Bastille district in the 11th arrondissement, the Loft Bastille (220 m²) is a stylish and multipurpose venue for up to 180 people with a wide range of configurations possible.  You can personalize the decor and the layout as you wish! The seminar room (50 m²) has a terrace and is ideal for meetings or training sessions for up to 50 people. You can also organize a cocktail party here for up to 200 people.Get a proposalVilla MontparnasseTucked away at the end of a courtyard, the Villa Montparnasse, (325 m²) a delightful tranquil haven spread over 3 levels, offers an elegant setting for your events. The large lounge boasts huge bay windows and a glass roof and the walls are tastefully decorated with contemporary artworks. It gives on to a wonderful 150 m² shady terrace furnished with comfortable chairs, tables and a barbecue (80 people for a cocktail party).Get a proposalVilla Violet      Behind the front door of number 115 on the chic shopping street of Rue Saint Dominique, the Villa Violet offers a confidential tranquil and welcoming setting with bamboo plants, purple walls...Choose from the Atelier (50 m²),  le Bureau des fleurs (30 m²) or the Salon (50 m²), three spaces fully equipped with a HD video conference system. ‘Study Days’ packages are available to you and include a welcome coffee, a snack and lunch on-site or at one of the nearby restaurants.Get a proposal
