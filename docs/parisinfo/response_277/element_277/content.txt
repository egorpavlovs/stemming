                            Accommodation
                            What's new in Paris on the accommodation scene: openings, closures for renovation, renovations without closure etc.
                        

                        Upcoming openings and renovationsOpening by Accor of a Jo&Joe, a new 500-bed hotel concept in Gentilly, close to Cité Universitaire (1 April 2019)  Reopening after a complete makeover of the Hôtel du Louvre, an Unbound hotel by Hyatt (10 April 2019)  Opening at Barrière Le Fouquet's Paris of 19 new rooms and suites overlooking Champs-Élysées Avenue (April 2019)  Opening by Evok Hotels Collection group of a deluxe hotel at 116 Rue du Temple in the Marais: 44 laidback rooms (spring 2019) Opening of JK Place, deluxe boutique hotel at 82 Rue de Lille with 30 rooms with an Italian design, 5* category requested (spring 2019) Opening by the Société des Hôtels Littéraires of the Arthur Rimbaud hotel in Gustave Goublier street in the 10th arrondissement, themed 43-room hotel. 4* category requested (spring 2019) Opening by Goralska group of a 25-suite hotel in Castiglione / Vendôme area, 5* category requested (spring 2019) Opening of a 150-room Hilton Garden Inn at Paris-Charles-de-Gaulle airport with 5 meeting rooms, "Babette" restaurant and fitness room. 4* category requested (May 2019) Opening by LOV Group of a charming & deluxe 23-room hotel at 12 Rue de l'Indépendance américaine in Versailles, in 17th-century buildings "Grand Contrôle", "Petit Contrôle" and "Premières cent marches" pavilion, with a view over the Orangerie of the palace and over the "Pièce d'eau des Suisses" lake, as well as a restaurant by Alain Ducasse (May 2019) Opening by Marriott of the first Autograph Collection hotel in Paris, a 50-room hotel with conference center in the former Pentemont Abbey at the corner of Grenelle and Bellechasse streets in the 7th arrondissement. 5* category requested (2019) Opening of a new Maison Albar near the Opéra Garnier, in Rue du Helder (2019) Opening at 69 Rue Riquet in the 18th arrondissement of Simon's Hotel with 66 rooms, panoramic suite and ice bar (2019) Opening at 13-15 Rue de Nancy in the 10th arrondissement of Le Grand Quartier, 84-room hotel with meeting rooms, cafe, shop and rooftop (June 2019)  Opening of a Ibis hotel in Puteaux at Rond-point des Bergères, with 84 chambres (June 2019) Opening at Porte de Vincennes of a Meininger hotel with 249 rooms / 1,000 beds, low-cost accommodation with private or shared rooms and a large array of services (July 2019) Unveiling of the first 194 renovated rooms at the InterContinental Paris - Le Grand (August 2019), until the full renovation of the 458 rooms of the hotel (January 2021) Opening of a 205-room Mama Shelter within the Paris Expo Porte de Versailles exhibition, conference and convention center. The hotel will include a 350-seat restaurant run by Michelin-starred Guy Savoy, 500 sq. mt. of workshop space and a 350-sq.mt. rooftop bar and restaurant. Architecture and design by Jean-Michel Wilmotte and Michel Gaugain (September 2019)  Opening of a 152-room Hilton Garden Inn in Massy, 4* category requested (October 2019)  Re-opening after full renovation of the Pullman Paris Montparnasse**** with a rooftop terrace on the 25th floor (3rd quarter 2020) Opening at Domaine Montcel in Jouy en Josas, near Versailles, of a hotel dedicated to business events with 180 rooms, pool, restaurant and 3 000 sq. mt. of meeting rooms (2019) Opening of a 235-room new-generation Novotel within the Paris Expo Porte de Versailles exhibition, conference and convention center (2019) Creation at 47 Rue Vieille du Temple in the Marais of an apartment-hotel of 12 deluxe apartments in the former 15th century Amelot de Bisseuil private mansion, with spa and restaurant (2019) Opening of a 3* Ibis Style hotel with 185 rooms at 18 Avenue de la Porte d'Italie (2019) Opening near Gare Montparnasse of a second Okko hotel in Paris, with a new French concept of 4-star-hotel with a single rate (€250) all-inclusive: Wi-Fi, telephone, movies, open bar, fitness etc. 146 rooms (2019) Opening of the Hilton Paris Eiffel Tower at 55 Avenue de Saxe in the 7th arrondissement with 118 rooms, design by Philippe Starck, 4* category (2019) Opening of a 65-room hotel at 40 Rue de la Convention in the 15th arrondissement. 4* category requested (2019) Opening at  Gare de l'Est of the third Okko hotel**** in Paris, part of the "Balcon vert" project to link Est and Nord train stations. 170 rooms with a unique all-inclusive rate. 2,600-m² garden on the roof, accessible to all (2019) Opening after full renovation by Elegancia Group of the Coucou hotel, former Richmond Opéra. 63 rooms, 4* category requested (late 2019) Opening of the Babel hotel at 3 Rue Lemon in Belleville area, 38 rooms and restaurant (late 2019)  Opening of a 37-room hotel within the Mk2 Nation cinema (January 2020) Opening of the first Safestay hostel in Paris, at 44 Rue Lamarck on Montmartre hill, with 249 beds and 46 rooms (early 2020)  Opening with Best Western brand of the Hôtel de la Cité de la musique et de la Philharmonie at 190 Jean-Jaurès Avenue in the 19th arrondissement ; 78 rooms, 4* category requested (early 2020) Transformation by Japanese architects Sejima and Nishizawo of the former department store La Samaritaine into a deluxe property, Cheval Blanc, with 72 rooms and suites with a wide Seine river view, cultural areas, restaurant, garden terrace and shops (early 2020)  Opening by Elegancia Group of a 73-room hotel at Porte de Vincennes, 3* category requested (early 2020) Creation by Laurent Taieb (GLT) and Novaxia of a deluxe hotel in the 18th century building that was the former central post office in Rue du Louvre in the 1st arrondissement, 80 rooms, 2 restaurants including one on the roof-terrace, shops (spring 2020)  Opening by Hilton, close to Porte de la Villette, facing Saint-Denis canal, of a 92-room Hilton Garden Inn with meeting rooms, "Babette" restaurant, fitness room; 4* category requested (May 2020) Opening of a SLO Living Hostel at 61 Rue de Buzenval in the 20th arrondissement, with 160 beds and a rooftop for parties (2020) Opening at 24-30 Boulevard Pasteur in the 15th arrondissement of Hôtel M, 4* with 50 rooms (2020) Opening of the first Bulgari hotel in Paris, at 30 George-V Avenue, with 76 rooms and suites, 25-mt pool, 5* category expected (2020) Opening of the first Kimpton hotel in Paris by InterContinental Hotels Group, near Opéra Garnier, with 149 rooms and suites, spa & fitness, meeting rooms and panoramic view (2020) Opening of a MGallery by Sofitel hotel designed by Philippe Starck from the 17th to the 27th floor of one of the Duo Towers by Jean Nouvel in the 13th arrondissement with 139 rooms, skybar and restaurant (2020) Opening in the Morland Tower of a 161-room 4* hotel and 404-bed hostel (2020)  Opening near Paris-Charles-de-Gaulle airport of a 309-room Hyatt Place hotel and a 121-serviced-apartment Hyatt House with conference rooms, bar, restaurant, pool and fitness room (2nd semester 2020) Opening of a 152-bed hostel at L'Usine des cinq sens, former factory near the Seine river in the 16th arrondissement (late 2020)  Opening by Elegancia Group of a 42-room hotel in the Latin Quarter, 4* category requested (late 2020) Opening of a Life hotel at 11 Rue Béranger in the 3rd arrondissement, off Place la République. Architecture by Cabinet Wilmotte, bar and restaurant with a view on the roof (2021) Opening of a 125-room hotel at Austerlitz train station, 2* category requested (2021)  Opening in Saint-Ouen near the Flea market of MOB House with 100 rooms (35 to 100 sq. mt.), pool, fitness room, 2,000 sq. mt. garden and sustainable restaurant (November 2021) Opening by football player Cristiano Ronaldo and Pestana Group of a CR7 210-room hotel on the Left Bank, close to train stations Austerlitz and Lyon. 4* category requested (2021)  Opening in Meudon et La Défense of two Okko hotels (2021)  Opening by Elegancia Group of a 91-room hotel in Montreuil, 4* category requested (late 2021) Opening of a So Sofitel at 150 Champs-Élysées Avenue with 100 rooms, spa, panoramic rooftop with a 25 x 7 pool, bar and restaurant, 5* category requested (2022) Opening of a hotel and serviced apartments in one of the Sisters towers designed by Christian de Portzamparc in La Défense: 273 rooms and 36 apartments, restaurants, auditorium, pool and 100-metre-high view (2022) Opening at 198 Rue d'Aubervilliers of a 125-room 3* hotel and a 224-bed hostel (2022) Opening of 2 hotels in the Pleyel Tower in Saint-Denis, for a total of 700 rooms in 3* & 4*, pool, rooftop bar, conference center and restaurants (2022) Opening of three hotels by InterContinental in the Jardins de l'Arche tower in La Défense: Holiday Inn, Staybridge and Crowne Plaza. A total of 700 rooms as well as a restaurant, spa and pool, shops and 200-metre-high rooftop (December 2022) Opening of a SLO Living Hostel facing the Halle Freyssinet in the 13th arrondissement, with 179 beds (2023) Opening of the first 1 Hotel in Europe, facing the Halle Freyssinet in the 13th arrondissement, with sustainable development values. 140 rooms, cafe, gym, spa and flower shop (2023) Opening on Boulevard Pershing at Porte Maillot of a 250-room 4* hotel (2023)  Opening at the tip of Seguin island of a luxurious 220-room hotel with bar, restaurant, pool and spa, on a contemporary art theme: a room / a piece of art (2023) Opening at Porte de Pantin of a 109-room hotel by Louvre Hotels group, in the heart of a disabled sports complex. The rooms will be fully accessible, with disability facilities (summer 2024)  Opening of a 120-room 4* hotel in the Triangle tower at Porte de Versailles (2024) Opening by Helzéar group of serviced apartments at 7 Rue des Grands-Augustins in the 6th arrondissement, in the same building where in 1937 Picasso painted Guernica (date to be announced) Opening in Rue de Grenelle in Saint-Germain-des-Prés as well as in Rue Saint-Roch of two boutique hotels by Zadig et Voltaire, with restaurants and boutiques, with a concept of confidentiality and exclusivity (date to be announced) Opening at the foot of Montmartre hill in the 18th arrondissement of several apartments with hotel services (date to be announced) Opening by the Hilton group in Marne-la-Vallée, near Disneyland® Paris, of a Hilton Garden Inn (date to be announced) Opening in Saint-Ouen in the Flea Market of a 170-room hotel (date to be announced) Opening in Saint-Ouen of a sustainable hotel, code name 11Bauer (date to be announced) Opening in Rue Jean Goujon in the 8th arrondissement of a high-end 13-room hotel in a listed 1907 private mansion redesigned by Jean-Michel Wilmotte (date to be announced) Opening of a 5* hotel at 3 Rue Laromiguière in the 5th arrondissement, with 80 rooms, spa, pool and restaurant (date to be announced) Opening after full transformation of the former Hôtel du 7e art, renamed Charles V hotel, at 20 Rue Saint-Paul in the Marais. 4* category requested (date to be announced) Opening of deluxe serviced apartments in the former Gulbenkian town house, as well as a gallery, at 51 Iéna Avenue in the 16th arrondissement (date to be announced) Opening of a 147-room So Sofitel and 400-bed youth hostel in the former Morland Tower in the 4th arrondissement (date to be announced) Opening of a deluxe hotel in one of the twin Hermitage Towers at la Défense (date to be announced) Opening of a 169-room hotel in the Latin Quarter, at 7 Rue Lacépède / 18 Rue Larrey with restaurant and pool, 4* category requested (date to be announced) Opening of a 123-room hotel at 16 Avenue d'Eylau in the 16th arrondissement, off Trocadéro; 4* category requested (date to be announced)  Opening of a MOB Hotel near Gare de l'Est train station (date to be announced)  Opening at 8 Rue Armand Carrel in the 19th, near Buttes Chaumont park, of a hotel dedicated to athletes (date to be announced)  Opening of a hostel at 48 Rue Sedaine in the 11th arrondissement (date to be announced)Closures for renovationClosure for full renovation of the Wallace Hotel in the 15th arrondissement (until late 2019) Closure for full transformation into a Marvel-themed hotel of the hôtel New York in Disneyland Paris (until 2020) Closure for renovation of the hôtel Lotti****; reopening as part of the Costes group, 5* category requested (reopening date to be announced) Closure for complete renovation of Nord et Champagne Hotel** at 11 Rue Chabrol, near Gare de l'Est. Upon reopening, the hotel will offer 39 rooms (date to be announced) Closure for renovation of the Méridional hotel in the 17th arrondissement. Had 38 rooms, will go down to 33 (spring 2019 until early 2020)Renovations without closureRenovation of the Hôtel Scribe (until fall 2019) Extension by 36 rooms of the Snob hotel (late 2020)  Renovation of the Normandy Hotel (until 2021)  Renovation of the Bradford Élysées hotel**** (date to be announced) Renovation by Les hôtels EMERAUDE of the Lodge du Centre*** (date to be announced)
