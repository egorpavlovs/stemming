                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The friendly atmosphere of a bouchon lyonnais a few steps away from l’Opera Garnier !
A quality and traditional establishment since 1890, this Parisian bouchon interprets fabulous recipes from Lyon with a light and modern twist. From the north of Chablis to the south of Avignon, the wine list takes you on a journey throughout the domains that have made the reputation of the Burgundy region and the Rhone valley.
