                        Description
                        
                                                    
        A stone's throw from the Place Vendôme, the Musée du Louvre, and the Opéra district, the Hôtel Royal Saint-Honoré is ideally situated in the heart of Paris, home of luxury fashion boutiques.
The 64 rooms and 4 suites with refined decoration offer air conditioning, safe, Pay TV, international channels, free internet access.
The hotel has a restaurant, Café 221, with a warm, intimite atmosphere. A vaulted basement lounge offers allows for private meetings in a studious, refined atmosphere.
