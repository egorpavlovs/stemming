                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The InterContinental Paris le Grand, built under Napoleon III, is located in the heart of Paris: opposite the Palais Garnier Opera House and a few steps from the biggest Parisian attractions, department stores, and the magnificent Place Vendôme. The hotel has 470 guestrooms, all richly decorated with colours inspired by Opéra, and the Verrière offers a welcoming haven of peace and quiet in the heart of the buzzing city.
