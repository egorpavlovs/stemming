                        Description
                        
                                                    
        At 30 km from Paris, this 4 stars apparthotel has an ideal location for your business trips. Our residence is conveniently situated in the heart of the Val d’Europe business area, and close to Disneyland®.
For your business meetings the residence offers a 45 m equipped room as well as subcommittee rooms. And to finish the day, relax in the residence bar and dine in the Brasserie Flo®, our partner restaurant.
