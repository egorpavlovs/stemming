                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Welcome to Memories France !
We are a new operator in Paris offering high quality guided tours and experiences in and around the city. 
We operate FIT, group and private tours of the major sites and monuments in English - often combined with gourmet meals - as well as lesser known sites and authentic local experiences. 
Based locally in Paris, we know our city inside out.  We work closely with the museums and monuments and have carefully sourced a network of quality, local suppliers and a group of expert and passionate licensed guides. Our booking engine and API connection allow for immediate, real time booking confirmations. We can customize tours and develop itineraries according to your requests. We can also put together a wide variety of packages and arrange transportation and pick up and drop off services. 
Our team of experts is at your disposal ready to assist you ! 
Don’t miss tours such as our exclusive wine tour, a day in the footsteps of Napoleon or tours designed specially for families with young children.
                            
                        
                                                

    
        
        
    
            
            
                Clients
            
            
                Leisure groups, Target individuals
            
        
                
            
                Description of leisure groups
            
            
                
        With our team of friendly local guides, leisure groups can visit world famous museums and monuments, take a stroll through the most charming and typical neighborhoods of Paris, share and experience local food and wine, or enjoy gourmet meals in spectacular settings. Memories can customize or develop tours to your group’s requirements and put together a wide variety of packages for you. We can also arrange all transportation services. Let us take care of all your arrangements and ensure your groups have quality and unforgettable experiences in our beautiful city.
