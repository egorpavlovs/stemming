                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Welcome to the Relais Spa Val d'Europe de Chessy, where the team are happy to welcome you 24/7. Ideally situated close to Disneyland Paris, the residence is also near the Val d'Europe shopping centre and the Vallée Village Outlet Shopping Park.
The Flo brasserie is open 7 days a week for breakfast, lunch and dinner in a welcoming atmosphere. The menu will thrill you with its varied, traditional French cuisine. Each of the bedrooms has a desk space, an equipped kitchen (creamic hobs, refrigerator, microwave, espresso machine, dishwasher, washer...), a bathroom, a flat-screen TV and safe that a laptop can fit into. Each room also has individual air-conditioning and free broadband access. The SPA - an 800m² space with a pool, sauna, hammam, whirlpool bath and a sports hall - offers you NUXE treatments.
