                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The historic PLM Saint-Jacques, re-baptized as Paris Marriott Rive Gauche Hotel & Conference Center in 2006, proposes multiple services. Easy access – nearby 2 metro stations and linked to both international Parisian airports - enables to host guests conveniently.
Ranked as 4 star category with 757 rooms, a restaurant, bar and more than 4800 sqm of meeting space, the hotel can offer all-inclusive options for meetings.
The conference center of the Paris Marriott Rive Gauche has been fully renovated in 2014, now featuring 55 flexible meeting rooms adapted to all types of events (seminars, exhibitions, convention, fairs, formal gala dinners or celebrations).
With our special “Red Coat” service on site, your events will be perfectly coordinated from beginning to end.
