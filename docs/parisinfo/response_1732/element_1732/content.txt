                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Located a few minutes from Charles de Gaulle International Airport, our hotel offers the charm of an Anglo-Saxon architecture.
It combines with a unique sense of style and character, modern amenities, a heated swimming pool and a fitness center.
The hotel offers exceptional tranquility while offering easy access to all Paris attractions such as the Villepinte Exhibition Center, Disneyland Paris and Parc Astérix.
Each of the seven air-conditioned meeting rooms provides a natural daylight, a host of advanced technical equipment and superior sound-proofing. 
Dedicated meetings and events team
Complimentary Wi-Fi connectivity
Natural daylight in all meeting rooms 
Audio-visual support
Multifunctional lighting
Dedicated reception and foyer areas
Syndicate and breakout rooms available
Italian Restaurant “Da Vicenzo”/ Bar Jules Verne/ The Pub
24 hour business centre

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: car park with specific disabled spaces, lowered reception desk with leg room underneath, 5 adapted rooms, bathrooms equipped with toilets with sapce to manoeuvre and gab rails, restaurant with aisles wider than 1.4 m.
- Hearing impairment: online booking and payment on the website
- Mental disability: trained and educated staff, main entrance monitored with CCTV and visible from the reception desk.
