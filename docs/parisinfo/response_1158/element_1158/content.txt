                            Disneyland Paris and its surrounding areas 
                            Did you know? You can plan your event at the Disney Events Arena. You can also enjoy a teambuilding in the park.
Discover also many activities (shopping, accommodations...) near to the park!
                        

                        Disneyland Paris Park is home to the Disney Events Arena (Disney Business Solutions), with a surface area of 7,200 m², capable of hosting up to 6,000 people for a cocktail reception. At the end of your event, you can enjoy a fun getaway in the theme park with for example a giant digital treasure hunt for a memorable teambuilding event!For a shopping spree near to the Park, head to Vallée Village, home to some 1,200 prestigious fashion and luxury brands. With its 916 cottages and apartments and one of the largest water parks in Europe (11,500 m²), Villages Nature® Paris also welcomes you for a leisure break with a focus on nature, sport and relaxation.
