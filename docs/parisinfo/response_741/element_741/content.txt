                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Campanile Roissy, your best 3-stars experience at Roissy Charles de Gaulle !
In our renovated 269 rooms, you will find complimentary tea and coffee, unlimited Wifi access, high quality bedding and reversible air conditioning. 
Just 5 kilometers away from Charles de Gaulle Airport and train station, our hotel is served by the « Pink Line » shuttle from 5 a.m to midnight. Also close to Paris Nord Villepinte Exhibition Center, it is easily accessible by public transport (RER B, bus) or by car thanks to the A1 motorway (Exit Louvres/Goussainville).
Our all-you-can-eat American buffet breakfast is served from 4 a.m to 10 a.m and our 160-seats restaurant is opened from 12 to 2.30 p.m and from 7 p.m to 11 p.m, offering a large choice of cold and hot buffets.
As for our 4 adjustable meeting rooms with daylight, they can accommodate up to 200 people and include free internet access, paperboards, beamers and screens. Other equipments can be included on demand (microphones, PA System, Translation…).
