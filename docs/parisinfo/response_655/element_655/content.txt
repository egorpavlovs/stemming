                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Espace Champerret is an accessible and intimate site. Each year, it hosts professional events as well as those for the general public, held in three independent and complementary exhibition spaces that total 8,450 sq. m. The site is built around a central plaza that functions as a meeting-place where everyone feels immediately at ease.
