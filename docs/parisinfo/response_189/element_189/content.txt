                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Doobie's is a party space, in the magical "Golden Triangle" and a few steps from the prestigious Champs Elysees, a charming place, a cozy universe out of fashion but always in the spirit of time adapted to a very wide range of events, both family and professional, conducive to the celebration of the success of trade shows and seminars, open to the world of fashion, arts and business.  The warm and flexible space offers private lounges from 14 to 50 guests and offers in its entirety to receive 140 guests for sit-down dinner, 150 for buffet dinners and up to 250 for cocktail dinners.  
The privatization of doobia's can be partial or total.
