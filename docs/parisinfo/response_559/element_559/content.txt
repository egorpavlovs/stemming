                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Theatre in Paris makes the very best shows in Paris accessible to international visitors, and creates exclusive and immersive events in Parisian playhouses. 
Unbeknownst to non-French-speakers, Parisian theatre is the city’s best-kept secret. From classic plays and comedies with state-of-the-art subtitling technology, to musicals or magical performances, our specialty is offering the most popular Parisian shows, with no language barrier. Among our iconic playhouses are historical monuments commissioned by French royalty as well as the most vibrant café-théâtres in town. 
We arrange events from 10 to 4,000 guests, offering a range of tailored services surrounding a theatre experience. Dinner or cocktail reception at the theatre, privatisation of a playhouse, subtitling in any language: anything is possible!
