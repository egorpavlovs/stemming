                        Description
                        
                                                    
        On the left bank of the Seine, the André Latin Hotel is located on the border of the 5th arrondissements, opposite the Panthéon, amongst the trees of the Quartier Latin and the Luxembourg Garden. An address easily accessible using the RER Line B or the Place Monge Metro station. Our 3-star hotel with contemporary décor is ideal for your professional trip at any time of the day. Relax in one of our comfortable air-conditionned rooms.
All of our staff are available to ensure that your business trip runs smoothly. After a good night's sleep in one of our comfortable beds, enjoy a hearty and delicious buffet breakfast in a welcoming setting to build up the energy you'll need to meet the day head-on. National and international newspapers, free Wi-Fi access throughout the hotel, a bar, a concierge service. We do everything it takes to ensure that you are as prepared as you can be to get your work done.
