                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Located on the Avenue des Gobelins at the crossroads of the 13th and 5th arrondissement of Paris, the Jérôme Seydoux-Pathé Foundation's headquarters opened in 2014. On the site of an old theater and a cinema, the new ensemble by Renzo Piano preserves the historic facade sculpted by Auguste Rodin. Behind, stands a five-storey glass high in the middle of a garden. The building includes reception areas and a projection room with a capacity of 70 places that can be privatized for all types of private events. Maximum capacity: 250 people. Quote on demand.
