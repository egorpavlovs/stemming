                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A new modern venue in the heart of the up-and-coming Saint-Ouen flea market district.
12 fully equipped daylight meeting rooms showcasing the movement through modular and adaptable furniture. 2500m² dedicated to companies for your one-day meetings, brainstorming sessions or training coursess for 2 to 100 participants.
Enjoy our convivial spaces and an outdoor terrace for your afterworks, cocktails and dinners to exchange informally!
