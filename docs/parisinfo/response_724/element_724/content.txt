                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Ideally located in the grandiose surroundings of the Château de Versailles and close to Paris, Trianon Palace Versailles is a peaceful country retreat, offering ideal settings for any event. Should you wish to plan a grand reception, an intimate private Michelin starred dinner in our “Gordon Ramsay au Trianon” restaurant, a director’s board meeting or a large symposium, we have a tailor made solution to cater for your every requirement. The historical building with 100 bedrooms offers a wide variety of fine dining options including outside dining in the extensive grounds of the hotel.
The Pavillon du Trianon Conference Center is a purpose built modern 4* conference center located in the Pavillon building offering 1000m² of flexible meeting space and 99 bedrooms.
The 2800 m² Spa Guerlain offers a range of treatments and activities adapted to groups. Our team also organizes great team building ideas such as a Segway tour in the Royal domain.
