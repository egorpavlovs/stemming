                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Like the playful constructions of the parks and gardens of the 18th century, 26 follies liven up Villette with their bright red coat. These small buildings of 40 to 200 m² can create events in the heart of the park. They can also be put at your disposal in addition to the rental of the Grande Halle or outdoor spaces.
Adjoining the Great Hall, this Folly is composed of a room with large circular windows, a work area and / or meeting upstairs and a terrace with breathtaking views of the park. The building opens on a forecourt extended by the biggest lawn of Paris, the meadow of the Triangle, with its 20,400 m².

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: car parking spaces available to reserve 24 hours in advance. All the spaces are accessible.
- Hearing impairment: Reception has an agent with LSF (French Sign Language) and a magnetic hearing loop.
- Inellectual disability: trained staff, easy-to-understand signage with pictograms.
More information at: https://lavillette.com/villette/informations-pratiques/politique-daccessibilite-villette/
