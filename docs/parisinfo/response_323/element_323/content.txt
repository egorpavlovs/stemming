                            Description
                            
                                
        An innovative gift and souvenir service offering the best of authentic “Made in Paris” artisanal foods in modern, sustainable packaging.
Are you looking for an original alternative to classic fine food brands for your event or business gift? 
Products of Paris offers a carefully curated selection of delicious, standout “Made in Paris” artisan foods, handcrafted by independent, small-scale artisans who showcase the very best of Parisian know-how and excellence.
Delivered straight to you in Paris, our gourmet selections are packaged in smart, environmentally friendly jute bags that make for ideal modern keepsakes of the City of Light. Each bag comes with cards telling the tale of the terroir and artisan behind each product. 
Whether for welcome packs or seasonal business gifts, our delicious, sustainable and socially responsible selections are sure to please.
We are always happy to tailor bags to specific requirements or budgets.
