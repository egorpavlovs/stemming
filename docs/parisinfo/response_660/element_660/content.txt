   
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Big events, a private reception, a prestigious transaction, a cocktail party, or a gala dinner: in France or overseas, Lenôtre organises more than 6,500 events every year. To the culinary emotions of haute couture gastronomy is added the experience of a setting, a staging and a service: Lenôtre's passion for the art of entertaining that turns every reception into a unique, exclusive party.
