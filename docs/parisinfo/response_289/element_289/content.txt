                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Molitor Paris by MGalley has taken over Paris's most famous swimming pool: Piscine Molitor. This hotel is arranged around the two huge pools of this site listed as a historical monument. Molitor Paris by MGallery has 124 rooms, including 19 suites, two restaurants, a bar, a rooftop cafe, a spa ... not forgetting use of the heated swimming pools (indoor and outdoor). The services and comfort match the high quality of this exceptional hotel.
