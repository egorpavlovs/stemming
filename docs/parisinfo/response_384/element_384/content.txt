                                                    Description
                            
                                
        One of the leading venues on the Champs Elysées, the Pavillon Gabriel is a splendid location that offers a range of spaces that can be transformed to suit your needs. With 1,200sqm² of rooms and 500sqm² of terraces linked to a television studio, the Pavillon can be reserved for prestigious events accommodating 10 to 2,000 guests. It proposes everything one could hope for to ensure the success of any private or corporate reception.
