                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Sofitel Paris le Faubourg puts your event under the spotlight, in an exclusive spot at the crossroads of art, fashion and politics. Prestige of the hotel, charm of meeting rooms with day light, terraces to hold breaks, a chic and trendy cuisine... Make every event an exceptional one.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        Motor disability: ground floor entrance or entrance with sloping surface, toilet facilities adapted and equipped with transfer bar and sink adapted for wheelchair users, 4 bedrooms adapted with bathroom equipped with floor-level shower (with folding or movable seat), a sink adapted for wheelchair users, toilet with lateral space and transfer bar, dining area with aisles wider than 1.4 metres and with adapted tables (height of 70cm so as not to obstruct wheelchair users).
Hearing disability: booking and/or payments available to do online, flashing lights when fire alarm sounds.
Mental disability: entrance under surveillance and visible from the reception desk.
