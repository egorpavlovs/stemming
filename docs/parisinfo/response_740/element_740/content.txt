                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Best Western Plus Paris Meudon Ermitage is located in Meudon-la-Forêt close to Paris on the edge of Meudon Forest, 800m from Inovel Parc Vélizy economic hub. 
The hotel provides 110 modern and spacious guest rooms with 4 categories with or without balcony perfectly furnished: individual air conditioning, phone, desk, Wifi, TV (Satellit, Canal+, BeIn), bathroom (air drying), minibar.
With 600m2 of meeting spaces renovated, the Best Western Plus Paris Meudon Ermitage is the perfect place to organize your professional events: Seminars, delegate day, business meeting, business meal, training…
The hotel provides 11 meeting rooms furnished (WIFI fiber, video projector, paperboard, notepads, pens, waters) and benefiting from daylight.
The Ermitage restaurant offers you home made cooking with season products. Our Chef prepares you the perfect meal according to your event: cocktail, buffet, at the plate lunch, barbecue outdoor…
