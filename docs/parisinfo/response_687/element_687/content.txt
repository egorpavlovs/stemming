                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Ideally located in the heart of Paris, just a few minutes' walk from the Champs-Elysées, the Hotel du Collectionneur is the largest 5 stars hotel in Paris. Its unique Art Deco style inspired by the transatlantic liners, invites you to rediscover the splendour of the 30s. With 1,700 m² (18,300 ft²) of meeting rooms and a superb reception room, the hotel can host events for up to 650 people. It has been awarded « France’s Leading Business and Conference Hotel 2014 » at the World Travel Awards. This luxurious hotel offers 487 rooms and suites, a spa, an executive lounge, as well as a French restaurant and a Parisian bar.
