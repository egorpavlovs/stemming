                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A prestigious 18th century mansion a short walk from the Musée d'Orsay, the Hôtel de Poulpry is situated between the Paris of ministries and the Paris of embassies. The building has preserved the imposing architecture and the decoration of the Second Empire. Completely restored, it offers all the comfort of the 21st century for your events.
