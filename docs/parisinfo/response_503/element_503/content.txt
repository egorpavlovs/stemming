                            Accommodation for young people and school groups
                            Accommodation for young visitors and school groups.
                        

                        With 500 beds, the Fiap Jean-Monnet encourages exchanges between young guests of all nationalities by offering a large number of cultural activities. 200 single, double, family and shared (up to 7 persons) bedrooms are available for groups and FITs of all ages, in the heart of the Left Bank in the 14th arrondissement. Bar, restaurant, cafeteria and terrace are inviting for relaxing. 23 meeting rooms from 8 to 200 seats, fully-equipped and connected, are available.Close to Charonne area and Père Lachaise, the Résidence Internationale de Paris, fully renovated in 2015, counts 330 beds in 102 single, double, family and shared (1-4 beds) rooms. A gourmet club, a brasserie, a cafeteria as well as 8 meeting rooms from 10 to 250 seats, complete the offer.At the edge of Paris, the two Centres internationaux de séjour Paris-Kellermann and Maurice-Ravel offer 363 and 194 beds in rooms of 1 to 8 persons, as well as restaurant areas. In a park, Kellermann center has 3 meeting rooms from 20 to 30 seats as well as a private bus parking. Maurice-Ravel center has 10 meeting rooms from 20 to 230 seats, an art gallery and a theater.For additional information on these addresses or for more group accommodation solutions, don't hesitate to contact us.
