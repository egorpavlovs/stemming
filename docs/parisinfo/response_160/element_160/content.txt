                            Chateau for a wedding in Paris
                            When you get married in a chateau just outside the capital, you are guaranteed a lavish wedding in an exceptional setting.
                        

                        Looking to do things on a grandiose scale and leave a lasting impression of a wonderful wedding?So, whether it be in the private reception rooms of a 17th-century chateau or within the grounds of a historic property, go ahead and plan the wedding of your dreams!And whether you are planning a dinner, a cocktail party, a reception or an evening function with dancing, you can take advantage of high-end and bespoke services. See below our selection of chateaux in the Paris region that can host your prestige wedding and make it a moment to remember.
