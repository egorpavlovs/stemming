                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The hotel of Rohan-Guéménée, place of Vosges in Paris is the place where Victor Hugo ( 1802-1885 ) stayed longer ( 1832-1848 ). He is then recognized as the leader of the Romanticism. He writes some of his major works there of which the sketch of the Miserable.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        -Physical disability: accessibility to different spaces.
-Visual impairment: magnified information devices, signs and documentation in Braille and large writing.
-Audio impairment: video guide in French Sign Language, reception and video room with hearing loop. 
-Mental disability: easy-to-follow visit guide.
For more information, go to http://parismusees.paris.fr/fr/votre-visite/accueil-des-visiteurs-en-situation-dhandicap
