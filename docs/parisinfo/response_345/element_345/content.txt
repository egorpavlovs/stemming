                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Opposite the Tuileries Gardens and just steps away from the Louvre, the Westin Paris – Vendôme welcomes you in to its 428 bedrooms and suites. Our meeting rooms are amongst the most beautiful in Paris and can accommodate up to 1,200 people for prestigious events of all kinds. Le First, restaurant boudoir Paris invites you to taste an authentic and inventive cuisine. Experience our outdoor haven of tranquility in the heart of Paris – La Terrasse and its restaurant, whether in winter or summer style. Recharge your batteries in the WestinWORKOUT® fitness centre, as well as the first Six Senses spa in France.
