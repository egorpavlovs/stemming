                                                    Description
                            
                                
        For more than 15 years, Paris Bike Tour has been organising bicycle tours of Paris and the surrounding area. Our multilingual professional conference guides accompany you starting either from our optimally situated agency in the centre of Paris or from the departure point of your choice thanks to our exclusive delivery service. 
From fun and original bike tours to well-planned combined visits or challenging rallies, your can offer your clients or business partners a well-earned break from it all. We offer a wide range of activities and services perfectly adapted to the profile and needs of the participants:
Classic bicycle tours (half-day): Paris en Seine, Paris Éternel, Paris des Parisiens
Team-building and goal oriented events designed to build incentive and initiative: bike tours combined with games, rallies, quizzes, pétanque, speed-boat ride…
Combined tours with food tasting and/or museum/monument: Bike tour + Louvre ...
Made-to-measure itineraries catering to your wishes thanks to the expertise of our guides and their love of Paris. 
Logistical management of all aspects of high quality services: breakfast, picnic, awarding of prizes…
Provision of official conference guides and/or accompaniers in French, English, German, Dutch, Spanish and Portuguese. 
Availability of our large fleet of bicycles, with or without support staff, for all types of events (fleet transfer, street marketing…)								
Bicycle rental/delivery and pick-up anywhere in Paris and the surrounding area
Large scale or small, our experienced and skilled team applies its know-how to reach your objectives and realize the full potential of your unique event and better serve your needs.
