                                                    Description
                            
                                
        City Monceau Velasquez, a private mansion that will transport your participants in the 19th century ! The theme of the "Grand Voyageur" is unveiled with a glimpse of deco and travel memories. This Cabinet of curiosities on the edge of Parc Monceau will host your meetings during your day meeting or half-day meeting from 2 to 40 participants. At the end of the day, the old ballroom will be the ideal place for an informal moment, a cocktail or a dinner!
