                            Retro Tour: a drive around Paris in a sidecar
                            Discover the City of Light in a whole new way, by sidecar!
                        

                        Léon, Marcel and André are the names of the Retro Tour retro-vintage style sidecars.On your own or as couple choose one of the motorbike and sidecars for an offbeat tour of the capital. Let the gentleman sidecarists be your guide; they are true Parisians who are passionate about the city and will ensure that you have a unique experience peppered with unusual and interesting anecdotes. Different itineraries are offered so that you can enjoy the sights of Paris by day and by night. The Retro classic trip lasts 40 minutes and enables you to discover or rediscover the capital’s top monuments. The Great Escape (1hr30) is the ideal choice for getting off the beaten track and seeing unusual places. Enjoy the City of Light at night by choosing the Retro by Night tour (1hr30). Finally, for some freewheeling, customize your very own tour to suit you with the Tailor Cut ‘sur-mesure’ tour (3hr30 to 7hr). To facilitate communication between all the passengers, each helmet has a Bluetooth connection. Plus, the sidecar has a heated seat and a blanket, and rain jackets and gloves are provided in the event of bad weather.
