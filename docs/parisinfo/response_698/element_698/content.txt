                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In the heart of Paris, ideally located between the renowned Avenue Montaigne and the majestic Grand Palais, lies an exceptional place : the Hôtel Le Marois - France Amériques.
Behind the doors of this typical Haussmannian mansion, discover 8 lounges decorated with 18th century woodwork and paintings and decorated with contemporary furniture. This prestigious 850 square meters venue is accessible via an outstanding formal staircase and offers a magnificent terrace facing the Palais de la Découverte.
In private or up to 700 guests, the Hôtel Le Marois magnifies your business and private events in the very heart of the City of Light.
