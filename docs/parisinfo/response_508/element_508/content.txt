                        Description
                        
                                                    
        On the Left Bank just a short walk from the Eiffel Tower, this sleek, 4-star hotel has become a prime destination for business travellers. Ideal for work meetings, equally suited for socialising, the Pullman Paris Tour Eiffel is currently undergoing a complete transformation under the watchful eye of designer Christophe Pillet. All our 430 non-smoking rooms recall the effortless urban elegance of Paris. We made them much more than a place to sleep. The stylish, design rooms provide an ideal environment to relax, work, play or simply unwind and do nothing. The Room by Pullman: broadband internet connection, cordless phone, connection control panel, Bose ® docking station and an iPad® tablet, flat screen LCD TV of 42” or more in all rooms. The Pullman Paris Tour Eiffel has 23 meeting spaces that can be configured for any function.Looking to make a grand impression? Give your guests a view of Trocadéro and the Eiffel Tower in the conference and food & beverage facilities up on the 10th floor. New in 2014: 9th floor Fitness Lounge fully equipped with the latest cardio and fitness training machines. Traditional tables and comfortable armchairs, bar stools and coffee tables set in lounge style decor invite you to take a break, an as you like it Frame restaurant break. At least, stay connected: complimentary, fiber optic powered high speed WiFi (200 mega).
