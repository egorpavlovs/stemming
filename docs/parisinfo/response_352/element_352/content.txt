                            Paris Convention Centre: the largest conference centre in Europe!
                            The biggest convention centre in Europe has opened in the heart of the French capital.



                        

                        Paris confirms its position as the world’s leading capital for congresses with a vast new facility. Inaugurated on 22 November, the Paris Convention Centre, in Hall 7 at the Parc des Expositions-Porte de Versailles, is now the largest convention centre in Europe, in terms of its size (72,000 m²), and capacity (up to 35,000 seats). This new venue also has a 5,200-seat plenary room with a view of the Eiffel Tower and a planted terrace. The challenge is bound up with the modernization of Paris Expo Porte de Versailles. ‘The common thread of our architectural project for Pavilion 7 was to give new life to a huge 1970s building and to install cutting-edge facilities and equipment for events’, explains Jean Pistre, the architect in charge of the project.Jean-François Martins, Deputy Mayor of Paris in charge of Sport and Tourism, comments on the objectives of this major new facility that is set to be a true accelerator for the Parisian economy.
