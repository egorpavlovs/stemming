                            La Place: a venue devoted to Fintech in central Paris
                            The second floor of the prestigious Palais Brongniart is now home to La Place (400 m²), where the goal is to bring start-ups and big corporations together to encourage innovation and tie-ups. 
                        

                        This new venue inside the Palais Brongniart has become an important rendezvous for finance, banking and insurance professionals, a platform for dialogue for new start-ups and big companies, and a convivial and vibrant spot with many events and activities such as talks, after-work events and feedback sessions.It benefits from a privileged location within the heart of the former Paris stock exchange – a listed historic monument since 1987 –, and is also available for private hire. The space has been renovated  and designed around the former trading floor and is ideally suited to small events: the Corbeille can host up to 30 people and the Business Lounge, up to 55 people. A warm and welcoming setting with comfortable spaces for your event in the capital!The Palais Brongniart has a total surface area of 3,000 m² for large scale events.
