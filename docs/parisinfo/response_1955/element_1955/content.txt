                                                                                    Description
                        
                            Paris Fintech Forum, biggest European event on digital finance, present during two days all key issues at the heart of the news of digital finance in general and Fintech in particular. In 2019, 2 600 people attended the event, and were spread out in 4 different exceptional stages, 2 exhibition halls, 2 workshop rooms, and 8 networking lounges. There were more than 220 speakers, mostly CEOs and directors of bank and insurance companies, but also more than 150 international Fintechs. This event takes place at Palais Brongniart - GL Events.
