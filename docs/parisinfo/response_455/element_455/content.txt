                             The Hotel Banke Paris unlocks its Salle des Coffres!
                            This 5-star boutique hotel, a few steps from the Grands Magasins (department stores) and Palais Garnier Opera House, is opening its Salle des Coffres (a timeless hidden vault) for private events.

                        

                        Housed in a former diamond bank with a Haussmann facade, the Hotel Banke Paris, inaugurated in 2009, has retained many features of the building’s prestigious past including former bank counters, railings in wrought iron on the first floor, a private lift, but also a truly unique place that until now has been closed to the public…Descend into the basement of the establishment and step into the sublime and mysterious Salle des Coffres (250m²). In total more than 450 safes (one of which apparently contained the wealth of a certain Gustave Eiffel) line the walls.Most of them are still sealed ...Let the imagination of your guests run wild! After four years of renovation work to make it accessible, this atypical space with its unique cachet welcomes any type of event up to 180 people: cocktail parties, exhibitions, meetings, concerts, etc. It has a private entrance.The hotel also offers several optional facilities and services such as security, a cloakroom, the rental of furniture and decor, and even entertainment.
