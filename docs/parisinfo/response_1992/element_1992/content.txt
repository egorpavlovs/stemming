                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Opened recently, Paris Event Center, located at Porte de la Villette in Paris intra-muros has grown to become a major event venue in Paris. PARIS EVENT CENTER welcomed last year major events as Parties, TV Shows, music festivals, Trade and Consumer Fairs, congresses and Conventions, and fashion shows.  
In figures, Paris Event Center is 12,250 m², split over 2 buildings : 1 Building (B) of 8000 m² and 1 Building (A) of 4250 m², plus 25,000 m² of outdoor space.
The facility is ERP classification (Class L, T, N and R) and meets the accessibility requirements for people with disabilities (access ramps, toilets for people with reduced mobility, visual and sensory markings). Paris Event Center offers around 350 parking spaces on site (depending on the configuration of your event). In addition, there are 2 large public car parks available in close proximity.
Well located and easily accessible, with direct transport links (Metro, Buses and Tramway).
