                                                    Description
                            
                                
        L’Île is a restaurant built inside an old gunpowder magazine, located in the Ile Saint Germain park since 1998. This exquisite Napoleon III pavilion has become an amazing place with a decor that combines iron, glass and flourishing vegetation.
Next to the restaurant, Le Pavillon de l’Île, a 200m2 function room, is the ideal place to organize any kind of event, overlooking the magnificent park.
