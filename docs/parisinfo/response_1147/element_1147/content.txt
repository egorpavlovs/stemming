                            Introducing the newly-renovated Hotel Sofitel Paris Baltimore Tour Eiffel!
                            After an 8-month makeover, the Sofitel Paris Baltimore Tour Eiffel has a new look and unveils a fully-refurbished restaurant, bar, lobby, corridors and guest rooms.
                        

                        Ideally situated on Avenue Kléber, a stone’s throw from the Arc de Triomphe, the Hotel Sofitel Paris Baltimore Tour Eiffel becomes the 5th Sofitel in Paris. The establishment, which has a Haussmannian-style facade, is the epitome of Parisian elegance. Comfort and modernity are in evidence as soon as you step into the lobby with its mohair, taffeta and velvet furnishings and cutting-edge technology like the brand new dynamic digital screen nicknamed TOM — providing guests with a rare new type of connectivity for a European hotel. The screen is an interactive Welcome Wall, a veritable virtual ‘advisor’ for finding out more about the hotel, planning your stay in Paris, following the latest news or having your photograph taken.The 103 guest rooms, including 27 junior suites and the splendid suite Eiffel on the 6th floor, are furnished with high-quality materials in harmonious colours creating a warm relaxing atmosphere.4 meeting rooms (from 36 to 102 m²) are available for events up to 90 people. Gourmet cuisine is on the menu at the ‘Carte Blanche’ restaurant which serves contemporary cooking made from seasonal produce. The chef Jean-Philippe Perol, who has previously worked in prestigious institutions, proposes a new menu every month. Organize a lunch or business dinner and enjoy an elegant dining experience in this stylish setting with its round wooden tables, velvet upholstered seats and beautiful lights. Finally, the lobby bar, open daily from 8am to midnight, also offers a catering service (smoked salmon, burgers, Caesar salad, tarts and cakes, etc.) and a drinks menu of creative cocktails in an intimate and friendly atmosphere.
