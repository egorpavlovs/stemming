                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Au Cadet de Gascogne has the capacity to welcome your groups to discover the authentic Montmartre (266 seats in total).
Georges Guinot, the Chef cooks French cuisine as snails, beef Burgundy, Saint-Marcellin’s cheese roasted, Upside down apple pie... tradition and home made cuisine.
Breakfast, lunch and diner, for business or leisure, your group would enjoy the intimate atmosphere of our room on the first floor (100 seats). This one has a view on the place du Tertre and its artists.
Equipped with a projector, a screen and a TV, you could also organize meetings.
We offer animations too as music during your diner, with our pianist and singer or you could visit the Vineyards of Montmartre or you go head for a caricature.
Contact our commercial service to know more about menus.
