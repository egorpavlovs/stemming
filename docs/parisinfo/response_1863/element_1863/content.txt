                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Right in the heart of Paris, opposite the Tuileries Gardens and a few minutes’ walk from the Louvre, Place Vendôme, Place de la Concorde, and the Palais Garnier Opera House, the Saint James & Albany Paris Hotel-Spa offers 175 bedrooms and suites as well as 700 m² of adaptable seminar rooms devoted to business tourism. With its brand new 500 m² spa and indoor pool, this 17th-century hotel is set around two private terraces for moments of peace and relaxation.
