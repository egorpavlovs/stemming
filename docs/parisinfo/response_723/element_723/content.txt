                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Stay connected in the heart of Roissy CDG International airport. Walking distance from boarding gates, CDG Val airport shuttle and the RER / TGV station (connected to Paris in 30 minutes), the hotel features 252 welcoming and fully soundproof guest rooms, all equipped with Sheraton Signature Sleep Experience. Meet customers and colleagues in one of the 15 high-tech meeting spaces for up to 80 attendees. Discover a refined cuisine accompanied by the greatest wines at Les Etoiles restaurant, a fresh and colourful stopover at Les Saisons restaurant or a welcoming and sporty break at the Galaxy Bar. Relax in the warm environment of the Club Lounge or stay fit at the Sheraton Fitness Centre.
