                            The Atelier H: the new events venue at the Novotel Paris Les Halles
                            A 2-story establishment with industrial style decoration reminiscent of the atmosphere of Baltard’s Les Halles: Welcome to the Atelier H, an innovative events venue!
                        

                        The Novotel Paris Les Halles Hotel has created a new concept based on a spirit of freedom and simplicity. The Atelier H can host a wide range of events including meetings, product launches, showrooms, private lunches or dinners, and photo shoots.On the ground floor (350 m²) are the Halles 1, Halles 2, the Rotunda, the Arcade and the Studio Jenkins. The first floor comprises several salons with a surface area ranging from 18 m² to 55 m² and which can host from 6 to 30 people. Each of them makes reference to a great inventor and is individually decorated. There are, for example, vinyl records in the Berliner Studio and even vintage phones in the Studio Bell.The hotel’s leafy patio is also accessible and available for full private hire on request. Throughout the day, there are areas to enjoy refreshments prepared by the hotel’s cooks. The Atelier H is more than a simple meeting area; it is a true living space.
