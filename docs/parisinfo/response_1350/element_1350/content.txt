                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Available for private hire in the evening (except on Thursdays) for your professional events (gala, reception, dinner, etc.). The Musée des Beaux-Arts de la Ville de Paris has 3 meeting rooms for up to 600 people for a cocktail reception. Built for the 1900 Universal Exposition, situated on Avenue Winston Churchill,  the Petit Palais offers a vast artistic panorama ranging from Antiquity to the beginning of the 20th century.

                            AcadémiqueXIXè siècle

                            Objets d'artPeintureSculptureTapisseries
                        
                        
                        
                                                Garden
                        
        Access to garden and cafe, 'Le jardin du Petit Palais', during museum opening hours.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physically disabled: Street-level access
- Visually impaired: Touch tours and workshops.
- Hearing impaired: Tours in French Sign Language and lip reading.
- Mentally disabled: Tours, stories and workshops.
- Free for disabled people and an accompanying person upon presentation of proof.
- Find all the information about adapted events on www.arianeinfo.org.
