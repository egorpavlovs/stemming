                                                    Description
                            
                                
         This meeting centre is the ideal place for hosting congresses, exhibitions, trade-shows, fashion shows, conventions and gala dinners. Les Docks de Paris (Pullman Dock, Haussmann Dock, Eiffel Dock) constitutes one of the capital's largest meeting centres, with 8,500m² of space. It is composed of 4 rooms equipped with the latest technology including a modular auditorium of 500 to 1,000 seats, which, combined with a standing area, can fit 3,500 people, and 19 modular rooms of 30m² to 200m² with high-quality for sound, light and projection, fibre optics and air conditioning, in the Studio adjacent to the Dock Pullman.
