                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Iconic symbol of the French cuisine, the restaurant Lasserre offers a unique culinary experience for your corporate events. Located in the heart of the 8th arrondissement, near les Champs-Elysées and Montaigne Avenue, we host your receptions, meetings, business meals... in our different rooms.  
With a capacity of 110 guests (private event) our main room, situated on the first floor, offers a spectacular environment thanks to its sunroof, permitting lunching or dining under a canopy of sky and stars. Private rooms reside on the ground floor and welcome you in a sophisticated and more intimate setting. On the kitchen side, our chef cuisine is remarkable for its positive energy and its flavors. The product and the season define his style. 
Our dedicated and experienced staff will do their very best to guarantee the success of your corporate events. 
Offer your guests, clients or co-workers a top-quality event in a sumptuous venue.
