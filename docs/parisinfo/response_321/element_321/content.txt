                            Description
                            
                                
        Located in the Parisian Golden Triangle, very close from the Arc de Triomphe, Vintage & Cie is a unique place, where you can find one of the most beautiful collections of wines and spirits. Vintage & Cie is the place to find rare and old vintages wines and spirits. Château Lafite Rothschild 1900, Romanée-Conti 1899, Paul Jaboulet Hermitage La Chapelle 1961... In addition, Vintage & Cie offers promising young winemakers' wines at affordable prices while guaranteeing a high level quality. Free tastings every Thursday, exclusive events or the privatization of its tasting room for private recpetion, those are the extra-services offered by Vintage & Cie. Its tasting room can accommodate 10 people seated and up to 20 people standing. Its stone walls remind the spirit of the wine cellars. This is where you can experience a personalized wine tasting according to your needs, organized by our experts in fine wines, all animated by the same passion. For groups up to 40 people, a privatization of the tasting room and the shop space can be considered.
