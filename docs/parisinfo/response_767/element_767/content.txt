                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In the center of Paris, with an exceptional view, Flow, a floating establishment, is docked at the Port des Invalides. With its original aesthetic and unique LED mapping, it brings a contemporary touch to this historic site.
With several modular spaces, we’re pleased to host your events throughout the year.
We’re available to offer private tours of the space in order to help plan your next business meeting or public event. 
We have spaces to accommodate all types of privatizations.
As a venue for select meetings and exchanges in the heart of historical Paris, Flow offers different formulas for expertly hosting your events.
Each space can be privatized individually or in combination.
