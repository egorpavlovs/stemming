                                                    Description
                            
                                
        If Paris is the most beautiful city in the world, then the river Seine is its most beautiful avenue, and Yachts de Paris is its best ambassador. Founded in 1993, Yachts de Paris offers luxury cruises on the Seine. Eight splendid boats for all types of events,private tailor-made events, lunch, dinner cruises and cocktails from 2 to 800 guests. Brand New in 2011: 45 minute and 1hr15 minute mini cruises with tasting on board for groups of 20 or more tourists.

                    Description of fleet
                    
        Cachemire: Onboard, every detail is deliciously sophisticated, from the Baccarat crystal glasses to the marble fireplace. For 2 to 12 people.
Victoria: A neo-classical decor combining brown, silver and splashes of red in keeping with the Art Deco furnishings. For 35 to 90 people.
Acte III: Pure lines, a slender stem, rich panelling the two levels of this yacht have a rare elegance. For 10 to 30 people.
Excellence: Exceptionally high ceilings, a splendid upper deck, incomparable light  - the Excellence is well named. For 70 to 180 people.
Don Juan II: With its Dutch prow, club-style library, Italian furniture, and bar in copper and mahogany, the Don Juan IIcannot but seduce you. For 30 to 60 people.
Mirage: Decoration in black and white, a hi-tech area with sound technology and space for a DJ this yacht is designed to pulsate to the rhythms of evening dance parties. For 100 to 230 people.
Acajou: Decoration in shades of red, 19th century paintings, mahogany mouldings the Acajou exudes sophistication. For 30 to 90 people.
The Paquebot: The spirit of the transatlantic liner lives on in this gigantic dining room, with its triple deck and two magnificent outside chimneys. Monumental! For 180 to 800 people.
The Escale Lounge, on quayside, enables any kind of event and offers a pleasant transition before boarding. This sublime lounge with nautical decor, boasts a pair of ancient olive trees. It is entirely modular and can accommodate between 40 and 500 people.
