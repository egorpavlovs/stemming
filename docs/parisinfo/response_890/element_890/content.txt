                                What's on?
                                
        The Museum is closed for works until summer 2019. In the meanwhile, we will propose you outdoor activities and monthly events. The library remains open (only by appointment). Please feel free to follow us on Facebook, Twitter, and to consult our website maisondebalzac.paris.fr. 
    
                            
                        
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Maison de Balzac, an old famous novelist's house, holds personal memories of Balzac along with a large number of original editions, manuscripts and illustrations. Located in a garden in the heart of the old Passy village, the literary museum is available for private hire during weekday evenings.
