                            Parisian suites available for private hire to host your prestige events
                            Looking to organize your professional event within a prestige setting? Paris, the capital of luxury and French lifestyle, is home to many outstanding establishments. Check out our selection of private hire suites in luxury palace hotels and 5-stars hotels to amaze your guests. 
                        

                        We invite you to contact our service providers to get a tailor-made quote. All in all, a magical and unforgettable event (meeting, seminar, product launch, showroom, business dinner, cocktail party ...) where Parisian chic is at its zenith.Royale Mandarin Suite - The Mandarin Oriental ParisThe Mandarin Oriental Paris lies within walking distance of the Place de la Concorde and Place Vendôme. The palace graciously combines Parisian elegance with the refinement of the Orient. On the top floor, the 350 m² Royale Mandarin Suite is decorated in a colour palette of white, gold, beige and plum. Spread out over 2 levels, the suite has a dining room for your meetings or business meals and a large lounge with a terrace affording 360° views of Paris and emblematic monuments such as Montmartre, the Opéra Garnier, the Grand Palais and the Louvre. All of the suites on the top floor are available for private hire and have connecting doors to give a total surface area of more than 1,000 m²!Request an estimateThe Suite Shangri-La - The Shangri-La hotel ParisAfter passing through the elegant wrought iron gates, you are greeted by the harmonious facade of the hotel. Welcome to the Shangri-La hotel Paris. The palace hosts your professional events in this upscale neighbourhood, a few steps from the Place du Trocadéro. In total, 65 rooms and 36 suites, including 3 signature suites. Guests will be enthralled by the Suite Eiffel (220 m²), which features classic French furniture in sober colours, with an Asian twist. Why not take advantage of the large teak private terrace (100 m²) for your outdoor event? And enjoy the stunning views of Montmartre, Trocadéro, the Grand Palais, Notre Dame de Paris, Invalides and of course the Eiffel Tower.Request an estimateSuite Alfred Sommier - Hôtel Alfred SommierBuilt in 1860, the Alfred Sommier hôtel particulier, restored in 2018 and transformed by a descendant of the family into a luxury hotel, is located in the heart of the Madeleine district. The 20 quiet and bright suites of the hotel reflect French elegance with their mouldings, period cornices parquet flooring and marble. On the first floor, the Alfred Sommier Suite (52 m²) is the most spacious. With its mirrors, wood panelling and original fireplace, this magnificent room with its high ceiling has retained its charm of yesteryear. The large windows looking onto a private balcony let in ever-changing natural light. The Alfred Sommier Suite is ideal for an after-work event, a private cocktail party, or a product launch for up to 20 people.Request an estimateThe Raffles Presidential Suite - The Royal Monceau Raffles ParisA veritable contemporary masterpiece, the Royal Monceau Raffles Paris, parisian palace located close to the Champs-Elysées has 3 presidential suites, designed like Parisian apartments, and ideal for business clients. The Raffles Presidential Suite (350 m²) is the largest and features a huge reception room opening onto a dining room, perfect for private business meals. Light grey and pale pink tones together with resolutely modern furniture ensure a sense of comfort. Request an estimateThe Belle Etoile Suite - Le MeuriceThe Meurice’s 18th-century architectural splendour epitomizes the spirit of French art de vivre. The Belle Etoile Suite (300 m²), on the palace’s 7th floor is decorated with unique works of art and elegant furnishings and fabrics. On fine days, welcome your clients and colleagues on the sumptuous private terrace (250 m²) with views of the Eiffel Tower, the Grand Palais, and Notre-Dame. An unmissable thrill is watching the sun go down as you wait for the lights on the Eiffel Tower to start twinkling … Simply divine.Request an estimateThe Presidential Suite - The Hôtel du Collectionneur Arc de Triomphe Paris   A few minutes’ walk from the Arc de Triomphe and the Avenue des Champs-Elysées, revel in the art deco interiors of the Hôtel du Collectionneur Arc de Triomphe Paris. The Presidential Suite boasts a large reception room, a lounge, and a kitchenette. This fully modular 142 m² space can be adapted for your business events. For a break outdoors, enjoy the balcony overlooking the hotel’s Andalusian Patio planted with roses, Palm trees and orange trees, as well as a view of the Eiffel Tower. Snacks and drinks are available throughout the day.Request an estimateThe Suite Niepce – Hôtel NiepceThe Niepce, a recently opened and charming boutique hotel just a few steps from the Montparnasse train station, has interiors themed around the photography of the 1920s. Among the 52 modern rooms and suites with elegant and glamorous interiors inspired by the work of photographer Janine Niépce is the striking Niepce Suite. This chic 62 m² duplex apartment is full of tranquillity. It has a luxurious bedroom and a lounge leading onto a 60 m² private terrace with a jacuzzi and stunning views over the rooftops of Paris. Meals and cocktails can be served in this wonderful outdoor space right in the heart of Paris.Request an estimateThe Grande Suite - Molitor‘Pool, Art, life’ is the motto of the Molitor, which has had a perfect makeover and sports a summer pool and a winter pool, a restaurant, bar, fitness room, and a range of spaces for your corporate events. The Grande Suite, a most unique, timeless and creative place in Paris, has a surface area of 60 m². With pared back stylish interiors, it benefits from a terrace with a view of the outdoor pool. A special getaway break for your clients and colleagues and a plunge into a mythical venue!Request an estimateThe JW Marriott Presidential Suite - The Paris Marriott Champs Elysées HotelThe Paris Marriott Champs Elysées Hotel, with its lavish heritage-listed facade, has 25 suites. A must is the JW Marriott Presidential Suite (170 m²) (with dedicated access from the underground car park), which combines to perfection French classic and modern architecture. On the 7th floor, under the glass roof of the hotel, the suite decorated in shades of brown and sand is bathed in natural light. A sumptuous black marble table is ideal for a business meeting. The lounge area is perfect for less formal meetings. Its view of the atrium provides a view of the flurry of activity in the hall below.Request an estimateThe Suite Peninsula - The Peninsula ParisSituated on Avenue Kleber, The Peninsula Paris hotel is within a short distance of the capital’s monuments, museums and emblematic boutiques. The lobby features an exquisite suspended installation of hand-blown crystal leaves. You’ll be equally charmed by the decor of the 200 rooms and 34 prestigious suites. The Peninsula Suite(318 m²) in pearl grey and cream shades is elegantly furnished and adorned with carefully selected works of art, including a magnificent piano in black marble.  Organize your delegations and official visits thanks to cutting-edge security equipment.Request an estimateThe Saphir Suite - The Prince de GallesStep into the world of art deco at the chic and cosy Prince de Galles hotel on Avenue George V. This jewel of a hotel with 115 rooms and 44 suites welcomes your exclusive events. The 7th-floor Saphir Suite with blue details has a soft and soothing atmosphere. The interiors with bright notes, furniture inspired by Ruhlmann, and portraits of women by Lempicka convey a literary ambience evoking the Paris of the 1930s. The Saphir is the perfect hideaway on the Paris rooftops with two private terraces overlooking the Avenue Georges V.Request an estimateLoft Terrasse LAZ' - LAZ’ HôtelLocated in the 9th arrondissement, a few metres from the Gare Saint-Lazare, the LAZ' Hotel is a boutique hotel with 56 rooms and suites designed in the industrial and Bohemian spirit of a New York loft. Occupying the entire 7th and last floor of the hotel, the Loft Terrasse LAZ' is a modern suite with exposed brick, steel and light-coloured parquet flooring. A 16 m² private terrace with a view of the Sacré Cœur and the Paris rooftops offers a privileged environment for a private after-work event for up to 8 people.Request an estimateThe Suite 114 - Hotel Banke Paris  The Hotel Banke Paris is located in the Opéra district, at the intersection of Boulevard La Fayette and Rue Pillet. You will not fail to be impressed by the majestic lobby crowned with a glass cupola. The 91 rooms and suites including Suite 114 can host your subcommittees in stunning baroque interiors. The latter has an upstairs bedroom configured for events that can host up to 12 people. The ultra-chic spiral staircase leads to the Salon Dupleix which can accommodate up to 5 people for a more intimate event in a cosy and pleasant atmosphere.Request an estimate
