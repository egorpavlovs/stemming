                                                    Description
                            
                                
        DSO is an incoming and event agency specialised in corporate events (conferences, meetings, receptions, team building, business travels…)
Our field of expertise are: 
-	Events production, 
-	Business travel, 
-	Incentive and team-building
-	Light, sound and video
With its dedicated team, DSO will offer you a personalized service providing you the help you need to achieve your objectives, either for a global event management or for specific aspects of your project.
Key figures: 
-	25 years of expertise
-	2 offices for a nationwide coverage (Paris and Lyon)
-	80% of regular clients
