                            The first Okko Hotels Paris - Porte de Versailles!
                            The latest offshoot of the young French hotel chain Okko Hotels has opened near to the tramway and Porte de Versailles.  A new 4-star stopover, ideal for a business trip or a Paris city break!
                        

                        The hotel, with interiors by Patrick Norguet, offers 149 rooms that are a perfect blend of functionality, elegance and modernity. Decorated in two predominant colours, Riva and Black Out, they are around 18 m² in size. Each room is equipped with Nuxe brand cosmetics, flat-screen TV with French and international channels, a video on demand option, a radio alarm clock, and high-quality furniture for maximum comfort.For its first Parisian establishment, Okko Hotels Paris Porte de Versailles  continues to focus on originality, which has been the secret of its success: optimal customer experience with an all-inclusive offer. Central to this is ‘the Club’, a spacious stylish and open-plan living area on the ground floor of the hotel. Open 24/24, with facilities such as a modern business corner, bookcase and lounge space, it is an ideal place to socialize, relax or work stress-free, and drinks and snacks are available throughout the day!
