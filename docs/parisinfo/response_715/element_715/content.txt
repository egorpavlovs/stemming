                        Description
                        
                                                    
        This new luxury hotel is located at the entrance of the hotel zone of the Paris-Charles de Gaulle airport and 10 minutes from the airport itself. Guests can use the free shuttle service to reach the airport or the RER train station, which will take you to the centre of Paris in 30 minutes.
Numerous services are at your disposal: 24 hours a day welcome desk, restaurant, bar, fitness room, meeting room…

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        -Physical disability: car park with reserved disabled parking spaces, ground-level entrance or with access ramp, reception with adapted counter, adapted communal toilets with a transfer bar and adapted sink, adapted apartments and bathroom equipped with walk-in shower and seat/fold-up seat and adapted sink. 
-Audio impairment: reservation and/or payment possible online, lift with visual announcements and/or sound with magnetic induction loop, bright flash and loud fire alarm (in places where the person may be alone such as their bedroom, bathroom, and toilet).
