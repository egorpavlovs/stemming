                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Entirely flexible with its 3 spaces, the Cirque Alexis Gruss can be partially or entirely reserved for private hire during the day or night. Several rooms offer different ambiances, adapting to any type of event. The incredible flexibility of the spaces enables it to host a cocktail party for 100 people, a seated dinner for 900 people, or a conference for 3,000 people. For your private or professional events, come and discover a greatly renowned breathtaking show, refreshed each year and orchestrated by the Gruss family, circus experts for over a century and a half. From the show to the decoration and dinner, we will take care of your event and make your evening as quirky and as magical as you have always dreamed it.
