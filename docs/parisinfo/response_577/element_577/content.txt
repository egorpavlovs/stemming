                        Description
                        
                                                    
        With its 22 rooms and amphitheatre, the FIAP is a multi-purpose space that welcomes more than 2,000 organizations every year. These facilities are available during your stay. Classrooms, meeting rooms and workshops also make the FIAP an ideal workspace.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Mobility impairment: ground-level entrance or with access ramp, reception/till with adapted counter, 11 adapted bedrooms and bathroom equipped with walk-in shower (with folding seat or mobile seat), adapted sink, dining room/breakfast room with 1.40 m passing spaces and adapted tables (70 cm height under tables).
- Visual impairment: transparent glass doors/walls clearly marked with contrasting elements, no obstacles at eye-level, lift with audio announcement for each floor, information and signs in large print, signs at an appropriate height.
- Mental impairment: easy-to-read and easy-to-understand signs with pictograms, monitored entrance visible from reception/till.
