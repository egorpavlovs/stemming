                        Description
                        
                                                    
        OKKO HOTELS Paris Porte de Versailles is the first hotel in our young urban four-star chain to be located in Paris. The proximity of the tramway, of Balard Metro station and of the Porte de Versailles transport terminal will make this hotel the ideal destination for visitors on a business trip to Paris.
The Club is the heart of our hotels. A design and warm space available 24h/24 to our clients. Reserved for you, this area offers a business corner, a shop, a fitness and well-being space, and different refreshments and snacks depending on the time of day.
We offer design yet comfortable rooms, real urban cocoons, which meet the needs of today's travelers and will guarantee you 4* nights, without any cloud.
Finally, with OKKO HOTELS, your stay is all-inclusive. Breakfast, aperitivo in the evening, access to the gym, Wifi and VOD ... all our services are included in the price of the room.
