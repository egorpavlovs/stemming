                            What's on?
                            
        3 Tourist Packages for the groups : shopping trip with a gourmet experience, private guide tour throught the best spots of Le Marais, afterwork at Le Perchoir Marais rooftop, VIP delivery service... 
    
                        
                    
                                            
                            Description
                            
                                
        It is the place of quintessential Parisian shopping for organising your affairs and making the most of the superb terrace with stunning views over Paris for cocktails! The BHV Marais, in the heart of the capital, offers several departments: cultural, women's fashion, luxury, decoration, art of French living ... without forgetting the DIY department in the basement. Many events are organised throughout the year (culinary workshops, Christmas activites ...). For a gourmet break with stunning views of the City Hall, venture up to the 5th floor of the store. A deluxe version of Bazaar is available for trendy shopaholics.
