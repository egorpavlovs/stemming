                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A stone's throw from Rue le Coeur, the Dilettantes champagne house opens its doors.
Dilettantes offer a selection of more than 130 rare and authentic champagne from the 'grand cru' vineyards and made by true winemakers.
Try a glass or 2 of champagne in a 17th century wine cellar surrounded by other champagne bottles.
You can then continue the tasting of champagne if you wish and explore the 4 great regions where champagne grapes flourish (pinot noir, chardonnay, pinot meunier). 
Taste and discover the magic and mystery of champagne, including territory, grape, year and blending...
You will discover that the champagne is not only the favorite drink of French people as an aperitif, it is also a wonderful wine that accompanies a meal, provided that attention be paid to the agreement with the dishes!
You can also start with the wines tasted. All bottles are already cooled and ready to be bought.
