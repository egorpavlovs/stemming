                            "Paris - a bargain" - agency Smoozy
                            Let’s discover or rediscover Paris during 3 days with a mini budget
                        

                        Choice of theme: Mini budgetNumber of participants: 25Length of programme: 3 days Sector(s) of activity of client:AllSpecimen programme Day 1 – Your way to ParisAs soon as you arrive in Paris, your Smoozy representative will welcome you at the airport or Gare du Nord with the essential French accessory : A beret (1 per person).Your design hotel will be located in the new trendy-boheme district of Paris. A unique design made by Philip Starck, a perfect spot for drinks with their unique bar, a welcoming restaurant with home-made French food served in cocottes.Your first afternoon will be dedicated to the orientation tour in Paris with very special bikes (with a numeric tablet on).The evening will be at the hotel with drinks and food to relax and enjoy the crazy atmosphere of this amazing place.Day 2 – Sure your know Paris?This morning discover a bizarre place you would never thought you would visit with a group: The out of the box Pere Lachaise Cemetery visit where Oscar Wilde, Edith Piaf and Jim Morrison are buried. We will add a special touch with a guide enjoying black humour. Funny anecdotes, celebrities megalomania, a truly memorable visit.Right after you would need a little time to relax with a petanque game, drinks and a traditional baguette sandwich. Afternoon at leisure.In the early evening, go for a private cruise on the river with a glass of champagne before heading to a small bistro on the river banks. For the after dinner drinks discover a very funky club with an industrial design at 20min walk from the hotel.Day 3 – Paris: the shopping versionBefore leaving Paris, the must is to enjoy a shopping time. The main Parisian malls will not have secrets anymore for you. Located in the Opera district, you will enjoy the two main malls in Paris: Galeries Lafayette and Printemps. Your lunch will be served below the amazing Art Deco coupolla of the Printemps. Trendy brasserie serving traditionnal French food. Time to say good bye !
