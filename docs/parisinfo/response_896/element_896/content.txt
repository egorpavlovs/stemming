               
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The restaurant La Verrière Paris is located in the heart of the Montparnasse district in the 14th arrondissement of Paris, within the Niépce Paris Curio Collection by Hilton hotel. It has its own entrance, independent of the entrance to the Niépce Paris hotel. The restaurant La Verrière Paris is ideal for your business lunches or dinners. Discover the charm of this establishment, which is consistent with remarkable venues in Paris. The decoration of the restaurant is contemporary and refined, and the large dining room is bathed in natural light with an impressive glass roof. Chef Kayori Hirano and his team invite guests on a culinary journey through reimagined flavours. The main menu and other menus proposed at La Verrière Paris reflect the chef’s refined and multicultural personality: the flavours of Japan associated with the delicacy of French cuisine. These influences that inspire Kayori Hirano daily make each visit to La Verrière Paris a unique experience.
The restaurant La Verrière Paris offers you a fine wine cellar within the restaurant, with exceptional vintages. Close to the restaurant is the Montparnasse seminar room which can host 16 people (seated). The Montparnasse room is ideal for your business meetings; it is equipped with audio conference telephony, a 55-inch screen, a wireless barco TV connection system, Screen Mirroring for Android and Apple TV for iPhone and tablet. There is also Wi-Fi access; management of all features is via the wall touchpad. A dedicated sound system is also available for seminars. The Montparnasse room is available for private hire according to your events.
