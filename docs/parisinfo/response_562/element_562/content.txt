                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Lido, besides its legendary name which has evoked prestigious dream-like evenings for over 50 years, has many assets to attract and amaze your guests and make your event extraordinary! Discover 'Bonheur' (Happiness), a remarkably stunning show, perfect for your events: you will experience a marvellous journey full of emotions and discoveries. A subtle mix of tradition and innovation, the catering at the Lido offers an authentic and delicious cuisine.
Only 'happiness' in the sparkling show at the Lido de Paris!
