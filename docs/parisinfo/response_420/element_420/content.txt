                            Rediscover the Hotel Auteuil Tour Eiffel 4*
                            After a seven-month makeover, this beautiful Parisian townhouse in the 16th arrondissement reopens this summer with a friendly, chic, and modern vibe.
                        

                        Conveniently located in the peaceful Auteuil district, and close to some of the capital’s top sights like the Eiffel Tower and the Champs-Elysées, the Hotel Auteuil Tour Eiffel offers comfort with understated and timeless interiors decorated in a palette of taupe, gold, and yellow. The number of rooms and suites (from 16 m² to 30 m²) has increased from 94 to 102, and some of these have a balcony.Other new features: the hotel now has a sauna, a 24-hour fitness suite, and a lounge bar where you can enjoy afternoon tea or a delicious cocktail with a plate of charcuterie. Private parking and a concierge service round off the offering.The hotel has created a Business Class programme specifically for corporate guests, with negotiated rates, available rooms, and a simple booking process. Dedicated services include express check-in and check-out, express breakfast from 6am, working spaces, and a ‘business corner’ with computer, printer, and scanner.
