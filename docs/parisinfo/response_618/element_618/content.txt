                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A true institution that shows Paris to the world, the Opéra National de Paris consists of two theatres - the Palais Garnier and the Opéra Bastille - and invites you to organise your events in prestigious surroundings. The Opéra National de Paris offers its auditoriums and reception rooms for any type of event, whether it's a product launch, a catwalk show, film shoots for adverts, or any other activity related to business.

                        Educational activities
                                                    Educational activities. Workshops for families with artist participation to familiarize children with the performing arts. 
Some 60 performances each year for children and teenagers to introduce them to the world of the music and dance. 
For more information, email jeunepublic@operadeparis.fr
                                            
                                    
                
                                
                    Business area

                                        
                        Number of meeting rooms:1
                    
                    
                                        
                        Number of private rooms:7
                    
                    
                                        
                        Room hire:
                        
        The Opéra Bastille offers a unique range of spaces with a large auditorium with 2,745 seats, an amphitheatre wtih 500 seats, a studio with 230 seats surrounded by foyers. The Foyer Panoramique is an area with extensive views of the most beautiful Parisian monuments, can welcome up to 500 people for cocktails and 320 people for a seated dinner.
