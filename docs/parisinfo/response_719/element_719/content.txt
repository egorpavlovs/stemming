                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Our hotel is the brand flagship in France, located at the heart of the airport with a direct connection to Paris. Our 305 refined and comfortable rooms ensure immediate relaxation, as they are functional, hyper-connected, completely soundproofed. A unique space at the heart of the hotel, the restaurant Culinaire Bazaar concept came from the desire to rediscover tastes and recipes that have been lost over time. Waiting staff with an offbeat style – Converses, bow tie and braces - are trained in a different approach to service, inviting you to experience the subtle blend of discovery and nostalgia. Co-Meeting by Pullman integrates thoughtful rooms with bespoke planning. More than 1,600 m² of meeting space are distributed over two floors: 13 modular rooms with 18 configurations, including a flexible plenary room, “Business Playground” a creative room designed by Matthieu Lehanneur, and banquet hall. These panoramic spaces, unique to Roissy CDG Airport, offer plenty of natural daylight and views of the runways. Enjoy complete privacy, direct lift access, and 100% failsafe service. The Fit Lounge is designed for maximum well-being, right in the centre of Roissy, with a spectacular indoor heated pool, hot tubs, Turkish bath and sauna.
