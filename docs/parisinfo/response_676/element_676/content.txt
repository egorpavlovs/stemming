                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Majestically presiding over the Place de la Concorde, the Hôtel de Crillon, a Rosewood Hotel, is an 18th century architectural masterpiece exuding the art of French lifestyle since 1909. 
In March 2013, the Hôtel de Crillon closed for complete renovation and in December 2013 announced that it would be managed by the Rosewood Hotels and Resorts group. The Hôtel de Crillon reopened on July 5, 2017.

                                                                                                                Reception/Welcome 24/24

                        Other services at the establishment
                        High-speed Internet access, 24 hour business centre, Clefs d'Or concierge service, private transport between the hotel and the aiport, free luxury car service (subject to availability), valet parking service.
Three lounges, terrasses, boardroom, wine cellar for private events.
Gastronomic restaurant L'Ecrin, the Brasserie d'Aumont, the Jardin d'Hiver, the bar Les Ambassadeurs, two exterior courtyards.
Sense, A Rosewood Spa®: 24 hour fitness studio, two boutiques, Rose Buds® programme for children, baby equipment.
Groups accepted (over 7 rooms).
