                            Restaurants for groups
                            A restaurant in Paris to host your evening event, lunch meeting or business dinner. Restaurants available for private hire.
                        

                        Welcome to the French capital – and its wide selection of great restaurants available for private hire to make your event a success. Your guests will enjoy a gourmet menu drawn up specially for your event in a restaurant that meets all your requirements.Private dining rooms in Paris restaurants offer a quiet atmosphere, discreetly attentive service, elegant decor and excellent food. They are the perfect option if you want to make your group lunch or dinner a gourmet experience and an event to remember.Whether you prefer a traditional and/or gastronomic restaurant, a bistro or a brasserie, a tea room or an unusual eating place, you’ll find a wealth of choice in our guide to restaurants available for private hire in Paris for your lunch meetings, business dinners or private events (a birthday party, wedding reception, evening with dancing or gala dinner). Book an evening organized down to the last detail in a venue devoted exclusively to your guests and take advantage of the range of services on offer. Our comprehensive selection includes venues with capacity for groups of many different sizes.You can use our search engine to select specific criteria depending on your requirements (number of guests, district, event type etc.). The photographs and descriptions of the private dining rooms on our list, and the entertainment options they offer, will help you to narrow down your choice and find the perfect venue.Find more ideas in our thematic files:Classic restaurant, bistros and brasseries in ParisGastronomic restaurants in Paris
