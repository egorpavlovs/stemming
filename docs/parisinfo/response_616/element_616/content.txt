                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Less than half an hour from Paris and only 30 minutes from Charles de Gaulle airport, the Domaine de Chantilly, a jewel of French heritage, comprising the Musée Condé, the park, the Great Stables, the Musée Vivant du Cheval and the hippodrome, opens its doors to those organizing private or professional events who are eager to associate their events with this prestigious historical setting, filled with art and nature.

        French garden designed by Le Nôtre, English garden, Anglo-Chinese garden.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        Chateau 
-Physical disability: reserved parking spaces close to the chateau, the large princely apartments and the shop are at ground level, adapted toilets at the right of the chateau entrance. 
-Audio impairment: ticket office equipped with hearing loop for the hard of hearing
-All disabilities: visits or activities can be adapted to your needs (only in reserved time slots lasting 60 to 90 minutes). 
Chateau Gardens
-Physical disability: gardens mostly accessible to visitors in wheelchairs but with help (pavement can sometimes be loose or bumpy), the small train can seat those in wheelchairs in the end carriage.
-Visual impairment: audio commentary in the train to discover the gardens.  
Stables / Musée du Cheval
-Physical disability: reserved parking spaces near the stables, stables and museum all accessible at ground-level, floor is paved cobbles, adapted toilets with automatic taps, 12 reserved places for visitors in wheelchairs in the dome of the stables (where the horse shows take place), on request. 
-Audio impairment: disabled toilets equipped with light and sound signal for fire alarm.
