                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A listed historical monument, this luxury brasserie has a 1900s decor that is unique and typical of Paris: ceramics, mosaics and art nouveau style ceilings. 
This century-old restaurant is one of the most beautiful and oldest brasseries in Paris. The chef, Joël Prodhomme serves up traditional French cuisine made using only the freshest quality ingredients. 
Located opposite the Saint Lazare train station, and just 2 minutes from the Grands Magasins, Place de la Concorde and Palais Garnier Opera House, it is ideally located to host any type of event.
