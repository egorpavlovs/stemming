                            Destination Paris Bercy: the new hub for business and leisure tourism
                            Whether you want to bring together from 2 to 20,000 people, discover ‘Destination Paris Bercy’, a ‘village’ boasting numerous event spaces and accommodation.
                        

                        12 key business and leisure tourism players* have joined together to create the new Parisian ‘village’  Destination Paris Bercy. This district, bordered on one side by the River Seine and on the other by Bercy Park, can be reached quickly from the centre of Paris and the main train stations and airports. What’s more, it has major advantages for a business or leisure trip. With hotels, restaurants, show venues and meeting rooms, there are numerous places available to accommodate all types of events.See the 70 seminar spaces, 22 amphitheatres, 1,800 hotel rooms (3 and 4 stars). An 8-minute walk is all it takes to get from one place to another.Destination Paris Bercy is a great place to stroll and a lively area with leisure and recreation: live concerts, exhibitions, shops, wine tasting and treasure hunts. It provides a turnkey stay with 4 essential elements: hotels, leisure, shopping and culture!*12 key business: Pullman Paris Centre-Bercy:Pullman Paris Centre BercyNovotel Paris Centre Bercy Ibis Paris Bercy Village AccorHotels ArenaEspace Van Gogh & Espace du Centenaire - Espace SEDPKyriad Paris Bercy VillageLes Salons de l'AveyronLes Pavillons de BercyBercy VillageIbis Styles Paris Bercy Apparthotel Adagio Paris Bercy Villlage UGC Ciné Cité Bercy
