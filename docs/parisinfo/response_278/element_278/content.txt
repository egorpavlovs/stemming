                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Peninsula Hotels group makes a noteworthy entrance in Europe with the opening of Peninsula Paris. This hotel, open since 1 August 2014, redefines standards in design, luxury and comfort and marks a new stage in the development of the brand, bringing an unprecedented level of refinement to the City of Light.
The Peninsula Paris is ideally situated in the heart of the city, at 19 Avenue Kléber, a stone's throw away from the Arc de Triomphe and some of the most famous monuments, museums and luxury boutiques in the world.
The Peninsula Paris is situated in a classic late 19th century Parisian building with numerous Haussmanian and neoclassic features, which initially housed one of the most luxurious hotels in Paris, opened in 1908. This building full of history has been restored with care and subtly brought up to date to offer an exceptional level of comfort appropriate for the 21st century. The Peninsula has 200 luxury rooms, including 34 of the most spectacular suites in the city.
Combining classic Parisian chic and world-class hospitality, The Peninsula Paris is a coveted location for memorable events in the French capital. Set amid the city’s premier attractions, the hotel’s portfolio of meeting and reception venues ranges from small boardrooms and private dining suites to a traditional Parisian salon connecting to the majestic Pérouse Gallery.
Whichever you choose, their expert meeting consultants will help ensure that your event captures the hotel’s unique blend of timeless elegance, modern luxury and delectable dining.
