                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Pavillon Dauphine was built on the edge of the Bois de Boulogne, at the bottom of Avenue de l'Impératrice, now known as Avenue Foch, at the beginning of the last century. Surrounded by gardens, it can hold from 25 to 2100 people, and is ideal for fostering communication in the prestigious setting of its reception rooms, where charm and discretion are combined with tradition and the high standards of today.
