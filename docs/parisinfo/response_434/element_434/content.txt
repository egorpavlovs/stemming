                            The Salons de lâ€™Aveyron take on a new look!
                            At the heart of the Bercy district, in the 12th arrondissement, the Salons de lâ€™Aveyron rental spaces have been given a new lease of life with a recent renovation and revegetation project. An ideal events venue with a cosy and hip atmosphere!
                        

                        The modular spaces (1,300 mÂ²) of the Salons de l'Aveyron, near to the Seine riverbanks and former warehouses have had a complete revamp and are suited to all types of professional events (meetings, exhibitions, dinners and product lunches). The spaces vary from 60 to 800 mÂ² and are now equipped with the latest technology (Wi-Fi, 4G, fibre optic 100 Mb and vehicle access ramp). The new surfacing, the play of light and the high ceiling offer pleasant and design interiors for an event ranging from 100 to 800 people.A catering service provides meals prepared onsite based on fresh and certified or local regional products according to a large choice of menus. The reception of guests is personalized with a hostess and concierge service.With 21 years of expertise, the Salons de lâ€™ Aveyron make every effort to make events unique.
