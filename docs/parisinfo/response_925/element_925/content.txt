                            Printemps du Goût: new gourmet hot spot
                            A delight for the eyes and taste buds! Printemps has opened Printemps du Goût, a new food space over two floors, in the recently renovated menswear building Printemps de l’Homme, completely devoted to the best of French gastronomy.
                        

                        This new address, on the 7th and 8th floors of Printemps de l’Homme, combines good food (100% French) with a 360° view of Paris. It is a unique and elegant showcase for French regional produce, a mix of market, delicatessen and restaurant spaces.The 7th floor (900 m²) is given over to fine food suppliers: quality products, chosen with care and reflecting the expertise of French craftsmanship with, for example, smoked salmon, wine, chocolates, jams, cakes, tea and coffee. A true temple of gastronomy!The 8th floor is a big ‘market in the sky' (800 m²), offering take-aways or sit-down meals (260 seats including 70 on the terrace). The current pick of creative chefs and food artisans have tasting areas around large mosaic-tiled counters, luminous cooker hoods, and glass lights: Akrame Benallal for meat, fish and fruit and vegetables; Christophe Michalak for cakes and pastries; Gontran Cherrier for bread; and Laurent Dubois (Meilleur Ouvrier de France) for cheese.
