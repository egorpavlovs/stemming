                                                            The Seine Musicale: a truly unique new international cultural and events venue! 
                                                
                                    



        
            
                
                    
                        
                            The Seine Musicale: a truly unique new international cultural and events venue! 
                            With architecture reminiscent of an ocean liner, the Seine Musicale has transformed the Ile Seguin — the site of the former Renault factories. The venue’s large concrete volumes respect the identity of the latter whilst bringing a bold touch of innovation with the spherical shape of the auditorium and its solar sail. 
                        

                        A veritable hub for cultural life extending over 36,500 m², the Seine Musicale is a creative space where all types of music are played: world music, jazz and classical music within the 800 m², all-wood, 1,100-seat auditorium (format 360°); and performing arts - including the musical West Side Story in the autumn – in the Grande Salle.  The latter, which has three different stages and retractable rows of seating, can accommodate up to 6,000 peoplefor product launches and 4,000 people in plenary format.  The space can be used in association with the Grand Salon, the Club or the Grand Foyer for your business meals or cocktail receptions, catered for by Lenôtre (Sodexo). The Seine Musicale also offers areas for musicians (such as rehearsal studios), areas for private hire (up to 10,000 people), as well as services such as shops, restaurants ... With so much to discover, entertainment, and musical instruction for children and adults whatever their level, this is an outstanding alternative venue.

            Unusual venues for an event ‘just like at home’
        
        Looking to organize your next professional event at a Parisian venue with an intimate and welcoming atmosphere ‘just like at home’, a private evening function in a magnificent Parisian apartment, or are seeking an unusual rental in the heart of Paris for a group trip? Then, hurry and check out our selection of venues that combine modernity and originality, tailor-made services, and upscale amenities in an atmosphere that’s just like home. With choices like New York style lofts with roof-top terraces, chic and sobre apartments with views over the rooftops and famous Paris landmarks, and vaulted cellars, you are sure to find the perfect venue!
