                            Club 13, spotlighting a mythical venue!
                            Once through the understated entrance, you’ll start to feel the charm of the legendary Club 13 … Created by film director Claude Lelouch 50 years ago, the venue, which has seen the greatest cinema stars from Chaplin to Scorsese, has been sublimely redecorated by Sarah Lavoine.
                        

                        Soft lighting, shades of ochre, greens, and reds, velvet upholstery and original wood panelling revive this temple of the movie world, and confer a hushed and cosy atmosphere. The interior designer has remained faithful to the spirit of Claude Lelouch, even printing the number 13 like a logo on the carpet, in reference to the filmmaker’s production company ‘Les Films 13’.The 2 projection rooms with their large soft leather chairs are now available for private hire (15 and 65 people), for an original event. Note that Club 13 also has a restaurant (54 seats) serving family dishes at lunchtime, a piano bar and a karaoke for chic and glamorous evenings.
