                            The Westin Paris-Vendôme 4* hotel unveils its 'Jardin d'hiver'!
                            The magnificent terrace of The Westin Paris-Vendôme hotel turns into a ‘Jardin d'hiver' for the season. A delightful haven for your cocktail parties, business lunches or dinners close to the Louvre Museum and Tuileries Gardens.
                        

                        Furnished and decorated with cane furniture, pastel-coloured cushions, lights and plants suspended from the ceiling, the 227 m²-terrace of the Westin Paris-Vendôme welcomes you in a chic and verdant setting. Receive your guests (180 people) for a cocktail party, a lunch or dinner (120 people). The space can also be privately hired for a gourmet snack during your seminar, meeting or conference. Bespoke options are available: DJ sets, culinary workshops, video projections...For the festive season, discover the irresistible creations of Chef David Réal and Pastry Chef Florence Lesage...A true timeless interlude in the midst of the Parisian winter!
