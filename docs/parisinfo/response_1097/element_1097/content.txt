                            The Pullman Paris Roissy CDG Airport, new hotel located in the Paris-Charles-de-Gaulle hub
                            Stylish and comfortable, the latest addition to the Pullman family has opened its doors, located right in the centre of the Paris-Charles-de-Gaulle hub, with direct connections to terminals 1, 2 and 3, and the TGV/RER station.
                        

                        The Pullman Paris Roissy CDG Airport boasts 305 bedrooms and 11 suites that tastefully combine functionality and elegance. The bedrooms are decorated with a sober colour scheme and equipped with spacious beds and a large screen television, ensuring a comfortable stay.Guests can enjoy a pre-dinner drink at the bar with its impressive and innovative drinks menu. At the restaurant, Bazaar, chef Thierry Coutable has concocted a delicious menu, with an emphasis on local produce, prepared in the open kitchen, and accompanied by an extensive wine list. In fine weather, enjoy outdoor dining on the terrace.For corporate events, the hotel has 12 meeting rooms (capacity 250 persons) and a large banquet room of 1,600 m2. The meeting rooms and banquet room have views over the airport’s take-off runways.The heated indoor swimming pool, 140 m2 fitness centre and spa are the ideal way to unwind after a long flight. Perfect for relaxing in a cosmopolitan environment.
