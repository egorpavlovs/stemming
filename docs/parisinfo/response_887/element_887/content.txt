                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A museum on the history of Paris, the Mus√©e Carnavalet has collections that show the development of the city, from prehistoric times to the present day. Established in two mansions at the heart of the Marais, it displays, a vast range of art and mementos evoking the daily intellectual life of the capital in a historical setting. Available for private hire in the evening except on Thursdays.
