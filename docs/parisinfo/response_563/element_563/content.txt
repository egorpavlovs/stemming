                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Its revue 'Paradis à la Folie' is a show which people from all over the world come to watch! Paris sings here and the French Cancan is danced here, the quality of attractions and the spirit of the Master of Ceremonies recreate the typical enthusiasm, atmosphere and scent of the City of Light!
With a capacity to seat 700 people, the Paradis Latin is also a favourite place for corporate events.
An adaptable space with modern and professional equipmnt available in this jewel of French architecture. A warm and personalized welcome, a sparkling revue and high quality food: all the ingredients are brought together for an amazingly successful event in a true French atmosphere!
