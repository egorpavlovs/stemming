                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Golden Tulip CDG Villepinte, your personalized 4-stars experience at Roissy Charles de Gaulle !
Our 152 rooms provide you with complimentary amenities, unlimited Wifi access, high quality bedding, reversible air conditioning and access to our fitness and sauna center. In deluxe rooms, fridges, bathrobes and coffee machines are also included.
5 kilometers away from Charles de Gaulle Airport and train station, our hotel is served by the « Pink Line » shuttle starting 5 a.m. Close to Paris Villepinte Exhibition Center, it is easily accessible by public transport (RER B, bus) or by the A1 motorway (Exit Louvres/Goussainville).
Our all-you-can-eat buffet breakfast is served starting 5.30 a.m and our 100-seats restaurant provides you with homemade and refined cuisine buffets for lunch and dinner.
As for our 6 adjustable meeting rooms with daylight, they are equipped with microphones, paperboards, beamers and screens and accommodate up to 160 people for all types of private and professional events.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Mobility impairment: car park with reserved adapted parking spaces available, ground-level entrance or with access ramp, adapted communal toilets with transfer bar and adapted sink, 5 adapted bedrooms (bathtubs) with toilet with side space and transfer bar, dining room/breakfast room with 1.40m passing spaces.
- Visual impairment: no obstacles at eye-level, adapted command buttons in the lift (buttons in relief and/or braille) and audio announcement for each floor.
- Audio impairment: reservation and/or payment possible online, lift with visual announcements and/or sound announcements with magnetic induction loop.
- Mental impairment: easy-to-understand signs with pictograms, monitored entrance visible from reception/till.
