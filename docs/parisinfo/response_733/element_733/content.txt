                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Allow the Courtyard Paris La Defense West - Colombes to set a brand new standard for comfort and convenience. We showcase an unparalleled hotel location in Colombes, France, only minutes by tramway from La Defense, Europe's largest international business center. In addition, we're a short tram ride from downtown Paris and Stade U Arena. You'll feel right at home when you step into your spacious, newly remodeled hotel room; all lodging showcases terrific perks like a flat-screen TV, deluxe bedding, a coffee and tea maker, a mini-fridge and a large workspace. Savor handcrafted Mediterranean cuisine at our hotel's signature Oleo Pazzo Bar & Restaurant, which features a lovely terrace, or keep in shape at our on-site fitness center. Choose our hotel to host your next meeting or social event here in Colombes; we boast elegant venue space, including our restaurant's stunningly appointed outdoor terrace. Make the Courtyard Paris La Defense West - Colombes your destination of choice for visit to France.
