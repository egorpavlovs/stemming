                            Luxury hotels for a wedding reception in Paris 
                            It’s always hard to locate the perfect place to hold your wedding reception. The venue you choose will make all the difference when it comes to ensuring it’s an unforgettable occasion. Play it safe and pick the right spot with our selection of wedding venues in Paris hotels. 
                        

                        Choosing a private party room at a Paris hotel is a guarantee of a fantastic evening or weekend, with none of the fuss that goes into organizing a wedding reception. Every detail of the reception can be organized to suit your personality. If you’re a romantic, the lavish comforts of a Paris palace hotel will appeal to you. If you always have your head in a book, you might prefer an intimate ceremony amid the traditional furnishings of a cosy salon in a hotel steeped in history. Or you could invite your guests to relax at a charming hotel with well-being facilities such as a spa, hammam or pool.And, to round off your wedding day with a wonderful dinner, cocktail party, reception or dancing, our wedding specialists can help you create a bespoke event. They offer top-of-the-range services covering every single aspect, from the food and music to the decorations, flower arrangements and table layout.Here is a selection of Paris hotels that make the perfect venue for a memorable and unique wedding reception.
