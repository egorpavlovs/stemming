                        Description
                        
                                                    
        At the heart of Paris with direct access to various Parisian airports and train stations, a few minutes from Rue de Rivoli, the Louvre and Notre-Dame. With its 285 guest rooms, this Novotel offers you modern comfort with a unique sense of space and a contemporary design. More than 480 m² of adaptable rooms are available for dynamic meetings. Discover a working environment where innovation and technology are combined to help you achieve greater efficiency.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: ground-level entrance with access ramp, adapted welcome desk, adapted toilet with transfer bar and adapted sink, 7 adapted bedrooms (5 with baths and transfer bars, 2 with walk-in showers).
- Visual impairment:  lift with adapted command keys (in braille or embellished) and audio announcement for every floor.
- Hearing impairment: lift with audio announcement and magnetic induction loop.
