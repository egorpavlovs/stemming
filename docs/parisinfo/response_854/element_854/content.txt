                                                    Description
                            
                                
        We offer a three-hour excursion around Paris, the goal being to discover the city in a new way through anecdotes, discovering some of the little streets that are often missed on foot or in the car!
Here are a couple of options that can be added for your group :
- Wine tasting 
- Pétanque games
- Picnic
- Duration: extended or shortened (average tour duration: 3 hours) 
- Stops to be added during the tour
- Group size : maximum 100 personnes. (divided into smaller groups during the tour)
                            
                        
                                                

    
        
        
    
            
            
                Clients
            
            
                Leisure groups, Target individuals, School groups, Business groups
            
        
                
            
                Description of leisure groups
            
            
                
        We often organize guided bike tours for groups. Here are a couple of options that can be added for your group :
- Wine tasting 
- Pétanque games
- Picnic
- Duration: extended or shortened (average tour duration: 3 hours) 
- Stops to be added during the tour
- Group size : maximum 100 personnes. (divided into smaller groups during the tour)
This tour can be adapted for businesses, school groups, as well as for special occasions such as stag/hen parties.
