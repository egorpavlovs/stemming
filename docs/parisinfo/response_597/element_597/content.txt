                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The historic home of the French National Team, the Centre National du Football is also an important training and conference centre.
Located at Clairefontaine, less than 50 minutes from Paris, it offers a unique setting for all your professional events at the heart of the Haute Vallée de Chevreuse Regional Natural Park.
Since March 2016, the Centre National du Football has offered modern, flexible facilities, equipped with the latest technologies, for all your large events:
- 1 auditorium of 245 seats
- 1 auditorium of 110 seats
- 9 multimedia meeting rooms for 20 to 50 people
- one modular 500 m² space
The centre also offers accommodation space of 140 spacious rooms over five residences.
