                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The hotel Hilton Paris Opera has 6 meeting rooms with elegant decor and special charm. 
They can host up to 180 people, and most are lit by natural daylight. 
They provide an ideal setting for any event, conference or meeting in the centre of Paris.
