                            Opening of the chic and contemporary hotel Square Louvois****	
                            Just off the Rue Sainte-Anne, a few steps from tourist sites in the capital such as the Louvre and the Palais Garnier opera house, the facade of this hotel with its uncluttered contemporary style is certain to catch your eye.
                        

                        Step inside the Hotel Square Louvois situated at no. 12 Rue Louvois. You’ll discover an establishment impregnated with history and modernity, with interiors that mix noble materials like leather or attractively veined wood with more classic colours.On the ground floor, everything has been designed to make you feel ‘at home’, in a hushed and friendly atmosphere. The lounge library, available for private hire, is one of the most original rooms. It can host your business meetings in total privacy: for groups of 15 to 20 people according to the configuration required.  The hotel Square Louvois does everything possible to facilitate the stay of its business guests with a simplified check-in /check-out and free Wi-Fi. On the same floor, a gourmet snack is served every afternoon from 4pm to 6pm near to the magnificent La Cornue piano — ideal for a break in between business meetings.Go down to the next floor to reach the Wellness Area. Under the natural stone vaults, you can relax in the superb swimming pool, which has a hydro-massage bench, and enjoy a cup of tea or a refreshing drink in the herbal tea room in a zen atmosphere. You can also work out in our elegant and cosy fitness room.Finally, upstairs the 50 guest rooms (from 16 m² to 37 m²) all offer a serene atmosphere. The decor is sober and comfortable, with light oak parquet, blue velvet curtains, and black and white portraits of famous writers, recalling the artistic and literary success that marked the district for decades. The duplex at the top of the hotel is certain to impress you.Treat yourself to this quintessentially chic Parisian address.
