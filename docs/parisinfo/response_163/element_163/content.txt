                            Boats and barges for private hire in Paris 
                            Our guide to boats and barges available for private hire. Host a workshop, dinner cruise or private event (a birthday party or wedding reception) on the Seine or the Paris canals. 
                        

                        Always wanted to cruise along the Seine and admire some of the most iconic monuments in Paris (the Eiffel Tower, Notre Dame Cathedral, the Musée d’Orsay, the Louvre and the bridges)? Hiring a boat for a private function in Paris can be a great way to spend an enjoyable few hours and impress your guests. Organize your event on a boat or barge and take your clients and co-workers on a memorable sightseeing trip along one of the most beautiful avenues in Paris – the Seine.Hiring a boat in Paris is not just guaranteed to entertain your guests and make your private or corporate event a success, it’s also an opportunity to see the sights of Paris in a completely different way. So why not invite your guests to join you on board and enjoy a memorable gastronomic dinner or reception, or simply a sightseeing cruise. In search of private boat hire in Paris or a barge for private hire to host a workshop, group cruise, dinner cruise or private event, such as a birthday party or wedding reception?  Here is our selection of boats available for private hire in Paris. Leave your everyday routine on dry land and set off on a relaxing cruise.
