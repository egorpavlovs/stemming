                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Hidden behind a discreet carriage-entrance doorway, the hotel Les Jardins du Marais has a pleasant private open-air garden comprising a 1,500 m² area of small courtyards and seating areas, surrounded by palm trees to make your event truly relaxing. The main courtyard is none other than the terrace of the restaurant (55 seats), which serves delicious summer dishes.
