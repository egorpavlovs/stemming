                            Organize your event in the vicinity of Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport
                            There’s a comprehensive selection of hotels around Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport to suit all budgets and requirements. Many also have meeting rooms for successfully hosting seminars, team-building sessions or conferences. Convention facilities and corporate venues also abound in the wider area, within easy reach of Disneyland Paris, Aéroville shopping centre, and the exhibition centres of Paris-Nord Villepinte and Le Bourget. See below our selection of parisian partners!
                        

                        Pullman Paris Roissy CDG Airport       The recently opened 4-star Pullman Paris Roissy CDG Airport hotel offers a first-rate stopover particularly suitable for groups and business travellers. Throughout the hotel, staff put the accent on great hospitality, a tranquil atmosphere, and flexibility. The 13 on-site meeting and conference rooms are fully modular and enjoy an unrivalled view of the take-off runways. Don't miss the lounge designed by Matthieu Lehanneur, the Business Playground by Pullman (capacity: 12 people), a travel-themed cabinet of curiosities that fires creativity.The 305 rooms and suites (including the Exclusive suite with its 100 m² terrace) in soft, natural colourways may be reserved for overnight stays or booked for just a few hours. To enjoy a moment of wellbeing between flights or after a day's work, treat yourself in the FitLounge (swimming pool, Turkish Baths and sauna). The hotel also boasts a fitness centre, a welcoming restaurant and a sophisticated cocktail bar.Paris Marriott Charles de Gaulle Airport HotelThe 4-star Paris Marriot Charles de Gaulle AirportHotel welcomes guests in a designer setting. Sixteen meeting rooms are available for conferences and seminars, including a plenary hall that holds 550 people.You can sample French cuisine at contemporary bar-restaurant Le Trivium or take a break with delicious cakes at the Biscoti coffee shop. Facilities include 300 rooms and suites, a fitness centre with machines that connect with your smartphone, and two saunas.Golden Tulip Paris CDG Airport Villepinte HotelAlong with its 152 bedrooms, the 4-star Golden Tulip Paris CDG Airport Villepinte Hotel’s facilities include six modular conference rooms (51 m² to 153 m²) that can accommodate up to 160 people, and 2 breakout rooms. The 100-seat restaurant serves an American-style breakfast from 5.30 am. On the 6th floor a bar named 6ème Sens (Sixth Sense) offers a selection of cocktails and snacks with a fresh twist. The "Vallée Verte", two minutes' walk away, is an oasis of tranquillity for nature lovers. Also very nearby is the 3-star Campanile Roissy Hotel. Note that these two establishments can offer combined packages for business trips or large-scale events.Relais Spa Paris-Roissy Charles de Gaulle"A wellness break for body and mind" is the proposition of the 5-star Relais Spa Paris-Roissy Charles de Gaulle Hotel. You’ll see what they mean as soon as you enter their elegant, light-filled lobby, designed for conviviality and conversation. Then there’s the luxuriously equipped 40 m² Executive Lounge that can be privately booked to provide a haven of peace between two flights, stocked with magazines, snacks and board games. Seven meeting rooms (75 m² to 380 m²), a plenary hall (380 m²) and eight breakout rooms are available for event hire. For meals, you can opt for the prestigious Brasserie Flo, before heading back to your room in the soothing atmosphere of this 465-room establishment. If you feel like a little time out, the Spa & Fitness area – open till 11 pm – offers a sauna, a Turkish Bath, a fitness centre, and a complete range of treatments using Nuxe® skincare products.Novotel Convention & Wellness Roissy CDGThe 4-star Novotel Convention & Wellness Roissy Hotel has 17 meeting rooms (1,700 m²) that can be tailored to suit your meetings or conferences. Its 295 bright and spacious rooms provide cosy comfort, and guests enjoy complementary access to the 600 m² spa zone (swimming pool, sauna, Turkish Bath and fitness room). A 50 m² Suite for up to four people offers a modern, welcoming design in an atmosphere of chic and calm. The restaurant serves a buffet breakfast from 5 am and is open until midnight.Hyatt Regency Paris-Charles de GaulleThe Hyatt Regency Paris-Charles de GaulleHotel hosts all kinds of meetings and conferences in its 28 modular meeting rooms, which include the 800 m² Campus space designed by Viserius Studio (seven rooms holding from 10 to 70 people) where delegates enjoy services tailored to their needs. The hotel offers 388 soundproofed, modern bedrooms. For a moment of relaxation, the Regency Club is a special area set aside for privileged guests. The hotel's striking atrium space holds the Apollo restaurant, where the menu changes daily, while the Cosmos bar welcomes guests in an ambiance of refined elegance.Hilton Paris Charles de Gaulle AirportThe 4-star Hilton Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport Hotel contains a business centre with 24 meeting rooms, as well as the Concorde Hall, whose outstanding capacity (650 seated, 1,200 for a buffet) makes it ideal for conferences, weddings, and seminars. The Hotel's 385 rooms fall into two categories: the Hilton room, and the Executive room with access to the Executive Lounge, where you can enjoy breakfast in total privacy. Beneath a magnificent glass atrium, the Skylight restaurant serves delicious French cuisine until 11 pm. There’s 24-hour room service and a bar where you can try out original cocktails. A wellbeing and fitness area is also open to guests. Les Fontaines High-tech meeting rooms, a 460-seat auditorium, numerous restaurants and seven detached residences providing 300 bedrooms within a 128-acre park – welcome to Campus Capgemini Les Fontaines in Chantilly! This is the perfect setting for meetings, seminars and conferences lasting one day or several. Set in the heart of this huge complex, the Rothschild Château seats up to 400 people for dinner in its reception rooms. The venue also features a VIP area comprising fifteen meeting rooms ranging from 22 m² to 240 m² and four workshop rooms with private terrace. A Normandy-style farm is available to small groups for outdoor workshops. Whether you want to eat Indian or organic, a brunch or a barbecue, the chef and his team are at your service to meet your culinary requirements with catering tailored to suit your particular event. There are plenty of sports facilities, both indoors and out, for individual or team activities: a swimming pool overlooking the park, mountain bike hire, a fitness centre, tennis courts, a football pitch, golf, and more.  Domaine de ChantillyLess than an hour from Paris, the Domaine de Chantilly offers a relaxing, bucolic break that combines pleasure with learning thanks to its rich history and cultural programming designed for all ages. The estate covers more than 19,000 acres and its most notable features include the Condé Museum, an enormous park, the Great Stables and the Museum of the Horse. Its huge spaces are used for events: the Hippodrome, which hosts internationally renowned equestrian events throughout the year, has three dining rooms seating from 180 to 450; the Great Stables boast several splendid rooms including the Grande Nef with a capacity of 450; the Jeu de Paume hosts temporary exhibitions, with private views followed by cocktails and prestigious dinners for up to 200 guests. Enjoy an unforgettable experience in a venue steeped in the art of French living.

            Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport: a dynamic environment for travel and business
        
        Paris-Charles de Gaulle, also known as Roissy Airport, is constantly reinventing itself to provide the technology, shopping, and food outlets that guarantee a unique customer experience.The surrounding area has also seen an uplift, with more than 20 hotels close to the airport, masses of meeting rooms, a high-end shopping centre with 200 retail units, and event venues providing distinctive, top quality hospitality services. And what about getting into central Paris? Nothing could be simpler, thanks to the city’s extensive modern transport network.
