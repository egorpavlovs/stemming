                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The restaurant and theatre ReminiSens invites you on an enchanting journey through time to discover the customs of the French Court. 
Located in Versailles, in the city chosen by Louis XIV to be the symbol of French wealth and power, you will dive into the heart of the 17th and 18th centuries!
Dishes inspired by old recipe books, baroque costumes and theatre sketches at your table telling you stories and anecdotes of life at the chateau: history comes to your table!
ReminiSens welcomes you from Tuesday to Saturday evenings between 7pm and 11pm for a spectacular dinner show!
For team building exercises, we also organize private participatory evening shows upon request: ‘Plot at Versailles’ dinner: foil the conspiracy plotted against the King; Baroque Orchestra; Magic at the Court.
Together, we invent your dream-like night!
