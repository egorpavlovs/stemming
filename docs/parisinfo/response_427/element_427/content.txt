                            An insider’s guide to the capital with Paris on the Way
                            Paris on the Way and its team of tour guides takes you to all parts of the French capital for guided tours to experience just like a Parisian.
                        

                        Ideal for groups and incentives, Paris on the Way tours take you to see another side of Paris, placing emphasis on arts & crafts, gastronomy and the daily life of Parisians, through a catalogue of more than 100 guided tours, in 5 categories: visits to museums,  and visits to monuments, with classics such as the Louvre Museum, but also more unusual places such as the Conciergerie or Sainte Chapelle; thematic visits, on for example the French Revolution or the second world war; gourmet visits, to see how croissants are made in the Montmartre district or a visit to Saint-Germain-des-Prés for chocolate and cakes & pastry; and visits to chateaux and abbeys,  including a visit to the Palace of Versailles. Tours are available in 7 different languages: French, English, Spanish, Portuguese, Russian and Chinese from 1 to 25 people.Paris on the Way also offers consulting services such as the creation of tailored visits, the search for suppliers and logistics support (entry tickets, bookings, etc. ...).
