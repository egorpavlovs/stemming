                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Since 1911, the Hôtel Plaza Athénée has welcomed the world's most renowned guests. Situated on the prestigious Avenue Montaigne, a stone's throw from the Champs Elysées and the Eiffel Tower, the hotel exudes a delicate balance of luxury and refinement. Decoration, gastronomy and lifestyle are celebrated in a magical decor with 'Plaza red' tones.

                        Other services at the establishment
                        Dior Institut:
Exception ensured by two prestigious brands... The Dior Institut at the Plaza Athénée offers five treatment booths, including one double booth, a relaxation room, a hammam and a sauna for a dream break just a few minutes from the Champs Elysées. To tone, reoxygenate, rebalance the skin and body, Dior uses exclusive methods such as 3D lifting, microdermabraison with with sapphire particles, and Dior Anti-Ageing active concentrates.
