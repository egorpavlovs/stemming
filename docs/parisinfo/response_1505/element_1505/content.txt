                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Eight magnificent reception rooms and a latest-generation conference room for your event
With high ceilings and a muted atmosphere, the 9 reception rooms can be adjusted according to the size of your group and have a view of the Eiffel Tower or the garden.
The rooms can be adapted to suit your event, while retaining the charm of the Hôtel des Arts et Métiers that your guests will love.
La Rochefoucauld conference room, air-conditioned and equipped with the latest multimedia technology, can host up to 300 people.
