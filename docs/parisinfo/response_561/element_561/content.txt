                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A prestigious venue with a five-star hospitality program!
Since its reopening, the AccorHotels Arena offers 3000sqm of VIP hospitality spaces for corporate events. 
4 Reception rooms from 330 sqm to 600 sqm offering a unique atmosphere, 4 Sub commissions rooms from 100 to 200sqm.
Fully modular, these spaces can be adapted to all types of events: seminar, exhibition area, cocktail, seated dinner, dance party ...
The privatization of the spaces comes with high quality service: Unlimited WIFI, Modern Audiovisual Equipment, Host/Hostesses to welcome your participants, Cloakroom service, Security staff, Cleaning permanence, On-site catering service, Dedicated coordinator.
Incentive: the Arena offers unique services to add an unforgettable experience to your event: from the organization of the Welcome Coffee inside the bowl for an immersion in the mythical venue, to the privatization of the ice rink or the possibility to package your event with one of the 140 incredible shows scheduled at the Arena!
