                            The Belles Plantes café brasserie within the Jardin des Plantes
                            The Belles Plantes café brasserie, beautifully situated within the Jardin des Plantes, just a stone’s throw from the buzzing Latin Quarter and Seine quaysides, is a splendid venue for your events.
                        

                        Nestled in the midst of the greenery of one of the finest parks in Paris, Les Belles Plantes offers exceptional surroundings. The reception spaces consist of two terraces: on the courtyard side (50 people seated and 80 for a cocktail reception) and on the garden side (48 seated and 50 for a cocktail reception), the patio (44 seated and 60 for a cocktail reception) and the small dining room with floral decoration. The venue is also available for full private hire with a capacity for up to 170 people seated and 230 for a cocktail reception. All types of events can be hosted such as study days, business meals, after-work drinks ... Plus, there are many other private hire possibilities at the Jardin des Plantes.The restaurant menu features fine fresh and seasonal produce with substantial dishes, and Parisian brasserie classics. Sample the ‘summer platter’ of focaccia with basil, tapenade, crispy prawns and vegetable antipasti! The restaurant is open daily for lunch, and from April to September for dinner. In addition to the hire of spaces and catering, other services are available such as video projection equipment, floral decoration, welcome hostesses and musical entertainment.

            The Monnaie de Paris is now home to world-renowned, Michelin-starred French chef Guy Savoy!
        
        A highlight of the celebration of French culture, the Monnaie de Paris is now home to world-renowned, Michelin-starred French chef Guy Savoy who is at the helm of the restaurant that bears his name. In an exclusive interview, he touches upon the unique character of the ‘Maison Guy Savoy’ that has led to it been crowned the best restaurant in the world.  

    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            ‘The Mandarin Oriental is the Paris of today and tomorrow’ - Thierry Marx interview
        
        The Mandarin Oriental Paris, a symbol of luxury and elegance, is a 5-star hotel on Rue Saint-Honoré Street near to Place Vendôme and the Louvre. Its different restaurants offer inventive dishes with amazing flavours, by famous chef and pioneer of molecular cuisine Thierry Marx. An exceptional place with an offer particularly adapted for business customers.

    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            Printemps du Goût: new gourmet hot spot
        
        A delight for the eyes and taste buds! Printemps has opened Printemps du Goût, a new food space over two floors, in the recently renovated menswear building Printemps de l’Homme, completely devoted to the best of French gastronomy.
    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            Parisian gastronomy is making headlines!
        
        Paris is one of the must-visit cities to test the ‘gourmet meal of the French’, listed since 2010 as part of the world’s cultural heritage by Unesco.
