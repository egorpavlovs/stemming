                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Ideally located a few minutes away from La Défense business district, and directly connected to the Palais des Congrès, Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile was recently transformed by a complete renovation. On top of its 995 rooms and Suites, the hotel offer 3350sqm of meeting space, adapted to every event type. 14 Labs, 7 Towers, 14 Regency: each of its 35 meeting rooms offers natural daylight, state-of-the-art audiovisual equipment and high-speed Internet. The hotel capacity is one of the most important in Europe.
Directly connected to the Palais des Congrès, the hotel offers everything under one roof: shopping center, bars and restaurants, cinema, public parking, public transportation… Only skyscraper hotel inside Paris, the Hyatt Regency Paris Etoile offers breathtaking panoramas over Paris, especially from its panoramic bar, Windo, located 140m above the city.
