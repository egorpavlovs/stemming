                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Salle Wagram welcomes prestigious and large-scale events (receptions, congresses, seminars). Up to 1,300 people for a cocktail reception over the two floors, 800 for a conference and 600 for a sit-down dinner with a ceiling height of 12 metres. The Salle Montenotte is a 600 m² flexible space that can be used in addition to the Salle Wagram. Classified as a historical monument, the fully-renovated Salle Wagram has retained its original decor while integrating the latest technology.
