                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Situated on the quays of the Seine, in front of the Eiffel Tower, the Mona Bismark American Center for Art & Culture welcomes you into an elegant town house in the 16th arrondissement.
Built at the end of the 19th century, this sumptuous town house was entirely redesigned for Mona Bismarck at the end of the 1950s by the illustrious decorator, StÃ©phane Boudin. Superb rooms where woodwork from an old chateau and Chinese wallpaper are brought together, giving the centre its unique charm.
This elegant residence extends across three floors and boasts a spacious terrace overlooking a garden. The floor opens onto large rooms bathed in light offering a beautiful setting for your functions.
