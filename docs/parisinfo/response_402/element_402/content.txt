                            Discover the Fabrique Événementielle, a new events venue in the east of Paris!
                            Ideally located a few steps from the République district, this brand new events venue opens its doors for your professional events. 
                        

                        The Fabrique Événementielle (Paris Evénement) offers a surface area of 700 m² over 2 levels. Spaces may be hired together or separately according to the requirements of your event. The ground floor, characterized by its industrial interiors, is suitable for study days as well as evening functions with dancing. That little extra: there are no sound constraints and no time constraints ... your event can last all night! Climb the stairs to appreciate the fine glass roof, channelling natural light. A charming little terrace adds conviviality to this floor.On the two levels, it is possible to organize a conference (140 people), a meeting (35 people), a sit-down dinner (100 people) or a cocktail reception (250 people).
