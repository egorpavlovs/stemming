                        Description
                        
                                                    
        This prestigious hotel is located in the heart of Paris on the sophisticated & cosmopolitan Boulevard Haussmann, just a stone's throw away from the best Paris has to offer, including the Louvre, Musée d'Orsay, Sacré-Cœur, and the Avenue des Champs-Elysées.
The blend of his unique style with contemporary luxury and comforts makes the Millennium Hotel Paris Opéra a residence of great distinction
Each of the six air-conditioned meeting rooms not only provides you with a setting of traditional charm and character, but a host of advanced technical equipment and superior sound-proofing. The sumptuous private foyer, leading to five suites, evokes an atmosphere of quiet repose and is an impressive site for every occasion.
Dedicated meetings and events team
Complimentary Wi-Fi connectivity
Audio-visual support
Multifunctional lighting
Dedicated reception and foyer areas
Syndicate and breakout rooms available
Brasserie Haussmann / Bar Le Douze / Café AU
24 hour business centre

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: ground-level entrance or with access ramp, adapted communal toilets with transfer bar and adapted sink, 2 adapted bedrooms and bathroom equipped with walk-in shower (with folding seat or mobile seat), adapted sink and toilet with side space and transfer bar, breakfast room with large passing spaces and adapted tables.
- Visual impairment: information and signs in large print, signs at an appropriate height.
- Hearing impairment: reservation and/or payment possible online.
- Learning disablity: easy-to-read signs with pictograms, monitored entrance visible from reception.
