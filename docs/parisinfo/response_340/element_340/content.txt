                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Nestled on the roof of the museum and dressed in an elegant glass structure, the restaurant les Ombres unfolds on an outdoor terrace with a breathtaking view of the Eiffel Tower and the banks of the Seine. The subdued atmosphere that reigns in this refined setting leaves everything to the city lights and makes it a must for your special evenings.
Privatization:
Breakfasts, lunches, evenings, every day of the week
110 in seated format
200 in cocktail format
Group lunch & dinner for 11-40 people:
One service at lunchtime
Two evening services, at 7pm and 9.30pm.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Motor disability: level or slightly inclined entryway, access by lift, adapted bathroom equipped with a transfer bar and an accessible sink, dining room with aisles greater than 1.40 m wide and with adapted tables allowing the approach of a person in a wheelchair.
- Visual impairment: transparent glass doors / walls preceded by contrasting signage, elevator with adapted control keypad (raised and / or Braille keys) and with sound stage with announced floor.
- Hearing impairment: menus and online booking, elevator with visual and / or audible announcement with magnetic induction loop, flash light coupled with fire alarm.
- Mental disability: guarded entrance door, visible from reception / cash desk.
