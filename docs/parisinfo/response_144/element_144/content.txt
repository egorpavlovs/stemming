                            Upscale venues and luxury rooms in Paris
                            Paris has many prestigious venues and elegant rooms for exceptional events, with a luxury stamp.
                        

                        Upscale venues and luxury rooms in ParisParis’s upscale events offer lives up to the capital’s reputation: eclectic and magical. Paris has many prestigious venues and elegant rooms for exceptional events, with a luxury stamp. All you have to do is choose, and to help you with your decision, we have put together our top 10!Les Salons du Pré-Catelan LenôtreLa Maison Lenôtre offers unique venues like the Pré-Catelan. Tucked away in the heart of the Bois de Boulogne, it has 14 modular rooms available for private hire for events of all sizes. This elegant Napoleon III Pavilion, with listed interior decor, befits the most elegant private and professional events. You can organize professional dinners, meetings, fashion shows and gala evenings here as well as private functions like weddings, balls, themed buffets, shows and kosher functions. A haven of peace just a few minutes from the Champs-Elysées.From 8 to 1,800 peopleWe like:The ideal location with its green surroundingsPavillon Cambon CapucinesHold your event in a Potel et Chabot pavilion, an exceptional venue. In close proximity to some of the most renowned jewellers, the Palais Garnier Opera House and the Olympia Concert Hall, the Pavillon Cambon Capucines enjoys a unique location in Paris. Available for private hire, the venue boasts a surface area of 1,600m² spread across four rooms where elegant marble, glass and wood fittings provide the setting for unforgettable meals, seminars, private parties and cocktail parties.From 10 to 1,500 peopleWe like:The Salon Mewès: a 700m² function room with stunning architecture, with a 400m² mezzanine and an impressive glass roofPavillon GabrielInspired by classical architecture, the Pavillon Gabriel, a former café-concert hall, was built in 1841. Recently renovated and managed by Potel and Chabot, it welcomes you right in the heart of the gardens of the Champs-Elysées. The venue is a haven of peace bathed in daylight with large bay windows and a high glass roof, and a stunning place to hold a reception in the evening thanks to its interior decor, fixtures, lights, and stage lighting. The Pavillon Gabriel’s 4 reception rooms are suitable for any type of event.From 10 to 2,000 people.We like: The huge terraceThe highlighting of features of historical heritageHôtel d’EvreuxAlso managed by Potel et Chabot, the Hôtel d’Évreux, a private mansion situated in Place Vendôme, hosts the most luxurious events in the capital. With its small and large rooms in 18th-century, Directoire and Napoléon III style, the Hôtel d'Evreux can be hired out for lunches, cocktail parties and prestigious dinners but also for exceptional private functions.The Salle des Tirages, bathed in light from its glass ceiling, is a truly luxurious venue for your events.  From 10 to 350 peopleWe like:The uninterrupted view of the Place Vendôme and its famous columnStudio HarcourtThe Studio Harcourt, an essential reference for photography in Paris – all the celebrities have their photo portraits taken here – offers you its spaces for your private and professional events. Situated at the heart of Paris’s Golden Triangle, the venue combines privacy, discretion and magic.For breakfast, lunch or dinner, the Studio Harcourt stages your events in a decor reserved for stars. Dance events are not authorized.Up to 1,000 peopleWe like:The portraits of the rich and famous which adorn the walls of this legendary place. You can even have photos taken of your guests on the day.Salomon de RothschildA prestigious listed monument full of history, this magnificent neoclassic building, built in 1878 is the former mansion of Baroness Adèle de Rothschild. Right in the heart of Paris, a short walk from Place de l’Etoile, the Hôtel Salomon de Rothschild is a venue entirely devoted to private functions and corporate events. The interior spaces available for full private hire total 3,900m² and include a 500m² events stage with high quality audiovisual equipment and recording equipment (shooting of TV programmes possible).Up to 1,200 peopleWe like:The authentic French lifestyleThe three enclosed gardens and park Yacht JoséphineThe Yacht Joséphine, a luxurious houseboat moored opposite the Eiffel Tower, offers an exceptional setting and many bespoke services, such as a cruise and tastings of top-quality products and blind wine tastings. Ideal for small private receptions or seminars, it has 4 double cabins, a large indoor lounge with cutting-edge technology, an upper deck with a surface area of more than 80 m² and access to a jacuzzi, plus a rear deck equipped with a professional kitchen. From 2 to 60 peopleWe like :The wide range of activities on offerThe breathtaking view of the Eiffel TowerPalais BrongniartLocated between the place de la Concorde and the Louvre Museum, the Palais Brongniart benefits from high level congress equipment thanks to its two auditoriums of 600 and 100 places. Their multiple possibilities of use set them as perfect spaces to host the most significant events of the French capital, in a prestigious business district. You can also enjoy its legendary spaces such as “La Nef”, the “Salon d’Honneur” or the “Espace Corbeille” in order to organize your most beautiful reception up to 1,200 people into this symbolic and history-filled place. Le Palais Brongniart provides you a vast work area made up of four meeting rooms.Up to 1,200 peopleWe like:The immensity of this historic monument, located in one of the most famous places of ParisHôtel Le Marois - France AmériquesIdeally located between the Avenue Montaigne and the Grand Palais, in the heart of Paris, the Hôtel Le Marois – France Amériques is an exceptional place for your events. This hôtel particulier with a typical Haussmannian architecture offers 8 historic lounges, decorated with 18th century woodwork and paintings and contemporary furniture.This prestigious 850 m² venue can be hired out for cocktail parties, diners, evening with dancing or seminars. Up to 700 peopleWe like:The magnificent terrace facing the Palais de la DécouverteLa Maison des Polytechniciens Whether it be a seminar, a cocktail buffet or a wedding, the Maison des Polytechniciens hosts the finest Parisian functions in an elegant setting.This mansion, located close to the Musée d’Orsay, provides businesses and individuals with entirely renovated rooms, a courtyard, a delightful garden, a terrace and superb vaulted cellars – a perfect location in which to organize dance evenings!From 10 to 600 peopleWe like:Benefiting from the garden and terrace in good weather
