                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Ibis Paris La Villette Cité des Sciences hotel has 284 rooms including 6 rooms for guests with reduced mobility , situated along the Canal de l’Ourcq, near the Cité des Sciences , the Villette park, the Zenith and the Philharmonie de Paris. If you need to relax, a bar, a Ibis Kitchen Pizza&Pasta restaurant and 24-hour snack service are at your disposition. A business corner and Wifi access are also available for free. Parking with 16 seats (reservation required).
