                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The contemporary brasserie drawn from the past…
Opposite Saint-Eustache church, Champeaux is the brasserie of the 21st century. From breakfast to dinner, this open and bright place bustles with activity all day long. The menu takes a fresh look at the brasserie cuisine classics. The dynamic wine menu with a European flair and the sophisticated cocktails evolve with the seasons.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        Motor disability: adapted and reserved parking spaces for wheelchair users, ground floor entrance or entrance with sloping surface, low reception desk adapted for wheelchair users, toilet facilities adapted and equipped with transfer bar and sink adapted for wheelchair users, accessible dining room, dining area with aisles wider than 1.4 metres and with adapted tables (height of 70cm so as not to obstruct wheelchair users).
Visual disability: transparent glass doors and walls with contrasting elements, absence of obstacles at face level to impede circulation.
Hearing disability: booking and/or payments available to do online, flashing lights when fire alarm sounds.
Mental disability: attentive staff, legible and easy-to-understand signaling system using pictograms, supervised entry and visible from the reception desk.
