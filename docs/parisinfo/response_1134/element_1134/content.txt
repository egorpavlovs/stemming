                            The Louis, MGallery by Sofitel 4* sparkles at Versailles
                            The Louis Versailles Château MGallery by Sofitel, formerly the Pullman Château de Versailles, opened its doors in late July after complete renovation. At this 19th-century hotel, opposite the Palace of Versailles, contemporary elegance is matched with classic refinement in interiors where light plays a prominent part.
                        

                        A few steps from the Palace and the Palais des Congrès de Versailles and 30 minutes by car from Paris expo Porte de Versailles, the Louis enjoys a perfect location, both for business or a leisure break.The 4-star hotel has 152 luxury rooms, including 11 suites, some with a balcony. Modern and comfortable, they feature minimalist lines and soft colours conducive to relaxation.The Louis’ 8 fully-modular renovated meeting spaces (600 m² in total), equipped with the latest technology, can accommodate up to 180 people. The Marquis rooms (Montespan, Maintenon and Pompadour), and the Orangerie provide a prestigious setting for major events ... A dedicated team of experts is on hand to assist you in the planning of your professional or private project.The 60-coverAlcôve restaurant decorated with golden lighting and the bar, beneath a glass roof and a huge contemporary light feature, are a reminder of the splendours of the Palace. The flavoursome cuisine mixes traditional and modern, like the hotel.Finally, the hotel offers a wellness space with a spa, hammam, sauna and fitness room — a wonderful place to unwind after a seminar or a day’s sightseeing.
