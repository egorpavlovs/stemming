                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A short walk from the Champs Elysées, in our bright Scandinavian-inspired Business Centre, 2 fully equipped conference rooms are available for you to hold meetings, seminars, press conferences and company events. The terrace situated on the 9th floor offers you a breathtaking view of Paris, perfect for hosting your cocktail parties and company events.
