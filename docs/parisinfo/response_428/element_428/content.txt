                            Paris pour un jour turns the capital into a playground
                            The events agency Paris pour un jour, which specializes in organizing cultural visits, is giving you the chance to discover the City of Light in a different way, thanks to its themed programmes which are a little off the beaten track.
                        

                        And what if you became the hero of a cinema-themed mystery trail for half a day? This is THE new event from Paris pour un jour! Named Macadam Heroes, this game puts you and your teammates into the shoes of a film crew: urgently summoned to the set of the new Bernard Audiard film, your mission is to find the film’s star, Marion Cotillon, who has disappeared! You set out on foot to search the streets of Paris, taking many tests along the way: filmmaking, quizz, etc. You will have at your disposal digital tablets, suitcases of accessories, and personalized badges. This crazy adventure is available in English and French for groups of 6 to 200 people, split into teams of 6 to 10 people.Paris pour un jour also offers other made-to-measure, themed programmes that are a mix of relaxation, originality, and conviviality, such as treasure hunts, VIP visits, or team-building activities in secret locations across the capital. Teams of experienced tour guides will show you around in a way that is fun and unusual. You can also be accompanied on VIP evenings and choose from a selection of unique places.
