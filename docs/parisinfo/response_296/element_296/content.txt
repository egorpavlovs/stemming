                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Palais des Congr√®s located at Porte Maillot, only a short distance from the Champs-Elys√©es, is a leading venue for international congresses and trade shows, and an excellent location for an event.
Spread over a total area of 32,000 m¬≤, including 19,000m¬≤ of exhibition surface area, the venue has 4 amphitheatres on 3 levels, 20 break-out rooms and 85 meeting rooms. The Palais des Congr√®s is an internationally renowned venue hosting major international congresses, corporate events and shows by top-name performers. This leading Paris events centre is also an ideal space in which to organize trade fairs.
The gigantic venue has a number of flexible spaces, making it perfectly suited for organizing workshops, team building weekends, cocktail parties and corporate dinners. A team of highly experienced event planners is on hand to help you put together an unforgettable evening for your guests.
Conveniently located in the centre of Paris, the legendary Palais des Congr√®s is both prestigious and very functional. Please use our venue-specific contact form to request an estimate and select the most suitable configuration for your event. The venue offers the guarantee of welcoming your clients or co-workers in optimal conditions, and putting on a successful conference or event.
