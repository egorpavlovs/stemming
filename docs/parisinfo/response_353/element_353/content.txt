                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The new JO&JOE brand
JO&JOE has created the Open House, a new form of hospitality. A house that’s open to all, including locals and travelers alike, or “Townsters” and “Tripsters” as we prefer to call them. A cool and considerate social hub, with a vibrant atmosphere and surprising design.
It’s a hybrid product that’s a mix of:
a youth hostel, for its social experience, meeting people, sharing and interacting with others
a hotel, for the quality, security and comfort it offers
a rented apartment, for the freedom and independence offered, with flexible and versatile areas to be used independently and in complete freedom. 
JO&JOE’s disruptive design was jointly created with the British design agency PENSON, known for creating spaces that are not only smart but look fabulous too, for the world’s most visionary brands. Flagship designs include the campuses for Google, YouTube, PlayStation and Jay Z ROCnation as well as the interior design of the world’s 5th tallest tower, the Lotte World Tower.
JO&JOE’s landed in Paris!
JO&JOE PARIS is located south of the capital, between Porte d'Orléans and Porte de Gentilly, just a 5- minute walk from the Paris Cité Internationale Universitaire international student accommodation site and 10 minutes away from Charlety Stadium. It’s right by public transport facilities to get from Orly and Roissy-Charles-de-Gaulle airports in 35 or 45 minutes respectively.
We offer double or triple rooms as well as rooms for 4, 5 or 6 people. They each have their own bathrooms. The dormitories accommodate between 8 and 12 guests, with access to a bathroom.
Happy House is a completely private place reserved for people staying with us. It includes a shared kitchen, washing machines, ironing board, lockers and travel items such as adapters, eye masks, toiletries etc.
Our bar & restaurant
A continental buffet breakfast is served every morning between 7 am and 10 am (and up until 11 am at weekends). The 140-seat restaurant serves freshly-prepared authentic dishes, at prices starting from €7. 
JO&JOE Paris also has a lively bar, a self-service beer wall and a welcoming DJ stage with concerts. The garden that stretches over 200 m2 allows those staying with us and bar guests to relax, have a drink and take part in the activities on offer, such as a yoga class, a cocktail master class and so on.
We’ve got a different way of working at JO&JOE!
There’s WIFI throughout, all the time. An 80m2 meeting space with unconventional design and natural lighting is available for rent. It can accommodate up to 80 people and can be partitioned into two entirely separate rooms. It’s also got all the necessary equipment for holding meetings, press conferences or product launches. You can also enjoy exclusive use of part of the garden.
