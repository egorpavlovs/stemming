                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: car park with adapted parking spaces, ground-level entrance with access ramp, adapted welcome desk, adapted toilet with transfer bar and adapted sink, 9 adapted bedrooms with bathroom with walk-in shower (with moveable seat) and adapted sink, adapted toilet with transfer bar, restaurant with adapted tables so wheelchairs can fit underneath.
- Visual impairment: doors/walls with contrasting elements, staircase with continuous handrail, no obstacles at eye level in the corridors, lift with adapted command keys (in braille or embellished) and audio announcement for every floor.
- Hearing impairment: reservation and/or payment possible on the website.
- Mental disability: trained staff.
