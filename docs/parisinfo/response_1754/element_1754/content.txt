                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Maison France Voyage, the French division of Maisons du Voyage, offers customized stays for Asian customers, particularly Chinese companies wishing to organize trips to France. With over 25 years experience and a bicultural team, we offer a 360 ° service in French-Mandarin Chinese through leisure and business tourism proposals tailored to each client.
    
                            
                        
                        
    
        
            
            
        
    
    
                    
                
                    Year agency created
                
                
                    1991
                
            
                            
                
                    Priority markets
                
                
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        China, Korea, Hong Kong, India, Pakistan, Indonesia, Japan, Singapore, Thailand, Vietnam
                
            
                                    

                        

    
        
        
    
            
            
                Clients
            
            
                Leisure groups, Target individuals, Business groups
            
        
                
            
                Description of leisure groups
            
            
                
        Groups travelling as families, friends or couples, trips as business delegates, for a seminar or event, we offer internaries and stays that are close to the expectations of our customers.
    
            
        
                
            
                Description of business groups
            
            
                
        Groups travelling as families, friends or couples, trips as business delegates, for a seminar or event, we offer internaries and stays that are close to the expectations of our customers.
