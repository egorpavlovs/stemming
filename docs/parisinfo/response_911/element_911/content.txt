                            What's on?
                            
        New in 2018: RATP welcomes Chinese travellers with its RATP WeChat Chinese account. Available on AppStore or Google Play.
    
                        
                    
                    
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        If you’re planning or need to attend an event, seminar, congress, trade show in Paris and the Paris region, don't forget to plan your travel in Paris and its region, in advance. For this the RATP offers you different transport solutions: the Paris Visite pass for zones 1-3 or 1-5, for from 1 to 5 days (unlimited access to all public transport, with reductions on 14 tourist attractions), 10 tickets T +. , Paris/suburbs tickets (Paris to Parc des Expositions at Villepinte, Paris / La Défense, etc), all connections to airports (Orlyval, RER B Roissy CDG, Orlybus, Roissybus), etc. Please contact the RATP, Patricia Lyonnard, a minimum of 2 weeks prior to your event and for a minimum purchase of €300. Payment by bank transfer only. The tickets/passes can be shipped worldwide.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Motor disability: the entire Paris Bus network (63 lines), more than 70% of the Bus network in the suburbs (i.e. more than 200 lines), 63 RER A and B stations out of the 65 in the RATP network, all tramway lines, and line 14 of the metro are accessible to people with disabilities, and to wheelchair users.

                            Sustainable development approach
                Energy savings and reduction of CO2 emissions (bus plan 2025 with 80% of electric buses and 20% of biogas buses); economy of resources for a circular economy; reduction of emissions of any nature and protection of biodiversity; promotion of employment and regional development.
