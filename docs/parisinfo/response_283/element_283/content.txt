                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Located on the prestigious avenue George V, this historical Art Deco style hotel built in 1929 features 159 guestrooms, including 44 suites.
L’Appartement Parisien, a duplex suite of 165 m² with a private terrace offers panoramas of Paris, including a view of the Eiffel Tower. Gastronomic experiences await in our Michelin starred restaurant La Scène headed by Chef Stephanie Le Quellec and in the cocktail Bar Les Heures, both extending to the famous mosaic patio. A privileged meeting space for the most exclusive events up to 70 attendees. Dedicated Clef d’Or concierges to discover the most beautiful Maisons of French artisanal know-how and Paris’ hidden treasures. State-of-the art fitness centre and Wellness Suite by Olivier Lecocq.
