                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A new cultural venue for your events.
Settled in a 19th century entirely restored foundry, the Atelier des Lumi√®res is a new venue holding monumental immersive exhibitions. In a more than 1 300 m¬≤ area, images and videos are showed, in music, covering a total surface area of 3,300 m2, extending from the floors to the ceilings and over walls up to ten meters high. With its unique equipment, the Atelier des Lumi√®res offers you the possibility to transform and modernize your seminars‚Äô presentations and to create a personalized environment. Flexibility for made to measure events:
The Halle is the main area of the Atelier des Lumi√®res. Its digital equipment allows you to personalize your environment with different options: the original industrial setting, the projection of the current immersive exhibition or your own animation on the walls of the
Halle.
