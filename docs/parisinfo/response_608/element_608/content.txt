                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The hotel is strategically located in the center of the pedestrian Bercy Village neighborhood, The Pullman Paris Bercy has 1,800 m2 of meeting rooms on three floors including 20 modular meeting rooms and an amphitheater that can seat up to 300 people. The meeting rooms can be configured into a wide range of customized layouts. The 396 spacious rooms and suites offer the best of the Pullman experience: a large desk and desk armchair, the Pullman bed with memory foam pillows, a rain shower, guest amenities, etc.
The restaurant, L’Esprit du 12ème is one of Eastern Paris’ business meal hot spots. Here, the cuisine evolves with the seasons and inspires itself from the local products. In his creative mind, the Chef and his team create bistronomique dishes whereas in the restaurant greeting you in a modern and warming atmosphere.
At the Point Bar, the art deco Cube indoor or the outdoor terrace are perfect places for a drink in the afternoon or an evening cocktail.
It is a stone’s throw from La Grande Bibliothèque and just a few minutes from central Paris by metro. The hotel provides easy and fast access to the capital’s key sites for both business and leisure guests.

                                    Accessibility information

- Visual impairment: no head-level obstacles in the corridors, lift with adapted keypad (buttons in relief and/or braille).
- Hearing impairment: online booking and/or payment on the website.
- Learning disability: trained and educated staff, signs with pictograms, entrance monitored with CCTV and visible from the reception.
