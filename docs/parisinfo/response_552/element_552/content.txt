                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Where authenticity meets modernity: This space, completely renovated in September 2011, is situated at the heart of Bercy in Paris. This new, modern and natural district benefits from exceptional accessibility. 1,300m² of flexible and dividable function rooms is dedicated to corporate events and can host meetings for between 100 and 700 people with high quality food services.
