                            MesSortiesCulture launches an offer for companies
                            MesSortiesCulture, a leading website for guided tours and cultural talks in France — thanks in particular to its search engine that makes it possible to find a visit according to a date, a theme or a district —, unveils a brand new offer for companies. 
                        

                        Ideal for a team-building event or a turnkey cultural incentive activity, MesSortiesCulture now offers a wide choice of visits for companies, combined with a souvenir box for participants to take away with them. Private tours of districts, exhibitions, monuments, by theme, such as the ‘2-hour haunted Paris circuit’, or even ‘businesses at work’ are available for groups of up to 50 people.MesSortiesCulture selects a guide and organizes the creation of a personalized ‘surprise box’, in consultation with the business concerned, and composed of a game booklet about the outing, a souvenir object, a private Internet address related to the event, as well as a few pages on the company that booked the visit. A perfect way to combine an original activity and loyalty, and impress guests!Other complementary services include booking a restaurant near the venue for an all-round successful event.
