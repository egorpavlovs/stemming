                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Hyatt Regency Paris-Charles de Gaulle welcomes your meetings and events of all sizes, with a full range of efficient services. Located on the ground floor and Mezzanine level, our modular spaces are configured to accommodate meetings, seminars, banquets and cocktail receptions.
With an outward looking and innovative spirit, the Hotel Hyatt Regency Paris-Charles de Gaulle offers Le Campus, a new concept aimed at revolutionizing the professional events industry. Designed by the firm Viserius Studio, this new offer consists of 7 labs (modular 38 to 85 m², with a capacity of 10 to 70 people, available for private hire and equipped with the latest technology (HD screens, Apple TV, AirPlay technology).
In addition, there are common areas for relaxation, designed to create a friendly atmosphere conducive to dialogue, collaboration and creativity. The Hyatt Regency Paris-Charles de Gaulle offers a selection of packages including a residential package and a day or half-day package.
