                                                                                    Description
                        
                            DRAWING NOW Art Fair is the leading contemporary art trade show dedicated to drawing in Europe.During the last edition in March 2018, the show gathered 72 international galleries on the two levels of the Carreau du Temple to introduce you to the diversity of the last 50 years of contemporary drawing. The goal was to present over 400 artists and more than 2 000 artworks! 
                        
                    
                                        
                                                                            
                                                    Website
                            
                                https://www.drawingnowparis.com/?lang=en
                            
                                                                        
                                        

                                        
                                            



    
        Place 
    
    
        
            
                Le Carreau du Temple
                
                    
        The Carreau du Temple, a former clothes market, underwent a transformation in 2014! There is now 6,500 m² of space available to welcome numerous multidisciplinary events: sports, art, culture, music ... as well as fashion shows and artists' residence. The venue is available for private hire for a range of projects like covered market halls with a surface area of 2,300 m². Recognisable by its glass and metal structure, which is both monumental and gracious, the Carreau du Temple is an iconic industrial building (built under the reign of Napoleon III) in the Haut-Marais district.
