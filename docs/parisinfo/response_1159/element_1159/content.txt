                            Tribute to Michael Jackson with the exhibition ‘On the Wall’
                            From music to fashion and dance, the King of Pop continues to fascinate. Learn more about his art and his personality through the works of many of the artists that he inspired, at the exhibition ‘On the Wall’ at the Grand Palais from 23 November 2018 to 14 February 2019!
                        

                        One of the most influential stars of the 20th century, Michael Jackson has always been considered a reference in many areas. This exhibition aims to explore his cultural impact on contemporary art from the 1980s to today, through photographs, installations and other works by artists such as Andy Warhol, Isaac Julien, and David LaChapelle, for whom he was a source of inspiration.Until now Jackson’s influence on the art world has never really been brought to light. Don't miss this unique event at the Grand Palais!
