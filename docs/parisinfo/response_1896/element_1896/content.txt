                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A stone's throw from the Porte de Saint-Cloud, each of the four spaces in this 800m¬≤ universe was designed with a specific purpose, but nothing is stopping you from reorganizing the place to meet your wishes.
For your cocktail party or seated dinner, we offer several 'home-style' options as well as several other menus (healthy, chef's special, etc.). You also have the freedom to work with the caterer of your choice.
An AV technician will ensure the success of your event here in this very well equipped venue.
Finally, after a day of hard work, you will have also satisfied the wishes of your guests.
