                                                    Description
                            
                                
        At the heart of La Défense, in the famous Cnit building, we are offering you close to 3,000 m² of high-tech space, dedicated to hosting your staff retreats, training days, conferences, or even cocktails …
Built in 1958 and inaugurated by General de Gaulle, the Cnit, a true architectural feat, was the first building of the future business district of La Défense. Situated directly under the Cnit’s large freestanding vault, your new centre, bright and spacious, has been converted and designed in the spirit of the fifties.
