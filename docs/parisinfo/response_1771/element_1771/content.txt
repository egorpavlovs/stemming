                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In the heart of the Saint-Germain-des-Prés district, the Musée Eugène Delacroix occupies the great artist's apartment, workshop and garden. In a charming atmosphere, it offers visitors a chance to see his collections, paintings, drawings, lithographies and some of his memorabilia. In the evenings, receptions, cocktail parties and seated dinners can be hosted in the artist's workshop among his paintings, enabling visitors to enjoy the unexpected tranquillity of his little garden.
