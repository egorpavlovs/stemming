                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Located in a beautiful art deco building in the 8th arrondissement of Paris, a few steps from the Champs-Elys√©es, the Elys√©es Biarritz has a 245-seater auditorium with adapted comfort and equipment for conferences, company meetings, and film projections. There is also a rotunda and a modular reception room for events with a decor that is perfect for a prestigious event. 
The Elys√©e Biarritz can welcome up to 400 people for cocktail parties, 220 people for a sit-down meal, and 600 people for a dance evening. 
The reception room, with a sophisticated decor, can be adapted for all types of events: dinner, conference, product launch, fashion show, showcase, performances, etc.
