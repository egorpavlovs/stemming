                            Fairs and trade shows
                            Calendar of professional trade shows  taking place in Paris in the coming months and 2019, 2020.
                        

                        Every year, about 400 professional and mixed trade shows are held at numerous congress and exhibition venues in Paris. Fashion, textiles, art, culture, beauty, well-being, automobile, computer, multimedia, telecommunications, trade, industry, tourism, habitat, social life ... The topics covered are wide-ranging and there is a rich programme of informal meetings and debates.Below, you will find the major professional trade shows taking place in Paris in the next few months.
