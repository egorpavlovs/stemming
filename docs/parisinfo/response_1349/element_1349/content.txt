                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Organize an elegant professional or private reception (cocktail parties up to 500 people, sit-down dinners up to 250 people) just off the Champs-Elysées in this magnificent 'hôtel particulier' boasting collections of Flemish, French and Italian paintings, rare furnishings and more. The Musée Jacquemart-André presents the most beautiful private art collection in Paris in the setting of a grand 19th century mansion.
    
                            
                        
                        
                                                Architectural style
                        
                            Haussmannien
                        
                        
                                                Collections
                        
                            ArchéologieArchitectureArts décoratifsBeaux-ArtsDécors intérieursInstruments et objets d'artMobilierObjets d'artPeintureSculptureTapisseries
                        
                        
                                                Epoque des collections
                        
                            Byzance (IVe - XVe)Moyen Age (Ve - XVe)Gothique (XIIe - XVe)Renaissance (XVe - XVIe)Baroque (XVIe - XVIIe)Rococo (XVIIIe)Néoclassicisme (XVIIIe - XIXe)Romantisme (XVIIIe - XIXe)

                        Visitor tools
                                                    All visitors are provided with a free audioguide, allowing them to discover the museum at their own pace. Available in French, English, German, Italian, Spanish, Japanese, Russian and Dutch.
Audio guide for temporary exhibitions: €3
                                            
                                        
                        Educational activities
                                                    A free educational pack is available for teachers (primary school, secondary school, college). Fully illustrated, it includes a students' book and teachers' book which will make the visit informative but also exciting. This pack will help to prepare for the tour, to fill out a questionnaire on site and to extend the return.
                                            
                                        
                        Restaurant
                                                    Café Jaquemart-André is open from 11.45am to 5.30pm Monday to Friday and from 11am to 3pm Saturday and Sunday.
                                            
                                        
                        Coach park
                                                    Coaches may park on Boulevard Haussmann, in front of the museum.
