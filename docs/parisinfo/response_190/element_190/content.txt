                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Located on the banks of the Canal Saint Martin, the Hotel du Nord is the ideal place for your meetings, breakfasts, lunches, business dinners, cocktails ... It enjoys a prime location in a trendy area of Paris , near the Gare de L'Est, Gare du Nord and Place de la Republique. Its facade, classified as a historical monument, from Marcel CarnÃ©'s movie - HÃ´tel du Nord, released in 1939, as well as its typically Parisian bistro will transport you to the popular atmosphere of the 30s. The restaurant part will offer you a warmer and refined atmosphere
Its surface of 220M2 and its capacity of reception (Up to 150 PAX) allow us to modulate the spaces according to the type of event which you wish to organize, and thus to answer very different requests;
On the kitchen side, we offer a menu of homemade dishes combining tradition, creativity and generosity, signed by our Chef Benjamin Matthieu. It is also possible for us to adapt to all types of requests and to different budgets.
Do not hesitate to contact us to tell us about your projects so that we can send you a personalized proposal, which corresponds to your expectations, and make your event a unique and unforgettable moment.
