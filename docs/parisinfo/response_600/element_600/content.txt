                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Situated 5 minutes from République metro station, near the lively terrace restaurants and bars of the canal Saint Martin area, Chateauform' république offers a venue of 2,500m² comprising 22 air-conditioned, naturally lit seminar rooms and a 180 seat auditorium. A modern, functional venue to accommodate seminars, conferences, meetings or corporate events.
