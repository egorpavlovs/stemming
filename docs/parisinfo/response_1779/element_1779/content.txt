                            Museums, monuments and chateaux
                            Many Paris museums, monuments and chateaux are available to hire for daytime or evening events. Rent a private room to hold your party, catwalk show or conference in Paris.  
                        

                        If you want a really exceptional venue, a museum is an unusual and intriguing choice. Host your event amid the permanent collections and temporary exhibitions of a Paris museum. You’ll be given privileged access to areas that are usually off limits, and enjoy a private tour in the daytime or the evening. Other unique venues in which to host a prestigious event are Paris monuments,or one of the fabulous chateaux in the Île-de-France region. A superbly organized workshop or lavish banquet in one of these stunning settings will be an unforgettable experience for all your guests.If you wish to hire a private room hosting either the permanent collections or the temporary exhibitions of a museum, you’ll have no dearth of choice. There are many private rooms available to rent in Paris. We have listed a wide selection of them here, with descriptions and photographs to help you quickly find the perfect venue for your event.
