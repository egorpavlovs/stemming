                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Since 2005 Meeting the French has welcomed thousands of visitors worldwide to take them beyond the traditional tourist circuit to show them the French way of life.  Should your clients be interested in culture, lifestyle, gastronomy, or shopping, our products will let your clients discover Paris as true Parisians would, and to see the best that the city has to offer.  We keep our groups small to ensure a personalized experience, and offer a wide variety of tours and excursions in both freesale and private options to fit your clients’ budgets and wishes.  Our team is small to ensure you and your clients receive the attention to detail you deserve, and we work closely on a daily basis with our guides, chefs, and partners to ensure that your clients’ experience with us is unforgettable.  We’re eager to work with you to welcome your clients to Paris and show them true French hospitality.
