                                                    Description
                            
                                
        RobertPINK, founded by Marion Prouteau, is an agency specialized in organizing custom-made events around art, history and the "art de vivre" offering unique moments of fun and sharing while (re)discovering Paris. With RobertPINK you have a partner who is human-sized, responsive to your needs and always ready to adapt. From start to finish, we put together events tailored to your expectations, whether it’s for one person or hundreds. Some examples include a walk filled with art, history and good food around the Palais Royal for a group of colleagues at the end of a working day, a custom-made program of cultural discoveries for 100 international clients attending a convention, a dynamic, engaging presentation on 2000 years of architecture in Paris given at an international convention, a visit to a temporary exhibition followed by a champagne tasting for an executive committee. These are just a few of the many possibilities we have to offer !
