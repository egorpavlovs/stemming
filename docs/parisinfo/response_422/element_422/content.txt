                                                            The Club Laffitte is expanding!
                                                
                                    



        
            
                
                    
                        
                            The Club Laffitte is expanding!
                            After several weeks of renovation work, the Club Confair has been transformed into the Club Laffitte and extended. This events space in the 9th arrondissement of Paris unveils its new Van Gogh reception room. The 150 m² space is designed to accommodate large-scale professional receptions.
                        

                        Close to Opéra Garnier and the St-Lazare train station, Club Laffitte has 11 bright and flexible rooms (600 m²), available for private hire, as well as an auditorium (130 people), spread over 1,000 m² of elegant and welcoming spaces, all equipped with the latest technology.The new Van Gogh room is available on request and is created by sliding back the partitions that divide the Salon Degas and the Salon Picasso to create a common space. Independent and bathed in natural light, the room is suitable for all types of events: conferences, seminars, meetings, product presentations, as well as sit-down meals or cocktail receptions (up to 150 people).A dedicated team accompanies every event.This year, the icing on the cake is ‘Arty Days’! The Club Lafitte is opening its doors to contemporary photographers, painters and sculptors for temporary exhibitions, private viewings, and encounters with an artist.
