                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Buddha-Bar opens its doors for your professional events.
A reception area of 500 yard composed of three spaces privatizable together or separately.
A team of professionals that puts at your disposal all its know-how for tailor-made events.
A place with character and unique aesthetics.
The Buddha-Bar adapts itself to any kind of event: Breakfast & press conference, fashion show, product launch, lunches & dinners seminars, team-building, gala evening, etc. ...

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical impairments: ground-level entrance with access ramp. Adapted toilet(s) with transfer bar and adapted sink. The dining room is accessible and laid out in a way that enables those in wheelchairs to move around freely. The tables have also been designed to enable those in wheelchairs to move closer to the table (70 cm high, with table legs that do not cause an obstruction).
- Sight impaired: stairs with continuous hand rail and contrasting steps.
- Hearing impaired: reservation available online.
