               
                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Close to Montmartre, Pigalle and Opera department stores, the Il Cuoco Galante restaurant strikes the perfect balance between the Italian tradition and modern taste: fresh homemade pasta, Italian regional dishes and other recipes dating from the 18th and 19th centuries revisited. Unique in Paris. The setting is intimate and elegant with an open kitchen where you will look over the garofoli, papardelle, timbale but also risotto di pesce, di carne and brutto my buono being prepared by the team. A la carte or Il Cuoco Galante menu, you will enjoy cold cuts, cheese and exclusively Italian wines.
The restaurant Il Cuoco Galante can be privatized and hosts your business meals, in a room of 38 seats with comfortable seats (up to 75 for cocktails, 10 at the counter).
It is located near:
- tourist centers of Montmartre, Pigalle, Opera
- Gare du Nord and RER B, D, E (10 minutes walk)
- Metro Anvers (M2), Cadet (M7)
- Bus n°42 station.
