                                                    Description
                            
                                
        The Philharmonie de Paris, a contemporary venue with original architecture, offers Parisian concert halls and hosts your professional events. Give your guests a musical experience, around a concert, discovering the collections of the Museum of Music or a temporary exhibition. The different spaces (concert halls, amphitheatres, foyers or lounges) are perfectly adapted to your professional events, be it a press conference, a meeting, or a product launch, whether you have 100 or 2,400 guests!
Benefit from top-of-the-range "turnkey" services (personalized welcome, first-class "prestige" seats, support for the organization of your reception, etc.).
    
                            
                        
                                                Viewpoint
                        
        Accessible to the public from Wednesday to Sunday from 12pm until dusk. The belvedere of the Philharmonic ofers a unique view of north-east Paris. This space is open from mid-March to the beginning of November. More details on the website.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: places available with prior booking.
- Visual disability: tactile maps, information with raised text, audio description.
- Hearing disability: magnetic loop, lip reading, audio guide.
- Mental disability: workshops, talks.
20% discount for disabled individuals, 30% for a group. To find out more: www.philharmoniedeparis.fr - email: handicap@cite-musique.fr or telephone +33 (0) 1 44 84 46 13.
