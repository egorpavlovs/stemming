                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Renaissance Paris Republique, designed by Didier Gomez, features 108 rooms, 12 suites and a penthouse, the Bar Martin, the restaurant Origin of 70 seats with 2 large terraces and a wellness center & spa. Our 3 meeting spaces can host up to 50 people. 
Our Restaurant Origin just has received the distinction “Assiette” from the Michelin Guide.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: car park with reserved, adapted spaces, level entryway, adapted bathroom fitteed with a transfer bar and accessible sink, 4 adapted rooms with toilet with side space and transfer bar, all entrances to the different areas of the hotel are accessible (business center, meeting spaces, restaurants and lounges, spa, fitness center).
- Visual impairment: absence of obstacles above shoulder height, staircase with tactile and visual contrast before the top steps, with continuous handrail, with contrasting steps, lift with adapted control keyboard (raised and / or in Braille), with sound system that announce the floor, information in large print, displayed at a reasonable height.
- Hearing impairment: reservation and / or possible payment (s) on the website, lift with visual and / or audible announcement with magnetic induction loop, flash light coupled with fire alarm.
- Intellectual disability: trained staff, simple to understand signposting, legible, associated with pictograms, supervised entry door, visible from reception / cash desk.
