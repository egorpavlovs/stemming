                            ‘Silicon Sentier’, Paris’s innovation district
                            The headquarters of dating app ‘Happn’ has been located in the Sentier, in the heart of Paris, since the app’s inception four years ago.
Claire Certain, its spokesperson, describes how economically dynamic the district is. 
                        

                        Over the past few years, this historic textile district has transformed into a hotbed for start-ups. It is home to numerous creative spaces: tech incubators, co-working spaces, and digital learning spaces – hence its nickname ‘Silicon Sentier’.Its location in the heart of Paris, close to Châtelet-Les Halles and the Marais to the south, Opéra and the Grands Magasins to the west, and the Grands Boulevards and their many theatres to the north, contributes to the attraction of this district, now sought after by entrepreneurs and young professionals. Discover below addresses not to be missed near the event:
