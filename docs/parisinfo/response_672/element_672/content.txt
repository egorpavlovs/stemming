                                                    Description
                            
                                
        At number 153 of the pleasant rue Saint Martin, a curious and creative cocktail bar and restaurant opened, over 3 floors in the heart of Paris, near the Centre Pompidou.
Business days: 
Le 153 offers to organize business days or breakfasts, as well as seminars in a private and pleasant setting.
Business evenings: 
Strengthen the cohesion of your teams through a varied range of activities and workshops offered at Le 153. 
Enjoy an outstanding setting for your afterworks, business evenings or team-building activities.
This living space offers creative, aromatic and bold cocktails as well as a fabulous selection of wines and champagnes. And for a bite to eat: plates to share, tapas and home-made hot dish of the day.
