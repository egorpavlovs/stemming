                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Located in the 11th arrondissement of Paris, between place de la Nation and Père Lachaise, the Pan Piper opened in a changing neighbourhood, at the core of new trends and cultural innovations. Pan Piper was completely redesigned, with human and environmental commitments being taken into account, including an easier access for persons with disabilities and sustainable development standards. Built on three levels (1500m2) it offers various spaces. You can rent the whole building or the spaces of your choice. Each space is modular and able to host companies as well as cultural and culinary events in optimum conditions.
