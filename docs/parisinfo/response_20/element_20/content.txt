                        Your wedding in Paris


                    
                
            
        
                    
    
    


    
        

            
                
                    Hire a private wedding venue in Paris and make your dream come true, with an unforgettable celebration in a unique and sophisticated setting. 
Whether you’ve invited 30 people or 300, Paris is bursting with exceptional wedding venues where you can celebrate your special day and captivate your guests. There are any number of beautiful places in Paris to host a wedding reception. Anything is possible, whatever your dream setting - a superb function room in a luxury hotel, an unusual venue such as a pavilion set amid acres of lush parkland, a banqueting room straight out of a fairy tale or a boat on the Seine. 
Here are our top picks of Paris event spaces in which to hold your wedding reception. 
You can send your brief directly to each establishment listed here by using the "Contact" button. Shortly afterwards, you’ll receive a proposal tailored to your needs and budget.
