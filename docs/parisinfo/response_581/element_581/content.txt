                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Choosing La Place is wanting to embody the values ​​of innovation, energy and openness in an exceptional environment. Situated in the heart of Paris, under the recently opened Canopée des Halles, La Place is a space in perpetual motion and unique in its missions.
La Place can accommodate can adapt its fully flexible spaces to a diverse range of events: press conferences, seminars, product launches, parades, concerts, gala dinners, dance shows, breakfasts, cocktail parties ...Equipped with state-of-the-art technology in terms of acoustics, sound and audiovisual, La Place enables you to rely on its programming and atmosphere to make your operation a unique experience.
La Place has 8 creative spaces, adaptable to all kinds of activities, available for rent: video-editing studio, rehearsal and recording studio, dance studio, graphic arts studio... Visit our website to find out more!
