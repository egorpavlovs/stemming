                                                                                    Description
                        
                            The Rendez-Vous en France Travel Market is back in Paris on March, at Paris Expo Porte de Versailles.First international trade show of the French tourist offer, this event gives to the professionals of Paris and region the possibility of promoting their products and of developing their sales during preprogrammed commercial appointments. About 900 French and international buyers are expected.Come and see the Paris tourist Bureau on its stand!
                        
                    
                                        
                                                                            
                                                    Website
                            
                                https://www.rendezvousenfrance.pro/
                            
                                                                        
                                        

                                        
                                            



    
        Place 
    
    
        
            
                Paris expo Porte de Versailles - VIPARIS
                
                    
        Paris expo Porte de Versailles is Europe's largest exhibition complex, and is renowned for its hosting of large-scale international events. On site there is a newsagent, ATMs, car hire, and taxi rank. There is a branch of the Paris Convention and Visitors Bureau to answer all your questions.
