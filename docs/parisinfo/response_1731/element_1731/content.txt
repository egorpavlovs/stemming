                        Other services at the establishment
                        The Bistrot de l'Echanson opened in June 2005 where the former Bistrot de la Gaité used to be. Clients are offered a choice of wine and staff will be happy to advise them on which dishes would go well with their choice. The bistro also has an area available for private hire.
