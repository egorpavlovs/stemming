                             Opening of the first Fauchon L’Hôtel on the famous Place de la Madeleine
                            Fauchon, the famous Paris fine food store, unveils its first hotel in the heart of Paris. An eagerly awaited event in the upscale hotel industry!
                        

                        Ideally located in the Madeleine district since 1886, the brand (recognizable by its magenta, black and gold décor) is extending its expertise to the hospitality industry with this new luxury establishment, Fauchon L'Hôtel offering 54 rooms among which 17 contemporary suites. These guest rooms, in a boudoir or rock'n’roll style according to your taste, are decorated with high-quality materials such as mouldings in stucco and parquet flooring in a chevron pattern, while retaining a touch of audaciousness, dear to the Fauchon group. They offer a clear view of the Eiffel Tower, the Place de la Madeleine and the Garnier Opera House.Emphasis is placed on a highly personalized service to meet the slightest desires of clients. For example: a private dinner cooked by one of Fauchon’s chefs, in-room 'Gourmet Bar', at the discretion of the occupant, beauty treatments at the spa Carita, or private visits and shopping.On the food side, Fauchon celebrates French culinary art in its eponymous Grand Café. Quirky and arty in style, it is open continuously from breakfast to dinner with a bar and terrace with 50 seats. It is available for full private hire (150 pax), just like the Jardin des Thés (40 seats) or the private dining room. Please note, for private events: there is a private access through a door on the side of the hotel with a dedicated reception desk.
