                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Unforgettable and unique experiences, full of discoveries, encounters and emotions, gourmet tour and treasure hunt, themed guided walking tours and rallyes around various themes, gastronomy, fashion, French “artisanat”, guided tours of both must-see and little-known museums, activities in confidential venues (with their own place in the 8ème arrondissement): wine, chocolate tastings, cooking class, arts of table and French savoir-vivre workshop, perfume creation, themed conferences, cocktails… Experience Paris with Promenade des Sens®, on and off the beaten path! Promenade des Sens® with its founder, Marie-Bénédicte, have developed a wide range of activities, from 1 to 300 participants, after having innovating with their new concept of discovering the city, by combining history with visits of boutiques and encounters, in late 2006. Experience great time of fun and conviviality with your teams, sharing with them the pleasure of discovering!
