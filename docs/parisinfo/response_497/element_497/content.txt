                            Around the Eiffel Tower
                            Accommodation, restaurants, visits and activities around the Eiffel Tower.
                        

                        A day around the Eiffel TowerAlthough the amazing Tower by engineer Gustave Eiffel merits half a day to fully appreciate it, the monument is also a good departure point from which to discover other places and monuments in the district – and there are many of them! Or one could simply board a boat for a cruise through the heart of Paris.And to finish the day, sleeping in the shadow of the Eiffel tower - yes it’s possible! From palace to unique bed and breakfast or major international hotel chain, accommodation offers suit all needs, in the close vicinity of the world’s most famous monument.
