                            Description
                            
                                
        Our ambition is to keep modernizing the beret and push it forward with true innovations. To do so, Laulhere preserves the priceless heritage of which we are now one of the last guardians. We have never ceased to manufacture this mythical object. The beret is a combination of an ancestral know how and a unique sense of innovation. To develop this exceptional hat, we must combine top quality materials, and men and women experienced in the arts of knitting, felting and many other specific to the manufacturing of a Laulhère beret.
The first store in the world opened in December 2016 in the famous Rue Saint Honoré in Paris. Its personality and sober design pays tribute to this timeless hat, symbol of elegance and glamour.
