                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A mythical place of the rock scene since the 60s, the BUS PALLADIUM is a great nightspot in Paris.
From Mick Jagger to Eddy Mitchell, all the greatest artists have been on the stage at the Bus. Today it is not uncommon to come across Louis Bertignac, Matthieu Chedid, or Izia and on the stage referent groups, pointed or completely crazy like now cultissimes "Airnadette".
More than a club, the Bus has become a real home when living between Rock'n Roll festivals, an inescapable Parisian scene and a Made in USA restaurant! A space for both private and professional events between rock aperitifs, dinner and live music events in the heart of Pigalle.
Privatisation available Sunday-Thursday.
