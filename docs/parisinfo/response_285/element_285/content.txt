                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Need an address for your business meeting? Do you have a seminar to plan or perhaps a wedding to organize? A unique location in the middle of Paris: the Marriott Hotel Champs-Élysées. Organize meetings, cocktail parties, seminars or dinners in the heart of the Champs Elysees. 7 meeting rooms, a magnificent private terrace, a welcoming and contemporary restaurant and a bar lounge are at your disposal at the Paris Marriott Hotel Champs-Élysées. Direct access to our private car park.

        Floor-level main entrance, adapted parking spaces, reception desk, restaurants and bars, 5 adapted guest rooms (door width: 81 cm) with floor-level shower in bathroom for those with reduced mobility.
