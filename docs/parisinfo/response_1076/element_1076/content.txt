                             Alain Ducasse cooking school
                            Discover Alain Ducasse cooking school in Paris and learn some of the techniques that helped to build the reputation of the most starred chef in France. 

                        

                        Are you looking for a teambuilding? Alain Ducasse cooking school, you will find what you are looking for, no matter your level or expectations ! You can choose between a cooking classes and wine tastings.Fish and shellfish, bistro cuisine, choux pastry, haute cuisine, wine … the Alain Ducasse Cooking School teaches you to master some of the techniques that helped to build the reputation of the most starred chef in France. Perfect for team building activities, informal meetings, tastings, or cocktails, the school has two locations: on rue du Ranelagh in the 16th arrondissement, and on the 3rd floor of LE BHV MARAIS department store at the Hôtel de Ville. The school welcomes corporate groups, for up to more than 100 guests, for tasty and convivial courses.Address :64, rue du Ranelagh, 75 016 ParisEcole du BHV Marais: 54, rue de Rivoli, 75 004 ParisWebsite :http://www.ecolecuisine-alainducasse.com/en/cooking-classes
