                            Close to the capital, but far from the hustle and bustle of the city, are numerous events venues and convention and exhibition centres ready to welcome your important events. 
                        

                        Whether it be a garden party set in a lush and green setting, a cocktail party, a meeting, or a conference in a prestigious reception room, all kinds of events are possible, according to your needs and wishes!For groups, discover a selection of hotels, close to Roissy Charles de Gaulle or Paris-Orly airports, Disneyland Paris, the Aéroville shopping centre or the exhibition centres Paris-Nord Villepinte or Le Bourget. Don’t hesitate to contact our service providers for a tailor-made quote.If you want to narrow down your search, discover the list of venues in Ile-de-France and accommodations in Ile-de-France.
