                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Yooma Urban Lodge is a hotel located 1km from the Eiffel Tower. The hotel is designed by Ora Ito with the artistic complicity of Daniel Buren. The hotel offers rooms to accommodate up to 6 people - a concept of accommodation tailor-made for micro groups. All rooms have a private bathroom and toilet.
A range of services is available at this hotel: a restaurant, seminar rooms, artist residences, a fitness room and sauna, temporary exhibitions, a cooking school and a vegetable garden on the roof.
