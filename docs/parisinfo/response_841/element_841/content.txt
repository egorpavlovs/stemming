                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Team Break is a new concept of a "Live Escape Game" where 2-6 players live through an immersive adventure in a locked room in order to escape by solving riddles and living the experience.Team spirit and communication will help you to solve the mission: physical strength isn't needed!For each universe, a mission in the form of a themed scenario is given to teams to accomplish in less than 60 minutes. The riddles are created like film scripts in which the participants are the main actors.
Our establishment is in the heart of Paris and can accommodate up to 17 people at any one time.
As part of groups, we offer a rotation system that allows you to play the other participants. In this case, the participants who don't play can wait in restaurant 5m away from our establishment.
TEAM BREAK EXTERNE
Team Break offers companies, advertisers and brands the declination of the concept depending on the audience and moves to the place of your convenience (local business, hotels, event venues, festivals ...) from 3 to 1,000 participants.
For your Team Building or product launches, Team Break is the first Live Escape Game which which can be taken to your chosen location!
Team Break has 4 scenarios (French / English bilingual) for a capacity up to 1,000 people and during which participants play simultaneously: Contagion, The Enigma of Detective Shadow, Get Out of School and Unexpected Live Game.
We also build fully customised games in the image of your brand.
