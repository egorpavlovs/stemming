                             Chicago, the musical: the must-see show!
                            A Broadway hit for more than 20 years, the musical Chicago opens at the Mogador Theatre in Paris on 26 September 2018! A show not to miss on any account when holding your event in Paris or during the end of year festive season to thank your colleagues.
                        

                        Performed for the first time in New York in 1975, the American musical Chicago tells the story of a cabaret artist and a jazz singer, both murderesses, who meet in prison and then join forces to become successful stars. The show plunges us into the cabaret, jazzy and sultry 1920s atmosphere of Chicago. The French production of this show, staged by Walter Bobbie, will top the bill at the Mogador Theatre (inaugurated in 1919 and a classified historical monument) from 26 September 2018 to 30 June 2019.Thanks to English surtitling by the company ‘Theatre in Paris’, the musical Chicago will be accessible to non-French speaking audiences. Surtitles, translations of the dialogue, are projected just above the stage in real time, so no more language barriers! A dream opportunity to see this irresistible show with clients or colleagues and enjoy some wonderful entertainment!Get your tickets here!
