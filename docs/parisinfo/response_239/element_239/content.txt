                            Parisian sporting events 
                            List of sporting events in Paris: major international competitions as well as regular events such as the Marathon, the French Open or the Finish of Tour de France ...
                        

                        Major upcoming sporting events in Paris:27th Harmonie Mutuelle Paris half marathon, 10 March 2019  5th Eiffel Tower's vertical, 14 March 2019 Saut Hermès (10th international show jumping competition CSI 5*) at Grand Palais, March 2019  43rd Paris marathon Schneider Electric, 14 April 2019  6th Color Run of Paris, April 2019  Start of the 28th Tour Auto Optic 2000 from Grand Palais, 30 April 2019 (public access to the cars 29 April 10am-5pm)  4th E-Prix de Paris of Formula-e at Invalides, 27 April 2019  141st Grand Steeple-chase of Paris at Auteuil racecourse, 18-19 May 2019  118th French Open at Roland Garros May 20 - June 9, 2019 8th FIFA Women's World Cup in France; in Paris at Parc des Princes stadium from 7 June until 7 July 2019  HSBC Paris Sevens, international rugby at 7 tournament at Jean Bouin stadium, 1-2 June 2019 170th Prix de Diane Longines at Chantilly racecourse, 16 June 2019 6th Paris Eiffel Jumping (Longines Global Champions Tour CSI 5*) on the Champ de Mars, 3-7 July 2019  Finish of the 106th Tour de France, on the Champs-Élysées on Sunday July 28, 2019  Handisport Open Paris, Stade Charléty, 29-30 August 2019    Final phase of the volley-ball Euro at AccorHotels Arena, 27-29 September 2019  98th Qatar Prix de l’Arc de Triomphe, on Longchamp racecourse, 5-6 October 2019  Paris Legends Championships at La Boulie Racing Club France in Versailles, October 2019  41st running event "20 km de Paris", October 2019  103rd Open de France HNA (golf) at Golf national in Saint-Quentin-en-Yvelines, 17-20 October 2019  13th Yonex badminton French Open at Pierre de Coubertin stadium, 22-27 October 2019  Rolex Paris Masters at AccorHotels Arena, 26 October - 3 November 2019  Finales of League of Legends World Championships at AccorHotels Arena in November 2019  99th Grand Prix d’Amérique Opodo (world championship trotting international race) at Paris Vincennes racecourse, January 2020  Judo Paris Grand Slam at AccorHotels Arena, February 2020  33rd European Championships in Women's Artistic gymnastics at AccorHotels Arena from 30 April until 3 May 2020  Finish of the 107th Tour de France, on the Champs-Élysées on Sunday July 19, 2020 (to be confirmed)  25th European Athletics Championships at Charléty Stadium, August 26-30, 2020  10th Rugby World Cup at Stade de France and in 8 French cities, September 8 - October 21, 2023  23rd Olympic and 17th Paralympic Games in Paris, July 26 - August 11 and August 28 - September 8, 2024  29th BWF badminton World Championships at the Arena Porte de la Chapelle in August 2025
