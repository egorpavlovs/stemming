                            Meeting Planner's Guide 2018-2019 edition
                            Discover, browse and download now the new 2018/2019 Meeting in Paris guide published by the Paris Convention and Visitors Bureau. An indispensable bilingual French/English tool to help you plan your event!
                        

                        The new edition of the Meeting in Paris guide has been specially designed to help you best plan your event. You will find:> Service provider(s) to plan your event with> Why choose Paris> When to plan your event> How to be supported in the organization of your event> Browse the guide online> To download the guide for free in PDF format we invite you to fill in the formSERVICE PROVIDER(S) TO PLAN YOUR EVENT WITHSome 700 Parisian event professionals are listed! Whether you are looking for a hotel, a conference and exhibition centre, a reception venue or an original incentive activity for a seminar or evening function, you will find your ideal service provider in this comprehensive and quality listing:> 13 conference and exhibition centres> 224 accommodation establishments> 164 reception venues and 121 restaurants> 5 PCOs and 27 DMCs> 69 special event companies> 44 service providers (catering, transport, other).Service providers are listed according to several broad categories of activity, with:> On the introductory pages of each category: an index of venues or services in the category> A presentation of each service provider with one or several photo(s), key information (capacity by type of configuration, surface area, services offered, etc.), and dedicated contact detailsAn index at the end of the guide lists all the service providers together in alphabetic order. Useful if you already have a place or service provider in mind!WHY CHOOSE PARIS?The advantages of the French capital are presented in key figures, infographics, and ‘Did you know?’ insets, through 4 main thematics:> A connected and accessible city> A leading business destination> A city with a wide range of accommodation> A city to discover over and over again thanks to its vast and varied cultural and events scene.So, choose Paris, the city is waiting to welcome you!WHEN TO PLAN YOUR EVENTTwo pages are devoted to the calendar of major events in Paris so that you may if you wish plan your event in relation to festive events in the City of Light:> Major recurring events of each month> Major events of the next few years> A clear presentation, with pictos to easily identify the category of the event (trade shows and congresses, sport, culture, gastronomy, concerts, fashion, etc.)HOW TO BE SUPPORTED IN THE ORGANIZATION OF YOUR EVENT> With the help of the Paris Convention Bureau: presentation, contacts, and major trade shows where you can meet us> Through media and services at your disposal : website, newsletter, guides and brochures, and all the other tools helpful for the organization of your event in Paris> Browse the guide online> To download the guide for free
