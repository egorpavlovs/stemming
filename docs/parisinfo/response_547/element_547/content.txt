                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A magnificent residence in the Paris of embassies and ministries. The peaceful atmosphere of a private mansion with classic prestige in the heart of the Left Bank area of Paris. Dedicated to meetings of research scientists, the Maison de la Recherche has become a special meeting-place for people seeking an original, elegant and peaceful venue for their events.
Fine wood panelling, warm colours and elegant furniture. A rare charm comes into play as soon as you enter. Delicate harmony brings out the slightest detail. Above all, you will receive a friendly, discreet and nonetheless extremely attentive welcome.
The Maison de la Recherche is a very special address and can be privatised, even for small groups.
