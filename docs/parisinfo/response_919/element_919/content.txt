                            Casual Picnic is revolutionizing your gourmet snack breaks
                            It is not always easy to eat out both in a group and ethically. But Casual Picnic has come up with an original solution: their picnic packages! 
                        

                        This gourmet picnic hamper delivery service is a new form of catering! It brings together authenticity and quality produce, for you to enjoy indoors and outdoors, for professional and private events. The products are carefully chosen from suppliers, artisans, and small local producers in the Paris region. Their offering is also environmentally friendly, thanks to short food supply chains and reusable or recyclable picnic hampers.Casual Picnic prepares your lunches, dinners, and buffet selections with sweet and savoury nibbles, and delicious sharing plates for a convivial event, and delivers them all to your office, park, or other event venue! There are a number of set basket compositions, as well as the chance to design your own. They come in several formats that can be adapted for any occasion. On fine days, enjoy the gourmet picnic box: mini wraps and veggie burgers, cocktail skewers, pâtés en croûte with foie gras, and an assortment of macarons.
