                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Whether you come to the doors in the North of Paris for a business trip, meeting, reception, you will be delighted by its ideal location, modern and confortable facilities, private parking, dynamic environment and also the flawless availability of its teams. Enjoy the concept of “Live and Life” in our bar and restaurant. The place is perfect for a gourmet and musical break for your company party!
