                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Just a stone's throw from the Champs Elysées and the Arc de Triomphe, near to trendy restaurants and clothes designers on the avenue Montaigne and not far from the jewellers in place Vendôme, the Crowne Plaza Paris Champs Elysées has pride of place for a stay at hotel in Paris. It is also the meeting point for businessmen with its 300-m²  reception rooms. The Porte Maillot, the Congress Palace, La Défense and Orly and Roissy airports are very easily accessible. Harmonious modernity, comfort, design, unique objects, the Crowne Plaza Paris Champs Elysées is the new essential venue. This private mansion which was once the property of the Comte de Breteuil displays a Haussmanian facade hiding two other monuments with amazing architecture. Just after the porch, decorated with work by Raymond Moretti, who was the pupil of Picasso, there is a green patio and little Renaissance tower. In this venue, visitors enter a world, mixing warm colours, pure white, works of art and noble materials. Signed Bruno Borrione, Philippe Starck's colleague for 20 years, the style of this luxurious hotel offers customers a warm and comfortable atmosphere.
