                        Description
                        
                                                    
        At the foot of the Grand Arche de La Défense and at the heart of Europe's largest business district, the Hilton Paris La Défense offers an exceptional location. The 153 recently-renovated guest rooms, including 49 relaxation rooms, invite you to relax and unwind. The hotel has an entirely-renovated 5th floor dedicated to conferences and events, with naturally-lit, adaptable meeting rooms.
