        Place 
    
    
        
            
                Les Salles du Carrousel - VIPARIS
                
                    
        The Salles du Carrousel's superb location within the Louvre Museum make it an ideal destination for visitors to the French capital. In the centre of Paris, a unique blend of culture and shopping awaits: a wide variety of events can be combined with a visit to some of the Louvre's many shops.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        All levels of the gallery are accessible by lift, as well as access via 99 rue de Rivoli, the car park and the métro. The cark park is accessible via avenue du Général Lemonnier (15 disabled spaces near meters - maximum height 3m). You can access the car park from Monday to Sunday, 7am to 11pm and leave 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
The toilets are situated next to Nature & Découvertes, allée de Rivoli (€1).
