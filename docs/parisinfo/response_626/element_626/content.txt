                                                    Description
                            
                                
        City George V offers you 3,000m² of flexible naturally lit rooms, changing according to your events. A 350 seat auditorium, 14 meeting rooms and a 450 m² function room overlook the indoor garden enabling you to host professional meetings for between 5 and 1,000 people. At the Cercle Privé Galliéra, you will find the right setting for the success of your press conferences and management committees.
