                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Paris-Massy residence is situated 200 meters away from the TGV station in the Massy business area, near the Saclay Valley of Science and Technology and 20 minutes away from the centre of Paris.
Our welcoming and attentive team provide you with a range of personalised services to meet all of your needs during your stay.
For dining, cocktails or buffets our restaurant Les Moulins Bleus® will know how to satisfy your collaborators with its healthy and sophisticated cuisine.
After a long day of work, offer to your collaborators a moment of relaxation at the edge of the swimming pool or in the spa.
