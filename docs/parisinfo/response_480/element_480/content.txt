                            Our top 10 terraces and gardens for private hire in Paris
                            Make the most of spring and summer with a private reception, a lunch party or a corporate event on the terraces and in the gardens of Paris. Whether you are looking for a park for a bucolic banquet, a terrace on a boat for a night on the water, a rooftop for an after-work party or even a garden in a museum for an original event, Paris offers a fine array of terraces and gardens for private hire. Check out our top 10 now!
                        

                        Travel to Italy on the rooftop of the Auteuil BrasserieThe Auteuil Brasserie restaurant, housed in the former Auteuil train station in the 16th arrondissement, has just reopened with new interiors by Laura Gonzalez! Ideal for an after-work event, the rooftop (250 m²) has a top-notch cocktail bar and accommodates up to 300 people for a cocktail party. Olive and orange trees, lavender, and garlands of lights provide an enchanting backdrop for your event. Italian flavours on the menu include delicious burrata and gourmet platters to share. The chefs can also provide bespoke menus. The restaurant (600 m² on two floors) is available for full or partial private hire.Request a proposalRevel in luxuriant green surroundings on the terrace of the Île      Occupying a Napoleon III building on the Île Saint-Germain at Issy-les-Moulineaux, the Île is a 400-cover restaurant in the colonial style with glass and ironwork features. The terrace is a must for cocktails in the sun or for a lunch or dinner in this beautiful green setting. This piece of paradise can be privately hired for your private receptions together with the interior of the restaurant for an event of up to 300 people.Request a proposal(Re) discover the retro-exotic setting of Les Jardins du Pont-NeufLes Jardins du Pont-Neuf, a boat moored along the Seine, offers you a 500 m² venue just below the Pont Neuf - one of the most beautiful bridges in Paris. In addition to a restaurant available for private events (400 for a cocktail reception and 220 for a sit-down meal), it offers a 100 m² terrace as well as two heated verandas, both of which are furnished with palm trees and cane furniture, and which can host your corporate events, weddings, or anniversaries. Tethered to the boat, the stylish Riva Aro Miooffers a trip along the Seine River together with a classy aperitif. An essential address for a Parisian evening!Request a proposalMake a toast opposite the Arc de Triomphe on the terrace of the Arc Paris  For an exclusive evening just across from the Arc de Triomphe, go to the terrace of the Arc Paris, one of the most iconic nightclubs in the capital, designed and created by singer Lenny Kravitz. With a capacity to host up to 400 people for a cocktail party and 120 for dinner, it affords an unforgettable view of Parisian life. For a lunch, dinner at sunset or for a more festive evening, don't miss this inimitable garden-terrace furnished with black velvet banquettes, and plants and shrubs.Request a proposalTreat yourself to an artistic interlude on the terraces of the Renaissance ParisRépublique           The 5-star Renaissance Paris République is at the centre of arty and cosmopolitan Paris. The guest rooms, inspired by art of the 1950s and 60s, the cinema of Godard and post-war works by Le Corbusier, reflect the artistic creativity of this neighbourhood. For a break in the open air between two meetings or seminars or for an after-work event, there is the Martin bar terrace, available to hire for up to 50 people for a cocktail party! You’ll love the comfortable sofas, designer furniture, large plant wall and fireplaces. For a private reception (up to 90 people for a cocktail party), the terrace of the Origin restaurant welcomes you in a peaceful setting, away from the sounds of the city. Request a proposalGet away to the romantic garden of the Hôtel Salomon de RothschildNear the Champs-Elysées, discover the prestigious hôtel particulierSalomon de Rothschild, a gem of a place for your events. This listed historic building is spread over 1,300 m² and opens onto a large intimate and elegant garden. Sculptures, columns, rotundas and pastel-coloured roses recall the romanticism of the 19th century. The indoor spaces are available for private hire, and for an unforgettable occasion, you can also organize a lunch or a cocktail reception on the garden terrace. Kaspia Réceptions, with their premium ‘Kaspia Signature’ elaborated by the multi-starred chef Pierre Gagnaire, and exclusive to the Hôtel, takes care of your catering requirements.Request a proposalEnjoy a total change of scenery in the savanna garden of the Musée du Quai Branly Jacques-ChiracThe Musée du Quai Branly Jacques-Chirac is recognizable by an impressive planted facade. The main museum building sits on pilotis above a vast 18,000 m² savanna garden. Stroll along the paved pathways and revel in the tranquillity of the different outdoor spaces. Outside museum opening hours, the garden is available for private hire for an event of up to 1,000 people! At nightfall, an installation of fluorescent tubes provides an enchanting effect for your reception.…Request a proposalAdmire the panoramic views from the terrace of the Balcon de la PhilharmonieFancy a gourmet light meal before or after a show, a concert, or a meeting? Head to Le Balcon (a 180-cover restaurant) on the 6th floor of the Philharmonie de Paris. Designed as a contemporary bistro with warm-coloured furnishings, it offers stunning views of the City of Light through its large bay windows and from its wonderful terrace, available for private hire (110 people), complete with a bar. On the menu, a choice of cold meat platters, fresh salads and tapas with a modern twist. The restaurant also provides bespoke packages for groups.Request a proposalDance on the avant-garde barge FlowStep on board Flow, a sleek avant-garde 40-metre-long metal barge moored below the Alexandre III bridge. A unique place to party as you take in the sights! This trendy venue layers a modern concert hall (up to 600 people), a club for wild parties, a restaurant (55 seats), plus a temporary beach from (April to November). That little extra? The rooftop (for private hire, 200 people) with a spectacular 360 ° view of Parisian landmarks such as the Grand Palais. Perfect for a drink beneath a starry sky.Request a proposalRelax in the shade of a crab-apple tree in the very private garden of the Villa ModiglianiYou’ll be charmed by the Villa Modigliani and its lovely garden (for private hire, 20+ people) shaded by a Japanese flowering crab-apple tree, in the Montparnasse district. It can host up to 45 people for a cocktail party and 25 people for a sit-down meal. For your private receptions, the hotel's chef conjures up an irresistible selection of sweet and savoury appetizers.Cocktail and lunch packages in the garden on request. The Villa also offers 300 m² of spaces for your private event.Request a proposal

            Birthday party in Paris
        
        Maybe you’re entering a new decade of your life, and want to mark the occasion. Or you’d like to throw a party to give a friend an unforgettable birthday surprise. If your apartment isn’t suitable for hosting the event in style, hiring a prestigious birthday party venue in Paris could be the perfect solution.
    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            Parisian suites available for private hire to host your prestige events
        
        Looking to organize your professional event within a prestige setting? Paris, the capital of luxury and French lifestyle, is home to many outstanding establishments. Check out our selection of private hire suites in luxury palace hotels and 5-stars hotels to amaze your guests. 
    
                            


    
        
                            
                     
        
            Unusual venues for an event ‘just like at home’
        
        Looking to organize your next professional event at a Parisian venue with an intimate and welcoming atmosphere ‘just like at home’, a private evening function in a magnificent Parisian apartment, or are seeking an unusual rental in the heart of Paris for a group trip? Then, hurry and check out our selection of venues that combine modernity and originality, tailor-made services, and upscale amenities in an atmosphere that’s just like home. With choices like New York style lofts with roof-top terraces, chic and sobre apartments with views over the rooftops and famous Paris landmarks, and vaulted cellars, you are sure to find the perfect venue!
