                             Corporate presents: gift wrap Paris and its savoir faire for Christmas!
                            The festive season is just around the corner! Think about thanking your employees and customers by offering them a corporate gift that is typically Parisian.
                        

                         The City of Light has lots of shops with ancestral know-how and original products - an ideal souvenir of a business trip to the capital: berets made in the purest French tradition, cups decorated with a starry Eiffel Tower, trendy accessories, etc. From gourmet to glamorous, from chic to whimsical, hurry and check out our quintessentially Parisian corporate gift selection for the 2018 festive season.LaulhèreFinely displayed on elegant brass heads, Laulhère berets beautifully combine traditional know-how and creativity in their eponymous flagship on the Rue Faubourg Saint-Honoré. Trendy and refined, the Laulhère beret is the accessory par excellence for the festive season. For autumn/winter 2018/2019, France’s last beret manufacturer reveals an exclusive collection. Decorated with rhinestones, sequins or printed with a leopard pattern, these berets will amaze your loyal business partners ...Request an estimatePetits Trésors de FranceAn exclusive high-end assortment of goods all made in France, customizable bespoke gift boxes, and a delivery service … That's what Petits Trésors de France offers you. Who could resist the fully-customizable Christmas gift box? All you have to do is choose the items to put into it: chocolate, honey, home fragrance, candle, table linen, cookie plate, soap, children's game, book, snow globe, black& white photo, travel diary … Petits Trésors de France, a gift for every occasion!Vintage & CieA gift of a fine bottle of wine to a client or as a thank you to one’s partners: what could be more typical and appropriate? Vintage & Cie, located in the 8th arrondissement of Paris, has an impressive collection of wines that will meet the most demanding requests. Each wine area and bottle is clearly presented by a team of experts who you will relate the history of the product to you and explain its special characteristics. A private lounge with plush furnishings is also available for bespoke wine tastings for up to 20 people.Request an estimateLaduréeFor a gourmet and glamourous end to the year, Ladurée and the famous duo BRIGITTE join together to create an irresistible collection. Mesmerizing and delicate, this collection invites you on a culinary journey to the East with a flowery and spicy black tea consisting of rose, jasmine, ginger and cinnamon; and a candle to spread soothing jasmine, rose, sandalwood, patchouli and amber fragrances. Note that this temporary creation supports the fight against breast cancer.Request a proposalEspace Sensoriel Dans le Noir ?      The Espace Sensoriel Dans le Noir? is a unique kind of deli! It offers artisanal products selected by a panel of experts in total darkness, as well as blind tasting workshops. What’s new? Gift boxes each containing what is necessary for a sensory experience in the dark, and a book of short stories Dans le Noir et Autres Histoires, together with a reading light and a bookmark. The gift voucher gives access to a themed workshop such as ‘Le parfum et vous’ (perfume and you), ‘La malle aux parfums’ (suitcase of fragrances), ‘Le vin sans étiquette’ (wine without a label), etc.Molinard ParfumIn addition to its famous delicate perfumes, the Maison Molinard offers candles and soaps with spellbinding scents. Perfect for refined corporate gifts. You can also offer a bespoke perfume creation workshop to give your customers the opportunity to write their own olfactory composition ... These workshops can take place at a Molinard venue or in a place of your choice.Request a proposal
