                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Specializing in corporate events since 1998, Stade de France, with its unique setting, brings an added value to the destination of Paris. Give your guests an amazing experience in a legendary venue with strong values such as performance and pushing yourself to the limits. Whatever the type of event, for anything from 10 to 10,000 people - seminars, workshops, conventions, gala evenings, cocktail parties, etc - Stade de France offers you a range of options for organizing your event, from turnkey solutions to bespoke packages.
