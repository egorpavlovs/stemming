                        Description
                        
                                                    
        In front of the Cité des Sciences et de l'Industrie, the Forest Hill Paris La Villette is the perfect location to hold any kind of event due to its large adaptable rooms. There are 12 meeting rooms which can hold up to 180 people, a banqueting area for up to 270 people and an area for cocktail areas which can accommodate 470 people.
                                                

                                                    
                                
                                                                            Number of guest rooms : 250
                                                                                                                Reception/Welcome 24/24
