                        Description
                        
                                                    
        A member of the Leading Hotels of the World, Fauchon L'Hôtel Paris is the first hotel by this iconic brand. It is located in the heart of Paris, on Place de la Madeleine. With 54 rooms and suites, as well as a restaurant, the hotel can host a variety of events from cocktail parties to showrooms.Always looking to provide personalized service, the hospitality teams at Fauchon L'Hôtel Paris works to make each event exceptional.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: car park with reserved adapted space(s), level or ramped entryway, reception desk with dropped section, collective adapted bathroom with transfer bar and accessible sink, three adapted rooms with bathrooms equipped with level shower (with folding or movable seat), accessible sink, toilet with lateral space and transfer bar. Accessible and adapted Spa space.
- Visual impairment: transparent doors/walls preceded by contrasting signage, no obstacles above head height, stairs with tactile and visual contrasts before first downward step, with continuous handrail, lift with floor announcements and relief and/or Braille buttons.
- Hearing impairment: booking and/or payment available online, flashing light accompanies alarm (where the deaf person may be alone).
- Intellectual disability: trained staff, easy-to-understand signage with pictograms, monitored entryway visible from cashdesk.
