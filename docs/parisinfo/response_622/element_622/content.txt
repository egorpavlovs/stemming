                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Built by Prince Albert 1st of Monaco in 1911, The Maison des Océans is located in the renowned and lively Latin Quarter in Paris. The building is part of the Oceanographic Institute, which upholds and shares its mission following the wish of its founder: « Knowing, Loving and Protecting the oceans ».
More than a simple convention center, the « Maison des Océans » carries within its walls a strong environmental commitment! 
The Maison des Océans regularly hosts international conferences bringing together experts, entrepreneurs and political decision-makers to discuss the main challenges of sustainable management of the oceans.
These exceptional and prestigious spaces can also host seminars, cocktails and other events up to 500 guests.
