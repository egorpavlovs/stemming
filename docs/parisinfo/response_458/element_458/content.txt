                            Paris Marriott Rive Gauche Hotel & Conference Center makeover
                            The Paris Marriott Rive Gauche 4-star hotel, situated close to the Montparnasse train station, presents its 757 newly-revamped rooms and suites and event rooms!
                        

                        Guest rooms of the Paris Marriott Rive Gauche Hotel & Conference Center, in bright and clear tones, are now resolutely contemporary in style and echo the modern architecture of the hotel’s facade.  Some rooms offer stunning view of the Parisian skyline.The recently-renovated 4,800 m² conference centre and 55 flexible event rooms are suited to all kinds of events for up to 2,000 people. For your coffee break, lunch or business dinner, discover our banquet and catering space (up to 1,350 guests seated). As the jewel in the crown of a hotel group at the cutting edge of technology, the establishment offers you a 24/24 Mobile Requests service on its mobile app: in a click, you can book your room, verify your check-in, request amenities, extra towels, luggage assistance and other services that will simplify your stay!

            Our top 10 terraces and gardens for private hire in Paris
        
        Make the most of spring and summer with a private reception, a lunch party or a corporate event on the terraces and in the gardens of Paris. Whether you are looking for a park for a bucolic banquet, a terrace on a boat for a night on the water, a rooftop for an after-work party or even a garden in a museum for an original event, Paris offers a fine array of terraces and gardens for private hire. Check out our top 10 now!
