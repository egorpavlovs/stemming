                                                    Description
                            
                                
        We are delighted to welcome you around a live Rock & Roll concert, a meet'n'greet with a Star, a pass food cocktail, a meeting, a Rock & Roll City Music Tour, a showcase, A birthday, A weeding, a hen party, a Christmas tree, an adult or student group, etc...
Not only are we located in one of the most beautiful cities in the world, but we also offer event space for the perfect party. Our stylish cafe features indoor and outdoor event venues, with space for anywhere from 20-450 guests in one of the best boulevard in Paris, the Grand Boulevard.
Our Chef, Alejandro Argueta, will know how to impress you with dedicated menus with fresh products and homemade cuisine !
Our professional staff is on hand to make your next event easy and memorable, and we cater to all events, corporate or private. We will offer you the French service mixed with the warm welcome from USA !
