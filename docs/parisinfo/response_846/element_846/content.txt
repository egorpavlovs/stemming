                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Paris on the Way created in 2010 has become a leader company in the field of guided tours services with more than 120 Licensed Guides accredited by the Ministry of Culture. They are polyglot (7 languages: French, English, German, Spanish, Portuguese, Italian and Russian) and experienced in tending to VIP costumers. 
Our mission is to welcome you as our special guest and to share with you a moment of life "à la Française, la Parisienne" through more than a 100 customized  guided tours :
Museums and Monuments Tours: Louvre, Orsay, Eiffel Tower, Notre Dame or more atypical and unusual sites (Conciergerie, Sainte Chapelle ...)
Thematic Tours: the French Revolution, World War II, Jefferson in Paris, English and American literature of the years 1905-1930, Vincent Van Gogh in Auvers sur Oise…
Gourmet Tours: Come also to our gourmet guided tours and discover the production of croissants and baguettes in Montmartre; chocolate and pastry in Saint Germain des Prés and other flavors in the historical district of the Marais.
Family Tours with children: Go for a treasure hunt in the Louvre, Orsay, Notre Dame and the Ile de la Cité or in Montmartre.
Visit of Castles and Abbeys: Versailles and the most beautiful castles and abbeys in the provinces.
Paris on the Way also can care of all the logistics (purchase in advance of the tickets of entries / reservations in the museums) a reactivity to any request.
