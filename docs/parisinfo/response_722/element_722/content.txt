                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Novotel Convention & Wellness Roissy CDG opens its doors to a modern concept, where work and relaxation complement each other harmoniously. It is among one of 13 Novotel "ProAct" properties (organization of large-scale meetings) and offers 1,200 m² of meeting spaces. 289 Novation bedrooms, a restaurant and a bar open 7 days a week. The Wellness Club, a 600 m² spa with an institute and a qualified team. Free shuttle service to the Charles de Gaulle airport and the RER & TGV stations. Privative car park.
