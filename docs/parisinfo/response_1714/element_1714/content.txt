                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Sofitel Paris Arc de Triomphe, with 124 rooms and suites, is ideally located near the Avenue des Champs Elysées, home to some of the biggest companies and prestigious shops. 
Its Haussmannian facade and interior design follows a elegant and sophisticated theme.
Our meeting rooms, business centre and team of events organizers all ensure that your events and seminars go off without a hitch.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        -Physical impairments: ground level entrance with ramp, public disabled toilet with transfer bar and adapted sink, 4 adapted rooms with bathroom equipped with walk-in shower and fold-up/mobile seat.
-Visual impairment: tactile and visual contrasts 50cm before the stairs, extended hand rails, adapted controls in lift (relief/Braille touch controls), information in large print at an appropriate height.
-Audio impairment: reservation and/or payment possible online.
-Mental disability: easy-to-understand and legible signage, monitored entrance visible from the reception desk.
