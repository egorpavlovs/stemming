                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Our establishment has naturally lit meeting rooms. Our main meeting meeting is 120sqm and have a terrace for you to relax. This room is ideal for meetings, press conferences, lunches with collaborators, team building exercises...
The hotel is easily accessible by the A1 autoroute, 15 mins from Paris, 15 mins from Roissy (CDG), 10 mins from Expositions de Villepinte. We also have secure parking facilities on site.
The hotel offers a team-building activity on the airport to dynamize the work day. During the two hour pedestrian rally, participants will be led through the airport by a series of riddles and challenges, which will make them discover the many different aspects of the location.  
A unique team-building event for a successful meeting.
