                            Paris hosts the final of the European Women's Handball Championship 2018!
                            After playing host to the Men's World Handball Championship in 2017, France is organizing the Women’s EHF Euro 2018 from 29 November to 16 December 2018, in five cities: Brest, Monbeliard, Nancy, Nantes and Paris.  
                        

                        This event, which brings together the best players in the world, is a celebration of women's sport, which will also be in the spotlight during the Women’s Football World Cup, in France next year. After the organization of the Gay Games and the Ryder Cup in 2018, and the upcoming 2024 Olympic Games and Paralympics, Paris is establishing a track record for hosting major sporting events.The preliminary rounds began on 29 November with the opening match between France and Russia in Nancy, and continued until 5 December in four cities in the provinces. They are followed by the main round from 6 to 12 December in Nancy and Nantes. The handball finals will take place in Paris, at the AccorHotels Arena, the biggest event space in Europe, from 14 to 16 December. Good to know: take advantage of this event to combine business and a great spectacle! For your professional event or business meeting, you can privately hire one of the AccorHotels’ 54 boxes renovated in 2015, or one of the many lounges available, before or after the match! In total, 3,000 m² of space are available for private hire in this temple of Parisian entertainment.More information here.
