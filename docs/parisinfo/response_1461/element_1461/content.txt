                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physically disabled: entry at street level or via access ramp, lowered and partially recessed counter at reception, adapted communal toilets with transfer bar and recessed washbasin, 102 adapted guest rooms with floor-level shower and toilet with extra space and a transfer bar. The brasserie, club and service restaurant are all adapted with larger paths and adapted furniture.
- Visually impaired: glass walls and doors are indicated with contrasting elements, no eye-level obstacles along pathways, secured stairway with continual handrail, lift with buttons in relief or Braille and each floor audibly announced.
- Hearing impaired: possible to book and pay for rooms using our website, flashing light coupled with audible fire alarm in guest rooms.
- Mentally disabled: trained staff, easy to understand signage with accompanying pictograms, monitored entrance which is visible from reception.
