                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Entertaining at the Ritz Paris is to celebrate the art of excellence. 
Whether decorated in Grand Siècle or neoclassic style, the private salons at the Ritz Paris offer singular settings for receptions and unforgettable special occasions. The Ritz Paris is also making available for you its most exceptional spaces: Prestige Suites, private terraces, views over the Parisian rooftops, private dining room, Grand Jardin and the École Ritz Escoffier, places that illustrate perfectly the art of entertaining.
Spaces range from intimate to grandiose, always offer leading-edge technology and Haute Cuisine by our Executive Chef Nicolas Sale, combine with the discreet, attentive and professional service of our team.
An unrivaled tradition of excellence and savoir-faire in the heart of Paris.
