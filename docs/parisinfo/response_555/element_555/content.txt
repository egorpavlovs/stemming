                                                    Description
                            
                                
        For a successful event, don't settle for anything less than the excellence of Club Laffitte, a cozy but refined venue situated in Paris' coveted 9th arrondissement between Bourse and Opéra. With comfortable fittings and the latest technology, our auditorium can seat up to 130 people and is perfectly suited to all kinds of events from seminars to meetings to conferences, all with a spirit of conviviality.
The Club also offers 12 modular, high-tech and well-lit meeting rooms with a total area of 1,000 m², dedicated to hosting events for anywhere between 10 and 150 people. These elegantly-decorated but functional rooms are perfect for seminars and receptions. Whatever your event, the Club Laffitte offers a personalized range of services with an expert finish. All of the Club's spaces can be reserved for private events on request.
