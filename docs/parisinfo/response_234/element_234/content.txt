                            Fashion show at the Louvre -  agency Alcep
                            Enjoy all the prestige of an evening at the Louvre!
                        

                        Choice of theme: FashionNumber of participants: 800Length of programme: 1 eveningSector(s) of activity of client:Mail orderSpecimen programme Enjoy all the prestige of an evening at the Louvre!After being greeted with music, the evening begins with a tour of the Louvre Museum, which has been privately hired for the occasion.Participants have the honour of a guided visit of the famous Louvre Museum, reserved just for them! A wonderful way to discover this exceptional place, in small groups with an experienced guide.An exquisite dinner awaits guests in the Salons du Carrousel du Louvre (VIPARIS).The highly qualified staff from POTEL & CHABOT wait on their special guests throughout the evening. A series of delicious dishes feature original and modern cooking that will appeal to all palates.Wonderful musical entertainment is provided during the dinner by opera singers from CESAM INTERN!A Fashion Show is then staged for prestigious guests! Models dressed by famous haute couture fashion designers such as Armani, Chanel, Christian Lacroix, Prada, Sonia Rykiel, Jean Paul Gaultier or John Galliano parade down the catwalk creating an exclusive and stunning spectacle.It is also possible for some of your guests to take part in this prestigious fashion show!Dressed in outfits from GALERIES LAFAYETTE by great fashion designers, they will parade alongside professional models. Stars for a night, this will remain an unforgettable evening, engraved on everyone’s mind!Presentation of the agency AlcepThe LLC Alcep Travel & Events, created in 2002 by Philip GOMBERT, is a travel agency specializing in incoming Business Tourism.We are located in Paris and Nice, which enables us to devise programmes in France and in Monaco.We deal with groups of all sizes, from 10 to 5,000 people, mostly from abroad.We manage the logistics of these groups from A to Z, i.e. hotel, transfers, restaurants, meetings, cultural, gastronomic or sporting activities and gala evenings.In order to do this, we have a team of 8 project leaders in Paris and Nice, all speaking 4 languages from among French, English, Italian, Spanish, German and Chinese.
