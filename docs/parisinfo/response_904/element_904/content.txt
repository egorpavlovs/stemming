                            Description
                            
                                
        The Espace Sensoriel Dans le Noir ? is a delicatessen in which you can discover artisanal products of high quality, selected in the total darkness by a jury of experts.
Blind tasting sessions of 40 minuts are also organized and facilitated by a blind sensory expert. A fun and unifying moment during which you will learn to rediscover your senses and share your impressions with your collaborators.
The Boutique is a convivial and refined place that you can privatize for your afterwork, teambuilding or product launches.
We can offer you tailor-made services and you can benefit from our lighted spaces as well as our tasting sessions adapted to your dietary restrictions, allergies and tastes!

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: Level or slightly inclined entryzay, adapted bathroom fitted with transfer bar and sink with recessed bottom.
- Visual impairement: Transparent glass doors/walls preceded by contrasting panels, no obstacles above shoulder height.
- Intellectual disability: Trained staff, guarded entryway visible from counter.
