                            Events venues in Paris
                            Many Paris museums, monuments and chateaux are available to hire for daytime or evening events. Rent a private room to hold your party, catwalk show or conference in Paris.  

                        

                        Looking for a room to rent in Paris to hold a party, catwalk show or conference? Then why not hire a private room in Paris to give your event a touch of real prestige?If you want a really exceptional venue, a museum is an unusual and intriguing choice. Host your event amid the permanent collections and temporary exhibitions of a Paris museum. You’ll be given privileged access to areas that are usually off limits, and enjoy a private tour.Paris monumements or one of the fabulous chateaux in the Ile-de-France region are also available for private hire. A superbly organized workshop or lavish banquet in one of these stunning settings will be an unforgettable experience!You can hire a private room hosting either the permanent collections or the temporary exhibitions of a museum. There are many private rooms available to rent in Paris. We have listed a wide selection of them here to help you quickly find the perfect venue.
