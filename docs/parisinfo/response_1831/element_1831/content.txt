                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In central Paris, the Café du Musée Rodin has a privileged setting in a garden of more than 3 hectares with sculptures. Private hire is available for breakfasts, aperitifs, dinners and cocktails. Outdoor receptions can be held in the summer, and throughout the year it is possible to have a private tour of the museum's permanent collections or temporary exhibition.
