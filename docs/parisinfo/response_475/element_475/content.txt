                            Printemps unveils its new exclusively men’s store!
                            The Printemps Haussmann, a true temple of fashion, has been undergoing transformation. At the start of 2017, it unveiled a brand new men’s department in the building formerly devoted to beauty and house & home goods. 
                        

                        The new men’s store (Printemps Homme) now offers some 250 brands over 5 floors and a surface area of 11,000 m²! For a new shopping experience, it brings together concepts and exclusive fashion ‘corners’:Printemps house brand ‘Brummel’ with its essential key menswear items; the Bar Denim with its specialist jeans brands; and L’Endroit and its selection of trendy international designers. A 300 m² reception room for customers provides private areas for exclusive Personal Shopping services. A Guest Relations team is all entirely at your disposal. Starting from June 11th, the Printemps will be open every sunday from 11am to 7pm!
