                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Opposite Opera Comique, near Opera Garnier, Les Noces de Jeannette is a traditional French restaurant accommodating up to 230 guests in five private rooms with a cosy Parisian bistro atmosphere, for all your professional or private events. Try one of the great classics of French cuisine and a wine menu with around 100 different wines.
