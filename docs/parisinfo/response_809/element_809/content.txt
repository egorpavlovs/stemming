                            What's on?
                            
        From March 2018 until autumn 2019, due to improvement works, the conference rooms are closed and only 140 rooms are available for booking.
    
                        
                    
                    
                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Hôtel Scribe, the legendary neighbour to the Opéra Garnier, offers 213 rooms and suites, as well as 5 meeting rooms. This hotel welcomes all types of event: cocktail parties, press conferences, dinners, teambuilding days, product launches, meetings, residential seminars, exhibitions, elegant soirées and more. This 19th century building was once home to the aristocratic Jockey Club, and even saw the birth of cinema in 1895. Decorated by the renowned Jacques Grange, the Hôtel Scribe embodies the spirit of Parisian luxury. Be seduced by the elegantly-styled rooms, prestigious suites, Le Lumière restaurant, trendy cocktail bar and charming cafe-library.
