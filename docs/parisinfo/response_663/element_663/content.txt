                        Description
                        
                                                    
        In the heart of Paris, the Hotel Barrière Le Fouquet's Paris and its famous brasserie offer historical or contemporary spaces, completely renovated in 2017 and perfectly adapted to your meetings and professional events.
Make our home your privileged address, close to your office and enjoy the attentive service of our teams.
2 Restaurants welcome you for your breakfasts, lunches and dinners:
- Established in 1899, the famous Fouquet's brasserie embodies the Parisian spirit and offers a menu by Chef Pierre Gagnaire in a cozy atmosphere.
- The Joy on the 1st floor of the hotel opens onto an interior garden and offers you a peaceful and discreet setting.
6 spaces for your private events:
- Small lounges, for your lunches and dinners in all confidentiality, from 2 to 12 people.
- Large salons for your seminars, conferences and receptions, all with natural lighting, welcoming up to 200 people.
- A Boardroom for your management committees.
- An event terrace overlooking the rooftops of Paris, for your cocktail parties of up to 100 guests.
From the business breakfast at 7.30 am, to gala receptions, meetings in private lounges, Fouquet's and its dedicated teams cover all your expectations.
