                        Description
                        
                                                    
        At the heart of Paris' 14th arrondissement, a stone's throw from Gare Montparnasse, the Villa Modigliani – a charming hotel, full of character – offers a combination of warmth and French elegance ideal for seminars, cocktail parties, meetings, etc.
Make organizing your meetings easier with our 300 m² of available space and our wide range of on-site equipment.
