                            Events at Buddha-Bar restaurant Paris
                            Press breakfasts, product launches, fashion shows and showrooms in the daytime and cocktail parties, exhibitions etc. in the evening
Whatever the time of day, the number of guests or the type of event, one of our three spaces will be ideal.


                        

                        Over the last few years, the Buddha-Bar Paris has led the way in creating a veritable lifestyle concept. This restaurant/bar/lounge on the ultra-chic Faubourg St-Honoré, right in the centre of Paris, is not to be missed. Looking like a film set with its quality fittings and furnishings, Buddha-Bar Paris is laid out over three levels around a huge buddha and features three different spaces: La Salle Dorée, La Salle Rouge and the Mezzanine. Press breakfasts, product launches, fashion shows and showrooms in the daytime and cocktail parties, exhibitions etc. in the evening.  New prestige event packageWhatever the time of day, the number of guests or the type of event, one of our three spaces will be ideal. With its expertise and organization, Buddha-Bar Paris ensures a unique experience, and is now offering a new prestige event package.Hiring our Salle Rouge (Red Room) for a morning, a half-day or a day will give another dimension to your event. In a timeless setting suitable for 10-120 people, this space is a blend of comfort and simplicity. Our chefs will introduce you to different creative Pacific Rim menus with Asian influences for your event lunches in the Salle Dorée (Golden Room). All the conviviality of a shared tasting experience. The Mezzanine running around the Salle Dorée has a magnificent bar and cosy little alcove lounges. This space is ideal for getting together during breaks and at the end of your event. All the necessary equipment for your seminar or event will be supplied.  Our team has the flexibility to offer you a personalized service according to your needs.  During your seminar, cocktail-making and wine-tasting workshops led by an enthusiastic team are available for an original team building experience. Indulge in our interactive workshops – new skills and pleasure rolled into one.
