                            Parisian rooftops for your events!
                            Parisian rooftop terraces all afford amazing views of the capital’s landmarks. With plant decor or ultra design, casual or chic ambiences, atop museums, hotels, restaurants or department stores …. 
                        

                        Check out our top  rooftop venues for your next business meal, after-work event or cocktail party in Paris!Atop the roof of a museumBalcony of the Philharmonie Do you fancy a delicious snack before or after an exhibition, a concert, or a meeting? Go along to the restaurant Le Balcon (180 covers) on the 6th floor of the Philharmonie de Paris and enjoy a stunning view of the City of Light. The terrace is available for full private hire (110 people) and has an adjoining bar. On the menu, choose from plates of charcuterie, fresh salads, or tapas with a new twist. Menus for groups are also available.Get a proposalLes Ombres - Musée du Quai BranlyThe restaurant Les Ombres (110 covers) is perched on the rooftop terrace of the Musée du Quai Branly, around which is a luxuriant garden. Right next door is the Eiffel Tower! Wonderful panoramic views can be had of Paris and the Eiffel Tower through the all-round glass windows of the restaurant and from the terrace. The food is flavoursome with fusion accents, and the decor design. An ideal venue for your next group lunch or dinner (up to 40 people).Get a proposalGeorge - Centre PompidouTake the ‘caterpillar’, the iconic escalator of the Centre Georges Pompidou, to reach the establishment’s George restaurant. With industrial, chic and futuristic interiors, the venue is available for full private hire (250 people).The Centre offers private tours of the collections, followed by a cocktail party or a dinner, up to 50 people.Get a proposalOn the rooftops of department storesLa terrasse - Galeries LafayetteRendezvous at theTerrasse - Galeries Lafayette, at the top of the dome store of Galeries Lafayette. This huge 1,500 m² rooftop is open for private hire, for events for up to 400 people. Surprise your guests with a breathtaking panorama of the Parisian rooftops, including the majestic Palais Garnier Opera House. The original savoury menu is signed by the young Japanese chef Fukimo Kono. Dessert is a real treat with delicious cakes and pastries by the celebrated Pierre Hermé. Head to the 8th floor to reach 7th Heaven!Get a proposalPerchoir Marais – BHV Marais Near the Hôtel de Ville (City Hall), on the roof of the BHV Marais store, the Perchoir Marais welcomes your cocktail party event for up to 130 people. The decor changes with the seasons. Palm trees in summer, from where you can enjoy watching the sun set; in winter, a mountain chalet type setting and blankets to wrap around you to keep snug. The cocktail menu has some originally named concoctions. And the icing on the cake? A unique view over the Paris rooftops !Get a proposalPerruche – PrintempsSituated on the roof of the Printemps Haussmann store is the Perruche, a cocktail bar and restaurant that is the new Parisian hotspot! People sit around the bar, designed like a pool house, to sip a signature cocktail by Nico de Soto or savour delicious meat or fish dishes such as quail from the Dombes, ceviche of black mullet and grilled octopus. A 500 m² oasis of greenery in the heart of Paris: the ideal place for your next relaxed business or after-work dinner!Get a proposalOn the roof of a hotelSkyline Paris Lounge & Bar - Melia Paris La DéfenseOn the 19th floor of the hotel Melia Paris La Défense, ther SkyLine Paris Lounge & Bar, with a New York vibe, offers breathtaking views of Paris’s business district. On sunny days, take advantage of the terrace (seats 30). At sunset, continue your evening with musical entertainment provided by a team of DJs. Two cocktail menus are available: a classic menu with tapas, and another more elaborate one, ‘Les Constellations’, inviting you to discover surprising creations.Get a proposalLounge bar view – Novotel Paris Vaugirard MontparnasseHead up to the 7th floor and see the sights! After your professional meeting, continue with an after-work event at the Lounge Bar View of the Novotel Paris Vaugirard Montparnasse (120 m²). This sleek and modern rooftop terrace bar is available for full private hire for up to 60 people. Choose a high chair, a sofa, or a pouffe and enjoy a signature cocktail.Get a proposalL’Oiseau Blanc – Peninsula Paris Close to the iconic monuments, museums and shops of the capital, the Peninsula Paris hotel dominates the Avenue Kléber. On the roof, stands L’Oiseau Blanc (40 covers), a restaurant and bar. The minimalist menu (two starters, two main courses, two desserts) features seasonal products. In summer, enjoy the terrace for an unforgettable view of the French capital’s finest buildings.Get a proposalOn the roof of a brasserieAuteuil BrasserieWith its wooden pergola, fairy lights, olive trees, cane chairs, and outdoor bar, the rooftop restaurant of the Auteuil Brasserie (250 m²) guarantees you a wonderful setting for an after-work event (up to 300 people). On the menu, creamy burrata, mini pizzas, plates of food to share, and many other Italian specialities. If you wish, chefs can tailor the food to your event. La dolce vita, of course!Get a proposal
