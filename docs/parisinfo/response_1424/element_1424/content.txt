                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Pullman Château de Versailles is a 4-star hotel, located within the city’s World Heritage Site. The hotel features a pleasant terrace, a modern French cuisine restaurant and is perfectly integrated into the historic environment of the Chateau de Versailles and its extensive Royal Gardens. The hotel was fully renovated in 2017, with respect of the site’s architectural elegance. Its 7 bright, functional meeting rooms and relaxing spa and fitness area are enthusiastically run by its proficient team, dedicated to excellence of service.
