                                                    Description
                            
                                
        A real specialist for 65 years, the Bateaux-Mouches takes care of your private or professional events on the river Seine. 
The Bateaux-Mouches do everything to make your event unique: French cuisine and fine wines, festive musical atmosphere, tables with panoramic views, and uncovered upper deck. The perfect setting for a meeting, cocktail, reception, product launch, or just a lunch or dinner; our 3 restaurants boats can be privately hired to welcome your events of 50 to 320 people and up to 49 in regular service. 
New: Our restaurant 'Le Club' with a stunning view of the Eiffel Tower, where a  cosy and trendy atmosphere is guaranteed.
