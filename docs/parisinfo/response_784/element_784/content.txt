                            Unusual wedding reception venues in Paris 
                            Paris is a city where anything is possible. If you want to make your wedding extra special and really unique, don’t be afraid to step off the beaten track and book an unusual venue to host your wedding reception in Paris. Your guests will be talking about it for years to come! 

                        

                        Bring out your inner romantic: hire a chateau hotel set in a beautiful woodland garden with trees and flowers. Go crazy: throw a lavish reception on the rooftop of a fabulous luxury hotel with a sweeping view of Paris monuments. Or be adventurous and take over an entire boat for a Seine cruise so your guests can admire the City of Lights from a different perspective.Give your imagination free rein and create any kind of atmosphere you want by hiring an unusual venue for a Paris wedding. And do use the services of top-quality service providers, who will organize the whole thing beautifully and ensure you have an unforgettable wedding. They can cater to all your wishes, from the simplest idea to the most unusual request.Here is a useful selection of unusual venues in Paris, run by professional service providers keen to satisfy your every need.
