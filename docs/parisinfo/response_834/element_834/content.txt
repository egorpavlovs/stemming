                        Description
                        
                                                    
        With 6 rooms for 15 to 60 people, the CISP Kellerman is the ideal venue for your meetings.
All of our rooms are equipped with a paper board, a whiteboard, and a projection screen upon request. You will find a self-service restaurant, meeting rooms and guest rooms all under one roof.
The CISP provides various audiovisual equipment as well as refreshments to optimize your meetings:
- 'Pause classique': €2.90 per person (hot drink and fruit juice)
- 'Pause Gourmande': €4.70 per person (hot drink, fruit juice, assortment of small pastries)
