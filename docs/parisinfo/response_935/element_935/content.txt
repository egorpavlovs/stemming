                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Ideally located on the left bank of the Seine and within the Jardin des Plantes, Les Belles Plantes hosts you thanks to a unique atmosphere. Close to the Latin Quarter: Mouffetard/Le Pantheon area, near the Quai de Seine effervescence: Cité de la Mode et du Design and the François-Mitterand Library, come to enjoy our wonderful terraces. After your lunch, dinner or any private event, you might visit the National Natural History Museum.
Partly or fully privatized, the restaurant can fulfill your desires in order to let us organize your professional events the best way (Seminars, Garden Parties,Christmas Parties, Farewell parties…)
