                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Calm, Comfort and tranquility sums up this elegant hotel, located in a charming street in the Opera area.
Just a few steps from the Opéra Garnier, the Grands Boulevards and the top Department Stores, in a semi-pedestrian street, full of local shops and restaurants
85 rooms and suites in categories Standard, Superior and Suite, can accommodate single, double or twin, triple and quadruple occupancy.
The rooms are spacious, functional and fully equipped
In a cozy setting in the Art Deco style, with a charming garden in the inner courtyard, and a veranda offering daylight, more than half of the rooms have a view on that garden.
