                                                    Description
                            
                                
        It’s possible to organize conferences, cocktails or dinners at the François - Mitterrand site.
The Grand Auditorium can accommodate 350 and 200 people for the Small Auditorium.
The Belvédère can accommodate up to 400 people and the Globes Hall 200 people for dinner sitting at 600 people for cocktails.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physically disabled: parking spaces available upon presentation of proof. Lifts to reading rooms, access gates enabling independence.
- Visually impaired: portable electric magnifying glasses, video magnifiers and reading machines, Vocale Presse software, Braille print or large print. Adapted exhibitions.
- Hearing impaired: reception in East entrance hall, auditoriums and some public telephones are equipped with induction hearing loops, tours and conferences often interpreted into French Sign Language, audio guides with induction hearing loops available.
The reservations office offers tours of the François-Mitterrand site upon reservation, aimed at disabled visitors.
To find out more, visit http://www.bnf.fr/en/for/visitors_with_disabilities/s.prepare_your_visit.html?first_Art=oui
Contact: +33 (0)1 53 79 49 49
