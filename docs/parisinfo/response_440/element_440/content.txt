                            The Yacht Joséphine, a top-class boat for an event on the water
                            A luxurious pleasure boat moored opposite the Eiffel Tower, the Yacht Joséphine will win you over with her modern decor, magical setting, and many high-end services including accommodation, cruises, and incentive activities. This original place is perfectly suited to any kind of intimate reception, from meetings and seminars to film shoots and product launches!
                        

                        Like a private house sailing on the Seine, the Yacht Joséphine has four 10 m² double cabins that are tastefully furnished and well-appointed with en suite bathrooms. A spacious interior lounge benefits from natural light and is equipped with state-of-the-art technology; it can be transformed into a reception room to host 50 people.Upstairs, the front deck has an 80 m²-space with access to the jacuzzi. The rear deck offers a pleasant space (for 10 or so people, only when docked) and a 5 m² professional kitchen. The Yacht Joséphine can host up to 47 people whencruising and 60 people when moored at the quayside. A dedicated team is present to ensure that your bespoke event is beautifully organized with every attention to detail.Don’t miss the large array of activities on offer: Paris Landmark Cruise (2hrs30), Cruise to the Stade de France (3 hrs, from 2 to 46 people), oyster tastings, blind wine tastings, cookery workshops, an escape game, or a pétanque tournament for an original and memorable touch!
