                            The Nolinski Paris, a lively new address on Avenue de l’Opera
                            The 5-star Nolinski Paris (Evok Hôtels Collection) is just a few minutes’ walk from the Comédie Française and the Palais Royal Gardens.

                        

                        The Nolinski Paris, recognizable by its elegant Haussmannian facade, has 45 rooms and suites. Each has a dominant colour scheme:  green, beige, raspberry… set off against an elegant palette of grey and waxed concrete. Spacious and bright, the rooms feature high-quality materials, and bespoke furniture signed Jean-Louis Deniot. On the 5th floor, the iconic Suite Josephine, with its incomparable view of the Avenue de l’ Opéra, is decorated with magnificent works of art and can be used to host your exclusive events (20 people for a cocktail reception and 12 people for a sit-down dinner and meeting). The 5th floor is available for full or partial private hire.For dining, the hotel’s Brasserie Réjane is already a staple neighbourhood address. Perfect for your business lunches or dinners; choose to be seated on the velvet banquettes or in the attractive space under the glass roofed winter garden. Traditional brasserie fare includes onion soup or duck pie. For afternoon tea, the excellent home-make cakes and pastries by pastry chef Yann Brys ‘Meilleur Ouvrier de France’ are alone worth going for. Of note, the classic ‘opéra’, crème caramel, and Pavlova. Slightly secluded with an elegant boudoir atmosphere, the Grand Salon is furnished with a green piano, bronze fireplace and velvet armchairs, conducive to relaxation, and is ideal for a private meeting, lunch or dinner.Finally, the spa by La Colline and its 16-metre swimming pool round off the elegant facilities of this unique place for moments of total relaxation.
