                                                    Description
                            
                                
        In the heart of Paris, bordering the Seine and overlooking the Place de la Concorde, the Musée de l'Orangerie offers a unique setting for your private event.
Enjoy exclusive access to its prestigious collections: Claude Monet’s Water Lilies, a monumental work known the world over, and the Jean Walter – Paul Guillaume Collection with works by Renoir, Cézanne, Matisse, Picasso and Modigliani, amongst others, showcasing the artistic exuberance of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Tours of the collections, or temporary exhibitions outside public opening times, can also be followed by a reception in one of the museum spaces.
A number of options are available:
- Cocktail & Exclusive Tour
- Lunch or Dinner & Exclusive Tour
- Glass of champagne & Exclusive Tour
- Breakfast & Exclusive Tour
- Exclusive Tour
- Study days / AGMs / Lectures.
- Individual by agency
- No fashion shows
- No residential seminars
- No weddings
The museum can be booked for private events all day Tuesdays (morning and evening) and all other days before 8.45am and after 6-6.15pm.

                        Room hire:
                        
        The transparent glass space has a unique character and will give your events charm and prestige with a view directly onto the Tuileries Gardens. The two Waterlilies rooms can be specially booked for prestigious private events in the magic of the venue (concerts, general assemblies, conferences, etc.).
The museum can be booked for private events every day from 6pm and all day on Tuesdays.
