                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The essence of the Hotel Duo is in perfect harmony with its location. Nestled in the heart of Paris's Marais district where traditions and histories rub the creative effervescence of chic designers and art galleries. A real mix of cultures and visual entertainment and tastes to experience.
Main places of interest such as the Pompidou Centre, the Louvre museum and the Notre-Dame de Paris are all close to the hotel.
The bar, with its calm, relaxed atmosphere, is a refuge far from the bustling city noise. Relax in the unique setting of this contemporary bar with its colourful armchairs, huge lampshades and 'trees' made from driftwood with poetry printed on. Enjoy the soft lounge music and choose one of our many cocktails, or let the bartender surprise you with one of their recipes. We also have a smoking area outside.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        Motor disability: ground floor entrance or entrance with sloping surface, low reception desk adapted for wheelchair users, toilet facilities adapted and equipped with transfer bar and sink adapted for wheelchair users, 1 bedroom adapted.
Visual disability: transparent glass doors and walls with contrasting elements, absence of obstacles at face level to impede circulation, stairs with continuous handrails.
Hearing disability: booking and/or payments available to do online.
Mental disability: easy-to-understand signaling system using pictograms, supervised entry and visible from the reception desk.
