                        Description
                        
                                                    
        In the heart of La Défense business district, the hotel offers a calm, clean-lined and luminous experience. Within quick and easy reach of Orly and Roissy Charles de Gaulle airports and just 10 minutes from the Champs-Elysées. The Pullman Paris La Défense's 382 rooms offer outstanding comfort in a calm ambiance. Rooms (Classic, Superior and Deluxe) and suites of up to 100 sq. m. offer the best of the Pullman experience (free fiber-optic Wi-Fi, Nespresso® services, LED TV screens up to 46' wide, the Pullman bed, Docking Stations and a rainshower). At the Pullman Paris La Défense, chef Jean-Paul Corbillet wanted a kitchen opening onto the dining area. An inspiring cuisine using five cooking techniques provides diversity, choice and surprise. The 12 meeting areas (drawing rooms, private dining room and boardroom from 26 to 1,503 sq. m.) with a hosting capacity for up to 180 people, are ideal for organizing all types of business and private events.
