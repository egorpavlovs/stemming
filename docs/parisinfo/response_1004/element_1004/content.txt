The capital reclaims the number two spot in the city rankings for international congresses.Discover below the programme for 2017, 2018, 2019 and 2020.> Are you an association or institutional organization that wishes to organize a professional event in Paris?Please consult our file dedicated to congresses and discover the resources placed at your disposal by the Paris Convention Bureau.
