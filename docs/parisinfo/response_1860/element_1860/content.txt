                        Description
                        
                                                    
        A few steps away from the Parc des Expositions, the 4-star Oceania Paris Porte de Versailles Hotel is a true haven of peace with its relaxing and welcoming atmosphere. 250 modern, comfortable bedrooms with unique designs, a 700m2 garden with exotic vegetation, a relaxing well-being space with swimming pool, jacuzzi, 2 hammams, louonge bar and gym.
