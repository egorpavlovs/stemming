                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Would you like a unique and prestigious location for your private function? The Muséum national d’Histoire naturelle welcomes you into its astonishing universe where science, nature and heritage are one and the same. The Muséum national d’Histoire naturelle offers event organisers original and prestigious venues right in the heart of Paris, at the Jardin des Plantes, the Musée de l’Homme and the Parc Zoologique de Paris. Surrounded by bold, stylish architecture combining powerful tradition with dazzling innovation, introduce your guests to the permanent and temporary exhibitions showcasing the extensive collections of the Muséum national d’Histoire naturelle.
