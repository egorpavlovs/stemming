                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The Hotel L’Échiquier Opéra Paris, ‘a little bit of the unexpected in the heart of the grands boulevards’, became part of the MGallery Collection in October 2015, after several months of complete renovation. The hotel consists of 88 rooms and suites, a restaurant for breakfasts and seminars, a lounge bar, 6 fully-equipped meeting rooms and a fitness centre. Ideally located in the vibrant neighbourhood of the grands boulevards and close to the Garnier Opera house, this hotel full of charm and character welcomes guests in beautiful early 20th-century belle époque interiors.
