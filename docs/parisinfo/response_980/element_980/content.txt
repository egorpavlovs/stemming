                            Description
                            
                                
        The Galeries Lafayette Paris Haussmann can provide bespoke activities for your group:
- Fashion shows
- French wine tour 
- Macaron workshops
- Beauty masterclasses
- Heritage and culture visits
There are also many services on offer: VIP services and welcome packages, gift selection, hotel delivery, and more than 20 eateries.
Discover our spaces that are available for private hire:
- Salon Opéra, which can accommodate up to 250 people in a cocktail party format
- The Terrasse, a rooftop with a panoramic view over the Parisian rooftops, which can host up to 400 guests.
Because at Galeries Lafayette, more than just a visitor, your customer is our guest.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: ground-level entrance, adapted lifts, adapted public toilets.
- Hearing disability: French Sign Language spoken by a department store employee.
A detailed description of accessibility at Galeries Lafayette is available on accessible.net, and guaranteed by the Paris Île-de-France Regional Tourist Board.
Tested and approved in 2015 for 'Paris by Wheelchair': https://www.facebook.com/parisenfauteuilroulant/
