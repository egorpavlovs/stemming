                                                            The revamped Elysées Biarritz!
                                                
                                    



        
            
                
                    
                        
                            The revamped Elysées Biarritz!
                            The Elysées Biarritz art deco events space, two steps from the Champs-Elysées, reopens after two months of work. A completely redesigned Interior and cutting-edge technology equipment are two more reasons for hosting an event there.
                        

                        The Elysées Biarritz, the leading venue in an upmarket district of Paris now has relooked design interiors. The ‘La Rotonde’ reception room is brighter and airier with more trendy bars. On the ceiling, a light system offers the possibility of different coloured atmospheres for your event. The modular reception space (300 m²) space is suited to all kinds of functions: a dinner (up to 220 guests), conference, product launch, fashion show, showcase, show or cocktail party (up to 400 people).The mythical 240-seat cinema auditorium at the Elysée Biarritz has also been renovated. It is equipped with a Dolby Atmos sound system for optimum sound sensations, and USB ports integrated into the seats. The 7 m 30 x 4 m screen and a 10 m 70 by 3 m 40 stage make this auditorium ideal for a plenary, premiere, 3D screening, etc.Don't miss: old movies remastered, in partnership with Warner Bros. Companies can attend a film evening preceded by a cocktail reception provided by Kaspia Réceptions and followed by an evening of dancing. Success guaranteed!
