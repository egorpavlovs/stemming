                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Between old Montparnasse and the Jardin du Luxembourg find a unique space in a disused factory: the Lucernaire. Built like a street, where cobblestones, Wallace fountain, public benches and street lamps intersect, Lucernaire is a hive where the arts meet and mix with the desire to promote the exchange between cinema, theatre and gastronomy. Since its creation, it hosts projects in various artistic disciplines, and its mission is to be a pool of creativity. This unique and atypical place can accommodate any type of event, professional or private, in a totally Parisian cultural setting. Real place of life, it is ideal for your conventions, seminars, product launches, birthdays or other private events.

                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: level or ramped entryway, adapted bathroom with transfer bar and accessible sink, dining room with aisles larger than 1m40cm.
- Visual impairment: no obstacles above head height, stairs with contrasts before first downward step, with continuous handrail.
- Hearing impairment: booking and/or payment available online.
