                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Located on Rue de Grenelle, right in the heart of the seventh arrondissement of Paris, the Musée Maillol houses the largest collection of works by Aristide Maillol, an artist who revolutionised modern sculpture. Ever since the eighteenth century, this elegant mansion, with its paved courtyard and famous façade decorated by Edme Bouchardon, has been devoted to art. The museum provides a comprehensive panorama of Aristide Maillol’s oeuvre and holds major temporary exhibitions of modern and contemporary art. Its elegant and historical decor makes the museum an ideal setting for cocktails, diners, business breakfasts, and product launches.
