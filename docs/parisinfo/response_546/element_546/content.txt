                            Meeting rooms for private hire 
                            Our guide lists meeting rooms available for private hire in Paris to organize a corporate event, be it a conference, workshop, meeting or reception.
                        

                        The following is a list of meeting rooms in Paris to organize any kind of corporate event: a conference, meeting, workshop, product launch, press conference or annual general meeting. All the providers on the list are members of our network, and can guarantee the high quality of service you would expect. To help you narrow down your choice, the table alongside the description of each establishment gives total capacity for each of its meeting rooms and a photograph so that you see what the venue looks like.Draw up your list of requirements for a top-quality workshop in Paris. A wide range of prestigious venues is available: from high-tech events venues and ideally located business centres to quiet, private spaces designed for small groups. Whatever the size or style of venue you need, you’ll find it right in the heart of Paris, so you can hold your corporate meeting in optimal conditions.Browse our wide choice of meeting rooms in Paris to pick your perfect venue. These spaces boasting all the comfort of a conference centre and equipped with the latest technology provide optimal conditions for a productive meeting.
