                                                            Polpo: seafood by the Seine!
                                                
                                    



        
            
                
                    
                        
                            Polpo: seafood by the Seine!
                            Polpo, formerly the Ô restaurant, is a chic new brasserie barge serving seafood, by the banks of the Seine.
                        

                        It is a great place to enjoy delicious seafood in a trendy rustic setting. Overlooking the Ile de Jatte, Polpo has a total surface area of 600 m². There is a seaside atmosphere with views of the water and a rustic vibe to the place with straw hanging from the ceiling, over the large bar with thatched decoration, cane chairs, wooden tables and green plants. You can host a cocktail buffet (100 to 600 people), a sit-down dinner (10 to 300 people) and even an evening with dancing (up to 200 people) ‘on the water’! On the menu: whelks, salmon tartar, ceviche of sea bream, clams, or a plate of oysters are certain to tempt you…As for desserts, choose from home-made pancakes, lemon pie or the irresistible Baba Polpo. Every day, from 5pm to 8pm, the bar offers a signature cocktail menu, ideal for an afterwork.
