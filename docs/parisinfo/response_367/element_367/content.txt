                International Paris Air Show
                            

            
                                                            from 17 June 2019
                                                                                        to 23 June 2019

                                                                                    Description
                        
                            The 53rd show will take place from 17 to 23 June 2019, and once again will bring together all the players in this global industry around the latest technological innovations. The first four days of the show is dedicated to trade visitors, followed by three days open to the general public.Every two year, during the show, more than 150 aircrafts are presented to visitors. A number of them are involved in the flying displays held each afternoon. These displays which are always spectacular and often amazing are one of the show's high points, giving exhibitors the opportunity to showcase their technological expertise and allowing visitors to discover the latest innovations.2017 key figures (the last edition):- 322,000 visitors (142,000 trade visitors and 180,000 general public visitors)- 2,381 exhibitors from 48 different countries- 140 aircrafts exhibited including 45 performing flying displaysThis event takes place at the Parc des Expositions Paris le Bourget - VIPARIS.
                        
                    
                                        
                                                                            
                                                    Website
                            
                                https://www.siae.fr/en/
                            
                                                                        
                                        

                                        
                                            



    
        Place 
    
    
        
            
                Paris le Bourget - VIPARIS
                
                    
        Paris Le Bourget is the legendary venue for the Paris Air Show. Just a few kilometres north of the capital and easily accessible via public transport, Paris Le Bourget hosts trade shows for the both the general public and professional groups.
