                                                    Description
                            
                                
        Hold your prestigious events at Laurent, an iconic establishment in the heart of Paris.
Our five elegant private rooms, with a capacity to host between 10 to 60 people, are the perfect place to organize your social gatherings, whether business lunches, dinners or cocktails or to celebrate special occasions with your family and friends.
Each banqueting room was named according to the avenue that it overlooks: Marigny, Elysée, Matignon with its balcony, Gabriel that features a superb terrace. Our largest room ‘Imperial’ offers a very peaceful atmosphere, in calm oasis of Champs Elysées gardens, the ideal setting to host in privacy.
The restaurant main room, which boasts magnificent natural daylight, accommodates groups from 80 to 200 people. We can also organize tailored events by offering both the restaurant room and private rooms located at first floor, for groups from 100 to 300 people.
