                        Description
                        
                                                    
        Conveniently located at Porte de Saint-Cloud, the Radisson Blu Hotel Paris-Boulogne is only 10 min from iconic Parisian landmarks like the Champs Elysées, the Eiffel Tower and the Trocadéro. The subtle combination of the hotel's contemporary decor and the private vineyard offers guests peaceful and green surroundings with a focus on wine. 
All benefiting from natural light, the six meeting rooms have a surface area of 37m² to 272m² and are equipped with the latest seminar technology. The two plenary rooms, Sauvignon opening onto a private terrace and Cabernet with a direct view of our vineyard, can accommodate up to 120 people. The Radisson Blu Hotel, Paris Boulogne, also offer modular guestrooms and break-out rooms for a customized service.
