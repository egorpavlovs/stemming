                                    Accessibility information
                    
        - Physical disability: ground-level entrance or with access ramp, adapted public toilets with grab rail and adapted sink, 4 adapted bedrooms with bathroom equipped with walk-in shower (with folding seat or mobile seat), and toilet with side space and transfer bar, dining room/breakfast room with aisles wider than 1.4 m.
- Visual impairment: transparent glass doors/walls clearly marked with contrasting elements, no obstacles at eye-level, adapted keypad in the lift (buttons in relief and/or Braille) and audio announcement for each floor.
- Hearing impairment: reservation and/or payment possible online, bright flash and loud fire alarm (in places where a deaf or hard of hearing guest may be alone).
- Mental disability: monitored entrance visible from reception.
