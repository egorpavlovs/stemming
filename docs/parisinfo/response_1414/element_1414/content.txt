                        Description
                        
                                                    
        The newly built Courtyard by Marriott Paris Saint-Denis hotel is the only four star establishment in the dynamic area of Carrefour Pleyel in Saint Denis.
Near Paris and the Stade de France, the hotel offers 150 new generation guest rooms providing comfort for the travellers of today. The multi-functional hotel lobby opens up onto the Oleo Pazzo restaurant and the business centre made up of 3 adaptable meeting rooms.
