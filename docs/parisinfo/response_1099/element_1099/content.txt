                            OFF Paris Seine: Paris’s first floating hotel! 
                            Fancy being rocked to sleep by gentle waves in the heart of Paris? Then step aboard the OFF Paris Seine, a floating hotel on the Seine with 58 rooms (including 4 suites), a lounge offering panoramic views, a bar, a solarium, a 400-m² summer terrace and a built-in landing stage at which small pleasure craft can be moored.
                        

                        Fancy being rocked to sleep by gentle waves in the heart of Paris? Then step aboard the OFF Paris Seine, a floating hotel on the Seine with 58 rooms (including 4 suites), a lounge offering panoramic views, a bar, a solarium, a 400-m² summer terrace and a built-in landing stage at which small pleasure craft can be moored.The floating hotel will also host a programme of art and festive events in summer. This one-of-a-kind hotel is 80 metres in length. Docked at the Quai d’Austerlitz, near the Gare de Lyon and the Gare d’Austerlitz, it is only a 13-minute walk from Notre Dame Cathedral.The ‘OFF Paris Seine’ was designed by Seine Design, who specializes in river engineering projects. It is committed to exemplary sustainable practices: these include a water recycling system and low energy consumption. Address : 20-22 Port d'AusterlitzWebsite : offparisseine.com/fr
