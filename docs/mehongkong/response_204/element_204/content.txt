                  How to Get There
                  
                    
                      
                        
                        Located on Lantau Island, midway between Central and Hong Kong International Airport, Auberge Discovery Bay Hong Kong is accessible by land or sea:

Hotel Coach:
We offer coach service exclusively to hotel guests, transferring guests directly from and to Sunny Bay and to Hong Kong Disneyland.

MTR:
Guests can arrive at Sunny Bay Station and switch to public bus DB03P (Exit A), or Tung Chung Station (Exit B) and change to public bus DB01R to reach the hotel.
 
Discovery Bay Ferry:
Cruise to the hotel from Hong Kong Central Pier No. 3 in 25 minutes via a high-speed ferry. Change to bus T4 or T9 to arrive at Hotel. 

Airport Bus:
 Take one of the two public buses from Bus Bay No.18 of New Coach Station adjacent to Car Park 1 of Cheong Tat Road next to Hong Kong International Airport Terminal 1 and reach the hotel in 25 minutes.

DB02A - Arrive at hotel directly in 25 minutes

DB02R - Arrive at Discovery Bay Plaza. Please alight at the final stop, then change to Bus T4 or T9 to Hotel.

Pick-up service:
Hotel pick-up service transfers guests from point to point.
