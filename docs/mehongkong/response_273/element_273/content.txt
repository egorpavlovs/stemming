      As of 2016, Hong Kong host the below exhibitions which are the largest of their kind in Asia;

•World's largest jewellery fair 

•World's largest electronics fair

•World's largest timepiece event

•World's largest gift fair

•Asia's largest beauty trade exhibition

•Asia's largest toy fair

•Asia's largest houseware fair

•Asia's largest wine and spirits showcase

•Asia's largest fashion event

•Asia's largest entertainment market

•Asia's largest licensing show 
 

UFI Approved Events in Hong Kong:

•APLF - Fashion Access

•APLF – Materials, Manufacturing & Technology

•Asian Elenex (The Asian International Electrical, Electronic Engineering, Lighting Technology and HVAC & R Show)

•Cosmoprof Asia (International Cosmetic, Perfumery, Toiletries and Hairdressing Exhibition)

•electronicAsia

•HOFEX (Asian International Exhibition of Food & Drink, Hotel, Restaurant & Foodservice Equipment, Supplies & Services)

•Hong Kong Baby Products Fair

•Hong Kong Electronics Fair

•Hong Kong Fashion Week

•Hong Kong Gifts & Premium Fair

•Hong Kong Houseware Fair

•Hong Kong International Diamond, Gem & Pearl Show

•Hong Kong International Jewellery Show

•Hong Kong International Jewelry Manufacturers’ Show

•Hong Kong International Lighting Fair

•Hong Kong Optical Fair

•Hong Kong Toys & Games Fair

•Hong Kong Watch & Clock Fair

Source: UFI – The Global Association of the Exhibition Industry
