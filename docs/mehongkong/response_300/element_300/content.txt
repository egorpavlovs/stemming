                  Gourmet Spots

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

    
      If your internationally-minded MICE visitors prefer to take a stroll and see what dining delights get their stomachs rumbling, then they should head to Hong Kong’s most popular dining districts, where they will be dazzled by choices of cuisines, restaurants and bars. They can soak up the buzzy atmosphere at street level, choose from a whole host of indoor and outdoor eateries, and see for themselves why the locals head to these dynamic areas after dark for a nightcap.

    


	
		




        
          
              
          
          
            Knutsford Terrace - The Place to be Seen in Kowloon
            The pedestrianised road, located close to major hotels and shopping malls, is packed with restaurants and bars. 
            
              Read more about Knutsford Terrace - The Place to be Seen in Kowloon
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Always Ready for a Good Time at LKF
            Lan Kwai Fong is the city’s most popular dining and entertainment destination that stretches from exciting supper clubs to casual delis and inviting alfresco dining.
            
              Read more about Always Ready for a Good Time at LKF
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            SoHo-the Cosmopolitan Chic
            By day it’s full of boutique shopping, curios, fine art and souvenirs. At night, this eclectic entertainment district incorporates some of the best little bars and restaurants in the city.
            
              Read more about SoHo-the Cosmopolitan Chic
