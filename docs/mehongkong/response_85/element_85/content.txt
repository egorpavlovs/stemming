                  World-Leading Venues 

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

  
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      With world-leading design, space capability, versatility, and service standards, Hong Kong’s state-of-the-art venues will accommodate events of all shapes and sizes in style and comfort – leaving you rested and unhindered to concentrate on the bigger picture.

 

A venue for every event

 

Hong Kong’s purpose-built meeting, convention and exhibition facilities stand tall on the world stage, with stunning design, space versatility and high-capacity potential. The city’s primary venues are the AsiaWorld-Expo integrated with the airport and the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre (HKCEC) in the heart of town. The newly developed Kai Tai Cruise Terminal, which is also set to become one of the city’s most impressive and prestigious MICE venues.
