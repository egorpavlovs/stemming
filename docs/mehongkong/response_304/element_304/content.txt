                  Swanky Spots

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

    
      MICE visitors favouring exclusivity and privacy as they dine in Hong Kong may prefer to gather for a welcome cocktail or team-building dinner by taking a break from the bustling city and stepping into the tranquil and unique atmosphere of Hong Kong’s prestigious private clubs, which compete for class and service on a global scale. Accustomed to catering to the needs of Hong Kong’s well-to-do, these exclusive havens will have no problem meeting the demands of even the most discerning diners.

    


	
		




        
          
              
          
          
            A Holiday Vibe at The Verandah
            The Verandah at The Repulse Bay has the relaxed setting harks back to the Colonial era, an undisturbed place to get down to business!
            
              Read more about A Holiday Vibe at The Verandah
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            A Winning Combination at The Hong Kong Jockey Club
            The HK Jockey Club offers private tours on race days, it is also available for private exhibitions, press conferences and parties on non-race days.
            
              Read more about A Winning Combination at The Hong Kong Jockey Club
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            An Elite Welcome at The Aberdeen Marina Club
            The Aberdeen Marina Club features stylish banquet venues for a range of events, including conferences,training lectures and gala theme parties!
            
              Read more about An Elite Welcome at The Aberdeen Marina Club
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Discover the glamour of Hong Kong at Aqua

            With dazzling views of Hong Kong’s harbour and skyline, ultra-stylish interiors and an innovative menu offering the very best contemporary Italian and Japanese cuisines, Aqua tops the list of the city’s sophisticated dining destinations.

            
              Read more about Discover the glamour of Hong Kong at Aqua

            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Dine on Jet-fresh Seafood on the 101st Floor at Dragon Seal
            If you want to experience something special away from the bustle of urban life without trekking to the outskirts of town, Dragon Seal would be the ideal place.
            
              Read more about Dine on Jet-fresh Seafood on the 101st Floor at Dragon Seal
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Impressive old China-style venue overlooking Hong Kong’s modern skyline
            Long-standing, award-winning restaurant Hutong serves fiery Northern Chinese dishes, offering a modern interpretation of traditional and authentic Chinese cooking.
            
              Read more about Impressive old China-style venue overlooking Hong Kong’s modern skyline
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Mountain-top Meals at The Peak Lookout
            The Peak Lookout is a refined dining experience, surrounded by spacious terrace overlooking the Pokfulam Country Park and South China Sea. 
            
              Read more about Mountain-top Meals at The Peak Lookout
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Panoramic Seaview at the Airport World Trade Centre
            Airport World Trade Centre offers spacious and a secluded retreat for networking and meetings, MICE groups can reserve any sized workspace there!
            
              Read more about Panoramic Seaview at the Airport World Trade Centre
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            The World’s Highest Bar - OZONE
            OZONE is proudly positioned on the 118th floor (490 meters above sea level) of The Ritz-Carlton, with the astonishing view of Victoria Harbour!

            
              Read more about The World’s Highest Bar - OZONE
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Urban Class and Country Manners at The American Club
            The American Club offers MICE groups a retreat from city life in an idyllic, green setting that suits alfresco cocktail parties and barbecues.
            
              Read more about Urban Class and Country Manners at The American Club
