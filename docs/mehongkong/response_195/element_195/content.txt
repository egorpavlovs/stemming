                    Regal Airport Hotel

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Consistently recognised as the world’s best airport hotel by Skytrax, Business Traveller Magazine and the World Luxury Hotel Awards (among others), the Regal’s meeting rooms and conference facilities are as state of the art as its guest accommodation. With 30 function rooms including the largest pillar-free grand ballroom in Hong Kong, the hotel is ideal for large-scale seminars, conferences, meetings, exhibitions, banquets and cocktail receptions for up to 1,500 guests.
