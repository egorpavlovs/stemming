                    Professional Support

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Choosing to host your event in Hong Kong is the first step – the city’s comprehensive chain of support services and experienced experts will make sure it’s a success right through to its final stride with precise and reliable execution.

      
      
    
    


    
      A city built for business
      
      
    
    


    
      Consistently ranked as one of Asia’s most business-savvy cities, part of Hong Kong’s global pulling power for MICE events is its expansive network of supportive elements and facilities, all serviced by highly-qualified experts who will go the extra mile to ensure each of your requirements are met and surpassed.

      
      
    
    


    
      A wealth of professional experience
      
      
    
    


    
      Hong Kong’s wide pool of motivated and efficient professionals will leave no stone unturned when providing practical assistance for your event, from initial brainstorming meetings, to designing and constructing unique on-site elements, to final packing and freight forwarding, to arranging post-event tours.

      
      
    
    


    
      MEHK - Always here to help you
      
      
    
    


    
      Meetings and Exhibitions Hong Kong (MEHK), a division of the Hong Kong Tourism Board, provides free advisory services and support to help you plan any event from start to finish. Whether it’s helping you secure hotels that suit your unique needs and budget, or providing city-wide hospitality and trade offers; erecting dedicated immigration counters at Hong Kong International Airport, or presenting spectacular cultural and leisure experiences that will give your event a memorable edge – step by step, MEHK will lay the groundwork that takes your event to unprecedented heights.
