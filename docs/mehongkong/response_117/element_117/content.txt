                    2016 MDRT Experience and Global Conference 

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      This year, Million Dollar Round Table (MDRT) appointed Hong Kong to be the host city for its bi-annual event. More than 8,500 attendees representing 35 countries attended the premier association for financial professionals.   Bringing a one-of-a-kind experience to members and non-members, executives from the MDRT committee took the stage to share cutting-edge strategies and sales ideas for those in the life insurance and financial services business. This is the second time this globally recognized conference was held in Hong Kong since 2004. 

With Hong Kong’s position as a financial centre and its strategic location, the three-day event concluded with a great turnout of attendees. 

      
      
    
    
    
      “Hong Kong is a global financial hub, what brought us here was the density of our members in Hong Kong and China together.  We are so proud to have achieved a record breaking attendance of 8,500 attendees from 35 countries. The facilities in Hong Kong are incredible and it is very easy to get around the city.”

    
      Jeanne Malone
      Director, Meeting Services, MDRT (USA)
