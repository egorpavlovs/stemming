                  Exhibitions

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      Hong Kong has a powerful pull for exhibitions and trade fairs that stretches globally, hosting close to 130 major exhibitions every year that attract over 2.4 million visitors. The city hits the nail on the head in every area that appeals to exhibitors looking to showcase products to a business-savvy audience – world-leading facilities and infrastructure, valuable proximity to China and Asia’s dynamic markets, a free and fair trade climate, zero import tax on exhibits, professional support services, a low tax rate, and plenty of irresistible attractions to reenergise at the end of a productive day.
