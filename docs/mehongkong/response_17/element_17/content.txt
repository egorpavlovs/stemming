                  MICE Activities

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      If your exhibitors are keen to take advantage of Hong Kong’s world-renowned dining, shopping and sightseeing attractions while they're here, they won’t travel home disappointed. They needn’t step far beyond the city’s strategically located exhibition venues to unwind over a traditional hot pot, hunt for a buyer’s bargain after the show, or take in an historical monument during their down time. For those looking to stretch their legs a little further, the city’s outlying islands are home to numerous small fishing villages and excellent seafood restaurants, while protected country parks boast hiking trails with stunning vistas and pristine sandy beaches. Come and see why more than 56 million visitors choose to come to Hong Kong in 2016 – for the glitz, the glamour and the simple serenity.
