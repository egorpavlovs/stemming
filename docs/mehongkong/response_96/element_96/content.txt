                    Cruise back in time

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      The last authentic traditional fishing junk plying the harbour,
the Duk Ling has a history as colourful as the city. Built in nearby Macau as a fishing boat, more than 50 years later she has found a new life as a completely restored pleasure craft for scheduled tours and charters. The vessel has scored a modest amount of fame in promotions for the city, with her vivid red sails featuring prominently against the backdrop of the spectacular skyline.

Once onboard, the Duk Ling – or clever duck – is a perfect way to experience Hong Kong from the ocean at any time of day or night. The boat is a firm favourite with corporates, who have enjoyed countless sensational sunsets over the Western District and day tours to the outlying islands for cocktails and canapés. Perhaps they appreciate the small touches, such as custom-made banners to welcome guests onboard.
