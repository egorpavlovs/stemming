                    NU SKIN 2012

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      One of the world’s largest anti-aging products direct-selling companies, NU SKIN has made Asia’s world city the base for its biennial Greater China Regional Convention for the past 10 years, attracting a record-breaking 20,000 delegates from all over the world to its 2012 meeting – double the attendance of the 2010 event.

The organisers say Hong Kong continues to be the company’s preferred destination because of its convenient location with worldwide air links, ample professional companies supplying all aspects of event management, and most importantly, the AsiaWorld-Expo (AWE) venue and its proximity to the airport. AWE’s multi-functional space allowed NU SKIN to fully utilise the whole venue to conduct meetings, conference, exhibitions and catering under one roof, making logistics easy.

The huge range of quality hotels located near the venue and all over town made it easy for delegates to arrange their own accommodation.  And with the sophisticated and safe transport network, delegates could explore Hong Kong’s fascinating culture, attractions and famous diverse food options.

      
      
    
    
    
      “The central location is very easy to access by business partners coming from Mainland China, Taiwan and Southeast Asia, as well as other parts of the world.”

    
      Mr Patrick Yeung
      Vice President, Strategic Planning and DevelopmentGreater China Region
    
    
    
      “Coming to Hong Kong is good, not only for the convention, but also because the nightlife is very safe. I feel safe and that’s very important for us.”

    
      Mr Bill Yoo
      A delegate from Korea
    
    
    
      ”I love Hong Kong, especially as I like to eat and I find food here, such as dim sum and roasted meat, particularly enjoyable..”

    
      Mr Chua Syer Cin
      A delegate from Malaysia
