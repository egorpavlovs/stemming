                    Hong Kong Leads Winners at 23rd World Travel Awards 2016 with Two Prestigious Awards

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      20 October 2016

Asia’s world city leads by taking home two prestigious awards, ‘Asia's Leading Meetings & Conference Destination’ and ‘Asia's Leading City Break Destination’ for two consecutive years at the World Travel Awards 2016.

World Travel Awards is one of the travel industry’s most sought-after accolades rewarding leaders in the tourism, airline, hotel and hospitality sectors around the world. Once again, Hong Kong cements its reputation as one of the world’s most popular destinations for meetings and conferences. 

Together with the city, four Hong Kong hotels are leading the pack by earning region-wide recognitions, with Hong Kong SkyCity Marriott Hotel claiming the title of ‘Asia's Leading Airport Hotel 2016’, InterContinental Grand Stanford Hong Kong awarded ‘Asia's Leading Business Hotel 2016’,  The Peninsula Hong Kong taking home the title of ‘Asia's Leading Luxury Hotel 2016’ and  Tai O Heritage Hotel recognized as ‘Asia's Leading Heritage Hotel 2016’. Other Hong Kong winners are listed below:
