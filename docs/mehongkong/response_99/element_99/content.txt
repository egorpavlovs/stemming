                       Hong Kong Wine & Dine Festival 
										    (26–29 October 2017)
										

				   
                  
                 
                  
                  
                  
            Every year, the Hong Kong Wine & Dine Festival brings together world-class food, drinks and  entertainment right next to the stunning Victoria Harbour skyline. Aside from  wine-pairing menus crafted by master chefs and tasting classes with industry  experts, there are wines and food booths featuring everything from single malts  to artisanal cheese to keep one’s palate refreshed.
            In the 2016 event, some  145,000 visitors came to taste wines from 28 countries at over 300 booths. The 2017  event is just around the corner! Bring your MICE group here at late October for a delicious harbourside experience. 
                
								  
               
                                





                             
   
                      
                      
                      
                      


								
								

									
									
                                       
              
							
							    
										
								
								
									
										
											Hong Kong Great November Feast 
										    (1-30 November 2017)
										

									   
										  Set your tongue tingling with Hong Kong Great November Feast — a month-long series of gourmet happenings,  including wine fairs, street carnivals, dining offers and more,  in the culinary capital of Asia. Be sure to make time to slurp and nom the city’s  greatest like a local on your visit! 
									  
								  
 
				       
                      
                      
                      
                      
                      
                      
                      
                      
                      



                             
   
                      
                      
                      
                      


								
								

									
									
                                       
              
							
							    
										
								
								
									
										
											A Sporting Playground for Adrenaline Junkies! 
										

										
										  Whatever your sporting passion, Hong Kong won’t  let you down!  Let’s start with these major crowd pleasers on  your next trip!
										   
									      Bike fever has swept Hong  Kong and the city is celebrating all things cycling with the Sun Hung Kai Properties Hong Kong Cyclothon to be held on 8 October 2017.  The event kicks off a season that turns the city into a sports ground for  adrenaline junkies from around the globe! Plan your next MICE event and be  inspired by the energetic spirit of the city.



  
    
  
  
    


      Hong Kong Tennis Open (7–15 October 2017)


      
    
      Hong Kong Tennis Open, organised by the Hong Kong Tennis Association, is a Women’s Tennis Association-sanctioned International Series Event that bring international stars to the city.
      
  



  
    
  
  
    
    
    Hong Kong Squash Open (12–19 November 2017)


Hong Kong Squash Open is rated as a Grand Prix event on the international squash calendar. The popular competition stars dozens of world-ranked players battling it out in front of enthusiastic fans.


  


  
    
  
  
     Hong Kong Open (23–26 November 2017)
       First held in 1959, the Hong Kong Open is the city’s oldest professional sporting event. What’s known as the 'jewel in the crown' of Asian golf has a rich history of winners, including nine major champions.  
      
  


  
    
  
  
          FIA Formula E Hong Kong ePrix (2–3 December 2017)

      
      The FIA Formula E Championship is coming to Hong Kong as part of its tour of major cities around the world. Unlike conventional motorsports races, Formula E combines energy, entertainment and environmental friendliness to delight spectators.
