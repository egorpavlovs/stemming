                    MEHK Support

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Our comprehensive advisory services will assist you through the entire planning process, from providing bidding support, including coordinating site inspection visits, assisting with written proposals, and providing audio-visual materials about Hong Kong; to help with sourcing appropriate venues, hotels, suppliers and contractors; to engaging with relevant government departments for support; to suggesting enticing social programmes and tour itineraries, to conducting pre-event promotion; to supplying useful travel information; to compiling hospitality and trade rewards.

Ways to welcome your delegates to Hong Kong include dedicated immigration counters at Hong Kong International Airport for some events, as well as a digitalised welcome message display at the airport’s Visitor Centre. Other complimentary possibilities include customised welcome packages for your delegates, and thrilling cultural experiences to impress and entertain them, including fortune-telling, lion dancing, a Chinese drum show, a kung fu show, and many more.
