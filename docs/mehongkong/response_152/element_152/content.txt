                    Herbalife North Asia Extravaganza 2017

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Herbalife Nutrition, one of the world’s premier nutrition companies, gave an enthusiastic stamp of approval to Hong Kong when close to 9,000 mission-driven Herbalife Nutrition members from the neighbouring countries and regions, including South Korea, Japan and Taiwan, gathered at the AsiaWorld-Expo from 14–16 June to take part in the company’s 2017 North Asia Extravaganza.

Herbalife Nutrition members packed AsiaWorld-Expo, the multi-functional mega event space located next to the airport, in a three-day extravaganza of lights, camera and action. They enjoyed an electrifying line-up of programmes which included top-notch trainings by Herbalife leaders and nutrition experts, fitness sessions to help attendees maintain their healthy active lifestyles, a shake bar for an energy boost between meetings, a sports sponsorships area for attendees to catch a glimpse of the latest achievements of sponsored athlete Cristiano Ronaldo, and a photo-op area where everyone shared exciting moments of the event that went beyond the Expo. 

      
      
    
    
    
      “Hong Kong’s connectivity and accessibility made it a destination of choice for our 2017 North Asia Extravaganza. The city’s vibrant lifestyle, diverse culture and plentiful options in everything from hotels to leisure offerings impress Herbalife Nutrition members, while the city’s ‘can-do’ spirit dovetails with our theme ‘Driven by Our Mission, Inspired by Our Results’.  Our event planning went very smoothly and we are very pleased with the support we were given every step of the way and how it turned out.”

    
      Stella Tsai
      Vice President, North Asia, Herbalife Nutrition
