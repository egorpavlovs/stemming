                  Seafood Delights

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

    
      A MICE visit to Hong Kong would not be complete without an opportunity to savour an authentic Chinese food experience. The city’s Cantonese cuisine typically involves fresh off-the-boat seafood and a view overlooking the fishing boats, but as the region’s culinary capital, Hong Kong also offers Chinese food from almost every Mainland province. Your guests will be spoiled for choice as they get together around a banquet table and savour one of the most appetising and authentic meals the city has to offer.

    


	
		




        
          
              
          
          
            Local Flavours in Lei Yue Mun
            The fishing village of Lei Yue Mun first came to prominence in the 1960s
when it became a destination for fresh seafood, sparking a trend for the
customer selecting live seafood.
            
              Read more about Local Flavours in Lei Yue Mun
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Sai Kung’s Own Eat Street
            What was once a destination for few people other than fishermen is now a fashionable city retreat, with great seafood restaurants.
            
              Read more about Sai Kung’s Own Eat Street
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Spectacular Seafood on Lamma Island
            The island is a fabulous destination to experience the culture of a traditional fishing village.
            
              Read more about Spectacular Seafood on Lamma Island
