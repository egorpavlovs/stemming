                    Hong Kong Garnered the ‘Best City for Meetings’ Award for the 11th time  

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Voted by frequent travellers around the world, Hong Kong was once again crowned the ‘Best City for Meetings’ in Asia (formerly named ‘Best Business City in Asia’) by Smart Travel Asia in the 2017 Best in Travel Poll. The fact that Hong Kong has earned the accolade for the 11th time reinforces the city’s position as a premier MICE destination in the region, thanks to its excellent connectivity, business-friendly environment and world-class infrastructure.

Our counterparts were also recognised by the awards, with Hong Kong International Airport ranking second in the ‘Top 10 Airports’ category. Accommodation-wise, The Peninsula Hong Kong and Island Shangri-La Hong Kong obtained the top spots in the ‘Top 25 Business Hotels’ category, while Grand Hyatt Hong Kong and Four Seasons Hotel Hong Kong were among the top three in the ‘Top 25 Conference Hotels’ category.

Congratulations to all our winning partners!
