                  Corporate Social Responsibility

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

  
    
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      Hong Kong caters to MICE groups looking for hands-on CSR activities that encourage team-building within a socially responsible setting, promote environmental awareness, and give guests an up-close look at the community of Hong Kong. Let your MICE visitors have fun, feel good, and give back, returning to their workplaces with a sense of accomplishment and renewed inspiration.

    


	
		




        
          
              
          
          
            Dialogue In The Dark
            After hours of bright lights and intensive exchanges at a demanding MICE event, your attendees may welcome a spell of darkness!
            
              Read more about Dialogue In The Dark
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Hong Kong Wetland Park
            As a corporate social responsibility initiative, the park offers the Outdoor Wetland Conservation Workshop to MICE groups. 
            
              Read more about Hong Kong Wetland Park
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Tai O
            This stilted fishing village on Lantau Island offers several activities to engage hearts and minds.
            
              Read more about Tai O
