                    Hong Kong International Airport

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Hong Kong International Airport (HKIA) is one of the busiest airports in the world. It serves flights to and from around 190 key destinations worldwide, including 50 cities in Mainland China, through about 1,100 daily scheduled flights by over 100 airlines. With its world class facilities and services, HKIA has been recognised as the world’s best airport over 60 times. 

HKIA provides comprehensive dining, shopping and entertainment options to travellers, including over 80 restaurants ranging from Michelin-recommended gourmet outlets to affordable food courts; around 300 shops with a wide range of about 40 world famous brands; and an array of entertainment facilities such as UA IMAX Theatre with the largest IMAX giant screen in Hong Kong.
