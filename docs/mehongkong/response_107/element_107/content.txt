	Hong Kong Just Got Even More Rewarding!
	
		
			
			
		
	

  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      
Recognised as one of the world’s most exciting and vibrant cities, Hong Kong is a destination of superlatives for your convention delegates or meeting and incentive groups.

MEHK has joined forces with industry partners to develop privileges that take corporate events to new heights. Extensive privileges and add-on benefits start with your airport arrival and continue through your hotel stay, MICE event and sightseeing up to the day you depart the city! Read on to discover why Hong Kong should be your first choice for any event!
