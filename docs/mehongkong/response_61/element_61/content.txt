                    Hong Kong Brings Home the ‘Best Business City’ Award for 10 Consecutive Years

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Having received the prestigious ‘Best Business City in Asia’* award for the 10th time, and recognised by the Business Traveller Asia-Pacific Awards 2017 in the category of ‘Best Business City in the World’, Asia’s world city has reinforced its reputation as the ideal destination for trade.

With a strategic location, superb infrastructure and global connectivity, the city has once again been acknowledged by some of the world’s most seasoned travellers as a premier MICE destination. 

MEHK would like to take this opportunity to congratulate our industry partners’ great achievement in the awards:

      
      
    
    

    
      
        Business Traveller Asia Pacific Awards 2017
      
      
        
          
	
		
			Airline:
			 
		
		
			Cathay Pacific
			Best Frequent-Flyer Programme
			Best Airport Lounge in Asia-Pacific
			Best Premium Economy Class
		
		
			Hotels: 
			 
		
		
			Grand Hyatt, Hong Kong
			Best Business Hotel in the Asia-Pacific
		
		
			Island Shangri-La Hong Kong
			Best Business Hotel in Hong Kong
		
		
			Mandarin Oriental, Hong Kong
			Best Business Hotel in the World
		
		
			Regal Airport, Hong Kong
			Best Airport Hotel in Asia-Pacific
		
	


        
      
      
    


    
      * Previously known as ‘Best Business City in Northeast Asia’
