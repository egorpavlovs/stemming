                  Heritage Sites

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

    
      Shaped by an East-meets-West heritage, the city's unique melange of architecture offers an unforgettable backdrop for your Hong Kong meeting experience.

    
    
      
    


	
		




        
          
              
          
          
            Heritage Venue of The Mills
            The Mills, having witnessed the heyday of the manufacturing industry in the1960s, was once the thumping heart of Nan Fung Textiles.
            
              Read more about Heritage Venue of The Mills
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Cultural Venue of Tai Kwun
            Tai Kwun, the former Central Police Station, Central Magistracy and Victoria Prison, has been revitalised as a cultural haven. 
            
              Read more about Cultural Venue of Tai Kwun
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Contemporary Art Curated in the Historical Pawn
            Dating back to 1888, The Pawn is housed in one of Hong Kong’s most historical buildings. 
            
              Read more about Contemporary Art Curated in the Historical Pawn
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Entertain at the UNESCO Heritage Site of Crown Wine Cellars
            Located in Shouson Hill, an exclusive suburb on Hong Kong Island and which is also of immense historical significance having been declared a UNESCO Asia Pacific Heritage Site in 2007. 
            
              Read more about Entertain at the UNESCO Heritage Site of Crown Wine Cellars
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Historical Backdrop of the Fishing Village of Tai O
            To delve into the heart of Tai O, taste the home-made local specialty of shrimp paste at Tai O Lookout.
            
              Read more about Historical Backdrop of the Fishing Village of Tai O
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Luxurious Industrialism at AMMO
            AMMO is a multi-award winning restaurant located in the stunning heritage site of a former 19th century explosives compound.
            
              Read more about Luxurious Industrialism at AMMO
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Prestige and Heritage at The Grand Stage
            The Grand Stage is situated inside the century-old Western Market, a heritage building protected for its Edwardian-style architecture and Gothic interior.
            
              Read more about Prestige and Heritage at The Grand Stage
