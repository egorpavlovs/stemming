                  Free Cocktail Reception at Your Hotel

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                


	Mingle and relax with your group
	
		
			
			
		
	

  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      Choose from over 50 participating hotels* around town where you and your delegates will be treated to a complimentary cocktail reception. This is a great opportunity for touching base and networking with event participants over drinks, at the comfort of the accommodation of their choice.

    
    
      Offer Details:


	
	Complimentary cocktail reception for MICE groups at a designated hotel venue, with a selection of beverages (including beer, wine, soft drinks, juices etc.) and light refreshments for each delegate
	
	
	From 1 April 2018 to 31 March 2019
	
	
	Meeting, Incentive and Convention groups with at least 50 non-Hong Kong delegates staying in the participating hotel for two consecutive nights or more are eligible for the offer

	(Please apply for the complimentary cocktail reception at the time of hotel room booking, and quote the reference code ‘HKR1819’.)
	


    
    
      This fabulous offer is available at over 50 hotels located in different parts of the city, ranging from luxury to high-quality budget ones. Check out the full list of participating hotels and their additional offers below:

    
    
        
      Hong Kong Island - 16 hotels

Kowloon - 22 hotels

New Territories - 9 hotels

Outlying Islands - 8 hotels

 

    

	Terms and Conditions for Complimentary Cocktail Reception at Hotel
	
		
			
			
		
	

    
      
	The offer is valid for Meeting, Incentive and Convention groups with a minimum of 50 non-Hong Kong delegates staying at a participating hotel for at least two (2) consecutive nights in Hong Kong.
	The offer is valid from 1 April 2018 to 31 March 2019, both dates inclusive.
	The offer is subject to availability and hotel confirmation.
	 All booking confirmations/amendments/cancellations are handled between hotels and guests directly. Final confirmation of all bookings is at the discretion of the hotels.
	The offer is subject to change by participating hotels without prior notice.
	The offer has no cash value and cannot be redeemed for cash.
	The offer cannot be used in conjunction with other promotional/discount offers.
	The Hong Kong Tourism Board (HKTB) is not a supplier of the products and services and shall not be responsible for any liabilities in relation thereto.
	Hotel offers and information (including text and photos) are provided by hotels.
	In case of disputes, the decision of HKTB and participating hotels shall be final.
	HKTB disclaims any liability for the quality or fitness for purpose of third-party products or services; or for any errors or omissions.
	These Terms and Conditions are written in English and Chinese. In case of discrepancies, the English version shall prevail.
