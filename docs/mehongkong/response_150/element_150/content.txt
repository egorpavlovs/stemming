                    Amway ANZ Achievers 2015

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Amway would like to pamper its 320 top performing distributors with a five-day incentive trip. The key objective of the Amway ANZ Achievers 2015 was an enticement for active achievers to qualify for next year. Stories from this trip could provide further encouragement. 

The company continues to yearly raise the bar to exceed the expectation of sophisticated achievers who have already attended many such events.

Leveraging on the Hong Kong’s Asia hub status and its treasure trove of diverse activities, a memorable journey was crafted combining top-notch culinary, shopping, sightseeing in lavish style. 

 

      
      
    
    
    
      “We look at destinations that can provide an exceptional experience for our qualifiers. This city is great for that as its infrastructure is fantastic, and it offers a complete array of significant experiences from food to attractions.”

    
      Michial Coldwell
      General Manager, Australia and New Zealand, Amway
    
    
    
      “ When talking about benefits of coming to Hong Kong , the superiority of hotels and venues in Hong Kong, everyone knows the quality here and you don’t have to convince other people.”

    
      Renato Fabbro
      Event Manager, Amway Australia
