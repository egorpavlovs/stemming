                  How to Get There
                  
                    
                      
                        
                        Bay Bridge Hong Kong is conveniently located in Tsuen Wan which has the benefits of a modern transportation network which includes the MTR station Tsuen Wan and direct access to Tsuen Wan Road interchange, Highways number 3, 5, 7, 8 and 9, West Kowloon Highway, Hong Kong International Airport, Tai Lam Tunnel and North Lantau Highway.
•It takes only 7 minutes from MTR Tsuen Wan Station to The Bay Bridge Hong Kong by shuttle bus (complimentary)
•30 minutes driving distance to and from the airport
•MTR direct access to: Tsim Sha Tsui - 20 minutes 
•Central: 30 minutes
