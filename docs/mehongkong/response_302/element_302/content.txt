                  Panoramic Vistas

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

    
      Even Hong Kong’s most time-pressed MICE visitors won’t have to choose between the city’s celebrated cuisine and world-famous skyline. Hong Kong is home to a wealth of top-notch restaurants that offer astonishing views of the city from multiple breathtaking angles – each of them an easy commute from key MICE venues and hotel districts. With choices ranging from Western fine dining to Asian authentic dish, business associates will share a visual and culinary feast that will become one of the most unforgettable parts of their MICE visit to Hong Kong.

    


	
		




        
          
              
          
          
            Celebrate with 360° Views of Hong Kong
            Sky100 offers you 16,000 square feet - the largest space available on one floor in all of HK, plus the stunning 360 degree views!
            
              Read more about Celebrate with 360° Views of Hong Kong
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Elegant Dining Against the Dazzling Harbour at Ambrosia
            Ambrosia Oyster Bar & Grill features an extensive variety of freshly imported oysters, seafood and modern European cuisines.
            
              Read more about Elegant Dining Against the Dazzling Harbour at Ambrosia
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Floor-to-ceiling Views at Wooloomooloo
            Occupying the 31st Floor of The Hennessy, Wooloomooloo Steakhouse (Wan Chai) encompasses an exclusive 270-degree view of Happy Valley, Victoria Harbour and the Wan Chai metropolitan district with floor-to-ceiling windows. 
            
              Read more about Floor-to-ceiling Views at Wooloomooloo
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Harbourfront Dining at the Watermark
            Watermark is located at Hong Kong Central’s Star Ferry, offering unparalleled waterfront dining next to the city’s favourite landmark – Victoria Harbour.
            
              Read more about Harbourfront Dining at the Watermark
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Penthouse Prestige at SEVVA
            SEVVA, created and founded by Bonnae Gokson, is the quintessential HK dining & lounging experience in the city, which offers 360-degree panoramic view!
            
              Read more about Penthouse Prestige at SEVVA
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Quality Italian Fare with ISOLA
            Isola Bar & Grill is internationally renowned for its chic interiors, spectacular views of Victoria Harbour and superb cuisine designed by world -renowned star chef, Chef Corrado Michellazzo. 
            
              Read more about Quality Italian Fare with ISOLA
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Sail Away with Treasures aboard Nanhai No.1

            Combining contemporary Chinese cuisine, cutting-edge interior design and famous city panoramas, Nnanhai No. 1 is Hong Kong's newest destination restaurant.
            
              Read more about Sail Away with Treasures aboard Nanhai No.1

            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Seaside Escapes at D'Deck
            D'Deck is an outdoor boardwalk bordered by the sea, with 20 food and beverage outlets and a spectacular 180-degree view back towards Hong Kong!

            
              Read more about Seaside Escapes at D'Deck
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Views Inside and Out at Cafe Deco
            With stunning views of Tsim Sha Tsui, Cafe Deco makes for a great venue for everything from lunches to dinners, from press conferences to team building events.
            
              Read more about Views Inside and Out at Cafe Deco
