                  Dining

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

    
      With an enormous variety in restaurants, cuisines and settings for MICE groups of all sizes and budgets, Hong Kong’s excellent and versatile dining scene is celebrated the world over. Asia’s culinary capital is the perfect place for MICE visitors to celebrate at the end of a productive day over food that will leave them craving another visit, whether they are looking for home-style Chinese cooking for an agreeable price, authentic dim sum served aboard a floating restaurant, or a tour of one of the city’s many gourmet hotspots – the choices are limitless.

Aside from the large range of hotel meeting rooms and facilities on offer, here are some off-site venues that can cater for a hundred guests or more for your next event in town.

    


	
		


        
          
              
          
          
            Panoramic Vistas
            HK is full of top-notch restaurants that offer astonishing views of the city from multiple angles, they are near to key MICE venues and hotel districts!
            
              Read more about Panoramic Vistas
            
          
        




















        
          
              
          
          
            Swanky Spots
            MICE visitor may prefer to gather for a cocktail party or team-building dinner by taking a break from the busy city and stepping into the swanky spots.
            
              Read more about Swanky Spots
            
          
        






















        
          
              
          
          
            Floating Venues
            Invite your MICE visitors to experience the beauty of Hong Kong’s harbour with these floating restaurants and harbour cruises serving up authentic cuisine. 
            
              Read more about Floating Venues
            
          
        










        
          
              
          
          
            Heritage Sites
            Shaped by an East-meets-West heritage, the city's unique melange of architecture offers an unforgettable backdrop for your Hong Kong meeting experience.

            
              Read more about Heritage Sites
            
          
        
















        
          
              
          
          
            Gourmet Spots
            Foodies will want to delve into Hong Kong’s hottest dining districts that dish out a multitude of cuisines in one spot. 
            
              Read more about Gourmet Spots
            
          
        








        
          
              
          
          
            Seafood Delights
            Present the best of Hong Kong’s Chinese dining culture to your guests, from fresh seafood at a scenic island, to a decadent feast around banquet tables spinning with Lazy Susans. 
            
              Read more about Seafood Delights
            
          
        








        
          
              
          
          
            Local Flavors
            Enjoy uniquely Hong Kong food experiences. 
            
              Read more about Local Flavors
