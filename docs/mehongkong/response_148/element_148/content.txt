                  What’s Hot

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

  
    
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      Hong Kong’s rich variety keeps meeting and incentive trip visitors entertained with ease, but several unique activities have won hearts as the city’s favourites. From Hong Kong’s best-loved team-building experiences, to popular dining locations, must-see attractions, and celebrated chartered cruises – these are Hong Kong’s number one ways to enrich your next meeting or incentive trip.
 

    


	
		




        
          
              
          
          
            Hong Kong Disneyland
            Magic, adventure and the world’s favourite cast of characters await. Embark on a journey with magical adventures!
            
              Read more about Hong Kong Disneyland
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Ocean Park Hong Kong
            Thrill rides, giant pandas and a world-class aquarium keep Ocean Park on Hong Kong's list of favourites.

            
              Read more about Ocean Park Hong Kong
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Ngong Ping 360 and The Big Buddha
            Your sky-land-sea adventure starts with breathtaking cable-car views of mountains, forests and coast.

            
              Read more about Ngong Ping 360 and The Big Buddha
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Jumbo Kingdom 
            Home to the world’s largest floating restaurant.
            
              Read more about Jumbo Kingdom 
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            The Peak
            Out-of-this-world view of skyscrapers, the glittering harbour and the green hills of the distant New Territories.
            
              Read more about The Peak
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            sky100 Hong Kong Observation Deck
            100 floors above the harbour, marvel at the view from Hong Kong‘s tallest building.
            
              Read more about sky100 Hong Kong Observation Deck
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Lamma Island
            Find out why this island is loved for its pleasant blend of the quieter sides of Western and Chinese living.
            
              Read more about Lamma Island
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Chartered Boat
            Get into the pulsating heart of Hong Kong with a tour of our spectacular harbour!
            
              Read more about Chartered Boat
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Knutsford Terrace & Observatory Court
            Enjoy a tucked-away strip of dining and nightlife.
            
              Read more about Knutsford Terrace & Observatory Court
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Lan Kwai Fong 
            Hong Kong’s best known party hot spot.
            
              Read more about Lan Kwai Fong
