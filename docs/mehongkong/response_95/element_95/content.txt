                    Making Big Waves with the Bauhinia

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      When grand-sized groups hit the water, the size and scale of the Harbour Cruise – Bauhinia is ideal. The company’s four, twin-decked vessels cater for groups that need a fully functioning event space on the water. The fleet seats about 350 guests in banquet mode, with a bandstand, dance floor and bar included.

With their 280 square metres, the boats are hired regularly for dinner-dance events. Harbour Cruise – Bauhinia has executed top-flight charters for the East Asian Games cocktail party, the Olympic Rings Lights Up Ceremony, and the Miss Chinese International Pageant. And while size does matter, it’s panoramic views that keeps guests entertained.
