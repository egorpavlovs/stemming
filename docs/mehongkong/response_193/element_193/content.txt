        Grand Hyatt Hong Kong Located on the waterfront on Hong Kong Island, Grand Hyatt Hong Kong is directly connected to the Hong Kong Convention & Exhibition Centre.  The hotel is within walking distance from the Star Ferry Pier to Tsim Sha Tsui; only five minutes by car from shopping and business districts of Central and Admiralty; and 40 minutes by car from the airport.  The hotel features 542 comfortably appointed rooms including Grand Club residences on the top seven floors.  The hotel is also home to nine acclaimed restaurants and bars, and an extensive range of recreational facilities is available, including Plateau Spa for result-oriented body, facial treatments and massage, a 50-metre outdoor heated pool and 24-hour fitness studios.  A total of 22 multi-purpose event venues are available to cater events and meetings of all sizes.
