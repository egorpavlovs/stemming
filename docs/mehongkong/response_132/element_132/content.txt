      Hong Kong’s world-class exhibition venues are surrounded by dining, shopping and leisure activities that invite each participant to experience a slice of local life as they unwind after a long day. Explore the city’s tried and true favourites, which encompass Hong Kong’s hottest dining districts for quick meals nearby; the city’s best and closest shopping selections to scan for a souvenir; and popular sightseeing locations, from the thrilling to the tranquil.
