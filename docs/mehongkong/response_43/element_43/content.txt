                    Hong Kong Once Again Crowned Favourite Business City in the World

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      11 November 2016

On the heels of winning two prestigious awards at the World Travel Awards 2016, Hong Kong was once again crowned ‘Favourite Business City in the World’ based on votes from the highly discerning readership of Business Traveller China. This accolade marks the city’s 12th year running being recognised as one of the world’s top cities by Business Traveller China (BTC) Awards. 

“Hong Kong, is Asia’s world city and a prime business entry point to Mainland China. Its continuous achievement over the past decade is shaped by the endless efforts of highly efficient, service-oriented local stakeholders in everything from the international airport to business hotel sectors. It’s a great honour that Hong Kong and its valuable partners are selected by business travellers from China as their favourites for business travels,” said Kenneth Wong, General Manager, MICE & Cruise, Hong Kong Tourism Board. 

Meetings & Exhibitions Hong Kong (MEHK) would also like to take this opportunity to congratulate other winners of 2016 listed below: 

      
      
    
    

    
      
        2016 Business Traveller China Awards – Hong Kong winners 
      
      
        
          
	
		
			Best Frequent Flyer Programme 
			Cathay Pacific — Marco Polo Club 
		
		
			Best Airline Economy Class 
			Cathay Pacific and Cathay Dragon
		
		
			Best Airport in China 
			Hong Kong International Airport
		
		
			Best Hotel Brand in China
			Shangri-La
		
		
			Best Business Hotel in Hong Kong 
			Island Shangri-la, Hong Kong
