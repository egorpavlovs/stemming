                  Your Ultimate Rewards City

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      If you’re after a sophisticated destination that appeals to discerning minds gathering for a corporate meeting, a fun place to motivate teamwork at its best, or a rewarding location in which to give hardworking colleagues a pat on the back, Hong Kong inspires and outperforms on every level – motivating your colleagues to do the same.

Strategic Location: Hong Kong’s prime spot in the heart of enterprising Asia makes it the region’s most convenient and connected destination for international meetings and incentive trips. Half the world’s population is located within five hours of flying time, and it offers visa-free travel for visitors from about 170 countries.

Gateway to China: Sitting on China’s southern tip, Hong Kong offers speedy and straightforward commutes to and from the Mainland for enhanced incentive trips featuring multiple destinations. Its proximity to China also makes it an ideal meeting place for professionals from both locations.

Superb Infrastructure: World-class facilities and transport make Hong Kong the number one city in Asia for convenience and choice. Meeting and incentive trip participants will feel refreshed and energised as they navigate the city with ease, allowing them to make the most of its widespread business and leisure locations.

Dynamic Business Environment: As the regional headquarters of many of the world’s top business and finance organisations, corporate meetings are easily organised and executed in a city built for business. Offering access to the region’s leading experts, and with English as its first language of business, Hong Kong is Asia’s most powerful meeting destination.

Professional Support: Hospitable and hard-working, Hong Kong’s experienced professionals will ensure every meeting and incentive trip runs like clockwork, offering efficient expertise and supportive solutions at every stage.

Endless Experience: Few cities can rival Hong Kong’s enormous variety of sightseeing, shopping, dining, arts, and cultural attractions, which provide endless options for unique motivating and team-building activities that send their participants home in high spirits.

Proven Track Record: Hong Kong’s enviable array of attributes has inspired meeting and incentive trip organisers to return to the city time and time again, creating distinctive events that compete on an international level and leave the world talking.
