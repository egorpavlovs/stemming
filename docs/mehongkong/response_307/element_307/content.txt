                  Team Building

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

  
    
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  
    
      Unite your team-members and optimise their strengths with one of Hong Kong’s unforgettable and unique team-building activities. From Chinese cooking to lion dancing, boat racing and opera singing, invite your guests to get together and participate in group activities that encourage problem-solving, cultivate leadership, and inspire individuals to work together for common goals. You’ll send them home enthusiastic team-players.

    


	
		




        
          
              
          
          
            Chinese Cookery Classes
            Discover Chinese culinary techniques and work on building your team with some of Hong Kong’s remarkable cookery schools. 
            
              Read more about Chinese Cookery Classes
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Dragon Boating
            Paddlers and drummers must work together to keep pace and win the race – it’s all about team work!
            
              Read more about Dragon Boating
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Chinese Baking
            Teams can roll up their sleeves at Hong Kong’s famous Wing Wah bakery for insights into baking their sweet treats. 
            
              Read more about Chinese Baking
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Lion Dancing
            A testament to teamwork, lion dancers must all work to the rhythm to ensure a visual spectacle. 
            
              Read more about Lion Dancing
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Kung Fu
            Following open-air demonstrations, groups can try their own skills with the aid of kung fu masters. 
            
              Read more about Kung Fu
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Tai Chi
            Refresh the mind, body and soul with a group session of tai chi, followed by dim sum.
            
              Read more about Tai Chi
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Tea Appreciation Class
            A Tea Appreciation Class introduces the varieties of tea, its proper preparation and tea-drinking etiquette, learning the flashes of chinese culture!
            
              Read more about Tea Appreciation Class
