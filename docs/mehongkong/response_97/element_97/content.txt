                    Shining Star burning bright

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      The Star Ferry is an institution that cannot be missed. For Hong Kongers, it's a connection to their past and a symbol of their prosperity.  For MICE visitors, the Star Ferry represents a chance to get up close and personal with the world's most spectacular skyline. Besides running routes across the harbour, the Star Ferry Company runs the Shining Star, a re-creation of the timeless 1920s-era vessels, for meetings and incentives around the harbour.

With a fully equipped café, sundeck, viewing platform and audio-visual gear onboard, the Shining Star is an ideal environment to mix business meetings with pleasure. It offers a stress-free, iconic venue that creates a memorable MICE experience.

			Shining Star: 110 guests
		
		
			Opening hours:
			Daily 11:25am to 08:45pm
			Bookings required at least 14 working days in advance
