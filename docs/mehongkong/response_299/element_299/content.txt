                  Floating Venues

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                
                

    
      Give your MICE visitors a truly unique Hong Kong experience – dining on the water in one of the city’s floating eateries. Perfect for gala dinners, corporate gatherings and welcome drinks that combine mouth-watering cuisine with a spectacular backdrop, Hong Kong’s floating restaurants honour the city’s nautical heritage and serve some of the freshest seafood on the planet. From a red-sailed Chinese junk, to one of the world’s largest floating restaurants nestled in Aberdeen harbour, your visitors can dine on the high seas, in calmer coastal waters, or with sweeping views across Victoria Harbour.

    


	
		


        
          
              
          
          
            Float in Style aboard Jumbo Kingdom
            The famed floating restaurant includes conferencing and banquet facilities, cultural attractions and a cooking academy led by top chefs.
            
              Read more about Float in Style aboard Jumbo Kingdom
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Chartered Dreams on the Aqua Luna
            Possibly the last hand-crafted wooden junk to be made in Hong Kong, the Aqua
Luna represents the higher end of charter possibilities. 
            
              Read more about Chartered Dreams on the Aqua Luna
            
          
        




        
          
              
          
          
            Making Big Waves with the Bauhinia
            When grand-sized groups hit the water, the size and scale of the Harbour Cruise – Bauhinia makes it ideal for the biggest of events.
            
              Read more about Making Big Waves with the Bauhinia
            
          
        


        
          
              
          
          
            Cruise aboard the World Star
            Ideal for corporate cocktails, incentive cruise trips and social gatherings, World Star is an environmentally friendly ferry making use of the latest low emission technologies. 
            
              Read more about Cruise aboard the World Star
