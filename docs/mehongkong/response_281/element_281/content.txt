                    The Island Hopper Tour

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  

    
      
        Day 1
      
      
        
            
          Morning

Your guests will enjoy a smooth arrival at Hong Kong International Airport – ranked the world's best international airport eight times in 10 years.

Afternoon

Ocean Park’s lovable mascot Whiskers will personally welcome your visitors to one of Asia's leading theme parks and educational facilities. There, they’ll be able to enjoy thrilling rides, a stunning cable car trip, and aquatic marvels ranging from dolphins to an amazing assortment of jellyfish.

Evening

Your hungry guests will discover why Hong Kong is considered the ‘culinary capital of Asia’. With endless food options, they can dine in a luxurious hotel, a trendy restaurant, or a popular local eatery, to appreciate the skills of Hong Kong's Chinese and Western chefs.

See details here.

        
      
      
    

    
      
        Day 2
      
      
        
            
          Morning

Your visitors will start the day atop Hong Kong's most visited attraction – The Peak. They’ll reach its splendid heights via the modernised Peak Tram – an unforgettable funicular railway that has been running since 1888. The Peak Tower offers magnificent panoramic views of the city and Victoria Harbour below, and the Madame Tussauds Hong Kong wax museum displays more than 100 life-like waxworks of the rich and famous.

Alternatively, nature lovers may prefer to head to the 60-hectare Hong Kong Wetland Park, where the splendour of Hong Kong's biodiversity awaits.

Afternoon

Your guests’ chopstick skills will be put to the test with a dim sum  lunch at one of Hong Kong’s award-winning restaurants. They can then take some time out to explore this shopper’s paradise.

Alternatively, nature lovers may prefer to head to the 60-hectare Hong Kong Wetland Park, where the splendour of Hong Kong's biodiversity awaits.

Evening

A short ferry ride to a fresh seafood dinner on Lamma Island will give your visitors a dining experience they will long remember. Then, back aboard a chartered boat, they will be in for a feast of another kind. From the middle of Victoria Harbour, they’ll enjoy a front row seat to A Symphony of Lights – a magical sound and light show with its own Guinness World Record. More than 30 buildings on both sides of the harbour will dazzle your visitors’ senses with a spectacle of colour and sound.

        
      
      
    

    
      
        Day 3
      
      
        
            
          Morning

Ngong Ping 360 will present a breathtaking cable car ride over Lantau Island’s vast mountains to a themed village. There, your guests can marvel at the awesome Big Buddha – the world’s tallest outdoor seated bronze statue – as well as the spiritual Wisdom Path.

Afternoon

Your visitors will be transferred back to the nearby Hong Kong International Airport in time for their departures.
