                    Hong Kong Crowned Favourite Business City in the World for 14th Consecutive Year

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      Business Traveller China has been rewarding outstanding players from travel industry for 14 years and Hong Kong was once again crowned ‘Favourite Business City in the World’ based on votes from the highly discerning readership of Business Traveller China. This accolade marks the city’s 14th year running being recognised as the world’s top city by Business Traveller China (BTC) Awards.

It was a great honour for the city to win this title once again, which proved another year of excellence thanks to its valued partners, who have been playing a vital role in creating a business-friendly environment that caters to the preferences of Mainland Chinese business travellers. This year, Kerry Hotel Hong Kong, one of the city’s latest conference hotels, having opened in 2017, was also recognised as ’Best MICE Hotel in China‘ for the first time.

With the opening of the Hong Kong-Zhuhai-Macao Bridge and Guangzhou-Shenzhen-Hong Kong High Speed Rail (Hong Kong Section) this year, the city is even more connected and bringing a higher level of convenience to MICE visitors.
