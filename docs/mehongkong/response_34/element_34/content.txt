                    Celebrate the official launch of Indonesian President Joko Widodo’s world-first wax figure at Madame Tussauds Hong Kong

	
	
  

	
		
		
    
		
	
	
	

                  
                  
  
  
    
    
    
    
  
  
    
      
    
  


    
      The first figure in the world of Indonesian President Joko Widodo was officially unveiled on 1 May 2017 at Madame Tussauds Hong Kong. President Joko Widodo is the first world leader in history to unveil his figure in person at Madame Tussauds Hong Kong.

President Joko Widodo’s figure stands against a large LED backdrop displaying pictures of infrastructure, tourism and people in Indonesia. ‘It’s 99.9% the same!’  President Joko Widodo and First Lady Iriana Joko Widodo said about the figure.

“President Joko Widodo is a highly respected leader beloved by the public and we look forward to offering so many of our visitors the chance to get closer to one of the most influential leaders in the world.” says Jenny You, General Manager of Madame Tussauds Hong Kong.

MICE event planners may even treat their delegates to the special privilege of a two-hour event at the complimentary themed venue, under the special theme of ‘World Leaders of the Century’ or ‘A-list Stars’ with selected wax figures by participating in the ‘Hong Kong Rewards!’ 2017/18 programme.

Come to Hong Kong, visit President Joko Widodo’s wax figure and create your own themed party in the presence of ‘world leaders’!
