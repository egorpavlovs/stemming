								Convention Bureau 
								
									
										To apply to be listed on the Joburg Tourism website, you will be required to create an online profile by following the registration process for your establishment.

You will be asked to upload your logo onto the site, as well as you may be required to upload certain documents, as may be required for your type of establishment.
You will be able to create your profile and upload your documentation later on, by logging into your profile.

We will encourage you to upload photographs for your particular establishment, as well as any video footage that you may have.
Once your application has been approved by Joburg Tourism, you establishment will be published onto the Joburg Tourism website.
Please remember to update your details every 6 (six) months, otherwise your establishment will be automatically be deactivated from the Joburg Tourism website. You will be sent reminders before the time, and the day before.
If you need assistance, please do not hesitate to give us a call.
