        Bluemoon Marketing Inc.
    
        
          
        
      
        Bluemoon Marketing offers its services to clients in Tourism/IT-related industries, or to those who run global businesses. Supporting clients through web marketing and international online marketing research, we put special focus on the clients’ perspective, flexibility, and building long-lasting trust.<>・Marketing and PR support for Tourism/IT-related industries (Target area: within Japan and overseas-to-Japan)Comprehensive marketing support with a special focus on PR for Tourism/IT-related industries, as well as marketing research and PR support for companies outside Japan who are aiming to enter the Japanese market.・Marketing support for inbound tourism (Target area: Japan-to-overseas)IT-oriented marketing support including a basic omnibus study that allows one question to be asked for only 30,000 yen, and web marketing.・Marketing support with a special focus on Spain (Target area: Spain-to-Japan and visa versa)Comprehensive marketing support between Spain and Japan based on web marketing, utilizing a wide-ranging network in Spain.
