        OZAWA SHUZO CO., LTD.
    
      
        Sawanoi has more than 300 years of history of making sake. Free guided tours of the brewery are held four times a day.Sawanoi Garden has a shop, a tasting room and restaurants.Two museums are located near Sawanoi.You can enjoy various kinds of delicious sake, Japanese food, and traditional Japanese culture during your visit.
