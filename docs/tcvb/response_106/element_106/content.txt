        TOKYO JOYPOLIS
    
        
          
        
      
        Tokyo Joy Polis is the biggest indoor theme park in Japan. You can enjoy yourself even if it rains!There are over 20 types of attractions such as a roller coaster or the latest VR technology!Also you will see collaboration events with popular Japanese anime characters and artists.There are exclusive contents such as special shows or original goods waiting for you!We have a cafe restaurant with a full view of the Rainbow Bridge as well. You can have parties too. Please feel free to contact us!
