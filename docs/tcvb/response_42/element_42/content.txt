        HOTEL GRAND PALACE
    
      
        Situated just center of Tokyo overlooking the Imperial Palace. 1 minute walk to Kudanshita Subway Station that is the junction of 3 subway lines. Convenient for every purpose of travelers. Direct bus service is available between hotel and 2 airports, Narita International Airport and Haneda Airport. Every year half of our guests are people from foreign country.
