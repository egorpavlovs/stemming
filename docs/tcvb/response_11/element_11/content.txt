 Business events programs

       


As a city with global appeal, we work to promote opportunities for exchange by hosting numerous international business events and attracting attendees from around the world. Through hosting such events in Tokyo, we aim to boost the city’s image and stimulate significant economic ripple effects. In pursuit of these goals, we offer proactive support at every stage of attracting and hosting business events. Our services also include assistance with facility improvements in order to ensure a visitor-friendly infrastructure.
