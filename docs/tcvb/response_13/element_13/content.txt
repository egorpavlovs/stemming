 Tourism infrastructure programs

       


We partner with travel companies and organizations (tourism organizations, chambers of commerce, and industry groups) across the city to accelerate local tourism promotion initiatives in order to enhance Tokyo’s appeal and establish the city as a tourist destination that visitors will want to return again and again.



Providing tourism infrastructure support
We offer support to the hotels, restaurants and other related companies to accommodate various infrastructure needs.
Support examples

Inbound support
Support for Barrier free accommodations and tourist buses
Support for security and safety of accommodations
Accessible tourism support
Multilingual museums and art galleries support

Digital signage
Placing the multilingual digital signage in Tokyo for overseas tourists.

Support for municipalities
We support municipalities to improve various infrastructures for the tourists, including the multilingual tourist information, signboards, and free Wi-Fi networks.
