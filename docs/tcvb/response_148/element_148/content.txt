        Creative Minds LLC.,
    
      
        Creative Minds　LLC., is the producer of "Fly With Yaya" a series of content released over You Tube, Facebook, Instagram that introduces Japan in a totally new angle. The program has Yaya, one of the main characters of BOBOIBOY(the best rating animation in Malaysia and Indonesia)introducing to kids and family Japan. Topics range from interesting spots, back stories of the venue or a culture, eye opener for foreigners outside of Japan.One of the highlights of the program is also the fact that it will support muslim travelers with additional information on food, prayer rooms etc...First episode has launched on Nov. 23, 2018 on Monsta Channel (see below link)https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vD_fb6lLRlw&t=46sIt has reached over 550,000 views as of Dec. 27, 2018.2nd Episode has launched on Dec. 18, 2018https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Zv0Z06FuYkQ&t=10sViewership has exceeded 220,000 in a week.We look forward to working with companies looking for inbound visitors from South East Asian countries, and muslim countries. We also are flexible with working with companies, organizations willing to go into Malaysia !
