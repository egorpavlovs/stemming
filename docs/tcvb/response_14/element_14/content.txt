 Community tourism programs

       


We partner with travel companies and organizations (tourism organizations, chambers of commerce, and industry groups) across the city to accelerate local tourism promotion initiatives in order to enhance Tokyo’s appeal and establish the city as a tourist destination that visitors will want to return again and again.



Collaboration with local tourism organizations and other partners.
We work with tourism promotion organizations across the city and partner with businesses that have expertise in tourism-related fields. Our support services cover a broad range of initiatives, from the development of tourist attractions to tourism promotion campaigns.
Providing support for tourism promotion organizations
We support tourism promotion organizations to achieve their full potential to implement regionally led, independent campaigns.
Producing new tourism attractions
With the goal of attracting more repeat tourists to the city, we help develop new tourist attractions and launch various projects to attract people to the Tama area and the Tokyo Islands.
Official Tokyo city guide certification test
Tokyo Location Box
We support on-location shoots for films and TV dramas in Tokyo.
