                Sport & Wellness

                There are numerous sports possibilities in the city: hiking in the Vienna Woods, jogging in the Prater, inline skating on Danube Island or maybe you would prefer to go for a swim in a historic pool? You can explore the entire city during the Vienna City Marathon or by bicycle. Parks and green spaces make up more than half of the metropolitan area, offering plenty of opportunities for recreation.
