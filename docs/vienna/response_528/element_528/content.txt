                Green spaces and recreation areas

                There's so much green … Statistically, there are almost 100 square meters of green space for each of Vienna's 1.8 million inhabitants. Or: Roughly half of the urban area consists of parks, forests or agricultural land. Making Vienna one of the greenest cities of over a million inhabitants in the world!
