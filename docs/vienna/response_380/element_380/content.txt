        Austropa and Mondial obtain Ethical MedTech certification

        

        April 2018 - Austropa Interconvention and Mondial Congress & Events are the first Austrian PCO agencies to be certified as Trusted Partners by MedTech Europe.

        As of now it is possible for Professional Congress Organisers (PCOs) to get certified as MedTech Europe Trusted Partner. Vienna-based agencies Mondial Congress & Events and Austropa Interconvention are the first Austrian PCOs to obtain the certification. With the MedTech Ethical Charter, a platform was created to foster ethical and compliance initiatives in the medical technology industry. Since January 1st 2018, the initiative serves to ensure transparency and compliance of medical and scientific congresses with the self-imposed regulations of the industry. This also results in new demands on the interaction between the MedTech industry and medical professionals as well as healthcare organisations.In order to be certified as MedTech Trusted Partner, staff of the two freshly certified agencies had to undergo a stringent examination process. As a result, the two specialist teams are perfectly familiar with the content of the Ethical Charter and dispose of all the necessary expertise to advice organisers in matters relating to destination, venue and networking location selection, and many other critical aspects.For further information please visit www.austropa-interconvention.at and www.mondial-congress.comSponsor newsÂ Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â Â
