        Austrian Airlines

        

        Austrian Airlines is Austria's largest carrier and offers a global route network of round 130 destinations. In Central and Eastern Europe, the route network is particularly dense: With 37 destinations Austrian Airlines is the market leader throughout the region.

        Thanks to its favourable geographical location at the heart of  Europe, the company's hub at Vienna International Airport is the ideal  gateway between East and West. Austrian Airlines is part of the  Lufthansa Group, Europe's largest airline group, and a member of the  Star Alliance, the first global alliance of international airlines.Member of Star Alliance The world's biggest alliance 28 member airlines Over 21,900 flights per day Over 1,328 destinations in more than 195 countries worldwideOver 1,000 lounges worldwideNetworkAustrian Airlines carried nearly 10.8 million passengers in 2015 in total.Austrian Airlines operate a fleet of 81 aircraft.Austrian Airlines offers with more than 100 destinations the biggest European network.Austrian Airlines operate numerous flights to the Middle East and give access to the key economic centers of these regions.Austrian Airlines offer flights to 7 destinations in North America and to 4 destinations in AsiaAustrian  Airlines offers a comfortable long haul flight experience as well as  smooth nonstop flights to the Star Alliance hubs in Asia and the  Americas. Austrian Airlines offers also extensive "interline agreements"  with Star Alliance partners to enable easy connections.The  Austrian hub Vienna features a geographically ideal position for  passengers travelling to Europe. With a "minimum connecting time" of 25  minutes only, passengers move on to their connecting flights easily and  very comfortably.ServiceAs network carrier the airline group offers high service quality, for which it has been awarded with many international prices.Austrian Airlines represents an exceptional high service level in business and economy class.Personally caringDo&Co Catering on board of all flightsHigh comfortAustrian charm and hospitalitywww.austrian.comOfficial CarrierAustrian  Airlines is your logical choice for international congresses and  meetings in Vienna and offers you support as "Official Carrier" for your  event.www.austrian.com/meetingsFor further information please contact: conventions@austrian.com
