        Take the chance to use the web for free at numerous places across the city with your WiFi-enabled Tablet PC, mobile or notebook.

        WiFi locations in Vienna Use around 400 free WiFi hotspots (40 in the 1st district) across the city, e. g.: on City Hall Square, on Stephansplatz, in the MuseumsQuartierat Naschmarkt, in the Prater and on Danube Islandin train stations and many subway stations The interactive city map of Vienna shows you all WiFi hotspots in the city - Map of Vienna (under Map content, menu Communication & Business/WLAN). You also have free WiFi access in the tourist information office on Albertinaplatz (9.00 am - 7.00      pm daily)in the wienXtra-jugendinfoin numerous food outlets: www.freewave.at (German and English) FLINK - unlimited surfing with a personal hotspot.
