                Sustainable Vienna

                If you’re looking to have a healthy and sustainable holiday, then Vienna is the place to come. Everything can be had here, from ecological body care to fair fashion to natural food shops. What’s more, the city of Vienna is one of the largest organic farmers in Austria. Over 860 hectares of organic growing land are cultivated on three city farms.
