        Detailed information
      
      
        
          
          
            Category: Contemporary locations
          

          

          
        
        
          „Little Shabby, Mostly Chic". This is the flair of the new location close to the Vienna Central Station. 120 years old Gösserhalle spans over 5.000 m² of event-space. The rooms can be individually used from 200 to 1.300 m² and offer you a place for different events, experience and culture.
          
          
            
              Daylight in meeting area
            
              Outdoor area available
            
              WiFi free of charge
            
              Free choice of caterer
