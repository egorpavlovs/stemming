                Incentive Ideas & Social Events Programmes

                Even outside the meeting room Vienna offers countless ways to network or to increase the motivation of participants. 

                Are you interested in one of the ideas below? Please contact one of the agencies in Vienna who will be pleased to assist you in planning and organizing your programme.

    Viennese originals

      







  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                The capital of music 
              

              
                Welcome to the world’s music capital! More famous composers have lived here than in any other city – in Vienna, music is literally in the air.
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                History and Tall Tales
              

              
                Fun and thought-provoking programs brimming with Viennese charm and amusing historical anecdotes. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Ballet of the White Stallions
              

              
                The Lipizzaner stallions at the Spanish Riding School, one of the oldest in the world, are the perfect embodiment of the fine art of classic …
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Celebrate like an Emperor
              

              
                Opulent city palaces, museums and historic locations provide the perfect setting for out-of-the-ordinary evening events and costume balls. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Cocktail on top
              

              
                The Giant Ferris Wheel in the Prater is one of the city's symbols. Almost 65 meters tall, it offers a breathtaking view of the city on the Danube.
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

    
    

    
    



  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Animal magic 
              

              
                Schönbrunn Zoo is the world's oldest zoo – and it is certainly one of the most picturesque. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Third Man Tour
              

              
                Tour of Vienna's labyrinthine sewers. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Empress for an evening 
              

              
                Show your participants Schönbrunn Palace in an exclusive, intimate soirée – as part of a private tour outside normal opening hours. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                In perfect time
              

              
                No visit to Vienna would be complete without dancing the waltz. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  






  





  

    Sports activities

      







  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Puzzling Vienna
              

              
                A treasure hunt through the old town is an unforgettable way to discover the city, the people and the Viennese dialect. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Viennese wilderness
              

              
                Thrillseekers will be in their element in the Vienna Woods, on the Danube Island and in the extensive Prater park, where outdoor pursuits are very much …
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                 The wind is picking up 
              

              
                Many people dream of flying. This dream can come true in Vienna!
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Rollercoaster fun
              

              
                Events at the Prater amusement park.
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Dragon boat racing
              

              
                Bring your team closer together with one of the most successful team building exercises out there. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

    
    

    
    



  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Marina magic in the heart of Vienna
              

              
                And plenty of action for adrenaline junkies.
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  






  





  

    Culinary activities

      







  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                The Viennese Heurige
              

              
                The epitome of Viennese enjoyment: an informal evening at a typical Heurige wine tavern.
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                The art of Apple Strudel baking
              

              
                It is time to pick up some tips from a master of the art!
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                The art of cake making
              

              
                For more than two centuries Demel has been famous for its fine confectionery and baked goods.
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Cook like the pros
              

              
                Did you know that Viennese Cuisine is the only cuisine in the world to be named after a city? 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Cheers
              

              
                Wine, sparkling wine and gin tasting.
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

    
    

    
    



  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Beer brewing workshop
              

              
                Key in on the high art of craft beer brewing and discover skills you didn't know you had. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  






  





  

    Artistic activities

      







  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Artistic Pursuits
              

              
                Let your imagination run free at an art workshop in one of Vienna's museums. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Art out of a can
              

              
                Vienna is not only the home of classical art – the city is also a place where you can try your hand at modern graffiti. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Polaroid Tour of Vienna
              

              
                Explore Vienna through the lens of a Polaroid camera on an Instant Tour.  
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

    
    

    
    



  






  





  

    The journey is the reward

      







  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Danube so blue
              

              
                Enjoy a trip along the blue Danube to the Wachau Valley. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Time travel 
              

              
                The Majestic Imperator Train de Luxe is the epitome of imperial opulence from the golden age of railways. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                On tour
              

              
                The journey back in time starts right here!
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Motorized soapbox rally through Vienna
              

              
                These 14 horsepower soapbox cars are a great way to explore Vienna. 
              
            
          
      
    

  

  
  

      

  


  

    
    

    
    

    
    
      
          
             
            
              
              
            

            
              
                Vienna Sight Running
              

              
                Discover Vienna and its sights at speed, on your own two feet.

                The high-speed catamaran whisks guests from Vienna to Bratislava in just 75 minutes.
