	[InterContinental Seoul]Presents the ‘Winter Choice Package,’ where guests may select options 

	Writer : Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas		 / Date : 2015/1/21 13:27	

	

						
								
								
		Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas &
InterContinental Seoul COEX

Presents the ‘Winter Choice Package,’ where guests may
select options to fit their preference

 

- Under
the theme, ‘Winter in the City,’ guests may relax in the city with a selective
package

- Enjoy a comfortable room, hotel
restaurant, premium movie theater, aquarium and more in one package

   

   The
Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas (www.grandicparnas.com) and InterContinental Seoul COEX (www.iccoex.com), located in Samsung-dong, will be presenting for the
first time a package with different options that guests may choose that fit
their preferences starting from November 21st until March 3rd,
2015.  The Winter Choice Package are for
guests wanting to relax at a 5-star hotel in the middle of the city during the
winter. 

 

   With
the theme, “Winter in the City,’ guests may take comfort during this freezing
winter by choosing among the options provided on how they would like to relax
in the city.  The biggest feature of the
Winter Choice Package are structured into two types, Gourmet and Comfort.

 

   For guests
whom prefer to experience a luxurious relaxation, the Gourmet package will
provide a comfortable night at either hotel. 
But also a delicious feast at the hotel’s main restaurant and enjoy the
night out watching a movie or tour the aquarium all in the same complex.  Exclusively for this package the 4 main
restaurants’ (Table 34, Hakone, Sky Lounge and Asian Live) top chefs has meticulously
created a course menu and package customers will be provided with couple set
menu for lunch and dinner.  For breakfast, guests may choose between the Grand
Kitchen and Brasserie.  Guests
may select the style of food at their preferred time and at the restaurant they
would like to dine in.  All dining
facilities are available with prior reservation during their stay.  Located in the COEX complex,
guests will receive two movie tickets to the Megabox COEX’s premium boutique M
Comfort Theater (2 coffees at the Megabox Boutique open-up bar included) or two
entrance tickets to the COEX Aquarium, where you may experience nature’s
mysterious sea life.

 

   The
comfort type package provides a comfortable night’s rest at the hotel guestroom
and receive two movie tickets to the Megabox COEX’s premium boutique M Comfort
Theater (2 coffees at the Megabox Boutique open-up bar included) or two
entrance tickets to the COEX Aquarium.

 

Both type of the package will receive a 20% discount to
both hotel restaurants, and a coupon book to the premium casual shopping mall, Parnas Mall and for quests staying longer than two
nights will receive an additional special discount pass to ski at the Gangchon
Elysian.  For guests accompanied with
children will receive a limited edition I-Bear collaborated between the Teddy
Bear Museum and InterContinental Seoul. 
At prior request, the guestroom will prepare a gown and slippers for
children, so the whole family may relax and enjoy their stay together.  Lastly, guests may enjoy an additional 20,000
won discount to future package purchases.

Especially, guests whom select
the club rooms will be able to select between tumblers (valued at KRW 35,000)
prepared by each hotel or Moroccan oil to protect your hair during the
winter.  The club will also benefit from
2 pm late check-out and free use of the club lounge, fitness club, swimming
pool and sauna.

 

   Hotel official that planned this package
commented “We are presenting this selective package to satisfy all guests that
may want to experience a luxurious retreat in the comfort of a 5-star hotel
during the cold winter weather or to spend a comforting and rich winter
vacation in the heart of the city,” “We are planning to constantly create
various packages that will consider guests’ preferences.”

 

The Grand InterContinental Seoul
Parnas Deluxe Gourmet type will cost beginning at 295,000 KRW and Comfort type
will be begin at 225,000 KRW. 
InterContinental Seoul COEX Superior Gourmet type will cost beginning at
285,000 KRW and Comfort type at 215,000 KRW (tax and VAT not included /
Standard: 2 people, 1 room, 1 night).
