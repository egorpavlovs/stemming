From transfers & accommodation to corporate events and activities, we provide the most complete service: venue selection, catering, cultural performances, entertainment, leisure, and booth construction. KR Travel helps you create the maximum out of your stay in South Korea!
Conventions, Exhibitions and Events
We are an experienced partner during medical congresses, sports events (Worldcup Football, Formule 1 Racing and Special Olympics) and official delegations (trade, economic & government missions). We work together with world class booth suppliers offering our clients a complete booth package: design, construction & dismantling.
Accommodation, transfers & general sessions
We help you select the proper accommodation that meets your specific needs. Meeting rooms for general or breakout sessions can easily be arranged via us. We provide all kind of transfers: private bus, train, boat and domestic flights. For VIP customers we provide special VIP airport assistance, VIP cars and helicopter transfers.
Special (evening) events in unique venues
KR Travel arranges cocktail parties, welcoming receptions, gala dinners etc. Depending on your need we can select unique venues for small to large scale events. Transfers, F&B and a full entertainment program (performances, DJ, MC) can easily be arranged.
Incentives & leisure programs
KR Travel organizes unique incentives and team building programs for young, old, active or less active people. We bring creative ideas into practice!
