        Dugahun, located within the grounds of Gallery Hyundai on Samcheong-dong 
Road, means ‘a very beautiful house’ and is an ideal venue for high-concept 
cultural experiences. At the restaurant, diners can enjoy the tastes and traditions 
of the West through a selection of wines in its modern interior, which resembles 
an early 20th Century traditional Korean house.In addition, nearby Gallery Dugahun, 
located in the adjacent Russian style brick building, exhibits an array of art 
pieces from both East and West, adding to the stylish ambience.
