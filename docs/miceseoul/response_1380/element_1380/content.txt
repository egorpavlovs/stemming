		Seoul Mayor Unveils Masterplan (VIDEO) – International Meetings Review (December 2013)
		2013-12-18
		
			
			
Seoul hosted the recent UFI Congress at the Coex Convention & Exhibition Centre, where the great and good of the international exhibition organizer community built up its awareness of the opportunities emerging from the Asia Pacific region. But while this enormously potent audience was developing its knowledge, the Mayor of the Korean capital, Park Won Soon, took the opportunity to unveil a Masterplan that committed funds and infrastructure support for servicing other large meetings markets in economic sectors of excellence including the industry's Holy Grail: the associations sector.
