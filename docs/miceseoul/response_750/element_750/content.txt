	Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas & InterContinental Seoul COEX Presents the EXO Hotel Package

	Writer : Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas		 / Date : 2015/11/06 17:24	

	

														
		Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas, located in
Samseong-dong and InterContinental Seoul COEX, in collaboration with SMTOWN
TRAVEL, will be presenting the ‘EXO Hotel Package’ featuring K-pop Group
EXO-themed rooms and other related promotions, up to the end of this year.

The special package has been designed specifically
for families and friends to an EXO-themed photoshoot at the COEX’ SMTown studio
(SMTOWN@coesartium). Other special perks include the limited edition items
placed in the hotel rooms, two special MD (Merchandise) items, over 10 kinds of
amenities, and various discount deals to be enjoyed at SMTown. 

Complementary gifts for fans include the EXO
cushion, mini notepad, slippers, towel, passport holder, and a disposable bag
to carry the items. Along with these basic gifts, tickets for two to enter the
SMTown studio, 50% off the SMTown theater, and 20% off at the SMTown library
cafe will also be provided. An EXO decorated cake will be delivered via room
service on the check-in day, and the wifi, fitness club, swimming pool, and
various hotel breakfast discounts (Grand Kitchen & The Brasserie W30,250 per
person) will all be offered as part of the package as well.

The limited EXO-themed room package is offered
at W295,000 for the superior room, W315,000 for the deluxe room, and W427,000
for the junior suite room (for 2 persons; taxes not included). 

 

For reservations, please contact 02-559-7777
