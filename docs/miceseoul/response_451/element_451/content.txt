			Introduction
Seoul’s leading city resort featuring a city hotel, Banyan Tree Club & Spa Seoul is located an hour’s drive from Incheon International Airport and just 10 minutes from downtown Seoul. The hotel provides a special experience by providing relaxation pools in every unit, and caters to guest privacy with a maximum four rooms per floor. Our Crystal Ballroom, with its stunning display of over 20,000 Swarovski crystals, provides a beautiful setting for wedding, business seminars and banquets.

Special Features

This luxurious hall is host to international conferences, high-end fashion shows, brand launch parties, car showcases, and a dynamic range of significant meetings.▪ Boardroom: Our boardrooms will assist your business meetings from set-ups to wrap-ups with high-tech systems.
