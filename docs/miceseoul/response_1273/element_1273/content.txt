		Etiquette & Business in Korea
		2012-08-23
		
			
			 
Seoul Convention Bureau Content Editor Ben Jordan discusses doing business in Korea with British meetings magazine Conference & Meetings World (C-MW)
 
"Korean ways of doing business and socializing - which very much go hand in hand – may seem a little daunting at first. Faced with the many cultural differences between them, the foreign businessperson might initially feel unsure as to whether or not they have made progress with their Korean hosts. However, it’s just a matter of correctly reading the signals, and once introductions have been made and the first attempts to break the ice succeeded, the outsider will find Koreans to be a very warm and loyal people always looking for ways to make their guest feel part of the group."
