		PLUS SEOUL

					Seoul’s many benefits, all in one package. Add Seoul to your meeting! 
					
					
						The PLUS SEOUL Package consists of MICE support from the Seoul Metropolitan Government and Seoul MICE Alliance benefits for your event, all in one go. 
Don’t miss out on travel and unique venue benefits for MICE events in Seoul! 

						
						
						
			
			PLUS SEOUL leaflet PDF Download
		
		
		
				
				
				
				
				When to Apply 
			
      Applications must be submitted at least 1 month before the event begins. For future MICE events, listed benefits are available until December 31st, 2018. 
		
		
		How to Use 
			
      Benefits can be accessed through discount codes or by presenting MICE event organizers' and participants' name tags, PLUS SEOUL leaflets, printed materials from website.
