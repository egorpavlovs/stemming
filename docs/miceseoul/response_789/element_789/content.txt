	[Conrad Seoul] Early bird benefits for Year-end meetings

	Writer : Conrad Seoul		 / Date : 2014/10/21 10:23 / Modified Date 2014/10/21 10:24	

	

						
												
		Conrad Seoul offers Early bird benefits for Year-end meetings-	Book before November 30 and earn Triple HHonors points as an early bird bonus! -	Receive 1 complimentary buffet for every 10 persons at ZEST-	Explore ATRIO & 37GRILL, for dramatic venues to host your private events. SEOUL, Korea – October 21, 2014 – Conrad Seoul offers the ideal venue and customized service to meet whatever your year-end parties may require. Whether your events are for clients, employees, families or any other social occasion, Conrad Seoul offers a range of options with early bird benefits for customers who make their reservations by 30 November. Banquets for unique and elegant eventsLooking for high-tech facilities and luxurious style? A banquet at Conrad Seoul is the ideal place for differentiating your year-end event from all others.  As a thank you, Conrad is offering all event organizers a special opportunity to earn triple HHonors points, opening up a world of special benefits at Hilton Hotels worldwide, when making banquet reservations by November 30(Only apply to events held by December 30). A Conrad Seoul banquet can accommodate from 30 up to 600 guests, with prices per person starting from KRW85,000won, including tax.For the love of diningConrad Seoul suggests ZEST, ATRIO and 37 Grill for events that call for dramatic venues with a memorable dining experience, personally prepared by our top chefs. Any of these theme restaurants are available for either exclusive or partial usage, to make your event special and exclusive. Zest Buffet Dining offers an extravagant selection of international delicacies along with 1 complimentary meal for every 10th person. It has both private dining rooms and larger venues which are ideal for gatherings between 10 to 100 guests, for either lunch or dinner. Prices start at KRW80,000 per person, including tax.Atrio features authentic Italian dining as you have never experienced before. Its stylish décor and 28-meter ceilings are perfect for your private event for up to 100 guests. You are free to choose from a special menu prepared by our Italian chef or an extensive buffet menu. The price starts from KRW85,000 per person, including tax.37 Grill, the signature restaurant of Conrad Seoul, offers the ultimate grill experience-with breathtaking views to match. 37 Grill is perched high atop the 37th floor, with panoramic, 180-degree city and Han-river views, ideal for a private VIP event from 100-200 guests. The price per person, including signature cocktails, starts from KRW125,000, including tax. Conrad Seoul, is celebrating its second anniversary of its opening on November 12, offering a Smart Luxury experience defined as the “Luxury of Being Yourself”. Conrad Seoul is the closest luxury hotel in Seoul to both Incheon and Gimpo International Airports and conveniently attached to Yeouido station, the transfer station of lines 5∙9, and a 5 minute walk from the Yeouido Transit Center  For more information, please visit www.conradseoul.com(English language) or www.conradseoul.co.kr (Korean language). Reservation or inquiries 02-6137-7000
