								KAAS, composed of some of the nation’s top cosmetic surgeons, annually hosts the Asia Forum for Aesthetic Surgery & Medicine (AFAS). The event, organized by KAAS and sponsored by related academies from other countries, represents Asia in the field of aesthetic surgery and takes place in Seoul during the spring. It features world-class academic speakers and a platform for knowledge-sharing between experts from across the globe. In conjunction with the forum, AFAS also includes a medical equipment exhibition showcasing the latest equipment in the field.
