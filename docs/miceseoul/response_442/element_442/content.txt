		Introduction
This hotel is close to business districts in Gangnam such as Coex, Samsungdong and Apgujeong-dong. It offers exquisite customized services with 482 rooms, a business center, fitness center and medical clinic. Since a beautiful lake and the world's largest indoor theme park, Lotte World Adventure, are nearby, it is a perfect destination for family tourists.

Special Features
Lotte Hotel Seoul World is located nest to a mart, arcade, department store and duty-free shop allowing you to enjoy shopping and a variety of tasty food at good prices. This hotel also has easy access to public transportation. You can participate in diverse Coex events anytime. If necessary, a shuttle bus can be provided.
