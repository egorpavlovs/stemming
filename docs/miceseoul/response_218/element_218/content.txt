								Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel is located
at the southwestern part of Seoul, with convenient access to public
transportations including Sindorim subway station line number 1 and 2,
Yeongdeungpo train station and more. The hotel is connected to the D-Cube City
multi-complex building which offers one-stop service for shopping, culture and
leisure.
Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel accommodates 269 rooms, including 12
beautifully appointed suites. One of its most highlighted features is the 41st
floor lobby, where the floor-to-ceiling glass windows display panoramic views
of Seoul. The hotel features a signature restaurant ‘Feast’ offering
international buffet and ‘Lobby Lounge. Bar’ offering signature cocktails and
wines which you can enjoy with the nightscape. Also, the hotel is equipped with
a luxurious spa and the Sheraton Fitness with a wide gym, indoor
swimming pool, screen golf, sauna, GX room and more.
Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel offers 10 meetings and event spaces, including
the 900sqm Grand Ballroom, which can accommodate up to 500 guests in banquet
setup and 1,200 guests in theater style. Also, other facilities such as VIP
room and coat room is ready for events such as business seminars, conferences,
launching shows and more and small to medium sized business meetings for 20 to
150 guests can be arranged at the meeting rooms located on the 7th and 8th
floor.
