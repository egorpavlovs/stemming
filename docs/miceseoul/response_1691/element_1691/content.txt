			Introduction
Grand Ambassador Seoul associated with Pullman is centrally located in Seoul and has easy access to major shopping, entertainment, and business centers such as City Hall, Gwanghwamun, Dongdaemun, Myeongdong and Namsan. The 5-star cosmopolitan luxury hotel offers comfort stay with 413 rooms, 5 restaurants and a bar including Michelin recommended Chinese restaurant and indoor swimming pool. The large-scale Grand Ballroom and versatile meeting rooms will be the ideal choice for successful business events. Free shuttle bus touring around the city's attractions and the nearest subway station make the hotel even more perfect for business and leisure travelers
