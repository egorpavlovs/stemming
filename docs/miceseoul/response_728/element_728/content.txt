MILLENNIUM SEOUL HILTON NEWS RELEASE

  

 

WINTER PARADISE AT MILLENNIUM SEOUL HILTON

 

3 December 2015, Seoul, Korea… Millennium Seoul Hilton
is offering Seoul urbanites a unique opportunity to enjoy a winter getaway
without getting away from the city.  Guests
can choose from several well-priced room packages – perfect for those seeking a
little winter rest and relaxation. 

 

The packages are available from December 1,
2015 to February 29, 2016.  Please note
that only our special Christmas and New Years packages will be available on
December 24th, 25th and the 31st. All packages
will include complimentary internet access, two complimentary bottles of water,
complimentary access to the Fitness Center and swimming pool, and complimentary
weekday Aerobics classes. In addition, Millennium Seoul Hilton is set up with a
breathtaking Christmas tree, manger scene and Christmas Alpine Village with
running trains!  The display will be up
until January 17, 2016.  Don’t forget to
enjoy this holiday display while you are here!
