	InterContinental Seoul COEX, "A Delicious Getaway" Tasty Weekend Package

	Writer : InterContinental Seoul COEX		 / Date : 2015/10/08 17:41	

	

						
												
		We have set aside a very special package exclusively for those staying with us on the weekends or during holidays at the Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas and InterContinental Seoul COEX, located in the heart of Seoul’s Samseong-dong. Now through November 29th, along with your stay you will enjoy a couple brunch set menu at one of our two signature restaurants; French restaurant “Table 34” or Italian restaurant “Sky Lounge.”With this package you not only get a comfortable overnight stay in a 5-star hotel in the heart of the city, but you also partake in brunch for two prepared by the chefs of our finest restaurants, the Sky Lounge and Table 34 - along with other benefits.Guest staying at the Grand InterContinental Hotel get to enjoy brunch at Table 34, located on the 34th floor of the hotel, while guests staying at the InterContinental Seoul COEX will enjoy brunch at the Sky Lounge on the 30th floor. Table 34 offers exquisite contemporary French cuisine while the modern Italian restaurant Sky Lounge offers spectacular views of the Han River to go with the elegant dining. ‘Tasty Weekend Package’ customers who stay on the club floor will receive complimentary breakfast at the Club Lounge and Club Lounge benefits with food and beverages free of charge. In addition, guests can request a later check out time (until 2:00 pm) and will have access to the sauna, golf center, and fitness recreation center during their stay. Free wifi will also be provided. At the Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas, deluxe rooms for two will be available from W350,000. Pricing for a superior room for two at the InterContinental Seoul COEX starts at W330,000. Taxes not included. For more information and reservations for the Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas, please call 02-559-7777. For more information and reservations for the InterContinental Seoul COEX, please call 02-3430-8888.
