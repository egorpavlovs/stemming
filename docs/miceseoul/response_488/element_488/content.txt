		Introduction
The Westin Chosun Seoul is a premier five-star luxury hotel located conveniently in the heart of Seoul's vibrant financial and shopping districts. Distinguished by world-class facilities and exceptionally personalized services, The Westin Chosun Seoul has been chosen as the "Best Business Hotel in Seoul" by FinanceAsia. Ideally situated in the heart of downtown Seoul, The Westin Chosun Seoul is walking distance to the headquarters of global companies, embassies and the historic landmarks such as Deoksugung palace and Gyeongbokgung palace, Seoul City Hall, Cheonggye Stream and Gwanghwamun Square.

Special Features
The Westin Chosun Seoul is the first and only hotel in Korea that offers Airport One Stop Service. This exclusive service feature lets guests bypass the check-in process at the hotel front desk and go directly totheir hotel room upon arrival at the hotel. PTOC(Personal Touch of Chosun) is a VIP-only service which provides personalized amenities with the guest's name. And Leave Your Belongings lets guests leave their personal belongings with the hotel until they come back again to stay at the hotel. Guests' personal belongings will be waiting in their room when they return to the hotel.
