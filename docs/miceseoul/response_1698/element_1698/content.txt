			Introduction
As its name suggests, the Novotel Ambassador Seoul Gangnam is located in the heart of Seoul's most famous district, where can also be found the massive World Trade Center complex, the Coex convention and exhibition center, and a wealth of leisure and entertainment facilities. Guests can easily ride between the hotel and Incheon International Airport via the KAL limousine bus and travel through Seoul via the nearby Seoul subway network. On-site, the Novotel's 332 guest rooms – including suites and premier executive floor rooms – are complemented by flexible meetings, conference and event spaces.
