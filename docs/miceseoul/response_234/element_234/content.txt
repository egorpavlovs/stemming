								Strategically situated in the heart of Gangnam, where business, shopping and entertainment flourish, Sheraton Seoul Palace Gangnam is an urban oasis for discerning business travelers. The hotel is located near government offices, international convention centers including COEX and SETEC, and more. Nearby 3 subway lines and Express bus terminal, put all of the country’s business and historical highlights within easy reach.

With its convenient location, Sheraton Seoul Palace Gangnam is ideal for holding intimate board meetings, seminars, and conferences with 9 banquet halls including 2 specious ballrooms that are fully equipped with the latest technology. 

Guests can recharge in the hotel’s 341 rooms including 22 suites all with amendments such as comfortable beddings and high-speed Internet access. Meanwhile, the club lounge offers attentive service for smart business guests. The hotel also provide an ultimate relaxation in a fitness center, indoor pool and spa, featuring the latest equipment to help guests fully rejuvenate and keep up with their fitness regime.
For the dining, indulge in gastronomic pleasures at our acclaimed 4 on-site restaurants or to explore unique and vibrant dining destinations nearby the hotel such as Seorae Village and Itawon.
