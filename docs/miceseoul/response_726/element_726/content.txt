	Imperial Palace Seoul Christmas Package

	Writer : Imperial Palace Seoul		 / Date : 2015/12/02 10:52	

	

						
												
		If you dream of a romantic Christmas:
Imperial Palace Seoul Christmas Package
 
- Three types of Christmas packages for friends, lovers, and families
 
Imperial Palace Seoul presents a Christmas package for anyone to have a special day with loved ones this Christmas season.  The lobby exhibits a large-sized gingerbread house made of cookies, Christmas tree, and snowman, and some of the employees will be dressed up as Santas to enhance the Christmas mood.  
 
Imperial Palace Seoul‘s Christmas package comes in three types for friends, lovers, and families.  The Christmas Night-out Package invites you to have a comfortable rest in the guest room and a party.  It includes one night in a cozy guest room, a cheese platter and a bottle of wine, and a surprise gift with Pico Blanco’s party supplies.  Christmas Night-out Package is available from December 24 to December 26 and the rates start at KRW 295,000 (not including taxes and service charges).
 
For a romantic time with your lover, there is the Romantic Eve Night Package.  You can spend a night in the guest room and receive two tickets for the Christmas party at the Lobby Lounge Delmar (KRW 200,000 in value) and the party supplies by Pico Blanco.  The Christmas party at the Lobby Lounge Delmar serves unlimited wine with a steak course for a romantic dinner and is filled with various events to see and enjoy such as the live carol concert and gift lottery.  The Romantic Eve Night Package is available on December 24 and the rates start at KRW 370,000 (not including taxes and service charges). 
 
The Family Holiday Package is for the entire family to enjoy together.  It includes one night in a cozy guest room, breakfasts for a family of 3 at Café Amiga, a Christmas mini cake, and 20% discounts to Kids’ Palace Christmas Party.  Kids’ Palace Christmas Party includes a special Christmas buffet and the kids’ play zone with various programs for children such as cake baking class, Christmas tree making, and card making, and it will take place at the Dubhe Hall and Selena Hall.  The Family Holiday Package will be available on December 24 and the rates start at KRW 312,000 (not including taxes and service charges). 
 
 This Christmas, make unforgettable memories with Imperial Palace Seoul’ various Christmas packages. For reservation and inquiries: 02.3440.8000 / www.imperialpalace.co.kr
