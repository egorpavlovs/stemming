SPOTLIGHT ON SRI LANKA AT CAFÉ 395 

 

29
January 2016, Seoul, Korea… Millennium Seoul Hilton’s flagship buffet
and all-day-dining restaurant, Café 395, will feature the fiery and exciting
cuisine of Sri Lanka in honor of Sri Lanka’s National Day celebrated on
February 4th.

 

In cooperation with the Sri Lankan Embassy
in Seoul, the special food festival will take place from January 28th
to February 7th and will be in addition to Cafe 395’s bountiful
buffet offerings. Cafe 395 will welcome guest chefs from the Colombo Hilton in Sri
Lanka who will lead the kitchen to creating authentic and tasty national
dishes.  Don’t miss this opportunity to
taste this exciting cuisine.

 

During this food festival, Cafe 395 will
also offer Afternoon Tea Sets, which will include a selection of Ceylon and
Korean Teas served with Sri Lankan snacks. 


 

Sri Lankan cuisine has long been renowned for its spices.  Since ancient times, traders from all over
the world who came to Sri Lanka brought their native cuisines to the island,
resulting in a rich diversity of cooking styles and techniques.  One of the most complex cuisines in the
world, the food of Sri Lanka uses bountiful local ingredients including some of
the hottest peppers in the world.

 

Café 395 introduced the market to table
concept to buffet fine dining.  Utilizing
only the freshest premium ingredients in various action stations located
throughout the restaurant, Café 395 welcomes diners to a vibrant dining
experience that delivers vibrant foods prepared just the way you want. The buffet is priced at KW91,000 per
person (lunch and dinner).

 



For
reservations or enquiries, please call Café 395 at (02) 317-3062. Price quoted
above is already inclusive of tax and service charge.
