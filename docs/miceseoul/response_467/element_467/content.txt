			Introduction
The InterContinental Seoul COEX is a stylish business hotel in the center of Gangnam in Korea, where shopping, entertainment and business come together consistently. You will have a view of a beautiful Hangang River from our hotel facing Bongeun Temple, a beautiful traditional temple. It is also directly connected to COEX Mall, casino and duty free shops where you can enjoy shopping, culture and other activity. The restaurants at The InterContinental Seoul COEX include: the Sky lounge where you can enjoy your meal with a view of the fantastic skyline of Seoul; Asian Live where you can taste Korea, Japanese, Chinese, Indian, and Arabic cuisine all in one place; the beautiful Lobby Lounge with its violet interior design and the Brasserie, a buffet restaurant where a la carte menus are also served. The banquet rooms have been renovated and transformed into stylish and modernized place. They are installed with new LED lighting system which is ideal for various events with different concepts. They are suitable for conventions, contemporary weddings and special family gatherings. They are installed with simultaneous interpretation equipment (up to 8 foreign languages) and a video conference system for convenience sake.
