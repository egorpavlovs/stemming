			Introduction
Tmark Grand Hotel Myeongdong is a luxury business hotel located in the shopping and cultural center of Seoul. Tmark Grand Hotel Myeongdong is conveniently located in the heart of Seoul with convenient transportation access to Myeongdong, Namdaemun market, Mt. Nam, Seoul Station, National Theater, Seven Luck Casino, Cheonggye Stream and Insadong…etc. Tmark Grand Hotel Myeongdong hopes our guests will be able to experience beautiful views of Seoul, the nightscape of Mt. Nam, and our caring services so that every guest may leave with precious memories.
