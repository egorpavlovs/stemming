		C-Festival - Global No.1 MICE Festival
		2016-05-03
		
			
			 

C-Festival - Global No.1 MICE Festival will be held in Coex, Seoul from
May 4th – 8th, 2016. 

 

C-Festival contains a variety of cultural performances and exciting
programs supported by Seoul Metropolitan Government.

Enjoy the best festival in the heart of the city with five exciting codes
- Exhibition/Convention code, Picnic code, Getaway code, Romance code and
Healing code.

It is expected to be crowded with foreigners since the festival will be
held during Chinese Labor day and 2016 Global Costume Festival.

 

 Visit the official website for further information: http://c-festival.com/en/
