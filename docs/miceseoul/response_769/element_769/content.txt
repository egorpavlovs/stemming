	[Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel] Over the Sky Party

	Writer : Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel		 / Date : 2014/11/19 14:04 / Modified Date 2014/11/19 14:05	

	

						
												
		CELEBRATE THE NEW YEAR 2015 IN STYLE, ‘OVER THE SKY PARTY’Enjoy a bottle of Moët & Chandon and unlimited snacks with the New Year’s CountdownSeoul, November 19, 2014- Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel celebrates the New Year on the last day of December overlooking the dazzling nightscape of the city at its 41st floor, Lobby Lounge.Bar with ‘Over the Sky Party’.‘Over the Sky Party’ starts late at night on December 31, 2014 at the 41st floor Feast at 11pm. Guests can enjoy the end-of-the-year mood with popera performances overlooking the panoramic nightscape of the city. A bottle of Moët & Chandon and unlimited snacks will be arranged for the guests to toast to the New Year celebration. A countdown event will start to announce the end of year 2014 and the New Year 2015. ‘Over the Sky Party’ begins at 11pm to 2am, on December 31, 2014. Price is KRW 180,000 for two persons, including a bottle of Moët & Chandon and unlimited snacks at the Feast. An additional KRW 60,000 is charged per additional person joining to enjoy unlimited snacks. [Over the Sky Party]Date: December 31, 2014Hours: 11pm~2amLocation: Feast 41FPrice: KRW 180,000 for two persons (inclusive of 10% VAT)*additional KRW 60,000 per person for unlimited snacks Details: - One(1) bottle of Moët & Chandon- Unlimited snacks for two personsInformation & Reservations: 02.2211.2100, sheratonseoul.dcube@sheraton.comwww.sheraton.com/seouldcubecity
