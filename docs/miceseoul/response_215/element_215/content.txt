								Located just opposite the Coex convention and exhibition center, the Park Hyatt Seoul is a stylish personal residence in which the number of guestrooms has been specifically limited in order to provide a level of service, comfort and attention to detail unparalleled elsewhere. All 185 guestrooms, lobby, lounge, restaurants, bars, swimming pool, fitness studio, spa treatment rooms and meeting rooms offer panoramic views of the city with natural sunlight or romantic night scenes streaming through 3.4m floor-to-ceiling windows.
The stylish hotel offers six meeting rooms and boardrooms ideal for corporate meetings, conferences, seminars and formal gatherings. With stylish and contemporary furniture creating a residential ambience, the Park Hyatt Seoul’s meeting rooms offer a warm and pleasant atmosphere during the day, while the night views create the perfect mood for an atmospheric and sophisticated setting. All meeting rooms are equipped with a built-in LCD projector, a flat-screen TV, a DVD/CD player, teleconferencing facilities and espresso & coffee machines, and offer access to high-speed broadband internet and wireless LAN. From business meetings to social events, the Park Hyatt’s staff will help to create tailored packages that best suit the needs of the individual or the group.
