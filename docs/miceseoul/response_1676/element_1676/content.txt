			Introduction
Located in Gangnam, Seoul's major convention district, the Hotel Riviera Seoul offers 320 guest rooms equipped with high-speed Internet service, as well as attractive night views of the Hangang River. The Hotel Riviera Seoul also features a traditional Korean housethemed Grand Ballroom replete with all the modern fittings needed to conduct functions of all kinds.
