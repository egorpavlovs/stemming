	Romantic Christmas Celebration at The Palace

	Writer : The Palace Hotel Seoul		 / Date : 2015/12/04 11:30	

	

						
												
		Seoul, December 4,
2015- The Palace Hotel Seoul (www.thepalaceseoul.co.kr) presents ‘Romantic
Holiday Package’ from December 23th to 26th for couples who
are looking for a romantic getaway this Christmas.

 

‘Romantic Holiday Package’ includes
a one-night stay, a bottle of ‘Moet&Chandon’
and handmade Grissini, along with breakfast and happy hour at the 12th
floor club lounge, exclusive to guests staying in the club floors. The
package also provides common benefit of complimentary use of the gym, indoor
swimming pool and sauna for two.

 

Our ‘Romantic
Holiday’ package is available from December 23th to December 26th,
priced start from KRW300,000 (excluding service charge and taxes) For
enquiries, call (02)2186-6797, 6766~8.
