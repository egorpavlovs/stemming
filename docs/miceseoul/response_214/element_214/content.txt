								Novotel Ambassador Seoul Gangnam, located at the center of Gangnam’s business district, recently completed its renovation for the executive floor and superior floor; it is now equipped with 332 guest rooms with modern design. At the small and large banquet halls that can accommodate 10 to 500 people, events such as weddings, seminars, business meetings, various receptions, and all kinds of banquet can be held. You can have internet access anywhere inside the hotel through wired/wireless internet. In addition, the business center located at the lobby provides the best services for business customers. We operate 5 elegant restaurants and bars and offer 24-hour room service. Moreover, you can enjoy state-of-the-art swimming pool, driving range, and gym equipped with spa sauna. We are located near the COEX World Trade Center, Gyeongbu Expressway, Gangnam Station (Line no. 2), and Nonhyeon Station (Line no. 7). It takes only 5 minutes on foot from Sinnonhyeon or Eonju Station (Line no. 9). Besides, it is easy to get to the Incheon airport from the hotel; the KAL limousine bus runs every 20 minutes in front of the hotel’s main gate.
