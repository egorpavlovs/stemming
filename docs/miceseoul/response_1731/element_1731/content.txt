        Seoul Women’s Plaza is an 8-story building (5 levels above ground, 3 levels 
below) available for all kinds of events. Its facilities include an International 
Conference Hall that seats up to 450 persons, Art Hall ‘BOM’ with seating 
for 300 persons, various small lecture rooms, and seminar rooms. The building 
also contains Women NGO centers, several club activity rooms, a book gallery 
and more. The Plaza is open to both females and males alike and can accommodate 
180 overnight guests in either Korean-style (flood bedding) or Western style 
rooms.
