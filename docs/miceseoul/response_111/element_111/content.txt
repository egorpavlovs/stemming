	Cherry Blossom Package

	Writer : Belle-Essence (Former Renaissance Seoul Hotel)		 / Date : 2016/4/05 10:31 / Modified Date 2016/4/05 10:32	

	

						
												
		

Former Renaissance Seoul Hotel, Belle-Essence Seoul Hotel
Cherry Blossom Package Belle-Essence Seoul Hotel is promoting 2 kinds of ‘Cherry Blossom Package’ until April 17th for those looking to spend abeautiful
moment with their loved ones this cherry blossom season.
All packages include
complimentary use of the indoor swimming pool, fitness center and sauna use for
two.The “Cherry Blossom Package I” is recommended
for all couples and includes one night in a deluxe room, breakfast for twoat Café
Elysee for a surcharge of KRW 30,000, complimentary two breads with coffee at The
Bakery,complimentary two cherry blossom cocktails at the Trevi Lounge, early
check-in at 2pm,free shuttle sending service to Namsan mountain from the hotel
and free in-room internet access.The Package also includes two padlocks for
decorating padlock tree with the guests’ wish on top of the N-Seoul Tower.The
package is KRW 175,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and
10% tax.).The ‘Cherry Blossom Package II’ is recommended
for families, and it includes one night in a deluxe room,complimentary
breakfast for two at Café Elysee(two children under the age of 12 dine for
fee),as well as two cherry blossom cocktails at the Trevi Lounge,
complimentary two ham & cheese sandwichwith two coffee or soft drink at The
Bakery, early check-in at 2pm, free shuttle service to Namsan mountain from the
hotel,and free in-room internet access. A special gift set is also included.This
package is KRW220,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10%
tax.). Also, in celebration of
the upcoming 28th hotel anniversary in July 6th, the 28th
booking guest will receive aKRW100,000 worth gift certificate that can be used
at HanCook and The Steak House restaurants located at N-Seoul Tower.Plan an enjoyable and
memorable cherry blossom season with your loved ones.

Period: Until 2016. 4. 17Price: From KRW 175,000 (Exclusive of 10%
service charge and 10% tax.)Information & Reservation: 02-2222-8500
