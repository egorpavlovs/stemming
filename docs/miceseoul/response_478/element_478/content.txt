			Introduction
SEJONG HOTEL is located in Myeongdong which is the main hub of shopping, sightseeing and business in South Korea. It provides a panoramic view of beautiful Namsan Mountain and a splendid view of seoul. SEJONG HOTEL has total 333 comfortable rooms in various style. Also it is equipped with 4 banquet halls for holding various events like international conventions, business seminars, smallsized meetings and weddings. SEJONG HOTEL will provide all visitors the greatest attention to any small details to give the best customer satisfaction.
