		The Seoul MICE Alliance Annual Conference 2015 Concludes Successfully
		2015-09-23
		
			
			  Thank you to all our cherished Seoul MICE Alliance (SMA) members who came out for 2015 SMA Annual Conference. It was a memorable night reflecting on the SMA's collective accomplishments and strengthening in the past year, networking, and renewing our commitment to work together in our efforts to make Seoul a top destination for business events.
