								‘Seoul Grand Park’, people and animals are happy togetherOpened in 1984, Seoul Grand Park consists of the Seoul Zoo- the largest in Korea and managing the care of some 3,700 animals-, Seoul Landan amusement park-, a Rose Garden, Children’s Zoo, Themed Garden, and Campsite. Seoul Grand Park is set among forested hillsides and is a perfect place for relaxation as a comprehensive theme park.We provide diverse activities for visitors, including families, such as educational programs, feeding activities, festivals, and the largest rose garden festival in Korea.We pursue citizens’ happiness through distinctive festivals and cultural activities.Seoul Grand Park will continue its effort to help ensure the happiness of animals and people and to achieve the goal of being a world-class cultural park.
