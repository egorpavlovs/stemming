With the unique interpretation of Korean dining, Soo Man Lee, the founder of SM Entertainment co., has created a contemporary concept and menu with vibrant flavors using fresh ingredients. Located on the lower level of the restaurant, the PLAYGROUND has plenty of options for those who would like to have lunches, teas, dinners, alcohol drinks in one place.
