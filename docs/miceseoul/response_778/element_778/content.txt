	Conrad Seoul Introduces a ‘Wish Your Luck’ Package for Lunar New Year

	Writer : Conrad Seoul		 / Date : 2015/1/22 9:44	

	

						
												
		

Conrad Seoul Introduces a ‘Wish Your
Luck’ Package for Lunar New Year

-     
Conrad Seoul is offering a
special Lunar New Year package for a relaxing and enriching holiday in the
city.

-     
Participate in our ‘Lucky
Yut Game’ with a 100% chance of winning and start your New Year with luck and
fortune

-     
Book through our official
Korean website and win a chance to receive a ‘Le Creuset tea pot set’ as a gift
for the woman in your life, as thanks for the holidays 

 

SEOUL, Korea – January 30,
2014 – Conrad Seoul is offering a ‘Wish Your Luck’
package, providing guests with a relaxing and enriching Lunar New Year holiday
escape to the heart of downtown Seoul, as you celebrate the dawn of a lucky new
year.

 

This special holiday
package includes one night’s stay in a spacious, luxurious guest room with
beautiful views of the Han River and city. It also offers the opportunity to
participate in the traditional game of Yut with a 100% chance of winning a lucky
prize. Package guests will receive a ticket to
participate at time of check-in at the reception desk. According to the
results, guests will win either a Conrad Seoul signature Teddy Bear, a
complimentary breakfast at Zest, a restaurant & bar cash voucher, a Le
Creuset mug set or more. Everyone wins! All package guests are also entitled to free access
to the fitness center and indoor swimming pool during their stay.

 

In
addition, those guests who book this package through Conrad Seoul’s official
Korean website are entitled to participate in a lucky draw. A winner will
receive a ‘Le Creuset tea pot set’ gift for their loved one, as a thank you for
their Lunar New Year holiday contributions.

 

This package is available from January 19 to
February 22 and prices start from KRW230,000 based on double occupancy,
excluding tax. For reservations or inquiries, please call on: 02-6137-7777 or visit Conrad’s official
Korean website, www.conradseoul.co.kr.

 

Conrad Seoul is superbly designed for smart luxury,
boasting a standard room size of 48 m2 , the largest in its class in Seoul. The hotel
features great open views of the Han River, with each of its 434 rooms equipped
with Wi-Fi, an Apple-based in-room multimedia and entertainment hub, a
Nespresso coffee machine with 6 Nespresso capsules, a separate bathtub and
shower and 4 different kinds of bath amenities to choose from. 

 

For more details
please visit www.conradseoul.com (English).
