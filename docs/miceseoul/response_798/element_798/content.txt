	[Grand Ambassador Seoul] 2014 Autumn Package

	Writer : Grand Ambassador Seoul associated with Pullman		 / Date : 2014/8/22 9:47	

	

														
		

Enjoy the romantic autumn season with 

Grand Ambassador Seoul’s
2014 Autumn Package



From September 1st to November 30th, the Grand Ambassador Seoul is
offering its fall packages to help guests enjoy the romantic fall weather. Guests
can take advantage of quick and easy outing with the ‘Autumn Picnic package’, a
scenic and brisk trek on the paths of Namsan with the ‘Autumn Trekking package’.


 

The ‘Autumn Picnic package’ includes a picnic basket with sandwiches,
cake, and fruit expertly crafted by the hotel chef along with a bottle of sparkling
water and tickets for the observation deck of the N Seoul Tower from which to
view the scenic landscape made beautiful with the autumn leaves..

 

The ‘Autumn Trekking package’ includes a one (1) night stay at the hotel,
breakfast room service for 2 and a Namsan Trek program consisting of a course
hand picked by the Grand Ambassador Seoul. The Namsan Trek program includes a
sports bag, cooling pack, towels and bottled water along with a Polaroid camera
and 10 pack of film. 

 

All packages allow for complimentary use of the hotel swimming pool and
hotel fitness center as well as in-room Nespresso coffee machines including two
(2) free espresso capsule coffees. Package prices start at KRW 242,000. All
prices are subject to 10% VAT.

 

Information and reservations: 02-2270-3111~3, grand.ambatel.com
