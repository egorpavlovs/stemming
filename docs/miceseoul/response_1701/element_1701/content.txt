			Introduction
With an ideal location in Gangnam, Park Hyatt Seoul provides an unparalleled level of luxurious experience that exceeds every expectation for business travelers. The hotel is also directly opposite the Coex convention and exhibition center, giving guests easy access to international meetings and other events. The hotel itself is a distinctive 24-story 'glass box'- like structure, allowing for stunning panoramic views of the city through floor-to-ceiling windows on every level including all meeting and event rooms.
