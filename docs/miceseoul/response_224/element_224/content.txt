								Seoul property near Bongeunsa with fitness facility and shopping arcade

Location
Located near Korea World Trade Center, the hotel is 50 km (31 miles) from Incheon International Airport; in close proximity to COEX and Olympic stadium.

Property amenities
Guests of the Hotel Riviera have access to the fitness center, spa services, and aerobics studio. There is an indoor swimming pool, a golf range, and a sauna. The hotel's shopping arcade includes a barber shop, beauty parlor, tailor, jeweler, flower shop, travel agency, car rental facility, and a night club. The Hotel Riviera Seoul offers 4 restaurants and a bar, a grand ballroom, seven banquet halls, and a 24-hour business center. Banquet facilities are suitable for meetings, seminars, dinners, and weddings.

Conference/function room(s)
The grand ballroom is 2,486 square feet (231 square meters) and can accommodate up to 1,000 people. The 7 banquet halls range in size from 194 square feet (18 square meters) to 1,830 square feet (170 square meters). The grand and medium ballrooms can accommodate 16 to 350 people. The grand ballroom offers computers with Internet access, a sound system, LCD projector, and a large screen.

Guestrooms
All 320 guestrooms feature complimentary wireless Internet access, satellite TV, telephones with voice mail, minibars, safe-deposit boxes, and hair dryers.
