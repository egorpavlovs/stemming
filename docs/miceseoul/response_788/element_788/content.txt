	[ Imperial Palace Seoul ]  ‘The Present : Opera AIDA 1963’ Package 

	Writer : Imperial Palace Seoul		 / Date : 2014/10/20 14:28	

	

						
												
		 
Imperial Palace Seoul sponsors the opera ‘AIDA 1963’ 
and presents ‘The Present : Opera AIDA 1963’ Package 
 
Imperial Palace Seoul presents ‘The Present : Opera AIDA 1963’ Package from October 17 to November 30. The package is made in commemoration of sponsoring the opera ‘AIDA 1963’ of La Scala Theater in Milan, Italy. Imperial Palace Seoul sponsors the opera’s foreign casts and staffs of La Scala Theater for hotel stay for a month. 
 
‘The Present : Opera AIDA 1963’ Package includes various benefits such as one night in a comfortable room, the opera ‘AIDA 1963’ tickets for two (worth 330,000won per ticket), a coupon for the opera ‘AIDA 1963’ handbook, a bow tie or a telescope for the opera, ‘AIDA special course’ menu’s 30% discount benefit at the Italian restaurant Verona, free Internet service, and free access to a fitness center. The opera tickets are only available on November 28 (7:30 p.m.) and November 29 (7:00 p.m.). The opera tickets and the coupon for the opera ‘AIDA 1963’ handbook can be exchanged at Seoul Arts Center on the day of the show. 
 
The package price starts from 660,000won. (excluding tax and service charge)
 
There are 3 types of ‘The Present : Opera AIDA 1963’ Package : deluxe room type, club deluxe room type, and the  ‘Royal Package’ (corner or duplex suite room) type. 
 
If guests choose a deluxe room, they will be offered breakfasts for two at Café Amiga. If guests choose a club floor’s room (club deluxe room, corner suite or duplex suite), they will be offered breakfasts for two at Club Imperial Lounge and various Club Imperial Lounge’s benefits. Moreover, guests who choose the ‘Royal Package’ type will be offered the Royal Package’s course menu (worth 200,000won per person) for two and 1 bottle of the Italian wine ‘San Zopito Montepulciano d’Abruzzo’ (worth 200,000won per bottle) at the Italian restaurant Verona, and limousine service from the entire metropolitan area of Seoul to Seoul Arts Center. Limousine service is available by an advance reservation. 
 
Meanwhile, La Scala Theater will perform the opera ‘AIDA 1963’ in Korea, for celebrating the 130th anniversary of the establishment of diplomatic relations between Korea and Italy. The theater is planning to put on the opera ‘AIDA 1963’ from November 25 to November 30 at the opera house of Seoul Arts Center in Seocho-dong, Southern Seoul. The opera ‘AIDA 1963’ is a version in 1963 of Italian composer Verdi's masterwork and it is considered a historical piece of music.  
 
Inquiries: (02)3440-8000 / www.imperialpalace.co.kr
