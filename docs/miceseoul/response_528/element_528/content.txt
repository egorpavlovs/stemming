        Ibis Styles Ambassador Myeongdong is the first hotel to open under the
name of Ibis Styles, the most stylish & trendiest Ibis brand boasting of
more than 1,750 hotels worldwide. 180 guestrooms offer big comfort
and the Rooftop Bar & Restaurant on the 21F provide expansive view
along with the attractions that Seoul has to offer. Located at the heart
of business and tourism in Myeongdong, with easy access to the
airport bus stop and subway station. Le style restaurant &bar located
on the 21st floor of the hotel, It is a modern and stylish rooftop bar that
provides a grand cityscape along the skyline of Myeong-dong and N
Seoul Tower.
Transportation
From Incheon Int’l Airport: Take the limousine bus, No. 6015 (to Myeong-dong Stn. / Sejong Hotel Stn.) @ Gate 5B or 12A
Subway Station: Line 4, Myeong-dong Stn. exit 10(2 minutes walk)Line 3 & 4, Chungmuro Stn. exit 5 (5 minutes walk)Line 2 & 3, Euljiro 3-ga Stn. exit 12(10 minutes walk)
Nearby Attractions
Streets: Myeongdong shopping street, Insadong, cheonggyecheon stream
Shopping: donghwa duty free shop, lotte department store, shinsegae department store, dongdaemun market
Historical and cultural sites : gwanghwamun square, gyeongbokgung palace, changdeokgung palace, National palace Museum of Korea, Seoul Museum of history, Sejong Art center, Jogyesa
Others: Namsangol Hanok Village, Namsan N-tower
