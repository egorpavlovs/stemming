Uniqueintegration of Korean traditional instruments and percussion will give you anunforgettable experience. It is a golden opportunity for you to be infected bya fun virus. It is more than whatever you have imagined.
Synopsis
In the beginning, there were a man and awoman who loved each other. God loved them so much that he gave a Heavenly drumto the man and a Heavenly flute to the woman. But the woman tore off theHeavenly drum because the man got obsessed by the Heavenly drum. As a result,God hid the Heavenly flute and made her family become ghosts. The only way toturn back into human is to play ‘Fanta-Stick’ music with the lost heavenlyflute. Guess what will happen to them next?
