K-Turtle
Convention Center which is located in Sinchon Seoul, is well known to many
people as GEOGUJANG since it has opened on March 1958 through the first and
second generation of the manager. K-Turtle is formed with Main Building (Korean
traditional interior), New Convention Hall (Modern style), La fenetre Hall
(Annexed hall) and also various scale of halls so it is possible to produce
many customized events which can fulfill customer's purpose. The New Convention
Hall can accommodate up to 700 people and has the best audible devices with
large LED full screen at the front stage. Meanwhile, The Annexe La fenetre Hall is connected with outside of the building by overall
glass folding door so it is possible for up to 100 people to have outdoor
wedding and many outdoor events. K-Turtle can provide a hotel style wedding,
corporate events, seminar, outdoor events in one place. Also it is very famous
to foreign tourists as Korean essential tour course by holding many different
events which is recommended as the best place for galadinner according to
internal inbound travel agencies. K-Turtle provides smoothness and dignity to
customers' events based on expert service and know-how from many successful
event progresses.
