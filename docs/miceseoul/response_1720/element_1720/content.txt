        Designed by internationally-renowned design firm Wilson Associates, the Aston 
House mansion is a secluded mountain getaway in East Seoul offering high quality 
services . It can be divided into four distinct event spaces, with the second 
floor providing a luxurious dining room for the most sophisticated, elegant, 
and memorable functions. Located on the slopes of Achasan Mountain, the building 
offers stunning views of southeast Seoul and the Hangang (River), while its 
lush garden is suitable for special occasions and social gatherings. Event concept 
and decorations can all be customized to suit the event, and the space is also 
used as a luxurious lifestyle setting in many Korean television dramas and films.
