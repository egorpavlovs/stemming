			Introduction
Grand Hyatt Seoul, located in the heart of the nation's capital, is situated amidst beautifully landscaped gardens on historic Mt. Namsan, offering guests a diversity of extraordinary experiences. The contemporary guest rooms provide spectacular views through floor to ceiling windows, with fashionable furnishings and contemporary décor. The hotel is ideally located for convenience, equipped to host virtually any type of meeting or event accommodating 20 to 2,000 guests, with a professional service team at your disposal to ensure perfection. Grand Hyatt Seoul also offers a diversity of exceptional culinary options for our guests with a selection of 12 restaurants and bars. And to fully recharge and relax, The Spa Grand Hyatt Seoul features 10 private Spa rooms and a 24 hour gym to keep you in the best of health.
