        Seoul Arts Center is one of Korea’s premier cultural facilities, attracting  almost 2.5 million visitors annually to its rich program of performances and  exhibitions. It holds around 1,500 performances and over 100 exhibitions in  7 specialized spaces annually, all featuring world-renowned domestic and international  artists. The complex is comprised of five multipurpose buildings (Opera House,  Music Hall, Calligraphy Art Museum, Hangaram Art Museum, and Arts Library),  as well as a 1,000-seat outdoor stage, plaza, conference hall, and other facilities  available for all manner of events such as concerts, shows, conferences, and  banquets.TransportationFrom Incheon Int’l Airport: 60~70 minSubway: Nambu Bus Terminal (Line 3) Nearby hotels & attractionsWithin 3km: Centro Hotel, Snow HotelWithin 5km: Renaissance Seoul Hotel, Ibis Ambassador Seoul
    Shopping: Central City Complex, Coex Mall
    Entertainment: Gangnam CGV, Megabox Central (cinemas)
    Historical and cultural sites : National Center for Korean Traditional 
    Performing Arts
    Others: Umyeonsan Park, Yangjae Citizens Park
