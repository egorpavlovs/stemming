	Conrad Seoul offers ‘Oyster dishes’ in January

	Writer : Conrad Seoul		 / Date : 2014/12/16 11:20	

	

										
								
		

Conrad
Seoul offers ‘Oyster dishes’ in January

Conrad
Seoul introduces special Oyster dishes from Tonyeong, for winter stamina

 

SEOUL, Korea –
December 03, 2014 – Conrad Seoul will be offering
a wide variety of fresh and delicious Oyster dishes in all its restaurants
during the month of January. Guests can enjoy nutritious Oysters harvested
fresh, in season from the waters of Tongyeong with special wine pairings
suggested by the hotel’s sommelier. 

 

Italian
restaurant ATRIO will be offering 3 kinds of Italian style Oyster dishes with
Italian wine pairings selected by our sommelier. Enjoy the sweet and savory
taste of fresh Tongyeong oysters on ice with lemon, ginger, orange and chili
dressing, exquisitely matched with the creamy texture of a N.V. FRANCIACORTA,
BELLAVISTA, CUVÉE BRUT, LOMBARDIA, ITALY. Or try the Tongyeong oysters with
homemade chittara pasta, bottarga, parsley, celery, cherry tomato and lemon
zest, perfectly harmonized with a 2010 VIGNA IL PINO, TORRE DI GIANO,
LUNGAROTTI, UMBRIA, ITALY embodying the rich taste of minerals. You may also
try the Tongyeong baked oysters with homemade bread crumbs, spinach, herbs, sun
dried tomato and mixed nuts, paired with a 2011 CHARDONNAY, JERMANN, VENEZIA
GIULIA, FRIULI VENEZIA GIULIA, ITALY that imparts a fluent aroma of fruit with
a touch of acidity  to complement the
seafood. 

 

The
open kitchen and live cooking stations at ZEST where guests can discover a
variety of world class delicacies, will be presenting a selection of Oyster
dishes in Korean, Chinese and Japanese styles. Guests can enjoy Oyster Gratin,
Cold Raw Oyster Soup, Braised Oysters, Spicy Seafood Noodle Soup with Oysters,
Napa Wraps with Oysters and Raw Oysters, all in one place. 

 

37
Grill & Bar perched high atop the hotel on the 37th floor, will
be offering 2 kinds of Oyster dishes in a fine dining presentation. Enjoy fresh
Tongyeong Oysters in an assorted vegetable concase with yuzu ice, revealing the
natural taste of raw Oysters. Or try the Tongyeong Oysters with Béchamel sauce &
crispy bacon, vegetables, bread crumbs and cheddar cheese, allowing the Oysters
to stay moist inside while crunchy outside.

 

This
is the season to enjoy fresh Oysters, often called “the milk of the ocean” because
of its nutritional properties. Oysters are rich in proteins, essential amino
acids, vitamins and minerals which aid metabolism and the production of energy.
Oysters from Tongyeong are wonderfully plump and renowned for their milky white
colors in the height of the season. 

 

Conrad
Seoul’s ‘Oyster Dishes’ promotion will be held during the month of January at
all of its restaurants. Ala carte dishes start from KRW21,000, lunch buffet from
KRW70,000, dinner buffet from KRW80,000/ Weekends lunch price from KRW85,000
and dinner price from KRW90,000, all inclusive of tax. For reservations or inquiries 02.6137.7000

 

For more information, please visit www.conradseoul.co.kr (Korean language).
