Our
Sky Garden, located on the 11th floor of Hyundai Department Store,
Trade Center offers a relaxing place to take a refresh from the urban
surroundings and it also often works as a place to create inspirational
and entertaining events for the customers and the locals to promote cultural
lifestyle. It is capable of holding more than 300 people at once and has held
events such as Jazz and Wine Festival, Sculpture Park, Rooftop Beer Festival,
and Entertaining Park for the Kids.
