								Where the refreshing fragrance of pine trees and the beauty of the forest tempts travelers to escape from the busy city life and relax, Mayfield Hotel is the perfect destination. Our hotel shines the most with its elegant features where the European sensibility lies harmoniously with natural beauty.
Mayfield Hotel is gracefully built in five stories and our hotel offers a wide range of five-star amenities, services and facilities. Guests can enjoy senses of Europe and the nature preserve of pine forest, as well as our professionally staffed business meetings, various conventions and international events providing the key to a successful business trip. Boasting naturally harmonious botanical garden, our Mayfield hotel’s amenities includes Korea's top- quality Fitness Club.
Mayfield Hotel is conveniently located near connecting airports, only five minutes away from the Gimpo International Airport and thirty minutes away from the Incheon International Airport.
