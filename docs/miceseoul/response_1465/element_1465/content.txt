			Introduction
Experience the modern and cultural vibe of Seoul at the Courtyard Marriott Seoul Namdaemun Hotel, the first Courtyard branded hotel located in the heart of the historic, cultural, business district of Namdaemun. Whatever your travel purposes are, the Courtyard Marriott Seoul Namdaemun Hotel is your perfect home away from home for both business and leisure travelers. Be fully cozy and productive at the same time in one of our 409 guest rooms and suites equipped with comfortable workspaces, high-speed Internet access and stunning views overlooking the country's national treasure No. 1 Sungnyemun Gate (Namdaemun Gate) Be sure to stop by the all-day dining restaurant MoMo Café for a delicious meal or visit MoMo Lounge & Bar for a casual drink during your time in Seoul. If you have plans to host an event, our meeting spaces accommodating up to 150 guests is the ideal venue located in the heart of Seoul. And our convenient hotel location which allows you to walk to City Hall and Hoehyeon Station within 5 minutes, as well as, the adjacent Namdaemun Market and Myeongdong shopping district within the walking distance is just another plus. Make room for a little fun at the Courtyard Marriott Seoul Namdaemun Hotel.
