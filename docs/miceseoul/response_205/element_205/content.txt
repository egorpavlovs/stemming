The
Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas, near the Korea's business and cultural
center such as World Trade Center Seoul, Coex Convention Center and CALT, is
the best suited for international business travelers.Our
15 event venues are ready for you with the high-tech audio and lighting
facilities for multi-purpose events such as large-scale conventions, middle and
small-scale meetings, high-quality weddings and private family gatherings.The
Grand Ballroom is designed for the video conference, simultaneous
interpretation equipment (up to 8 different languages) and large screens.In
addition, various menus, from the sophisticated and modern French cuisine to
Italian, Asian, and Japanese dishes, are prepared by our experienced executive
chefs by using the selected ingredients to ensure that our guest tastes an
unforgettable dishes.Grand
InterContinental Seoul Parnas is the first five-star hotel in Korea that has
met HACCP (Hazard Analysis and Critical Control Point) standards established by
the International Hygiene Evaluation Agency. It has also been listed as top in
NCSI (National Customer Satisfaction Index) for five years. Furthermore, Grand
InterContinental Seoul Parnas has been a exemplar to other companies by
achieving Silver Level in 'Earthcheck' establishing itself as a globally
eco-friendly leader.
