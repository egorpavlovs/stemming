	Renaissance Seoul Hotel_Sweet Autumn Package

	Writer : Renaissance Seoul Hotel		 / Date : 2015/10/07 17:43 / Modified Date 2015/10/07 17:46	

	

						
												
		 
Renaissance Seoul Hotel
Relaxing in the City ‘Sweet Autumn Package’
-          Various packages suit for different guests and different occasions, including diverse benefit
from room service to special kit and many more
 

Renaissance Seoul Hotel is promoting 3 kinds of ‘Sweet Autumn Packages’ Until November 31st for those looking to spend
precious moment with loved ones this autumn season. All packages include complimentary use of the in-door swimming pool,
recreation center and sauna for two.
 
The “Sweet Couple Package” is an affordable and cost-saving option which includes one night in a deluxe room and
free in-room internet access for KRW 150,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.).
The ‘Sweet Ladies Package’ is recommends mother and their daughters and includes one night in a deluxe room, complimentary breakfast
for two at the buffet restaurant Café Elysee, ‘Kim Hwal Ran Musseneuf’ nail care vouchers worth KRW50,000 for two, and
free in-room internet access. The package also includes two beauty item gifts from ‘TREEANNSEA’, a beauty line make
with all natural ingredients and free from animal testing.
This package is KRW180,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.). 
The ‘Sweet Family Package’ is recommended for families, and it includes a night in a deluxe room, complimentary breakfast for two
at the Café Elysee(two children under the age of 6 dine for free), as well as a room-service order for pizza, buffalo wings, and soft drinks.
A special gift set from ‘TREEANNSEA’, a beauty line made with all natural ingredients and free from animal testing, is also included.
This package is perfect for families with a price of KRW190,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.).
 
The Renaissance Seoul Hotel looks forward to having you this autumn season as you make new memories with your loved ones.
 
Period: Until November 30th
Price: From KRW150,000 (Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.)
Information & Reservation: 02-2222-8500
