								In the heart of Seoul’s iconic Gangnam District, Le Meridien Seoul is an ideal base for discovering the local area’s distinctive blend of the ancient, the cultured and the vibrant ? where temples and malls, galleries and parks, sports and music, all provide a sensorial experience in a vibrant setting. Nearby attractions include Bongeunsa Temple, COEX Exhibition Center and Mall and Jamsil Baseball stadium.

Experience our 336 stylish rooms and suites, decorated in a soothing palette of blue, lavender and green tones infused with European design flair.
Le Meridien Seoul offers superb fine dining at a selection of five restaurants. 
Terrace buffet and dining restaurant Chef's Palette serves international cuisine in a natural light-filled setting, while star chef Edward Kwon's Elements offers Korean, Japanese, Chinese, Southeast Asian and halal dishes. 
Mid-Century is the perfect destination for enjoying light cuisine against the backdrop of a 14-metre media wall, while Latitude 37, located in the lobby, is the perfect spot for socializing with a sparkling beverage or a cup of Illy Coffee. 

Take advantage of our multi-compatible meeting spaces 1650 square metres intended to offer versatility to your corporate and social events.
Located on the hotel’s first floor, M Contemporary is an unprecedented cultural destination and recreational space in the heart of Gangnam. This art centre comprises seven exhibition venues throughout the hotel, including an LED media facade, a 1157-square-metre exhibition space, a private gallery for professional collectors, and a cafeteria that serves finger foods.
