								 Since January 2017, WALKERHILL HOTEL & RESORT has been newly managed as Walkerhill’s own brand, ‘WALKERHILL HOTEL & RESORT’. Not only was former Sheraton Grande Walkerhill reborn as Grand Walkerhill Seoul, but Vista Walkerhill Seoul (former W Seoul Walkerhill) was also re-opened in April after the 3-month construction for renewal, based on the concept of ‘Sustainable Luxury’.Recently, the Executive Floor ‘Grand Club by Walkerhill’ is re-opened after the construction for renewal. It is fully equipped with premium rooms and Grand Club Executive Lounge that is best known for its sky view. New amenities and various services of ‘Grand Club by Walkerhill’ improve customer satisfaction. In addition to special facilities consisting of Aston House and Camping Zone, the Convention Center includes 9 banquet halls with different sizes and various purposes ? Grand Hall, Vista Hall, Walkerhill Theatre, Cosmos Hall, Calla Room, Art Hall, Pine Room, Oak Room and Aida Room. The high-quality service of skilled professional staff as well as a simultaneous translation service and state-of-the-art system of projection, sound and lighting provides the first-class banquet service for high-end events, including family gatherings, weddings, largescale international conferences and business events.
