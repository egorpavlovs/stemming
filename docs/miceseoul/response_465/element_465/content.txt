Hotel SAMJUNG is close to Korea World Trade Center, Coex, Teheranro and the city airport. It is also close to the luxurious shopping districts of Apgujeong-dong and Cheongdamdong, making it convenient to do business or enjoy shopping at all times. Hotel SAMJUNG offers exquisite services with its 160 guest rooms, banquet halls, sauna, bars, and clubs
