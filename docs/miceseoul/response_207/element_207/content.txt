								For over 20 years, Hotel Samjung has been providing its many guests with deluxe-class service right in the heart of the Gangnam district. The hotel is an oasis of calm in the busy city with its elegantly-designed interiors, gardens, and nearby park space. Spanning 9,900 sqm, Hotel Samjung features 159 guest rooms, three large-scale banquet halls, and five restaurants. Meanwhile, the hotel's very modern convention center is well- suited for high-class events such as banquets, weddings, and special functions, while Samjung's massive car park ensures easy access to its many facilities for large groups.
Hotel Samjung:
a quality venue for the discerning traveler.
