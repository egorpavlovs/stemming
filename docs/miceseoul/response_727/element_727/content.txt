	Exclusive December packages from The Ritz-Carlton, Seoul

	Writer : The Ritz-Carlton Seoul		 / Date : 2015/12/02 10:56	

	

						
								
								
		

Exclusive December packages 

from The Ritz-Carlton, Seoul

 

 

The
Ritz-Carlton, Seoul presents two seasonal packages for celebrating the end of
2015 in style. Get Together, a package available in three versions, is
designed for those planning a luxurious end-of-year party to remember and runs
from December 1 to 30 (excluding December 24), while the Ritz Kidz-Mas package,
the perfect option for creating Christmas family memories, is available from
December 1 to 31. 

 

Get
Together is a day-use package with a check-in time of 3pm and a check-out time
of 1am, ideal for those booking celebrations and parties. 

 

Three
versions are available, according to room size and type of wine or champagne,
while the party menu, served in-room, includes dishes such as smoked salmon,
fruit, cheeses, assorted tempura and chopped steak. 

 

The
original Get Together package, ideal for 4-6 guests, includes use of a
Corner Deluxe Room, two bottles of Woodbridge red wine and a party menu. It is
priced at 424,000 won (plus tax and service charge). 

 

The Get
Together I package offers a luxurious combination of use of a Corner Deluxe
Room, Laurent-Perrier Brut Rosé and caviar, and is priced at 767,000 won (plus
tax and service charge). 

 

Finally,
the Get Together II package, designed for 6-8 guests, features use of a
Club Premier Room, Laurent-Perrier Grand Siècle and caviar. Access to the Club
Lounge is not included. The Get Together II package is priced at 1 million won
(plus tax and service charge). 

 

Ritz
Kidz-Mas, available from December 1 to 25, is the perfect package for families accompanied by children. Spend
the night in a Corner Deluxe Room, where kids are served Christmas cookies and
two cups of hot chocolate or milk. The package also includes gifts of a mini
Christmas tree and a toy Ritz Lion for a fun family time enjoying the seasonal
joy of tree decoration. Families are invited to create their own unique tree
designs, take a photo and upload it to Instagram – the family receiving the
most likes for its photo will be offered a certificate for two adults and two
kids to dine at Oksan Buffet. The Ritz Kidz-Mas package is priced at 350,000
won (plus tax and service charge). 

 

 

Inquiries and reservations: (02) 3451-8114
