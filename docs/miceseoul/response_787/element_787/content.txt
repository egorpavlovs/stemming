	[Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel] Signature Dessert Buffet

	Writer : Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel		 / Date : 2014/10/20 10:48	

	

						
												
		INDULGING IN A WEEKEND OF ENDLESS SWEET DESSERTS, ‘SIGNATURE DESSERT BUFFET’Enjoy the most loved dessert items in a buffet style at Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel Seoul, October 20, 2014- Every weekend at the signature restaurant ‘Feast’, Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel presents a special dessert buffet with coffee or tea for the guests. ‘Signature Dessert Buffet’ presents the most popular dessert items, from the chocolate fondue to dip marshmallows and mini muffins, fresh seasonal fruit tarts and pies, rich chocolate mousse, various flavors of macarons, cherry madness cake topped with fresh sweet cherries, crème brûlée, pistachio cream  and more deliciously rich desserts, available in unlimited quantities. Guests can indulge in the sweetness with a cup of coffee or tea, and enjoy a leisurely time overlooking the panoramic view of the city from the 41st floor of the hotel. ‘Signature Dessert Buffet’ is available every Saturday and Sunday from 3pm to 5pm. The dessert buffet includes coffee or tea and price is KRW 27,500 per person(inclusive of tax).[‘Signature Dessert Buffet’]Period: Every Saturday and SundayHours: 3pm~ 5pmPrice: KRW 27,500 (*inclusive of tax)Location: Feast 41FInformation & Reservations: 02.2211.1710www.sheraton.com/seouldcubecity
