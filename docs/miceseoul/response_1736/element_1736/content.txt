        Villa de Bailey, a house wedding and party venue that is both exotically splendid
and relaxing like a vacation retreat, features two unique halls: the Oriental Banquet
Hall connected to a poolside garden and a Classic Banquet Hall that offers a classy
ambience. Villa de Bailey is capable of operating sophisticated banquets through
highly qualified services provided by professional and delicate arrangements that
can perfectly suit each occasion. The inflow of sunlight and panoramic view of the
city from the terrace allows for the space to be ideal for hosting a range of events such
as brand launching shows, business parties, and large scale corporate banquets.
