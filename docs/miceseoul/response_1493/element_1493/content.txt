			Introduction
A beautiful youth hostel with a splendid view of Olympic Park and the Hangang River. It features a convention hall for an international conference and a large outdoor performance stage and offers delicious meals made from the freshest ingredients. In addition, other modern conveniences such as a sauna, fitness center and indoor golf driving range ensure comfort and relaxation.
