	[Grand Ambassador Seoul]Winter Package

	Writer : Grand Ambassador Seoul associated with Pullman		 / Date : 2014/12/15 13:32	

	

						
								
								
		

For a Romantic Winter Rest 

[Grand Ambassador Seoul] 2014 Winter
Package

Grand Ambassador Seoul is offering
winter packages from December 1, 2014 to February 28, 2015 for you to have a
memorable time with your family, friends and loved ones. There are three different packages: the Winter Rest
Package, which is designed to relieve your daily stress with a high-quality
massage; the Winter Namsan Trekking Package, which will allow you to enjoy a
romantic winter trek on Namsan; and the Winter Delight Package, which offers
sweet dessert and leisurely relaxation. 

 

The
Winter Rest Package will relieve your daily stress and ease your tense muscles
from the cold winter weather. It consists of one night in a comfortable room
and a massage at the OCELAS spa (a choice of foot massage or solium massage). The OCELAS foot massage lasts for 30 minutes and
helps relieve the fatigue of tired feet. The Solium massage lasts for about 25
minutes and instantly relieves fatigue with treatment while sitting on a
specially-designed massage chair. A beauty box consisting of cream, ampoule,
and pore pack made by Treeannsea, a
functional premium natural cosmetics brand, is also provided for you winter
skin care. The package starts from KRW 275,000. 

 

If you fancy a romantic winter
trek on Namsan, the Winter Namsan Trekking Package is just the thing for you.
The package includes one night in cozy romantic room, the Namsan Mountain
Trekking Program, and a leisurely breakfast for two in your room. The Namsan
Trekking Program includes a trekking map of interesting Namsan courses
carefully selected by the hotel; a bottle of mineral water and a bottle of
vitamin water; and a sports bag containing a hot pack and towel. A Polaroid
camera and 10 rolls of film are also included. The package starts from KRW
242,200. 

 

The Winter Delight Package includes one night in
cozy room and a leisurely dessert consisting of an Ambassador roll filled with
whip cream; sweet macaroons, and coffee or tea. The
package price starts from KRW 199,100. 

 

All
packages allow for complimentary use of the hotel swimming pool and hotel
fitness center as well as in-room Nespresso coffee machines including two (2)
free espresso capsule coffees. All prices are subject to 10% VAT.

 

■ 문의 및 예약: 02-2270-3111~3, grand.ambatel.com
