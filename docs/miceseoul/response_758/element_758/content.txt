	Press release: Kim Byung-tae Appointed Seoul Tourism Organization's New President and CEO

	Writer : Seoul Convention Bureau		 / Date : 2015/6/29 16:22 / Modified Date 2015/7/01 14:17	

	

						
								
								
		Byung-tae
Kim Takes the Helm of Seoul Tourism Organization as New PresidentSeoul, South Korea, June 29,
2015 – The Seoul Tourism Organization (STO) has welcomed its new president and CEO. Byung-tae
Kim officially took charge of STO on Monday, June 29, with an inaugural
ceremony and speech at the STO head office. He is STO’s fourth president since
the organization’s founding in 2008. Kim has vowed to work closely with
Seoul city to foster growth in the city’s MICE businesses, increase visits to
Korea by overseas tourists, and spur Seoul’s competitiveness as a global
city.







Prior to leading STO, Kim was the CEO of BT&I, one of
Korea’s major travel management companies. His career is heavily
rooted in the culture and travel industry, in which he has over 20 years of
experience, having led many major organizations like the classical music
academy Pung Wol Dang and previously acting as adviser for the Kim Chong Yung Museum.
