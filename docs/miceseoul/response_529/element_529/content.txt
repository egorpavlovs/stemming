        You can experience the true taste of rooftop at ”Floating,” the best rooftop bar in Myeongdong. The night view of Namsan
Mountain and Myeongdong is more beautiful and fantastic than any scenic view you can find in Seoul.
• You can enjoy various cocktails, beers, and dishes with sophisticated music.
• Korea`s special Fried Chicken &Beer with L7 signature cocktails.
• Rooftop parties and events with celebrities and DJ.
