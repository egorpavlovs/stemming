	Writer : Millennium Seoul Hilton		 / Date : 2014/8/14 17:10	

	

						
												
		

OPENING OF ATRIUM
– THE NEW MEETINGS AND EVENTS VENUE AT MILLENNIUM SEOUL HILTON:

A BRAND NEW SPACE
IDEAL FOR SPECIAL EVENTS

 

14 August
2014, Seoul, Korea… Millennium Seoul Hilton is pleased to announce
the opening of its new meetings and events venue, the Atrium. This facility is
located on the 3rd floor of the hotel and was part of an extensive
renovation project that also included the opening of Café 395, update of the
hotel lobby and relocation of Cilantro Deli. 

 

The Atrium is a mid-sized event space with
a glass ceiling skylight and large windows all of which allow ample day light
to flow into it. These sources of day light can also be closed off during
presentations. Built into 2 locations of the venue are electronically operated
drop down screens, rendering maximum use of the space. This sophisticated new
area can be customized to suit a variety of configurations, making it ideal for
conferences, presentations, lectures, weddings, large dinners and more. The
Atrium also boasts an outdoor terrace large enough to accommodate up to 120
persons for seated weddings, banquets or stand-up cocktails.  

 

Oval Configuration: up to 200 seats

Oval Wedding Configuration: up to 160 seats

Classroom Configuration:  up to 165 seats

Theatre Configuration: up to 320 seats

U Shape Configuration: up to 63~69 seats

Hollow Configuration: up to 78~84 seats

 



For
further information or to enquire about hosting an event at the Atrium, please
call Banquet Reservations at (02) 317-3200.
