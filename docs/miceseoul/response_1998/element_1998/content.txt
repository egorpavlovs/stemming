								The Korea Societyof Management Information Systems is the nation’s leading body on IS, or thestudy of complementary networks of hardware and software that people andorganizations use to collect, filter, process, create and distribute data.Under the theme of “ICT Convergence in the Era of Creative Economy, the societyfocuses upon the sharing of IT, strategies and policies for new leaps ininformation and communications technology resources, and managementinnovation-related industries. It is the official organizer of the 2017International Conference on Information Systems (ICIS), which will take placein Seoul at the Coex convention and exhibition center.
Held annually indifferent countries on a rotational basis, ICIS is the largest meeting of theAssociation for Information Systems (AIS), which has over 4,000 membersrepresenting universities in over 95 countries worldwide. It is the mostprestigious gathering of academics and practitioners in the IS discipline, andprovides a forum for networking and knowledge-sharing amongst the ISprofession. Each year, over 1,000 IS academic professionals from around theworld participate in the conference program, which includes about 60 sessionsand 200 presentations, in addition to keynote speakers, CIO panels and researchpanels.
 
ICIS 2017 isexpected to include participants from South Korea, the US, Canada, the UK,Australia, China, Singapore and more than 1,200 researchers and practitionersworldwide.
