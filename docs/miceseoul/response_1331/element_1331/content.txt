		2016 Rotary International Seoul Convention Tour Information
		2016-05-23
		
			
			 This is information about the tours supported by Seoul Metropolitan Government and the Seoul Tourism Organization for participants of the Rotary International Seoul Convention 2016.Please do not hesitate to participate in a half-day tour of Seoul conducted after the '3K Walk for peace'.Tours should be booked in advance until 16:00pm 25th May.Tours can be closed in accordance with prior reservation.If you want to participate in a tour, please fill out the attached reservation form.Information on the Tour programs

      13:00pm DDP (Dongdaemun Design Plaza) -> 
14:00pm K-live Hologram  show (Kpop experience) -> 16:00pm Tour concludes at City Hall
