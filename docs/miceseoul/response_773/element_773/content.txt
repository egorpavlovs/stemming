	[InterContinental Seoul]Luxurious Weekend Spa Package in downtown Seoul

	Writer : Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas		 / Date : 2014/12/16 14:18	

	

						
								
								
		Grand Continental Seoul Parnas &
InterContinental Seoul COEX 

Luxurious Weekend Spa Package in downtown
Seoul! 

 

Grand
InterContinental Seoul Parnas and InterContinental Seoul COEX are receiving
favorable responses for ‘Weekend Spa Package’ that allows a good night’s rest
and a spa for looking for a place to unwind without leaving the city.

 

‘Weekend Spa
Package’ is more than just a simple relaxation in the hotel as it also offers two
coupons (worth 340,000 KRW) that provide a full body massage (60 min.) at “In
Spa” where one can experience a professional and scientific anti-aging treatment
along with the spa which the hotel jointly provides with AG Clinic, a renowned clinic
specialized in the field of anti-aging located on the second floor of
InterContinental COEX. 

 

In order to
provide as much leisure time as possible for ‘Weekend Spa Package’ guests to enjoy
their massages from skilled therapists, InterContinental Hotels will also allow
a late checkout service up until 2 p.m. as well as free usage of swimming pool,
golf course and sauna within the fitness club. If ‘Package’ guests take a room
at the club floor they will also be granted the benefits of the club lounge that
include free breakfast, free invitation to Happy Hour Event as well as free
snacks and beverages. 

 

The
price of Grand InterContinental Seoul Parnas’ Deluxe Room starts from 420,000
KRW and InterContinental Seoul COEX`s Superior Room starts from 400,000 KRW
(tax & service charge not included). There will be an additional charge of 60,500
KRW (tax, service charge included) when including a breakfast for two.

 

 ‘Weekend Spa Package’ is available only on
weekends and holidays. For further inquiries please contact Grand
InterContinental Seoul Parnas at 02-559-7777 or InterContinental Seoul COEX at 02-3430-8888.
