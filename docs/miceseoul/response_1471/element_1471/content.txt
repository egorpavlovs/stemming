			Introduction
JW Marriott Seoul is ideally located within a comprehensive multi-purpose complex in Gangnam. Situated in the heart of the business and entertainment districts, the JW Marriott Seoul is close to the Convention and Exhibition Center. Subway Lines 3, 7 and 9 are also directly linked to the hotel. All rooms are equipped with the latest technology including 40-inch LCD TV, the Remote Jack Pack, which offers a  single source interface for audio, video, iPod, and computer devices to connect to the in-room TV and wireless Internet.
