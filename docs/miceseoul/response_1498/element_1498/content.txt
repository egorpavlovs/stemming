Seoul’s leading city resort featuring a city hotel, Banyan Tree Club & Spa Seoul is located an hour’s drive from Incheon International Airport and just 10 minutes from downtown Seoul. The Hotel provides a special experience by providing Relaxation pools in every unit, and caters to guest privacy with a maximum four rooms per floor. Our Crystal Ballroom, with its stunning display of over 20,000 Swarovski crystals, provides a beautiful setting for wedding, business seminars and banquets.
 
• Hotel Grade: 5 Star
• Total Area(m²): 70,000 m²
• No. of Meeting Rooms: 4
• No. of Guest Rooms: 50
• Year of Establishment: 2010
• From Incheon Int’l Airport: 60 min./ 65km
• Subway Station: Line #3, Dongguk Univ. Station

Special Features ① Crystal Ballroom: Using around 20,000 Swarovski crystals, our Crystal Ballroom will make your function brighten up with lights glistening on the ceiling. From private events such as weddings and celebrations to business events such as seminars and launching shows, our professional banquet managers will assist you into making your events flawless. This luxurious hall is host to international conferences, high-end fashion shows, brand launch parties, car showcases, and a dynamic range of significant meetings. ② Boardroom: Our boardrooms will
assist your business meetings from set-ups to wrap-ups with high-tech systems. Boardroom offers high-quality, exceptional service. To ensure that your meetings are conducted smoothly and successfully, specially trained associates are on hand to provide secretarial support.
