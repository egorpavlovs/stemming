	Writer : Millennium Seoul Hilton		 / Date : 2015/12/16 16:46	

	

						
												
		

MILLENNIUM SEOUL HILTON’S CANDY HOUSE 

IS FULL OF CHRISTMAS TREATS!

 

16 December 2015, Seoul, Korea… Millennium Seoul Hilton has
opened its Candy House, a colourful and happy shoppette filled with sweet
treats and cozy decorations – just perfect for holiday gift giving!  Get all your Christmas shopping done here as
you really get into the spirit of Christmas! 
And for a really special keepsake, there is even a novelty photo wall
where you can take a commemorative photo. 

 

Located in the lower lobby of the hotel
next to the Christmas village and Christmas train display, the enticingly
decorated Candy House features a variety of Christmas baked goods, chocolates
and candies, quality Christmas decorations and gift items, holiday wines and
gift baskets, and so much more.  We are
confident you will find many things to make your holidays that much more enjoyable!
Items range in price from KW3,000 to KW800,000.

 

The Candy House will remain open until December
26th, so be sure to stop by and pick up that “just right”,
one-of-a-kind gift today!

 

While you’re at the hotel, don’t forget to
view Millennium Seoul Hilton’s Christmas Train, an elaborate miniature train
system set in an exquisite Christmas Alpine village surrounding the hotel’s holiday
showcase, the impressive 10-meter-tall Christmas tree. The Christmas tree and
train will remain on display through to January 17, 2016. 

 



For
additional information, please call Millennium Seoul Hilton’s Christmas Office
at (02) 317-3012.  All quoted prices are
inclusive of tax and service charge.
