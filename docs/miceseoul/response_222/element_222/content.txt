								The hotel is located strategically in the heart of Dongdaemun, where entertainment, culture and commerce converge. Access to town is convenient via the linked subway, or taxi 5-10 minutes to the central business district. The hotel features 170 guest rooms, including 15 suites, along with an indoor lap pool, 24-hour fitness center and full-service spa where guest can relax and indulge.

Accommodation Facilities
Offering luxury and service synonymous with the JW brand, the hotel provides for 170 guests rooms including 15 suites and 19 Executive Sky View Rooms. A dedicated Executive Lounge with an open balcony serves the Executive Floor rooms.

Meeting & Conventions
With a total of 10,570 square feet of meeting space and a Grand Ballroom that can accommodate up to 750 guests, the hotel is the perfect choice for the MICE (meetings, incentives, conferences and exhibitions) market. Among its state of the art meetings facilities is Seoul’s first Samsung LFD (Large Format Display) 98- screen media wall. Four meeting rooms is suitable for corporate events, training sessions, and meetings.


1 Pillar-less 10,570 square feet Grand Ballroom (Divided into Ballroom 1, 2 & 3)
4 Meeting Rooms (Meeting Room 1 & 2, Conference Room, Board Room)
Bridal Room
Wedding Consultation Room (Atelier)


Restaurant & Bars
For the gourmets, embark on an epicurean journey that will inspire and tantalized your senses ? featuring the internationally renowned New York steakhouse BLT Steak; a casual all-day dining restaurant Tavolo24 featuring live cooking stations; The Lounge which specializes in artisan chocolates, martinis and afternoon tea and Seoul Baking Company, which serves freshly baked cakes and pastries from its open kitchen. For visitors and locals alike, the roof-top Griffin Bar overlooking the East Gate, is set to become one of the most sought after social senses in the city where guests can sit outside and enjoy stunning view.

Others
The hotel’s extensive health and fitness facilities are an indoor 25-meter lap pool, the exclusive Club 501 Fitness Centre and the indulgent The JW Spa.
