								Rising tall above the Han River, the majestic 63 Building in Yeouido is home to Southern Seoul's most skybound unique venue, the 63 Convention Center. Designed by the renowned international design company PERKINS+WILL, it is quite literally the highest-class party venue for special conference, various company events, stylish weddings, and family parties, Ideal for international conferences and large events, the 2nd-floor Grand Ballroom features a large party hall taht can accommodate up to 2,000 persons, and comes equipped with the latest lighting and presentation facilities. The Grand Ballroom also features a very large seperate stage, and is connected to a heavy duty elevator-making functions of all kinds possible.
