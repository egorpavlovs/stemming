			Introduction
The JW Marriott Dongdaemun Square Seoul is located in the heart of Dongdaemun, Seoul's most popular shopping destination, and boasts 170 guest rooms, including 19 Executive View Rooms, 14 Suites and one Presidential Suite. Host business meetings or social events in our 641m² pillar-less Grand Ballroom or in one of our seven additional meeting rooms. Access to the downtown area is convenient via both subway or taxi, with the central business district only 5-10 minutes away.
