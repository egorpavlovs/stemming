		Renaissance Seoul Hotel
My Sweet City Package
 
Renaissance Seoul Hotel is promoting a special package ‘My Sweet City Package’ until November 30th for the ladies to
pursuing luxury life style in the city.
 
‘My Sweet City package’ is recommended for ladies, and it includes one of the trend dessert Éclair afternoon tea promotion for two at the Trevi Lounge, complimentary use of the in-door swimming pool, recreation center and sauna for two, and
free in-room internet access, and if guests surcharge KRW 40,000, guests can enjoy breakfast for two at Café Elysee.
Additionally, this package will offer a special present luxury spa brand ‘THANN’ travel kit. This package is perfect for ladies
with a price of KRW 190,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.).
 
The Renaissance Seoul Hotel looks forward to having you this autumn season as you make unforgettable memories
with your loved ones.
 
 
Period: Until November 30th
Price: From KRW 190,000 (Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax).
Information & Reservation: 02-2222-8500
