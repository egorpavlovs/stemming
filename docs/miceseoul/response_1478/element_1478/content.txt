			Introduction
The Riverside Hotel is an ideal base for shoppers as its location on Jamwandong puts it only a few minutes from the Itaewon shopping district. Car rental is available for visiting nearby attractions like the Hangang River or the several markets and shopping centers in the area. The The Garden Kitchen Buffet specializes in local Korean and Western cuisine and the hotel also provides morning buffet. Also providing for business travelers, the banquet hall is available for corporate functions or wedding receptions, while the conference center can host smaller meetings. With a convenient location close to the shopping district, and numerous onsite facilities, the hotel is ideal for business and leisure travelers.
