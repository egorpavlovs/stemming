		Shopping & Entertainment
		Green & Hightech
	
	
		Seoul is a shopper’s heaven. The city has numerous shopping malls, department stores, duty free shops, and traditional markets, making shopping available 24/7 all year-round. The streets of Gangnam, Cheongdam, and Shinsadong offer world famous brands and designer shops; Insadong and Namdaemun sell traditional handmade crafts and affordable products; and the Coex Mall, Myeongdong and Dongdaemun areas are among the most popular shopping spots in the city. Seoul also offers a variety of cultural entertainment such as performances, movies, plays, and musicals that run until late in the evening, which is a convenience for travelers who have time constraints. You can enjoy a wide range of performances from traditional Korean ones to non-verbal and dance performances.

			Rodeo Street
			
			The main street of Apgujeong is famous for being the center of fashion. The area offers a dynamic and exciting atmosphere with a variety of entertainment.
			Line 3, Apgujeong Station, Exit 2
		
		
			
		
		Performances
		
			Seoul Arts Center
			
			As the mecca of Korean arts, SAC houses an opera house, art gallery, music hall, and an arts library.
			Line 3, Nambu Terminal Station, Exit 5
			www.sac.or.kr
		
		
			LG Arts Center
			
			This modern multi-purpose performing arts theater was awarded the USITT Architecture Award.
			Line 2, Yeoksam Station, Exit 7
			www.lgart.com
		
		Dinner & Theater
		
			Korea House
			
			Enjoy eight types of performances including the fan dance for about one hour before and after your meal.
			Line 3, Chungmuro Station, Exit 3
			
				www.koreahouse.co.kr
			
		
		
			Pulhyanggi
			
			The restaurant offers a 1-hour traditional Korean performance and its dishes were featured in two recent Korean movies, Shi-ge-jun and Hwangjinyi.
			Line 2, Samseong Station, Exit 5
			
				www.pulhyanggi.co.kr
			
		
	

	
		Streets
		
			Insadong
			
			Recommended as the number one must-see site for foreigners, the street is full of shops selling handmade crafts, traditional items, and antiques.
			Line 1, Jonggak Station; Line 3, Anguk StationLine 5, Jongro 3-ga Station
			www.goinsadong.co.kr
		
		
			Jongno
			
			Jongno is popular with its attraction combining tradition and modernity. There are hundreds of food items, and its location near Insa-dong and Samcheong-dong as well as five palaces make it a top tourist destination.
			Line 1, Jonggak StationLine 3, Jongro 3-ga Station

			Nanta Theater
			
			This non-verbal performance consists of only rhythm, beat and a storyline, and breaks down the conventional barriers of language and can be enjoyed by everyone.
			Line 1 & 2, City Hall Station
			www.Nanta.i-pmc.co.kr
		
		
			Sejong Center for the Performing Arts
			
			Recognized as among the world’s top 10 theaters, it serves as the gateway to entering the Korean and international performing arts circle.
			
				www.sejongpac.or.kr
			
		
		
			
		
	

	
		Streets
		
			Namdaemun
			
			Namdaemun Market dates back to 600 years ago and stands as Korea’s oldest market. You can find anything here from clothes, traditional Korean items and handmade crafts, glass products.
			Line 4, Namdaemun Market Station, Exit 5
			Bars: J.J. Mahoney’s
		
		
			Dongdaemun
			
			The area is a complex of large-scale shopping malls consisting of 27,000 retailers/wholesalers. As the fashion street of Seoul, it is highly popular among the young people and offers a variety of restaurants, stage performances and a movie theater.
			Lines 2, 4, 5, Dongdaemun History & Culture Park Station, Exit 1
		
		Performances
		
			Itaewon
			
			A unique tourist zone offering restaurants and shops of various countries for foreign travelers and residents. Festivals and holidays of countries around the world are celebrated in the area.
			Line 6, Itaewon Station, Exit 2
		
		
			National Theater of Korea
			
			A variety of traditional and global performances and festivals are held all year-round at the theater.
			Line 3, Dongguk University Station, Exit 6
			www.ntok.go.kr
