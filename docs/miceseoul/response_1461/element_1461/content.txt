Overview
The Outstanding Convention center with Professionalism & Convention The Country’s Only Professional-Level University Convention Center:Unbeatable Convenience and Professionalism A modern facility set against a backdrop of the stunning Acha Mountain and the lush forest of the Children’s Grand Park, the Sejong Convention Center is an international level facility specially designed to provide “onestop” service for professional customers. Located in the 15-story Gwanggaeto building with four-levels underground on the Sejong University campus, it has a main banquet hall with a seating capacity of 2,700, 10 small- and medium-sized conference rooms, an exhibition hall, a concert hall and guest rooms. It provides a one-stop solution that effectively serves all needs of convention center customers in one place. Foods prepared and provided by a five-star hotel will add class to your event. The partnership between the banquet specialists and the University’s Hospitality and Tourism Management Department with knowledge and expertise accumulated over a long period of time will help you become a successful host of any event from international conferences to academic seminars, cultural events, concerts and to weddings. The Sejong Convention Center is operated by Sejong University. Trust us with all your convention needs.

From Operas to Concerts
A Truly Versatile Open Space for Cultural Performances
A multipurpose auditorium with a wide stage, Dae-Yang Hall at Sejong University is a cultural space at its finest that can serve as the perfect venue for corporate workshops, lectures and seminars as well as artistic performances. With a seating capacity of 2,030 and its grand exterior, it is an ideal place to stage live concerts, dance, theatre and musical performances as well as corporate workshops and seminars. Only 1 minute walk from the nearest subway station, it has unbeatably convenient access. In addition, it is located right across from the Children's Grand Park to allow visitors to enjoy nature in the middle of Seoul. The Hall has been recently renovated and boasts a new stylish, modern look. Experience being the host of a wildly successful event at the newly renovated Dae-Yang Hall of Sejong University.
