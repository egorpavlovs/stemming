Overview
SETEC, a cradle from which businesses can launch into the global market, consists of 3 exhibition halls and 3 meeting rooms with a floor space of 15,630m² inside the building, which covers a 34,600m² site. The space can be organized in many different ways to accommodate demands for various functions, including specialty exhibitions, EXPOs, international meetings, events, and performances. This world-class exhibition and convention center is fully equipped with a wide range of annex and support facilities.
FACTS

	Total Area(m²): 15,630
	No. of Meeting Rooms: 4
	From Incheon Int’l Airport: 60min. / 71.284km
	Subway Station: Line #3 Hangnyeoul Station


Conventions
SETEC has 3 exhibition halls in which various exhibitions can be held depending on the nature, size and characteristics of the exhibition. In addition, with modern convention facilities for international conferences and seminars, it offers an optimal business environment. Since SBA Trade Center in Beijing, KOTRA, Seoul City Credit, ExportImport Association and hotels are networked, one-stop service is available.

Convention Hall
Mid-sized convention halls in which all seats are arranged in a square shape, and a conference mike is installed in each seat are available. This venue is useful for various events such as directors’ seminars, business fairs and business talks as well as conventions. In addition, a professional seminar room organized by exhibition experts is also available along with an international conference hall.
