	[Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel] Autumn Refresh Package

	Writer : Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel		 / Date : 2014/10/20 10:42 / Modified Date 2014/10/20 10:43	

	

						
												
		ENJOY AN AUTUMN ESCAPE WITH AFTERNOON TEA, ‘AUTUMN REFRESH PACKAGE’Relax overlooking the clear autumn sky from the guest room of Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel   Seoul, September 1, 2014- Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel presents ‘Autumn Refresh Package’ including two Afternoon Tea Sets with views of the clear autumn sky for guests seeking a relaxing getaway.Guests can relax in the beautifully appointed rooms overlooking the high autumn sky from the 29th floor and 40th floor of Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel. A choice of coffee or tea and a variety of desserts with the Afternoon Tea Sets on its signature tiered stand will be delivered to the room, where guests can enjoy a private time with their family, friend or loved one. To experience deep relaxation and rejuvenation, Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel’s Aequalis Spa offers a special discount of KRW 30,000 for body massage and KRW 10,000 for a facial treatment. Aequalis Spa’s concierge team can assist in recommending the perfect type of spa treatment to aid the skin for the changing weather in autumn. ‘Autumn Refresh Package’ includes a one night stay at Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel, and free use of hotel gym and swimming pool. ‘Autumn Refresh Package’ includes an in-room Afternoon Tea Set for two persons, including a cup of coffee or tea. The package starts from September 15th to November 30, and price begins from KRW 250,000 (exclusive of 10% VAT).[‘Autumn Refresh Package’]Period: September 15~ November 30, 2014Price: from KRW 250,000 (*exclusive of 10% VAT)Package Details: - One(1) night stay in a Deluxe Room- Two(2) Afternoon Tea Sets in room- Aequalis Spa body massage KRW 30,000 discount coupon, Facial treatment KRW 10,000 discount coupon- Complimentary use of Sheraton Fitness and swimming poolInformation & Reservations: 02.2211.2100www.sheraton.com/seouldcubecity
