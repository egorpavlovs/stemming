BLEU DIAMOND MEMBERSHIP PROGRAM

LAUNCHED AT MILLENNIUM SEOUL HILTON

 

13 January 2016, Seoul, Korea… Millennium Seoul Hilton is excited to
launch its brand new Bleu Diamond Membership program starting January 1st, 2016.  A privileged membership program exclusive to
Millennium Seoul Hilton, Bleu Diamond members will be treated to a wide variety
of preferential discounts, complimentary coupons, gift certificates and other
perks.  Enjoy the epitome of VIP
treatment with this exclusive membership! 

 

Aimed for modern professionals who aspire
to live a lifestyle enjoying gourmet foods and ultimate relaxation, this
membership program lets you avail of valuable discounts and perks at the hotel
and with our partner organizations.

 

Blue Diamond members will be able to
choose from two of the following three perks. 


1 certificate for a complimentary 1-night
stay in a Deluxe City View Room (some restrictions apply)2 certificates each worth KW50,000 for use
at the restaurants/guest room (some restrictions apply)1 certificate for buffet for 2 persons
(some restrictions apply) The following perks are also available for
all Bleu Diamond members. 









Discounts (10%-33% depending on number of
guests) when dining/drinking at one of the hotel’s restaurants or bar 2 complimentary entrance tickets for use
at the Executive Health Club5% discount on catered food for a booked banquet
event (party, meeting, wedding, etc.)40% discount off of published room ratesDiscounts at the hotel’s partnered
establishments (including Hilton Namhae Golf and Spa Resort, Hilton Gyeongju,
Haeundae Grand Hotel, Elysian Gangchon/Jeju, Cha-eum, Seoul University Hospital
Healthcare System Gangnam Center, Lao Lao Bay Golf and Resort, etc.)  The cost of membership is KW430,000 for
one year.  A premium membership is also
available at the cost of KW800,000 for one year.  We invite you to become a Bleu Diamond member
and start enjoying the perks and exclusive privileges that come along with VIP
membership! 

















For
additional enquiries, please call (02) 317-3280. Prices quoted above are
already inclusive of tax and service charge.
