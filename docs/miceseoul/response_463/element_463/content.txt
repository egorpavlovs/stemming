		Introduction
Hotel Prima Seoul is a 4 Star Korean domestic brand promoting a new concept of hospitality: hotel of culture and art. From the moment one enters our hotel, he or she can get a unique sense of mixture between Western and Eastern cultures. Hotel Prima also proudly displays, throughout the entirety of the hotel, the works of various genres and artists from all around the globe. Moreover, during your stay, please do not forget about our exceptional oriental spas and Chinese dishes at Gong Restaurant prepared by top chefs.
