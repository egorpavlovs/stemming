								aT Center, Seoul’s most favored cultural and recreational venue, consists of exhibition and conference spaces, which are fully-equipped with state-of-the-art AV systems and storage for any type of meetings: conferences, seminars, banquets, events and performances, and exhibitions. Also, with a simultaneous interpretation system available, this venue offers a complete package to meeting planners for a successful event.

	2 Exhibition Halls ? Total of 8047 sqm. (382 booths)
	Grand Hall ? 400 pax
	8 meeting rooms

Specialized facilities include: Agro-Trade Information Center, Export Publicity Pavilion, which offers a variety of export support, ranging from year-round exhibition of high potential export product promotion, counseling & trade partner mediation for foreign buyers, and up-to-date overseas market information, all in a convenient one-stop service.
Within the center’s premises are 3 restaurants and a cafe, a flower shop, a convenient store, and a stationery store.
