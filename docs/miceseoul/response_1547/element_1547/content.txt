        Namsangol Hanok VillageLocated in the heart of Namsan Mountain, Namsangol Hanok Village(NHV) comprises five hanok buildings, the Gukakdang(Traditional Music Theater), Traditional Garden, and Seoul Millennium Time Capsule Square. The NHV offers diverse cultural programs both traditional and contemporary, and tries to incorporate them in a harmonious way. By doing so, we hope to help visitors reflect on Korean tradition from a fresh perspective, discover its hidden values, and ultimately, make the past feel closer and more relevant.A Space in Seoul Dedicated to Korean Traditional Arts and CultureNamsangol Hanok Village(NHV) provides a wide range of programs and events designed to offer fresh insight into Korea's traditional culture through music
