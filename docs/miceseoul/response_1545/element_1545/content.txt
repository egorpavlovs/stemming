        Located within the distinctive southern Seoul multi-island complex of Some 
Sevit on the isle of Some Gavit, Floating Island Convention (FIC) is a stylish 
meeting space offering facilities suitable for all kinds of business events, 
including international conferences, cultural performances, and much more. FIC’s 
dome-style main banquet hall is able to accommodate up to 500 persons, affords 
stunning views of the Hangang (River), and features an 18m-wide ANSI screen, 
as well as a car elevator for motor shows. Catering is provided by an adjacent 
kitchen facility staffed by culinary professionals able to provide a wide range 
of menus to suit various needs. Dining services can also be provided by the 
medieval-inspired European restaurant Villa de Noche, which offers a private 
balcony for more intimate social events such as small parties or even pre- or 
post-event gatherings.
