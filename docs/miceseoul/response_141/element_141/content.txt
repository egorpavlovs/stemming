								KOREALINX, the only performing arts travel agency in Korea, provides concert planning, inbound/outbound tour consultation and international cultural exchange management. Our KOREALINX Tour Manager works with you to determine your group’s tour goals, budget, size, travel dates and itinerary. We have arranged tours for some of the most prestigious performing arts tour groups from home and abroad, from local community choirs to nationally-recognized symphony orchestras. Whether you are an amateur or a professional, wherever you are from, and whatever music you perform, KOREALINX will create a customized tour best suited for your group’s needs.
We know that a great performance of something even as short as a three- minute motet requires hours of rehearsal. So relax in the knowledge that while you prepare for your concerts in Korea, we will take care of all the technical and logistical details, securing the performance opportunities your group desires, and balancing your performance, sightseeing and cultural schedules.
