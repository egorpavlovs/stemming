		Indulge in a Taste of Korea at the 2009 IT & CMA’s Seoul Soiree.
		2009-10-07
		
			
			Indulge in a Taste of Korea at the 2009 IT & CMA's Seoul Soiree.Be sure not to miss the upcoming Seoul Soiree dinner on October 7th from 6 to 9 pm at this year's IT & CMA held in the Grand Ballroom at Bangkok's Central World.  Hosted by the Seoul Tourism Organization (STO) this dinner event will showcase Han Style (Korean/한/(한자?) style) in one spectacular evening filed with traditional music, fashion, fine dining, and art. Feast on Asia is this year's event theme, but now you'll really have the chance to feast on a selection of delicacies that Seoul has to offer. Guests will be treated to the sights, sounds and savory dishes of Korea at this unique event. Come and experience warm hospitality and Han Style during an exciting evening with the elegance and splendor of traditional Korean clothing (Han-bok) artistically blended together with romantic traditional Korean music and mouthwatering Korean cuisine (Han-sik). You will also have the opportunity to enjoy the beauty of Korean Paper (Han-ji), traditional Korean architecture and building design (Hanok) as well as the Korean language (Hangul).The evening will also provide a great opportunity to discover and understand why Seoul has become one of the world's top MICE destinations. The Seoul Convention Bureau's (SCB) friendly and knowledgeable will provide information and an informative presentation about excitement and adventure that Seoul has to offer including incentives, and many new support programs for your next successful event. Come and get a taste of Seoul and experience a uniquely Korean event which promises to delight all of the senses, and find out what makes Seoul your Complete Convention City!   For more information please contact Martin Kim at the Seoul Convention Bureau (+82 2 3788-0822, martin@seoulwelcome.com), the IT & CMA website (http://itcma.com.sg) or visit Seoul's booth at this year's IT & CMA in Bangkok, Thailand (6 - 8 October 2009)
