								The Korea Society of Management Information Systems is the nation’s leading body on IS, or the study of complementary networks of hardware and software that people and organizations use to collect, filter, process, create and distribute data. Under the theme of “ICT Convergence in the Era of Creative Economy, the society focuses upon the sharing of IT, strategies and policies for new leaps in information and communications technology resources, and management innovation-related industries. It is the official organizer of the 2017 International Conference on Information Systems (ICIS), which will take place in Seoul at the Coex convention and exhibition center.Held annually in different countries on a rotational basis, ICIS is the largest meeting of the Association for Information Systems (AIS), which has over 4,000 members representing universities in over 95 countries worldwide. It is the most prestigious gathering of academics and practitioners in the IS discipline, and provides a forum for networking and knowledge-sharing amongst the IS profession. Each year, over 1,000 IS academic professionals from around the world participate in the conference program, which includes about 60 sessions and 200 presentations, in addition to keynote speakers, CIO panels and research panels.ICIS 2017 is expected to include participants from South Korea, the US, Canada, the UK, Australia, China, Singapore and more than 1,200 researchers and practitioners worldwide.
