	Belle-Essence Seoul Hotel_Spring Weekend Package

	Writer : Belle-Essence (Former Renaissance Seoul Hotel)		 / Date : 2016/3/04 16:45 / Modified Date 2016/3/04 16:46	

	

						
												
		

Former Renaissance Seoul Hotel, Belle-Essence Seoul Hotel

Relaxing in the City ‘Spring
Weekend Package’
Various packages suit for
different guests and different occasions, including diverse benefitrom room
service and many moreBelle-Essence Seoul Hotel is promoting 3 kinds of ‘Spring Weekend Package’ from March 4th to May 29th
forguests looking for a relaxing and refreshing time during the weekends
in this spring season.Three different Spring
Weekend Packages are offered and all include complimentary use of in-door
swimming pool, Belle-Essence fitness center and sauna as general benefits.The “Stay Alone Package” is relaxing
break for enjoy-along guests with an affordable and cost-saving option which
includes one night in a deluxe room, complimentary breakfast for one at the Café
Elysee, free in-room internet access and late check-out till 3pm at KRW 160,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10%
tax.).The ‘My Sweet Spring Package’ is
recommended sweet couples or friends and includes one night deluxe room,
complimentary breakfast for two at the buffet restaurant Café Elysee, in-room
one bottle of red wine, premium praline 4 pieces with 2 cupcakes and free
in-room internet access. This package is KRW210,000(Exclusive
of 10% service charge and 10% tax.). The ‘Family Together Spring Package’ is recommended for families, and it includes a
night in a superior deluxe room, complimentary breakfast for two at the Café
Elysee(two children under the age of 12 dine for free), as well as a complimentary
one margherita pizza, and 2 soft drinks. Guests can experience one of the
Chinese restaurant Kibin’s signature menus such as sweet and sour pork with 2
crab meat soup per table. This is perfect for families with a price of KRW250,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.)In addition, this
package gives one night autumn package for two people through random drawings. Get the big chance and make
new memories with your loved ones in this spring season.Period: From March 4th to May 29th (Fri, Sat, Sun & public holidays)Price: From KRW160,000 (Exclusive of 10%
service charge and 10% tax.)Information & Reservation: 02-2222-8500
