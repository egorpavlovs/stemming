Floating Island was
cornerstone in Seoul’s Han River Renaissance project. It is first artificial
island in the world covering 9,629m2 which located in Sevit-Some of Hanriver
making it a landmark of Seoul. The huge artificial island structure is built on
a floating platform. This is characterized by an aluminum metal panel shell
that is delicately enclosed with metalframed petals. After 7 pm, all LED lights
in windows will provide an amazing ambiance to amplify the beauty of the Han
River. And also every 30 minutes, you can see the rainbow fountain waterfall
show from the Banpo-bridge, there are Main Convention Hall and Villa de Noche
on 2nd floor, marvelous panoramic view Vista and Rive Gauche on 3rd floor, we
proudly host 5 banquet halls. Highly technical video and sound system equipped Convention hall is designed
for International Conference, Banquet, Seminar, Launch show and much more. The
white dome shaped hall has 13m high ceilings, which could accommodate up 500
people for an event. Villa de Noche is an ideal place to hold receptions with
champagne or cocktail before and after the event. The 3rd floor consists of the
Vista, where there is a panoramic view and the Rive Gauche, where you discover
the Rainbow Waterfall Show. The 4th and 5th floor can accommodate up to 2000
people standing. We offer a barbeque party on this terrace on the Bay our
Catering service is provided by an adjacent kitchen facility staffed by
culinary professionals who are able to provide a wide range of menus to suit
various needs including vegetarian, Western/Asian course meal, Halal and so
forth. Our outstanding chefs and service staffs in line with high quality
facilities will promise you to make the best experience and do our best to
satisfy your expectation beyond imagination. We are perfectly ready to welcome
all of your event.
