        KT&G SangsangMadang opened in September 2007 at the Picaso Street near  Hongik Univ. and it is a multi-culture complex with seven stories above ground  and four below. KT&G SangsangMadang was established in order to support  artists for their creative activities and to provide the public with opportunities  to enjoy cultures in the space for the purpose of cultivating and exchanging  artistic activities including a movie theater, a concert hall, a specialized  design shop, a gallery, an academy, a studio and a cafe.
