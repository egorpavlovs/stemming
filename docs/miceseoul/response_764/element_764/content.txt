	Welcome to ISTC-Korea Forum on Nanotechnology 

	Writer : EurasTech Corp.		 / Date : 2015/8/12 16:47 / Modified Date 2015/8/18 17:44	

	

														
		On November 3d, 2015 Eurastech will organize ISTC-Korea "NanoCon" forum on 
nanotechnology hosted by the ROK Ministry of Science, ICT and Future 
planning and supported by National Research Foundation of Korea. 

NANOCON
Seoul is an interdisciplinary international conference that invites academics
and independent scholars, researchers and business people from all over the
world and CIS to meet and exchange latest ideas and views on nanotechnology in
a forum encouraging respectful dialogue. 

NanoCon
serves as an outstanding platform that gathers the relevant communities
together, an international forum for researchers and industry practitioners to
exchange the latest fundamental advances and in the field of nanotechnology,
energy and other. Related experts and scholars will discuss academic issues,
seize future development opportunities and exchange the latest researchers
and academic thinking. Experts, representatives of business community and
policy makers will present the latest innovations and advances on
nanotechnology, share the experience and insights and discuss the policy,
economics and social implications.



Korean companies & research institutions interested in exchanges in nanotechnology  field are invited to participate on a first-come-first-served basis. Inquiries and more 
info: www.eurastech.com EurasTech is a consulting firm focused on commercializing and 
exporting of technology as well as MICE Industry in Russia and CIS 
region. With a growing interest in Russia from local businesses, our role
 has become significant. We strive to provide good quality services. On 
April 7th, 2014, a subsidiary EurasTech Rus was established in Russia 
to enhance the ROK – Russia business relations. As a PCO, EurasTech 
organizes exhibitions,academic conferences, presentation sessions, 
seminars, and other meetings and events to connect Korean and Eurasian 
businesses. Besides that  EurasTech provide market research, buyer 
matching programs, exhibition and convention promotion,consultation and 
marketing/PR services to domestic state and private firms, Russian mid-sized firms,
 governmental organizations, research institutes, and universities. Pls use official forum hashtag #nanocon if you wish to twit abt or from forum. Thanks!
