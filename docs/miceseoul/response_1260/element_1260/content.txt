		[Conworld] Seoul to Host Over 10,000 Participants for Nuclear Security Summit
		2012-02-06
		
			
			[original article found on http://Conworld.net] 
The South Korean capital is gearing up to welcome 55 heads of state and international organizations next month for the 2012 Seoul Nuclear Security Summit.
The event will be held at Seoul’s COEX Convention Center, which has also served as host for the 2010 G-20 Seoul Summit.
Similar to the positive response following the G-20 Seoul Summit, the successful conclusion of the 2012 Seoul Nuclear Security Summit is expected to showcase the city’s meetings infrastructure to the world and bolster the city’s reputation as the world’s number five convention destination...
