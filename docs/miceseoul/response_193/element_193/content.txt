Namsan Seoul Tower
Hanbok Culture Experience Center is located on the lobby level(B1F) of Seoul’s
landmark Namsan Seoul Tower. The center is designed around the theme of the
Joseon Dynasty and features both large photo zones and hanbok of various
styles, ranging from traditional to mofern and even wedding.

Namsan Seoul Tower Hanbok Culture Experience Center offers a wide range of
experiences related to Korean traditional culture, including hanbok,
traditional weddings, traditional architecture, traditional refereshments(tea
& snacks) and Korean food.

Wear hanbok and become the main character of Korean historical dramas at Namsan
Seoul Tower Hanbok Culture Experience Center.
