	HOLIDAY DINING DELIGHTS

	Writer : Millennium Seoul Hilton		 / Date : 2015/12/15 10:06	

	

						
												
		

HOLIDAY DINING DELIGHTS

 

15 December 2015, Seoul, Korea… Several of Millennium Seoul Hilton’s acclaimed restaurants will be offering special dinners
to help you celebrate the Christmas and New Year holidays with family and
colleagues.  

 

At Genji, Millennium Seoul Hilton’s impeccable Japanese restaurant, diners
will be treated to a special feast on Christmas Eve and New Year’s Eve.  The eight-course meal will be comprised of
traditional Japanese foods that will help bring prosperity and good luck to you
in the new year.    The incredible dining
experience will include: an appetizer of abalone, shishamo and salmon;
swellfish soup; hamachi, halibut, sea bream and salmon sashimi; grilled black
cod and fresh eel; steamed sea bream; assorted sushi, and much more!  The dinner is priced at KW120,000 and is only
available on Christmas Eve (December 24th) and New Year’s Eve
(December 31st).  

 

Seasons, Millennium Seoul Hilton’s
acclaimed French restaurant, will host a truly special New Year’s Eve menu for
December 31st.  Priced at
KW150,000, the supremely epicurean six-course dinner will include: poached
prawn tails with Beluga caviar and sweet and sour mango sauce, served on an
edible flower; sautéed Foie Gras with green leaf salad and a port wine balsamic
sauce; U.S. beef tenderloin and Maine lobster served with green asparagus and
tomato linguini; and so much more!

 

Il Ponte, Millennium Seoul Hilton’s popular
Italian restaurant, is also offering a specially-crafted dinner to help diners
celebrate the end of 2015 and get 2016 off to a good start. The celebratory
meal will feature: grilled fennel and marinated lobster with sundried tomato
salsa and gazpacho; squid ink tortelli filled with shrimp and ricotta cheese in
saffron sauce; a choice of  grilled beef
tenderloin on radicchio in Chianti red wine and served with a shallot sauce or
sole fillet roll on grilled tomato with tomato concasse and lemon butter
sauce.  The six-course meal will only be
made available on December 31st and January 1st, and is
priced at KW125,000. 



 

Start
the year 2016 off with a great gastronomic celebration.  Enjoy a memorable dining experience with
friends or valued colleagues.  Welcome
the new year and all the possibilities it brings with it.  

 

For
reservations or enquiries, please call Genji at (02) 317-3240, Seasons at (02)
317-3060, or Il Ponte at (02) 317-3270. All prices quoted above are inclusive
of tax and service charge.  Advance
reservations are recommended.
