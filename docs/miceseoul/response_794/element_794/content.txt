	[Imperial Palace Seoul]  Winter Package ‘Wake your sensibility Package’

	Writer : Imperial Palace Seoul		 / Date : 2014/11/12 9:43	

	

						
												
		Imperial Palace Seoul presents the Winter Package,
‘Wake your sensibility Package’
 
Imperial Palace Seoul presents the winter package, ‘Wake your sensibility Package’, for enjoying a comfortable stay at the hotel while waking sensibility in sleep with a unique perfume. The package is available from November 29, 2014 to February 28, 2015 (except for December 24, 2014). 
 
‘Wake your sensibility Package’ includes one-night stay in a comfortable room, free access to a fitness center and a swimming pool, breakfasts for two, and a bottle of perfume  ‘Sanctus for the almighty’ in collaboration between a fragrance brand ‘N+1’ and a designer ‘KAAL E.SUKTAE’. The perfume ‘Sanctus for the almighty’ is made of fragrance of wood, musk, frankincense and myrrh in the Bible. The perfume is a unisex perfume. 
 
Especially, if the package’s guests choose a suite room (a duplex suite, or a corner suite room), they can be offered ‘Special Champagne Set’, which is made of a bottle of Champagne ‘Alenya Cava Brut’, an assorted cheese platter, assorted chocolates, and a mini cake, at lobby lounge Delmar.  
 
In addition, when choosing club deluxe, duplex suite, or corner suite room, traditional Korean-style house concept’s Club Imperial Lounge’s various benefits (including the sauna, Internet service, breakfasts for two, day snacks, and happy hour service, etc.) are offered. 
 
The package price starts from 220,000 won (excluding taxes and service charges). 
 
For reservation and inquiries:  (02)3440-8000 / www.imperialpalace.co.kr
