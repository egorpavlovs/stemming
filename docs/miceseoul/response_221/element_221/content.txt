								The Westin Chosun
Seoul is a premier five-star luxury hotel located conveniently in the heart of
Seoul’s vibrant financial and shopping districts. Distinguished by world-class
facilities and exceptionally personalized services, The Westin Chosun Seoul has
been chosen as the “Best Business Hotel in Seoul” by FinanceAsia and
“Recommended Hotel” by Forbes Travel Guide. Ideally situated in the heart of
downtown Seoul, The Westin Chosun Seoul is walking distance to the headquarters
of global companies, embassies and the historic landmarks such as Deoksugung
and Gyeongbokgung, Seoul City Hall, Cheonggye Stream and Gwanghwamun Square.Two spacious
Ballrooms and five function rooms, which are equipped with state-of-the-art
lighting and sound system, as well as the detailed decor, can accommodate a
various types of events from business conferences to press events, galas to
social gatherings. Our dedicated staff will ensure that your event is a
success, right down to the smallest details. The Orchid Ballroom and function
rooms on the 2nd floor provide modern and sophisticated ambience with a touch
of Korean motifs.The luxurious and
commodious guest rooms offer the leisurely comforts of home combined with
incredible business convenience. A delightful dining experience is also
guaranteed at distinctively attractive Japanese Sushi Cho, Chinese Hong Yuan,
buffet Aria, Italian OsteriaRubrica, The Ninth Gate Wine & Dine
restaurants, Chosun Deli bakery and Lounge & Bar.
