			Introduction
Hosting numerous state visits and international events, The Shilla Seoul is consistently voted South Korea's top hotel by influential publications, most recently ranked among the 'World's Best Hotels' by Institutional Investor and 'World's Top 500 Hotels' by Travel + Leisure. In addition, it is a member of The Leading Hotels of the World. With stunning views of Mt. Namsan from a private 23-acre sculpture park in the heart of Seoul, The Shilla Seoul remains one of Asia's most admired hotel groups renowned for luxurious accommodation with state-of-the-art facilities.
