	The Ritz-Carlton, Seoul unveils 'Winter Grill at The Garden' promotion

	Writer : The Ritz-Carlton Seoul		 / Date : 2015/11/05 10:11 / Modified Date 2015/11/05 10:11	

	

						
												
		 
 
The Ritz-Carlton, Seoul unveils 'Winter Grill at The Garden' promotion
 
 
 
The Garden, the all-day fine dining restaurant at The Ritz-Carlton, Seoul, presents Winter Grill at The Garden, the perfect chance to enjoy a selection of grilled dishes with friends, family or your partner. 
 
This seasonal promotion includes unlimited servings of boned ribs, lamb ribs and king prawns on one plate, plus a course menu of Spanish-style gambas, super salad, tomato soup and grilled lobster, roast potato and aubergine, blueberry pot and grilled lemon cheesecake. The Winter Grill menu is priced at 85,000 won per person (including tax and service charge), with unlimited servings of wine and beer for available for a supplementary charge of 30,000 won. 
 
“This promotion combines a grill menu popular among families and company workers with unlimited beer and wine,” says Kim Ju-hwan of The Garden. “We have a lot of bookings and inquiries for weekend gatherings and company events.” 
 
The Garden, a secret oasis of greenery in the heart of the city, seats 124 guests indoors and 126 outdoors. Its European-style outdoor area combines water features with luxuriant trees and foliage, creating the ideal atmosphere for a special dinner. 
 
 
Inquiries and reservations: 02) 3451-8271
