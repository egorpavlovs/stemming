								An outstanding, professional university convention center A modern facility with the stunning Acha Mountain and the lush forest of the Children’s Grand Park in the backdrop, the Sejong Convention Center is an international-level facility specially designed to provide a full “one-stop” service for professional customers.Located in the 15-story Gwanggaeto building on the Sejong University campus, the convention center encompasses four-levels underground.It has a main banquet hall with a seating capacity of 2,700, 6 small- and medium-sized conference rooms, an exhibition hall, a concert hall and guest rooms.Catering is provided by chefs with five-star hotel restaurant backgrounds to add class to your event. With many years of experience, the banquet specialists and the University’s Hospitality and Tourism Management Department will help ensure the successful hosting of any event, from international conferences to academic seminars, and cultural events.The Sejong Convention Center is operated by Sejong University and our professional and experienced team will fully support your event to its success.
