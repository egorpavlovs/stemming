        Walking On The Cloud takes your dining events skybound at the iconic 63 Square 
tower in Yeouido, central Seoul. The facility includes the European-style Garden 
Restaurant, overlooking the Hangang (River) and whose chefs can prepare a range 
of authentic European dishes including tenderloin steak, lobster, and roast 
lamb. There is also a wine and whisky bar with seating next to stunning views 
of Seoul’s night scenery.
