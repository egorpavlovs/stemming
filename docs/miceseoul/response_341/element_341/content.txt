								Since its foundation in 1983, Gana Art Gallery has organized over 600 exhibitions, representing both Korean and international artists. In that time, it has established itself as a truly multi-cultural institution. Gana Art Gallery features 3 state of the art exhibition halls, an outdoor performance stage, an art shop, a full service restaurant and a seminar room. The three exhibition halls are divided into regular and special exhibition halls. A 300-seat outdoor stage is available for various events such as concerts, plays, movies, fashion shows and performance art. In addition, the Gana Art Center offers an art shop in which art pieces are on display and for sale.
