								Located in the heart of metropolitan city of Seoul, Grand Ambassador Seoul associated with Pullman overlooks the magnificent cityscape while access to the major shopping center including Myeongdong, Namdaemun, Itaewon, Dongdaemun and business district is incomparable. The 413 guestrooms at the hotel are decorated in a modern style while featuring Nespresso coffee machines with daily complimentary capsules, Ambassador bedding and pillow selection and premium amenities. The hotel’s 12 versatile banquet rooms are various in size. The event rooms can be the perfect venue from large-scale events and small business meetings. Also, the Business Center is equipped to provide confidential secretarial service for successful business. The Fitness Center offers an opportunity to relax with facilities including indoor swimming pool, indoor golf range, gym and sauna. The hotel has 5 restaurants and a bar offering all-day-dining buffet, Korean contemporary dining, authentic Chinese and Japanese cuisines and coffee shop with bakery. Whether guests are in Seoul for business or leisure, the hotel will be the ideal place for comfortable and unforgettable stay.
