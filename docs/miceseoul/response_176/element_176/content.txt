								Rising tall above the Han River in Central Seoul, the 63 Building is both a major urban landmark and a popular multi-complex in Yeouido, home to the new International Finance Center development. Offering something for everyone, the 63 Building has a large selection of shopping options from a range of well-known brand outlets, as well as a large international food court where can be found all kinds of Korean and international cuisine.
For entertainment, the 63 Building has something for everyone, including an IMAX 3D movie theater, 63 Sea World, the 63 Sky Art Hall, and the 63 Wax Museum. It's also a one-of-a-kind unique venue for events, with the 63 Convention Center providing a variety of differently-sized meeting spaces, serviced by highly-professional staff.
