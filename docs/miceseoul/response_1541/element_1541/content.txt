        Ibis Styles Ambassador Myeongdong is the first hotel to open under the
name of Ibis Styles, the most stylish & trendiest Ibis brand boasting of
more than 1,750 hotels worldwide. 180 guestrooms offer big comfort
and the Rooftop Bar & Restaurant on the 21F provide expansive view
along with the attractions that Seoul has to offer. Located at the heart
of business and tourism in Myeongdong, with easy access to the
airport bus stop and subway station. Le style restaurant &bar located
on the 21st floor of the hotel, It is a modern and stylish rooftop bar that
provides a grand cityscape along the skyline of Myeong-dong and N
Seoul Tower.
