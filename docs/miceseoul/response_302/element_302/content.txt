corporate training programs as well as interpretation/translation services. Conventions,
forums, and symposiums are examples of events we provide interpretation
services for.

Embracing the C.E.O. mindset with experience in communication, second language
education and interpretation, it is our top priority to provide practical,
efficient language services to businesses and individuals in Korea and worldwide.

knowledge of each language.  As part of
our mission, we promise to support your growing MICE industry and your business
communications with utmost accuracy in the light of the cultural differences
and unique features of each language.
