JACK WORLD is an Inbound Tour and
Travel Agency which located in Seoul, South Korea. We are specialized in
Southeast Asia customers, particularly for the Indonesian market. 
Our Vision is to be the most trusted and reliable inbound tour in South Korea.
Our Mission is to deliver cost-efficient, reliable and joyful tour experience.
To achieve our mission, we are supported by experienced operational teams who
have various experiences in handling VIPs, FITs, Series and Incentive Groups
for more than 10 years. Our in-house local guides are excellent in hospitality,
rich in knowledge, fluent in Bahasa Indonesia, and experienced in taking our
Indonesian client across South Korea.
Our hotels and restaurants network are vast across South Korea, and they serve
not only the best taste in food but in hospitality as well. Furthermore, we
have our own bus transportation fleet of various sizes. They are all equipped
with free-wifi service, entertainment system, always maintained regularly, and
ready to bring our client across South Korea in style, comfortable and most
importantly, safely to the destinations. Our client will experience South Korea
as it meant to be experienced, an elegant combination of traditional
hospitality with a modern taste that suits our customers.

and joyful travel experience for our client. That is our commitment, this
commitment of JACK WORLD.
