	[Renaissance Seoul Hotel] Unlimited Pleasure @ Club Horizon

	Writer : Renaissance Seoul Hotel		 / Date : 2014/12/16 16:08	

	

						
								
								
		 
Renaissance Seoul Hotel
Club Horizon ‘Unlimited Pleasure’ Promotion
Unlimited wine with 6 different snack menus
 
Renaissance Seoul Hotel’s sky lounge bar Club Horizon presents new ‘Unlimited Pleasure’ promotion where you can enjoy unlimited wine with a delicious selection of snack menus at a reasonable price.
 
Located on the 23rd floor with arch-domed glass ceiling, additionally famous for movie, and drama site, Club Horizon provides a comfortable and relaxing space for guests looking for a place to enjoy a quite evening. Every evening starting from 5pm, guests can enjoy unlimited wine served with six different kinds of snack menus. Snack menus include freshly salad, smoked salmon canapé, mini pizza, chicken wings, assorted sausage, seasonal fresh fruits and assorted cheese plate. Also, a selection of wines made from such different grape varieties as Chardonnay, Cabernet Sauvignon, Shiraz, Pinot noir and more are offered for enjoyment. If you choosing unlimited beer, local bottled beers Cass and Dry Finish are provided. ‘Unlimited Pleasure’ promotion is available from 5pm to 9pm from Monday through Sunday. Price is KRW65,000 for unlimited wine & snack. (Inclusive of 10% service charge & 10% tax.)
 
Come to Club Horizon in the evening with your friends and colleagues and enjoy this great offer to the fullest.
 
Period: Ongoing 
Time: Monday ~ Sunday – 5pm ~ 9pm
Price: Unlimited wine & snack – KRW65,000
          (All prices are inclusive of service charge and tax.)
Information & Reservation: 02-2222-8639
