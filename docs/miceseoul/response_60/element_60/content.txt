	Writer : Millennium Seoul Hilton		 / Date : 2016/1/13 9:11	

	

						
												
		

LUNAR NEW YEAR GIFT SETS ON SALE 

 

13 January 2016, Seoul, Korea… In anticipation of the
Lunar New Year gift giving season, Millennium Seoul Hilton is offering several
luxury gift sets for sale from now until February 6th (February 4th for
deliveries outside of Seoul).  Ideal
gifts for work colleagues, clients and treasured family members, these gift sets
are a wonderful way to wish others a very happy new year.  

 

In addition to the classic best-sellers,
we have launched new items including: a premium lobster, caviar and wine
hamper; a T-Bone Steak and wine hamper, and a smoked salmon hamper.  

 

Top-grade
Galbi Gift Sets (KW380,000-KW930,000)

These gift sets offer prime-grade unfrozen
Hanwoo beef and prime U.S. beef.  The
gift sets come with Millennium Seoul Hilton’s own marinade.

 

Yellow
Croaker with Roe Gift Sets (KW400,000-KW720,000)



These
yellow croaker are filled with roe, and are similar to the ones served at the
Emperor’s own dinner table.  Available in
various sizes, these premium gift sets make a marvelous gift.

 

Premium Food Gift Sets (KW200,000-KW600,000)

Premium
sets include: a hamper with 2 Canadian lobsters paired with Beluga caviar and
Perrier-Jouet Brut champagne; Soy Sauce Marinated Crabs; a hamper with 6 T-Bone
Steaks and Chateau Camensac Grand Cru Classe wine; and smoked salmon with
capers and horseradish dressing. These sets are succulent to eat and will be
appreciated by the gourmet diner. 

 

Premium Wine Gift Sets (KW1,650,000)

A
variety of the world’s finest wines for the true connoisseur.

 

Wine Gift Sets (KW150,000-KW330,000)

A
variety of sommelier sets have been prepared that will delight any wine lover.  

 

Champagne Gift Sets (KW200,000/KW300,000)

A
choice of Moet & Chandon, Dom Perignon and Veuve Clicquot are available to
help make someone’s new year that much more of a celebration.

 

Deli Gift Sets (KW450,000-KW700,000)

A
variety of gourmet gift sets have been created to help the gift recipient enjoy
the finer things in life.

 

Nationwide
delivery is available and included in the cost of the gift sets.  Orders made by 6pm on Thursday, February 4th will
be guaranteed delivery anywhere on the peninsula (excluding Jeju-do).  Orders made from February 4th through the 6th can
only be delivered in the Seoul area.   All
prices are already inclusive of 10% VAT. 
Please note that there are delivery limitations for certain hamper
items.

 

For
Lunar New Year Hamper enquiries, please call (02) 317-3066.
