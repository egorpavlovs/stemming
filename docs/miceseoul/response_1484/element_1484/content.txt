			Introduction
The Mayfield Hotel & Resort is the perfect high-class accommodation facility for those needing quick and easy access to both Gimpo and Incheon International Airport. A center of natural beauty within the city limits, the Mayfield is close by the AREX airport rail link and less than half an hour from the KINTEX convention center. All of which makes this European-style five star hotel the perfect secluded getaway for business travelers.

Special Features
Re-opened after undergoing renovation in May 2011, the Mayfield Hotel Banquet facilities consist of a grand banquet hall, the Mayfield Ballroom, and five other stunning banquet halls. The Banquet facilities offer different occupancy capacities and boast a state-of-the-art facility that will satisfy both class and style fitting for any type of occasion or event such as weddings, international meetings, business conferences or family parties. Also, sophisticated services, a modernized and unique atmosphere and a beautiful nature view are all promises made to each and every one of our guests and visitors and to be only found at Mayfield Hotel. Furthermore, the Mayfield possesses an outdoor driving range and Par 3 Golf, and this is the only Hotel as a five star on in South Korea.
