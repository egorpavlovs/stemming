								T-money is the ultimate in payment convenience. A versatile and rechargeable form of smart card, T-money can be deployed in lieu of cash on every kind of public transport, including buses, subways and taxis. T-money can also be used to pay parking and tunnel fees, and even for goods and services in vending machines, participating convenience stores and businesses. T-money is also a fully-integrated medium of payment, available in a wide range of forms, such as cards, mobile phones, accessories, watches, USB sticks, and much more.
Visitors to Korea can purchase and charge their T-money devices at convenience stores, Incheon/Gimpo International Airports, subway station, tourism information offices, and major tourism districts in Seoul. Businesses or machines accepting T-money payment will have signs with the T-money logo.
