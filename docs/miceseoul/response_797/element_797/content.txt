	2014 Drawing Show News  - The original drawing show is in a high school textbook

	Writer : Drawingshow		 / Date : 2014/8/18 12:18 / Modified Date 2014/8/18 12:25	

	

														
		

Original Drawing show

오리지널드로잉쇼

 + since 2008



* What is the ‘Drawing show’? 

Wonderful integration of arts and a stage! The World’s first non-verbal
drawing performance! It is the only performance where audiences can watch the
whole drawing process of an art work and gives an everlasting impression and
laughs to audiences through unbelievable techniques and effects. 


 
  
  
 

  
  
   
  * Episode 《THE LOOK》SINCE 2008
  "THE LOOK" is composed in the form of an omnibus.
  And there are a variety of fun episodes by four aliens, “LOOK”, communicating with
  a picture. 
  

 

Light Drawing / Finger Drawing /
Flower/ Luminous Drawing / Landscape / Marbling Drawing / Speed Drawing

 

<2014 Drawing
Show News >

*The
original drawing show is in a high school textbook!!

The
original drawing show, which opened a new chapter on nonverbal performance art,
is in a high school textbook in 2014 thanks to the Ministry of Education. 

 


 
  
 

  -The recorded contents of the textbook-
  Besides
  drawing techniques combining art, theater and musical, this is a performance
  that needs various types of art techniques such as the plastic arts,
  stamping, frottage, marbling, and the new art techniques that are developed
  independently.
   (2008/Kim,
  Jin-gyu)
  Mijinsa publisher, on page 79
  of the text book named “High School Art Culture”>
