	[Grand Ambassador Seoul] Chuseok (Thanksgiving) Package

	Writer : Grand Ambassador Seoul associated with Pullman		 / Date : 2014/8/22 9:49	

	

														
		

Spend a leisurely Chuseok (Thanksgiving)
Holiday 

at Grand Ambassador Seoul 

[Grand Ambassador Seoul] ‘Fall Love’ Package



Grand
Ambassador Seoul is offering a special ‘Fall Love’ Package for those who want
spend a relaxing Chuseok (Thanksgiving) holiday in downtown Seoul from
September 5 to 10. Guests who are unable to make it back to their hometowns for
Chuseok can still enjoy a special holiday with this package which includes a
one night stay in a comfortable room, a KRW30,000 room service voucher, and
discounts on breakfast. Furthermore, Rubelli Hand Packs will also be given as a
special present. The package is priced from KRW162,800. (This does not include
tax, and there are no service charges). 

 

All
packages include complimentary use of the hotel’s swimming pool and fitness
center for guests along with a Nespresso Coffee Machine in guest rooms that
include two free espresso capsules.

 

■
Information & Reservations: 02-2270-3111~3, grand.ambatel.com
