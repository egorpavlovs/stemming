	WHITE ANGEL CHARITY BAZAAR

	Writer : 밀레니엄 서울힐튼		 / Date : 2015/8/06 13:56	

	

						
								
								
		WHITE ANGEL CHARITY BAZAAR
 
Millennium Seoul Hilton is excited to host the White Angel Charity Bazzar in collaboration with the Green Umbrella Foundation, AMANG, and White Angels.  The bazaar will be held on Wednesday, August 12 from 11AM to 8PM in the Grand Ballroom, and will be an incredible opportunity to have fun, purchase some incredible products, meet celebrities, and help support the projects of incredibly dedicated charity organizations! 
 
The bazaar will be emceed by popular comedian Jeong Seong Ho, and will feature a celebrity auction of valuable goods donated by celebrities such as Kwon Sang Woo, Son Tae Yong, Yoon Hae Young, Jun Su Kyung, Kim Tae Hwi, Joo Won, Kim Hwi Seon, Ivy, IU, and more!  
 
The event will also feature a Kid’s Flea Market where over 50 famous brands such as Uptown Girl, Paper Rabbit, and more will offer their products for sale at large savings.   
 
There is an entrance fee of KW9,000 for the bazaar, however advanced tickets are available for a discounted price of KW5,000. Children in elementary school and younger may enter free of charge.  
 
A portion of the proceeds from the sales as well as the entrance fee will be donated to the Green Umbrella Foundation, where the money will be used to help bring hope and aid to disabled children.  
 
Don’t miss this wonderful opportunity to have some fun, purchase incredible products – at the same time, knowing your money is going to help bring smiles and happiness to disabled children. 
 
For further enquiries, please call Millennium Seoul Hilton’s Wedding Gallery at (02) 317-3430~2.
