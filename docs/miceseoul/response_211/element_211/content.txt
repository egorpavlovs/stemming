								Located within the picturesque Songpa-gu lake district and just a short distance away from Gangnam, the Lotte Hotel World is a well-placed business hotel providing ultimate convenience and easy accessibility to the best the city has to offer. The hotel is also well-suited for families, with Magic Island and Lotte World Adventure, the world’s largest indoor amusement park, just next door.
Accommodation Facilities
The Lotte Hotel World’s 469 rooms offer all the comfort and convenience of home. The Club Floor caters to the discerning needs of the business traveler, and separate floors are available for smokers and non-smokers. All rooms come with high-speed Internet access.
Meetings & Conventions
For events, the hotel has 9 banquet rooms, including the Crystal Ballroom, the Emerald Room, the Pearl room, and the Ruby Room, boasting elegant interiors and a comprehensive range of services. From large international conferences to business functions, shows and events of all kinds, the Lotte Hotel World caters for all needs.
Restaurants & Bars
Providing a wealth of dining options to suit all palettes, the hotel’s stylish and modern restaurants and bars welcome you to a world of exquisite haute cuisine. The newly renovated Club Floors come with the latest facilities to enhance the convenience and comfort of business travelers and offer the following:

	Complimentary use of the Club Lounge (breakfast buffet, lunch snacks, afternoon tea, cookie hour, after-dinner snacks)
	Free conference room use for up to 2 hours, Internet access, and mobile phone rental (paid service)
