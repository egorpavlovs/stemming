			Introduction
The 4 Star Novotel Ambassador Seoul Doksan is located in Seoul's thriving Gasan & Guro Digital Complex district. Less than 30 minutes drive from downtown Seoul, the hotel is the ideal destination for business trips and features 218 rooms with a variety of accommodation facilities such as the executive lounge, business center and two extended floors occupying the ultramodern Health Center that includes a state-of-the-art gym, swimming pool, GX room, spacious sauna and indoor golf-driving range. Meanwhile, diners can enjoy a selection of Korean and international dishes from various facilities including the hotel's restaurant & bar.
