			Introduction
Lotte Hotel Seoul, Korea's 5 Star best luxury hotel, is conveniently located in the heart of Seoul's central business district near Myeongdong and features two hotels-Main Building (offers 776 rooms and 14 banquet rooms) and New Wing (newly renovated 270 rooms to be added in August, 2018). Lotte Hotel Seoul is a perfect hotel of 5 star hotels in Seoul for your tourism providing luxurious accommodation options designed by 4 world`s leading design firms. It offers Club Floor for successful business and Ladies Floor designed only for women boasting its class and sophistication. Lotte Hotel is also preferred by many VIPs and leaders from different countries as a Korea's representative hotel holding the largest number of international events and cultural festivals. Experience ultimate comfort and unbeatable luxury in the center of Seoul, Choose Korea`s best hotel, Lotte for your exclusive stay.
