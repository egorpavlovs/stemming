	[Novotel Ambassador Seoul Doksan] 2014 Winter Package

	Writer : Novotel Ambassador Doksan		 / Date : 2014/11/13 13:39 / Modified Date 2014/11/13 13:41	

	

						
												
		

Novotel
Ambassador Seoul Doksan launches its 2014 Winter Package 

 

The Novotel Ambassador Seoul Doksan is
offering three Winter Packages between December 1, 2014 and February 28, 2015.
The new packages are designed to enable customers to enjoy a break from the
cold winter season at our comfortable hotel equipped with an indoor pool and
other amenities without having to travel to warmer countries.

 

Winter Package I - Smart Winter

The Smart Winter Package consists
of one night in a comfortable room, use of the hotel’s swimming pool and
in-balance gym, and an auxiliary attery for a mobile device, enabling families
to spend a cold winter night in downtown Seoul in
a smart way.

Period: December 01, 2014 ~
February 28, 2015, Price: KRW 140,000 (Tax not included).

 

Winter Package II - Lovely Winter

The Lovely Winter Package offers
Demeter Body Products that are famous for their memorable perfume. The package
consists of one night of accommodation, Demeter Body Lotion (240ml)+ Demeter
Body Gel (240ml), use of the hotel’s swimming pool and in-balance gym, allowing
lovers and families to spend an enjoyable time. 

Period: December 01, 2014 ~
February 28, 2015, Price: Starts at KRW 150,000 (Tax not included).


Winter
Package III–Delicious Winter
The Delicious Winter Package offers a BOGO 1+1 (Buy One Get One Free) coupon
that allows guests to use the buffet restaurant ‘Garden Terrace’ on weekdays or
weekends for lunch or dinner, so customers can enjoy a delicious evening of
rest and relaxation. The package consists of one night in a comfortable room, a
BOGO 1+1 (Buy One Get One Free) coupon for lunch or dinner at the ‘Garden
Terrace’ on weekdays or weekends, and use of the hotel’s swimming
pool and in-balance gym. 

Sale Period: December 01, 2014 ~
February 28, 2015, Price: Starts at KRW 160,000 (Tax not included).


All packages include the benefit of using the
in-balance gym and indoor swimming pool free of charge. For customers who
reserve rooms higher than executive class, breakfast for two people at the
buffet restaurant ‘Garden Terrace’ is also included. A free meal is offered to
two children under
the age of 16 when
accompanied by their parents.

 

Inquiries & Reservations 82-2-3282-6160
