	Renaissance Seoul Hotel_Autumn Weekend Package

	Writer : Renaissance Seoul Hotel		 / Date : 2014/9/19 18:04	

	

										
								
		 
Renaissance Seoul Hotel
Making romance in the City ‘Autumn Weekend Package’
- Various packages targeting different guests
 
The Renaissance Seoul Hotel is offering “Falling in LOVE Package” for those wanting to make romance and
relaxing autumn during the weekends Until November 30.
 
Three different packages are prepared to suit different guests and needs. All packages include complimentary use of the indoor swimming pool, Renaissance Recreation Center and 50% discount on sauna as general benefits.
 
The ‘Falling in LOVE Package I’ is an affordable and cost-saving choice which includes a night
in a comfortable deluxe room and the general benefits for KRW180,000(Exclusive of service charge and tax).
 
The ‘Falling in LOVE Package II’ is designed for couple guests with a night in a deluxe room,
complimentary breakfast for two at the Café Elysee, free in-room internet, free in-room a bottle of red wine and assorted Cheese plate,
one Renaissance soy candle as a special gift and the general benefits for KRW230,000(Exclusive of service charge and tax).
 
The ‘Falling in LOVE Package III’ is designed for family guests with a night in a deluxe room,
complimentary breakfast for two at the Café Elysee(two children under 12 can eat free maximum), one Renaissance soy candle as a special gift,
free in-room internet, one Renaissance indulgence pizza and two soft drinks for children and a bottle of red-wine and
assorted cheese plate for adults as room service along with the general benefits.
The package is offered at KRW240,000(Exclusive of service charge and tax).
 
Come to Renaissance Seoul Hotel for a comfortable stay and discover the true meaning of romance and relaxation. 
 
Period: September 7, 2014 ~ November 30, 2014 (Friday ~ Sunday & Public Holidays)
Price: From KRW180,000 (Exclusive of service charge and tax)
Information & Reservation: 02-2222-8500
