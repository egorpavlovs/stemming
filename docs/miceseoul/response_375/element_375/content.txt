								ENJOY THE WORLD CELEBRITY TOUR !Grevin Seoul, opened in 2015, is located in the heart of the city, MyeongDong and is the first Asian branch of the famed Grevin Museums.You can meet more than 80 life-sized wax figures of celebrities from around the world and take selfies with them.Visitors can also enjoy eight interactive digital experience games such as a flight simulator, casino roulette, create your own wax figure image, and more. Korean celebrity merchandise as well as exclusive Grevin items are available for purchase at Grevin Boutique. Make sure to also enjoy the flavors of Paris right in Seoul at Cafe Grevin offering various local and French classic menu items, including desserts.Both groups and individuals are welcome to visit and Grevin Seoul is also an optimal venue for special events such as private cocktail parties, incentive/MICE tours, business tours, conference events, and other events
