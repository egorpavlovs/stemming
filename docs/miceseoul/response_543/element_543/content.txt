        Space 7 is an event house located at the foot of Mt. Bukak and consists of about 3300 square meters of private space. It’s a place where you can enjoy the tranquil, green scenery and atmosphere of Mt. Bukak and enjoy different cultural events. It is actively used as drama shooting and advertisement shooting space, but it can also serve as the perfect setting for a corporate event and private event.TransportationFrom Incheon Int’l Airport: 110 MinutesSubway Station: 15 minutes walk from Jahamu Tunnel. Seokpajeong Bus Stop (Take #1711, 7016, 7018, 7212, 1020 bus at exit number 3 of subway line 3 Gyeongbokgung Station)Nearby Hotels and AttractionsWithin 3km: Four Seasons Hotel SeoulWithin 5km: The Plaza Hotel, Lotte Hotel Seoul, The Westin Chosun SeoulHistorical and cultural sites: Gwanghwamun Square, Gyeongbokgung Palace, National Palace Museum of Korea, Sejong Center for the Performing Arts, Seoul Museum of History, Yoon Dongju Literary Museum, Whanki Museum, Seoul MuseumOthers: Bugaksan Mountain, Hill of Poet – Yoon DongjuFACILITIESName of HallLocationLocation(㎡)StandingSeatedReception Hall1F995030/40Kitchen Room1F403015/20Lounge Room2F362010/15GardenOutside-15070/100Special FeaturesAs the only private place at the foot of Mt.
