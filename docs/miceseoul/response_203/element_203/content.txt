								The Grand Hilton Seoul is the gateway to the spirited city of Seoul. Providing the most luxurious service, The Grand Hilton Seoul offers stunning 396 guest rooms, 6 superb dining, fitness club (Gym, Swimming pool, Sauna), and Convention Centre. Setting the highest standards for facilities and service, The Convention Centre is capable of accommodating from small group seminar up to 2,500 people.
Grand Hilton Seoul is located only 15 minutes from the heart of central Seoul.
A courtesy shuttle bus service provides a convenient and comfortable way to travel to and from downtown Seoul, Itaewon’s global food and shopping hub, and the trendy Hongik University area.
