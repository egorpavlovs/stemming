	Science of Lamb & Gingseng, the powerful Root

	Writer : Millennium Seoul Hilton		 / Date : 2015/10/05 14:53	

	

						
								
								
		



LAMB AND ALCHEMY AT IL PONTE



5 October 2015, Seoul, Korea… Il Ponte, Millennium
Seoul Hilton’s popular Italian trattoria, will host a month-long promotion showcasing
lamb dishes and the culinary alchemy that occurs when combined with certain flavour
profiles.  Throughout the month of October,
diners will be able to enjoy a special set menu featuring flavourful and
aromatic Italian lamb dishes. 

The five course menu entitled “Science of
Lamb” will feature delectable dishes such as: Grilled Lamb Skewer on Arugula,
Cucumber Goat Cheese Salad with Lime Dressing and Foccacia Bread; Penne Pasta with
Spice Lamb Meatballs in a Chunky Tomato Sauce; Grilled Rack of Lamb with an
Olive-Pistachio Crust, Mushroom Risotto, Vegetable Ratatouille and Marsala
Sauce; and more.  

This menu is priced at KW89,000 per
person.  

Also at Il Ponte, every Saturday and
Sunday at lunchtime (11:30 a.m. – 2:30 p.m.), your children, the chefs of the
future, are invited to explore their culinary and creative talents by making
their own pizza!  Our Chef will then bake
your child’s masterpiece to simmering perfection!  

Il Ponte features the finest Italian
cuisine from tempting pasta and wood-fired brick oven pizza to mouthwatering
regional dishes.  Il Ponte also has an
extensive choice of exquisite Italian and international wines.  

For reservations or enquiries, please
call Il Ponte at (02) 317-3270. Tax and service charge is already included in the price quoted above. 

 

 

EXPERIENCE THE POWER OF GINSENG AT TAIPAN

 

5 October 2015, Seoul, Korea… Millennium Seoul Hilton
is showcasing the powerful, medicinal ginseng at Taipan, the hotel’s popular Chinese
restaurant, throughout the month of October. 
During this time, in addition to Taipan’s regular menu offerings, diners
will be able to choose a variety of special menu items that feature the
highly-prized root.

 

Ginseng has been touted to have many
benefits when consumed – from boosting immunity to lowering blood sugar.  An endurance booster, ginseng is widely
studied for its ability to treat many ailments and improve health.  

 

Some of the wonderful dishes to be
featured include: Cantonese Roast Duck served with Honey Glazed Fresh Ginseng;
Stir-fried Live Lobster and Fresh Ginseng with Garlic and Red Chili; Fried Rice
with Fresh Ginseng and Black Chicken wrapped in Lotus Leaf; and much more.  

 

The a la carte dishes range in price from
KW28,000 to KW160,000.  

 

Don’t miss this opportunity to experience
the wondrous powers of ginseng cooked with Chinese flair!

 

For reservations or enquiries, please
call Taipan at (02) 317-3237.  Tax and service charge is already included in
the prices quoted above.
