	Writer : Millennium Seoul Hilton		 / Date : 2015/11/02 14:00	

	

						
												
		

SNAPPER KAISEKI AT GENJI:

A MEAL TO SNAP UP RIGHT AWAY!

 

2 November 2015, Seoul, Korea… Genji, Millennium Seoul
Hilton’s Japanese restaurant, is pleased to announce a special Kaiseki meal
showcasing snapper that will be offered for a limited time during the month of
November.  

 

The Snapper Kaiseki will feature nine
glorious courses that will delight your tastebuds!   As part of this gastronomic extravaganza,
you will feast on: 3 different types of Appetizers; a Clear Snapper Soup; a
variety of Sashimi including Hamachi, Halibut, Seabass and Salmon; Grilled
Snapper with Salt; Steamed Snapper with Yams and Pumpkin; Fried Snapper with
Shallots, Shrimp, and Sweet Potato; Vinegared Octopus; Steamed Rice with Miso
Soup; and Fresh Fruits to top off the meal!

 

This special Kaiseki meal is priced at
KW108,000 per person.  

 

Available
for lunch and dinner, Genji offers the best in Japanese dining.  It features a new Sushi bar, impressive
Teppanyaki tables, unique Tatami rooms and private dining rooms in an elegant
and temporary setting.

 

Tax and service charge is already included
in the price quoted above. 

For reservations or enquiries, please
call Genji
at (02) 317-3240.
