brings new surprises and inspiration. Located in Gangnam, the dynamic southern
heart of Seoul, Hotel Cappuccino is the perfect home away from home for
international travelers and Seoulites seeking a new, yet friendly, fun, and causedriven
hotel. The spirit of Hotel Cappuccino strives to create shared value with our
guests through entertainment and attractive spaces filled with thoughtful detail.
