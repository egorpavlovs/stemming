			Introduction
The Courtyard Marriott Seoul Times Square combines convenience with modernity in the bustling Yeondeungpo district, just a few minutes away from the international finance district of Yeouido. The hotel is close by all major forms of public transport and guests can take advantage of Times Square's many shopping, dining and entertainment facilities. On-site, the Courtyard Marriott offers an upscale accommodation experience, with luxurious bedding, modern furnishings, high-speed internet, and a spacious working area.
