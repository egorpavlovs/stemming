	2014 Hallyu Award Winner Musical Bibap Praised for establishing a palce in Global Contents

	Writer : PERSONA Inc. 'BIBAP'		 / Date : 2014/12/16 10:12 / Modified Date 2014/12/16 10:19	

	

														
		2014 Hallyu Award Winner Musical Bibap Praised for establishing a palce in Global Contents Delicious musical performance Bibap is a sensuous non-verbal performance which was brought by two representatives (Persona rep: Choi Chul Ki and CJ E&M Rep: Kim Byung Seok) in 2009, bringing the concept of mix and harmony from famous Korean cuisine BiBimBap.       In 2010, the musical Bibap participated in the England's Edinburgh Fringe Festival and got great praises and also has been recognised as one of the Global Contents.In April 2012, Bibap started from 2000 seats in Esplanade, Singapore, and by now it has been performing in 56 cities in 15 countries including 28 cities in China.        To their constant effort, Bibap became more suited to the world class and successfully established its position. On the 5th of December 2014 in Korea, Bibap held Ceremony for the best Hallyu Awards and had the honour of winning an award for Pop Culture in Theatre/Musical section.In return, Bibap is offering 10% discount to anyone who purchases Bibap ticket throughout 2015.       Until today, more than 700000 people enjoyed watching musical Bibap and some of the restaurants and theme parks also holding special package deals to commercialize to the domestic people who still yet haven't watched this musical yet.  At the moment the musical Bibap is performing in Cinecore Theatre in Jongro.          Time : Mon. 8pm /Tue.~Sat. 5pm, 8pm /Sun. 3pm, 6pm          Address: Cinecore Bldg., 33-1, Gwancheol-dong, Jongno-gu, Seoul, Korea 110-111          Ticket Price : VIP Seat 60,000 Won / R seat 50,000 Won / S Seat 40,000 Won          Running Time : 75 minutes          Inquiry : +82 2 766 0815          Homepage : www.bibap.co.kr
