								The First Social Venue in Korea, The RAUM The RAUM is more than just a building; it is a forum that allows its patrons to expand their human network as well as a culture and arts space that enhances the quality of life. TRAUM HAUS Co., Ltd., the builders of TRAUM HAUS luxury apartments, has been planning the social venue for four whole years. Based on the company’s philosophy and dedication to master craftsmanship, The RAUM took three years to construct. When it was finally completed in May 2011, the building was four stories high with total floor space of 33,000m2, heralding another landmark built by TRAUM HAUS Co., Ltd. Now patrons can visit The RAUM for exquisite performances, exhibitions, banquets, and weddings. Known as the first social venue in Korea, it continues to serves as a space for social exchange and highbrow culture.Social Events Exquisitely Hosted by The RAUM for Precious Guests The RAUM is located in Gangnam, Seoul, which is easily accessible from every part of the city. The RAUM offers five banqueting halls, each with its own style and modern facilities. Right outside the door is a 1,300m² garden that is perfect for hosting parties and other social events in good weather.Host receptions, after-parties, and banquets catered for by a six-star chef; enjoy the refined ministrations of our professional service staff on your special day.
