		ASAE’s Great Ideas in Association Management Conference, Asia Pacific  
		2017-02-02
		
			
			ASAE’s Great Ideas in
Association Management Conference, Asia Pacific

The 2017 Great Ideas in Association Management Conference, Asia
Pacific will be held from
22-24 March 2017 at Grand Hyatt Seoul, Korea. 

ASAE’s Great Ideas in
Association Management Conference is a unique opportunity to connect with other executives from the
region, the US, and Europe on best practices in association management. 
The content for the conference has been developed by the 2017 ASAE Asia Pacific
Advisory Task Force, a group of your association peers from the region. The
content will be delivered by executives with a wealth of association management
experience. Presenters will encourage discussion and the expression of diverse
perspectives, so come willing to share your experiences.

Visit the
conference website for more details.

Be sure to register by 21 February 2017 to take advantage of the
early bird rates. Special discounts are also available for delegations of 5 or more
people.

For
questions or to set up your delegation, please contact Megan Kuhman at giap@asaecenter.org 

or
Kimberly Kim at kimberly.kim@mci-group.com for Korean
delegation. 

About ASAE

ASAE is a
membership organization of more than 21,000 association executives and industry
partners representing 10,000 organizations, based in Washington, DC, USA with
offices in Seoul, Korea and Beijing, China. Our members manage leading industry
associations, individual membership societies, and voluntary organizations
across 50 countries around the world. For
more information about ASAE, visit asaecenter.org.
