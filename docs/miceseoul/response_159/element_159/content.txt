Korea
is a wonderful and fascinating destination with unique culture and kind-hearted
people, a mixture of the old and new. By taking a small group or a private
tour with us, you can experience firsthand the best surrounding in all of
Korea.
Grace Travel is designated as one of the 2002 FIFA World Cup Transportation and
Tourism Service Agency by KOWOC (The Korean Organizing Committee For the 2002
FIFA World Cup Korean / Japan). We are a successful and independent travel
agency that specializes in daily city tour products, arranging an international
convention tour program and a private tour around Korea. Our consultants are
experienced, highly trained experts, dedicated to finding the best travel deal
for any kinds of tour in Korea.
All inquiries are handled immediately, and the majority receive an answer
within 24 hours of initial contact.
We also promise to provide the highly experienced professional tour guides who
can give you unforgettable memories while you’re staying in Korea.
Grace Travel is always happy to serve you.
