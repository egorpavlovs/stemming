								Since its establishment in 1979, Coex
Convention & Exhibition Center remains Korea’s premier
venue of choice and providing nearly 40 years of experience and know-how in
organizing and hosting business events in Korea. The center is conveniently
located in the heart of Gangnam, Seoul’s business
district, and annually hosts an average of 200 exhibitions as well as over
2,000 separate meetings and events. Coex’s main
facilities consists of 4 main exhibition halls, with a combined 36,007m² of exhibition space and which can be partitioned into 9 separate
spaces, and 55 dividable meeting rooms of all sizes and types including a
column-less space for 7,000 people and a tiered-theater for 1,080. On-site
amenities include three 5-star hotels, one of Asia’s
largest underground shopping mall, a city airport terminal, four international
office towers, aquarium, casino, two subway stops, and more. Coex’s infrastructure ensures that all your needs - be they business,
entertainment, shopping, or culture - are met right here under one roof.
