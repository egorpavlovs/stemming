Seoul Dragon City is located in the
center of Seoul, Yongsan and the first hotel-plex in Korea consisting of 1,700
rooms of 4 Accor Hotels brands from luxury to economy. Its unique features are
the dragon-like appearance of the building exterior with three tall connected
towers. Seoul Dragon City is a complete lifestyle hotel-plex including multiple
hotels, restaurants & bars, large meeting & convention space, and
world-class entertainment facilities.Seoul Dragon City’s large meeting
& convention spaces consist of 17 meeting rooms, 2 multi-function grand
ballrooms, and 8 private dining rooms, making it an ideal option for various
events. Each Grand Ballroom measures 1,189 square meters, equipped with the
most up-to-date 12-16m LED media wall and cutting-edge sound facilities. It can
accommodate up to 1,800 people and enables division of the whole venue into
three or four sections. Additionally, 17 meeting rooms facilitate easy progress
of events of different sizes, from seminars and business meetings to wedding,
banquets, and parties.
