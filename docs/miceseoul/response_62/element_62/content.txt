	LOVE IS IN THE AIR  AT MILLENNIUM SEOUL HILTON! 

	Writer : Millennium Seoul Hilton		 / Date : 2016/1/29 10:30	

	

						
												
		LOVE IS IN THE AIR

AT MILLENNIUM
SEOUL HILTON! 

29 January 2016, Seoul, Korea… Millennium Seoul Hilton
is inviting Seoulites in love to celebrate the day of love, Valentine’s Day, on
February 14. The hotel is offering a variety of romantic specials at its
restaurants and bar for the perfect night out with the love of your life.  Don’t forget to take advantage of our early
bird specials and receive even more perks and savings!

Seasons, the hotel’s highly acclaimed
gourmet French restaurant, is offering a romantic Rose Petal Dinner available
for dinner on February 12th and 13th, and for lunch and dinner
on February 14th.  Couples can
indulge in the six-course set menu for two persons, which will include Charcoal
Grilled Sea Bass Fillet, Grilled Prawn, Green Asparagus and Brown Risotto as
well as Rose Water and Vodka Sherbet. 
The dinner is priced at KW229,000 for two and includes 2 glasses of
champagne. Early birds who book before February 11th will receive a
complimentary bottle of house wine!

You can also take advantage of our
Valentines Hotel Room Package which includes the Season Rose Petal Dinner set
menu, 2 glasses of champagne, and 1 night Hotel Room Stay (valid only on
February 12th, 13th or 14th). The Valentines
Hotel Room Package is priced at KW459,800.

Il Ponte, the hotel’s award-winning
Italian restaurant, will sweep your sweet love off their feet with Amore di
Napoli, a delicious set menu for two inspired by the coastal cuisine of Naples.  The five-course menu is priced at KW193,600
for two and includes two glasses of Prosecco. This shared dining experience
will include lobster prepared four ways, and will be sure to wow your date!  Early birds who book before February 11th
will receive a voucher for 1 complimentary pizza that can be used on a future
visit to Il Ponte.



Genji,
the hotel’s Japanese restaurant, will offer a Shabu Shabu & Sashimi set
menu for two.  The six-course meal is
meant to be shared by two people and will feature assorted sashimi, Hanwoo Beef
Shabu Shabu, as well as Abalone, Salmon and Fresh Water Eel!  Slurp, savor, and satisfy your cravings with
the sublime creations by our chef!  This
romantic meal is priced at KW242,000 for two and is inclusive of 2 Sake
Dokuri.   Early birds who book before
February 11th will receive a complimentary bottle of house wine!

 

Cafe
395, the hotel’s flagship restaurant will offer a Valentine’s Jazz Brunch on
February 14th from 11:30am to 2:30pm.  The brunch will cost KW91,000 per person and
will include the extensive buffet featuring freshly prepared food items from
around the world as well as free-flowing wine, beer and soft drinks.  Or for KW110,000, guests can enjoy one glass
of champagne followed by free-flowing wine, beer and soft drinks and buffet.   There will also be a special area with
beautiful romantic creations including Strawberry Towers and Rose Petal
Cakes.  A jazz band will also perform,
setting the mood for a very lovely brunch!

 

And
finally, Oak Room is celebrating those who aren’t attached on Valentine’s Day
by throwing a 70s/80s Valentines Bash on February 13th!  Partygoers will be able to mingle and have
fun to the backdrop of fabulous music from the 1970s and 1980s, spun by a
groovy live DJ!  During the night there
will be an opportunity to win attractive prizes in our lucky draw.

 

For
reservations or enquiries, please call Service Center at (02) 753-7788. All
prices quoted above are already inclusive of tax and service charge.
