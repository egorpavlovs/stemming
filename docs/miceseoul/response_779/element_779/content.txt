	[Renaissance Seoul Hotel] Be My Valentine day package

	Writer : Renaissance Seoul Hotel		 / Date : 2015/1/22 16:49	

	

						
												
		Renaissance Seoul Hotel
Valentine’s day package
 
Renaissance Seoul Hotel offers romantic Valentine’s day packages From February 14th, 15th to celebrate a special moment with their lovers.
 
Three packages, ‘Be my Valentine package’, ‘Sweet suite package I’ and ‘Sweet suite package II’ provide diverse privileges including common benefit of complimentary use of the indoor swimming pool, recreation center and sauna for two, complimentary breakfast for two at Club Horizon, as well as late check-out benefit.
 
The ‘Be my valentine package’ is for which includes one night comfortable deluxe room, in-room special Valentine chocolate & Cheese plate with a bottle of house wine, as well as late check-out benefit until 2pm for KRW 250,000(exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.)
 
The ‘Sweet suite package I’ includes one night a Royal suite room, free in-room internet, in-room special Valentine chocolate & Cheese plate, in-room a bottle of house wine with setting flowers, in-room Chef’s cooking private dining dinner for two, as well as late check-out benefit until 3pm. Additionally, guests will be staying on White day, you can get an exclusive 10% discount when booking for a White day at Renaissance Seoul Hotel for
KRW 500,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.)
 
The ‘Sweet suite package II’ highly recommended for those looking forward to a more luxuries with love ones. Along with the common benefits, one night a Presidential suite room, free in-room internet, in-room special Valentine chocolate & Cheese plate, in-room a bottle of house wine with setting flowers, in-room Chef’s cooking private dining dinner for two, as well as late check-out benefit until 3pm. Also guests will be staying on White day, you can get an exclusive 10% discount when booking for a White day at Renaissance Seoul Hotel for KRW 700,000(Exclusive of 10% service charge and 10% tax.)
 
This year, celebrate this only special day for two at Renaissance Seoul Hotel.
 
Period: February 14, 15 only
Price: Be my Valentine package - From KRW 250,000 (Excluded of 10% service charge & 10 tax).
Sweet suite package I - From KRW 500,000 (Excluded of 10% service charge & 10 tax).
Sweet suite package II - From KRW 700,000 (Excluded of 10% service charge & 10 tax).Information & Reservation: 02-2222-8500
