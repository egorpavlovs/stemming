        Seoul Grevin Museum, opened in 2015 as the largest wax figure museum in Asia. You
can meet more than 80 life-size wax figures of celebrities from around the world and
take selfies with them. Visitors can also enjoy eight interactive digital experience games
such as a flight simulator, casino roulette, discovery atelier (create your own wax figure
image), and more. You can also Korean celebrity merchandise as well as exclusive
Grevin items including French souvenirs at Grevin Boutique. Make sure to also enjoy
the flavors of Paris right in Seoul at Café Grevin offering various local and French classic
menu items, including desserts.
Both groups and individuals are welcome to visit and Grevin Museum is also an
optimal venue for special events such as private cocktail parties, incentive/MICE tours,
business tours, conference events, and other events.
TransportationFrom Incheon Int’l Airport: 60 minutes
  Subway Station: 50m from exit 1-1 of Euljiro 1(il)-ga Station

  Attractions: Jonggak, Insadong, Cheonggyecheon, Samcheongdong
  Shopping: Donghwa Duty Free, Lotte Mall, Shinsegae Mall
  Historic sites: Gwanghwamun Square, Gyeongbokgung Palace, Changdeokgung, National Folk Museum, Seoul Museum of History, Sejong Center, Jogyesa, Gilsangsa
  Other: Namsan Park, Myeongdong

Special Features

    The Grevin Museum is located in Seoul’s downtown area and is Asia’s largest wax figure museum. The space is suitable for various business events, including group functions, with catering, event, and meeting spaces among life-size celebrity wax figures.
