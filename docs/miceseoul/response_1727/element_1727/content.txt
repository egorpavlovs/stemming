        Lotte World is a theme park located in Seoul’s famous Gangnam district. 
It offers a range of exciting rides and attractions at its two main locations, 
“Lotte World Adventure” indoor theme park and the open-space “Magic Island” 
nearby, where different kinds of festival are held every season. Lotte World 
also houses Seokchon Lake, the nation’s largest indoor ice rink, where visitors 
can enjoy skating or sit back and watch a show performed on ice. There is even 
a folk museum designed to showcase the best of Korean culture.
