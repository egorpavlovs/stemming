			Introduction
RAMADA Seoul is located in Gangnamgu, Samseong-dong - the heart of the Seoul Business District. The surrounding Korean cultural heritage and green environment, including a jogging course in Seolleung Park next to the hotel, will add pleasure to your stay. The hotel has 110 Superior rooms, 32 Executive rooms, 36 Premier rooms, 5 Suites rooms, and 62 Regular Rooms all equipped with the latest communication system. Other facilities include an Italian restaurant, membership bar, coffee shop, bakery, sauna, fitness room, wedding hall and large banquet halls. The refreshing views of nature will provide you with relaxation during your business trip.
