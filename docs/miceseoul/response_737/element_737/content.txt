CHUSEOK GETAWAY AT MILLENNIUM SEOUL HILTON 

 

16 September 2015, Seoul, Korea… Millennium Seoul Hilton
is offering Seoul urbanites an opportunity to relax this Chuseok holiday!  Skip the heavy traffic and all the stress that
goes along with this holiday season by hiding away in luxury at Millennium
Seoul Hilton! Situated on beautiful Mt. Namsan, Millennium Seoul Hilton is just
steps away from Namsan Botanical Gardens and the scenic N Tower where you can
glimpse an obstructed view of the city. As the autumn leaves start to turn
brilliant shades of gold, crimson and ochre, you’ll really enjoy the splendour
of this holiday!

 

The Chuseok Holiday Packages are only
available from Thursday, September 24th to Wednesday, September 30th.  

 

Chuseok Deluxe
Package (Deluxe Room/KW150,000++):

For an extra KW45,000, guests can enjoy a breakfast
buffet for 2 persons at Café 395, the hotel’s all-day dining restaurant.

 

Chuseok
Executive Package (Executive Room/KW260,000++):

This package includes complimentary access for two to
the Executive Floor Lounge, which offers an American buffet breakfast in the
morning and happy hour cocktails and hors d’oeuvres from 5:30 pm to 8:30 pm. 

 

Common Benefits
for Guests

Complimentary use of the fitness center and indoor
swimming poolComplimentary Wi-Fi access in guest room All packages are based on double occupancy, and are
only available to Korean citizens or to foreigners who have an alien
registration card.  Check in time is at 2
pm, and check out time is at 12 noon.  The
prices quoted above do not include tax and service charge. 











For
reservations or enquiries, please call (02) 317-3000.
