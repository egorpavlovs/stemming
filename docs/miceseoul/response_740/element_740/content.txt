	Conrad Seoul – 37 Grill Introduces an ‘Express Lunch Menu’ optimized for business meetings

	Writer : Conrad Seoul		 / Date : 2015/10/07 9:35	

	

										
								
		

Conrad Seoul – 37
Grill Introduces an ‘Express Lunch Menu,’ 

optimized for
business meetings

Power lunches with “Quicker” and “Healthier” menus for a
well-balanced diet

 

2015, October 7 Seoul – Conrad Seoul’s authentic grill restaurant 37 Grill & Bar is now
offering an enhanced ‘Express Lunch Menu’, optimized for business meetings
finishing within an hour.

 

The Express Lunch course
menu offers a simplified semi-buffet style service with a nutritionally
balanced cuisine, allowing you to finish your meal within an hour. The menu
features either a two-course option, including an appetizer and entrée, or a
three-course option including appetizer, entrée and dessert. Each menu provides
access to a mouthwatering buffet of appetizers and fresh salads made from
seasonal vegetables, rich in fiber and vitamins. The entrée selections feature
a choice of five dishes, including meats (beef and pork), poultry (chicken and
duck), seafood, pasta and rice dishes, rich in protein or carbohydrates. For
those opting for desert, your meal will conclude with a selection of seasonal
fruits, homemade gelato ice-cream and other health conscious delicacies. The
Express Lunch menu enables guests to enjoy a more efficient lunch hour without
sacrificing time, taste or nutrition.

 

The Express Lunch menu
is available for lunch every weekday, between the hours of 11:30am and 2:30 pm
at 37 Grill & Bar, located on the 37th floor of the hotel. The
price for the two-course menu is KRW48,000 and KRW59,000 for the three-course
menu (per person, tax included). To accommodate more private business meetings,
37 Grill offers two private dining rooms which can host up to eight people. Reservations or inquiries: 02-6137-7110

 

37 Grill
& Bar is an authentic grill restaurant, offering the best atmosphere and
finest cuisine, as Seoul’s new landmark of fine dining. Guests are invited to
enjoy the breathtaking views of the Han River and city of Seoul, as they dine
from a selection of authentic grill dishes. This popular lunch time venue is a
favorite place for business meetings, frequented by the city’s top executives
from the Yeouido area. It is also the perfect choice for entertaining foreign
guests, given its central location and unsurpassed views of the city of Seoul
from the hotel’s highest floor. 

 

For more information please visit www.conradseoul.co.kr
