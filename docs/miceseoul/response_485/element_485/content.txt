		Introduction
This resort-like hotel is located at the foot of Mt. Umyeonsan and Citizens' Grove in Yangjae-dong. It offers 252 guest rooms plus banquet halls, convention halls, a fitness center and a large theater. Featuring meeting rooms in a variety of sizes, this hotel is available for corporate training, seminars or workshops, family gatherings and banquets.
