			Introduction
JW Marriott Seoul is ideally located within a comprehensive multi-purpose complex in Gangnam. Situated in the heart of the business and entertainment districts, the JW Marriott Seoul is close to the Convention and Exhibition Center. Subway Lines 3, 7 and 9 are also directly linked to the hotel. All rooms are equipped with the latest technology including 40-inch LCD TV, the Remote Jack Pack, which offers a  single source interface for audio, video, iPod, and computer devices to connect to the in-room TV and wireless Internet.

Special Features
From corporate meetings to cocktail parties, private gatherings and special events, when the occasion calls for a different perspective that will leave a lasting impression, talk with the JW Marriott Seoul's courteous and professional meeting planners. The Grand Ballroom, the Boardroom and three additional meeting rooms, provide the perfect solution for all your conference or social gathering needs. Also, cutting-edge technology such as high-speed internet, and video-conferencing, and our professional team are great reasons to select JW Marriott Seoul.
