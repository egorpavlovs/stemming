	[Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel] My Christmas Wish Package

	Writer : Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel		 / Date : 2014/11/19 14:00 / Modified Date 2014/11/19 14:02	

	

						
												
		

A ROMANTIC MOMENT TO REMEMBER
WITH YOUR LOVED ONE, ‘MY CHRISTMAS WISH PACKAGE’

Enjoy the sparkling wine and Chocolate Ball while overlooking the fantastic
nightscape of Seoul

 

Seoul, December 1, 2014- To cherish the memorable moments of Christmas, Sheraton
Seoul D Cube City Hotel presents ‘My Christmas Wish Package’ for a romantic
stay with their loved one. 

 

A lovely Chocolate Ball
with jellies, berries, red velvet cake and chocolate brownie inside will
delight the guests with a sweet surprise. Along with the Chocolate Ball, one
bottle of Chandon sparkling wine will be available to enjoy in the beautifully
appointed rooms of Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel, where a panoramic view of
the city awaits the guest for their stay. With the Christmas tree ornament
included in the package, guests can write their Christmas wish and hang the
ornament on the dazzling Christmas tree standing in the 41st floor
lobby for a memorable moment to share with their loved one. The seasonal
afternoon tea set for the festive season, ‘Christmas Delight’, is offered at a
special price for package guests, allowing a leisurely time on the 41st
floor Lobby Lounge.Bar. 

 

‘My Christmas Wish
Package’ includes one night stay at Sheraton Seoul D Cube City Hotel, and free
use of hotel gym and swimming pool. ‘My Christmas Wish Package’ guests can
enjoy the Christmas Ball and a bottle of Chandon sparkling wine, along with a Christmas
tree ornament where guests can put their Christmas wishes on to hang on the
hotel’s Christmas tree. A special discount price for ‘Christmas Delight’
afternoon tea set and breakfast at ‘Feast’ is offered to package guests. The package
begins from December 19, 2014 to December 30, 2014, and price is KRW 299,000 (exclusive
of 10% VAT).
