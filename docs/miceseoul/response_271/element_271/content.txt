People, Passion and Trust
P.O.D
Communications Inc. is trying to be a professional and realizing your dream as
a strong creative MICE Marketing Group. Based on thorough professionalism and
honesty, we are a professional conference organizer that not only conducts
execution, but provides specialized consultations to establish and integrated
strategy by establishing a strong brand for our clients and establishing
visions for creating new values. We provide our know-how on promotion services
accumulated from working together with various governmental agencies, including
the Seoul Metropolitan Government, the Ministry of Strategy and Finance and the
Ministry of Culture, Sports and Tourism, Ministry of Maritime Affairs and
Fisheries, Ministry of Foreign Affair, as well as renowned global companies
like LG Electronics, LG Display and Jordan.

 

Since
2004, P.O.D has successfully organized many congresses, exhibitions, travel
businesses and producing videos which is a regular member of KAPCO and
KMA.  Over the course of its nearly 14
years, we are working in the fields of businesses such as Mice Industry, Sports
Marketing & BTL Marketing, Service Design and Campaign. For attesting to
our businesses as well as features, we are granted two certifications of patent
and registration certificate as a video producer. 

In
addition, we can provide the services like Creatives and Speakers Bureau
through in-company research institute.

 

As
a professional convention organizer(PCO) that pursue our client’s dream by
looking at the world through our client’s eyes, we will be a trustworthy
partner in our clients.
