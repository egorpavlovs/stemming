        Seoul Museum is located in Jongno, Seoul, a place where Korea's vivid culture and history
lies underneath. Seoul Museum is opened to deliver the true beauty and inspiration
of art to our audience. Seoul Museum lets you truly experience the very best of Seoul
with some unique luxury deals. The iconic landmark National Tangible cultural Asset
No. 26 "Seokpajeong" and 650-year-old pine tree protected by Seoul Metropolitan
Government(Recored as No.60) always welcome you.
Moreover, Seoul Museum is conveniently located within walking distance of the city's
business district, major attractions and retail hub, an ideal base from which to explore
the city or conduct business in Korea. Total 5,000 square meters(54,000 square feet) of
total event space, all equipped with the latest in specialised audio-visual equipment
to ensure your private event is successful. From industry conferences, to office social
events, to confidential business meetings, our venue suits for every event. An attentive,
expert events agent will help coordinate and accommodate every request. Feel the
perfect blend of convenience and sophisticated comfort here.
