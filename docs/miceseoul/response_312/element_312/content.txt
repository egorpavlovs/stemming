SHILLA IPARK DUTY
FREE Shop is a world-class duty-free shopping venue presented by HDC Shilla
Duty Free Shop, a joint venture between HDC Group, a global construction company,
and Hotel Shilla, a local and global leader in the duty free market.

 

SHILLA IPARK DUTY
FREE allows customers to conveniently experience both duty free shopping and
diverse entertainment activities simultaneously.

 

SHILLA IPARK DUTY
FREE features 600 brands through its five floors and is located within I’PARK
mall, Korea’s biggest multi-purpose complex and home to various types of
leisure including a movie theater, restaurants, futsal field, cafes, and many
more.

 

As a special urban
shopping place for both domestic and international travelers, SHILLA IPARK DUTY
FREE will continuously deliver high quality services for duty-free shopping
along with other memorable experiences.
