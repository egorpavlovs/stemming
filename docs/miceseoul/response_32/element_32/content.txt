		BIDDING SUPPORT


		
			
				
					
						
					
					
						Assistance in Bid Preparation
						The Seoul Convention Bureau assists meeting planners, association leaders and other event organizers who plan on bringing events to Seoul. Our services include assistance in the preparation of proposals, a one-on-one presentation workshop and consultation on a successful bidding strategy.
					
				

				
					
						
					
					
						Venue Search and Site Inspections
						Hotel accommodations and transportation within Seoul will be provided by SCB for your board members or executives considering Seoul for their upcoming international meeting. City tours, site inspections of convention facilities and unique venues and information on tourist attractions are also offered.
					
				

				
					
						
					
					
						Bid Support Declarations
						The SCB will arrange for you to receive a letter and video message from the mayor of Seoul and CEO of Seoul Tourism Organization pledging support for your bid.
					
				

			
		



		Promotional and Hosting Support

		
			
				
					
						
							
						
					
					
						Seoul Promotional Materials & Seoul Welcome Kit
						SCB provides MICE leaflets highlighting 10 reasons to choose Seoul and promotional videos to assist organizers in the bidding and promoting their events in Seoul.SCB also support Seoul Welcome Kit including promotional materials and a souvenir to international attendees of MICE event in Seoul.
						How to Apply
						Please send the application form at least 1 month prior to event. [Application Form Download]


					
				

				

			
		
		
			
				
					
						
							
						
					
					
						Airport Greeting Message
						A warm welcome message will greet your delegates on the multi-screen display above the baggage carousels at Incheon International Airport.
						
						How to Apply
						Please send the application form at least 2 months prior to event. [Application Form Download]

						
					
				
			
			
		
		

	

		
			
				
					
						
							
						
					
					
						Seoul MICE Supporters
						A highly trained and dedicated pool of multilingual personnel chosen from convention-related departments of universities able to provide support for a wide variety of operations for your international conferences or corporate meetings in Seoul.
						
						How to Apply
						Please send the application form at least 1 month prior to event. [Application Form Download]
