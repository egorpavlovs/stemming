        With “coopetition” into the future: the Berlin MICE Summit

    
  

  


          
          How will the event industry develop in the future? More than 300 guests from the worlds of culture, politics and the event industry met in January at the Berlin MICE Summit, to develop creative ideas and solutions.

        Market knowledge: Chinese MICE market

    
  

  


          
          Berlin has become a trend with Chinese guests – not only for those on leisure trips but also for participants at conferences, trade fairs and congresses. Which cultural differences should be noted? What do Chinese guests look for, especially at events? Our expert Robert Luft talks about developments and trends in the Chinese market in this interview.

        10 years of the World Health Summit

    
  

  


          
          The World Health Summit is a Berlin success story and celebrated its tenth anniversary in 2018. We spoke with its founder, Professor Detlev Ganten, about the significance of Berlin as a knowledge hub and congress city.

Read the interview here

        Your partner for event planning in 2019 as well

    
  

  


          
          Whether it’s celebrating 30 years since the fall of the Berlin Wall or the opening of the new multi-purpose event hall “hub 27”: Berlin is an exciting city for event planners worldwide in 2019! The Berlin Convention Office of visitBerlin will continue to be at your side in the planning of incentives, conferences, meetings, seminars and congresses in the coming year. We will be happy to assist you in the search for an unusual location, the perfect hotel or a suitable event service provider. Simply get in touch with us at: convention@visitBerlin.de. We are looking forward to your enquiry!
