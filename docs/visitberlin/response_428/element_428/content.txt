            Camp Berlin 2019

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
The first BIM Camp for executives in architecture and planning offices Endlich - a platform where you can exchange ideas with colleagues on the subject of Building Information Modeling. Ask questions, exchange experiences, discuss challenges and opportunities - at eye level, live and in practice.Planning industry in upheavalThe future of building and planning is called Building Information Modelling (BIM). From 2020, BIM is to become the standard for public tenders. BIM is already standard in the Scandinavian countries and Great Britain. BIM will fundamentally change processes in the construction industry. There will be a space for innovative and new forms of work.BIM Camp - No speakers, no agenda: the BIM CampThe BIM Camp is an un-conference. It combines the freedom of the open conference format Barcamp with the focus on the specific topics of the construction industry. There are no classical speakers and no predefined program. The participants themselves determine the topics about which they would like to exchange, discuss and work on in small groups (sessions). The result is a varied programme consisting of individual and topical topics from the brewing industry.
