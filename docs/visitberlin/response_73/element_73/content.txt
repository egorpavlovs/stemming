            moderatorenpool-deutschland

                      

                    

                            
                          As the largest mediator in Berlin, we offer professional moderators for congresses and conferences, trade fairs and public events, panel discussions and workshops. Our moderators speak many languages and have specialist knowledge of a wide range of industries such as health, IT and politics. Journalists who can lead any discussion, any symposium, and speakers, coaches and consultants can book you.
Mail us the key data of your event. Questions will be answered by telephone. We are broadly positioned and look forward to receiving your enquiry! As a rule, you will receive at least 3 moderator suggestions that fit your budget and your requirement profile.
