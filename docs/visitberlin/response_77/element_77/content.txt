        ICCA

      
        With members from 86 countries, the International Congress & Convention Association is one of leading organisations for the int. association meeting industry.

        The European Cities Marketing platform promotes European cities through an exchange of knowledge and best practices in conference and tourism marketing.

          
     
  


      

  
      

            
                
              
              
              
              
            
            

  



  


    
      
        MPI

      
        Meeting Professionals International is the largest meeting and event industry association worldwide, connecting people in over 80 countries on four continents.

        The Association of British Professional Conference Organisers (ABPCO) is UK’s leading professional body for association conference and event organisation.
