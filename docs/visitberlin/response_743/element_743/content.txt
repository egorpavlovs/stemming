            Victor´s Residenz-Hotel Berlin Tegel

                      

                    

                    
                
              Victor's Residenz-Hotel Berlin-Tegel is a 3-star superior business and conference hotel with 177 stylishly furnished rooms. A restaurant with bar, as well as an event and conference area and an event location "THE MIX" with a capacity of up to 250 people. The hotel is located in the district of Berlin Reinickendorf, near the Schäfersee. Our hotel is conveniently located in the immediate vicinity of the underground station Franz- Neumann- Platz (U8 direction Alexanderplatz). There is a bus stop directly in front of the hotel. There is an in-house underground car park with 40 parking spaces (for a fee) at your disposal.
