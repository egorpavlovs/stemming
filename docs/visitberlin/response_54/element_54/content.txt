            Asanta

                      

                    

                    
                
              ASANTA is an event location that combines classic architecture with modern design and exclusive equipment. Innovative lighting, sound and media technologies mean the perfect realization of your event in a space with 12 metre high ceilings and brilliant acoustics. The VIP gallery offers a comfortable, lounge atmosphere to round off your event. This unique event space ticks all the boxes for meetings, corporate events, product launches, and exclusive galas. We look forward to realising your vision for your event.
