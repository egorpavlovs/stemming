            Novotel Berlin Am Tiergarten

                      

                    

                    
                
              * 4 star hotel in central location near S–Bahnhof Tiergarten
* 2 stations from Berlin Hauptbahnhof (main train station)
* with direct fair ground connection (5 stops either way CC or BCC)
* 20 minutes drive to Airport Tegel (TXL)
* parking garage with 110 spaces
* 274 rooms, including 11 suites and 23 executive rooms
* Airconditioning, phone for direct calls, flatscreen TV, Safe in laptop size
* free Wi-Fi
* Roof terrace with view over Tiergarten and City West
* Fitness, sauna and steam bath
* 11 modern and light conference rooms from 19 – 361 sqm for up to 350 persons
