            Sofitel Berlin Kurfürstendamm

                      

                    

                    
                
              The hotel is situated within metres of the Kurfürstendamm and Messe Berlin tradefair grounds. Two floors of the building offer 16 elegantly designed conference rooms including the Ballroom Concorde with parquet flooring, mirrored walls and room for up to 350 people. The most commanding room is the semicircular Salon Opéra with panoramic views. The hotel offers a total of more than 2,200 sqm of elegant, airy conference rooms that are also easily subdivided. InspiredMeetings™ concierges are in charge of the comprehensive equipment system as well as the Saint Germain buffet restaurant.
