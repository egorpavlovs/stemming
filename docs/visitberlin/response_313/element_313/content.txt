        Welcome to Berlin!

    
  

  


          
          Book the visitBerlin information desk (on demand) for your event or convention and create an added value for your attendees. Designed in a modern and versatile style, the information desk is available in various sizes.

        
      
    

  

  
  
    
        Our Berlin experts

    
    
      
      
              inform your delegates on the spot about Berlin,
          
        
              give exclusive tips on restaurants, current events and the best locations for shopping,
          
        
              give advice on questions all about the stay in Berlin.
          
  
    
  

  


          
          On top of making recommendations for your participants, we provide further service like free berlin city maps and brochures. Visitors to the visitBerlin information desk can also purchase tickets for a wide range of Berlin attractions and the Berlin Welcome Card.

Further information, prices and availability details are available upon request.

We'll be glad to assist you with your event planning!
