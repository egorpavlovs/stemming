            EUREF-Campus

                      

                    

                    
                
              The EUREF Campus in the immediate vicinity of the ICE train station Südkreuz in the lively Schöneberg district is a reference location for the Smart City strategy of the state of Berlin. With a climate-neutral energy supply and numerous research projects, the campus is a unique European centre for innovative future projects. At the foot of the famous Gasometer there are 15 locations in the most beautiful industrial architecture with plenty of room for conferences and events for up to 600 people. All event services at EUREF-CAMPUS come from a single source, master chef Thomas Kammeier is responsible for the cuisine.
