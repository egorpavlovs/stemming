            Malzfabrik

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Gründerzeit red brick buildings of the industrial landmark Malzfabrik – Europes biggest malt producer in the past – offers unusual room for new impulses and creative dialog. Malzfabrik  is a great location for pictures, films and event productions, for product presentations, art exhibitions, parties, bigger company events ( in-and outdoors) and workshops. There are a lot of great locations for film and photo shootings in the original fabric halls and more, if you have at your disposal 9 buildings with almost 27.500 sqm and an open-air area of 15.000 sqm with several renovated event locations.
