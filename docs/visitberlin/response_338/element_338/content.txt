            CityCube Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              The multifunctional congress and exhibition arena CityCube Berlin opened in May 2014. On three levels with a total of 50 halls and spaces and a pillar-free convention hall for 5,000 persons, the CityCube and the directly adjacent exhibition halls can host events of all sizes. The flexible wall systems literally make the spaces in CityCube multifunctional. A direct connection to Berlin ExpoCenter City, in particular Hall 7, makes more than 150 spaces and 180,000 sqm available in the nearby vicinity.
