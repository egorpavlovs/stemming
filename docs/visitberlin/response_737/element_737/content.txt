            Hotel Aquino Tagungszentrum

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Aquino Hotel is located in the Mitte district of central Berlin. There are plenty of shops, restaurants and bars within close walking distance of the hotel. The Oranienburger Tor U-Bahn station and tram stop are just four minutes away, and Berlin's central train station (Hauptbahnhof) is about 15 minutes away on foot.
