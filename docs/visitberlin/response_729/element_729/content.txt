             Berlin-Pankow Inclusive Workshop

                      

                    

                    
                
              With MEET+CHANGE, we provide access to special locations for meetings and workshops in Berlin by making rooms in a wide range of social and cultural facilities exclusively available for event planners. visitBerlin’s Berlin Convention Office provides professional event management support before, during and after your event.
This inclusive workshop offers two separate meeting rooms and a light-flooded dining room for presentations, workshops or team building activities.
Availability: Mon–Fri, 8am–5pm, Sat+Sun by arrangement.
