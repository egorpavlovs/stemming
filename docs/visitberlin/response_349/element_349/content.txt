            Regent Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Regent Berlin is located on Gendarmenmarkt, an historic square in central Berlin symmetrically flanked by two churches and the Konzerthaus.Unter den Linden, the Brandenburg Gate and the exclusive boutiques on Friedrichstraße are all within a few blocks. The very personal service and luxurious facilities of this 5 star superior hotel with 195 rooms and suites makes it a sought-after address for stars, private and business travellers. Offering ultra-modern conference and meeting rooms as well as the Michelin-starred gourmet restaurant Charlotte & Fritz, the Regent Berlin offers the right ambience.
