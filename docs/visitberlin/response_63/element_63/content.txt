            Steigenberger Hotel Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Steigenberger Hotel Berlin is located in the center of the City West – only a 1-minute walk from Kurfürstendamm.
The Steigenberger Hotel Berlin offers you 17 conference rooms for up to 500 People and free High Speed Internet Access (HSIA) in the entire hotel. Professional days and sustainable action - at the exclusive Convention Center of Steigenberger Hotel, Berlin, these two aspects make for a successful synergy. For our new concept stands for a contemporary philosophy that has transformed our meeting floor. Here, high-tech and modern design merge with responsible ecological awareness, which consistently implements sustainability throughout.
