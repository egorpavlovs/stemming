            The Westin Grand Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Westin Grand Berlin is situated in a prominent location at the corner of Friedrichstrasse and Unter den Linden. With 400 rooms and suites, an experienced team and 10 individually designed conference rooms offering modern communications and conference technology, it is the ideal venue for conferences with up to 240 participants. The capacious foyer on the first floor offers ample space for receptions with up to 440 guests or spacious exhibitions with a view of the lobby and the imposing staircase. An insider tip among event planners: the 3,000 sqm garden with the lights-flooded Drachenhaus.
