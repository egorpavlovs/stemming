            H10 Berlin Ku'damm

                      

                    

                    
                
              The centrally located 4-star-hotel H10 Berlin Ku’damm is the suitable starting point for business travelers, trade fair and congress participants with a direct connection to the Messe Berlin, CityCube Berlin and the Berlin Congress Center. The H10 Berlin Ku’damm is equipped for meeting, seminars and conferences for up to 100 persons in the plenary room.
199 guest rooms – ranging from 24m² Deluxe Double  to a 52m² Appartment on seven floors complement the generous conference area for conference lasting several days.
