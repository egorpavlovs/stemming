            Stone Brewing World Bistro & Gardens - Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              Join us for a meal or a beer in our Bistro’s dynamic and expansive interior, which consists of a 2.400 square meter main hall, a private second-floor mezzanine and the Library Bar, with nearly 25 of its own taps and cozy fireplace seating. Or you can venture out to our organic gardens, designed with plenty of gathering spaces and a 120-square meter event space located in a historic train repair building newly repurposed as a garden house.
