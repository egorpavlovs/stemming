      The Berlin Convention Office    
  

  
    
    

      Planning an event in Berlin? Then you’re in the right place! Germany’s capital is a vibrant creative metropolis raising the bar on conference and event standards. Whether planning an event, needing support for your official congress application for Berlin or a recommendation – the Berlin Convention Office of visitBerlin helps you find the ideal location for every event, and the perfect service provider for every idea. As your key contact in Berlin, we support you free of charge on your way to a successful event.
