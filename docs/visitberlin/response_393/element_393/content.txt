            22nd Congress of Capital Medicine and Health 2019

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
The Capital Congress has established itself as a fixed date for politics, clinics, health care, medicine, care and cost-bearers. First-hand information and a meeting point for exchange and communication are the hallmarks of this congress. The current health and social policy developments will be discussed at the Capital Forum Health Policy, the umbrella of the Capital Congress. Here, the participants of the management congress Hospital Clinic Rehabilitation, the German Congress of Conferences and the German Physicians' Forum come together, all of whom have free access to the Capital Forum Health Policy. The annual management event of the health sector attracted significantly more than 8,000 participants in the Berlin CityCube. In around 200 individual events, top politicians, scientists and representatives of all health care groups discussed the future of health care.
