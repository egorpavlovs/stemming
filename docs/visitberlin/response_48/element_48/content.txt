            Making TV at Filmpark Babelsberg

                      

                    

                    
                
              Our TV professionals will take you under their wing and turn you into tomorrow's TV stars. With us you not only look "behind the scenes", but you actively create your own show. Learn how to create a TV magazine including external reports, expert panels, warm-up and audience - live, in colour and for participation. You may stand at the camera, assist our make-up artists, or actively participate in the design of the show as a guest in the audience. And watch your ready-cut TV magazine on the screen at an optional evening event - show business up close!
Duration approx. 3-5 hours
