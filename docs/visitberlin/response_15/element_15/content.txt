            11th Annual Meeting of the German Society of Biomechanics (DGfB)

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
The aim of the society is to further develop and promote the science of biomechanics to the greatest extent possible in its central importance for biology, medicine, veterinary medicine and sport as a whole. The conference will be accompanied by a specialist industrial exhibition.
