            Berlin - Capital of Culture and Creativity

                      

                    

                              

An enormous range of cultural events, cosmopolitanism and diversity, experimental space, global networks, and a high degree of creative input – Berlin is an incubator for ideas and trends and thus has a magical appeal to culture enthusiasts and creative people from all over the world.



Experience Berlin - 365 days a year, 24 hours a day



The city has 180 museums and collections to offer, not to mention 440 galleries, three opera houses, eight major symphony orchestras, 130 cinemas, around 150 theatres and stages, and numerous other cultural facilities. From the Blue Man Group to modern dance performances in the Radialsystem, through to the treasures of the world’s cultures in the restored museums on Museum Island – hardly any other city can keep pace with the range of cultural events Berlin has to offer. And that's not all: creative centres and networks such as betahaus, ÏMA Design Village, and Soho House promote networking and the exchange of ideas.



So much creative potential not only benefits the cultural and creative sectors, but they also make Berlin a highly attractive event and congress location. Be it exhibitions, theatre, opera, concerts or cabaret – the wide selection on offer invites culture enthusiasts to linger and curious people to explore. No matter where your cultural interests may lie, there is something to suit every taste!

For more information, see Berlin www.visitBerlin.de.
