              The bcc: Listed building, but state-of-the-art. Centrally located with charisma. A lot of style, but still comfortable. Functional, but also playful. Elements of Bauhaus architecture and startingly adjustable to needs. Every time different, but very experienced. Deliberate services, well balanced. Packages and flat rates give the collective joy of untroubled projects. Easy experience: exceptional service.
