            Estrel Congress Center

                      

                    

                    
                
              Host venue to upwards of 1,800 events a year, the Estrel Congress Center is Berlin’s premiere event location. Whether a conference, gala, trade fair or media event, the congress center’s 269,000 sq ft of multifunctional event space offers the flexibility to host the broadest spectrum of events. 75 conference rooms are available in a variety of sizes, all outfitted with the very latest in technical equipment. The adjacent 1,125-room Estrel Hotel coupled with the show productions presented at the in-house Estrel Festival Center, make the Estrel Berlin one of Europe’s most sought after event locations.
