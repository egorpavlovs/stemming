            ESMT - European School of Management and Technology 

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Berlin campus of the European School of Management and Technology is located in the listed building at Schlossplatz that once was the house of the East German Council of State. The 4,000 sqm of available space behind the historic façade of this private business school offers many possibilities for your event ideas. The rooms include two lecture halls, various seminar rooms, two large foyers, and the Auditorium Maximum, offering variable seating options for up to 360 people. Or you can use 4,000 sqm garden, right in the heart of Berlin’s historic centre.
