            Palais Veranstaltungs GmbH

                      

                    

                    
                
              As the central point of contact for event requests on the grounds of ‘Kulturbrauerei' the 'Palais Veranstaltungs GmbH' supports you with the utmost professionalism in the planning, implementation and follow-up of your event. The site offers an historic and industrial surrounding and a variety of spatial concepts, which can be efficiently and individually combined or booked separately. All venues are equipped with modern sound, light & presentation technology and offer the ideal setting for all kinds of occasions.
