            Berlin Speakers Pool - Home to the city`s most interesting minds

                      

                    

                            

          

      
        We will find you top-class moderators and inspiring speakers

    
  

  


          
          You are looking for an inspiring keynote speaker, an experienced workshop leader or presenter?

We have an outstanding network in Berlin with interfaces to the political sphere, industry, the arts and sciences in Germany’s capital city.

We locate high-profile speakers with the knowledge needed for your audience. We contact them on your behalf, establishing availability and fees. We will be glad to provide support until the contract is signed. There are no extra charges - neither brokerage fees nor travel costs. You simply choose your preferred speaker and we arrange the rest.

        
      
    

  

      
        Benefit from our network and meet local experts in the fields of: 

    
  

  


          
        
          

  
                
                
              
              
              
              
            
            

  



      
        
            visitBerlin

        
      
      

      
        Innovation / Future / Digital - Business - Politics - Science / Research - Society / Culture

    
  

      
    

  


          
          The visitBerlin Berlin Convention Office (BCO) now offers an innovative service for high-profile event programmes at conferences and meetings in the city. Berlin Speakers Pool brings together prominent figures, experts, moderators and presenters from Berlin, arranging the contacts to provide an inspiring contribution to your event.

We look forward to your inquiry!
