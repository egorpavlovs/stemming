            Adlershof con.vent. // Science and Technology Park

                      

                    

                    
                
              Berlin-Adlershof has been a beacon of the Spirit of Innovation for more than 100 years: once the cradle of German Aviation, Adlershof now Looks back on 10 years as one of the largest Technology parks in Europe and the most important Location for science, Business and media in Berlin. Close to Berlin`s future Airport, an area of 420 hectres is home to ten non-University Research Institutes, 6 Institutes of Humboldt University, and over 1000 companies and other businesses. The density of entrepreneurs, scientists, founders and students is unparalleled.
Your event will benefit from this inspiring environment!
