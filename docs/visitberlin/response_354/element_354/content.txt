            Waldorf Astoria Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Waldorf Astoria Berlin offers versatile spaces for events, meetings and celebrations of any kind, whether for small, intimate gatherings starting at ten people or for large functions for up to 200 guests. The 330 square meter ballroom on the second floor of the luxury hotel is the ideal setting for extravagant weddings, festive ceremonies or banquets. Five private salons, equipped with the latest technology and natural light, and a professional business center round off the offer. A highlight of the hotel is the open air event terrace on the sixth floor with space for up to 250 people.
