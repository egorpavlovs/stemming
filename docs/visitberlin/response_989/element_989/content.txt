            Eventwide Berlin

                      

                    

                            
                          To ensure customers can enjoy a range of independent and individual products, Eventwide designs and produces its entire event & rental furniture itself. Over the last few years, we have developed a furniture system with its own look and feel – and with all elements compatible, your choice is fully flexible.
We are constantly working on expanding our product range – both a challenge and an inspiration. This enables us to react directly and swiftly to trends and customers’ wishes – and, above all, quite aside from our own vision, realise your perfectly designed event. In this way, every piece of rental furniture we create optimises the entire spectrum of the Eventwide world.
You can find more details and view all our products at http://berlin.eventwide.com
