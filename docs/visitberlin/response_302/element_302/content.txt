            Meet Berlin at IMEX Frankfurt

                      

                    

                            

          

      
        From 15 to 17 May at booth F100/35

    
  

  


          
          With more than 3,500 national and international exhibitors, IMEX Frankfurt am Main is one of the leading trade fairs in the congress, meeting, event and incentive industry. Numerous networking events, workshops and seminars are aimed daily at trade visitors, exhibitors and hosted buyers. From 15 to 17 May you will meet the visitBerlin Berlin Convention Office at the Germany Pavilion F100/35 of the German Convention Bureau (GCB) and get a first impression of what makes Berlin such a unique and modern destination for conferences, conventions and incentives.

        
      
    

  

      
        BERLIN 365/24 and the world's smallest disco and meeting room

    
  

  


          
          This year you can expect a live illustration and information about the campaign "BERLIN 365/24". This slogan highlights Berlin's role as a centre for culture and events, for freedom and for countless opportunities for meetings. Meet the smallest location at the booth of Berlin: The visitBerlin Berlin Convention Office will show the teledisko, the world’s smallest disco, which hosts parties in a space the size of a phone booth.
