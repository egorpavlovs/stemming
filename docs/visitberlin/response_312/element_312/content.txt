            Our service for event planners

                      

                    

                            

          

      
        Helping you plan your event in Berlin

    
  

  


          
          As the city of Berlin’s official Convention Office, we provide you with free, impartial advice to help you plan your successful event in Berlin.

Looking for a conference hotel, event location or service provider? As a specialist for Berlin’s meetings and event industry with a strong network of partners from the Berlin MICE sector, we are sure to help you find the right partners for your event format.

        
      
    

  

  
  
    
        Our services for your planning phase

    
    
      
      
              Comprehensive information on Berlin as an event location
          
        
              Providing information material and image films on Berlin
          
        
              Availability enquiries in conference hotels and event locations
          
        
              Facilitating contacts to the event services you need
          
        
              Recommending suitable incentives and accompanying programmes
          
        
              Organising and accompanying site inspections
          
  
    
  

  

  
  
    
        Our services for your implementation phase

    
    
      
      
              Reserving and booking blocks of rooms and event locations
          
        
              Promoting your event in our Congress Calendar
          
        
              Arranging the Berlin Welcome Card for your guests
          
        
              Affiliate marketing: Our hotel booking screen on your webpage
          
        
              Our visitBerlin information desk for your event (on request)
          
        
              Berlin information as well as free city maps for your guests
          
  
    
  

  


          
          We also support you in making your official congress application and in preparing a bid book for Berlin.

        
      
    

  

      
        Interested?

    
  

  


          
          Leverage our team’s expert knowledge of event locations and service providers and the Berlin Convention Office’s large partner network! Our professional service actively provides the support you need to make your event ideas a complete success.

Simply email convention@visitBerlin.de or browse our Meeting Guide Berlin to find the right services for your event.
