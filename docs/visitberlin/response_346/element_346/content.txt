            Museum für Naturkunde

                      

                    

                    
                
              This evening will be unforgotten: the Museum für Naturkunde (Natural History Museum) offers the unique opportunity to hold an exclusive dinner or reception in the Exhibition halls. World famous fossil-celebrities, such as the highest mounted dinosaur skeleton Brachiosaurus brancai or the Berlin specimen of Archaeopteryx lithographica will be your hosts and overwhelm your guests in an unique atmosphere. Further theme halls attract with visual Highlights such as the "biodiversity wall" or the "wet collections". On Occasion, empty or Special Exhibition halls may be available.
