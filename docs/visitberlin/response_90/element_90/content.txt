            ProfitCard 2019

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
ProfitCard has a long tradition of concentrating on the economic aspects of card business - both on the private and corporate customer side.In 2019, the focus will be on current developments in the areas of digitization of the card business, online payment, mobile payment and debit card business. ProfitCard offers first-hand insider knowledge and excellent networking with the industry's decision-makers and designers.Berlin is the perfect conference venue: 25hours Hotel Bikini, one of the top 10 design hotels in Europe, opposite the Gedächtniskirche.Frank Braatz, publisher of the trade magazine SOURCE, develops the programme every year with the support of top people Michael Seifert and Jochen Siegert and top-class speakers.
