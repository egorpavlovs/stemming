            Travel relaxed to Berlin with the Deutsche Bahn Event Ticket

                      

                    

                            

          

      
        An excellent offer: Get the best price ticket with Deutsche Bahn!

    
  

  


          
          In cooperation with the Berlin Convention Office of visitBerlin and the Deutsche Bahn* you travel safely and conveniently to your congress, meeting or event in Berlin.

        
      
    

  

  
  
    
        Event Ticket for a single journey, for use only on one specific connection

    
    
      
      
              2nd class 54,90 €*
          
        
              1st class 89,90 €*
          
  
    
  

  

  
  
    
        Event Ticket for single journey, for use on all connections

    
    
      
      
              2nd class 74,90 €*
          
        
              1st class 109,90 €*
          
  
    
  

  

      
        Get the guaranteed lowest price!

    
  

  


          
          

        
      
    

  


          
          

Make the most of your travel time by using it for work, relaxation or enjoying the on-board services of Deutsche Bahn. You can also rest assured that your long-distance travel is completely powered by electricity generated by renewable resources.

The Event Ticket (travel distance greater than 100 km) includes the City Ticket for travel to and from over 120 German cities in the respective area of validity.

Click here (only in German) for the full range of information about Deutsche Bahn's Event Ticket.

We wish you a pleasant journey!

 

*REFERENCE: Prices are valid until 14 December 2019. If you would like assistance to complete your booking, click here. If you have any technical questions regarding the booking process, simply call +49 (0)1806 – 99 66 44. Our technical hotline is open every day from 8am - 9pm. Calls cost EUR 0.20 from a German landline, and max. EUR 0.60 per call from a mobile network.
