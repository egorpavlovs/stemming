            Capri by Fraser Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              This designer hotel meets all the needs of business and leisure travellers of the Millennium generation and has been designed according to their round-the-clock active lifestyle. The historical details in the building bear witness to the history of the location, which is in an enviable location. A total of 143 apartments are available, from a one-room studio to a two-room luxury apartment. Our Pow-Wow meeting room can accommodate up to 40 people. As a boardroom you can work in peace on business projects, do sports in the 24/7 fitness room or relax and enjoy with friends and colleagues in the restaurant.
