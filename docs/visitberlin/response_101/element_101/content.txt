      Sustainable Meetings Berlin for Sustainable Event Planning    
  

  
    
    

      Planning an event in Berlin – and looking for sustainable event services? At Sustainable Meetings Berlin, we offer an overview of the top partners for your green meetings in Berlin. Here, you find a wide range of information on sustainability and the latest news, soon to be joined by details on our Sustainable Partners – from conference hotels to event locations, catering and technical service providers.


            
            About Sustainable Meetings Berlin
        
    
  

    
        
            
                  


  
          

            




  


  
  
    
        Sustainable Meetings Berlin is the first platform for sustainable event planning in Berlin. Today, green meetings are no longer the latest trend, but a sign of social responsibly. We provide our customers with information and support – helping to ensure their events can be a sustainable success.

    
    
      Iris Lanz

        Director visitBerlin Berlin Convention Office

    
  

  


  
          

            




  


  
  
    
        With Sustainable Meetings Berlin, we are working towards the goal of a sustainable Berlin. We share our beliefs and ideas, and show the opportunities for sustainable business activities and practices. For Berlin Event, sustainability is about saving resources and protecting the environment.

    
    
      Olaf Marsson

        CEO Berlin Event

    
  

  


  
          

            




  


  
  
    
        We are confirmed sustainability fans – a commitment evident in self-generated energy, bees on the roof, EMAS certification and much more. Sustainable Meetings Berlin inspires us with innovative ideas for more sustainability in this sector.

    
    
      Anke Stopperich

        Director BESONDERE ORTE Umweltforum Berlin GmbH

    
  

  


  
          

            




  


  
  
    
        As the first climate-neutral event supplier, sustainability is an essential part of our company philosophy. The Sustainable Meetings Berlin network enables us to learn from our colleagues' experiences and to introduce further change processes.

    
    
      Manja Jessat

        Sales and Communication Management RENT4EVENT GmbH

                      Green travel

                  
                                      
                      
                    
                                  
              
                              
                  
                      With DB rail’s Veranstaltungsticket (Meetings Ticket), participants from within Germany enjoy a sustainable and relaxed journey to Berlin. With 100% green energy, and from €49.50 2nd class.
