            Radisson Blu Hotel, Berlin 

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Radisson Blu Hotel in Berlin offers 15 first-class event and conference facilities with a total size of 2,740 square meters. Choose between 2 very different and unique conference areas. The large and modern disposition area ideal for meetings, seminars and conferences, or you book our DomLounge, a 2-story meeting and event location at the top floor of the Berlin conference hotel. Centrally located, the Radisson Blu, Berlin offers a combination of congress hotel and event location. Our hotel, located directly at the Spree River and the Berlin Cathedral, is easily reachable by car.
