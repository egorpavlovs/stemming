            KÖRPERWELTEN at People Museum 

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Menschen Museum (People Museum) not only offers deep insights into the anatomy of the human body, but also a perfect ambience for events focusing on topics such as medical care, health and body&mind. Connected to the museum is the approx. 400 sqm large conference area with space for max. 200 people. It is suitable for presentations, events, product launches, lectures, conferences, company incentives, but also for seminars or training courses. The equipment includes high-quality conference furniture and modern presentation equipment. A professional event team takes over the complete organization of personnel, catering, technology and ambience supporting program.
