            Stiftung Preußische Schlösser und Gärten Berlin-Brandenburg

                      

                    

                    
                
              Are you looking for a historical and stylish location for your next event? Invite your guests to the historical palaces and parks of Potsdam, Berlin and the State of Brandenburg to experience a lively journey through time, to the glamorous era of the Prussian queens and kings. Palaces, historical rooms, orangerys and open park spaces offer various possibilities to enthuse your guests in a very special way. Receptions, gala dinner, conferences, readings or congresses ... we would be glad to prepare an individual offer for your desired location.
