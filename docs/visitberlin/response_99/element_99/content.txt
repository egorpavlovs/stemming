      Berlin Convention Office Services    
  

  
    
    

      As the central contact partner in Berlin, the visitBerlin Berlin Convention Office supports you on behalf of the city, providing free, impartial advice to help you plan your successful event in Berlin. Leverage our team’s many years of experience in the meeting and event sector and let us help you find the right partner for each event. Our strong network ranges from professional event services and MICE experts to well-known Berlin figures and speakers as well as scientific institutions, and the economic sector and the arts. Simply contact us – we look forward to your enquiry!

      Meeting Guide Berlin    
  

  
    
    

      The place to finding what you are looking for! A suitable conference hotel for your Berlin event? An outstanding event location to hire? Or full-service event agencies? Meeting Guide Berlin, our event planning tool, gives you an overview of first-class partners in sectors from conference hotels and locations to event catering, technical services and transport. Here, you are sure to find the right partner to realise your inspiring ideas for your event.
