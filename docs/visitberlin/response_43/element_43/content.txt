            Berlin RELAX

                      

                    

                    
                
              Experience an exclusive combination of personal city tour and charming glimpse behind the scenes of Berlin. In small groups we will take you on a relaxing journey through the capital city, accompanied and chauffeured by our experienced rickshaw drivers. In addition to attractions and quirky locations, we will also provide you with striking delicacies. A tour that invites you to enjoy, stay a while... and come back.
