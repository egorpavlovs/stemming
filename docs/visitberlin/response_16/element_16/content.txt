            BUS2BUS

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
BUS2BUS wants to bring the bus industry into the digital age. Therefore, there will also be a startup area on the BUS2BUS 2019. This platform is designed by providers of new, fresh ideas from a wide variety of industries, in particular from the digital media.The products provide inspiration for new business opportunities in the bus industry. Manufacturers and bus operators can get in touch with young companies, bus companies, manufacturers, start-ups and digital thinkers can get to know each other. The Startup Area facilitates the exchange and potential partnership between traditional business models and new digital solutions.As an umbrella organisation of around 3,000 bus transport companies, the umbrella organisation of the German bus industry convenes in parallel to the BUS2BUS 2019 in Berlin.Top forum for all current topicsPlatform for Strategies, Trends and Future Topicsmore than 500 expected participantsclose interlocking and direct impulse exchange with the BUS2BUS
