            International Congress »Fontane’s Media 1819-2019«

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
As a literary archive, research institution, and place of cultural 
memory of significant national importance, the Theodor Fontane 
Archive—together with its national and international partners—will be 
contributing a rich academic and cultural program to fontane.200.The Theodor Fontane Archive invites you to experience the academic 
highlight of fontane.200, which will take place from June 13 to June 16,
 2019. In collaboration with experts and other cultural players, the 
Theodor-Fontane-Archive will be organizing the multiday international 
conference, »Fontane’s Media 1819-2019«, at the Neues Palais Campus of 
the University of Potsdam. Lectures, presentations, and discussions will
 focus on the importance of media for Theodor Fontane’s body of work, 
and the historical and present-day relevance of the author and media 
worker will receive special emphasis.Contact and Information T. +49 (0)331/ 201396fontanearchiv@uni-potsdam.de
