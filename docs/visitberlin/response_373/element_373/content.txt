            RADIALSYSTEM V

                      

                    

                    
                
              A Future lab with exemplary character: RADIALSYSTEM V is a space for possibilities, for innovation, for the experimental and top notch dialogue. The flexible and multifaceted building with generously sized rooms, intimate studios, a large sun deck and terrace along the river is ideal for creative events, upscale conferences, symposiums and media productions. Spread over 6 stories an event space of approximately 2500m2 (26,909 ft2) is available and can accommodate up to 800 people. Unique highlights are the contrast between industrial architecture and modern design and the waterfront location.
