            Museum für Kommunikation Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Museum for Communication Berlin was opened in 1898 as the Imperial Postal Museum (Reichspostmuseum), making it the world’s oldest postal museum. The heart of the building is the light-flooded central atrium surrounded by a series of galleries. At night, light is emitted through the atrium roof so that the museum resembles a blue crystal – creating a fascinating landmark in Berlin’s cityscape. The museum’s historical ambience is an ideal setting for your event – whether a gala evening, concert, reception or conference.
