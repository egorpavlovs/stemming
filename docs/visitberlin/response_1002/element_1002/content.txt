            TUBS GmbH  TU Berlin ScienceMarketing

                      

                    

                            
                          TUBS has 15 years of experience as a reliable partner for the TU Berlin, as well as for scientific institutions, research-orientated companies, scientific societies and other organizations, from the whole of Germany. We offer our customers professional support throughout all phases of the event management process, from the application stage, right through to the accounting. Our event portfolio covers everything from small events, such as workshops and symposiums, to large trade shows and expositions, including international conferences of over 6,000 participants.
