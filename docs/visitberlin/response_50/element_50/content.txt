            Mr. X - a detective game in the heart of Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              Mr. X game takes you on a fast-paced chase in the centre of Berlin. In small detective groups, you try to track down Mr. X, either on foot or using public transport. You will find out from police headquarters about the most recent sightings of Mr. X. At the same time, Mr. X will receive information on how close on his heels the investigators are. Get your daily dose of adrenaline with this exciting detective game!
