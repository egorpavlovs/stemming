            Kommunikationskongress 2019

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
Innovative communication strategies, collaborative organizations, Influencer Relations, the future of the professionAlso in 2017, the bcc Berlin Congress Center will be the venue for the communication congress. On the three levels you will find a varied program with best cases, discussions and workshops. The spectacular dome hall also provides the perfect ambience for keynotes and other highlights.The organizer is the Federal Association of German Press (BdP).
