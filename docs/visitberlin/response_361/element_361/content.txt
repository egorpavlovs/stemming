            AXICA Kongress- und Tagungszentrum

                      

                    

                    
                
              A place for meeting and communicating, space for finding new prospects and inspiration. AXICA - the Conference and Event Center is located on Pariser Platz, right alongside the Brandenburg Gate. Within its expressive architecture, designed by none less than Frank O. Gehry, you´ll be enlived by the energy and dynamism of an inspired venue and uplifted by masterful cuisine. At the AXICA your conferences, meetings, presentations, receptions and gala dinners will acquire a unique face. With scale floor plans in 3D we create high-end location visualisations for your event. This gives you a realistic picture of the location right from the start. Inspired? We´ll be happy to assist you.
