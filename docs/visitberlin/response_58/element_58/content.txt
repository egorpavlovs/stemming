            Hollywood Media Hotel

                      

                    

                    
                
              217 airconditioned bedrooms including junior suites, deluxe rooms and suites are dedicated to famous international film stars and film directors. A couple of days or “9 ½ Weeks” you will experience the overwhelming atmosphere of the Hollywood Media Hotel. “Modern Times”- means our conference facilities are tailored to your requirements in 9 air-conditioned conference rooms with daylight and excellent conference service. The hotel-owned cinema theatre with 99 seats is an ideal venue for presentations, symposiums, movie shows and even “All that Jazz”.
