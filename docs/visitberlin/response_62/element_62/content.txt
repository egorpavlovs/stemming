            Park Inn by Radisson Berlin Alexanderplatz

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Park Inn Hotel Berlin Alexanderplatz with 1012 rooms & suites on 37 floors and a total height of 150m, is the second largest hotel in Germany and the tallest hotel in Berlin. The 4 star hotel provides a fantastic stay in a central location of Berlin with many amenities and facilities nearby. Hold your event in Berlin´s highest event location Panorama37 or place your meeting in one of our eleven variously combinable conference rooms. We are looking forward to welcoming you!
