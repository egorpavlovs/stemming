            The Ritz-Carlton, Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Ritz-Carlton, Berlin, centrally located at Potsdamer Platz, combines ideal event opportunities with exceptional service of the highest level. Ranging from exclusive board meetings and product launches to gala events: with one of the largest ballrooms in Berlin and a generous foyer, the 5* superior grand hotel forms the perfect framework for all kinds of events, for 10 to 1,500 guests on a total surface of 19,370 square feet. Besides, The Ritz-Carlton, Berlin offers a remarkable interior design and outstanding restaurants and bars, such as Brasserie Desbrosses, serving regional and seasonal food.
