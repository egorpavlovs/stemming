            ITB Berlin – The World's Leading Travel Trade Show 2019

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
The latest travel quotesVisitors who visit the ITB Berlin can organize their "world trip" geographically or thematically according to their interests.In several exhibition halls, the latest travel trends are also presented across countries and regions. These include, for example, cultural tourism, adventure or wellness travel. In addition to the exhibition stands of the individual countries, concerts, show inserts and culinary offers make the ITB Berlin an unforgettable experience.ITB Berlin - a success storySince March 1966, travel agencies, media representatives and interested parties from all continents have been coming to Berlin this year in order to inform themselves and to conclude their business deals for the coming year. At the first ITB Berlin, nine exhibitors from five countries took part. With the growth of the tourism industry, Berlin's travel trade fair has also grown from year to year.Since 1985, business travel has been an important topic, since 2004, the world's largest tourism congress has taken place with the ITB Congress Market Trends & Innovations.Visit visitBerlin at the ITB Berlin in Hall 12 Berlin has been exhibiting at the ITB Berlin in Hall 12 for several years, together with the Reiseland Brandenburg. Around 75 tourism partners present the trend themes and offers of the capital region. Visitors' magnets are the experience islands at the Berlin-Brandenburg booth, which invite the participants to participate, discover and linger each year with new ideas.
