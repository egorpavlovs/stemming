            L & D Event Catering

                      

                    

                            
                          With enthusiasm and dedication, L & D creates culinary pleasures, puts ideas perfectly into the scene and spoils you with extraordinary and sophisticated event catering. L & D creates individual and flexible concepts for events with 30 or 3,000 people at any desired location. The professional integration of gastronomic trends, absolute customer orientation and personal support and advice guarantee variety, enjoyment and perfect service. Numerous partners and exclusive premises make almost anything possible. From the first request to the offer until the end of your celebration, we will always be at your disposal professionally and personally.
