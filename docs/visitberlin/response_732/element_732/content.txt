            Eurostars Hotel Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Eurostars Berlin, opened in 2011, is a business hotel located in the German capital´s main business hub. One of the best hotels due to its striking architecture and avant-garde interior design. The Eurostars Berlin has 221 rooms ( 15 suites ), eight meeting rooms measuring a total of 600 sqm, a gourmet restaurant and a fitness centre with a indoor swimmimg pool.
