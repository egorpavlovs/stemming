            ewerk 

                      

                    

                    
                
              The event areas available at ewerk are diverse and each one highly individual, so that very flexible room offers can be submitted in size and style.
At the heart of the ewerk are two event halls with industrial charm, each with a floor space of 600 sqm. Hall C impresses with its warm appearance. Hall F, with its rough atmosphere, offers a wide range of space.
The roof terrace of the ewerk offers the visitor a magnificent view over the centre of Berlin and through the integrated skylounge, it can be celebrated here in style with up to 200 people even in bad weather.
