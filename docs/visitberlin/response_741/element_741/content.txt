            STATION - Berlin 

                      

                    

                    
                
              An historic and popular event location, STATION-Berlin lies at the heart of Berlin, right next to world-famous Potsdamer Platz. The site holds an approximate total of 23,000 m² of space available for lease. Originally the “Dresdner Bahnhof”, this former train station is now a protected historic landmark. There are eight different halls of various sizes ideal for conferences and conventions, exhibitions and trade shows, galas and events. They are also perfectly equipped to accommodate training sessions, smaller events or other kinds of gatherings. STATION-Berlin has an array of its own resources and a well-coordinated team of partner businesses to help in the execution of your event.
