            Crowne Plaza Berlin - Potsdamer Platz

                      

                    

                    
                
              Situated in the heart of Berlin’s political and cultural scene, just a short journey away from the Brandenburg Gate and Checkpoint Charlie, Crowne Plaza® Berlin - Potsdamer Platz is an ideal location for corporate or private events. Within the building, formerly of one of Europe’s most important mail centers, you will find a hotel that perfectly blends historic architecture with modern design. Our 256 contemporary rooms and 8 function rooms across 500 m² (5400 sqft) offer the perfect setting for creative meetings with up to 200 delegates or stylish events. The hotel also features a restaurant, an ambient bar and a spa area with a 24h fitness lounge.
