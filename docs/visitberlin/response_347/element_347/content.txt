            PANORAMAPUNKT

                      

                    

                    
                
              Experience Berlin from above with a 360° view of all the sights in the capital starts with the fastest elevator in Europe, which whisks you to the 24th floor of the Kollhoff-Tower. There guests will be greeted by floor-to-ceiling windows inside our café or outside on our 560m² roof terrace on two floors.
You can take advantage of our network of professional partners in catering, technical equipment and entertainment or simply rent the facilities and coordinate your event yourself.
If you are interested in planning an event at PANORAMAPUNKT give us a call or send an e-mail. We are also happy to show you around in person.
