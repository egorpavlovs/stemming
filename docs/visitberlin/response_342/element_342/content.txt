            InterContinental Berlin

                      

                    

                    
                
              Located adjacent to Tiergarten park and in the heart of City West, InterContinental® Berlin reflects the vibrancy of Germany’s capital city. With 558 comfortable rooms and suites as well as 45 modern conference rooms across 6200 m² (67,000 sqft), InterContinental® Berlin is one of the leading conference venues in Europe. From exclusive meetings in private dining rooms to balls for up to 1,500 people in our pillar-free ballroom, our experienced team will be happy to assist with the professional planning of your event. Gourmet restaurant Hugos on the 14th floor and Marlene Bar offer gastronomic variety. With its 1,000 m², the SPA InterContinental is the perfect place to relax.
