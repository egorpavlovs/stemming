            Berlin TV-Tower - Berliner Fernsehturm 

                      

                    

                    
                
              The TV Tower always takes centre stage. This landmark is not only at the geographical centre of the city: at 368 metres, it towers over the skyline, guiding people back to the heart of Berlin. But the Berlin TV Tower itself is always worth a visit too, as only here can you experience the perfect combination of fascinating history, unique style, stunning views, delicious food and friendly customer service. Whether it's a reception, party, product presentation or wedding celebration – we offer tailor-made catering options and a unique 360° view over Berlin.
