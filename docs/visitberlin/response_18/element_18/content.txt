            11th Jobmesse Berlin 2019

                      

                    

                            
                        
                
            
Berlin is not only the capital of Germany but also the capital of culture, economy and science. The country's largest city is the centre of public interest and not only because the state is governed from here. Berlin should therefore not be missing from the jobmesse deutschland tour calendar. Countless career opportunities and a high location quality are top prerequisites for the jobmesse berlin. More than 100 exhibitors regularly present themselves in the Mercedes world at Salzufer. This year even for the tenth time!
