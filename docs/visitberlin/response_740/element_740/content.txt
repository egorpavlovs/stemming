            Quatsch Comedy Club

                      

                    

                    
                
              We heartly welcome you to Quatsch Comedy Club! You can rent our location for nearly every purpose - from corporate events, conferences and incentives to concerts, shootings and parties. We offer you a very special and historical theatre atmosphere to present an unforgettable event to your guests. Fascinate your clients, appreciate your employees or just experience an incomparabely night with your business partners. 
You want to enjoy our show in your own location? Just talk to us! Together we’ll create an individual show concept and chose the best comedy act for entertainment program.
