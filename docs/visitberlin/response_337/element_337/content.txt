            Brandenburg Gate Museum

                      

                    

                    
                
              The Brandenburg Gate Museum is hidden in the pulsating heart of the metropolis Berlin on Pariser Platz. The location is an insider tip for exclusive events at a significant place. Already the entrance area impresses with minimalist architecture complemented by elegant interior and a unique atmosphere because of the direct view to the Brandenburg Gate. The absolute highlight is the 270° panoramic wall with 87-HD-Screens and a 38-channel sound system. In the so-called Immersive Showroom Berlin you can create different room atmospheres adjusted for every occasion. It´s also possible to use it for modern storytelling or to give a thrilling, innovative presentation.
