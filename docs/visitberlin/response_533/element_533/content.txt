            The Berlin Welcome Card

                      

                    

                            

          

      
        The official ticket to experience the German capital!

    
  

  


          
          Berlin is the event location of your choice? Then provide our Berlin Welcome Card to your attendees and guests to make their stay in Berlin an unforgettable experience.The city card includes free use of public transportation and reductions for 200 attractions. Benefit from attractive commissions for the resale of Germany’s most popular city card to your guests and attendees. Congress organisers who integrate the Berlin WelcomeCard into their catalogue, website or congress promotion, will likewise be able to make additional savings.Klick >>> HERE <<< to order your Berlin Welcome Card via our online ticketshop.
