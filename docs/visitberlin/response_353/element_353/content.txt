            Telekom Hauptstadtrepräsentanz

                      

                    

                    
                
              Actively connecting people – virtually and personally: Located in the center of Berlin, between Gendarmenmarkt and the City Palace, Deutsche Telekom provides an exclusive venue for your event. An elegant and inspirational atmosphere of old and new, the past and the future, awaits your guests. Originally built as the Central Imperial Telegraph Office, the Representative Office is the largest and oldest building from the early days of telecommunication that is still preserved. Deutsche Telekom's Berlin Representative Office provides an iconic setting where internal and external companies can hold conferences, galas, PR events, or political events, for up to 1.200 people.
