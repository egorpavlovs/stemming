			Desert Explorer	
		
		This fantastic and thrilling day gives clients the opportunity to experience something totally unique in the desert. It sees clients being picked up by our experienced guides in the afternoon and following a short briefing will drive for approximately an hour to the Desert to start the team building challenges which will include Rope maze, Scavenger hunt, The mine field, water Balloon volley ball and kart race.

As the afternoon activities come to an end and the sun goes down we will head to our traditional Bedouin style camp for an unforgettable evening of Arabian hospitality. At the camp try a camel ride, indulge in henna painting, smoke the traditional hubbly bubbly pipe or just simply relax over a drink before a sumptuous 3 course BBQ dinner is served, with the evening being rounded off by truly mystical belly dancer show following which we will transport you back to Dubai where you will reach by approximately 10pm.

Activity focus & benefits:
• Planning and strategy
• Time & crisis management
• Problem solving
• Understand synergy of team work, coordination, combined effort towards achieving common goals
• Communication
• Cooperation

Explorer Tours will provide the following for this package:
• Toyota land cruisers for transport from Dubai and back
• All above team building activities
• Experienced and qualified instructors to run the team building activities
• Shisha, Arabic coffee
• Belly dance show
• BBQ dinner
• Soft drinks and water throughout the trip
Kit list for participants:
• Loose ands comfortable clothing
• Hat and sun glasses
• Sun cream
• Trainers/ sand shoes
• Jumber/jacket (from Nov- March)
• Medicine (if you are under any medication)
