ABOUT

Partners
DUBAI CONVENTION BUREAU  works together with its Convention and Tourism Partners to promote and market the UAE  as an attractive destination for tourist and business visitors and as a venue for business events and congresses. The ultimate objective is to bring more business to UAE
All business people, institutions and organisations that stand to benefit from this objective and that operate in the tourist and business visitor markets are welcome to become affiliated. What’s more, partners are able to benefit from the activities and marketing communication resources of Dubai Convention Bureau
The advantages of being a partner
DCB  offers you the opportunity to join in UAE Partners’ diverse activities as part of your operations in the tourist and/or business visitor markets. Our work can open up the market for you or introduce you to contacts, such as event managers, congress organisers and tour operators. Just think of trade fairs or of FAM trips . You can also take advantage of online and offline marketing communication resources and other services around the business visitor and tourist markets.
