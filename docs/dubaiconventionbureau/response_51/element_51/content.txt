			Oil Hunt	
		
		Fill up the Oil Drum!

Economical crisis is eating up the World…. Iraq war is going to last a decade. Ships are hijacked from Somalia waters … all for what is OIL…., the black melted gold!!

You are now part of a Mission to discover and pitch a new well in the middle of desert, realizing your dreams to mint money!!  All our guests will participate in that exceptional operation. We are prospecting today a new territory

Team

Our guests will be divided in several teams and we will issue kit list:

 	Map
 	GPS
 	Compass
 	Survival kit in the desert

Mission

 	To locate the Geographical location for Well
 	Build an Oil Derrick
 	To dig to find the oil
 	Burn a Torch, announcing the discovery of oil
 	To collect the oil

Obstacles

Location to find based on clues given. This will be done using GPS systems, Map & Compass.  Participants will also build an Oil Derrick in the desert with the limited resources available. Each target is time bound and should be finished within time frames. It is done as different group competition.

Will your team meet the challenge and end the day with a glorious feast under the stars? It’s up to you and your ability to work together to achieve results.
Activity focus & benefits

 	Understand synergy of team work, coordination, combined effort towards achieving common goals
 	Communication
 	Cooperation
 	Fun & Motivating
 	Practical Leadership
 	Stimulating Creativity
 	Team Dynamics
 	Uniting Teams
