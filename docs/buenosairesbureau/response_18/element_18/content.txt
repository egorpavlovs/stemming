		Infrastructure and Technology
	

	
          
            
    		Buenos Aires features the necessary infrastructure to  meet your  requirements for any international event, congress, or convention as well as to make visitors enjoy a unique stay in the city.
Apart from the international 4 & 5 star hotel meeting rooms, the city features three fairgrounds with a total exhibition area of 150,000 m2 and several smaller-size multi-purpose event facilities.
The city also offers 5 to 3 star hotels including the necessary guests’ accommodation facilities. The city’s most prestigious international chains of hotels hold 30,000 vacancies just considering the 5-to-3 star hotels; this figure has increased year after year.
Besides, the city features leading convention centers, halls and fairgrounds offering cutting-edge technical equipment.
The city also offers the following technology services:
	Broadband Internet Connection
	WI-FI
	Video Conference Facilities
	ADSL
	Simultaneous Translation Service
	Equipment for any type of event.
Some of the most relevant international events held in the city:
	11th AOCA Congress
	LACA 2009 Congress
	Eyeforpharma SFE South America 2009
	WIND EXPO 2010
	2010 World Hemophilia Congress
	2010 Latin America and Argentina Industrial Engineering Congress
	IFTA Congress 2010 (International Family Therapy Association)
	LALCA Congress 2010 (Latin America Congress on Lung Cancer)
