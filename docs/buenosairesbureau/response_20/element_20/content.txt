		Security
	

	
          
            
    		Buenos Aires, considered one of the most secure destinations within Latin America, offers tourists such a structure that guarantees their relaxing stay. Buenos Aires people are friendly with foreigners and they are always willing to help. There are no racial or political related complications in the city or in the country as a whole.
For the inhabitants and tourists’ peace of mind, another independent security system has been created apart from the Argentine Federal Police; however it is advisable to take certain precautions such as not to walk along/take dark streets.
Also the City has a tourist devoted Police Station located at 263 Corrientes Av.; this Police Department offers advice and deals with any inconvenience tourists may suffer.
Due to the geographical and weather features, there is no natural disaster danger in the city of Buenos Aires.
