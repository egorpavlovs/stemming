		Why be a Partner?
	

	
          
            
    		Buenos Aires Convention & Visitors Bureau offers new opportunities to our members by positioning Buenos Aires city as the main destination for holding any event in Latin America.
Membership Benefits:
	Participation in the search for fairs, exhibitions, congresses, conventions and corporate events to be held within the City of Buenos Aires (ICCA member)
	Encouragement and provision ofinstitutional support for congresses, incentive trips, events or convention tourism, fairs, exhibitions, brand and product launching, cultural events, sports, religious events and any other event the Board of Directors considers as feasible to be held within the city of Buenos Aires.
	Participation and promotion of member companies making use of their logos for various communication tools (Websites, Newsletter, and promotion material, amongst others).
	Obtaining the necessary event permits from Governmental Bodies.
	Technical advice and Information on city services and infrastructure to hold your event (convention centers, accommodation, further services, tourism, amongst others)
	Meetings and discussions to boost interaction of member companies.
	Making collaboration agreements between institutions and business organizations
	Promotion and spreading of Buenos Aires City meeting-market statistics as well as their economic impact on the city.
BAC& VB keeps our members updated via the following tools:
	In-house Quarterly Newsletter, electronic-format
	News on the Web Site.
	National and International Travel Information
	Biannual Member Meetings
	Reports of the Tourism Observatory of CGBA.
