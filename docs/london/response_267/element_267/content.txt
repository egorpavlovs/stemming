              Overview

              International and national associations, government agencies and trade organisations partner with TFI Meeting Point to achieve outstanding meetings and excellent results. 
                  Clients, sponsors and audiences are secured through our classic professional conference organising services including legal and financial management and general consultancy; commercial relationship-building; web design and maintenance, including fully automated online abstract application, online registration, online banking, and online accommodation reservations; event logistics; venue and third party supplier negotiations and management; exhibition management; social programme design and delivery; onsite management and post-event evaluation.
                  TFI Group has over 30 years’ experience with 90% repeat business. We are members of ABPCO, IAPCO the Asconet European Partnership and EVCOM.
