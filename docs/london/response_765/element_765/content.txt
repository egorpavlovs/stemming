                Introduction
                
                  Twickenham Stadium, the home of English Rugby, is one of London's premier conference and event venues and a unique location for holding inspired business and social events of all types.

              Overview

              With excellent facilities and a wealth of flexible interior space, Twickenham can effortlessly cater for large-scale business conferences with state-of-the-art AV, private dinners and social receptions or meetings. In all there are 25 dedicated spaces and rooms, many providing attendees and delegates with a view of the famous playing field that has inspired the national rugby team.
                  There is parking for 2,000 cars, a 4* Marriott Hotel and a Virgin Active Club all onsite. Just 20 minutes from Central London and six miles from Heathrow, Twickenham Stadium is justly named the ultimate destination for business and sport.
                  This content has been supplied by Twickenham Stadium Events
