              Overview

              Innovative and inspirational, the Science Museum offers a huge diversity of stunning settings, from the contemporary Wellcome Wing accommodating receptions up to 2000 guests to the Director's Suite for daytime meetings for up to 100. Dine up to 430 alongside famous exhibits in Making the Modern World. 
                  The choice and versatility of the galleries together with a world-wide reputation for excellence and innovation, make it an excellent choice for cutting-edge organisations. The professionalism and experience of the Museum's Corporate Events Team will ensure your event runs smoothly and be truly memorable.
