              Overview

              The acclaimed Saatchi Gallery offers 70,000 sq ft over three floors featuring unique and arresting works of art in 12 expansive gallery spaces of interlinking areas. The gallery can host events from 50 to 1,500 people and is available for both day and evening hire.
                  The unique space boasts high ceilings and striking wooden floors. The Gallery combines a traditional exterior with modern and minimalist interior offering an ideal blank canvas for an elegant setting for any type of event. The space has played host to a variety of events, from sit down dinners to large parties and exhibitions.

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
