              Overview

              The Stafford is renowned for its warm, professional and discreet service. It offers five business venues.
                  The fabulous 380-year-old wine cellars provide a unique and atmospheric venue for private events for up to 44 people seated and 85 standing. Wine tastings are popular, and are carried out by our master sommelier. 
                  The Panel Room, Argyll Room, Pink Room and Sutherland Room offer elegant options for meetings or private dining events, accommodating from eight people for a dinner, to up to 50 people for a reception. 
                  Each of the private rooms are equipped for business meetings. Our expert events team can plan an event, private dining or business meeting and catering tailored to your occasion.
