              Overview

              The 4-star Radisson Blu Edwardian, Kenilworth Hotel is situated in a prime location in Bloomsbury, 50 metres from the British Museum.
                  Suffused with rich colours and decorated with fine artwork and designer furniture, the mood of this Central London hotel is welcoming and calming. This West End hotel's facilities and prime location make it the perfect base for both the business and leisure traveller.
                  The 186 rooms and suites at the Kenilworth Hotel London are richly decorated with sleek ergonomically designed furnishings and marble bathrooms. Advanced telephone systems with voicemail and US 110v plug sockets feature in every room. Deluxe rooms offer a seating area, extra work space and ergonomically designed chairs. Access to bedroom floors is by room key only.
                  This Central London hotel has six function rooms that can accommodate as many as 150 for any type of event from training courses to weddings. A dedicated events team will ensure the success of your event.
                  See below for criterion that have been met that are relevant for pharmaceutical meeting planners.
                  Have you hosted an event involving Healthcare Professionals (HCPs) or Healthcare Organisations (HCOs) in your venue in 2017?
                  - No		
                  Is your venue located within 15 mins travel time to a scientific or healthcare educational venue such as a university hospital, MedCity, research facility etc.?
                  - University College Hospital
                  Why do you feel your property is appropriate to host events involving HCPs & HCOs?
                  - Four star
                  - No casino
                  - Business focussed
                  - Located near to a transport hub
                  Has your venue been independently assessed for your appropriateness to host healthcare events involving HCPs & HCOs?
                  - Yes, by healthcare-venues.com
                  Have your staff been trained on how to manage and work with specialist agencies and corporate clients events involving HCPs and HCOs?
                  - Yes
