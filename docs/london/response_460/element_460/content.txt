              Overview

              This Central London venue provides fluid spaces, including sophisticated boardrooms and multipurpose function rooms, to comfortably accommodate 10 to 350 participants. Take advantage of many different staging options, modern technology and lighting facilities available, as well as onsite catering and banquet services.
                  Steeped in history and elegance, The Waldorf Hilton hotel is the perfect setting for refined celebratory events. Delight guests at private parties and lavish celebrations in the magnificent Adelphi Suite, an extraordinary venue for up to 400 guests. Host a memorable banquet in one of the world's finest venues, Palm Court, which features an historic, impeccably styled interior, with fabulously restored balustrades lending an air of chic decadence and sophistication.
                  Hilton Meetings delivers productive and successful meetings every time. The Waldorf Hilton can make your meetings smarter, environmentally friendly and more personal. Let the talented Team Members oversee every detail to fulfill your meeting and event requirements.
