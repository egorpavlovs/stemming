              Overview

              The award-winning Cafe in the Crypt  is just one of the event spaces available for hire at St Martin-in-Fields, Trafalgar Square.  If you are looking for an unusual central London venue then you have found it! There are three meeting rooms which can be booked individually or altogether; the St Martin's Hall with its stunning double height oak-panelled walls; and the Gallery with its atmospheric brick-vaulted ceilings. St Martin's is steeped in beautiful 18th century and modern architecture, which makes an ideal backdrop for  meetings, conferences, workshops and rehearsals. Catering is deliciously prepared to your requirements by an award-winning teams of chefs.
