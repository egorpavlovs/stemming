              Overview

              Inspire your team with a corporate day at Lee Valley White Water Centre. Choose from a range of thrilling activities that involve the whole team. Activities on offer include White Water Rafting, Hydrospeeding, HotDog sessions, Water Wipeout, dry land activities and more that are not only exciting but will help to build team motivation.
                  Catering is available for your corporate days, including breakfast, buffets, BBQ, afternoon tea or hog roast. 
                  We have three meeting rooms available for you to use; a small, large or combined meeting room. The large meeting room comes complete with AV including: HD data projector, 143 inch projector screen, Blu-Ray DVD player and integrated speakers.
                  This content has been supplied by Lee Valley White Water Centre
