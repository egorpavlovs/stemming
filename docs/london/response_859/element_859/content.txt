              Overview

              The DoubleTree by Hilton London ExCel provides excellent facilities in a stunning contemporary location. 
                  Located in the rejuvenated London Docklands, it is the closest hotel to London City Airport.
                  It is a just a three-minute walk to ExCeL London and ICC London, and the hotel is also easily accessible from Canary Wharf.  
                  Catering for a variety of needs and budgets, the hotel offers several benefits for corporate rate holders, including restaurant discounts, complimentary wifi and free suite upgrades.
                  There are two fully equipped and spacious conference rooms with stunning views. Conference rooms are equipped with latest technology for smooth business presentations and meetings.
                  This content has been supplied by Double Tree by Hilton London ExCel
