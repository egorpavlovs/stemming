          Join us at ITB Berlin
          
		Find us at the Visit Britain stand in hall 18/123: 6-8 March.

Business event organisers, MICE tour operators and meetings and incentives planners are cordially invited to meet the London Convention Bureau at ITB Berlin.
London offers planners:

The most connected city on the planet, with direct flights to 380 destinations
A greater breadth of unique spaces than any other city and more than 1,000 conference venues
More than 140,000 London hotel rooms, from budget to five star
An unparalleled cultural offering, including three of the world's top five museums

Key contactsMartina Heinsohn, Business Development Manager, Europe and Dr. Patrick Patridge, Tourism Marketing, MICE & PR Consultant & Facilitator.
Meet with usClick here to make an appointment. We'll be at the Visit Britain stand in hall 18/123 between 6 and 8 March.
Further informationGet inspired with our guide to what's new in London and find out about incentive programmes in London.
