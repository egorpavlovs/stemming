              Overview

              Planning a conference in Central London? Looking for a meeting and event venue in Hyde Park?
                  Sited in Central London, Corus Hotel Hyde Park is a two-minute walk from Lancaster Gate and eight minutes from Paddington Station on the Heathrow Express. This makes it the ideal venue to conduct conferences, business meetings and events in Central London.
                  With a range of conference venue facilities, four meeting rooms (all with natural light) and 389 rooms available, Corus Hotel Hyde Park can accommodate meetings, events and conferences, whether large or small. 
                  Wi-Fi high speed internet access is now free for all guests at the Corus Hotel Hyde Park.
                  This content has been supplied by Corus Hotel Hyde Park
