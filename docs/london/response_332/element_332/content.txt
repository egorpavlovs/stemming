              Overview

              Why not leave all the hard work to us? We specialise in providing an event unlike any other. 
                  You’ll be the hero for arranging the best company day out ever! It's entertaining, with our award-winning comedian guides on hand to keep the banter flowing and our highly experienced skippers ready to twist and turn your team Thames style! Suitable for all from visiting colleagues with minimal time to born and bred Londoners alike.
                  We work closely with many super swish bars and restaurants along the river from Tom's Kitchen to Gordon Ramsay at The Narrow. We'll tailor your trip for our Thames Rockets to pick you up or drop you off wherever you need, keeping your travel time to an absolute minimum.
