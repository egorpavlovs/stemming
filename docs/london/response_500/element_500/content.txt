              Overview

              Melia White House is one of the leading 4-star superior hotels in central London, at the doorstep of Regent’s Park.
                  An ideal choice for the business traveller, the hotel is within a 5 minute walk from three main underground stations (Great Portland Street, Warren Street and Regent’s Park) and a 5-10 mins taxi ride from St. Pancras (Eurostar), Paddington (Heathrow Express), Euston and Marylebone mainline stations. 
                  This impressive Art Deco property has 581 rooms and 112 apartments, featuring a modern Business Centre with secretarial services, 9 conference rooms, an Executive Lounge, two restaurants and a gym.
                  This content has been supplied by Melia White House
