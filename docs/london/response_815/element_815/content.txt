              Overview

              Smoozy provides a first-class destination management and special event service in London, Edinburgh and the UK.
                  We are specialists in incentives, meetings and special interest groups and offer a comprehensive service providing bespoke solutions for groups of all sizes. 
                  Our team has a wide-ranging knowledge of the corporate market and years of industry experience allowing us to locate, negotiate and coordinate the best suppliers to guarantee the perfect event execution, from start to finish. 
                  We believe that our experience and “can-do” attitude, allied with our creativity and professionalism, will ensure your event is a unique and truly memorable experience.
