              Overview

              E-voyages Group, is the leading incoming group tour operator for custom made voyages in United Kingdom and Ireland.
                  We have been elected DMC of the year 2014 & 2016 by the french Tour Operators.
                  We offer a personal, one-to-one service to our clients and are confident that whatever your budget, our team of experts can create a tailor made tour for you that is sure to delight and surprise your clients.
                  The group is divided in 4 departments : E-voyages Leisure, Incentive Spirit, E-motions Sports and Concierge Service. 
                  E-voyages Group is the preferred partner of The View from The Shard (the highest building in Europe offering a panoramic view of London).
                  The Group is also proud to be the official agent for Warner Bros. Studio Tour London – The Making of Harry Potter. We promote hospitality packages for football, rugby, tennis, golf, Formula 1, cricket.... The group also promote all the events and concerts. Contact us !
