              Overview

              Located in London's avant-garde creative quarter, this hotel has nine sleek new private rooms for up to 300. Unique in London, each room offers full HD video and data projection, with built-in Blu-Ray equipment for high-impact video playback, complimentary Wi-Fi and an atmospheric ambience to the chic Bloomsbury St Bar & Restaurant.
                  See below for criterion that have been met that are relevant for pharmaceutical meeting planners.
                  Have you hosted an event involving Healthcare Professionals (HCPs) or Healthcare Organisations (HCOs) in your venue in 2017?
                  - No		
                  Is your venue located within 15 mins travel time to a scientific or healthcare educational venue such as a university hospital, MedCity, research facility etc.?
                  - University College Hospital
                  Why do you feel your property is appropriate to host events involving HCPs & HCOs?
                  - Four star
                  - No casino
                  - No spa
                  - Business focussed
                  - Located near to a scientific or educational venue
                  - Located near to a transport hub
                  Has your venue been independently assessed for your appropriateness to host healthcare events involving HCPs & HCOs?
                  - Yes, by healthcare-venues.com
                  Have your staff been trained on how to manage and work with specialist agencies and corporate clients events involving HCPs and HCOs?
                  - Yes
