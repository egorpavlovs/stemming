              Overview

              Offering 18 versatile event spaces with a capacity of up to 750 – 1000 guests; perfect for conferences, presentations, meetings, exhibitions and award ceremonies. IET London: Savoy Place is the perfect venue for tech-driven events. 
                  Why choose IET London: Savoy Place? 
                  State-of-the-art technology throughout 
                  Exclusive roof terrace with 180° views across the iconic London skyline, plus exquisite outlooks from the Library balconies  
                  Easily accessible by rail, tube and river.  Just minutes' walk from Covent Garden, Embankment and Temple underground stations
                  Digital branding opportunities
                  In-house AV team of eight
                  500MB of bandwidth available, and capacity to support up to 2GB multiple devices Wi-Fi connection
                  Filming and live streaming capabilities, plus video link between the two lecture theatres to create a larger capacity of 626 two tiered lecture theatres (capacities: 451 and 175)
                  One event coordinator from enquiry to event
                  Innovative and delicious in-house catering
                  This content has been supplied by IET London: Savoy Place
