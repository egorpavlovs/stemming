              Overview

              Thistle City Barbican is a prime location for conferences and business meetings, located on the edge of the city. The hotel has 13 meeting rooms, with the largest room, The Clerkenwell Suite seating up to 170 theatre-style.
                  We also have a number of other rooms suitable for meetings, seminars, briefings and more.We offer a full range of food and drink during your time with us, including an extended range of healthy options.
                  This content has been supplied by Thistle City Barbican
