              Overview

              Park Plaza Westminster Bridge London is an iconic all-in-one meetings and events solution with AIM gold accreditation. A 1,200-square-metre pillar-free Westminster Ballroom for up to 2,000 delegates is complemented by convenient ‘layered’ conference floors and intimate City Boardrooms. Complimentary Wi-Fi, coach parking, group check-in desks and high-level security are available.
                  The 1,019 guestrooms and suites include Superior Rooms, Suites and Penthouse Apartments which can be block-booked for conference delegates. Dining options feature Brasserie Joël, Ichi Sushi & Sashimi, 1WB Lounge & Patisserie, Primo Bar and espressamente illy.
                  This content has been supplied by Park Plaza Westminster Bridge
