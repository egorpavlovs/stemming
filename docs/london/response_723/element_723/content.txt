              Overview

              Novotel London West is a 4-star hotel located in the heart of Hammersmith, west London.
                   
                  This hotel is a healthcare-assessed venue, ideal for national and international events. It also has a fully trained Healthcare Champion onsite and offers healthcare clients tailored packages to suit their needs.
                  The venue's convention centre offers space for up to 2,000. 33 meeting rooms make it one of London's most versatile spaces.The Champagne Suite caters for 1,000 theatre-style, 650+ diners or 2,000 for a reception. 17 of 25 syndicate rooms have natural daylight whilst the Chablis Suite offers 1,360 sqm of exhibition space. 
                  630 spacious and comfortable bedrooms plus three superb dining options complement the hotel's meeting facilities. 
                  The hotel is easily accessible thanks to excellent transport links with three Tube lines (Piccadilly, District and Hammersmith & City) within five-minute walk. It is also very conveniently located for Heathrow Airport and major road and rail links. 
                  Onsite chargeable car parking plus free wifi throughout.
