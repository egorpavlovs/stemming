              Overview

              The Museum of London is a cultural gem right in the heart of the City.
                  Guests can enjoy food and drink throughout the galleries and gardens, which tell the incredible story of London and its people.  
                  The Sackler Hall is the museum's single largest entertaining venue combining a contemporary space with a unique digital ellipse, suitable for dinners, drinks receptions, award ceremonies, product launches, screenings, presentations and dancing for up to 1000 people or dinner for up to 350 guests. 
                  The museum has recently launched three stylish meeting rooms, The Terrace Rooms, with a private garden area perfect for daytime conferences and evening receptions.
                  This content has been supplied by Museum of London
