              Overview

              With over 1600m2 of column-free function space and some of the most advanced lighting, audio-visual and presentation technology available today, the Millennium Conference Centre can cater for all types of events. All rooms offer the latest AV technology, wifi, visual display units and screening facilities. 
                  The diverse range of spaces can cater for up to 500 delegates, from small meetings to product launches. The function rooms include the Conservatory, which offers elegant and luxurious surroundings with its magnificent glass structure, enhanced by up-lit palm trees. The Sentosa Suite provides a contemporary setting, where guests can enjoy a relaxing and serene experience.
