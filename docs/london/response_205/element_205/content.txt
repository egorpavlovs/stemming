          EXCLUSIVE - 2019 WIMBLEDON NEW FACILITY - THE ROSEWATER PAVILION-  OFFICIAL VIP HOSPITALITY

          
            Twitter

LinkedIn


        

        
          

            
              Valid: 01 Jul 19 - 14 Jul 19

            The Championships, Wimbledon is the world's oldest tennis tournament and one of the most prestigious events in the summer sporting calendar. Taking place every summer at the magnificent All England Lawn Tennis Club since 1877, it’s one of the four Grand Slam tennis tournaments, a quintessentially British experience, and one of our most sought after events. We wanted to kindly offer you this update on this stunning brand new facility in 2019 and offer you first option of the opportunity to experience the benefits the quality this venue provides first hand with your important clients, we are committed to bringing the best experience you can possibly have at this unique event. The Tournament attracts more than a billion viewers across 200 nations – but nothing comes close to experiencing its unique, unparalleled atmosphere in person.
Guarantee your place today by getting your VIP Prime tickets on Wimbledon's Centre Court.
The Rosewater Pavilion is the most high profile VIP hospitality option, the extensive list of benefits in this very top end package are all listed below - to mention just a few are fine dining luncheon, rose champagne all day, the most exclusive facility inside the ground, VIP two tiered balcony with views across to Centre Court, live music throughout the day in the central bar, VIP private fast track entrance through Gate 10, exclusive parking area, and to top all that off the finest VIP seats in Centre Court.  They provide a personal hostess service who looks after your party all day along with a hand-picked, attentive front-of-house team who are on hand to ensure your every detail is taken care of for you.
Impressively located officially within the grounds, The Rosewater Pavilion also offers you and your guests a stunning outdoor two tier terrace with breathtaking views towards Centre Court - the terrace is the perfect area to step outside and soak up the exciting Wimbledon atmosphere that SW19 is famous for, while you enjoy your chilled pink champagne with your valued guests. And once play commences on Centre Court, you have just a short stroll to take your allocated courtside Centre Court VIP seats. This venue is perfect in every way.

Tuesday  9 July 2019 - LADIES QUARTER FINAL  - Preferential Rates £1799 per person +vat - 1 X VIP Table 12 Guests
