              Overview

              The definitive contemporary luxury hotel, One Aldwych is located where the City meets the West End, in the heart of Covent Garden. The hotel has six private event spaces including a screening room.
                  The private rooms are contemporary in style with individual artwork and natural daylight. The spaces are suitable for stylish meetings, exquisite dinners and parties by night. Delicious food, creative cocktails and stunning flowers will make any event an occasion to remember.
                  The 30 seat screening room has HD Blu-Ray technology and is perfect for presentations and film screenings.
                  One Aldwych is licensed for weddings and civil partnerships.
                  This content has been supplied by One Aldwych
