              Overview

              Formal meals or buffets can be served in either the House restaurant or brasserie-style Terrace Café with stunning views over the river. Kitchen is an all-day café on the river front. 
                  The theatre foyers can be hired for events such as exhibitions, receptions and press and product launches. Services include day-long refreshments of all kinds, cinema format seating in certain areas, screens, overhead projectors and TV and video hire. 
                  Normally has six productions in repertoire, from classics and new plays to musicals. Backstage tours, platform performances, restaurants, bars, bookshop and parking available. Group incentives include special group discounts, theatre packages and group menus.
                  This content has been supplied by National Theatre

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
                  Contact us directly if you are planning your event from outside the UK or working on behalf on an international client. We can help you and your delegates access the best that London has to offer.
