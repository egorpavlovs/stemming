              Overview

              Situated south of the river Thames, the Park Plaza Riverbank London offers an outstanding AIM Gold and Amber Healthcare Venue-accredited meetings solution in a multiple-level conference centre which houses 23 meeting rooms, including the Ballroom, Plaza Suites and Park Suites for up to 650 guests. 
                  Accommodation comprises of 489 guestrooms and suites, with many boasting iconic riverside views. Additional facilities include the award-winning Chino Latino pan-Asian restaurant and bar, and a full-service business centre.
                  This content has been supplied by Park Plaza London Riverbank
