              Overview

              The newly refurbished Olympia Conference Centre is an exceptionally versatile venue that can cater to between 250 and 600 delegates at a competitive price. With all of its facilities on one level, the conference centre contains an exhibition space and break-out rooms that can be used for large conferences or smaller events. 
                  Facilities include:
                  • Purpose built auditorium with tiered seating
                  • 5 seminar rooms
                  • 2 or 5 additional break-out rooms
                  • Spacious, fully furnished reception
                  • Disabled access
                  • 2 furnished organiser's offices
                  • Cloakroom
                  •  Exclusive branding opportunities
                  • Specialist, in-house catering
                  • Audio-visual and communications services
                  • Complimentary marketing support
                  This content has been supplied by Olympia Conference Centre
