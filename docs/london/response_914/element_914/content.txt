              Overview

              The Royal Academy is Britain's oldest, privately funded, arts institution and annually holds up to eight critically acclaimed exhibitions at Burlington House. The Grade II listed building boasts stunning 18th century rooms hung with art from the private collection. Breakfasts, receptions and dinners may be held all year round with a private view of the current exhibition.

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
