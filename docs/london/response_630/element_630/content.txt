              Overview

              etc.venues 155 Bishopsgate, located in the heart of the City of London, offers 20,000 sq feet of ultra-modern, versatile space, making it the perfect environment for hosting a range of events. 
                  The main conference suite holds up to 550 or 350 for a seated dinner and is equipped with the latest AV and IT infrastructure.
                  The striking, multi-function galleria can be used for exhibitions (50 stands), networking or catering and includes two multi-screen media walls for digital branding.
                  The total space allows for up to five breakouts for between 70 and 350 in theatre style. 
                  Your next big conference, exhibition, results announcement, product lunch or corporate function may have just found its perfect home.
