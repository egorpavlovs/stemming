              Overview

              Tobacco Dock is historic London's newest events' space. The Grade I listed Tobacco Dock comprises over 40 individual spaces from 100-1900m2 making it ideal for large scale cocktail receptions, conferences, product launches, awards dinners and fashion shows or one massive venue for exhibitions and trade shows. 
                  Tobacco Dock combines iconic, 200 year old architecture with state-of-the-art technology including a 1GB data network to provide a unique venue for extraordinary events. Our clients include bluechip brands from the technology, fashion, automotive, professional services, publishing and financial sectors, leading production and event agencies and unique festivals such as Meatopia, Taste and Feast.
