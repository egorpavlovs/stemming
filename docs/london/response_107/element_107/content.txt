                Introduction
                
                  The QEII Centre is the largest dedicated conference, events and exhibition space in central London. The venue offers world-class facilities for events with capacity of two to 2,500.

              Overview

              The newly refurbished QEII Centre offers seven large and versatile conference spaces allowing you to create a bespoke occasion suited to your requirements.
                  Rooms can be combined to create spaces ideal for large conferences, presentations, banquets and exhibitions, or separated to accommodate seminars or smaller conferences. 
                  Our rooms come fully AV equipped and our bespoke state-of-the-art technology packages can give your conference the professionalism it deserves. The Centre also has free Wi-Fi throughout and offers a new IPTV system.
                  Under an hour from five international airports, the QEII is not only in the centre of London, but also within easy access to the rest of the world.
                  This content has been supplied by QEII Centre

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
                  Contact us directly if you are planning your event from outside the UK or working on behalf on an international client. We can help you and your delegates access the best that London has to offer.
