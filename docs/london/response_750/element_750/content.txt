              Overview

              The Royal Opera House is a unique location set in the heart of London, full of classic and contemporary architecture, a highly sought after venue for special events.
                  With a variety of event spaces the house is eminently suited to private lunch and dinner parties, discreet undisturbed business meetings or large receptions.
                  This content has been supplied by Royal Opera House

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
                  Contact us directly if you are planning your event from outside the UK or working on behalf on an international client. We can help you and your delegates access the best that London has to offer.
