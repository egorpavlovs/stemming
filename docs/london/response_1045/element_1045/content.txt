                Introduction

              Overview

              Located in the heart of the City, this spectacular London venue promises to be the most unforgettable setting for your event.
                  155 metres above London, this unique space spans three storeys, boasting London’s highest landscaped public gardens and incredible panoramic views across the Thames.
                  Sky Garden offers two spaces for hire: exclusive hire of the whole garden (up to 750 guests) or the dedicated event platform, City Garden on level 36 (up to 250 guests).
                  Exclusive hire of the entire venue is available for the full day (6am to 6am) from £29,500* or half day (4pm to 2am) from £40,000*. Alternatively, City Garden can be hired from 7am to 9am (£5,000*) and 6pm to 11pm (£9,500*).
                  Sky Garden offers deliciously bespoke menus to make your event unforgettable and with dedicated planning and creative teams on hand to help you design the ultimate event, there’s no better venue for your occasion.
                  *prices are subject to catering spend, exclude 20% VAT and 12.5% service charge.
