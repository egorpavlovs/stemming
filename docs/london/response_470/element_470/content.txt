              Overview

              Hilton London Canary Wharf offers a prime location, just five minutes' walk to Canary Wharf tube station; travel to The O2 in just 11 minutes, or reach Green Park to see Buckingham Palace in just 30 minutes on the Jubilee Line.
                  South Quay DLR Station is only two minutes away and can take you to ExCel in just 16 minutes; London City Airport is only a 15 minutes' drive away and you can reach Heathrow in 15 minutes or Gatwick in 30 minutes, with express trains from Central London.
                  In each of our 282 stylish and spacious guest rooms and suites, discover a relaxing retreat where you can work without distraction.
                  Enjoy all the amenities of the 14th floor Executive Lounge whilst taking in the panoramic views of the city.
                  No matter how complex your event, the team has a room, the expertise and flexibility to accommodate your meeting, with six flexible Meeting Rooms, one Boardroom and one Large Function Room that will accommodate up to 400 delegates featuring it's own reception and bar area. All conference facilities are conveniently located on the first floor with ample break out space and business centre services and fully
                  equipped with state-of-the-art wireless technology to handle any type of occasion.
                  This content has been supplied by Hilton London Canary Wharf
