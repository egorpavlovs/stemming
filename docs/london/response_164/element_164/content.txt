              Overview

              This charming hotel, housed in a stunning Victorian building, combines stately elegance and homely charm. Inside, its rich period surroundings offer elegant accommodation and state of-the art meetings facilities. Located just a mile from the City and only a short walk from Euston Station and King’s Cross St. Pancras – with direct Eurostar rail links to France and Belgium – it’s your ideal base in the heart of London. 
                   Events and occasions:  
                   The contemporary-styled Stephenson Suite (132sqm/1,421sq ft) caters for up to 120 guests and offers a private reception area as well as a variety of technical facilities – absolutely the right ambience for larger groups. Wireless broadband Internet access is available throughout the hotel, including bedrooms; secretarial and business support services are also available. 
                   Relax in spacious, stylish and comfortable bedrooms, with marble bathrooms and an ergonomic work space. Deluxe rooms feature wide screen TVs as well as a soft, fluffy bathrobe. Woburn Place Dining room serves classic dishes in one of the largest conservatories in the area and includes an outside dining area. The Mulberry Restaurant is also available for private dining and lunch meetings. Other facilities include a gym, combining Victorian elegance and high-tech equipment with an external exercise space.”
                  This content has been supplied by Hilton London Euston
