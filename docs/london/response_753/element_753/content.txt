              Overview

              Kings, queens, statesmen and soldiers; poets, priests, heroes and villains - the Abbey is a must-see living pageant of British history. Situated in the heart of London and flanked by the Palace of Westminster, Westminster Abbey offers a spectacular location for corporate entertaining in a truly historic setting.
                  The Abbey provides a wide choice of unique settings for a prestigious corporate or charitable event; the Cellarium, Undercroft Museum, Cheyneygates, and in the summer months, the East and North Cloisters, and College Garden. The flexibility of choice allows for small receptions in intimate surroundings to large gatherings of up to 500.
