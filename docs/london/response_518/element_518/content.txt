              Overview

              The Lensbury is a four-star hotel, conference centre and leisure club located on the banks of the river Thames in south-west London.  
                  The conference centre has 30 contemporary meeting rooms, accommodating capacities of two to 250 people. Most rooms have natural daylight, while all rooms have high-tech AV equipment, furniture and complimentary technical support.
                  We offer free car parking and complimentary wifi throughout the venue. 
                  The hotel has 155 ensuite bedrooms and offers a variety of room types, including standard, superior and executive. A range of dining options are available to delegates, from the relaxed bar and lounge to the formal Dunbar Restaurant.
                  The leisure facilities include an indoor pool, a gym, tennis courts, a water sports centre, squash, a spa and a children's play area.
