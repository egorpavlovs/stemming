              Overview

              Jurys Kensington Hotel is the ideal four-star venue for business or please in South Kensington. Our lounge creates a welcoming, and cosy atmosphere to relax and order an IL Barista Coffee or a Cocktail form the bar. Our four conference suites cater for every corporate requirement. We boast rich traditional classic executive bedrooms & suites all with home from home amenity, flexible to suit families. 
                  Book a table in our contemporary international style Copplestones Restaurant where we pride ourselves on our service and mouth watering cuisine. Experience Kavanaghs Irish Bar, one of London's most friendly and popular bars, traditional music the latest sporting events live.

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
                  Contact us directly if you are planning your event from outside the UK or working on behalf on an international client. We can help you and your delegates access the best that London has to offer.
