              Overview

              Combining architectural heritage with contemporary design, Hotel Café Royal has been reincarnated as a luxury hotel in the heart of London. 
                  Located within walking distance of London’s finest shopping, tourist landmarks and Theatreland, the hotel is ideal for both business and leisure.
                  Fully dedicated meeting facilities are perfectly equipped for events ranging from a discreet business dinner, to an international conference. Event venues seamlessly blend the grandeur of Hotel Café Royal with modern technology and comfort.
                  Hotel Café Royal boasts some of London’s largest guestrooms and suites, as well as an array of restaurants and bars. The Akasha Holistic Wellbeing Centre is available to guests.
                  This content has been supplied by Hotel Cafe Royal London
