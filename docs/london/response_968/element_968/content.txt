              Overview

              Home to the renowned national collection of international modern art, Tate Modern is one of London’s most recognisable and best loved buildings, making it a true icon for the capitol.
                  Each of the venues available in this transformed disused power station exudes a sleek, contemporary and urban feel boasting stunning views across London’s skyline from its Bankside vantage point. Venues include the striking Starr Auditorium, vibrant Café 2, intimate Members Room, panoramic East Room and serene Realisms Gallery.
                  Accommodating both day and evening events, Tate Modern lends itself to all manner of corporate entertaining.
                  Tel: (020) 7887 8689
                  Email: corporatehospitality@tate.org.uk
                  This content has been supplied by Tate Modern
