              Overview

              Designed by leading architects using the highest environmental standards, DoubleTree by Hilton London Tower of London is proud to offer its guests quality accommodation that engages with its historic surroundings and blends seamlessly with the enigmatic City of London skyline.
                  Despite offering some 583 bedrooms, the hotel guarantees that each and every one of its suites offers the comforts of home and luxury in equal measure. The hotel features 10 light and airy function rooms with floor to ceiling windows.
                  Savage Garden - 12 storeys above and far removed from the city’s concrete jungle below, the hotel offers a bar like no other. A study in experimentation and temptation, the decadent décor, jaw-dropping views, dramatic cocktails and truly savage menus provide a standout venue like no other. 
                  If you can tear yourself away from the seriously seductive interiors, Savage Garden also boasts two terraces with stunning 360-degree vistas that take in some of the capital’s most iconic landmarks including The Shard, Tower of London, The Gherkin and Walky Talky. Wildside is an expansive all-weather terrace and events space complete with a retractable roof making it perfect for al fresco cocktail parties as well as indoor corporate events or Christmas festivities. Savage Garden also offers the Ferus Suite for boardroom meetings and private dining with a view. The entire rooftop can accommodate exclusive use receptions up to 380 guests in total.
