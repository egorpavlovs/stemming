              Overview

              Hilton London Metropole is situated in the heart of London, just 10 minutes from Oxford Street and Hyde Park and 15 minutes from Heathrow Airport via Heathrow Express.
                  Facilities include:
                  • 1,059 spacious guest rooms and suites
                  • Over 4,100 square metres of flexible pillar-free conference space
                  • 42 conference and meeting rooms
                  • Dedicated Business Centre
                  • 24-hour room service
                  • High speed wireless internet (charges may apply)
                  • Executive Lounge with complimentary food and beverage served throughout the day
                  • Parking for 172 cars
                  • LivingWell Health Club with 12.5m pool, steam room and sauna
                  • Contrasti Hair Salon and beauty treatments
                  This content has been supplied by Hilton London Metropole
