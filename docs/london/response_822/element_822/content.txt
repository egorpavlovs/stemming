              Overview

              Hilton London Heathrow Airport Terminal 5 boasts 350 spacious rooms and one of the largest event spaces in the UK. The hotel is set in 13 acres of immaculately landscaped grounds and boasts 350 spacious rooms. At the hotel is a choice of stylish restaurants and bars including the hotel’s signature restaurant, Mr Todiwala’s Kitchen, producing pan Indian Cuisine from renowned TV chef Cyrus Todiwala. To complement the hotel, guests are also able to benefit from the sumptuous treatments available within the luxury Imagine spa.
                  The Gallery Rooms accommodate up to 1,170 delegates theatre style and up to 800 guests for a banquet. The room divides into six sections and is located on the mezzanine level of the hotel with its own dedicated entrance and reception for our meetings and events guests.
                  This content has been supplied by HIlton London Heathrow Airport Terminal 5
