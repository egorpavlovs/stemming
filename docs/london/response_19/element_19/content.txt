          Radisson Blu Edwardian, London Hotels
          
		Edwardian Hotels London is a collection of 12 hotels, consisting of 11 Radisson Blu Edwardian, London hotels, in prime locations across London, Heathrow and central Manchester and The May Fair Hotel.
    
            Twitter

LinkedIn


        

        
            
        

      

            
		
				
				
		
		
Edwardian Hotels London has a versatile range of meeting and event spaces, for 2 - 700 people. All superbly connected for public transport and complete with popular restaurant concepts, all event spaces come with complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices and excellent AV technology. Whether you hold a meeting at The May Fair Hotel or any Radisson Blu Edwardian, London hotels, your event will run seamlessly. The group is also engaged in a major development in Leicester Square, incorporating a luxury lifestyle hotel, restaurants, bars, spa and cinema.

Radisson Blu Edwardian, Grafton Hotel
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Grafton is a historic building in trendy Fitzrovia, 10 mins’ walk from Regents Park, London Zoo and world-famous attractions like Madame Tussauds & Oxford Street. There are superb transport links from Warren Street opposite, Euston station (5 mins’ walk) and Paris/Brussels Eurostar (10 mins’ walk). Guests can enjoy: free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices; stylish singles, doubles, family rooms and suites; and Steak & Lobster, serving mouth-watering steaks, lobster, salads and toasted brioche rolls. The hotel has 11 meeting rooms, hosting 2-150 people.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Kenilworth Hotel
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Kenilworth hotel, is located in historic Bloomsbury offering comfortable, stylish rooms with cityscape views and free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices. Scoff & Banter serves a superb menu of modern British cuisine using only the finest locally sourced, seasonal produce, while 8 private meeting rooms can host 2-120 people. Close to the British Museum, Tottenham Court Road, Oxford Street, Covent Garden and Paris/Brussels Eurostar link, while nearby Holborn station connects directly to the City Financial District.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Heathrow hotel
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Heathrow hotel is an award-winning hotel and 15 mins by free transport from Heathrow. Stylish, comfortable rooms feature international plug sockets and free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices. Steak & Lobster serves mouth-watering steaks, lobster, salads and toasted brioche rolls, while Annayu blends classic and contemporary Indian cuisine. Bijou Lounge Bar is perfect for light bites, coffee and cocktails, and Pegasus Spa is a tranquil haven to relax. The hotel has a 400-space private car park & 37 function rooms hosting 2-1,000 people.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Bloomsbury Street
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Bloomsbury Street is an elegant hotel in historic Bloomsbury offering comfortable, stylish rooms with cityscape views, and free high-speed WiFi on unlimited devices. The Exclusive 7th Floor gives extra privacy, luxury and style while Steak & Lobster serves mouth-watering steaks, lobster, salads and toasted brioche rolls. Close to the British Museum, Tottenham Court Road, Oxford Street, Covent Garden and Paris/Brussels Eurostar, and nearby Holborn station links direct to the City Financial District. The hotel has 9 meeting rooms host 2-300 people.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Sussex
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Sussex is tucked away in a quiet spot beside Selfridges with Oxford Street, Bond Street and Hyde Park on the doorstep. Guests can enjoy free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices; comfortable singles, doubles & family rooms with original layouts from the hotel’s Victorian heritage; and 2 meeting rooms hosting 2-12 people. Steak & Lobster serves mouth-watering steaks, lobster, salads and toasted brioche rolls. Close to Marylebone’s cafés and rail links, Park Lane and Edgware Road, with great transport links via nearby Bond Street station.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Mercer Street
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Mercer Street, is a boutique haven in Covent Garden’s vibrant Seven Dials village. Guests can enjoy stylish, comfortable rooms, Mac Minis and free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices. Monmouth Kitchen serves mouth-watering Peruvian & Italian sharing plates and cocktails in a cool atmosphere, while 6 meeting rooms offer cutting-edge technology for 2-60 people. Close to Covent Garden’s Piazza, Tottenham Court Road, Oxford Street & Soho. The hotel has great transport links at nearby Leicester Square, Covent Garden and Tottenham Court Road stations.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Hampshire Hotel
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Hampshire is London’s only hotel with a Leicester Square address in the heart of film and theatreland. Guests can enjoy superb Mexican & Peruvian sharing plates at Leicester Square Kitchen plus drinks and bar bites in At The Hampshire Lounge Bar - all with close-up views of the movie red carpet. The hotel has free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices, stylish bedrooms and 6 meeting spaces hosting 2-120 people. Located close to Soho, Covent Garden and Trafalgar Square, with great links via nearby Leicester Square and  Piccadilly Circus stations.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Manchester Hotel
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Manchester is a modern luxury and stylish hotel in Manchester’s historic Free Trade Hall with elegant rooms and suites, free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices, luxurious Sienna Spa and large swimming pool. Steak & Lobster serves mouth-watering steaks, lobster, salads and toasted brioche rolls, Opus One offers afternoon tea, bistro bites and cocktails. Next to the Convention Centre and conference quarter, close to buzzing Deansgate and linked to Manchester’s rail stations by free shuttlebus. The hotel has 23 private meeting rooms host 2-500 people.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Berkshire Hotel
Radisson Blu Edwardian, Berkshire is London’s only hotel with an Oxford Street address, on the doorstep of the West End’s world class restaurants, bars and shops, including Selfridges. Guests enjoy: free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices; stylish, comfortable rooms with great views; superb cocktails and bites in Berkshire Lounge Bar; and traditional British afternoon tea in Scoff & Banter Tea Rooms. Close to Bond Street’s exclusive boutiques, Soho’s buzzing nightlife and Marylebone’s cafés and rail links and opposite well-connected Bond Street station.
Radisson Blu Edwardian, New Providence Wharf
Radisson Blu Edwardian, New Providence Wharf is a stylish hotel with a relaxed, boutique atmosphere. Guests can enjoy free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices, spacious rooms and the tranquil East River Spa. Riverside restaurant Scoff & Banter offers stunning views across the Thames and serves modern British cuisine, cocktails and afternoon tea using the best locally sourced, seasonal produce. A short walk from Canary Wharf and City Business District with great transport connections to the O2 Arena, ExCel Centre & London City Airport. The hotel has 8 meeting rooms host 2-250 people.
The May Fair
The May Fair is a stylish, luxury icon in sophisticated Mayfair with lavish rooms; 3D cinema; casino and May Fair Spa. Guests can enjoy free high-speed Wi-Fi on unlimited devices, and fabulous eating and drinking with May Fair Kitchen’s Mediterranean style sharing plates, May Fair Bar’s cocktails and vibrant ambiance, and May Fair Terrace’s quiet spot for drinks and cigars. Close to Bond Street’s boutiques, iconic restaurants Nobu & Sexy Fish, Buckingham Palace and Green Park station linking directly to the Victoria, Piccadilly and Jubilee lines. The hotel has 15 meeting and event spaces and can host 2-280 people.
