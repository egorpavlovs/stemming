              Overview

              Our 10 meeting rooms, boardroom and ballroom provide flexible spaces for a variety of business events including meetings, conferences, dinner dances, weddings, exhibitions and award ceremonies. Our dedicated Conference and Events Sales team manage the bookings and our C&E Operations Team ensure that everything runs smoothly.
