              Overview

              Whether you're planning a small or large event, our adaptable and unique venue will meet your needs. Lee Valley VeloPark's velodrome has a seating capacity of 6,000 and has already held some spectacular conferences catering for more than 2,000 delegates.
                  You can hire theatre-style meeting rooms for gatherings of up to 50 people, or a suite with a magnificent view for up to 100. If you need more room, you can spread out with our inter-connecting meeting rooms.
                  This content has been supplied by Lee Valley VeloPark
