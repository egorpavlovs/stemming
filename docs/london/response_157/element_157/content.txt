              Overview

              Our stunning Mayfair location speaks volumes about anyone using our luxury event space. Wonderful leafy views of Green Park from our floor to ceiling windows, contribute highly to any daytime event - people feel more alert, happier and at home. For night owls, we offer stunning private dinners and drinks receptions – all with the option to stay in a luxury bedroom to truly make a night of it.
                  A meeting for 30? A cocktail reception for 100? Our unique layout means that you have numerous options. Hyde Park, St James's Park and Green Park all have flexible layout options with 5 star atmosphere and service, original art and design.
