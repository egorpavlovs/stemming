              Overview

              Twickenham Stadium offers a state-of-the-art, modern and flexible meeting space for all conferences and events.
                  We can accommodate all types of events from presentations and conferences to private dinners, cocktail receptions and exhibitions. In total we have 25 dedicated conference and event rooms for you to choose from as well as 150 pitch view meeting rooms.
                  This content has been supplied by Twickenham Stadium

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
                  Contact us directly if you are planning your event from outside the UK or working on behalf on an international client. We can help you and your delegates access the best that London has to offer.
