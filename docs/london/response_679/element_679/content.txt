              Overview

              Standing on Old Park Lane since 14 June 1971, this is the one that started it all. Still housing the first ever piece of memorabilia donated, Eric Clapton’s Lead II Fender, the London Cafe is as charming and authentic now as it was 40 years ago. The Cafe is also as famous for attracting the best rock stars down for a legendary meal and the occasional “jamming session”.
                  Come lose yourself in our extensive memorabilia collection, enjoy some live music in our basement, enjoy the eclectic setting for a meeting or hire the entire venue for an experience that truly rocks!
