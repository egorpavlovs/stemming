              Overview

              Lime Venue Portfolio is the largest collection of unusual, sporting and cultural venues in the UK and Ireland. We provide a free service and one point of contact to make it easier to use our venues. 
                  From small meetings to large conventions, we believe in making every event extraordinary with an unusual venue. Our unusual venues enhance the delegate experience, increase attendance, and excite and wow your guests.
                  As a division of Compass Group UK, we’re passionate about food. Because it’s restaurant-quality food that brings all our venues together and we know it’s the food that can mean the difference between your events being good, and your events being great.
                  Our team of experienced event professionals make your life easier. We develop long-lasting relationships, tailored to suit your needs by offering you one point of contact, with detailed proposals from your chosen venues and, as our service is free, the price you pay is the same as going direct.
                  This content has been supplied by Lime Venue Portfolio
