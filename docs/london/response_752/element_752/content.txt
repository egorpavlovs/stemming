              Overview

              The Grand Ballroom has been lovingly restored and the sparkling crystal chandeliers and delicate period details echo its rich heritage.
                  You will be impressed by The Langham’s talent to cater to modern business needs with seamless service, anticipating every requirement with flexibility and flair.
                  Discover Private Dining by Roux, a unique dining concept for corporate meetings, conferences and incentives offering the Chez Roux hallmarks of classic French cuisine with a modern twist.
                  From intimate gatherings in the charming Private Carriage Rooms or state-of-the-art meeting rooms, to extravagant banquets, The Langham promises to create occasions that will be talked about for time to come.
                  This content has been supplied by The Langham, London
