              Overview

              The London Wetland Centre is a lush wildlife oasis with lakes, ponds and meadows just 10-minutes from Hammersmith. Located on the banks of the river Thames in Barnes, the centre is home to rare and beautiful wildlife. The wildlife changes constantly with the seasons, and there is always something new to see. 
                  Within the spectacular setting, a number of unique rooms are available to hire for a range of daytime and evening events, such as conferences, team away days, dinners and weddings (licensed for ceremonies).
                  As well as enjoying the perfect day in stunning surroundings, you'll also be supporting vital nature conservation work.
                  This content has been supplied by WWT London Wetland Centre
