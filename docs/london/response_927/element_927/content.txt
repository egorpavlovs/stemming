              Overview

              Entertain with a difference by holding your event at Churchill War Rooms.
                  This preserved historic site in the heart of Westminster is where Winston Churchill and his wartime government led Britain and her allies to victory during the Second World War.
                  This once secret underground wartime bunker can accommodate events for 10 – 350 people within the Harmsworth Room, HCA Auditorium and Switch Room and offers all modern facilities at a venue steeped in history.
                  This content has been supplied by Churchill War Rooms
