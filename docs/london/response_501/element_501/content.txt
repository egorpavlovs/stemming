              Overview

              From private board meetings to lavish celebrations, Corinthia Hotel London creates the perfect setting for your event. Whether you are planning a wedding, a conference or business event, your dedicated event planner will be on hand to help you find the perfect venue and guide you through every aspect of your event.
                  Incorporating some of the most stunning and stylish private dining rooms in London with up-to-date technology, personalised service and the finest cuisine, this 21st-century Grand Hotel can offer you a truly unique and memorable experience.
