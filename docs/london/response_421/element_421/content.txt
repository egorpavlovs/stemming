              Overview

              Voted 4th Best Business Hotel in the UK at the Condé Nast Traveller Reader’s Travel Awards 2010, Jumeirah Carlton Tower is a superb venue for your meeting or event. The hotel is ideally situated in Knightsbridge, with easy access to underground lines, London’s major airports and the city.
                  Our eight flexible function rooms, some overlooking Cadogan Gardens and bathed in natural light, provide an exclusive venue for up to 670 guests. The elegant and versatile rooms are perfect for events ranging from boardroom meetings to cocktail parties, weddings, conferences and presentations. All our function space benefits from the latest high-tech audio-visual equipment and we have a dedicated business centre on site providing an array of secretarial services and support.
