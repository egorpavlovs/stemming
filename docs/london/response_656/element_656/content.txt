              Overview

              The DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel London - Docklands Riverside provides the space, style, and personalised service that guests want and expect when planning an event or meeting. 
                  From grandiose galas and meticulous meetings to private parties and social soirées, every special occasion is our main event. Choose from 14 meeting rooms for the perfect setting to suit the event. Select this amazing riverside location as the site for wedding nuptials, or choose a BBQ on the terrace. Conduct board meetings for three or stage a black tie gala for 300 guests. Relax and let experienced Team Members oversee every detail from start to finish for a seamless success.
                  Show your team appreciation and impress clients with something informal yet spectacular. We offer recreational and team building activities including:
                  A voyage on The Thames, drinks and a Thames quiz, round trip through central London with the Thames Rib Experience, dinner and drinks reception cruises on The Thames, themed parties such as 'create your own cocktail' and interactive BBQ evenings, contacts for interactive dinner entertainment such as magicians, blackjack and roulette, private launch parties and team celebration parties.
                  Each of the hotel's unique spaces provide a comfortable setting for ceremonies and receptions.
                  This content has been supplied by DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel London - Docklands Riverside
