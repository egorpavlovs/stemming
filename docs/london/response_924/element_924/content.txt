              Overview

              Entertain your clients within the dizzying heights of one of London’s smartest and most unusual historic venues right in the heart of the West End. 
                  Wellington Arch has one of the capital’s most fashionable addresses, best locations, and the historic looks and elegant styling to surprise and delight your guests.
                  Download the hospitality brochure here:
                  http://www.english-heritage.org.uk/content/venue-hire/documents/wellington-arch-corporate-brochure.pdf
                  This content has been supplied by English Heritage: Wellington Arch
