              Overview

              Whether you want a captive audience or simply a venue with a difference, and of course views to die for, an event on the River Thames takes some beating. And no one knows more about them than City Cruises, London’s No.1 on the river. Our fleet of boats offers you an unparalleled choice of venues afloat. From our state of the art flagship the Millennium Diamond, to our classic cruisers, each and every one has a distinctive character and ambience. Add to this our experienced events team who can help you plan every aspect of your event, or work alongside your own organisers, and you’ve all the makings of an occasion to remember.
