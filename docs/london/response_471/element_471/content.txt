              Overview

              The More London district has emerged as one of the city’s most exciting commercial hubs. With an unrivalled location adjacent to Tower Bridge and opposite the Tower of London, the hotel sits between two cities, at the core of the nation’s history and on the doorstep of some of the most futuristic architecture in the world. It’s within easy access of attractions including the London Dungeon, Tate Modern, London Eye and Shakespeare's Globe Theater.  
                  Events and occasions: The glass-walled Tower Suite ballroom is a magnificent venue for a lavish banquet, charity ball or corporate dinner. The unique ‘light-wall’ creates a magical backdrop for any event in this spacious suite (336sqm/3,617sq ft, 4.6m/15ft ceiling height). Our ten flexible Hilton Meetings Rooms offer great choice for business gatherings. Located on one floor with a Business Center, they feature wireless Internet access and A/V equipment.  Panoramic views over the City can be enjoyed on the 9th floor City. Private events can be arranged for up to 25 guests or a barbecue for up to 15. 
                  Many of the 245 bedrooms will offer stunning views over the river. The Larder is a popular London restaurant specializing in uniquely British fare including delicious fresh-from-the-oven bread.
                  This content has been supplied by Hilton London Tower Bridge
