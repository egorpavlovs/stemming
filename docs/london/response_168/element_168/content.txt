              Overview

              The Radisson Blu Portman Hotel has one of the largest conferences spaces in Central London which can accommodate up to 600 persons theatre style. 
                  The 12 recently refurbished conferences and banqueting suites are located on the first floor, a dedicated area with its own separate entrance. The majority of meeting rooms benefit from floor to ceiling windows, providing our guests with a warm welcome and bright environment, as well as electronic blinds, individual climate control and complimentary Wi-Fi high speed internet access. 
                  All our suites can be equipped with high-quality amenities and conference technology; additional specialised equipment can be arranged for you upon request.
                  This content has been supplied by Radisson BLU Portman Hotel
