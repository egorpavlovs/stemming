              Overview

              Alexandra Palace is an iconic Victorian-styled north London venue, which offers versatile interlinking spaces of all sizes. 
                  Suitable for holding meetings for 100 up to banquets of 5,000 and beyond, it is the ideal venue to host an array of public and private events. Palm Court is the main entrance area to the halls and provides a stunning welcome space for any occasion.
                  December 2018 sees the reopening of the Victorian Theatre for the first time in more than 80 years. This space will host a range of events, from theatre shows and concerts, to banquets and awards ceremonies in a unique and historic setting.
                  With 196 acres of parkland, Ally Pally has its own ice rink, pitch and putt course and boating lake, making it the perfect location for unusual corporate away days, summer parties and BBQs – all with an unrivalled panoramic backdrop of the capital. 
                  There are also excellent transport links and plenty of hotels nearby.
                  This content has been supplied by Alexandra Palace
