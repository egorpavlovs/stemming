              Overview

              Cheyne Walk Brasserie is a chic French restaurant next to the river, overlooking Albert Bridge. Cheyne Walk Brasserie is a real gem, popular with the local Chelsea neighbourhood and a destination for lovers of high quality dining. The salon is a favourite for special occasions; wedding receptions, anniversaries and birthdays as well as more formal business events and lunches.
                  Exclusive hire for private parties of up to 200 guests. Provencal dishes are a speciality, cooked on the open grill and served with your choice of fine wines.
                  This content has been supplied by Cheyne Walk Brasserie
