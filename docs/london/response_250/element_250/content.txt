              Overview

              The dedicated award-winning meetings and event space combines state-of-the-art facilities and stunning views of London that bring the outside in. With a ballroom for up to 750 guests and a further 11 suites, InterContinental London Park Lane is an ideal location to host galas, award ceremonies, city weddings, and social events. 
                  The InterContinental Insider Collection programme also provides meeting planners and event bookers with a range of experiences to enhance any occasion.
                  This content has been supplied by InterContinental London Park Lane
