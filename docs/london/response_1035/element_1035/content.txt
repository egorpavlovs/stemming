              Overview

              The Wellcome Collection, a venue for the incurably curious conveniently located on Euston Road within short distance of Euston Square and Euston station and St Pancras International station. 
                  The Conference Centre offers a selection of flexible meeting and events space which include a tiered auditorium for 154 delegates with state of the art AV facilities; alongside this we have four meeting rooms to accommodate a variety of events from workshops and meetings through to press/product launches. 
                  In addition we can also accommodate corporate receptions for up to 200 guests within our thought pro-voking Medicine Now gallery.
                  This content has been supplied by Wellcome Collection Conference Centre
