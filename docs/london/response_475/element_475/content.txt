              Overview

              Situated on one of London’s most fashionable shopping streets, Hilton London Olympia is a relaxed and inviting contemporary hotel close to Kensington Palace, and just minutes from Earl’s Court and Olympia exhibition centres.   
                  All conference facilities are conveniently located together on the ground floor.  The Westminster Suite (280 guests for a banquet, or 250 for a theatre-style conference) is the ideal choice for banqueting events, being cleverly linked to the kitchen.  Smaller suites include the Pembroke (140 delegates theatre-style or 100 for a banquet), the Kensington Suite which takes 60 people banquet layout and can be sub-divided for groups of up to 15 guests, and the Olympia Suite (60 theatre style). Our Business Centre, located next to the meeting rooms, offers business support services and Internet access.  
                     
                  Drinking and dining options include the quintessentially British Society Bar & Restaurant and quick and fresh Costa Coffee. The complimentary fitness centre features the most up-to-date cardiovascular equipment and resistance equipment is open 24 hours a day. 
                     
                  The hotel is located just 40 minutes from Heathrow Airport and enjoys easy access to the rest of the UK via rail from Kensington (Olympia) station.
                  This content has been supplied by Hilton London Olympia
