              Overview

              Particularly rich in Victorian art and ranging from Pre-Raphaelites to depictions of London's colourful past, the Gallery offers a spectacular arena — and excellent talking point — for spacious drinks receptions.
                  To further enhance your guests' experience, tailor your event to incorporate a guided tour of the masterpieces on display.
                  Whatever your occasion, our dynamic and dedicated events team delivers a first-class service. Friendly and experienced, we will work with you to achieve the event you want — and ensure an experience at Guildhall Art Gallery to be remembered.
                  Guildhall Art Gallery is available for evening events with hire from 6pm.
                  If you are interested in holding an event in London's Roman Amphitheatre, please see the separate listing for The City Centre and Roman Amphitheatre.
                  This content has been supplied by Guildhall Art Gallery & Roman Amphitheatre
