              Overview

              Atlas Travel Solutions is a respected DMC providing destination management services to incentive and corporate meeting clients from all corners of the world.
                  Our reputation is directly linked to the passion and dedication of each member of our experienced, multilingual, dynamic and friendly team.
                  Our experienced team members, hand-picked from a variety of industries including travel, hospitality, event management and communications, bring a truly unique perspective to the development of customised programmes for our clients. Our goal is to exceed our clients' expectations, with unparalleled service, exemplary attention to detail and seamless programme execution combined with value for money.
