              Overview

              London Syon Park, A Waldorf Astoria Hotel, sits within the 200 acre Syon House estate, seven miles from the city centre, and seven miles from Heathrow Airport. Bringing contemporary luxury to London’s urban countryside, this brand new 5* property offers a tranquil escape with a tradition of grand hospitality.
                  Classical room amenities blend impeccably with modern elements; high-speed Wi-Fi and 40” LCD HDTV, in-room entertainment system powered by Apple, bathrooms with recessed TVs, illuminated rain showers and heated floors. Rooms look out onto lushly landscaped gardens and reflecting pools, most from private balconies.
                  The dining experience embraces a vision of fresh, seasonal and home-grown cuisine that can be found throughout the hotel’s unique dining choices.
                  London Syon Park is an ideal meeting venue for business guests desiring gracious surroundings and state-of-the-art conference facilities. The Grand Syon Ballroom, capable of hosting up to 450 guests, sets the stage for spectacular celebratory moments. For your smaller meeting needs, we offer our Astor, Empire suites, and The Clubhouse. For a more informal setting, opt for The Deck or Duke’s Parlour.
                  Escape the city, escape work, escape the world and marvel at the magic of the countryside.
                  This content has been supplied by Hilton London Syon Park
