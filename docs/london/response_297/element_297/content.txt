              Overview

              Tower Bridge has stood over the river Thames since 1894, enchanting admirers with its beauty and elegance.
                  Tower Bridge houses four exquisite and unusual venues.
                  Whether it's for a cocktail reception, wedding, promotional event or dinner, the Bridge provides the ideal space for any event that requires something extraordinary.
