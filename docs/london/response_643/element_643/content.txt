              Overview

              Drawing in Londoners for over two decades, OXO Tower Restaurant, Bar and Brasserie is an iconic landmark on London’s South Bank, comprising of three venues on the eighth floor of the OXO Tower. With an expansive garden terrace and floor-to-ceiling windows, OXO is a place for celebration.
                  OXO's three versatile areas, the Restaurant, the Bar and the Brasserie, can be hired individually or as a whole. Semi-private dining is also available in the Bar and adjoining terrace for up to 32 guests. OXO’s expert events team curates bespoke events in line with every client’s needs and budget. Whether it’s a wedding for four people or a reception for 750 people, the venue is an adaptable blank canvas.
                  OXO Tower Restaurant, Bar and Brasserie in London is just an eight minute walk from Southwark and Waterloo stations and a ten minute walk from Blackfriars station.
