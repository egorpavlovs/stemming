              Overview

              Established in 1997, Blue Ribbon Events are a unique full service, multilingual DMC, working in partnership with corporate clients from around the world, across all industries. 
                  Reliably we continue to produce truly memorable London & UK wide events - creative and fun incentive programmes, professional conference production, road-shows, product launches, gala dinners & exhibition stands.
                  We offer a huge range of up-to-date venue and destination knowledge, innovative design and professional logistics expertise.
                  Whether you are looking for an experiential experience with added social media elements, or, the traditional delivery of your event, our exceptional service is always guaranteed.
