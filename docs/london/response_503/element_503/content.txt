              Overview

              Clayton Crown Hotel London is a 4-star hotel with excellent conference and event facilities.
                  There is a total of four versatile meetings and events rooms, capable of holding a small meeting for two to a large conference for up to 450 people.
                  Facilities include:
                  - Conference facilities for up to 450 people
                  - Multiple break-out rooms and reception areas
                  - A dedicated meetings and events co-ordinator with your booking
                  - High-speed complimentary Wi-Fi
                  - Grade II listed building with original period features
                  - Flexible packages and clear pricing
                  - Competitive day delegate rates
                  - On-site parking with 40 spaces
                  - Wheelchair access to multiple meeting rooms
                  - 152 stylish Deluxe bedrooms
                  This content has been supplied by Clayton Crown Hotel London
