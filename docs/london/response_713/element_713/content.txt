          EULAR Congress at London's ExCel Centre
          
		London & Partners worked with EULAR to bring the UK's largest ever medical association meeting to London.
    
            Twitter

LinkedIn


        

        
            
              
			   			    	
          
        

      

            
		
				
				
		
		Â From 25-28 May 2011, London's ExCeL successfully hosted a total of 16,115 delegates for The European Congress of Rheumatology (EULAR).The much-praised congress and exhibition brought together leading global experts in the field of rheumatology to share ideas, develop contacts and learn about the latest developments and innovations.Highlights from the four-day event included a British Guards marching band, West End musical showcase and an evening reception within the Old Royal Naval College in Greenwich.How London & Partners helpedAlong with EULAR and the British Society for Rheumatology, London & Partners worked with the ICC London ExCeL, MCI Geneva, and MCI UK to manage the event.Our team helped to:arrange special travel dealssecure around 10,000 hotel roomsprovide welcome messages and registration for the 16,115 delegatesfind venues for the congress social programmeThe 2011 congress attracted its highest ever number of delegates (an increase from 15,489 in Rome, 2010) and brought in more sponsorship than ever before.
