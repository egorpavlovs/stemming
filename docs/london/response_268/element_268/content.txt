              Overview

              Working with MCI UK provides you with the reassurance of a stable, global partner, whilst providing you with a bespoke, local solution tailored to your needs. Our London office is located in the heart of this dynamic city and provides the perfect base to ensure we are where you need us to be. The benefits of a truly global organisation mean you have access to local expertise wherever your next conference takes you.

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
                  Contact us directly if you are planning your event from outside the UK or working on behalf on an international client. We can help you and your delegates access the best that London has to offer.
