              Overview

              Located in a prime location within South Kensington, the 4-star Radisson Blu Edwardian, Vanderbilt Hotel is the former residence of the Vanderbilt family. The facilities and prime location of this Central London hotel make it the perfect base for both business and leisure travellers.
                  Many of the home's original features have been carefully restored. The 215 rooms at the Vanderbilt London hotel have a warm, classic English ambiance, featuring a rich decor and marble-clad bathrooms. Larger deluxe rooms include upgraded amenities, such as extra workspace and a seating area. Access to the hotel’s bedroom floors is by room key only.
                  Scoff & Banter, Kensington has been stunningly updated with reclaimed furniture and fittings, intricately detailed high ceilings in keeping with the listed building, and elegant bay windows.
                  At one with its neighbourhood, the restaurant design is created to encourage interaction and a sense of stylish comfort, with high sharing tables and a private dining room. It is perfect for intimate dinners, events and family gatherings.
                  See below for criterion that have been met that are relevant for pharmaceutical meeting planners.
                   
                  Have you hosted an event involving Healthcare Professionals (HCPs) or Healthcare Organisations (HCOs) in your venue in 2017?
                  - Up to 25 events
                  Is your venue located within 15 mins travel time to a scientific or healthcare educational venue such as a university hospital, MedCity, research facility etc.?
                  - Imperial College, Bupa Cromwell Hospital, Kensington and Chelsea College
                  Why do you feel your property is appropriate to host events involving HCPs & HCOs?
                  - Four star
                  - No casino
                  - No spa
                  - Business Focussed
                  - Located near to scientific or educational venue
                  - Located near to a transport hub
                  Has your venue been independently assessed for your appropriateness to host healthcare events involving HCPs & HCOs?
                  - Yes, by healthcare-venues.com
                  Have your staff been trained on how to manage and work with specialist agencies and corporate clients events involving HCPs and HCOs?
                  - Yes
