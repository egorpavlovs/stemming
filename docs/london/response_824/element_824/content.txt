              Overview

              Recently imaginatively refurbished, Hilton London Heathrow Airport has a fresh, contemporary feel. The piazza-style atrium features relaxing water cascades, flooded in natural light from front and rear walls made entirely of glass. Any event here will enjoy enormous success, due to the fabulous interiors and the unique location. 
                  36 conference rooms offer limitless meeting choice, while the hotel’s two suites are superbly flexible for larger events. The Heathrow Suite (350 delegates) has two connected meeting rooms and a private foyer. The Concorde Suite (200 theater-style or 300 for a reception) can also divide into three sections, each able to seat 80 theater-style. All our meeting rooms are self-contained and located together on the ground floor. 
                  Hilton London Heathrow Airport’s three restaurants offer a delightful choice, from modern Chinese and Far Eastern cuisine to continental-style dishes, light meals and drinks 24 hours a day. There’s a great indoor swimming pool and an enticing LivingWell health club too.  
                  Central London is easily accessible via the Piccadilly line and Heathrow Express (20 minute journey to Paddington Station), which also provides a complimentary connection to Terminals 1, 2 and 3. The hotel offers a scheduled Hotel Hoppa service to Terminal 5.
                  This content has been supplied by Hilton London Heathrow Airport
