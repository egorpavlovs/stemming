              Overview

              Sitting on the elegant tree-lined avenue of Holland Park, Hilton London Kensington offers easy access to the West End and many of London’s major business centres; the great exhibition halls of Earl’s Court and Olympia are literally minutes away. For outdoor pursuits, Notting Hill and West London are within easy reach. Rarely are business and pleasure so well intertwined. 
                   
                  Events and occasions: The Christie Suite is the principal suite, holding up to 370 for a meeting or 250 for dinner dances. It can also be sub-divided into the Christie (250 guests or delegates), Gunnell (80) and Jackson (30) rooms. The Kensington Suite caters for up to 120 guests. We can also arrange for private events to be held in the elegant surroundings of the Victoria and Albert Museum. 
                  Choose from a vast number of bedrooms all under one roof – with Executive Rooms and suites for your most distinguished of guests. Should you require additional rooms Hilton London Olympia is just a short walk away. Feast on modern European fare at WestEleven Restaurant or unwind in the LivingWell Express gym. To ease your arrival and departure, we can arrange transport direct to Heathrow Airport.
