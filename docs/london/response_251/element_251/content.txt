              Overview

              In the heart of West London, St Giles London hotel is located in Bloomsbury. Just under a 5-minute walk from Tottenham Court Road Station, the St Giles London hotel is an easy reach via the Central or Northern line.
                  As one of the most central venues in London, the conference centre at St Giles London hotel is ideal for planners and delegates alike. With its own dedicated external entrance, the conference centre features six modern and flexible rooms finished to high specifications, which include LCD Projector or LCD TV projection link and WiFi service. Meeting room capacity ranges from 2 to 195 delegates.
