              Overview

              Spectra’s first-class track record, with over six decades’ experience in organising prestigious incentive programmes, executive meetings, conferences and events, means we’re renowned for an unbeatable level of professional expertise and sophistication. Financial stability, quality control, commitment to excellence and communication skills typify Spectra's operation.
                  Incorporating Premium UK (for incoming European MICE business), Spectra has dedicated multilingual teams based in London and Edinburgh, where our creative and flexible staff will work flat out to ensure the smooth running of your programme, meeting or event. Whatever the size of event, from 20 to 2,000, we will ensure that your expectations are met and exceeded every time.
                  As well as being a London & Partners Gold Partner, Spectra is a member of SITE and MPI.
