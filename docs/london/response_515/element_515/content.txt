              Overview

              The Connaught offers one of London's best-kept secrets when it comes to entertaining privately. Whether you are two or 200 our range of venues, from elegant and intimate function rooms to Mayfair's newest stunning ballroom, won't disappoint.
                  When the time comes, your event is our focus; your details become our obsession. We leave nothing to chance. We've spent over 100 years paying very close attention to the little details.

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
                  Contact us directly if you are planning your event from outside the UK or working on behalf on an international client. We can help you and your delegates access the best that London has to offer.
