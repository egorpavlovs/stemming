              Overview

              The London Stadium (former Olympic Stadium) has been transformed and is available for meetings and events, from awards dinners to private celebrations, meetings for six to presentations for 260, and receptions to Christmas parties for up to 1,000 people. 
                   
                  We boast some of the most stylish stadium spaces available, at a venue that comes to life during events. Combined with great food and service, our events create memories for everyone attending. 
                  You don’t have to be a football or athletics fan to appreciate the fabulous facilities, the contemporary and sophisticated nature of the rooms with views of the stadium bowl or the City of London. 
                  Embrace the venue by combining a meeting or event with a team building element or a tour of the changing rooms, player’s tunnel, dugout and indoor track.
                  This content has been supplied by London Stadium
