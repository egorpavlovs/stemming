              Overview

              Apex London Wall is a 4 star deluxe hotel offering contemporary and stylish surroundings. 89 spacious bedrooms and suites feature top of the range entertainment facilities, including 42” LCD televisions,Bose Docking Stations, complimentary WiFi and free local telephone calls.
                  ‘Off the Wall’ Restaurant Bar serves traditional, modern cuisine in stylish surroundings, boasting double height floor-to-ceiling windows. The hotel also has an on site gym. Ideal for business and leisure, just moments from St Paul’s Cathedral, the Bank of England Museum, Royal Exchange and Barbican Centre. The hotel is near to Moorgate, Bank and Liverpool Street Stations, in the heart of the city’s business and financial districts with direct links on the DLR to London City Airport and Excel.
