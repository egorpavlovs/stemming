              Overview

              Mix 1930s Art Deco design with medieval charm and stunning moated gardens and the result is Eltham Palace, one of London’s smartest, most unusual and talked about private event locations
                  Eltham Palace and Gardens is available for conferences, meetings, drinks receptions and dinners all year round.
                  Eltham Palace may also be hired for filming and photography.
                  Contact Sam Anderson, Hospitality Co-ordinator
                  sam.anderson@english-heritage.org.uk
                  020 8294 2577
                  This content has been supplied by English Heritage: Eltham Palace
