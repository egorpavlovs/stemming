          IMEX Frankfurt 2018 Partner Preview
          
		Join us in Frankfurt 15-17 May on stand F400 and meet our exceptional partners, from hotels and venues to DMCs and transport providers!
    
            Twitter

LinkedIn


        

        
            
        

      

            
		
				
				
		
		
London's official convention bureau invites you to stand F400 at IMEX Frankfurt. Book your meeting with us to get access to our fantastic partners and tap into expert knowledge of creating unforgettable events in London.
Stand Partners
Barbican Set in one of the most iconic Brutalist architecture buildings in the world, the Barbican Centre has a range of spaces which can be hired for conferences, meetings, receptions and weddings, including The Barbican Conservatory, one of the most unusual venues in London.
Central Hall WestminsterCentral Hall Westminster is a Grade II listed architectural masterpiece and is one of central London's largest conference venues, located opposite Westminster Abbey. From intimate meeting rooms to an auditorium, Central Hall Westminster hosts a remarkable range of flexible events with natural lights and blackout facilities for corporate, public and private events.
Church House WestminsterA historic and versatile conference centre and event space located in a surprisingly quiet and tranquil corner of central London. Church House Westminister offers 19 rooms ranging from the large and impressive to the small and intimate, and hosts over 800 events a year.
The Doyle Collection The Doyle Collection offers a selection of impressive hotels with meeting and event spaces in central London with tremendous state-of-the-art décor. Whether you're organising a conference, a banquet, or a press event, try The Bloomsbury Hotel and check out its enviable instagram images.
etc. venues From meeting spaces in London, to training facilities and conference needs, etc.venues has a venue to suit all your requirements. With 13 leading venues in and around London, you are spoilt for choice.
Chelsea Football ClubStamford Bridge, the home of Chelsea Football Club is one of London's most unique and versatile meetings and events venues. It boasts 24 function rooms and 60 syndicate rooms, alongside a wealth of onsite additional facilities including two hotels and luxury health club.
ExCeL LondonThe home of world leading events, ExCeL London offers an incredible 100,000sqm of flexible event space, including the UK's largest auditorium (up to 5,000 delegates), London's largest banqueting hall (3, 000 guests) and a stunning, multi-function conference suite with fabulous reception and registration areas (2,500 delegates).
Historic Royal PalacesWow delegates by hosting your prestigious event at one of London’s most famous landmarks. From the majestic Tower of London to the romantic Kensington Palace, from the flamboyant Hampton Court Palace to the intimate Kew Palace, make your event an unforgettable experience with Historic Royal Palaces.
Marriott London Complex Marriott London Complex provide innovative spaces, inspired experiences and personalised services across their properties in London. From meetings to corporate events, Marriott makes it easy to search for venues, compare rates and get every detail right.
Park Plaza Hotels & Resorts From start to finish, Park Plaza Hotels have a dedicated meeting & events team who will go the extra mile for you. With venues centrally located to London's main attractions, there is no doubt that delegates will get an unbeatable experience. Meet with them and make sure you book one of the rooms with a view of Big Ben across the river.
SpectraSpectra DMC's multi-lingual, multi-national team has years of experience working with MICE groups from all over Europe. Together with Spectra and Spectra in Scotland, Premium UK organise creative ideas and attractive programmes for incentives, meetings and conferences. Meet with them at IMEX Frankfurt to kick-start your event planning.
Principal. London DMC Principal. London DMC is a specialist London destination management company combining an in-depth knowledge of London with profound award-winning logistical and creative experience, and a dedicated team to manage your events and produce an out-of-this-world experience. Ask the locals.
QEII Centre The QEII Centre is the largest dedicated conference, events and exhibition space in central London. The venue offers world-class facilities for events ranging from an intimate gathering for 20 to a reception for 1,200 guests, or anything in between opposite Westminster Abbey.
Rosewood Hotel Centrally located on High Holborn, Rosewood London combines English heritage with contemporary sophistication. The hotel offers 262 guestrooms and 44 suites with the feel of stylish London residence. The event spaces include the Grand Ballroom, three heritage boardrooms, the Living Room and the Dining Room, accommodating groups from 10 to 550.
The Langham, London The Langham has a long and illustrious history of hosting lavish events since 1865, exclusive and private in location with an unmatched level of service. From fashionable meeting rooms to the lavish Grand Ballroom with adjoining Courtyard Garden, there's a venue for every event.
glh. Hotels glh Hotels offer a fantastic range of options including the Thistle Marble Arch, with 692 large, high-quality, and well-equipped bedrooms. This must-know-about hotel overlooks Oxford Street and just a minute’s walk from Marble Arch and Hyde Park. 
Edwardian Hotels LondonThe Edwardian properties in London include the Grafton, Kenilworth, and Bloomsbury Street hotels- not to mention the fabulous May Fair Hotel. This luxury hotel, opened by King George V in 1927, is an 11-minute walk from the boutiques and art auctions on Bond Street, and a mile (1.6km) from operas at the London Coliseum. Ask for a meeting at IMEX Frankfurt and find out about their meeting spaces and the properties than can cater for healthcare meetings.
EurostarEurostar, the high-speed passenger train linking London with the rest of Europe, has a tailored service for business travellers wanting to make the best use of their time. Or use a carriage as an events space for a truly international event?! Ask for more details at IMEX Frankfurt, stand F400.
Business Design CentreLocated 10 minutes from Kings Cross St. Pancras and a short walk from Angel Tube station, the Business Design Centre offers a stylish, flexible and convenient solution for conventions and events of up to 2,000 delegates. Ask them about the exhibition space of 6,000 sq m at IMEX Frankfurt this year. 
Rail Europe London is a city of connections and Rail Europe can get you from London to Brussels in 2 hours direct! Ask them about block bookings for your delegates and how to link your travel and your event in Europe seamlessly at IMEX this year.
