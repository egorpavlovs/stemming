              Overview

              The Sadler's Wells Theatre Complex, rebuilt in 1998, is a highly modern and flexible venue, offering space for meetings for 2 or 3 people through to major corporate events for 1,500 people. The complex comprises three theatres along with a variety of meeting rooms and rehearsal studios.
                  This content has been supplied by Sadler's Wells
