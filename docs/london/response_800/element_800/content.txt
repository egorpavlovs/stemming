              Overview

              The East Wintergarden, a spectacular 27m high arched glass atrium, centrally located in Canary Wharf's Bank Street is a unique and stylish venue. The East Wintergarden boasts 682 sq metres of coloured marble floor, steamed beech wood wall panelling and views overlooking Jubilee Park and the waterfront promenade and has a light and elegant atmosphere perfect for exhibitions, cocktail parties, product launches and receptions. 
                  Adjacent to the entrance to the Jubilee Line Station and a short walk from Canary Wharf  Heron Quays DLR stations, the East Wintergarden is less than 15 minutes from the City, the West End and London City Airport.
                  This content has been supplied by East Wintergarden
