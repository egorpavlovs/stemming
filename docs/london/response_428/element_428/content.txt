              Overview

              A modern, purose-built residence offering 737 single study bedrooms arranged in self-contained flats of four to nine rooms. All rooms have shower, wash basin and ensuite toilet. Easy access is available to tourist attractions on the south bank of the river, including Shakespeare's Globe, HMS Belfast, and Tate Modern.
