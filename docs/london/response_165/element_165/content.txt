              Overview

              Hilton London Paddington is one of London’s grandest hotels offering a full range of modern facilities in a fantastic central location. This deluxe hotel has 364 beautiful guestrooms, including 28 suites, the Great Western ballroom with space for 350 guests, 18 meeting rooms and 7 focus rooms for more intimate get-togethers.  Meetings and events space totals 1,530 sqm, and is complemented by a business centre and wireless internet access. 
                   You will also find within this elegant Victorian building, our cosy Brasserie restaurant, atmospheric Steam Bar, a lively casino and a gym.  The Executive Lounge on the ground floor offers complimentary access to guests staying in Executive or Suite accommodation.  Alternatively day-guests can buy a day pass to take advantage of all the benefits.  Centrally located at the heart of Paddington, we’re ideally positioned to connect those staying for business. 
                   We adjoin Paddington Station, and are just 15 minutes from Heathrow Airport via the Heathrow Express train. With the capital and the South and West of England conveniently accessible by rail and road, we’re the ideal choice to accommodate large numbers of delegates and event guests.  Whether you want to experience the vibrancy of the London attractions or find out the quickest route to your next business meeting, our 24-hour concierge service is always on hand to make the most of your stay.
