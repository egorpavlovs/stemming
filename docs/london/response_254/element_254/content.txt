              Overview

              With over 1700sqm of event space, ILEC Conference Centre boasts one of the largest convention spaces in London being the perfect place for conferences, gala dinners, wedding receptions and much more with capacity up to 1200 people.
                  The venue’s main feature is its spectacular 1200 sqm, pillar-free London Suite. Already best know for its sheer size and flexibility, the 2011 renovation will transform the room adding 3 extra modular space for optimum flexibility. The redesign will enable numerous configurations, creating new levels of bespoke event possibilities.
                  Also, five smaller meeting rooms, all with natural daylight and air conditioning, can accommodate from 2 to 60 delegates and are ideal as syndicate rooms or hospitality lounges.
                  The venue also hosts the international-class hotel, Ibis London Earls Court***. Built over 12 floors and with stunning views of the London skyline, the hotel features 504 en-suite bedrooms (inclusive of 6 suites) fully equipped, an international restaurant and a typical London pub.
                  Hotel and venue benefit from an excellent location in the heart of West London, opposite the international Earls Court Exhibition Centre and surrounded by Kensington, Chelsea and Fulham.
                  This content has been supplied by Hotel Ibis London Earl's Court
