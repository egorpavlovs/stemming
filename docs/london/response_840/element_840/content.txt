              Overview

              Hilton London Angel Islington is situated in the heart of fashionable Islington yet remains close to Central London, striking the perfect locational balance. 
                  The hotel offers 188 bedrooms and suites with a sleek, sophisticated design. WiFi is complimentary in the common areas; in-room Wifi is available at a premium of £7.50 inc VAT per 24h connection.
                  The executive rooms include access to the executive lounge, where guests can enjoy complimentary breakfast, soft drinks and snacks throughout the day.
                  Hilton London Angel Islington is just a five-minute walk from Angel Tube station and one stop from King's Cross St. Pancras Station, making it an ideal location to connect to Eurostar and Europe. 
                  Located right next door to the Business Design Centre and just a few minutes' drive away from the Emirates Stadium, the hotel offers an ideal venue for those attending a conference or event.
                  Within walking distance of the hotel, guests can see a play at Almeida or Sadler's Wells theatres and discover the street markets. Some of London's most popular attractions are just a short Tube ride away, including Tower Bridge, Tower of London, the Coca-Cola London Eye and Oxford Street.
