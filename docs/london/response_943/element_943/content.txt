              Overview

              Located in central London, this Grade II-listed building has a number of event spaces suitable for a wide variety of daytime events.
                  New exciting evening event spaces have been launched to mark the centenary of the First World War. A dramatic new atrium, perfect for seated dinners and receptions, and a breathtaking suspended gallery in the rooftop provide a unique space for a variety of events, including breakfasts, receptions, presentations and dinners.
                  This content has been supplied by IWM London: Imperial War Museum London

                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
