              Overview

              Constructed in 1923 for Cox's & Kings bank, this landmark building on the corner of Waterloo Place and Pall Mall has been restored to create a deluxe 5-star hotel offering 186 bedrooms and suites along with Brasserie Roux and 8 conference and banqueting rooms.
                  This content has been supplied by Sofitel London St James
