              Overview

              The London Marriott Hotel West India Quay is ideally situated in the heart of Canary Wharf. With its sleek, clean lines and its high-quality design, our hotel provides the perfect setting for both business and leisure travellers. 
                  Stay in the newly refurbished, spacious rooms and suites that offer an array of technological features. Enjoy the G&Tea Lounge featuring over 180 types of gin or the Manhattan Grill restaurant for our signature steak. 
                  The hotel is located just 10 minutes away on the DLR from ExCeL London and 11 minutes from London City Airport via public transport.  
                  The Executive Lounge overlooking Canary Wharf is a popular retreat and the perfect place to work or to relax after a busy day. It can be used to enjoy breakfast, teas, coffees and snacks, while from 5pm to 7pm each day canapés and chilled drinks are served. All guests staying in an Executive Room or a Suite have complimentary, exclusive access. Complimentary Afternoon Tea is also served at the Executive Lounge over the weekend. 
                  There are 20 conference and meeting rooms totalling 14,445 sq ft of flexible event space, catering for 2 to 300 delegates with innovative menus. State-of-the-art technology and skilled AV technicians provide support prior to and throughout events.
                  Organise your next meeting with our experienced conference and events team in our convenient and flexible meeting spaces. Experience success and comfort at our London Docklands hotel and let the area’s fascinating history inspire you every day!
