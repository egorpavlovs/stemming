                  The Grand Connaught Rooms is one of London's most sought-after venues. This iconic property has over 30 meetings and events spaces, ranging in size from intimate rooms for 10 to our Grand Hall for 1,000.

              Overview

              Grand Connaught Rooms is located in the heart of theatre land and surrounded by the buzz of Covent Garden
                  The iconic Grand Hall, one of London's largest and most impressive event venues, the opulent Champagne Bar, the indulgent Tempus Restaurant and the sleek and modern meeting rooms, collectively make the Grand Connaught Rooms the ultimate venue in London   
                  Our sister hotel, Hotel Russell, is only five minutes away in Russell Square and offers 373 beautifully appointed bedrooms, as well as our delicious Tempus Restaurant and Bar
                  With over 60 meetings and events spaces between the two properties, varying in size from space for 2 to 1000 people, we have something that suits almost any event.
                  This content has been supplied by De Vere Grand Connaught
