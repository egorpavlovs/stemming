              Overview

              Imperial is Central London's largest academic conference venue for conferences, banquets, exhibitions, dinner dances and summer schools. Situated in one of London's finest locations, just a short walk from Harrods and Hyde Park, with world-famous landmarks such as the V&A and Royal Albert Hall on our doorstep.
                  Imperial Venues offer more than 100 meeting rooms, 18 exhibition areas, comprehensive sporting facilities and fully catered dining areas. 
                  Deluxe ensuite accommodation is available on-site next to the Royal Albert Hall and the V&A museum for residential events during the summer vacation; as is standard accommodation with shared bathroom facilities in other halls of residence.
                  This content has been supplied by Imperial College Conferences
