              Overview

              On 3rd January after 3 months of work we closed the doors of St Ermins Hotel to embark on the final stage of our exhaustive refurbishment. Originally a “Grand Dame” hotel in London and over 100 years over history, careful and patient restorations are taking place to allow us to reposition this hotel back into the 4 star deluxe market.
                  Due to re-launch in early April 2011, we will have 331 bedrooms in total with 14 meeting rooms catering for up to 160 people for conference events and 400 for social or networking occasions. Another change will see the introduction of a landscaped garden at the front of the hotel which can be used for drinks receptions events during the summer months, and a terrace on our mezzanine floor for up to 60 people. Location wise we are close to all of the large Conference centres in Westminster with Queen Elizabeth Conference Centre, Central Hall, Church House and One Great George Street only a 2 minute walk from the hotel!
                  This content has been supplied by St Ermins Hotel

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
