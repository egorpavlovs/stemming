              Overview

              So much more than just a stadium, Stamford Bridge has grown to become one of London’s leading meetings and event venues. Boasting 24 function rooms and 60 syndicate rooms, alongside a wealth of onsite additional facilities including two hotels and a luxury health club, we offer something for everyone - for work, play or even both. 
                  Over 20 years’ experience in industry-leading events, with dedicated account management and inspiring, innovative cuisine has seen us set the new standard for success. Contact our events team today to find out how we can create your perfect event.
