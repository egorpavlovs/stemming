              Overview

              Ideally located in central    London   between King’s Cross and Euston railway stations (5 mins walk) and only 3 mins walk away from St Pancras International, the Eurostar terminal.   Oxford Street  , the West End, the   British   Museum   and the City are just a short distance away. It is the perfect location whether travelling for business or pleasure with easy access via the M40/A40 and M1.     
                   The hotel has 312 bedrooms over 16 th  floors and 17 meeting rooms including the Shaw Theatre (446), the Nobel suite (360) and the Skyline suite with stunning views of London from the 15 th  floor. All function rooms have state of the art technology, are air-conditioned and have internet connection (WiFi & broadband).Our awarded restaurant and bar ‘Mirrors’ serve a variety of modern British European food in a bright and spacious environment. All rooms feature air-conditioning, flat screen TV, MP3 player connection, high-speed internet connection, iron, ironing board, trouser press and tea and coffee making facilities, EU & UK power sockets and a working desk area. The hotel has a fitness centre, sauna and steam room, 24 hour room service and a business centre.
