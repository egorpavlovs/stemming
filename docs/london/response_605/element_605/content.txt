              Overview

              With 26 years of extensive, first-hand knowledge of London venues and meeting rooms, Ben Thompson Events offers a responsive and reliable first-class service that can be tailor-made to your specific requirements. We provide cost-effective, imaginative solutions for your event, whether it is a private dinner for executives or a global conference.
                  We are meticulous and professional, and 
                  deliver a highly personalised service that listens to your needs, and keeps within budget. 
                  Our long established, close relationships with the major and also the more intimate, exclusive venues enables us to negotiate excellent rates on your behalf, every time.
