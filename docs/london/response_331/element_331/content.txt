              Overview

              Silver Sturgeon's interiors and decks are created by leading British designers and architects, offering the perfect venue for 50 to 550 guests. 
                  The yacht can remain static at a pier or cruise the Thames, from Chelsea to the Thames Barrier, taking in London’s most famous landmarks, or be located outside ExCeL London as a VIP venue.
                  You are in good company celebrating at Silver Sturgeon. From Hollywood legends and political leaders, to rock stars and royalty, the venue has hosted many top names over the past 70 years. 
                  Silver Sturgeon's specialist sales and planning team can make sure organising your event is stress-free from start to finish. 
                  The Woods family are known for their beautiful river yachts, delicious food and passion for great service, and will wow your guests with the perfect London event.
                  (Total Max fleet Capacities Silver Sturgeon & Silver Barracuda - 580 guests for a formal seated meal, 780 guests for reception).
