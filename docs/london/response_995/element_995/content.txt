              Overview

              A unique space at the heart of London’s prestigious Square Mile, Banking Hall is a venue that has been designed to impress. 
                  Surrounded by towering marble columns and stunning Art Deco decor, you can host your event with a touch of Great Gatbsy glamour in an excellent location. At the very centre of London’s famous financial district, Banking Hall straddles Cornhill and Lombard Street, overlooking the Bank of England.
                  Whatever event you are planning, Banking Hall is the perfect venue to impress your guests.
                  This content has been supplied by Banking Hall
