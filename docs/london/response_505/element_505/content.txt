              Overview

              Ibis Styles London ExCel is located across from the Excel Exhibition Centre (2 minute walk) and is two stops from the O2 Arena.
                  The hotel is conveniently located just a few steps away from the Custom House Station of the Docklands Light Railway (DLR). The location and easy access to London's public transport network means that the hotel offers easy access to the O2 Arena and the city centre. London City Airport is just five minutes' drive.
                  The Custom House Hotel offers an excellent choice of high quality accommodation at great value. All our rooms are equipped with HD LCD, fast internet service and all the amenities.
