              Overview

              Trinity House, Headquarters of the Lighthouse service, built in 1796, has a unique, elegant suite of five rooms in a tranquil City location overlooking Trinity Square and the Tower of London.
                  Luncheons, dinner parties for upto 130 guests, training courses, board meetings for up to 40, and cocktail receptions for up to 180 guests, are accommodated in historic surroundings with many Royal paintings, and models of ships, as well as The Bell from The Royal Yacht Britannia.
                  This content has been supplied by Trinity House
