              Overview

              Thistle Kensington Gardens is conveniently located next to Hyde Park with easy access to Heathrow Express, Paddington and Victoria station.
                  The hotel has 4 meeting rooms, all air-conditioned and offering plenty of natural light to create a comfortable, productive environment. The largest room, the Park and Porchester, seats up to 45 theatre style.
                  The hotel offers a full range of food and drink, including an extended range of healthy options.
                  Call 0871 376 9024 or email mekensingtongardens@thistle.co.uk for further information.
                  This content has been supplied by Thistle Kensington Gardens

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
