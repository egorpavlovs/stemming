              Overview

              The Holiday Inn London – Wembley is perfectly located for events at the iconic Wembley Stadium and the SSE Arena, Wembley (formerly known as Wembley Arena). Just 20 minutes from Central London and 45 minutes to London Heathrow Airport - the location offers the best of both worlds!
                  The hotel is also within easy reach of the North Circular, M1, M4, M40 and the M25 with on-site parking accommodating up to 250 cars. The hotel has quick access to London by public transport, less than 10 minutes to Baker Street/Marylebone from Wembley Park Tube station or a direct train from Wembley Stadium Station to Central London in 12 minutes.
                  Discover a home away from home in one of our 336 bedrooms designed with comfort and convenience to help you unwind. For business, the Holiday Inn London - Wembley offers flexible meeting rooms and a banqueting suite, catering up to 500 people. On-site Business Centre and free Wi-Fi throughout provides convenience for organisers and delegates alike.
