              Overview

              The Wren Suite is available for both day and evening hire, and can cater for business meetings, lunches and dinners for up to 75 guests.  
                  The Crypt at St Paul's Cathedral is the largest in Europe and is a wonderful space in which to host larger corporate events. The west end of the Crypt can accommodate  250 guests for seated dinners as well as 350 for standing receptions. 
                  Also available for smaller daytime and evening functions is Sir Christopher Wren's Temple Bar. Temple Bar is a unique structure that marked the gateway to the City of London for 200 years, it was recently moved to its present location in Paternoster Square.
                  This content has been supplied by St Paul's Cathedral
