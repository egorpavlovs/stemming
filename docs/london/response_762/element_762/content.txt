              Overview

              Enjoy classes, courses and corporate events at Cookery School.
                  The Ultimate Afternoon Tea, where you can learn to make a delicious afternoon tea, is one of our most popular classes and a brilliant introduction to some British classics - perfect for visitors to the UK!
                  All classes end with a meal around the Cookery School table, served with a glass of wine or bubbly. 
                  Check out our website to see what we can offer or contact us to create an extra special event for your clients. 
                  Cookery School is based in central London and is the most sustainable culinary school in London, according to the Sustainable Restaurant Association.
                  This content has been supplied by Cookery School at Little Portland Street
