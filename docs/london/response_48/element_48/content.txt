          Migraine Trust International Symposium
          
		More than 750 delegates attended the 17th Migraine Trust International Symposium in London.
    
            Twitter

LinkedIn


        

        
            
        

      

            
		
				
				
		
		
The Migraine Trust International Symposium was recently held in London's Hilton Metropole. The event was a huge success and saw more than 750 clinicians, scientists and migraine sufferers gather to learn about the latest research findings and discuss current trends in headache treatment and prevention.
