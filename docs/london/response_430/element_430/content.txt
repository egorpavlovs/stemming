              Overview

              Step inside the Radisson Blu Edwardian, Berkshire Hotel to find a tranquil haven with sleek dark wood panelling, contemporary artwork and a friendly service.
                  Behind its glass doors, visitors can find all the comforts of a modern 4-star deluxe hotel. The hotel’s Oxford Street address means that it is a short distance from some of the capital’s most popular retail destinations. You’ll be close to Oxford Street, Regent Street, and the designer district of Bond Street, as well as the boutique fashion outlets and cafes of Carnaby and Soho.
                  Tea Rooms celebrates the quintessential British time-honoured tradition of afternoon tea. Tucked away just off Oxford Street, you’ll find a cosy retreat serving the very best of British afternoon tea, whatever the time. Let us take you on a journey from the savoury to the sweet, to the fabulous and the great. With a few little surprises along the way…
