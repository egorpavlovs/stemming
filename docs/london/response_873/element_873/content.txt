              Overview

              Centrally located in London, The Bloomsbury Hotel is just a few minutes’ walk from Oxford Street, Leicester Square and Covent Garden. Designed by the most celebrated architect of the 20th century, Sir Edwin Lutyens, The Bloomsbury Hotel is a listed neo-Georgian building which effortlessly blends old-world grandeur with memorable and modern luxury. With 153 luxurious guest rooms, 15 high-tech meeting and conference rooms, a book-lined Library and stoned-floored Chapel, whether for business or pleasure, a stay at The Bloomsbury Hotel is a guarantee of a truly classic experience.
