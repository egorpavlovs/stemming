              Overview

              Take your event to new heights at the ArcelorMittal Orbit.
                  Journey 80m into the clouds and enjoy a breathtaking panorama of up to 20 miles across London as you dine, dance, launch your new product or even get married at our award-winning events venue.
                  Our flexible spaces can be used for team building, conferences, parties and so much more.
                  With a feast of fantastic food packages from canape receptions and mouth-watering breakfasts to indulgent three-course dinners; there will be something to suit all tastes.
                  And the fun doesn’t stop there! Make your event truly unique by hiring The Slide: the world’s tallest and longest tunnel slide. Experience gentle curves, thrilling drops and a tight corkscrew, as you and your guests travel through light and dark sections at speeds of up to 15mph.
                  This content has been supplied by ArcelorMittal Orbit
