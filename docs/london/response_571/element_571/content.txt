              Overview

              The company’s philosophy of change and innovation, coupled with the expertise that sits behind it, has enabled us to offer radical new products and enhanced service levels that utilise the latest web technologies within the exhibition industry. 
                  Open Exhibitions have designed and built eye catching features and stands for clients such as Adidas at the London Marathon, London & Partners and the London Eye.
                  Our experienced project management teams can manage the whole process from initial design brief to the stand build for you.
                  With a reputation for providing creative sustainable stands cost effectively; we will create the right unique solution for you.
