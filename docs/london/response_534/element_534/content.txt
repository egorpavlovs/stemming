              Overview

              Wyndham Grand London Chelsea Harbour is London’s only five-star all-suite hotel. The hotel is only minutes from all the major sites and the legendary boutiques which line the fashionable King's Road.
                  The Chelsea Riverside Brasserie Bar and Terrace offers a dining experience with marina views. Pure indulgence is on offer at the Spa, with a gym, 17m pool including body and shoulder jets, a walk-in sauna, steam room and six additional treatment rooms.
                  Our air-conditioned meetings and events facility has six meeting rooms, which can host events for up to 800 delegates reception style up to 400 people for a seated dinner.
                  This content has been supplied by The Chelsea Harbour Hotel
