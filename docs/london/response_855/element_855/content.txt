              Overview

              The Park Lane Hotel features 6 meeting rooms and is the ideal place for your event from 8 to 1200 people. Our original art deco Ballroom has played host to many famous films and television programmes. Three contemporary meeting rooms can accommodate anywhere from 8 to 22 people.
