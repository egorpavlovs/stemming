              Overview

              At Pacific World, we are the ultimate mixologists, creating an ever-evolving landscape of opportunities for meetings, incentives, conferences & events in over 40 countries & 100 destinations around the world. 
                  We are passionate, with a team combining the best ingredients - experience, knowledge, creativity & fresh ideas - to concoct successful, bespoke meetings, incentives & events. With over 35 years’ experience of flawless destination management and event management services in Asia, Europe, Middle East, Southern Africa & Americas, Pacific World has earned a reputation for quality, value and creativity, made possible by successful long-term local partnerships. 
                  Turning visions into reality, Pacific World works diligently with clients & vendors to create & implement thoughtful, high-impact events that engage targeted audiences & heighten brands. We follow strict compliance rules along with health & safety procedures regulations, mitigating risks to ensure safe but fresh events.
