              Overview

              The futuristic building of The Peter Harrison Planetarium houses a modern astronomy centre, making it a perfect venue for product launches and intimate celebrations. 
                  The foyer boasts floor-to-ceiling windows and a star-studded floor, where you can dine and entertain. 
                  Transport guests far away during an exclusive planetarium show, fly them to distant galaxies, show the birth of a star or land on Mars. Each show is led by one of our expert astronomers and can be tailored to your event – see the night sky from anywhere in the world, a time in history or enjoy in the here and now.
                  This content has been supplied by Peter Harrison Planetarium
