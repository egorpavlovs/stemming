              Overview

              Sapphire Private is an exclusive multi-service events management agency that has experience in planning and delivering memorable events. Our event offering includes incentive trips, corporate and sports events, conferences, product launches and private parties.
                  Our meticulous attention to detail and uncompromising approach to quality will ensure that your event is delivered to an exceptional standard. This, and our large network of suppliers, are the key reasons why our global client base chooses to continue working with us.
                  Each event that we deliver is tailored to the preferences of our clients. We welcome events of varying complexity, size and budget and we strive to provide our clients with a truly personalised and unique experience.
                  We focus on working collaboratively with our clients and spend time learning about their business, understanding their challenges and planning bespoke events to support their goals.
