              Overview

              The Berkeley, in leafy Knightsbridge, is one of the most stylish choice in town for that very special event. 
                  With its own dedicated entrance hall and elegant gallery interlinking the entertaining spaces, it offers a flexible arrangement of rooms which can be used together to accommodate all manner of corporate and social occasions. 
                  Whether you are arranging the society event of the year, an intimate dinner party or conference, we can bring added inspiration and flair to your plans, with innovative themes, bespoke menus, fine wines, cutting-edge floral displays and most important of all, an enthusiastic and dedicated team of professionals, who will ensure the most exceptional results.
