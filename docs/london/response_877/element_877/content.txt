              Overview

              College Hall based in the Bloomsbury area of the city. The British Museum is just a 2-minute walk away. The shopping district of Oxford Street is within a 10-minute walk of College Hall and features a wide variety of designer shops, restaurants and bars. The West End, Covent Garden and Piccadilly Circus can all be reached within a 20-minute walk or 15-minute tube journey.
                  Free wired internet is provided in each room at College Hall, as well as a work desk and wardrobe. Guests can also relax in the small private garden. Laundry facilities are provided, and there are small tea pantries on each floor which contain a sink, microwave and refrigerator.
