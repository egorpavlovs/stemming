              Overview

              ZSL London Zoo understand the importance of releasing the ideas, creativity and expertise of delegates and guests to tame and cultivate a perfect result. At ZSL London Zoo they 'bring down the bars' by creating interactive, immersive and inspiring exhibits –why not do the same for your delegates? 
                  The elegant suites and stylish meeting rooms are encapsulated in 36 acres of zoological gardens, home to 800 different species of animals; an inspiring setting to nurture an unforgettable event. 
                  ZSL London Zoo's versatile suites can be set up in a number of styles, ideal for meetings, conferences, presentations, awards dinner, product launches, team building and away days.
                  This content has been supplied by ZSL London Zoo
