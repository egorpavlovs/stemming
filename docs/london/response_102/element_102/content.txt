              Overview

              Kings Place provide world class conference and events spaces, and since opening have quickly established themselves as one of the premier event destinations in Europe. 
                  Kings Place has only been open since late 2008, so it’s state-of-the-art . In the past 18 months we have hosted some of the most technologically demanding events and became the first London venue to have a dedicated 1GB internet line installed, offering, among other things, fantastic in-house HD streaming capabilities. 
                  We have 2 purpose built auditoriums, a large banqueting space with outdoor terrace for up to 220 for dinner or 330 for a reception, 2 art galleries and the canal-side Rotunda Restaurant.
                  This content has been supplied by Kings Place
