              Overview

              Situated in a historic, Grade-I listed building overlooking The Mall and St James's Park, and located a short stroll from Trafalgar Square, Prince Philip House is a dashing venue at 3 Carlton House Terrace. 
                  Fresh from an extensive refurbishment project, Prince Philip House may have history to its name, but the modern interiors provide the perfection location for meetings and events in London. 
                  Located in the heart of London, not only is Prince Philip House easily accessible, it's also housed the Royal Academy of Engineering since 2008, alongside prestigious neighbours including the Royal Society and the British Academy.
                  Boasting sophisticated facilities, Prince Philip House is a grand location for board meetings, stakeholder meetings, lectures, conferences, private dinners, prize and award celebrations, exhibitions, press conferences, symposia, film shoots, product launches, training, team building activities and drinks or canape receptions.
                  This content has been supplied by Prince Philip House
