              Overview

              We source and coordinate with local suppliers for a wide range of organisations dealing with sports events, meetings, hosted buyer programmes, incentives, conferences and congresses. 
                  b network is uniquely positioned to deliver cost effective and value-driven solutions. With the help of our international network, we can organise programmes globally. You can expect the same personal attention and tailor-made services from all our offices.
                  Our partners enjoy the benefits of a meticulous and professional organisation providing world class customer services and dedicated websites for online reservation & registrations allowing:
                  - Individuals to view availability, book, amend and pay for reservations.
                  - Key accounts to fully manage their group bookings whether for delegates, exhibitors, and organisers.
                  We do this by simplifying the booking process, managing allotments and inventory, reporting and letting you have full visibility of your event. Our aim is to make life easier for you.
