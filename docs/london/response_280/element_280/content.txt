              Overview

              The property is a re-designed platform, with a unique and central location. 
                  On the water and around the corner from London City airport (LCY), the hotel has direct travel links to central London by DLR and Tube. The Excel and Crystal are neighbours and The O2 arena is just a 10-minute cable-car ride away.
                  The meeting rooms range from S to XL. All offer river view, natural daylight and good delegate packages. The XL boasts an in-room pantry and holds up to 50 in classroom. The Living Room hosts up to 120 in cocktail.
                  The concept introduces the “profit for non-profit” business model.
                  The hotel features 148 rooms, all ranging between a cosy 13 and roomy 30 sqm. All rooms feature an en-suite bathroom with shower, working desk, in-room safe and free Wi-Fi. 
                  The Living Room serves drinks and food all day. The rooftop is an oasis of green, boasting a bar and terrace. 
                  The amenities include free Wi-Fi, 24-hour front desk, concierge services, bike rental and free iPads for guests’ use.
