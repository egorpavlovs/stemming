              Overview

              The London Aquarium’s multi-million pound transformation into SEA LIFE London Aquarium offers the UK a new world class aquatic experience and a spectacular corporate events venue like no other in London.
                  The SEA LIFE London Aquarium is perfect for awards dinners for up to 180 guests and parties and networking events for up to 1,000 guests. We have included event lighting, sound and furniture making the venue easy for you the event organiser.
                  Tel: 44 (0)207 487 0224
                  Email: events@merlinvenues.com
                  This content has been supplied by SEA LIFE London

                  London & Partners
                  As the official convention bureau for London, we offer free, impartial advice, guidance and support to event planners, associations and convention organisers.
                  Contact us directly if you are planning your event from outside the UK or working on behalf on an international client. We can help you and your delegates access the best that London has to offer.
