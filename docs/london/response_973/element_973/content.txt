              Overview

              Discover top-flight hospitality at the Royal Air Force Museum from a very affordable £47.50 per person ex.VAT.
                  Situated in Colindale, the museum offers a wide range of versatile areas for conferences, meetings, exhibitions and product launches along with stunning aircraft halls available for receptions, dinners and parties – just 30 minutes from central London.
                  The museum's dedicated and experienced events team endeavours to make your event planning and implementation as painless as possible. With free delegate parking for up to 280 cars, easy access by road, rail and Tube, the museum is the perfect venue for any event. For information, please call +44 (0)20 8358 4848 or visit www.rafmuseum.org/london
                  This content has been supplied by Royal Air Force Museum London
