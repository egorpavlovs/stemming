              Overview

              Rosewood London is centrally located in the heart of London on High Holborn, just moments from Covent Garden.
                  Combining English heritage with contemporary sophistication, the hotel has the feel of a stylish London residence and offers 262 guestrooms and 44 suites including the Grand Manor House Wing, an exclusive six-bedroom suite with its own postcode. 
                  Tony Chi and Associates created the interiors of the public areas including 12 event spaces encompassing 1,083 square metres (11,660 square feet) of indoor meeting space. Accommodating groups from 10 to 550, the event spaces include the Grand Ballroom, three heritage boardrooms, the Living Room, the Dining Room which includes a show kitchen, five flexible meeting rooms and the Event Studio. 
                  Some of London’s best cuisine features across the three restaurants; the Mirror Room, Scarfes Bar and Holborn Dining Room and Delicatessen. The hotel’s recreational facilities include a 24-hour fitness suite and Sense spa, including a luxury hair salon.
