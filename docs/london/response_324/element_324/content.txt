              Overview

              Set sail on Merlin Entertainments newest venture, The Barracuda! Designed with you, the event organiser in mind, the Barracuda’s retractable roof allows you to party under the stars whilst viewing London’s most iconic scenery along the River Thames. The Barracuda is a perfect dining and dance vessel, adding a timeless elegance through the art deco décor, framed with London’s skyline. She is ideal for intimate events or larger parties and dinners. The recently renovated vessel is moored next to the London Eye her sister attraction and can be integrated to make for a truly unique event.
