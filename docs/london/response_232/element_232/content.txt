              Overview

              Located in London's vibrant Angel district, Rosebery Hall contains the Myddelton Conference Suite. The hall is sited between the West End and the City.
                  Rosebery is a quick journey from major railway stations (Kings Cross/Euston) and a short walk from Angel Tube station.
                  Boasting a full bar, games area and TV room Rosebery can also accommodate over 400 guests with full English breakfast during student vacation periods with full on-site catering facilities also available. Perfect for training, interviews, team building days or classes. Contact us for further details.
                  This content has been supplied by LSE Rosebery Hall
