              Overview

              Bankside's eighth floor Conference Suite comprises of Auditorium, Balcony Room and three break-out rooms with other spaces available during the summer months. 
                  The spacious and pleasant rooms, with capacities up to 190, are ideal for conferences, training, interviews and receptions. With accommodation also available during the summer months (July to September). Bankside can also offer you the complete solution for residential conferences set in South Bank area. 
                  We offer you the flexibility and experience to make your event a success. Contact us for an information pack or to make a booking.
                  This content has been supplied by LSE Bankside House
