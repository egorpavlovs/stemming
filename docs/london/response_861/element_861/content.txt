              Overview

              Brand new state-of-the-art Conference Centre makes the Park Inn Heathrow hotel one of the largest conference venues at Heathrow Airport with 2,045 square meters of meeting space. It features 2 separate, multi-purpose conference centers and 41 bright, modern conference rooms with audiovisual equipment. A fully flexible approach enables the hiring of individual spaces or exclusive use of the entire convention center. Catering is available for up to 700 delegates. Our experienced team of event organizers uses their expertise to handle everything from the initial inquiry to last-minute requirements.
                  It's a haven of professionalism with its style, innovation and state-of-the-art equipment. Business Centre, 21 syndicate rooms with all natural daylight at competitive prices. Over 500 car-parking spaces. Dedicated conference desk and coordinators to serve our guests.
