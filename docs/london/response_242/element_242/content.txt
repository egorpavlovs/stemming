              Overview

              LSE is an academic institution of international standing.  With a global reputation, it draws students from across the world. 
                  Located in central London, LSE offers a wide range of conference facilities, from traditional committee style rooms seating up to 40, to the Peacock Theatre, one of a few venues in central London seating up to 1,000. 
                  If you are looking for a completely different style of venue, LSE's new year-round conference space BOX is a venue with a calm relaxed ambiance, combining home comforts with the latest facilities. A flexible space for up to 50, BOX is a versatile, interesting venue for varying sizes and types of events. 
                  LSE's Academic Building on Lincoln's Inn provides  world-class facilities to their conference portfolio.
                  This content has been supplied by London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE)
