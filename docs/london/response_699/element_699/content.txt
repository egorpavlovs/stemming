              Overview

              Cutty Sark is the only place in the world where you can entertain beneath the shining hull of a ship. This iconic historic venue provides flexibility in a beautifully unique heritage setting with amazing photography opportunities.
                  Dry Berth
                  Cutty Sark has the ultimate wow factor – the 963 ton sailing ship is suspended in her dock, creating a stunning space for entertaining underneath the hull. This amazing space can accommodate up to 300 guests for ceremonies, receptions, dinner and dancing.
                  The ’Tween Deck
                  Originally used to store the ship’s precious cargo the ’Tween Deck creates an atmospheric spot for your ceremony for up to 70 guests.
                  The Weather Deck
                  Overlooking the Thames with views across London and three towering masts and miles of rigging overhead, Cutty Sark’s Weather Deck makes a dramatic backdrop for drinks and photos.
                  Weddings
                  Capture the romance of the great age of the sail at this unforgettable London wedding venue; licensed for weddings and civil ceremonies.
