              Overview

              Situated in an exclusive 14,000m² site in picturesque Battersea Park, Battersea Evolution has a total footprint of 5,650m² split into four main areas; a 450m² glass fronted Atrium, a 3,750m² Main Arena, a 700m² mezzanine level and 750m² "back of house" area that can also be used as event space. The Main space can easily be subdivided to meet the capacity and function requirements of any event.
