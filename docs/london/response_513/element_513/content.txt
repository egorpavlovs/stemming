              Overview

              Embodying the very best values of the past through our impeccable service and attention to detail, Mandarin Oriental Hyde Park also looks to the future with cutting-edge dining at Heston Blumenthal’s two-Michelin starred Dinner and Daniel Boulud’s acclaimed Bar Boulud, an award-winning spa, stylish rooms and a range of exquisite event venues equipped with the latest technology.
                  Overlooking both the calming, leafy Hyde Park and the glamour of Knightsbridge, we are perfectly positioned in the heart of the city. Renowned for our versatility, we rise to the occasion for every kind of event, from intimate celebrations to important board meetings and major conferences.
