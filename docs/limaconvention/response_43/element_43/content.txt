			The Bureau of Lima makes successful presentation of destination in Mexico
																		
		
			Within the framework of IBTM Americas to be held on September 5 and 6 in Mexico City, the Lima Conventions Bureau yesterday made a successful presentation of its destination to prominent meeting planners of the Mexican market. 
The presentation that took place at the Presidente Intercontinental Hotel was carried out thanks to the support of the Commercial Office of Peru in Mexico, and in joint coordination with the Bureau of Conventions of Medellín, as part of the institutional agreement signed two years ago. which highlights the joint promotion in priority markets such as Mexico. 
It should be noted that the Peruvian companies co-exhibitors at IBTM Americas: Libertador Hotels, RLA SAV and Peru Specialist, were also part of the presentation, with the aim of delivering a complete offer to the audience.
