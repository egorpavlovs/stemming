	Infraestructure
					
  
	 
As the main point of entry to the country, Lima has a large investment and hotel infrastructure in recent years due to the favorable climate of investment and the economic stability that the nation is going through.                                                                                                                                                                        That is why today the hotel market in Lima has 9399 rooms between 5, 4 and 3 stars.
Como el principal punto de entrada al país, Lima ha venido desarrollando una amplia y moderna infraestructura hotelera en los últimos años debido al favorable clima de inversión y estabilidad económica por el que atraviesa la Nación.
Es por ello que hoy en día el mercado hotelero de Lima presenta 9399 habitaciones entre 5, 4 y 3 estrellas.
To date, the city is home to more than 20 of the main international hotel chains.
Lima has more than 330 meeting rooms with the most modern technology, to satisfy all the requirements of any type of event, national or international, and to turn the stay of the visitors into a unique experience.
On the other hand, the city has several tour operators, DMCs and PCOs with the highest standards of quality, extensive experience and professionalism, which ensure success in the organization and staging in any type of event. We have a wide portfolio of companies that provide complementary services for events such as: audiovisual equipment, translations, transportation, among others.
