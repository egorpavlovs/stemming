			Bureau of Conventions of Lima invites to participate in IBTM Americas 2018
																		
		
			The Bureau of Conventions and Visitors of Lima will participate for the third consecutive year in IBTM Americas 2018, the largest specialized fair in the region for all those professionals in the events and meetings industry who seek to expand their business opportunities.
This year, the fair experienced an important growth, merging with IBTM Las Americas and becoming the largest in the industry of events, congresses, conventions and incentive trips throughout the Americas, with pre-established appointments and unlimited opportunities with more than 1000 hosted buyers from Latin America, the United States and Canada.
In this sense, and after the success of the previous editions, the Bureau of Lima invites interested companies and institutions to participate in this important international fair, where the main actors involved in the organization of events and trips meet. of incentives of the continent.
IBTM Americas 2018 will take place at the CitiBanamex Center in Mexico City, on September 5 and 6 of this year. Those interested in participating can request more information by email: captacion@limaconvention.com (limited places).
