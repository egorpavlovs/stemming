	Mission and Vision
					
  
	
 
Our Mission
To position Lima as a world-class destination for the realization of congresses, conventions, events and national and international incentives, providing professional information, services and infrastructure, generating benefits for the community.
 
Our Vision
The city of Lima as a international highly competitive destination for the realization of events, offering quality services and infrastructure, as well as culture, modernity and entertainment.
