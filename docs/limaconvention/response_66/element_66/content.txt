			Jorge León Benavides, President of the Bureau of Conventions and Visitors of Lima, is the director of the Latin American and Caribbean Association of Conventions & Visitors Bureaus
																		
		
			The President of the Bureau of Conventions of Lima together with the members of the Board of Directors 2018-2020 of the international association
Jorge León Benavides, President of the Bureau of Conventions of Lima, was elected member of the Board of Directors of the Latin American and Caribbean Association of Conventions & Visitors Bureaus; institution comprised of 222 South American and Central American tourist destinations, whose main objectives are to contribute to the sustainable growth of the events industry in Latin America and the Caribbean and to the development of public and private policies that strengthen destinations.
The election of the new Board of Directors was held on June 6, in the framework of the 11th Latin American Meeting of Bureaus, of the 5th Latin American Political Forum on Meetings Tourism and of Fiexpo Latin America, trade fair specialized in the meetings industry It was carried out in Santiago, Chile.
“The interaction generated by this Latin American association is fundamental for the development of the region in the events industry. For each of our destinations, it means an extraordinary opportunity to be known as a block (region), with excellent infrastructure and world-class services in cities that generate unique experiences such as Lima, “said Jorge León Benavides.
As recalled, on April 27 Jorge León Benavides was elected President of the Bureau of Conventions and Visitors of Lima, a private association whose main function is to promote the city of Lima as a leading event destination covering all branches of the congress industry, conventions, fairs and incentive trips.
