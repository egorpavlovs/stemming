            Business Events Toronto
        
    

    
    
        
            
            Why Toronto?
            Toronto is the number one overall convention city in Canada, a top-five convention city in North America – and home to Business Events Toronto, Canada’s number one convention and visitors bureau.
            Why Toronto?
        

        
            
            Hotels & Venues
            With magnificent hotel properties, exclusive clubs, glamorous lounges, rooftop patios, showcase museums, picturesque wineries and much more, Toronto offers an extraordinary selection of inspiring and stimulating venues for your events.
            Discover Hotels & Venues
        

        
            
            TORONTO SHINES BLUE FOR #GMID19
            In celebration of Global Meetings Industry Day (GMID), some of Toronto’s most recognizable landmarks and meeting facilities were lit blue to showcase the value that business meetings, conferences, conventions, incentive travel, trade shows and exhibitions bring to businesses and the economy. 

            Read more here.
        

    

    

        
            
            Convention Facilities
            Toronto show facilities attract millions of attendees – and for good reason. With over 2.5 million square feet of exhibition halls and convention facilities available, there’s no event Toronto can’t handle.
            Explore Toronto’s convention facilities
        
        
        
            
            Attendance Marketing Toolkit
            Business Events Toronto is at your service. Take advantage of custom web pages, online photo library, b-roll footage and other resources to promote attendance at your event.
            Learn more about our Attendance Marketing Toolkit
