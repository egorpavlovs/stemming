    	Sport Event Testimonials
 

    
    
      
       	
        
          
        We couldn’t have said it better ourselves!
            
        World Ringette Championships 2018
        
        “From the bid process, to early promotion and planning, the team were always there to help!  The posters, banners and signs were absolutely beautiful and professional, which set the tone going forward with the project, and welcomed the athletes on their arrival.  We are extremely pleased with how everything turned out and so proud to have represented Mississauga and Toronto/GTA west.”
        
        — Abby Salb, President, Mississauga Ringette Association
        
         
        
        World Masters Athletics Championships 2020
        
        “Business Events Toronto was a vital part of our winning bid for the World Masters Athletics Championships in Toronto in 2020. The expertise, energy and guidance that they provided was critical in the development of our presentation, and their insights saved us time, kept us on track, and instilled professionalism in our entire organization. Their financial and material support has provided us with a smart, effective and meaningful approach to our bid and our partnerships, and has helped us focus on hosting a wonderful, world-class event.”
        
        — John Craig, CEO – Toronto Athletics Event Management Inc. (TAEM)
        
         
        
        North American Indigenous Games – NAIG 2017
        
        “We are extremely grateful to the team at Business Events Toronto. They played a critical role in our event every step of the way. From bidding to final reports, the Business Events Toronto team provided a plethora of services and event enhancements that made the 2017 North American Indigenous Games one of the most memorable from a participant/spectator experience perspective. We are extremely thankful to Tourism Toronto for becoming a significant partner in our efforts to bid AND host the Games in Toronto.”
        
        — Chris Charlebois, President & CEO – C2C Sports & Entertainment Inc.  – NAIG 2017
        
         
        
        Senior National Badminton Championships 2018
        
        “We had a fantastic tournament.  The feedback from the players, officials, Boulevard Club members and Badminton Canada staff and Board of Directors was extremely positive.  I do intend on hosting this event again in Toronto.”
        
        Stephane Cadieux – Badminton Director – Boulevard Club
        
         
        
        Divas Half Marathon and 5K 2017
        
        “GWN Events is grateful for the support Business Events Toronto provides. As a sport-tourism event planner, Business Events Toronto’s knowledge and resources provide in-measurable value. In launching the Divas Half Marathon and 5K in 2017, the team assisted in navigating the approval process, promoting the event and connecting us with key stakeholders.”
        
        — Aaron Soroka, Chief Operating Officer – GWN Events
        
         
        
        Share Your Experience
        
        If you’ve recently hosted a sports event in Toronto and want to share your experience or need information on how to host an event in the region, please contact our Sports Events team.
