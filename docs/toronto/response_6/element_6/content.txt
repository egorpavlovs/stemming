      Client Services
    
  

  
    
      
    
    
       
    
    
      
    
  

  
    

      Client Services at Business Events Toronto is a dedicated team that provides 360° Service to organizations from around the world meeting in Canada’s Downtown. Our team offers tools, resources and connections, all highly valuable assets for clients hosting their meetings in Toronto.

      From large city-wide conventions, conferences, trade shows and everything in between, the Business Events Toronto team offers unique market insight, industry experience, personal attention, with access to the best and brightest the city has to offer. Our team is the link between a client’s vision and the city resources available, including a network of more than 1,000 member businesses, with access to a wide variety of inspiring venues, spaces, places and people suited to their needs.

      Business Events Toronto provides the expertise that brings events to life, with consumer insight, advice and recommendations. The attention to detail and client needs have helped us to consistently be recognized as one of the top meeting and convention locations in North America.

      Business Events Toronto makes it easy to plan and organize events in the city, offering a myriad of other seamless services including but not limited to:

      
      Attendance-building and promotional resources commencing with year prior support to assist through planning, coordination and execution of events
      Assistance with site selection and visits
      Customized microsites
      Customs and immigrations liaisons
      Assistance in identifying local leaders in your field
      Access to innovative partnerships within the local community
      Media support including PR, social media and online resources
      

      Our team provides customer service, local relationships and partners, experience, ease of access, memorable events coming together to bring our client’s vision to fruition in Toronto.
